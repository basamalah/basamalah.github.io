8. .Maria has two printers – Printer Z and Printer A. She set Printer Z as the default printer. What will happen the next time she prints a document?


What will happen the next time she prints a document? --- The answer is Printer Z will print her documents.

Even when Maria has two printers, since she has set up Printer Z as her default printer, once she has printed her documents, Printer Z will be her default printer, meaning it will be chosen automatically. In any case that she would want to use her other printer, she must select printers on Print options. A default printer is a type of printer which print jobs will be automatically accepted unless she has changed the option.  

To Select default printers, user might navigate to Start Menu > Control Panel > Printers & Scanners then on the printer list, he must right - click on the name of the printer then select Set as Default.

17. upon connecting your printer to your PC which of the following is not essential step to do?​a.unplug printer from outlet b.use printer cordc.turn on your printerd.wait for printer to be detected


The correct answer is a. unplug printer from the outlet

To connect a printer to your computer, never unplug any device from the power supply. When a device is turned off, even if it is connected with other devices via cables, it will not be recognized by other devices because there is no power.  

To connect your printer to your computer, perform the following:

1. Plugged in both devices and turned the power on.

2. Connect the printer to the computer via printer chord or USB. This is for printers with cables or stand-alone printers only.

3. Sometimes, printer can easily be recognized by the Operating System or OS. Upon connecting each device, the OS can now automatically install the printer software to the computer. In some cases, auto installation cannot proceed. If this is the case, you must install the printer driver before you can use the printer. A driver is a special software that would allow the printer and computer communication smoothly.

4. After driver installation, you must perform a print test to see if the connection is successful.
