COUNT NOUN

Count noun refer to people, places, animals, and things that can be counted. For example, 2 frogs, 10 pillows, 24 eggs, and 40 people are all count nouns.

MASS NOUN

Mass nouns refer to liquid, powder, and things that can't be counted. For example, water, sand, glue, coffee, milk powder, and alcohol. are all mass nouns.
