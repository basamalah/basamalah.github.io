if mABC = 6x + 4 and mDAB = 4x - 14 what is the mDAB?

[tex]\underline{\mathcal{SOLUTION}}:[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\:\:\:\:\:\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\:\:\:\:\:\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\boxed{\begin{array}{l}\sf \small m∠ABC + m∠DAB = 180 \\ \\ \sf \small \: \: 6x + 4 + 4x - 14 = 180 \\ \\ \sf \small \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 10x = 180 + 10 \\ \\ \sf \small \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: x = 19 \\ \\ \sf \small m∠DAB = (4 × 19 - 14) \\ \\ \sf \small \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: m∠DAB = 62 \end{array}}\end{gathered} \end{gathered}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathcal{ANSWER}}:[/tex]

[tex]\small\boxed{\sf{m∠DAB = 62}}[/tex]
