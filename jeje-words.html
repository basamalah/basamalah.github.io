jeje and Ronald = amount paid for works

Hours work(+) = total amount paid

8.5 + 7.5 = P 1,128.00

Hours work (16) = P 1,128.00

per hour work = amount paid ÷ hours of

work

per hour work = P 1,128.00 ÷16

per hour work = 70.5

2) :amount hours of work × per hour work

= total amount to be paid

jeje. = 8.5 × 70.5

= 599.25

= 528.75

How animals obtain sulfur?

Animals obtain sulfur they need by eating plants or other animals, and digesting and assimilating their organic forms of sulfur, which are then used to synthesize necessary sulfur containing biochemicals. Sulfur (S) can occur in many chemical forms in the environment.
