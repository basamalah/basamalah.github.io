Rate: 19 golf balls/hour

19 × 23 = 437

437 golf balls/23 hours

Bill can hit 437 golf balls in 23 hours.

Let x = hour

Solve for golf balls hit per day

Math equation:

17x/24 = 323

17x = 7752

x = 456


Golf balls hit per day is 456


Solve for how many golf balls hit for 23 hours

x = 456 (23/24)

= 437

27. er the following problems: Bill has 5 balls of different colors. In how many possible combination can he choose two balls?​


Answer:

10

Step-by-step explanation:

To calculate the number of combinations, we can use the formula for combinations:

nCr = n! / r!(n-r)!

where n is the total number of items, and r is the number of items chosen.

In this case, n = 5 and r = 2, so we have:

5C2 = 5! / 2!(5-2)! = 10

Therefore, there are 10 possible combinations that Bill can choose two balls out of five.
