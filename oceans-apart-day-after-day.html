3. solve the problem on proportion. Twelve men finished in 18 days building a small apartment in a subdivision. If the landlord added 8 more men to help, how many days will it take for them to build another same apartment?


ANSWER

Answer: 10.8 days

SOLUTION

If 12 men finished building a small apartment in 18 days, then 1 man can build 1/12 of the apartment in 18 days.

If it takes 1 man 18 days to build 1/12 of the apartment, then it will take 18 x 12 = 216 days for 12 men to build the apartment.

If it takes 216 days for 12 men to build the apartment, then it will take 216/20 = 10.8 days for 20 men to build the apartment.

Answer:

To solve this problem, we need to use the proportion formula:

(time taken by smaller group) / (number of people in smaller group) = (time taken by larger group) / (number of people in larger group)

We know that the smaller group took 18 days to build an apartment, and that there were 12 men in the group. We can plug these values into the proportion formula like this:

(18 days) / (12 men) = (X days) / (20 men)We know that the smaller group took 18 days to build an apartment, and that there were 12 men in the group. We can plug these values into the proportion formula like this:

(18 days) / (12 men) = (X days) / (20 men)

We want to solve for X, so we need to cross-multiply:

18 days * 20 men = 12 men * X days

This simplifies to:

360 = 12X

To solve for X, we divide both sides of the equation by 12:

360 / 12 = 12X / 12

This simplifies to:

30 = X

So it will take the larger group of 20 men 30 days to build another apartment.

The expansion of objects is influenced by 3 factors, namely the size of the original object, the type of object and the change in temperature.

There are 3 types of expansion in solids

Linear expansion Area expansion Volume expansion

Linear expansion is the increase in the length of an object due to changes in temperature (changes in area and volume are negligible). Generally occurs in long but thin objects.

Can be formulated :

ΔL= Lo .α .Δ T

L = the final length of the object (m)

L0 = initial length (m)

α = coefficient of linear expansion (/ 0C)

ΔT = change in temperature (C0)  

Given

Lo = 50 m

t₁ = 10 °C

t₂ = 35 °C

Required

L

Solution

A copper telephone wire will experience linear expansion(length) when heated.

coefficient of linear expansion of copper : 1.7 x 10⁻⁵ /°C

10. Boundary found where plates are movingapart at mid-ocean​


Answer:

The phenomenon is known today as plate tectonics. In locations where two plates move apart, at mid-ocean ridges, new seafloor is continually formed during seafloor spreading.

Explanation:

When two plates are moving away from each other, we call this a divergent plate boundary. Along these boundaries, magma rises from deep within the Earth and erupts to form new crust on the lithosphere. Most divergent plate boundaries are underwater and form submarine mountain ranges called oceanic spreading ridges.

16. mountains are created when______A.two continental plates collideB.an oceanic plate subduct under a continental plateC.two oceanic plates pull apartD.two continental place pull apart​


Answer:

Explanation:

Pa brainliest po

Answer:

The answer is

A. Two Continental plates collide

Explanation:

At some convergent boundaries, an oceanic plate collides with a continental plate. Oceanic crust tends to be denser and thinner than continental crust, so the denser oceanic crust gets bent and pulled under, or subducted, beneath the lighter and thicker continental crust. This forms what is called a subduction zone. As the oceanic crust sinks, a deep oceanic trench, or valley, is formed at the edge of the continent. The crust continues to be forced deeper into the earth, where high heat and pressure cause trapped water and other gasses to be released from it. This, in turn, makes the base of the crust melt, forming magma
