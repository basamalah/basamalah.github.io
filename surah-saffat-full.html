30. my reflection in surah Al-imran​


Answer:

Surah Al-Imran, also known as The Family of Imran, is the third chapter of the Quran and contains many important lessons for believers. It highlights the importance of faith, family values, and the belief in one God. The Surah also narrates the stories of Prophet Jesus, Mary, and their family, emphasizing the concept of Miracles and the divine power of God. Through this Surah, we are reminded of the importance of following the teachings of Islam and the consequences of turning away from the path of righteousness.
