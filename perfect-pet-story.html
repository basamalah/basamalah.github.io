13. Let's Get Started Write your understanding for the following: Settings Characters PlotTitle of story: The pet shop ​


Answer:

Settings:

The pet shop is the primary setting of the story, where various types of pets are kept and sold to customers. The shop might be located in an urban or suburban area.

Characters:

There is likely a variety of characters in the pet shop story. The protagonist might be a pet store employee, a customer, or even a pet itself. Other potential characters include other employees or customers, and possibly pets.

Plot:

The plot of The Pet Shop could involve a variety of events. The story might have a basic plot about a customer coming in to buy a pet and finding the perfect one, or it could have a more complex plot involving a pet that is lost or stolen from the shop, or challenges that the employees face in the daily operation of the pet shop.

15. is the story "lam-ang and his magic pets" true in real life?​


Answer:

No

Explanation:

Biag ni Lam-ang is an epic poem of the Ilocano people from the Ilocos region of the Philippines. It is notable for being the first Philippine folk epic to be recorded in written form and was one of only two folk epics documented during the Philippines' Spanish Colonial period, along with the Bicolano epic of Handiong. It is also noted for being a folk epic from a "Christianized" lowland people group (the Ilocano people), with elements incorporated into the storytelling.
