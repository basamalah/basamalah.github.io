When we say A U B it reads as The union of A and B meaning, the combination of all the elements of A and B, combined it.

However, when we say A N B (N is the symbol of the upside down symbol U) it reads as The intersection of A and B meaning, the common elements. Common elements are elements that are both present in A and B. Gets?

Another example:

A has three elements (1 2 3)

B has three elements also which is (2 4 6)

The A U B is 1 2 3 4 and 6
