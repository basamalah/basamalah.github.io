6. Search for an example of a worksheet. Explain the different columns included in the worksheet.


Accounting Worksheet Columns Included:

-Unadjusted Trial Balance

-Adjusting Entries

-Adjusted Trial Balance

-Income Statement

-Balance Sheet

Explanations:

Unadjusted Trial Balance

A column contains all the company’s assets, liability, expenses, and revenue accounts, which are used in a concerned year.

Adjusting Entries

All the accounts of the company, which requires the passing of the adjustment entry will get listed in the adjustment column.

Adjusted Trial Balance

The adjusted trial balance will be prepared by combining the entries of the previous two columns, Unadjusted trial balance, and adjustments.

Income Statement

A column contains the values concerning the expenses and revenue accounts only. In this case, if the value of total revenue exceeds the expense column, then the difference will be the net income of the year of the company as it is earning more revenues in the year than what it is spending on its expenses.

Balance Sheet

A column contains the values with respect to the assets, liabilities, and owner’s capital only.

24. These are used for switching to other worksheets within the workbook. You can keep multiple worksheets together in a group called workbook. You can move quickly one worksheet to another by clicking the worksheet tabs ​


Answer:

Every workbook contains at least one worksheet by default. When working with a large amount of data, you can create multiple worksheets to help organize your workbook and make it easier to find content. You can also group worksheets to quickly add information to multiple worksheets at the same time.

Answer:

You can move worksheets with the keyboard by using Ctrl+PgUp and Ctrl+PgDown. Ctrl+PgUp moves toward the first worksheet in the workbook and Ctrl+PgDown moves toward the last.

Explanation:

On the Edit menu, click Sheet > Move or Copy Sheet. On the To book menu, click the workbook that you want to move the sheet to. Tip: To create a new workbook that contains the moved sheet, click new book. In the Before sheet box, click the sheet that you want to insert the moved sheet before, or click move to end.
