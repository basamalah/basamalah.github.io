6. 1.Ano ang trabaho ng PDIC? 2.Ano ang BSP? 3.Ano ang SEC? 4.Anoang CTD? 5.Ano ang ATM?6.Ano ang CTDs? Pa sagott po plsss​


1. PDIC stands for Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation. Its primary job is to protect depositors in case a bank is unable to pay its depositors' insured deposits due to financial problems or bank closure. PDIC provides insurance coverage to bank depositors in case of bank closure or liquidation.
2. BSP stands for Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas. It is the central monetary authority of the Philippines, responsible for maintaining price stability and promoting sustainable economic growth.
3. SEC stands for Securities and Exchange Commission. It is the government agency mandated to regulate the securities industry and protect the investing public against fraudulent and manipulative practices.
4. CTD stands for Cash and Term Deposit. It refers to a type of bank account that combines a checking account and a fixed deposit account. It allows customers to have easy access to their funds while earning interest on their deposits.
5. ATM stands for Automated Teller Machine. It is an electronic banking outlet that allows customers to complete basic transactions without the aid of a branch representative or teller.
6. CTDs stands for Certificates of Time Deposit. It refers to a type of deposit account in which a customer agrees to deposit a sum of money for a specific period, ranging from 30 days to several years, and earns interest on their deposit. The interest rate is typically higher than that of a savings account, but the money is not available for withdrawal until the end of the agreed-upon term.

11. Three gases, Ar, N2 and H2 are mixed in a 500L container. Ar has a pressure of 2 atm, N2 has a pressure of 3 atm and H2 has pressure of 2 atm. What is the total pressure in the container?a. 2 atm b. 3 atm c. 5 atm d. 7 atm​.


Answer:

[tex] \therefore [/tex] The total pressure in the container is 7 atm (D.)

Explanation:

We have three gases which is [tex] \rm{Ar, \ N_2, \ and \ H_2} [/tex] they all mixed in 500L. So, we want to there total pressure. To know how many total of pressure in the container, we will use Dalton's Equation.

[tex] \boxed{\bold{P_{Total} = P_1 + P_2 + P_3...}}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered}\begin{gathered}\begin{aligned} & \bold{Thus, \ the \ given \ are:} \\ & \quad \boxed{\begin{array}{l} \Longrightarrow \rm{P_1} = 2 \ atm \\ \Longrightarrow \rm{P_2} = 3 \ atm \\ \Longrightarrow \rm{P_3} = 2 \ atm \end{array}}\, \\ \end{aligned} \end{gathered}\end{gathered} [/tex]

Substitute it to the formula

[tex] \rm{P_{Total} = 2 \ atm + 3 \ atm + 2 \ atm} [/tex][tex] \rm{P_{Total} = 4 \ atm + 3 \ atm} [/tex][tex] \rm{P_{Total} = 7 \ atm}[/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] The total pressure in the container is 7 atm (D.)
