Answer:

Explanation:

The obligation is extinguished and A is not liable to B if B passed the board exam. A person, as a general rule, is not liable for a fortuitous event. (Art. 1174)

26. what is the difference between board and bar exam?


The difference between board and bar exam

One of the difference between board and bar exam is that board exam is a generalized term for the exam by whicg aspiring professionals need to take for them to be licensed in their own fields. Meanwhile, bar exam is a specialized type of examination for aspiring lawyers.

Here is a simplier representation of the differences between board and bar exam:

Board examTaken by aspiring professionalConducted by the PRC

Bar ExamTaken by aspiring lawyersConducted by the Philippine Bar Examination

Some careers which require board exam:

Accountancy.Aeronautical Engineering.Agricultural Engineering.Agriculture.Architecture.Chemical Engineering.Chemistry.Civil Engineering.
