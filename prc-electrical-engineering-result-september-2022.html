10. Give the different Genetic Engineering tool or processes, give the function and application of each.(GENETIC ENGINEERING)Tool/Processes:Restriction-EnzymesGelElectrophoresis-RecombinantDNA Technology-Gene Cloning-DNA Sequencing-Polymerase Chain-Reaction (PRC)​


Answer:

TOOL/PROCESSES;

RESTRICTION ENZYMES:

FUNCTION

-the process of cutting the DNA strands into fragments.

APPLICATION

-this is basically used to create DNA fragments with sticky ends or blunt ends that can join with other DNA fragments.

GEL ELECTROPHORESIS:

FUNCTION

-the process of separating DNA fragments by size.

APPLICATION

-this is used to study DNA fragments of various sizes.

RECOMBINANT DNA TECHNOLOGY:

FUNCTION

-the combination of a DNA fragments with the DNA from another source.

APPLICATION

-this is also called exogenous DNA.

GENE CLONING :

FUNCTION

-the production of the large numbers of identical recombinant DNA molecules.

APPLICATION

-used to create large amounts of recombinant DNA to be used in genetically engineered organism.

DNA SEQUENCING:

FUNCTION

-used to identify the DNA sequence of cloned recombinant DNA molecule to further study.

APPLICATION

-used to identify errors in the DNA sequences to predict the function of a particular gene and to compare to other genes with similar sequences from different organisms.

POLYMERASE CHAIN REACTION (PCR):

FUNTION

-used to make copies of specific regions of sequenced DNA.

APPLICATION

-used to copy DNA for any scientific investigation including forensic analysis and medical testing.
