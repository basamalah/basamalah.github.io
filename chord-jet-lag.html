The averaged speed of Jet Aircraft is 240 miles per hours.

Step-by-step explanation:

Is known :

720 miles jet + 80 miles helicopter = Total 800 miles tripTotal time trip = 4 hours (240 minute)Distanceof jet aircraft : Distance of helicopter = 4/5 or 80 : 1/5 or 20Jet averaged speed : Helicopter averaged speed = 3 : 1

This means that the combined average speed of a jet aircraft and helicopter on a business trip is 200 miles per hour with jet aircraft traveling 4 times more far than helicopters.

Averaged speed Aircraft Jet :

= (ratio distance) x (ratio averaged)

= (80) x (3)

= 240 Miles per hours

Is known that jet averaged 3 times the speed of the helicopter so the avered of helicopter is :

= (Averaged Aircraft Jet) / 3

= 240 / 3

= 80

So during a business trip, an executive traveled using a jet aircraft with a speed of 240 miles per hour for 180 minutes or 3 hours with a distance of 720 miles and continued with a helicopter trip at a speed of 80 miles per hour for 1 hour or 60 minutes with a distance of 80 miles. So the total journey is 4 hours with a distance of 800 miles (720 + 80).
