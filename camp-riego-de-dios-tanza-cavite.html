Answer: A. Tejeros Convention

In 1897 the Tejeros Convention was held in order to reconcile the differences between the two factions of the Katipunan: the Magdalo, which viewed Emilio Aguinaldo y Famy as its leader, and the Magdiwang, which gravitated towards Andres Bonifacio

This church has a historical significance because this was were the oath taking of Katipunero leaders took place.
