30. 1. What is the importance of Automata Theory in the life of human beings?​


The Importance of Automata Theory in the Life of Humans

Automata Theory has significantly improved our understanding about how computers work. It also has helped us to design new forms of computers, called universal machines. By understanding programming languages, we can create computer programs that can perform many more functions than the ones they were designed for. And exploration of computational complexity, has both aided in developing faster computers and has allowed us to understand what exactly makes some tasks so hard.

Automata theory is also important in the life of human beings because it can be used to create artificial life through simulation methods. A simple example is that it can be used to simulate evolution by using cellular automaton (CA) models. Simulation has led to the development of artificial intelligence (AI) systems that are able to learn, solve problems and even act intelligently without any pre-programming.
