27. 1. An equilibrium mixture contains H at 470 torr, N2 at 190 torr, and 286 torr. What is the totalpressure of the gases in the system?​


Given:

[tex]P_{\text{H}_{2}} = \text{470 torr}[/tex]

[tex]P_{\text{N}_{2}} = \text{190 torr}[/tex]

[tex]P_{\text{O}_{2}} = \text{286 torr}[/tex]

Unknown:

[tex]P_{\text{T}}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]P_{\text{T}} = P_{\text{H}_{2}} + P_{\text{N}_{2}} + P_{\text{O}_{2}}[/tex]

[tex]P_{\text{T}} = \text{470 torr + 190 torr + 286 torr}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{P_{\text{T}} = \text{946 torr}}[/tex]
