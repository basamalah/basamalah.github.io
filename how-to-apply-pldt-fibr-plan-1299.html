1. Ang PLDT Fibr ba cabled internet??


ANSWER:

Yes.

EXPLANATION:

All internet connections are connected through a cable. In PLDT Fibr's case, they are using a cable that is special, known as a fiber-optic cable which is then connected to the main line of the internet (commonly seen above the roads, near electricity wirings).

Fiber-optic cables are used as an alternative to serve high-quality speed internet. Fiber-optic cables are also light which is very convenient for others to replace. ISPs such as PLDT promise that upload & download speeds are equally high on Fiber plans because they are using fiber-optic cables.
