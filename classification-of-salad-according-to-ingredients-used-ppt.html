What is a salad?

Salad is a type of dish that consist of mixtures of fruits, vegetables, dressings and sometimes accompanied with meat and fish.

Some of the classification of salad according to the ingredients used:

Green Salad - almost all salad has green leafy veggies on it. But this type of salad has only green leafy veggies like lettuce that are accompanied with seasoning and sauce.Caesar Salad - It is the common type of salad, it is known for its dressing, that are made from cheese, egg yolks, mustard, anchovies, olive oil, lemon juice and seasonings.Chefs Salad - It is also the most basic type of salad, it is usually made from lettuce with strips of ham/turkey or chicken along with different types of cheese.Coleslaw - It is usually made from shredded raw cabbage with mayonaise as a dressing.
