Candidates in the 2022 Philippine presidential election

Commission on Elections is expected to publish a tentative list of candidates by late October 2021. By start of the election period itself on early January 2022, the commission would have finalized a list of candidates that would appear on the ballot.

A total of 97 individuals manifested their intention to run for president, while 29 did so for vice president.
