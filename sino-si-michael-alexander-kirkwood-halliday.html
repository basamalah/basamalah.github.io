Answer:

Sino si Michael A. K. Halliday?

Si Michael Alexander Kirkwood Halliday ay isang linguist na ipinanganak sa Ingles na bumuo ng internasyunal na maimpluwensyang systemic functional linguistics model ng wika. Ang kanyang mga paglalarawan sa gramatika ay tinatawag na systemic functional grammar.

Paano siya nag-ambag sa pagsusulong ng maka-agham na pag-aaral ng wika?

Ang pag-aaral ni Halliday ay nagpakita kung paano bumuo ang mga bata ng grammar sa mga yugto sa pamamagitan ng mga partikular na pakikipag-ugnayan ng kanilang buhay. Ito ay humantong sa paglalathala ng kanyang seminal na gawaing Learning how to Mean noong 1975, kung saan si Neil ay binigyan ng medyo hindi nakakumbinsi na pseudonym na 'Nigel'.

9. sino si alexander great​


ALEXANDER THE GREAT (ALEXANDER THE THIRD)Si Alexander III o mas kilala sa tawag na Alexander the great ay isang hari ng sinaunang griyego ng Macedonia. Ipinanganak siya noong 356 B.C. sa Pella, na siyang punong lungsod ng Macedonia. Sinundan niya ang yapak ng kanyang ama na si haring Philip II pagkatapos ng asasinasyon nito noong 336 B.C.. Siya ay namatay sa Babylonia noong 323 B.C. sa edad na 32 anyos.

Noong nanunungkulan si haring Philip II sa Macedonia ay napagpasyahan niya na pagkasunduin ang mga taga Macedonia at ang mga griyego, na siyang lalong nagpalaki ng nasasakupan ng Macedonia, at si Alexander ang nagsilbing kapitan-heneral nito na ang unang layunin ay sakupin ang Asya minor, dahil sa matagal na giyerahan ng griyego at persiano.


10. Sino si Alexander the great? ​


Answer: Why is Alexander the Great famous? Although king of ancient Macedonia for less than 13 years, Alexander the Great changed the course of history. One of the world's greatest military generals, he created a vast empire that stretched from Macedonia to Egypt and from Greece to part of India.

Explanation:

Answer:

Alexander the Great is one of the most legendary and influential figures in world history. He was a prominent and powerful military leader of the ancient world whose many accomplishments had a profound and lasting impact. He was born into the ancient Macedonian monarchy in 356 B.C., a member of the Argead dynasty, and rose to fame through his conquering of a vast empire, spanning from Egypt and Greece in the west to parts of India in the east.

Alexander was born to King Philip II of Macedon and Queen Olympia of Epirus. His mother was known for her ambition and intensity, and his father was a fierce and courageous warrior who sought to expand his kingdom and strengthen the Macedonian power base. Alexander was tutored in politics and philosophy by Aristotle at a young age, and inherited his father's ambition and talent for military strategy. By the age of 18 he had become one of the greatest generals of his time, leading the Macedonian army in numerous successful military campaigns against the Persians and other neighboring enemies.

Alexander inherited a well-trained and well-equipped Macedonian army, and with it he began a campaign of conquest that would extend his rule over a vast region of land. Alexander's military genius is well documented by ancient historians, with even his enemies marvelling over his tactics, skill and courage. He conquered the city of Troy and defeated the Persian King Darius III, capturing Babylon, Damascus and Jerusalem. He made Athens a subject state and integrated its laws and customs into the Greek mainland. Alexander's reign also saw an impressive cultural exchange between East and West, with numerous scientific, philosophical and political advances being made from the conquered areas.

Alexander's military ambition was only matched by his ambition for achieving greatness. He was a passionate advocate for diplomacy and understanding between different cultures, and strived for a unified world culture. He became a philosopher-king in his own right, and under his rule art, science, archaeology and education flourished. Alexander's legacy lives on to this day and he is renowned for being a leader of unparalleled greatness. He has been immortalized in a range of works such as literature, music and art, and his life is studied by academics and military strategists alike.

Alexander the Great was an inspirational figure who helped to shape the ancient world through his conquering of vast lands, pioneering cultural exchange and passionate pursuit of greatness. His legacy continues to this day and his achievements are seen as an example of one man's ambition leading to an incredible level of success.
