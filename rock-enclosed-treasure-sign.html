13. Line segment top enclose A B end enclose , measuring 130 feet, represents the slant height of the square pyramid. Line segment top enclose A C end enclose represents the altitude of the pyramid. Line segment top enclose B C end enclose is half the length of the base of the pyramid.slant height AB labeled 130 feet and side length measured 240 feet. The altitude AC and line segment BC are connected by a right angle.What is the length of altitude top enclose A C end enclose ?


Using the Pythagorean theorem, we can find the length of line segment BC:

BC = (1/2) * base = (1/2) * 240 = 120 feet

Now, we can use the Pythagorean theorem again to find the length of line segment AC:

AC^2 = AB^2 - BC^2

AC^2 = 130^2 - 120^2

AC^2 = 16900

AC = 130 feet

Therefore, the length of altitude top enclose A C end enclose is 130 feet.

If I were to choose which type of treasure to keep, I would prefer measurable treasures. While countable treasures can bring temporary satisfaction, they do not necessarily lead to long-term happiness and fulfillment. Measurable treasures, such as personal growth and experiences, can bring lasting satisfaction and can help shape a person's character and values.
