18. why lack of access to healthcare is important issue in the philippines


Answer:

Access to healthcare is an important issue in the Philippines for numerous reasons. Firstly, the country has one of the highest poverty rates in the world, with more than 25% of the population living in extreme poverty. This means that many people lack the financial resources to pay for medical care and health services. Additionally, there is a severe shortage of healthcare professionals across the country, particularly in rural areas. This leads to a lack of access to quality healthcare, which can lead to poorer health outcomes. Finally, many areas lack infrastructure such as hospitals and clinics, which limits access to care even further. All of these factors mean that access to healthcare is an important issue in the Philippines and one that needs to be addressed.
