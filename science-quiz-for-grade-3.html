Step-by-step explanation:

3. To maintain an average grade of 85, the sum of your grades for the first four exams must be (85 x 4) = 340.

The sum of your current grades is (85 + 82 + 75 + 90) = 332.

To find out what you need on the 5th exam, you can subtract the sum of your current grades from the total required (340 - 332) = 8.

Therefore, you must score at least 8 points on the 5th exam to maintain an average grade of 85.

4. To get a final grade of 90 in Math, the sum of your grades for all four quarters must be (90 x 4) = 360.

The sum of your current grades is (87 + 88 + 90) = 265.

To find out what grade you need on the 4th quarter, you can subtract the sum of your current grades from the total required (360 - 265) = 95.

Therefore, you need to get a grade of at least 95 in the 4th quarter to get a final grade of 90.
