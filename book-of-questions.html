1. The pen is at rest. 
2. Yes. The forces acting on the pen are the force exerted by the string on the pen and the force of gravity.

Situation 2: Book on a table  
4. The book is at rest. 
5. Yes. The forces acting on the book are the force exerted by the table on the book and the force of gravity.
