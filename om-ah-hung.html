27. Why are ome part of the philippineoften hit by typhoon and ome are no?.


The Philippines is an archipelago located in a region of the western Pacific Ocean called the “Typhoon Belt.” This area of the world experiences more typhoons and tropical storms than almost any other part of the world, making it highly vulnerable to typhoons. Some parts of the Philippines are hit more frequently by typhoons than others because of the geographical variations in the terrain, ocean currents, and wind patterns. Areas that are more exposed to the ocean and have more mountainous terrain tend to experience stronger winds and more frequent typhoons. Areas that are more sheltered from the ocean and have flatter terrain tend to experience fewer typhoons.
