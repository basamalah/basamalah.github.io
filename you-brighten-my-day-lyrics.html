11. smile delicate as dew sticks to a leaf of memory even if written in one line brightens up the dayPakistan explain po plss​


Answer:

The phrase "smile delicate as dew sticks to a leaf of memory" is a metaphor that compares a smile to dew that sticks to a leaf. The comparison emphasizes the delicacy and fragility of a smile, much like how dew is a delicate and fragile liquid that clings to a leaf. The metaphor also implies that a smile has a fleeting or transient nature, much like how dew quickly evaporates or dries up.

The phrase "even if written in one line brightens up the day" suggests that a simple reminder of a smile can bring joy and lighten up one's day, even if it is something as small as a single line of writing. It implies that something as small and seemingly insignificant as a smile can have a big impact on one's mood and outlook. Overall, the phrase is expressing the idea that a smile, even if it is small or fleeting, can make a big difference in a person's day.
