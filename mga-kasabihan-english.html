11. kasabihan tungkol sa lunar eclipse english po


Sayings and superstitious about Lunar Eclipse:

1. In the Philippines, there are sayings that monsters or aswang - which they called "Bakunawa",  "Minokawa",  "Tambanokawa", or "Arimaonga" - that eat the moon. And people has to make noises to drive one away or play music to make the monster fall asleep.

2.  In India's culture, they fast during an eclipse 

3. In some religions, it is a sign of apocalypse

4.  Native Americans said lunar eclipses are signs of a change and transformation that will come

5. South African's believe that according to the myth, the mighty Sun and the brilliant Moon fight during an eclipse and it is up to the humans to encourage them to resolve their disputes

6. Some religions believe that your good or bad actions you do during lunar eclipse will be multiplied tenfold.

7. Some parts in the world, women and children are advised to stay indoors during an eclipse because they might be abducted.
