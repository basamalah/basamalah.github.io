2. define social science , natural science , humanities and wisdom


Answer:

Social Science- refers to the scientific study of human society and social relationships.

Natural Science- refers to a branch of science which deals with the physical world, e.g. physics, chemistry, geology, biology.

Humanities- The humanities are the stories, the ideas, and the words that help us make sense of our lives and our world. As fields of study, the humanities emphasize analysis and exchange of ideas rather than the creative expression of the arts or the quantitative explanation of the sciences.

Wisdom- Wisdom, sapience, or sagacity is the ability to think and act using knowledge, experience, understanding, common sense and insight. Wisdom is associated with attributes such as unbiased judgment, compassion, experiential self-knowledge, self-transcendence and non-attachment, and virtues such as ethics and benevolence.
