19. A regular Coke costs ₱ 18 while a large Coke costs twice the price of a regular Coke. Michelle sold a total of 25 cups of regular and large Coke. The revenue for the soda sold was ₱ 702. How many cups of large Coke were sold?


Answer:

14 cups

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us represent the number of regular Cokes sold as R, and the number of large Cokes sold as L. Since larges cost twice the regular, they cost 2 * ₱18 or ₱36.

From the given, we can make the following equations:

R + L = 25 ... {1}

18R + 36L = 702 ... {2}

We can use the method of elimination,

{2} - 18 {1}

18R + 36L = 702

-18R - 18L = -450

         18L = 252

            L = 14

Therefore, 14 cups of large Coke were sold.

21. A dozen of coke in can cost (48x-72) pesos.How much does a can of coke cost?​


Answer:

A dozen of coke is equal to 12 pcs. can of coke.

To find each cost, divide (48x - 72) into 12.

[tex] \frac{48x - 72}{12} = \frac{48x}{12} - \frac{72}{12} = 4x - 6 [/tex]

Therefore, (4x -6) pesos is the cost of each can of coke.

Note:

[tex](48x - 72) \div 12 \\ is \: same \: as \\ \frac{48x - 72}{12} [/tex]
