16. WAN in the world of technology means? *​


Answer:

A communications network that connects computing devices over geographically dispersed locations. While a local-area network (LAN) typically services a single building or location, a WAN covers a much larger area such as a city, state or country

tagalog: Ang isang malawak na lugar na network (WAN) ay isang network ng telecommunications na umaabot sa isang malaking lugar na pangheograpiya para sa pangunahing layunin ng computer networking. Malawakang mga network ng lugar ay madalas na itinatag sa mga leased telecommunication circuit.      english: A wide area network (WAN) is a telecommunications network that extends over a large geographic area for the primary purpose of computer networking. Wide area networks are often established with leased telecommunication circuits.
