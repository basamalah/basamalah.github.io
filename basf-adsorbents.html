Question: Adsorbs all color except green​

Answer:

Chlorophyll

Chlorophyll, the green pigment common to all photosynthetic cells, absorbs all wavelengths of visible light except green, which it reflects. This is why plants appear green to us. Black pigments absorb all wavelengths of visible light that strike them.

Explanation:A surface that appears green absorbs all colors except green. White light is a combination of all colors -- as is apparent when you shine a white light through a prism -- so anything that appears white reflects all wavelengths of light.

23. Aluminum reacts with oxygen to produce aluminum oxide which can be used as an adsorbent, desiccant, or catalyst for organic reactions. 4Al(s) + 302(g) → 2Al2O3(s) A mixture of 82.49 g of aluminum ( = 26.98 g/mol) and 117.65 g of oxygen ( = 32.00 g/mol) is allowed to react. Identify the limiting reactant and determine the mass of the excess reactant present in the vessel when the reaction is complete.


Answer:

Al is the limiting reactant.

mass O2 remained = 44.27 g

Explanation:

Step 1: Compute for the mass of Al2O3 produced.

Using 82.49 g of Al

mass Al2O3 = 82.49 g Al × (1 mol Al / 26.98 g Al) × (2 mol Al2O3 / 4 mol Al) × (102.0 g Al2O3 / 1 mol Al2O3)

mass Al2O3 = 155.93 g

Using 117.65 g of O2

mass Al2O3 = 117.65 g O2 × (1 mol O2 / 32.00 g O2) × (2 mol Al2O3 / 3 mol O2) × (102.0 g Al2O3 / 1 mol Al2O3)

mass Al2O3 = 250.01 g

Step 2: Determine the limiting reactant.

Al is the limiting reactant. (less amount of Al2O3 produced)

Step 3: Determine the mass of the excess reactant present in the vessel when the reaction is complete.

Compute for the mass of O2 consumed.

mass O2 = 155.93 g Al2O3 × (1 mol Al2O3 / 102.0 g Al2O3) × (3 mol O2 / 2 mol Al2O3) × (32.00 g O2 / 1 mol O2)

mass O2 = 73.379 g

Subtract the mass of O2 consumed from the initial mass of O2.

mass O2 remained = 117.65 g - 73.379 g

mass O2 remained = 44.27 g
