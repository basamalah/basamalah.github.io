Answer:

In conducting research, I will apply PCU's core values of faith, character, and service by upholding ethical principles and striving for excellence in my work.

Faith: In conducting research, I will ensure that my work is guided by a strong sense of ethics and integrity. This means that I will be honest and transparent in my research methods and findings, and I will always seek to uphold the highest standards of academic excellence. Additionally, I will approach my work with a sense of curiosity and open-mindedness, recognizing that there may be multiple perspectives on any given topic.

Character: As a researcher, I will strive to embody the values of honesty, integrity, and respect for others. This means that I will always acknowledge the contributions of others in my work, and I will give credit where credit is due. Additionally, I will be committed to using my research to make a positive impact on society, whether through advancing knowledge or addressing real-world problems.

Service: Finally, as a researcher, I believe it is important to use my skills and knowledge to serve others. This means that I will be committed to sharing my findings with others in a way that is accessible and understandable. Additionally, I will seek out opportunities to collaborate with others in order to achieve common goals.
