1. how do you form a major chord? please I need this asap thanks ill mark you brainiest if you answer it correctly​


Answer: Major chords are built by adding the intervals of a major third and perfect fifth above the root.

Explanation: The root, by the way, is the starting note of the chord (in this example our root is C). The major third interval is the distance between the root and the note four semitones above it.
