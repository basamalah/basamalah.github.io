2. What research design is often conducted in a controlled setting with corresponding research


Experimental research design is a kind of research that often conducted in a controlled setting with corresponding research.

Explanation :

Experimental design (DOE) is a branch of applied statistics concerned with the planning, execution, analysis, and interpretation of controlled tests to evaluate factors that control the values of a parameter or group of parameters.

Experimental research is research conducted with a scientific approach using two sets of variables. The first set acts as a constant, which you use to measure the difference from the second set. Quantitative research methods, for example, are experimental in nature.

In experimental research, the research must be in control with the corresponding research, the corresponding research is the type of research that is judged according to the experimental design, usually the corresponding research is in the form of theoretical research.
