12. tomas put together 3/4 brown and 1/12 green colored paper to decorate his prodject. how much colored paper did he use in all​


Question:

How much colored paper did he use in all​

Answer:5 /6 of colored paper he use in all.#CarryOnLearning

Answer:

Asked: It was asked the total number of the used colored papers.

Given: There were ¾ brown coloree paper and 1/12 green colored paper.

Operation: Addition

Number Sentence: ¾+1/12=N

Complete Answer: The total number of the used colored paper was ⅚.

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution:

¾+1/12

=9/12+1/12

=10/12

=⅚

Checking:

1.

⅚-1/12

=10/12-1/12

=9/12

=¾

2.

⅚-¾

=10/12-9/12

=1/12
