2. Write a flyer presenting an advocacy that will spread the importance of respect for parents, elders and authority. Also identify the violations that should be avoided.


Answer:

Respect for Parents, Elders, and Authority: A Call to Action

We live in a world that often values individualism and independence over respect for those who came before us and those in positions of authority. But we must not forget the important role that parents, elders, and authority figures play in shaping our lives and society.

Respect for parents, elders, and authority is not just a cultural tradition, it is also a moral obligation. These individuals have earned our respect through their experiences, knowledge, and contributions to society. They have sacrificed much to provide for us and make our lives better. By showing respect, we honor them and their legacy.

However, there are many violations of this fundamental value that we must avoid. Disrespectful behavior, such as talking back, ignoring, or disobeying parents, elders, and authority figures can be harmful and disruptive. It undermines the stability of our society and can cause emotional pain to those we disrespect.

We must also avoid physical abuse, neglect, and mistreatment of elders and vulnerable individuals. These actions not only violate our moral code, but they are also punishable by law.

As a community, we can promote respect for parents, elders, and authority by setting an example through our own behavior. We must also teach our children and younger generations to appreciate and respect those who came before them. We can also advocate for policies that protect the elderly and vulnerable from abuse and neglect.

Let us remember that respect for parents, elders, and authority is not just a tradition, it is a universal value that strengthens our society and enriches our lives. By showing respect to those who deserve it, we create a more harmonious and just world.

19. Ano ang Flyers ? Ano ano ang nilalaman ng flyers ? 


Ang flyers na tinatawag din na leaflet o pamphlet ay piraso ng papel na kadalasan ay ginagamit sa pagpapatalastas o sa pag-aadvertise. Naglalaman ito ng mga impormasyon tungkol sa isang produkto o anumang serbisyo o mga kaalaman na nais ipaalam sa publiko. Maaaring itong gamitin ng mga indibidwal, sa mga negosyo, o organisasyon na itaguyod ang isang serbisyo tulad ng mga kainan, manghikayat o magpaalam sa publiko ng isang panlipunan, relihiyon tulad ng sa evangelismo o pampulitikang mensahe tulad ng ginagawa sa mga kampanya pampulitika. Maraming uri at maraming halimbawa ng flyers na naglalaman ng iba't ibang impormasyon. Ang isang tiyak na gampanin nito ay nakatutulong ang flyers na ipamahagi sa publiko ang anumang impormasyon o kaalaman.
