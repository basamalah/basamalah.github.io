3. What is the Category of GABBANG?


Answer:

Name: Gabbang

Classification: Xylophone

Description: Graduated bamboo slabs are suspended from a wooden resonator box. The gabbang is played by a pair of beaters while another taps a rhythmic pattern on the side of the box. It may also be played solo. This instrument was made by Manlilikha ng Bayan awardee, Uhang Ahadas.
