17. is the process of copying DNA sequence into RNA?


The process of copying DNA sequence into RNA is called Transcription. It is the first step in gene expression. Transcription is performed by enzymes called RNA polymerase, which connects nucleotides to form an RNA strand using a DNA strand as a template. Transcription has three stages which are initiation, elongation, and termination.
