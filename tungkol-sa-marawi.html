2. Elehiya tula tungkol sa marawi


Dumadaing pa din ang bawat sulok ng Marawi. Hindi niya iyon maiiyak kundi gusto niyang magpalahaw. Ang ugong ng pagsabog, putok ng baril at iyak ang siyang nakaririndi. Kaya ang kaniyang pagbagsak ay pagbagsak ng lipunan sa Marawi. Ang kamatayan ng mga inosente ay kamatayan ng ekonomiya, edukasyon, politika at pag-asa. Kung bubuhaying-muli ito, kailan pa? Ngayon ay naghihingalo siya sa pagdaing at nagtatanong ng - Bakit?

Answer:

During the conflict in Marawi, many residents were forced to flee their homes and were faced with limited access to food and other resources.

To survive, some residents relied on food aid and other forms of assistance from aid organizations and the government.

Others turned to foraging for wild fruits and vegetables, and some even resorted to fishing in the nearby lake to provide food for themselves and their families.

The local market and supermarkets were closed, so the residents had to find ways to get food.

There were also reports of residents scavenging for food in abandoned or destroyed homes and businesses.

It was a difficult situation, and many residents faced food insecurity and malnutrition as a result of the conflict.

25. Magbigay ng reaksiyon ukol sa isyu tungkol sa naganap sa Marawi at least 5 or 6 sentences lang po plss

Nakakatakot dahil kasama nito ang ISIS. Nakakaawa ang mga pamilyang nastranded sa Marawi, ang mga bakwit. Wala silang tirahan, lalo na ang mga bata, imbis na maglaro ay natatakot silang lumabas.
