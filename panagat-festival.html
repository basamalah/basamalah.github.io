4. Sumulat ng sariling likhang tanka at haiku tungkol sa mga “ Frontliners"(may panagat)​


Answer:

The Forest Management Bureau (FMB) Director Lourdes C. Wagan led the turnover of medicine and medical supplies to selected government hospitals in a simple ceremony with a theme “Isang Pasasalamat para sa mga FRONTLINERS ngayong Pandemya” at the Forest Management Bureau Office, Quezon City.

The Bureau donated P400,000.00 worth of personal protective equipment (PPE) sets, surgical masks, isopropyl alcohols, and assorted multivitamins for the medical frontline workers in support to the government’s effort to fight COVID-19 pandemic. The recipients of the donations were the following government hospitals in Quezon City namely: East Avenue Medical Center (EAMC); Quezon City General Hospital (QCGH); Philippine Children’s Medical Center (PCMC); and Lung Center of the Philippines (LCP).
