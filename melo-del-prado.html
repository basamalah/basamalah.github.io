18. 6kg of melos cost php 150.00 the cost of 4kg melon is? with solution please


Answer:

The cost of 4 kg of melons would be 100.00 pesos.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the cost of 4 kg of melons, we need to determine the cost per kilogram of the melons. We can do this by dividing the total cost by the total weight:

150.00 pesos / 6 kg = 25.00 pesos per kg

Now that we know the cost per kilogram, we can multiply that by the weight of the 4 kg of melons we want to know the cost of:

25.00 pesos/kg x 4 kg = 100.00 pesos

So the cost of 4 kg of melons would be 100.00 pesos.
