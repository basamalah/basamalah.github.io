30. In 5-6 sentences, explain how the Resurrection of Jesus became the foundation of the Christian faith?


Answer:

The Resurrection of Jesus became the foundation of the Christian faith because it confirmed that Jesus was the Son of God and that his teachings were true. The resurrection also demonstrated God's power over death and sin, providing hope for eternal life for believers. The Apostles and early Christians were eyewitnesses to the resurrection, and their testimony became the basis for the Christian faith. The resurrection also fulfilled Old Testament prophecies, further validating Jesus as the Messiah. Finally, the resurrection provided a powerful message of forgiveness and salvation, inspiring countless believers throughout history to live a life of faith and service.
