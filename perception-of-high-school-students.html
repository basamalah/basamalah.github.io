just picked any of literature Review I've give below

Poor internet connection is a significant challenge that many students encounter in schools. It can significantly affect their ability to access educational materials and complete assignments on time. The purpose of this literature review is to examine the perceptions of students regarding poor internet connection in schools.

Literature Review:

A study conducted by Kyeyune (2020) explored the experiences of students in Uganda with poor internet connectivity in schools. The findings showed that students perceived poor internet connection as a major barrier to accessing online resources for learning. Moreover, it resulted in delayed submission of assignments and reduced engagement with e-learning platforms.

Another study conducted by Folorunso, Ezenkwu, and Ojukwu (2020) in Nigeria found that students' perceptions of poor internet connectivity negatively affected their academic performance. The study revealed that slow internet speed caused frustration and decreased motivation among students. They also expressed dissatisfaction with the quality of online courses, which they attributed to the poor internet connection.

In a study conducted by Nazir, Arshad, and Muhammad (2021) in Pakistan, students' perception of poor internet connectivity was found to be related to their socio-economic status. Students from low-income families were more likely to have limited access to the internet and, consequently, experience more difficulties with online learning. They also expressed concerns about the high cost of internet services.

Furthermore, a study by Li and Li (2021) in China found that students' perceptions of poor internet connectivity were influenced by their familiarity with technology. Students who were more proficient in using technology were less likely to encounter problems with poor internet connectivity. They were also more likely to adopt alternative strategies, such as using mobile data, to access online resources.
