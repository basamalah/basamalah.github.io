Impact on the Accommodation Sectors During a Pandemic Disease

Currently, the Philippines is battling against a pandemic disease called Coronavirus. The president of the country implemented the Enhanced Community Quarantine that requires all establishment to stop their operations in the entire Luzon and other selected provinces in the Philippines. There were still exemptions to those establishments that cater to foods and provide medical supplies, though strict precautionary measures should be followed.  

Hotels, resorts, leisure and other tourism establishments were included in the mandatory temporary shutdown. Employees were the major affected in this matter. Other accommodation sectors owners offer their rooms to serve as temporary homes for the frontliners, this could help the hoteliers to continue their jobs and for the frontliners to secure their families' safety against infection from the virus.

8. what is journal entry


A journal entry, in accounting, is the logging of a transaction intoaccounting journal items. The journal entry can consist of several recordings, each of which is either a debit or a credit. The total of the debits must equal the total of the credits or the journal entry is said to be "unbalanced".

11. what is the difference between simple journal entry and compound journal entry?​


Answer:

Compound journal entry is an accounting entry which affects three or more account heads. A simple journal entry has just two rows i.e. one debit and one credit, whereas a compound journal entry has three or more rows.
