6. short story about early pregnancy​


Answer:

Maria was only 16 years old when she found out she was pregnant. She was shocked and terrified because she was still in high school and not yet ready to be a mother. Her boyfriend, who was the same age as her, didn't know what to do and they both felt overwhelmed.

Maria's parents were equally shocked when they found out. They had always been strict and didn't approve of their daughter having a boyfriend so young, let alone being pregnant. They told Maria that she had to leave their house and figure things out on her own.

Feeling lost and alone, Maria moved in with her boyfriend's family. They were kind and supportive, but they lived in a small house with limited resources. They struggled to make ends meet, and the stress of their situation only made Maria's pregnancy more difficult.

Despite all the challenges, Maria was determined to make the best of the situation. She continued to go to school and even took online classes to keep up with her studies. She also found a part-time job to help with the expenses. It wasn't easy, but she was proud of herself for not giving up.

Nine months later, Maria gave birth to a healthy baby boy. Holding him in her arms for the first time was a magical moment, and all the hardships were worth it. From that moment on, Maria was no longer just a teenager, but a young mother, who had to grow up quickly and become responsible for another life.

Years went by and Maria continued to work hard to provide for her son. She graduated from high school and even went on to university. She never forgot the challenges she faced during her pregnancy, but she was proud of the person she had become. Maria was a strong and independent young woman, who had overcome the odds and proved to herself and her family that anything was possible with determination and hard work.
