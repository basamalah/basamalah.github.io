5. All men were created equal in the sight of God but certainly not in the sight of fellow men. How does this statement encapsulate the history and relationship between Filipinos and the Spaniards?


Answer:

It is true that all men are equal in the sight of God as stated in Genesis 1:27, that men were created based on the image and likeness of God. In Romans 3:23, it is also stated that all have sinned against God. Though God looks at people equally, but people look at their fellow men differently.

In the bible, it is written, "But the Lord said to Samuel, “Do not consider his appearance or his height, for I have rejected him. The Lord does not look at the things people look at. People look at the outward appearance, but the Lord looks at the heart.” 1 Samuel 16:7. God does not consider looking at the person's physical attributes, accomplishments or possessions for He is not impressed on such things. While men looks at the superficial things, God looks deep into the hearts of men.

Explanation:

In the history of the Philippines, when the Spanish came here in the Philippines, one can really see the difference between the Spaniards and the Filipinos. The Spaniards have whiter skin, are taller, more handsome and beautiful, more powerful because they came from a rich country, are educated and skillful. The Filipinos on the other hand, have darker skin-tone, are shorter, plain looking, appears to be less educated and are naive.

Because of these differences, Spaniards think highly of themselves and looked down on Filipinos, especially those Filipinos who came from poor families. Thus, the relationship between Spaniards and Filipinos are like master and servants. The Spaniards as masters and the Filipinos as servants or slaves. This is because men look at the outward appearance but God looks at the heart.

17. choose an example of sensory word for sight​


Answer:

Sensory words are descriptive—they describe how we experience the world: how we smell, see, hear, feel or taste something. Words related to sight indicate colors, shape, or appearance. For instance: gloomy, dazzling, bright, foggy, gigantic.
