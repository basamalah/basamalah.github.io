4. Marivic earns P60.00 per hour. Write an equation that defines Marivic's earnings E as a function of the number of hours, h that she works.


Answer:

E(h) = 60h

Step-by-step explanation:

Earnings per hour:  Php 60.00Number of hours:  hTotal earnings:  E(h)  ⇒  earnings as a function of h, the number of hours.

Equation:

Total Earnings = Rate per hour ×  Number of hours

As function:

E(h) = 60h

To use this function, substitute the numbers of hours worked (h) to solve for the total earnings.

Let's say:

h = 8 hours

Find the total earnings:

E(h) = 60hE(8) = 60 (8)E(8) = 480

For 8 hours, Marivic earns a total of Php 480.00.

29. Marivic is five years older than Michelle. The product of Marivic's age in 3 years and Michelle'sage 5 years ago is 90. Find their present ages.​


Answer:

Michelle's age is 10, while Marivic's age is 15.

Step-by-step explanation:

It is said that Marivic is five years older than Michelle, so 15-10= 5. And then, Marivic's age in 3 years is 18 because 15+3=18. Michelle's age five years ago was 5 because 10-5=5. The product of them (18×5) is 90. So Michelle's age is 10 and Marivic's age is 15.
