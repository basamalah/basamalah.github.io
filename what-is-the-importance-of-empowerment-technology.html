22. Reflection/ Realization about the importance of empowerment technology ​


Answer:

Empowerment technology refers to tools and systems that enable individuals and communities to have greater control over their lives and environments. These technologies can include things like mobile phones, internet access, and educational software. Realizing the importance of empowerment technology means recognizing that access to these tools can have a positive impact on individuals and communities by providing them with the means to improve their education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. Empowerment technology can also help to bridge the digital divide between developed and developing countries, and can be an important tool for promoting social and economic development.
