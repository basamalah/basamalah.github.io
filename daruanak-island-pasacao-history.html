• Problem:

Anna bought beach front land property in Pasacao, Camarines Sur. Its width is 3x+ 5 while the length is 4x + 6. What is the land area of her property?

Formula: Area = (Length)(Width)

• Solution:

To find the area of the given land, we'll use the formula, A=l×w, in which A is the area, l is the length while w is the measure of the width.

Let's solve!

[tex]\large \tt A=l \times w \\ \large \tt A=(4x + 6) (3x + 5) \\ \large \tt A=4(3)x {}^{2} +4(5)x + 6(3)x + 6(5) \\ \large \tt A=12x {}^{2} + 20x + 18x + 30 \\ \large \tt A=12x {}^{2} + 38x + 30[/tex]

• Answer:

The land area of Anna's property is 12x² + 38x + 30 square units.
