- greater than, more than, exceeding, over, surpassing

- less than, below, within, beneath

2 - Loud

- producing or capable of producing much noise / easily audible.

- adjective

- resounding, ringing, roaring

- quiet, silent, mute

3 - Difficult

- hard to do, make, or carry out : arduous a difficult climb / hard to deal with, manage, or overcome

- adjective

- troublesome, crucial, tough

- easy, straightforward, simple

4 - High

- a high point, level, or figure / great, or greater than normal, in quantity, size, or intensity.

- adjective

- tall, excessive, soaring

- short, low, less

- Light is a source of illumination, whether a natural one (like the sun) or an artificial one (like your lamp).
