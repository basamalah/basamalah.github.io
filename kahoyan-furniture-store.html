1. Brandon's Furniture Store buys a sofa at a wholesale price of \$97.00$97.00. If the markup rate at Brandon's Furniture Store is 40\%40%, what is the markup for the sofa? \$\ $ S


Answer:

The markup for the sofa is $38.

Step-by-step explanation:

The wholesale price of the sofa is $97.00 with markup rate of 40%

To look for the markup, we just need to find 40% of the wholesale price which is $97.00

So we will 40/100 = 0.4

0.4 * 97 = 38.8

Thus, 40% of $97.00  = $38.8

10. A couch was bought at a wholesale price of $113, and Jessica's Furniture Store marks up all furniture by 45%. At what price should the manager sell the couch?


Answer:

$163.85

Step-by-step explanation:

First, solve for the amount of mark-up

let m = amount of mark-up

given: price = $113

rate = 45%

m = original price × mark-up rate

solution:

m = $113 × .45

= $50.85

Next, solve for the list price

let p = list price

given: price = $113

markup = $50.85

p = original price + amount of mark-up

solution:

p = $113 + $50.85

= $163.85
