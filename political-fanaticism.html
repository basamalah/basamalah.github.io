2. why are Filipinos practicing fanaticism in worshipping saints​


Answer:

It is due to the colonization of the Spaniards and othe countries that brought and strengthen Christianity in the Philippines.

Explanation:

Philippines, before it was colonized and Christianized by the Spaniard, is largely Animism, or those that worshipped objects, places, nature and even animals due to the belief that each has its own animate spirit that dwells within. When the Spaniards came and then colonized the country, they brought Christianity and introduced it to the natives. After it was wholly accepted, friars and some priests introduced saint worshipping.

According to Dave Johnson in his book, Theology in Context, he stated that pictures and maybe even saints have contributed to the accessible faith in where Filipinos are more bound to see and firmly grasp the totality of their faith.
