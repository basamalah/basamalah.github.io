Uniform circular motion is a specific type of motion in which an object travels in a circle with a constant speed. For example, any point on a propeller spinning at a constant rate is executing uniform circular motion. Other examples are the second, minute, and hour hands of a watch. 17. Discribing motion worksheet grade 5 with answer


Motion

Motion is the changing or action of moving or positioning a particular object over time. It is also described as displacements, velocity, distance, time, speed, and acceleration.

Describing the Patterns of Motions:

Linear motion- pertains to a thing that slides in a straight position.

Ex: when a person slides on a hill

Rotation- shows that something is spinning.

Ex: a top is spinning, a fan that rotates

Revolving motion- pertains to something that moves around in a circular position.

Ex: riding on a merry-go-round or a Ferris wheel.

Rolling motion- pertains to something that rotates while moving in a straight line.

Ex: bowling a ball, car tires.
