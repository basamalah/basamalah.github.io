24. The area of the concrete pathway is 350m^2 and it's perimeter is 90m. What is the length of the pathway?


let l  =  length of pathway
& w = width of of pathway
then 2x + 2y = 90  >  2(x+y) =90    >  x+y = 45    >  perimeter of rectangle
                                                              x =  45-y
and     xy    =  350    >      area of rectangle
         ( 45-y)(y) =  350
           45y-y² -350 = 0            /  . -1
           y² - 45y +350 = 0
       ( y -10)(y-35) = 0
         y = 10    or y =  35
so the length is 35m  &
  the width is 10m

27. The area of the concrete pathway is 360m² ang its perimeter is 90m.what is the length of the pathway​


Answer:

what is the length of the pathway

area of the concrete pathway is 350m²

perimeter is 90m

LW=area

perimeter=2L+2W

LW=350

2L+2W=90

2W=90-2L divide both sides by 2

W=45-L

substitute the value of W in equation LW=350

L(45-L)=350

45L-L²=350

L²-45L+350=0

(L-10)(L-35)=0 separate the two factors

L-10= 0, L-35=0

L=10, L=35

SO 35 IS THE BIGGEST NUMBER THAT CORRESPOND THE LENGTH
