1. B. Express your claim or counterclaim on the following topics below and explain your answers. 1. Black Lives Matter Claim/Counterclaim: Explanation: 2. Hazing for Fraternities Claim/Counterclaim: •Explanation


Answer:

Black Lives Matter

Claim: The Black Lives Matter movement is essential for addressing systemic racism and police brutality against Black people in the United States.

Counterclaim: The Black Lives Matter movement promotes division and is harmful to law enforcement and society as a whole.

Explanation: Those who support the Black Lives Matter movement argue that it is necessary to bring attention to the disproportionate use of force and other injustices faced by Black people in interactions with police and the criminal justice system. Those who oppose the movement may argue that it promotes an anti-police agenda and encourages violence and looting.

Hazing for Fraternities

Claim: Hazing is a harmful and outdated practice that should be banned in all fraternities.

Counterclaim: Hazing is an important part of fraternity culture that helps build bonds among members and prepares them for the challenges of adult life.

Explanation: Supporters of a ban on hazing argue that it can lead to physical harm, mental distress, and other negative consequences for those who are subjected to it. They may also argue that hazing perpetuates a toxic culture of masculinity and reinforces harmful gender norms. Those who support hazing may argue that it is a time-honored tradition that builds camaraderie and a sense of belonging among fraternity members, and that it teaches important life lessons such as perseverance and teamwork. However, hazing is often illegal and can lead to serious harm or even death, so many organizations have taken steps to prohibit it.

22. write a counterclaim for each claims below. Claims: Divorce should be legalized in the Philippines. Counterclaims: Claims: Death penalty should be legalized Counterclaims: Claims: Students learn well at home, not in school. Counterclaims: Claims: Vaccine for all against Covid 19 Counterclaims: Claims: War against Drugs is a success. Counterclaims:


Claims: Divorce should be legalized in the Philippines.

Counterclaims: Divorce should not be legalized in the Philippines as it goes against the country's strong cultural and religious values, which promote the sanctity of marriage and the importance of family. Legalizing divorce may also increase the number of broken families and harm children who are caught in the middle of their parents' separation.

Claims: Death penalty should be legalized.

Counterclaims: Death penalty should not be legalized as it violates the right to life and could result in the execution of innocent people. The justice system is not perfect and mistakes can be made, and there is no way to undo an execution once it has been carried out. In addition, the death penalty has not been shown to have a deterrent effect on crime.

Claims: Students learn well at home, not in school.

Counterclaims: Students may not learn as well at home as they do in school due to lack of structure, resources, and support from teachers and peers. Schools also provide socialization and hands-on learning opportunities that are not available in the home environment. Furthermore, not all families have the means or knowledge to provide their children with an adequate education at home.

Claims: Vaccine for all against Covid 19.

Counterclaims: Not everyone may choose to receive the Covid-19 vaccine due to concerns about its safety, efficacy, or personal beliefs. In addition, some people may not have access to the vaccine due to logistical, financial, or political reasons. Widespread vaccination is necessary for achieving herd immunity, but it is not a guarantee that the pandemic will end immediately.

Claims: War against Drugs is a success.

Counterclaims: The War on Drugs has not been a success as it has not reduced drug use or drug-related crime, but has instead led to increased violence, human rights violations, and mass incarceration. It has also focused on punishment rather than rehabilitation and has neglected to address the root causes of drug use, such as poverty, lack of education, and mental health issues.

26. Why need to use signal words when expressing your claim and counterclaim?​


Answer

Because, signal words used to correct the sequence of events.

What I Have Learned

The sequence of events in a story is the order in which things happen. Signal words like first,next,then,after, before, and finally are used to show the order of events.

Example:

First,Buboy planted a seed.

Next,out came a tiny leaf.

Then,it had more leaves and taller branches.

Finally,it had fruits.
