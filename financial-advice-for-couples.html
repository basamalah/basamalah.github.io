Responsible parenting is defined as parents' willingness and ability to respond to the needs and aspirations of their family and children.

Why is it important to be a responsible parent?

We most often learn social values such as kindness, honesty, altruism, and so on from responsible parents. Responsible parents also teach their children how to function in society. The values and parenting style of parents can fundamentally shape children as people.
