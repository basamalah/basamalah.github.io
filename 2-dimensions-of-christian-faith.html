3. Among the three dimensions of Christian Faith, which is the greatest? Justify.


Answer:

Among the three dimensions of Christian faith, the greatest in terms of numbers and influence would be the Catholic Church or the Westen/Latin Christianity.

This wouldn't come out as a surprise because among the three, It was the Catholic Church which is considered the primordial Christian Church.

In fact, if we try to trace the roots of Christianity it will lead us not to the Eastern Orthodox nor the Protestant Churches but to the Western/Latin Church.

Christ founded the Church on Peter, who is the first pope of the Christian Church.

Thus, among the three, it is indubitable that the Catholic Church is the greatest by virtue of it number of adherents and by its influence to the world.

Answer:
