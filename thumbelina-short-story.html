1. settings of thumbelina story?​


Answer:

The story's setting changes when Thumbelina and the bird fly to a land that stays warm all year around. The bird later returns to Denmark, where the story begins. This play is one of many adaptations of the Thumbelina story.

What point of view is Thumbelina?

female point

Fairy tale and folklore researchers Iona and Peter Opie have written that "Thumbelina" is an adventure story from the female point of view. They believe the story teaches the reader that people are happiest with their own kind. Thumbelina is a passive character and the victim of circumstances, they point out.

2. whats the plot on story of thumbelina​


Answer:

Thumbelina is a tiny girl no bigger than a thumb who lives in a flower. One day, a toad discovers Thumbelina and takes her away to be the bride of her son. Thumbelina escapes and finds her way to a fairy village where she befriends a fairy prince. They have many adventures together, but soon the fairy prince must leave to fulfill his duties as prince. Thumbelina eventually meets a kind-hearted mole who helps her find her way home. Along the way, she meets a swallow who helps her escape from a field mouse who wanted to keep her as a pet. With the help of her new friends, Thumbelina is able to return home safely.

5. simula ng kwento ni thumbelina


Answer:

Noong unang panahon, may isang mag-asawang naghihintay ng kanilang pinakamimithing anak. Matagal na silang nag-asam ng bata at nang magkaisang araw, biglang nagkatotoo ang kanilang mga dasal.

Ngunit ang kanilang sanggol ay hindi katulad ng ibang sanggol. Siya ay napakaliit na itinuturing na pinakamaliit sa kanilang lugar. Kaya pinangalanan nilang siya na Thumbelina dahil siya ay maliit na parang isang kamay.

Ngunit kahit na maliit siya, si Thumbelina ay isang masayahin at mapagmahal na bata. Ang mga bulaklak at mga insekto ay kanyang mga kaibigan, at siya ay nakikinig sa kanilang mga kwento at mga pakikipagsapalaran.

Isang araw, siya ay nakuha ng isang butiki at dinala siya sa malayong lugar. Doon niya nakilala si Mr. Mole, isang mayaman at matanda na nangangailangan ng kasama sa kanyang bahay. Si Thumbelina ay kinuha ni Mr. Mole bilang kanyang kasama.

Habang siya ay naninirahan kay Mr. Mole, nakilala niya si Mr. Beetle. Sa kanyang pagkakilala kay Mr. Beetle, siya ay nangako sa kanya na kung siya ay hindi magpakasal sa kanya ay hindi siya papayagan ni Mr. Beetle na umuwi sa kanyang tunay na pamilya.

Answer:

Plot: "Thumbelina" is the story of a tiny girl who is no bigger than a thumb. She is born from a flower and taken in by an old woman who raises her. One day, while out on a boat ride, Thumbelina is kidnapped by a toad who wants her to marry his ugly son. Thumbelina manages to escape but is then captured by a beetle who wants her to marry his pompous son. Thumbelina refuses and is left alone in the forest during the winter. She is rescued by a kind mouse who takes her in and provides her with shelter and food. When spring arrives, Thumbelina meets a tiny prince who falls in love with her and asks her to marry him. Thumbelina happily accepts his proposal and they are married in a grand ceremony.

Characters: Thumbelina, old woman, toad, toad's son, beetle, beetle's son, mouse, prince

Title: Thumbelina

Author: Hans Christian Andersen

Where and When: The story takes place in a fairytale world where flowers can give birth to human-like creatures. The time period is not specified.

Problem: Thumbelina is kidnapped by two different creatures who want her to marry their sons. She is left alone and vulnerable in the winter forest.

Solution: Thumbelina is rescued by a kind mouse who takes care of her until spring. She then meets a prince who falls in love with her and asks her to marry him. Their marriage solves Thumbelina's problem and she lives happily ever after.
