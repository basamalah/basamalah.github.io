4. benefits of an electronic file management system​


Answer:

An electronic file management system (EFMS) offers several benefits to organizations. Some of these benefits include:

Improved efficiency: EFMS allows for quick and easy access to documents, reducing the time it takes to locate and retrieve files. This saves time and improves efficiency in the workplace.Cost savings: EFMS reduces the need for physical storage space and the associated costs of paper, ink, and other materials. It also reduces the costs associated with managing and maintaining physical files.Increased security: EFMS offers advanced security features, such as encryption and access control, which help to protect sensitive documents from unauthorized access.Better collaboration: EFMS allows multiple users to access and work on the same document simultaneously, regardless of their location. This improves collaboration and makes it easier for team members to work together.Disaster recovery: EFMS offers backup and recovery features that help to protect documents in the event of a disaster or system failure. This ensures that critical documents are not lost and can be easily retrieved.Environmental benefits: EFMS reduces the use of paper and other materials, which helps to reduce an organization's carbon footprint and promote environmental sustainability.

Overall, an electronic file management system can help organizations improve efficiency, reduce costs, increase security, and promote collaboration while also promoting environmental sustainability.
