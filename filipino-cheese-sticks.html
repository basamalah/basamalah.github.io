3. What classification of appetizers are cheese sticks?


What classification of appetizers are cheese sticks?

Hors d'oeuvres (appetizers)

Hors d'oeuvres are tiny plates of highly-seasoned meals that were once served hot or cold to accompany a meal. The fundamental requirement for preparing the hors d'oeuvres should be simplicity. Although the majority of hors d'oeuvres are served cold, there are several that are served hot. A cheese stick is a culinary product that has been shaped into a rod-like, individual portion of cheese. It is made from mild varieties of semi-soft cheese. Cheese sticks are great as a snack or as the main ingredient in dishes like breaded cheese sticks fried in oil and served with the breading as a food wrap.

8. dynamite cheese sticks tools, utensils, material, equipment​


INGREDIENTS:15 pcs Chili Pepper15 pcs Lumpia Wrapper small1 block filled cheesecooking oil

INSTRUCTIONS Slice cheese into sticks.Using a sharp knife, slice across green chili from the stem down to the tip.Scrape seeds using a small spoon. If you can't handle too much spicy taste be sure no seeds are left.Stuff each chili pepper with cheese.On a large plate, lay a lumpia wrapper and place a cheese stuffed chili pepper.Wrap each chili pepper with the lumpia wrapper, make sure the stem is sticking out on one side of the lumpia.Roll the lumpia wrapper tightly and seal the edge with a dab of water.Do the same procedure on the rest of the chilies.Heat oil on a deep frying pan, make sure the oil is enough to deep fry the lumpia.Fry them in batches and do not overcrowd the pan.Fry until golden brown approximately 4 minutes in medium heat.Serve with mayo-ketchup dip.

20. Mhel loves cheese burgers. He enters a 24 hour cheese burger eating contest. During the contest Mhel eats a cheese burger every 15 minutes. How many cheese burgers will he eat during the entire contest? 32 cheese burgers 36 cheese burgers 54 cheese burgers 96 cheese burgers


Convert 24 hour into minutes
24 hours(60mins/1hour) = 1,440 minutes

Mhel eaten cheese burger = 1,440/15
Mhel eaten cheese burger = 96 cheeseburgers

Mhel will eat 96 cheese burgers during the entire contest.
