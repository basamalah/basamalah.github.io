The hiring standards for a cat cafe business may vary depending on the location, size, and specific needs of the business. However, there are some general standards that are typically considered when hiring staff for a cat cafe:

Knowledge and experience with cats: Staff should have a basic understanding of cat behavior, handling, and care. It is also helpful if they have experience working with cats in a previous job or volunteer position.

Customer service skills: Staff should be friendly, welcoming, and able to provide excellent customer service. They should be able to communicate well with customers and answer any questions they may have about the cats or the cafe.

Hygiene and cleanliness: Staff should understand the importance of maintaining a clean and hygienic environment for both the cats and customers. They should be able to clean and disinfect cat areas, litter boxes, and other surfaces regularly.

Attention to detail: Staff should be able to notice and address any issues or concerns with the cats or the cafe environment, such as a sick cat or a spilled drink.

Ability to work in a team: Staff should be able to work collaboratively with other employees to ensure that the cafe runs smoothly and that customers and cats are happy.

Availability: Staff should have a flexible schedule and be able to work during the hours when the cafe is open, which may include weekends and evenings.

These are some of the basic standards that a cat cafe may consider when hiring staff.
