When the wheat and barley were ready to be harvested, harvesters were hired to cut down the stalks and tie them into bundles. God’s law necessitated that the corners of the fields not be harvested. 6. The sum of the page numbers of chapter 3 in a certain book is 374, if there are 11 pages in chapter 3 on what page does chapter 3 starts


Answer:

Page 29

Step-by-step explanation:

The chapter begins on page 29

The sum of pages is equivalent to the mean value times the number of pages

Let the number of pages be

n Then n ¯ x = 374

But n = 11 ⇒ 11 ¯ x = 374 So  ¯ x = 374 11 = 34

Note that 11 is an odd number of pages. AS the trick is to split the pages up into pairs it means that you have one over. So you have the middle page and the remaining even number of 10 is split so that you have 5 on either side of the middle page giving 5 pairs of numbers

First page is 34-5 = 29

Last page is 34+5 = 39

check:

(29+39)×5+34=374

29+30+...+34+...38+39

You have 5 pairs of 68 with a single 34 in the middle

If you have an even number of pages there is no middle one to be added.

Don't mind this "¯"

16. It took Evangeline 1 hour to read 4 2/3 chapters of a novel.At this rate,how many chapters can she read in 3 hours?


1 hour = 4 2/3 chapters
3 hours = ?


Transform 4 2/3 to an improper fraction which is 14/3.
[tex]4 \frac{2}{3} = \frac{14}{3} [/tex]
Then multiply 14/3 and 3 hours.
[tex] \frac{14}{3} \times \frac{3}{1} [/tex]
[tex] = \frac{14 \times 3}{3 \times 1} [/tex]
[tex] = \frac{42}{3} [/tex]
Then simplify by dividing the numerator and the denominator by 3.
[tex] = \frac{42 \div 3}{3 \div 3} [/tex]
The answer is
[tex] = 14[/tex]
3 hours = 14 chapters of a novel

She can read 14 chapters in 3 hours.

27. Levi can read 3 chapters of a story book in 3/4 hour.How many chapters he read in 4 1/4 hours?​


Answer:

Levi can read 3 chapters of a story book in 3/4 hour.How many chapters he read in 4 1/4 hours?

To solve this problem, we first need to convert the time Levi reads to a common unit, such as minutes. 3/4 hour is equal to 3/4 * 60 = 45 minutes. 4 1/4 hours is equal to 4 1/4 * 60 = 270 minutes.

Now that we have the time in minutes, we can calculate the number of chapters Levi reads in that time. In 45 minutes, Levi reads 3/4 * 3 chapters = <<3/43=2.25>>2.25 chapters. In 270 minutes, Levi reads 2.25 * 270/45 = <<2.25270/45=6>>6 chapters.

Therefore, Levi reads a total of 6 chapters in 4 1/4 hours.
