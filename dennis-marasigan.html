9. together,dennis,rey and luis earn 72000pesos a month.if dennis earns 6000 pesos more than rey and 9000 pesos more than luis,how much does dennis earn?


Let:
x = Rey's earnings
y = Luis's earnings
z = Dennis' earnings

Given:
x + y + z = 72,000 eq. 1
6000 + x = z eq. 2
9000 + y = z eq. 3


6000 + x = z
x = z - 6000 eq. 4

9000 + y = z
y = z - 9000 eq. 5

Substitute the eq. 4 and 5 to eq. 1
x + y + z = 72,000
(z - 6000) + (z - 9000) + z = 72,000
z - 6000 + z - 9000 + z = 72,000
z + z + z - 6000 - 9000 = 72,000
3z - 15000 = 72,000
3z = 72,000 + 15,000
3z = 87,000
3z/3 = 87,000/3
z = 29,000

Therefore, the ANSWER is 29,000. It is the money earned by Dennis.

Checking:
Rey's earnings
x = z - 6000
x = 29,000 - 6,000
x = 23,000

Luis's earnings
y = z - 9,000
y = 29,000 - 9,000
y = 20,000

x + y + z = 72,000
23,000 + 20,000 + 29,000 = 72,000
72,000 = 72,000

11. Together Dennis,Rey and Luis earn 72000 pesos a month,Dennis earns 6,000 pesos more than Rey and 9,000pesos more than Luis.how much does Dennis earns?


29,000 pesos

Let x represent for Rey's and y for Luis' earnings every month.

Hence, Dennis' earnings every month can be represented by

x + 6,000   or   y + 9,000

Two equations can then be formulated

Equation 1:  [tex]x+6,000=y+9,000[/tex]

Equation 2:  [tex]x+6,000+x+y=72,000[/tex]

Simplify y in Eq. 1

[tex]x+6,000=y+9,000\\y=x+6,000-9,000\\y=x-3,000[/tex]

Substitute x-3,000 for y in Eq. 2 and find x

[tex]x+6,000+x+y=72,000\\x+6,000+x+(x-3,000)=72,000\\x+6,000+x+x-3,000=72,000\\3x+3,000=72,000\\3x=72,000-3,000\\3x=69,000\\x=23,000[/tex]

Dennis' earnings per month

[tex]x+6,000\\23,000+6,000\\29,000[/tex]

Additional solution: Luis' earnings per month

[tex]y+9,000=29,000\\y=29,000-9,000\\y=20,000[/tex]

26. Dennis was watching the news on television to get information about the upcoming typhoon. what type of channel is dennis using?


News channel

Dennis is using the news broadcasting channel to get information about the upcoming typhoon. It presents when, where, and some details of the weather which Dennis is looking forward to knowing.

The news broadcasting channel is the best channel to get updates about the news and weather forecasts. Some of the information presented in a news channel is the following:

International News
Headlines that create awareness of the world are one of the main priorities of a news channel. They publish stories about what is happening in the world. Their primary goal is to impart clean, legit, accurate, and informative news as soon as possible.

Weather forecasts
Upcoming weather can also be seen on a news channel. They also present the number of affected individuals as well as the help given by the ambassadors, volunteers, and organizations.

Prominent people interviews
To make the channel more entertaining, one of their strategies is to invite famous people to come and join the on the seat. This way, they can discuss a variety of topics that could be interesting to the viewers.

Live streams
Live streams can be very useful to news channels because they can provide urgent information as soon as possible. During emergencies such as storm surges or earthquakes, they can let the world know by providing live streams.
