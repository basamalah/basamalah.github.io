1. There are 60 beads in a bag. The beads are red, yellow or blue. 40% of the beads are blue. 5/12 of the beads are red. How many beads are yellow?


Answer:

11 yellow beads

Calculation of Blue

40% will be converted to decimal form which is 0.40

60×0.40 = 24 blue beads

Calculation of Red

Convert 5/12 to 25/60 by diving 12 from 60 and multiplying to 5

Therefore, 25/60 will show that there are 25 red beads

Calculation of Yellow

Add the blue and red beads then minus it at the total amount of beads

24+25 = 4960-49 = 11 yellow beads
