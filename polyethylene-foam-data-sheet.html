5. what is the weakness of polyethylene plastic? ​


Answer:

it is important to understand that polyethylene plastic is a type of plastic that is widely used in many things like packaging and toys, but it does have some weaknesses.

1. It is not biodegradable: This means that it takes a long time for it to break down in the environment and can cause problems if it's not disposed of properly.

2. It can be affected by the sun: When exposed to the sun for a long time, it can become weaker and lose its strength.

3. It can't be used in very cold temperatures: It becomes brittle and can break easily when it's too cold.

4. It's not resistant to certain chemicals: Certain chemicals can cause it to break down or lose its strength.

In summary, polyethylene plastic is a versatile and commonly used plastic, but it's not good for the environment when not disposed of properly, it can be affected by the sun, it's not good to use in very cold temperatures, and it's not resistant to certain chemicals.

16. A 15-gram bullet moving at 300 m/sec passes through a 2 cm thick sheet of foam plastic and emerges with a speed of 90 m/sec. what is the average force that impeded the bullet’s motion through the plastic foam?


Answer:

The average force is 30.713 kN

Explanation:

Here, we are going to solve for the average force using two concepts. First, the motion in straight line and second is the 2nd newton's law of motion.

For the formula, we will use:

1. V² = V₀² - 2ax

where

V     is the final velocity

V₀   is the initial velocity

a     is the acceleration

x     is the horizontal distance traveled by the bullet through the plastic

      foam

2. F = ma

where

F     is the force

m    is the mass

a     is the acceleration

Given information

m = 15 grams = 0.015 kg

V₀ = 300 m/s

V = 90 m/s

x = 2 cm = 0.02 m

F = ?

Solving the problem

First, let us solve for the acceleration of the bullet moving through the plastic foam using the first given formula

V² = V₀² - 2ax

Substitute the given data

90² = 300² - 2a(0.02)

Do some transposition

2a(0.02) = 300² - 90²

[tex]a=\frac{300^2-90^2}{2(0.02)}[/tex]

a = 2.05 x 10⁶ m/s²

Now, we can solve for the force using the next given formula

F = ma

Substitute the given data

F = (0.015 kg)(2.05 x 10⁶ m/s²)

F = 30712.5 N or

F = 30.713 kN

Therefore, the average force is 30.713 kN.
