6. In parallelogram BEST, diagonals BS and ET bisect each other at O.B1. If ES = 10cm, how long is BT?2. If BE = 13cm, how long is TS?3. If EO = 6cm and SO = 7cm, what is the length of ET? BS?4. If ET + BS = 18cm and SO = 5cm, find ET and BS.​


Answer with Step-by-step explanation:

(Final answers are underlined in bold fonts.)

1.)  ES = 10 cm

ES ≅ BT (Opposite sides of parallelogram are congruent.)

BT = 10 cm

2) BE = 13 cm

BE ≅ TS  (Opposite sides of parallelogram are congruent.)

TS = 13 cm

3)  Diagonals of parallelogram bisect each other.  Each diagonal divides the other into two congruent segments.

EO = 6 cm

EO + OT = ET;  EO ≅ OT

ET = 2 (EO)

ET = 2(6 cm)

ET = 12 cm.

SO = 7 cm

SO + OB = BS;  BO ≅ SO

BS = 2 (SO)

BS = 2(7 cm)

BS = 14 cm

4.) Diagonals of parallelogram bisect each other.  Each diagonal divides the other into two congruent segments.

ET + BS = 18 cm

SO = 5 cm

BS = SO + OB;   SO ≅ OB

BS = 2(SO)

BS = 2(5 cm)

BS = 10 cm

ET = 18 cm - BS

ET = 18 cm - 10 cm

ET = 8 cm.
