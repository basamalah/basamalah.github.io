30. why is it important of having complete facilities in school ​


Answer:

Here are some reasons why having complete facilities in school is important:

1. Enhances Learning: Complete facilities in school provide students with the necessary tools and resources to enhance their learning experience. For example, a well-equipped science lab can help students understand complex scientific concepts better.

2. Promotes Safety: Complete facilities in schools ensure the safety of students and staff. For example, a well-maintained playground with proper safety equipment can prevent accidents and injuries.

3. Attracts Students: Schools with complete facilities tend to attract more students. Parents tend to choose schools that offer a wide range of facilities that can benefit their children.

4. Supports Extracurricular Activities: Complete facilities in schools support extracurricular activities such as sports, music, and drama. For example, a well-equipped sports field can allow students to engage in sports activities and improve their physical fitness.

5. Improves Overall School Environment: Complete facilities in schools create a positive environment for students and staff. It promotes a sense of pride and ownership among students, which can lead to better academic performance.
