Answer:

The main verb: Says/Say

Explanation:

Everyone says maria is very friendly.

There are three types of verbs: action verbs, linking verbs, and helping verbs.

We have two verbs but we need to find the main verb.

The word "says" is an action verb.

The word "is" is a linking verb.

But how do we find the main verb of the sentence?

To find the main verb in a sentence, remember: A main verb will usually come right after the subject.

Our subject is "everyone", but why not maria?

The subject of a sentence is the person, place, or thing that is performing the action of the sentence.

The word "everyone" is performing the action word which is says.

So the main verb is "says"
