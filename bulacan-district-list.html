6. There are currently 297 seats in the house of representatives. There are 129 fewer party-list representatives than district representatives. How many members of each type of representatives are there in congress?


ANSWER: There are 213 district representatives and 84 party-list representatives.

Representation:

x = number of district representatives

x - 129 = number of party-list representatives

297 = total

Working Equation: x + x - 129 = 297

Solution:

x + x - 129 = 297

2x - 129 = 297

2x = 297 + 129

2x = 426

2x ÷ 2 = 426 ÷ 2

x = 213 (number of district representatives)

x - 129 = number of party-list representatives

213 - 129 = 84 (number of party-list representatives)
