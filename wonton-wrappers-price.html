Suppose Sharon wishes to calculate the wrapper price based on the markdown rate of just 20%, how much would her gift wrapper cost?kindly help me:>​


Answer:

Sharon is considering reducing the price of her gift wrapper from P30 to P21 after the Christmas season.

a. To calculate the markdown rate, we can use the formula:

Markdown Rate = (Original Price - Sale Price) / Original Price * 100%

Plugging in the given values, we get:

Markdown Rate = (30 - 21) / 30 * 100%

Markdown Rate = 9 / 30 * 100%

Markdown Rate = 30%

Therefore, the markdown rate is 30%.

b. If Sharon wishes to calculate the wrapper price based on the markdown rate of just 20%, we can use the formula:

Sale Price = Original Price - Markdown Rate * Original Price

Plugging in the given values, we get:

Sale Price = 30 - 20% * 30

Sale Price = 30 - 0.20 * 30

Sale Price = 30 - 6

Sale Price = 24

Therefore, if Sharon wishes to apply a markdown rate of 20%, the sale price of her gift wrapper would be P24.
