[tex] \sf \large \frac{3}{20} = \frac{12}{n} \\ [/tex]

Using Cross-Multiplication Method, identify the equation.

[tex] \sf \large \frac{ \blue{3}}{ \green{20}} = \frac{ \green{12}}{ \blue{n}} \\ \large \boxed{ \sf \blue{3n} = \green{240}}[/tex]

Divide both sides of the equation to 3 to find the value of n.

[tex] \sf \large \frac{ \cancel3n}{ \cancel3} = \frac{240}{3} \\ \large \therefore \boxed{ \sf \orange{n = 80}}[/tex]
