Answer: This condition demonstrates Addition Property of Equality. It states that whatever is added  to one side of the equation should also be added to the other side of the equation to keep it the same.

Answer: This condition demonstrates Subtraction Property of Equality. It states that whatever is subtracted to one side of the equation should also be subtracted to the other side of the equation to keep it the same.
