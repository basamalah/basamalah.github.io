21. Define what disco is?


During the 1970's, another form and modified pop music has emerged and it is infamously known as disco. This type of pop music is a rock music that can be accompanied by a dance and is usually danceable given its tempo and rhythm. The term disco came from the French word "discotheque" which literally means a library or set of/for phonograph records. More often than not, disco is accompanied by a 4/4 time signature accompanied by strings, horns, electric guitars and electric pianos or synthesizers.

27. 3. What is the next term in the sequence 8, -32, 128?A, 512B. -512C. 512D. - 512​


The next term is -512.

8, -32, 128

Find the common ration (r).

[tex]r= -32 ÷ 8 [/tex]

[tex]r= -4[/tex]

Then, multiply the common ratio to the last term to find the next term.

[tex]a4= a3(d)[/tex]

[tex]a4= 128(-4)[/tex]

[tex]a4= -512[/tex]
