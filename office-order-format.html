Mr. Lim is a Revenue Officer at the Bureau of Internal Revenue. He has an annual salary worth Php228,924. How much is his fixed monthly compensation?


Answer:

Mr. Lim has a fixed monthly compensation of Php19,077.00.

Step-by-step explanation:

(Php228,924/year) ÷  (1 year/12 months) = Php19,077.00 per month

Mr. Lim has a fixed monthly compensation of Php19,077.00.

Additional information:

Since Mr. Lim earns Php19,077 monthly compensation as Revenue Officer, he is not a minimum wage earner government employee. His compensation is Salary Grade 11 for the rank/position Revenue Officer 1 at Bureau of Internal Revenue.
