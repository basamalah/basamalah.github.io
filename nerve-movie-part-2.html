2. Write your reflection on the movie The Nerve​


Answer:

“The Nerve” is a gripping film that explores the dark side of social media and the impact it can have on individuals and society. The movie follows the story of a young woman named Vee, who becomes a participant in a dangerous online game called “Nerve” where players perform increasingly risky and potentially harmful dares in exchange for money and recognition.

Throughout the film, we see how Vee’s participation in “Nerve” leads to a series of unexpected and dangerous situations. The movie portrays how the pursuit of fame and fortune on social media can often lead to a loss of privacy, control, and personal safety. It also raises important questions about the role of technology in our lives and the impact it can have on our relationships, identity, and sense of self.

One of the key takeaways from the movie is the importance of being aware of the potential risks and consequences of our actions online. As we engage with social media and other online platforms, it is essential to consider the impact of our digital footprint on our lives and those around us. We must recognize the potential dangers of oversharing personal information, engaging with online strangers, and participating in potentially harmful challenges or games.

Another important theme in the movie is the need for critical thinking and independent decision-making. In the film, Vee is faced with several difficult choices, and the consequences of her decisions are often unpredictable and potentially dangerous. The movie encourages viewers to think critically about the choices they make online and in real life, and to prioritize their own values and personal safety above external pressures or desires

Explanation:

Overall, “The Nerve” is a thought-provoking and impactful film that raises important questions about the impact of technology on our lives and the need for responsible online behavior. It is a cautionary tale that reminds us of the potential dangers of our digital world and the importance of making conscious, informed decisions when engaging with technology.
