Dumadaing pa din ang bawat sulok ng Marawi. Hindi niya iyon maiiyak kundi gusto niyang magpalahaw. Ang ugong ng pagsabog, putok ng baril at iyak ang siyang nakaririndi. Answer:

A.lupang hinirang

Explanation:

The Philippine National Anthem, Lupang Hinirang traces its origin way back to the year 1898. It has undergone a series of changes until it became the most sung composition by the Filipinos. ... Upon the entry of the Americans, a prohibition under the Flag Act of 1907 was created, which banned the use of the composition.
