1. I know that I am stressed when I start to feel overwhelmed, anxious, and irritable. My body also responds to stress with physical symptoms such as headaches, muscle tension, and difficulty sleeping.

2. I would say that I am currently under a moderate amount of stress.

3. My stress signals are different for different types of stressors. For example, if the stressor is related to work or school, I may experience more physical symptoms such as headaches or muscle tension. If the stressor is related to relationships or personal issues, I may experience more emotional symptoms such as anxiety or irritability.

4. To reduce my stress levels, I usually try to take some time for myself and practice relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or meditation. Additionally, talking to a friend or family member about my worries can help me gain perspective and reduce my stress levels.
