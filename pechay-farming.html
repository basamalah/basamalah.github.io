→ 23.56 × 10²³ # of pechay seeds

→ 2.356 × 10²⁴ # of pechay seeds

Step-by-step explanation:

To find out how many thirds of the farm were planted with pechay, we need to first convert the fraction 12/18 into a mixed number. To do this, we divide the numerator by the denominator and express the remainder as a fraction. This gives us 12/18 = 0 + 12/18 = 0 + 2/3.

Since a third is equal to 1/3, this means that 2/3 is equivalent to 2 * 1/3 = 2/3 * 3/3 = 6/9. So, the farmer planted 6/9 of his farm with pechay, which is equal to 6/9 * 3/1 = 6/3 = 2 thirds of the farm. Therefore, the answer to the question is that 2 thirds of the farm were planted with pechay.
