3. A car is travelling from kabankalan to bacolod. The distance is 90 kilometers. It took the car 2 hours from the starting place to its destination. What is the distance of the car​


Answer:

The distance of the car is 90 kilometers.

Explanation:

The problem states that a car is traveling from Kabankalan to Bacolod, which are two places located at a certain distance from each other. The distance between these two places is given as 90 kilometers. It is also mentioned that the car took 2 hours to reach its destination from the starting place, which is not explicitly stated in the problem.

To find the distance traveled by the car, we can use the formula:

distance = speed x time

Here, we do not have information about the speed of the car, but we can calculate it by dividing the distance by the time taken:

speed = distance / time

speed = 90 km / 2 hours

speed = 45 km/h

Now, we can use the calculated speed and the given time to find the distance traveled by the car:

distance = speed x time

distance = 45 km/h x 2 hours

distance = 90 km

Therefore, the distance traveled by the car from Kabankalan to Bacolod is 90 kilometers.

30. what is the highlights of the event in bacolod??​


Answer:

Masskara Festival is held every October. This is the most popular event in Bacolod. The highlight of this festival is a street parade of people wearing colorful masks and costumes dancing to the rhythmic beat of drums. This festival commemorates the foundation day of the city. Masskara Festival was originated when the sugar industry (Bacolod’s chief industry) was not doing very well. The festival has become an event that celebrates the determination and resiliency of the Filipino spirit. Good or bad times it may be, they face it with a smile.
