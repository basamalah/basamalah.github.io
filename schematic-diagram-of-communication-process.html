5. advantage of schematic diagram​


Answer:

Hi there!

Knowing Schematic Diagram

A schematic diagram, is a representation of the elements of a system using abstract, graphic symbols rather than realistic pictures. A schematic usually elide all details that are not relevant to the key information the schematic is intended to convey, and may include oversimplified elements in order to make this essential meaning easier to grasp.

What are the use of Schematic Diagram

Schematic diagrams are used in repair manuals to help users understand the interconnections of parts, and to provide graphical instruction to assist in dismantling and rebuilding mechanical assemblies. Many automotive and motorcycle repair manuals devote a significant number of pages to schematic diagrams.

Advantage and Disadvantage

Advantage: it shows all the electronic parts and how they are interconnected in an easy to follow format.

Disadvantage: it falls down at high frequencies as it does not show parasitic components and lead lengths and other high frequency critical parts. Plus it does not show mechanical parts like heat sinks.
