10. balik-aral deped meaning


Ang programang Balik-Aral ay tumutukoy sa pagtanggap ng mga estudyanteng nahinto sa pag-aaral nang ilang taon ngunit nais nang magpatuloy nito.

Balik-Aral learners ang tawag ng Department of Education sa mga estudyanteng nagpapalista sa pasukan. Hindi tinatanggihan ng DepEd sa enrollment ang sinumang nahinto sa pag-aaral kahit pa nang maraming taon.

Kalimitang nahihinto sa pag-aaral ang marami dahil sa kawalan ng panustos. Kabilang sa nahihinto sa pag-aaral ng mga batang napipilitang magtrabaho para makatulong sa pamilya.

Mayroon ding nahihinto sa pag-aaral dahil kapos na kapos na sila sa perang pambaon, pambili ng mga gamit sa paaralan, o kahit pa pambili ng damit o tsinelas. Maraming biktima ng kahirapan ang nais makapag-aral ngunit hindi nabibigyan ng pagkakataon dahil sa mga hamon ng buhay.

Sa tulong ng programang Balik-Aral, buong giliw na tinatanggap ang mga nais ulit makapasok sa mga paaralan. Para sa mga matatanda nang nais makapag-aral, nariyan naman ang Alternative Learning System ng DepEd.

16. What is the meaning deped vision


Answer:

What is the meaning of Department of Education (DEPED) Vision?

The DepEd vision means that the organization dreams of making students who have love for their country and skilled enough to contribute to their country's success.

Teachers also dream of students who are competent and prepared enough to face everyday life. It is also must be learner-centered.

What is the DepEd Mission?

To keep everyone including students, teachers and staff safe and to promote the rights of every Filipino.

What is the purpose of DepEd?

The Department of Education or DepEd is the department of the Philippine government responsible for ensuring access to, promotes equity and improve the quality of education.

What is the difference between Mission and Vision of DepEd?

A mission statement defines what DepEd is currently doing, while a vision statement is the ultimate goal of what they'd like to accomplish. The mission is what DepEd do in order to achieve the vision.

17. What is the meaning of deped mission


What is the meaning of deped mission

THE DEPED VISION

We dream of Filipinos

who passionately love their country

and whose values and competencies

enable them to realize their full potential

and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

As a learner-centered public institution,

the Department of Education

continuously improves itself

to better serve its stakeholders.

Explanation

• The department of education envision to produce citizens who love country and pride in its rich heritage.  

• The department of education envision to produce citizens who instill good values and competence in their hearts and a citizen who will take account on the interest of the community and for the benefit of all in building a better nation.

• The focus of the learning is more on students holistically development.

THE DEPED MISSION

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:

Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.

Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.

Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.

Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.

Explanation:

• Department of Education envisioning to fulfill the mandate of the republic act 9155 to let the children to have a quality education because this is the right of all citizens.

• The mission of the Department is to make the students safe, to develop the students holistically so  that can be usein functional life skills or support services for the improvement of the quality of their lives.

• Teachers should act as a  key learning support for the students. They are responsible in supervising  and facilitating the learning process and activities of the learners.

• Administrators and staff has also a role in administrative and instructional supervision to make the school safe and conducive for learning.

• Family, community and other stakeholders has also a big  role or  a part in attaining schools’ mission and vision in promoting equity and improved basic quality education.
