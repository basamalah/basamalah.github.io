23. bakit bilog ang butas ng manhole​


Answer:

It's more than aesthetics. It's a design issue. Stress is at its greatest on corners. If manholes were square or triangular, constant repairs would be done because cracks would develop on the corners. And manholes face a barrage of stress from the vehicles driving over it, the pedestrians trodding over it, etc,. etc.. With a round design, the stress is evenly distributed since there is no point of concentration.

Ang takip manhole ay para hindi na kailangang paikutin. Walang mga sulok na haharapin. Gayundin, ang isang bilog na takip ng manhole ay hindi mahuhulog sa isang butas dahil ito ay pinaikot sa maling paraan, kaya ito ay mas ligtas.

26. Find the net area of a manhole cover 50 cm in diameter with 13 vent holes, each 5 cm diameter.​


Area of Plane Figure in Real Life Situation

Answer: 6 829.5 cm²

The given problem is an application of solving area of the plane figure in real life situation. You will apply your basic knowledge in mathematics and geometry especially in solving area of circle and how many items can be occupied in the space provided and identifying it's dimensions.

The given problem, "Find the net area of a manhole cover 50 cm in diameter with 13 vent holes, each 5 cm diameter."

Solution and Discussion

In mathematics, solving area of plane figure is commonly used in architectural and construction industry including floor covering, such as carpets and tiles. Calculation of dimensions and area measurements applied in wallpaper and paint. Solving area also applied in industry including machineries, clothing and other items that used length and width of the figure.

How to find the area of a circle? Area of a square is the space occupied by the shape express in square units. Finding area is the product of constant π and square of radius (A = πr²).

Step-by-step Solution

Step 1: Identify all the given in the problem

Dimension of a manhole cover is 50 cm in diameter

Dimension of vent holes is 5 cm diameter

There are 13 vent holes

Step 2: Identify what is being ask

What is net area of the manhole?

Step 3: Create mathematical equation using the formula.

A = πr², when r = 50 cm → manhole

A = 50²π cm²

A = 2500π cm²

A = πr², when r = 5 cm → vent holes

A = π(5 cm)²

A = 25π cm²

Net area is the unusable area.

Net Area = Area of manhole - Area of vent holes

→ A = 2500π cm² - (13)(25π cm²)

Step 4: Simplify the equation and solve for the area

A = 2500π cm² - (13)(25π cm²)

A = 2 500π cm² - 325π cm²

A = π(2500 - 325) cm²

Do not cancelled out the π, the value of π is 3.1416... It is constant.

A = π(2500 - 325) cm²

A = 2 175π cm², it is already the answer. But if your teacher ask for the approximate value of the area. Substitute the value of π.

When π = 3.14 (2 decimal places value of π)

A = 2175(3.14)

A = 6 829.5 cm²

Thus, the net area of the manhole is 6 829.5 cm²
