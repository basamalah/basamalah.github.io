A term, morpheme, or phrase that in a particular language has the exact same meaning as another word, morpheme, or phrase is said to be a synonym. For example, A synonym for enough is plenty. The words scarcity and shortage are interchangeable. As a shortage is to scarcity, plenty is to enough.
