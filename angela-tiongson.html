Then, Joyce’s Age = x/2 (half of Angela’s)
And Sally’s age = 4x (4 times of Angela’s)
Also Sally’s age= x/2 + 42 (42 yrs older than Joyce)
So:
4x = x/2 + 42
2(4x = x/2 + 42)
8x = x + 84
8x-x = 84
7x = 84
x= 12
Therefore, Angela’s age is 12 years old.
