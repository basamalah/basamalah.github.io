One's security is one's duty. Despite the existence of armed personnels, we should always put in our mind that securing ourselves from all sorts of incidents is one of our priorities. The best way for us to secure ourselves from any kind of injury, may it be planned or not is to Be Vigilant. Be vigilant of your surroundings. Look for things that are new to you and or suspicious. If you have seen one, report to the concern authorities. The next thing you should do is to report it to the authorities. Moreover, as much as possible stay away from places which are not well-lighted and if it is unavoidable, make sure you have someone accompanying you.

18. action plan for increasing the awareness to prevent and correct managing intentional injuries​


Answer:

Intentional injuries are those which are caused by violence and often manifest as trauma in victims. These include deaths and injuries caused by gun violence, physical and sexual assault and abuse, suicide, and bullying, to name a few.

Because these injuries are intentional, by their very nature they are often preventable. By implementing policies, laws, and safeguards at schools, homes, and the community, studies have shown that these kinds of injuries and deaths can be mitigated. Many of these policies are commonplace: firearm sales restrictions, hotlines and counseling to report instances of abuse, and after-school programs to keep children off of the streets are only some of the ways that communities try to prevent intentional violence.

Using comprehensive strategies to combat intentional violence has been shown to be effective at local, state, and national levels. Training both children and authorities in risk recognition, risk prevention, and risk intervention may help to curb intentional violence in schools, homes, and communities.

24. The best way to avoid the dangers of planned or intentional injuries is through?


The best way to avoid the dangers of planned or intentional injuries is through prevention. There are always dangers out there and the ideal is that criminals are captured and incarcerated. But this is not always the case. Hence, it is also on us to take care of ourselves. There are several ways in which we can do this:

Learn Defensive Moves

If you are walking and someone starts to follow you, walk confidently to a crowded area or a police station. This would hopefully deter anyone from following you further.  

If you are being shot at by a gun, run away in a zigzag pattern while keeping your head down. This will lessen the chances of you being hit.  

Always be alert and never use mobile phones in public unless necessary.

Learn Martial Arts

If possible you may also learn martial arts to be able to defend yourself. This is not advisable though if a gun is used or if you are not in full awareness of who you are dealing with.  

Have defensive equipment ready

Always bring an umbrella which could double as a defensive equipment. You can also bring pepper spray. There are also anti-rape whistles that can be used.  

Note: This is not shifting the responsibility to the would-be victims. Again, it is ideal that there are no criminals and that people are taught not to commit planned and intentional crimes. But it would not hurt to be ready as well.
