Step-by-step explanation:

weight ÷ number of pieces

300 ÷ 400 = 0.75

each manggo weights 0.75 pounds.

How to know if answer is correct?

weight per piece × number of pieces

0.75 × 400 = 300

Thus the weight of each manggo is 0.75 pounds.
