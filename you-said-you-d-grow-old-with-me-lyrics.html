3. "You don't stop playing because you grow old; you grow old because you stop playing!" EXPLAIN​


Answer:

Play is something we are all made to do, and it is vital to our overall health. But why is it that as we get older, we stop playing? Why does society make us feel that because we are now an adult that we can't play?

"You don't stop playing because you grow old; you grow old because you stop playing!"

Explanation;

Relating to physical education, an individual stays healthy when he/she plays sport and he/she doesn't forget go do daily routines. When you continue playing sports and recreational activities, the body remains healthy and strong from diseases. The person would only "grow old" or grow weak when he/she stops playing sports and physical activities. The quote only says to remain active in physical activities and sports to stay healthy and fit, and not to ignore and stop these routines to prevent sickness and fast aging.
