21. other country story that has philippine filipino culture and tradition​


One example of a story from another country that includes Philippine Filipino culture and tradition is the novel "America is in the Heart" by Carlos Bulosan. The book tells the story of a young Filipino migrant who travels to the United States in search of a better life and experiences discrimination and hardship in his new home. The novel also explores themes of identity, community, and the importance of cultural heritage, highlighting the Filipino American experience and the struggle for equality and justice. Throughout the book, Bulosan incorporates elements of Philippine Filipino culture and tradition, including language, food, music, and folklore, to convey the richness and diversity of his heritage and to celebrate the resilience and perseverance of his people.
