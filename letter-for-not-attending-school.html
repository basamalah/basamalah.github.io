2. Parent consent letter letting student attend activities in school


An example of a consent letter letting student to attend activities in school.

A consent letter is useful for many occasions. One example is how teachers know whether their students are allowed to take part in a school activity by means of a signed consent letter from the students' parents. The nature of this letter is formal, and usually it includes a signature from the guardian of the person who's asking for the permission to do something. There is also no fixed template, so any modification is always welcome in a consent letter.

A consent letter from parents goes something like:I, a guardian/ parent of [student name], who is a student at [school name and grade] have therefore allow [student name] to take part in [activity name] between [the schedule]. Here lies my signature, [signature and full name of the parents/ guardian].
