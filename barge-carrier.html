8. Transforms af signal and rf carrier wave to a modulated carrier


Transforms AF signal and RF carrier wave to a modulated carrier. — Modulator

Modulator

A modulator is an electronic device that overlays information from a low-frequency signal or input signal to a carrier or a high-frequency signal. Higher frequency signals can be received through shorter aerials which makes them more practical. The information stored on these signals can be digital or analog.

Modulation

It is the process where carrier signals are imposed with input signals. It is like changing the structure of the carrier wave by hiding information from the input wave. Modulation through a modulator helps in cheaper and more convenient communication. It requires us to store information while using less power.
