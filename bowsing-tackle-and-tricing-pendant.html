9. trice the difference of eight and five is is y​


To translate the verbal phrase “trice the difference of eight and five is y” to algebraic equation, we’ll have 3(8-5)=y, and that y = 9.

QUESTION:

Translate the verbal phrase “trice the difference of eight and five is y” to algebraic equation.

ANSWER:

To translate verbal phrase to equations, the first thing that you must do is to look for some key words in the statement or phrase that are used to describe common mathematical operations like addition, subtraction, division, multiplication, and even equation.

Then, you must assign variable/s to represent the unknown number. It can be any of the letters in the alphabet unless it is already provided in the problem.

trice the difference of eight and five is y

The key words are thrice (times 3, 3×), difference (-), and is (=). The variable used is y. There is no need for us to represent the unknown number by a different letter.The answer will be 3(8-5) = y.The statement is a mathematical equation.

Actually, you can get the value of unknown number, y, in the equation through solving.

3(8-5) = y3(3) = y9 = y

Therefore, the value of the unknown variable, y, is 9.
