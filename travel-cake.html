About active and passive sentences
Based on the relationship between subject, predicate and meaning value, sentences are classified into two types: active sentences and passive sentences.
The function of passive sentences in grammar is to understand the meaning of a sentence so that it is used correctly in writing.  Passive sentences are used in any language in every writing, and English is no exception.  In English, the active sentence can be changed to passive by changing the subject, then using the appropriate word to be and ending with a third verb.  The object in the active sentence changes to the subject in the passive sentence.
The role of the sentence in writing is very important in terms of its function as a founder of a more detailed language structure.  The existence of both active and passive sentences is universally used in all languages ​​in the world, including English.
