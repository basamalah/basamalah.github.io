10. 1) 6 weeks = ____ days 2) 42 days = ____ weeks 3) 600 days = ____ months 4) 6 months = ____ days 5) 3 years (with no leap year) = _____ days


Conversion:
1 week=7 days
1 month=30 days(approx)
1 year=356.242

Convert:
1)[tex]6 weeks(\frac{7 days}{1 week})=42 days[/tex]
2)[tex]42 days(\frac{1 week}{7 days})=6 weeks[/tex]
3)[tex]600 days(\frac{1 month}{30 days})=20 month[/tex]
4)[tex]6 months(\frac{30 days}{1 month})=180 days[/tex]
5)[tex]3 years(\frac{356.242 days}{1 year})=1,068.726 days[/tex]
