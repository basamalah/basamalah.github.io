29. Vegetable salad recipefruit salad recipe pasta salad recipegelatin salad recipebound salad recipecomposed salad recipe​


Cream Drop

INGREDIENTS

1 pack NESTLÉ ALL PURPOSE CREAM 250ml

1 can condensed milk 168 ml

1 can 532g fruit cocktail, drained

1 bottle 160g white Nata de Coco, drained

3 pcs banana (lakatan), sliced ¼-inch thick

400g seedless grapes (mixture of green and red), halved

Curve

STEPS

STEP 1

Combine NESTLÉ ALL PURPOSE CREAM and condensed milk.

STEP 2

Add fruit cocktail, nata de coco, banana and grapes. Mix well.

STEP 3

Transfer on serving container and refrigerate overnight. Serve chilled.
