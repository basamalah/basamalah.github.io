Fact: Some drugs can create addiction or dependence much quicker than others. 30. define drug use and drug abuse


Answer:

DRUG USE — Drug use is often referred to as a single episode of use of a substance both for medicinal or recreational purposes. The substance of choice can vary from person to person. Some of the most commonly used drugs include alcohol, marijuana, caffeine, ibuprofen, etc.

DRUG ABUSE — Clinically known as substance use disorder, drug abuse or addiction is caused by the habitual taking of addictive substances.
