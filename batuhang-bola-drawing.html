Dodgeball or Batuhang BolaDodgeball is a team sport in which players must avoid or catch balls thrown by their opponents while also attempting to strike their opponents.

The Objective of the Game Dodgeball

All opposing players must be "out" in order to be eliminated. If a player is declared out for the following reasons:

You hit an opponent with a thrown ball BELOW THE SHOULDERS before it touches the ground.BEFORE the ball makes contact with the ground or the walls, you catch an opponent's thrown ball.Any boundary lines you cross are crossed.

How to Play:Each team should begin the game with eight players, with at least two women. To avoid penalties, a minimum of one woman is required.Teams that do not have the required number of women will be forced to play short, minus the number of women who are missing.A team can have more than eight players. The team can begin on the court with a maximum of 8 individuals (1 woman required), and the rest can begin in the jail. A team may not have more than 8 players on the floor at any time throughout the game.There is a maximum of six male players on the court at any given time.A match is made up of nine games. Each game is accounted for independently in the standings and is unrelated to the others.Each team will pick a player to serve as the field captain. Only this player has the ability to interact with the referee.It is acceptable for team captains to invite players from other teams to "Sub or Fill In" open positions on game day during the regular season to avoid forfeits. A player can only participate and play for one team in the playoffs. If a player is discovered to be playing on two or more teams during the playoffs, all games are forfeited.

Batuhang Bola

Dodgeball originated in Africa. It was played there for over two hundred years ago. The original version of it is more painful because instead of rubber balls, they used rocks or putrefied matter. That version is played by African tribes to train them. Dodgeball or Batuhang Bola is a game in which players on two teams try to throw balls at each other while avoiding being hit themselves.The objective of each team is to eliminate all members of the opposing team by hitting them with thrown balls, catching a ball thrown by an opponent, or induce an opponent to commit a violation, such as stepping outside the court.

How to play batuhang bola

To play Batuhang Bola or dodgeball, you must first gather 2 teams and get a ball. The 1st team should stand in the middle of the court, while the 2nd team should be divided into two and should stand at both sides of the 1st team. The 2nd team should hit the players of the 1st team.
