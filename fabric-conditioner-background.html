23. jhon ver is repaking 510 ml fabric conditioner into 25.5 ml bottles.how many of these bottles does he needA)15 B) 18C) 20D) 24​


Answer:

C. 20

Step-by-step explanation:

This question is a simple one-step word problem.

In this solution, I will assume that Jhon Ver only has one bottle of 510 ml fabric conditioner to start with.

Think of this question like dividing the substance into groups. And when you do that, you need to divide the total amount by the number of groups. Thus, the operation is division.

510 ÷ 25.5 = 20 bottles

Therefore, Jhon Ver needs 20 bottles of 25.5 ml capacity to repack a bigger bootle of fabric conditioner that can hold 510 ml.

If the answer must be expressed in correct significant figures, the answer must be 2.0 x 10^1 or 2.0E1.
