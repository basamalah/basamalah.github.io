Answer:

100 stickers

Step-by-step explanation:

If Faizah gives Noah 40 stickers

Faizah : Noah

1. : 9

1 + 9 = 10

If Faizah gives Noah 60 stickers

Faizah : Noah

1. : 14.

1 + 14 = 15

LCM OF 15 AND 10 = 30

30 / 10 = 3

30 / 15 = 2

3 - 2 = 1

(60-40) / 1 = 20

20 X 3 + 40 = 100
