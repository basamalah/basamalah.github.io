Answer:

He gained Php 3,000

Explanation:

If Arnold bought 300 shares of MACQ at 400 pesos per share and sold half of it he will gain 3,000 because:

300 x 400 = 120,000 pesos

300÷2= 150

150 x 400 = 60.000

150 x 420 = 63, 000

60,000 + 63,000 = 123, 000

123,000 - 120,000= Php 3,000

21. 1. Explain the characteristics and the color which has been used that is being portrayed by the cao-cao​


Answer

In Peking Opera, the white color symbolizes sinister, treacherous, and suspicious traits. It also signifies powerful, cunning, and crafty characters. Being crafty means clever at achieving a goal by indirect or deceitful methods. That is why the white color is often used to emphasize powerful and cruel villains, like Prime Minister Cao Cao, in a Peking Opera.

Cao Cao is one of the main characters in stories like "Romance of the Three Kingdoms" by Luo Guanzhong and the Peking Opera, "Meeting of the Elite."
