1. sino Ang lumikha ng songbook?​


Answer:

The creation of songbooks can be attributed to the efforts of many individuals and groups over time. Songbooks are collections of songs that are compiled and arranged in a particular format, often for use in singing or music education. The earliest known songbooks date back to the medieval period, and were used primarily in religious contexts. Over time, songbooks have been created for a variety of purposes and genres, including popular music, folk songs, and hymns.

It is difficult to pinpoint a single individual or group as the creator of the songbook, as the development of this musical format has been an ongoing process of evolution and adaptation over many centuries. However, it is clear that songbooks have played an important role in the preservation and dissemination of music throughout history, and continue to do so in the present day.

11. While waiting for a video game to download, you notice that 30\%30% of 32{,}00032,000 kilobytes have been downloaded so far. How many kilobytes have been downloaded so far?


Answer:

9600 kilobytes of the video game have been downloaded so far.

Step-by-step explanation:

Finding the Percentage

Let us analyze the problem.

What is asked?

How many kilobytes of the game have been downloaded so far?

What are the given?

total number of kilobytes of the game - 32000

kilobytes that have been downloaded so far - 30%

To find the amount of kilobytes that have been downloaded so far, we need to get the percentage or the 30% of 32000.

To do that we have the formula:

Percentage = Base × Rate

Percentage

Percentage is the part of the base determined by the rate.

Base

Base is the whole on which the rate operates. It is the number represents 100% or the total value of something or the whole thing.

Rate

Rate is the number of hundredths parts taken. It is the number with a percent sign (%).

Based on our given, the base is 32000, the rate is 30% and the missing number is the percentage.

Solution:

Before getting the percentage, we need to convert the rate to decimal first.

Converting Percent to Decimal

We have two ways to convert percent to decimal.

Divide by 100.

Using a calculator is the easiest way to divide this but if there is no available calculator, do the long division.

30 ÷ 100 = 0.30

Move the decimal point.

Imagine that there is a decimal point after the last digit. Move the decimal point twice going to your left.

30. = 0.30

The rate is 0.30.

For example of converting percent to decimal, visit the links:

brainly.ph/question/1892802

Now we can proceed with finding the percentage. Substitute the values.

Percentage = Base × Rate

= 32000 × 0.30

= 9600

Final Answer:

9600 kilobytes have been downloaded.

Let:
x-be the cost for the number of songs

6x=4.68
Divide both sides by 6
x=0.78

To cost ten songs, multiply the value of each song by 10
10(0.78)
=7.8
