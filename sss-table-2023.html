Mga tip para sa pagsalubong sa 2023

1. Gumawa ng New Year's resolution para sa 2023

Karamihan sa mga tao ay maaaring isulat kung ano ang mga target na makakamit bilang mga resolusyon ng bagong taon, ngunit iilan lamang ang maaaring matupad ito. Dahil karamihan sa atin ay hindi matukoy kung alin ang long term target (vision) at alin ang short term target (mission).

2. Makamit ang mga target na hindi pa nakakamit

Magsagawa ng pagsusuri sa bagong taon at hatiin ang iyong malalaking target sa maliliit na target na madaling maabot ngayong taon. Sa ganoong paraan, sikolohikal na magiging masigasig ka tungkol sa pagkamit ng maliliit na target na ito.

3. Maghanda ng mas mabuting pagpaplano sa pananalapi

This new year is the right momentum para mas maging maingat ka sa financial planning. Simulan ang pagtatala ng kita at mga gastos at maghanda ng emergency fund. Sa pangkalahatan, ang pinakamainam na halaga ng emergency fund ay 3 beses sa buwanang paggasta.

30. Plane (2023) Summary?


A commercial pilot, Captain Brodie Torrance, crashlands in the middle of nowhere after being struck by lightning. As all the communications are down in the deserted land, matters turn worse as it's a war zone, and there's also a murderer on board. A mixed bunch of passengers - dubious, scowling, arrogant, happy campers, and a fearsome-looking Louis Gaspare (Mike Colter), who's an accused murderer being extradited by the F-B-I - boards Trailblazer's flight just before New Year's Eve. Even against his advice, pilot Captain Brodie Torrance (Gerard Butler) is forced to fly the plane on a route with stormy weather to save fuel and time. As lightning strikes and the plane loses one of its wings, Torrance makes a risky landing on the deserted island of Jolo in the Philippines. After the crash landing the passengers and crew face a new threat. The Jolo Island is home to a well-armed group of pirates who fund their operations by hunting for hostages. Short on options, Captain Torrance (a former member of the RAF) soon joins forces with Gaspare, who just happens to have spent time with the French Legion (!) to save the day. Louis Gaspare was freed from his restraints after they landed by Captain Torrance because the captain needed him to go with him in search of a phone or someone who could help them notify the authorities.

They discovered a warehouse, where Captain Torrance was able to get in touch with the Trailblazer and his daughter. They also learned that the warehouse is used to take hostages and kill them. Travelers were taken captive and taken to the terrorists' base after the terrorists went to their base. Brodie and Louis learned that the rebels had been torturing and filming their captives in exchange for ransom.

Plane is not an overly ambitious film. Like the title, it knows what it wants to do and gets the job done. It would be overselling things to describe what Butler and Colter have as a bromance. Instead there's a begrudging atmosphere of practicality. The jungle is filled with bad guys. Someone has taken the civilians. Let's find them and kill them.

Director Jean-Francois Richet smoothly ratchets up the tension as the film cuts back home to airline headquarters where Tony Goldwyn plays the fast-talking corporate troubleshooter who begins deploying resources, adding a team of mercenaries into the mix. Soon the body count and the tempo of Plane increases.
