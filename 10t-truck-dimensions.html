25. A truck with dimensions 9 feet long, 7 feet wide, and 5 feet high is delivering sandfor a sand sculpture competition. How many trips must the truck make to deliver945 cubic feet of sand?​


3 TripsLength of the truck = 9 ft.Width of the truck = 7 ft.Height of the truck = 5 ft.

» Volume of sand that can be carried by the truck = Length x Width x Height

= 9 x 7 x 5

= 45 x 7

= 315 cubic ft. of sand in one trip.

We have to find the no. of trips the truck has to take to cover 945 cubic ft. of sand, which is:

[tex]\sf{\longrightarrow\:\dfrac{Total\:volume\:of\:sand}{Volume\:of\:sand\:taken\:in\:one\:trip}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{\longrightarrow\:\dfrac{945}{315}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{\longrightarrow\:3}[/tex]

The truck needs to take 3 trips to deliver 945 cubic ft. of sand.
