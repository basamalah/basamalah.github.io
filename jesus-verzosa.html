7. Who was the first Filipina to win the Miss International beauty title in 1964? A.Margarita Moran B.Gloria Diaz C.Gemma CruzD.Kylie Fausto VerzosaNeed now...​


Answer:

The answer for question above is C. Gemma Cruz

Explanation:

Gemma Teresa Guerrero Cruz Araneta or Gemma Cruz was a representative for Philippine at international beauty pageant on 1964. At that time, she succeed to claim the title as “Miss International” and became the first Philippines representative and Asian who ever wins the international beauty pageant.

Gemma Cruz was born in Manila on September, 30th 1943. She was a politician, writer, and director who once win the Miss Philippines pageant on 1964, then she earned the right for being a Philippines’s representative on international beauty pageant. Gemma Cruz was raised in a renown family. She has a great-grandmother who was the sister of the Philippines’s national hero, Jose Rizal, and her uncle, Leon Ma Guerrero III was a Philippines’s Ambassador to Spain and England.

After winning the contest, she received an “Oustanding Manilena” and a “Golden Heart” Presidential decoration from President Diosdado Macapagal. She’s also donated all her Miss International $10.000 cash prize to build homes for children sleeping on the streets in Manila.
