11. Research title that is related to ABM strand


ABM (The Accountancy, Business, and Management) STRAND

The Accountancy, Business, and Management Strand offers students the fundamentals of business in the 21st-century era. This strand will unmask the different theories, perceptions, and concepts in business, accountancy, marketing, advertising, and entrepreneurship to prepare the learners for real-life situations and further studies.

The ABM Strand is for those who plan to take up business-related courses in higher education or engage in business, entrepreneurship, and other business-related careers. The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand would focus on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and all things that are accounted for. ABM can also lead you to careers in management and accounting which could be a sales manager, human resources, marketing director, project officer, bookkeeper, accounting clerk, internal auditor, and a lot more.

This strand is designed as an introductory course in accounting and business and management where students are trained to think logically and scientifically and are acquainted with the rudiments of accounting, business, and management concepts and principles in order to prepare them to pursue college degrees that focus on business and industry where their contribution as future accountants, entrepreneurs, and business leaders are vital to the progress and development of the economy and critical to the promotion of a sustainable green business.

14. quantitative research topic related to ABM strand​


Answer:

Choosing an ABM Qualitative or Quantitative Research Topic

The first step in writing an ABM research paper is choosing a topic. With the abundance of ABM research topics ideas on the Internet, it’s not an easy task. Simply picking one won’t do the trick. You will need to juggle relevance, applicability, and your own personal interest in the subject.

There are two main types of AMB research methods: qualitative and quantitative.

Qualitative research answers the why and the how questions. It tests customers’ reaction to new products and studies consumer behaviors. Case studies, interviews, and focus groups are the common methods of collecting such data.

Quantitative research collects numerical data and analyzes stats. The common methods include various surveys in target groups.

What ABM fields to explore are out there?

Financial accounting (aka accountancy) is creating financial statements to be distributed within and outside of a company.

Management accounting is creating operational reports to be distributed only within a company.

Banking and finance are all about financial services provided to customers, and the laws of investment.

Business administration is supervising and overseeing various business operations.

Marketing is all about the promotion of buying and selling services and products.

Entrepreneurship is all about the process of coming up with, starting and managing a new business.

Human resource development management is all about reaching the top potential of the employees.

Hospitality management is overseeing and supervising various administrative tasks of a resort or hotel.

Tourism is all about attracting, housing, and entertaining tourists, and organizing tours for them.

28. Example of quantitative research title about abm strand


The ABM strand in high school can be a good area for quantitative research. Titles range from the easiest of topics to the most difficult quantitative research in the ABM strand. It is critical that any research is carefully studied with the data carefully analyzed, especially if it involves numbers or statistics like in quantitative research. Below are examples of quantitative research tile about the ABM strand.  

Sample Titles

Trend analysis of ABM strand students and their respective courses in college Comparative analysis of the grades of ABM students in Math with non-ABM students Correlation of hours put in studies with results of the final examinations Five-year longitudinal study of the pass rate of ABM students in major colleges Analysis of enrollment in ABM strand versus non-ABM strands Patterns in quiz failure rates among ABM students Pass rate analysis of ABM graduates who took the CPA boards

The titles above use both trend analysis, statistics and data analysis, which are all elements of a quantitative study. Take note, however, that any study is always a combination of quantitative and qualitative study. There will always be numbers involved and some sort of description and analysis. Hence it is best to be good at both types of studies.
