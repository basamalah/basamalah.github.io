3. What is your interpretation in Elmer Borlongan's painting "Father's Whisper"?​


Answer:

In "Father's Whisper" by Elmer Borlongan, an enigmatic stillness pervades the scene, as a man and his son sit solemnly on a bench amidst the tranquil park. The father leans in to whisper an inscrutable message into his son's ear, who listens with rapt attention. The subdued lighting and subdued hues hint at a serene moment, yet the father's insinuations add an elusive veil of secrecy and suspense to the ambiance. The painting beckons the observer to contemplate what the father could be murmuring to his son, and how it would ripple through the boy's life.    


4. What is the meaning of one sack of rice by elmer borlongan painting


Answer:

"One Sack of Rice" by Elmer Borlongan is a painting that reflects the reality of poverty and hunger in the Philippines. It shows a young child carrying a sack of rice, which symbolizes the daily struggle of many families to secure food for survival. The painting is a commentary on the social and economic issues that continue to afflict the country and serves as a reminder of the need for greater equality and justice.

Elmer Borlongan is a prominent contemporary Filipino painter who is known for his realist style and his depictions of everyday life in the Philippines. Through his paintings, he addresses the problems of poverty, urbanization, and social inequality. "One Sack of Rice" is an example of his powerful imagery that serves to raise awareness of the challenges faced by many people in the Philippines.

17. Imelda is three times as old as Tony. Elmer is 5 years older than Tony. The sum of all their ages is 75 years. How old is Elmer?​


Age Word Problem

Elmer is 19 years old.

Age word problem deals with the ages of one or more people. It can be about the persons ages at present, in the past or in the future. For the given word problem above, it is asking for Elmer's age. Below is the explanation on how to find the answer.

Explanation:

Let us analyze the word problem. It is important to identify the given facts in the problem for the equation.

Given:

Let "x" be Tony's age.

Imelda - three times as old as Tony or 3x

Elmer - 5 years older than Tony or x + 5

sum of their ages - 75

Equation:

We will be using an algebraic equation to find the answer.

x + 3x + x + 5 = 75

Solution:

Combine similar terms and isolate the variable.

x + 3x + x + 5 = 75

5x + 5 = 75

5x = 75 - 5

5x = 70

[tex] \frac{5x}{5} \times \frac{70}{5} [/tex]

x = 14

This means that Tony is 14 years old. To find the age of Elmer, add 5 to 14, since he is 5 years older.

14 + 5 = 19

Elmer is 19 years old.

Checking:

To check if the answer is correct, substitute the value of "x" in the equation.

x + 3x + x + 5 = 75

14 + 3(14) + 14 + 5 = 75

14 + 42 + 14 + 5 = 75

75 = 75 ✔
