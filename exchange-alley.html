Blood vessels are the alleys and highways for the circulatory system. It is the passageway of the blood which are connected to different parts of the body in order for the nutrient and oxygen will be delivered accordingly.

Three type of Blood vessel:

Artery  

The blood vessel that carries oxygenated blood away from the heart and have more pressure compared to the vein.

Vein

The blood vessel that carries unoxygenated blood which goes back to the heart and will be transported to the lungs to get oxygen.

Capillaries

Tiny vessel which connect the vein and the artery as they distribute the nutrients in the different parts of the body.
