26. NOW PO KELANGAN3. On what stage of the communication process does the schemata activate first?A. ReceivingC. ConceptualizingB. EncodingD. Decoding​


C. Conceptualizing

The schemata activate first in the conceptualizing stage of the communication process. It is where the sender thinks, visualizes, and formulates his or her message for the receiver.

Communication is a process where the sender sends a message to the receiver in a particular medium. The messages are transmitted in different forms of medium such as print, oral, or even non-verbal actions.

Here are the 5 main stages of the communication process.

                                      Conceptualizing

Conceptualizing is a necessary stage in the communication process. It is where the ideas are formed and what information is needed to be relayed during a conversation. Every communication between individuals requires conceptualizing.
                                                           ↓

                                           Encoding

Message encoding is the stage where the sender of the message relays what is in his thought or mind. This can be done orally, through gestures, sign language, or written works to convey the information. Proper encoding of messages must be observed to make an impact and start healthy communication.
                                                           ↓

                                Message Transmission

Message transmission is the stage in which kind of medium the sender will be used to relay information. This can be done in a verbal way, visual, or written. Proper message transmission is one of the most crucial stages in the communication process.
                                                           ↓

                                           Decoding

Decoding is the stage where the messages reach the receiver. He or she will interpret the information to understand it. Proper decoding is also crucial to help the sender realize that you understand his or her message.
                                                          ↓

                                           Feedback

The last stage of the communication process is feedback. For some, it could also be the start of another communication process because this stage requires conceptualizing before sending out feedback. It is where you give your comments, questions, interpretation, agreement, or even clarifications about the received message.

29. Schemata is the basis on how we perceive things based on previous experiences or events.


Cognitive Development: Schemas/Schemata

True. Schemata or schemas, is the plural form of schema. They are based on associations with how events should turn out based on previous experiences with similar events. Schemas for events are powerful. Schema is a mental concept that informs a person about his expectations from different experiences and situations. It is developed based on the information provided by life experiences that are then stored in memory. It is used by our brains as a short cut to make future encounters with the same situations easier to navigate.
