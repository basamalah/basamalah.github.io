[tex]\purple{Answer}[/tex]

E

[tex]\red{Step-by-StepExplanation}[/tex]

To solve this problem, we can divide the total amount of grain by the amount of grain that can be loaded in one hour:

42000 tons ÷ 1500 tons/hour = 28 hours

Therefore, it will take 28 hours to load the ship with 42000 tons of grain, assuming a constant rate of loading.
