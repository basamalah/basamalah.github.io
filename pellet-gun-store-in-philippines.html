28. I came across this methodology and it states, 1:1 concentration of the supernatant and pellet to the solvents. So, if I have 1 mL of supernatant, I will use 1 mL of the solvent. How about for the pellet? If I have 1 mg of the pellet, how much uL of the solvent do I need to add to get 1:1 concentration?


Answer:

The methodology states to use a 1:1 concentration of the solvents with both the supernatant and pellet, so the same volume of solvent as the volume of pellet should be used.

If you have 1 mg of the pellet and need a 1:1 concentration, you would need to add 1 uL of the solvent.
