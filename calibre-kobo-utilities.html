Calibration (Health)

summary

the act of checking or adjusting (by delivering a standard) the accuracy of a measuring instrument

the thermometer needs calibration

Overview

Calibration in measurement technology and metrology is the comparison of the measurement values delivered by a device under test with those of a calibration standard of known accuracy. Such a standard could be another measuring device of known precision, a device that generates a volume to measure such as a voltage, tone of sound, or a physical artifact, such as a meter ruler.
