30. paano nakalilikha ng ulap? ano ang kahulugan ng mga sumusunod; cirrus clouds cumulus clouds stratus clouds nimbus clouds please po pasagot kailangan ko po talaga ng sagot ngayon. 60 points


Clouds are tiny water droplets that are very small and less dense that they float in the air. These droplets are formed when warm air rises and as it rises it expands and cools down. When this happens, water vapor in the air condenses into small dust particles or ice crystals in the air wherein tiny droplets forms around each dust particle. These particles clump together forming a cloud.

Cirrus clouds are clouds found higher in the atmosphere they appear separate, white and are feather-like.

Cumulus are separate, hill-shaped clouds,with flat, and often level, bases, it has a cotton like appearance and is fluffy. These clouds are found in the lower part of the atmosphere, 0-2km above sea level.

Stratus clouds are extensive, shallow cloud sheet, often yielding drizzle or light rain.

Nimbus clouds are what we see when it rain, it is extensive, very dark cloud sheet, usually yielding precipitation.
