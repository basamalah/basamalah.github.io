12. 11. A farmer planted 120 eggplants in his garden, out of this number 75% were successfully transplanted. How many of the eggplants were successfully transplanted? a. 30 eggplants b. 60 eggplants c. 90 eggplants d. 120 eggplants​


Answer:

C. 90 eggplants

Step-by-step explanation:

Percent means "per one hundred" or "out of one hundred". Thus, we can consider each ‘whole’ is divided up into 100 equal parts, each one of which is a single percent. Percent can be expressed in decimal or fraction. For instance, 1% is also [tex]\frac{1}{100}[/tex] or 0.001.

The question is asking us to solve for the percentage given the base and rate. Percent, base, and rate are interconnected in terms of computation. The percentage is the number we get when a number is multiplied by a percent ( P = BR). In solving for the percentage, it is important to convert the rate (%) into decimal. The rate is the ratio of the amount to the base and is written as percent (%). The rate can be computed using the formula R = [tex]\frac{P}{B}[/tex]. The base is the whole in the problem or it is the amount you are taking a percent of. It can be computed using the formula, B = [tex]\frac{P}{R}[/tex]. The question can be express as what is 75% of 120. 75% is the rate and 120 is the base. We convert 75% to [tex]\frac{75}{100}[/tex] or 0.75. Then, using the formula, P =BR, we can solve:

  P = BR

     = (120) (0.75)

     = 90

Thus, the answer is 90 eggplants.

Matthew planted 8,725 eggplants.

Find: Sum of all the eggplants.

Formula to find the sum of the eggplants is  [tex]S_n=\frac{n}{2}[2a_1+(n-1)d][/tex].

Substitute the given in the formula.

[tex]S_n\frac{n}{2}[2a_1+(n-1)d]\\S_{50}=\frac{50}{2}[2(3)+(50-1)7]\\S_{50}=25[6+(49)7]\\S_{50}=25(6+343)\\S_{50}=25(349)\\S_{50}=8,725[/tex]
