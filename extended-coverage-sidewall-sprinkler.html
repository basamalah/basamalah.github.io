21. A ROTATING LAWN SPRINKLER WATERS A CIRCULAR AREA THAT IS 45CM IN DIAMETER. FIND THE AREA OF THE LAWN WATERD BY THE SPRINKLER.​


The area of the lawn waters by sprinkler is 1,589.625 cm².

A circle is a flat shape that has one curved side and forms an angle of 360 degrees. The distance of each point on the outer side of the circle from the center of the circle is the same and is called the radius (r) or radius. The radius of a circle is equal to half the diameter. The definition of diameter is a line segment on a circle that passes through the center of the circle. The formula for the diameter of a circle is d = 2 × r.

In a circle, the circumference of a circle is the distance from a point on the circle in one turn to return to the original point. The quotient of the circumference by the diameter of a circle will get a number whose value will be close to 3.14159265358979… or shortened to 3.14 or you can also use the division of 22/7 which is called pi (π).

Circle Area Formula

The area of ​​a circle can be calculated by knowing the value of Pi (π) and the radius of the circle (r).

The formula for the area of ​​a circle:

            L = π × r × r

Information:

L is the symbol for the area of ​​the circle.

The value of π is 22/7 or 3.14.

Circumference of a circle:

K = 2 x π x r

K = π x d

Information:

K is Circumference of circle

π is 22/7 or 3.14

r is radius of circle
