17. ROYGBIV is the basic component of white light which color of light carries the highest energy​


Answer:

Violet

Explanation:

Violet Carries the Highest Energy Among ROYGBIV Colors

Violet carries the highest energy among ROYGBIV Colors because it has the highest frequency. The energy of a wave such as light is directly proportional to its frequency. In addition, violet has the shortest wavelength among the ROYGBIV colors. The wavelength, on the other hand, is inversely proportional to energy and frequency. Since it has the highest frequency and shortest wavelength, violet carries the highest energy among ROYGBIV colors.

More Information Why Violet Carries the Highest Energy Among ROYGBIV Colors

ROYGBIV are the colors of the visible spectrum or white light. They are Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, and Violet. Further, the arrangement of the letters ROYGBIV tell a lot about their characteristics. They are arranged not only based on their visibility but also on their wavelength. Red has the longest wavelength at 620 to 750 nm and it is on one end of the visible spectrum. Meanwhile, Violet has the shortest wavelength at 380 to 450 nm and is located on the other end. Since shorter wavelength means higher energy, the order ROYGBIV also tells about their energy characteristics.

Wavelengths of ROYGBIV Colors

Below are the wavelengths of ROYGBIV colors in the order of highest to lowest:

Red: 620-750nmOrange: 590-620nmYellow: 570-590nmGreen: 495-570nmBlue: 450nm-495nmViolet: 380nm-450nm
