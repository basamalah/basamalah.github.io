30. ben is eight years older than sarah, 10 years ago, ben is twice as old as sarah. how old is ben and sarah?


Present ages:

Sarah: x

Ben: x + 8


Ages 10 years ago:

Sarah: x - 10

Ben: x + 8 - 10 or x - 2


Equation:


x - 2 = 2 (x - 10)

x - 2 = 2x - 20

2x - x = 20 - 2

x = 18


Present ages:

Sarah: x = 18 years old

Ben: 18+8 = 26 years old


ANSWER: Ben is 26 years old while Sarah is 18 years old.
