11. Working together, it will take andrea,Bo and Celli 2 hours to finish a job. If only andrea and Bo work, it will take them 3 hours. If only Bo and celli will work, it will take 4 hours. how many hours will it take each of them to do the job alone?​


Answer:

It will take Andrea 4 hours, Bo 12 hours, and Celli 6 hours to do the job alone.

Step-by-step explanation:

To be able to answer this question, we must know the formula that Rate = Work / Time, wherein finishing a work equates to 1 in value.

[tex]R = \frac{W}{T}[/tex]

We can now assign variables for the rate of each person.

Andrea = x

Bo = y

Celli = z

Equation 1: It will take Andrea, Bo and Celli 2 hours to finish a job.

From the formula above, we can derive that W= RT

We can thus write our first equation as:

[tex]2x + 2y + 2z = 1[/tex]

[tex]x + y + z = \frac{1}{2}[/tex]

Equation 2: If only Andrea and Bo work, it will take them 3 hours.

[tex]3x + 3y = 1[/tex]

[tex]x + y = \frac{1}{3}[/tex]

Equation 3: If only Bo and Celli will work, it will take 4 hours.

[tex]4y + 4z = 1[/tex]

[tex]y + z = \frac{1}{4}[/tex]

We can now use the substitution method to substitute a variable in one of the equations with another.

In Equation 3, we can isolate z on one side:

[tex]y + z = \frac{1}{4}[/tex]

[tex]z = \frac{1}{4} - y[/tex]

We can now substitute z in Equation 1 with this expression:

[tex]x + y + z = \frac{1}{2}[/tex]

[tex]x + y + \frac{1}{4} - y = \frac{1}{2}[/tex]

[tex]x + y - y = \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{4}[/tex]

[tex]x = \frac{1}{4}[/tex]

Now that we have the value of x, we can now substitute it into Equation 2:

[tex]x + y = \frac{1}{3}[/tex]

[tex]\frac{1}{4} + y = \frac{1}{3}[/tex]

[tex]y = \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{4}[/tex]

[tex]y = \frac{1}{12}[/tex]

Now we can substitute y in Equation 3 with its known value:

[tex]z = \frac{1}{4} - \frac{1}{12}[/tex]

[tex]z = \frac{1}{6}[/tex]

Now that we know each of the person’s rates, we can now answer how many hours it will take each of them to do the work alone. Recalling our formula,  [tex]R = \frac{W}{T}[/tex] ,The time that each person needs to complete a job alone is simply the denominator of their rate. Therefore,

Andrea = [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex], or it will take her 4 hours to complete a job alone.

Bo = [tex]\frac{1}{12}[/tex], or it will take him 12 hours to complete a job alone.

Celli =  [tex]\frac{1}{6}[/tex], or it will take her 6 hours to complete a job alone.
