4. ____ is the play written by Jose Ma. Hernandez about a blacksmith from Pampanga who was the first Filipino who made a cannon. Select one: a. Panday Pira b. The World is an Apple c. Sa Pula, Sa Puti d. Sino ba kayo?


Panday Pira is the play written by Jose Ma. Hernandez about a blacksmith from Pampanga who was the first Filipino who made a cannon. The name "Panday Pira" literally translates as "Blacksmith Pira", panday being the Tagalog word for "blacksmith".

Panday Pira was known as Pandapira to the Spaniards.  In 1576, he died at the age of 88. His death was a great loss to the Spaniards who had to petition the King of Spain for a blacksmith to take his place.

Answer:

B. Amado V. Hernandez

Ang Luha ng Buwaya o, "Crocodile's Tear" sa pagsasalin, ay isang nobela na isinulat ni Palanca Awardee at nobelang Pilipino na si Amado V. Hernandez. Binubuo ito ng 53 na kabanata. Ang kwento ay tungkol sa mga mahihirap na magsasaka na nagkakaisa laban sa mga sakim na hangarin ng kilalang pamilya ng Grandes. Sa mga idyoma ng Filipino, ginamit ang "mga buwaya" upang sagisag sa mga taong masasama. Ang "buwaya" (buwaya) sa pamagat ay tumutukoy sa pamilyang Grandes, na sakim sa pera.
