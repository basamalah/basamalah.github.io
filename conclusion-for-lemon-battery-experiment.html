1. how to make a lemon battery 


1 polish the zinc and a penny ( a small copper coin) with sandpaper
2 squeeze the lemon without breaking its skin
3 cut two slits on the top of lemon 1 to 2 cm apart
4 insert the penny ( or copper coin ) into one slit and the zinc into the other
5 test to see if voltage is produced by touching leads of voltmeter to both the zinc and penny

10. draw a tree diagram associated with the experiment of selecting two batteries from among four in which one is defective​


Here's a tree diagram for the experiment of selecting two batteries from among four, in which one is defective:

       4/4

   /         \

D   3/3         1/3

   / \         / \

ND 2/2 1/2     2/2 1/2

Legend:

D  = Defective battery selected

ND = Non-defective battery selected

The first branch represents selecting the first battery. The second branch represents selecting the second battery. The numbers on the branches represent the number of batteries left to choose from. The probabilities of each outcome are shown next to the branches.
