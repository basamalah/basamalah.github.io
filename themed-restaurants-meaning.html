Mean  = [tex] \frac{ x_{1} + x_{2} + ... + x_{n} }{n} [/tex]

total salry of chefs = 4 * 68000 = 272000
total salary of waiters = 8 * 47000 = 376000

mean annual salary of all 12 employees = ((4*68000)+(8*47000))/12 = 54000

26. Meaning of suggestive selling techniques and upselling techniques used in a restaurant.​


Explanation:

Suggestive selling (also known as upselling) is the process of influencing guest purchases by enticing them with more expensive or higher margin items and add-ons. Basically, it's a sales strategy to see if your servers can convince your customers to add more to their order through suggestion.

Upselling is a technique used in restaurants to encourage a customer to spend more money. This can be done in a variety of ways. The goal may be to get a customer to switch from your house red to the higher-end cabernet. Or, it may be adding just a few extra toppings to increase sales.
