Answer:

Prepay and add is a type of payment method used in logistics. It involves the customer paying for goods or services before they are shipped or delivered. The customer pays the full amount upfront, and then the supplier adds any additional costs such as taxes, shipping fees, or other charges to the total cost.

Collect is another type of payment method used in logistics. It involves the supplier collecting payment from the customer after goods or services have been shipped or delivered. The customer pays for the goods or services after they have been received, and any additional costs such as taxes, shipping fees, or other charges are added to the total cost.
