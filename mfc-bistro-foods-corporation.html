Answer:

FAST FOOD - Ito ay mga kainan na nag-aalok ng mabilisang serbisyo at mura na pagkain, na karaniwang nakapaloob sa mga malalaking kadena ng kainan.

FAST CASUAL - Ito ay mga kainan na nag-aalok ng mas mataas na kalidad ng pagkain kaysa sa mga fast food chains, ngunit hindi kasing-mahal at hindi naman kasing-formal ng mga casual dining restaurants.

CASUAL DINING - Ito ay mga kainan na karaniwang mayroong upuan at mesang pambayan, nag-aalok ng mas malawak na pagpipilian ng pagkain at may mas mataas na halaga kaysa sa fast food at fast casual restaurants.

BUFFET AND SMORGASBORD - Ito ay mga kainan kung saan ang mga pagkain ay naka-display sa mga kadena ng lamesa, kung saan ang mga kumakain ay maaaring kumuha ng anumang gusto nila at kumain ng kung gaano karaming gusto nila sa isang fixed na halaga.

16. explain each 1 sentence only in your own understanding and opinion FAST FOOD FAST CASUAL CASUAL DINING FAMILY STYLE BRASSERIE AND BISTROBUFFET AND SMORGASBORD​


Answer:

FAST FOOD - A type of restaurant that focuses on serving quick and affordable meals, often with a limited menu and emphasis on convenience rather than quality.

FAST CASUAL - A step up from fast food, this type of restaurant offers higher-quality ingredients, a more varied menu, and a slightly more upscale atmosphere.

CASUAL DINING - A type of restaurant that offers a relaxed, family-friendly atmosphere and a moderately priced menu with a wide variety of options.

FAMILY STYLE - A type of restaurant where food is served in large portions meant to be shared among family and friends, often with a more home-cooked, comfort food style.

BRASSERIE AND BISTRO - Brasseries are traditional French restaurants that offer simple, hearty meals in a relaxed atmosphere, while bistros typically serve small, simple dishes with a focus on wine.

BUFFET AND SMORGASBORD - Restaurants that offer a wide variety of dishes for customers to serve themselves, usually for a fixed price, and often found in hotels or large events. Smorgasbords are similar but typically offer more traditional Scandinavian dishes.

What are the effects of the Corporate Food Regime?

The CORPORATE FOOD REGIME negatively affects society especially the very poor among us through their dependence on corporate manufactured food which lacks the needed nutrition. Farmers are also burdened by this through the passage of laws forcing them to buy patented seeds that are genetically modified by large corporations, failure to use these seeds would subject the poor farmers to face lawsuits by the company. And if you happen to grow these genetically identifiable seeds without permission, you can also be charged under the law, I mean, who really looks at seeds?  

The Corporate Food Regime is composed of institutions such as the World Trade Organization, the USDA, The World Bank, the Farm Bill, and others. These organizations have infiltrated and controlled many governmental bodies. Bribed politicians then create laws that force everyone to be dependent on corporate seeds and food.

This system which is spreading globally, really prevents small farmers from doing the natural thing which is just growing food without worrying about government intervention this, in turn, raises the food prices making it harder for the lower income people to buy natural food so they go for the corporate processed food alternative to feed themselves. Since these foods aren’t nourishing at all, they can expect to have health problems later in life. So, from there, we can say that the food industry creates customers for the health industry, but that is another topic.
