4. what is perimeter of the square neckerchief which is measures 8 inches a side​


Answer:

P= 32 in

Step-by-step explanation:

Here's the solution.

P = 4a

4 x 8 = 32

Answer:

Perimeter of the square neckerchief = 8 in + 8 in +8 in +8 in

                                                             = 32 inches

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the outline of this square neckerchief, I added 8 inches four times since there are four sides in a square. Each side in a square is equal to one another, since they form a right angle at each corner.
