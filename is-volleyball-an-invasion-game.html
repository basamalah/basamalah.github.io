14. Differentiate the target games and invasion games​


Answer:

Target games are those in which players send an object (such as a ball or dart) towards a target area. ... Opposed target games are ones, such as snooker or bowls, where the opposition's play affects the next move. In unopposed target games, such as golf and darts, the outcome is not affected by what the opposition does.

Invasion games are games in which the aim is to invade an opponent's territory and score a goal or point.
