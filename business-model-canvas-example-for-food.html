29. 1. If the area of the canvas is (x²-4x-12) square inches. a. What is the length of the canvas? (2 points) b. What is the width of the canvas? (2points)


To find the length and width of the canvas, we need to factor the expression x²-4x-12 to get the dimensions of the canvas.

Factoring this expression, we get (x-6)(x+2). This tells us that the dimensions of the canvas are x-6 inches by x+2 inches.

a. The length of the canvas is x-6 inches.

b. The width of the canvas is x+2 inches.
