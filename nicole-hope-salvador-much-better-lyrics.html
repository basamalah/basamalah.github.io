15. Nicole earns 30,000 php a month from selling baked goods. She saves 1/5 of it and gives 1/2 of what was left to her parents. How much money was left for Nicole to spend for herself?


Answer: P12,000.00

Step-by-step explanation:

First find the 1/5 of 30,000

30,000 x 1/5 = P6,000.00 (the money she saves)

Then we subtract 6,000 from 30,000 in order to obtain the money left

30,000 - 6,000 = P24,000.00 (money left)

Now, we'll find the half of the 24,000

24,000 / 2 = P12,000.00 (amount she gives to her parents)

Since she saves the 6,000 and gives her parents 12,000, we'll subtract them from 30,000 to find what will be the money left for Nicole to spend

30,000 - 12,000 - 6,000 = P12,000.00 (money left for herself)
