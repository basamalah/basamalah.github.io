How long will the ramp be? Round answer to two decimal places.

A right triangle will be formed: a^2 + b^2 = c^2

A = 3

B = 12

C = The hypotenuse which is the ramp

c^2 = 3^2 + 12^2

c^2 = 9 + 144

c^2 = 153

c = [tex]\sqrt[]{153}[/tex]

c = 12.37ft

Therefore 12.37ft is the length of the ramp.
