2. 500 soldiers in a fort had enough food for 30 days. After 6 days, some soldiers were sent to another fort and thus the food lasted for 32 more days. How many soldiers left the fort? ​


Answer:

125 soldiers

Step-by-step explanation:

500 soldiers × 30 days = 15,000 foods

30 days - 6 days = 24 days

500 soldiers × 24 days = 12,000 foods

12,000 foods ÷ 32 days = 375 soldiers

500 soldiers - 375 soldiers = 125 soldiers

125 soldiers left the fort.

27. In ancient times, it was very important to build castles with ______________ forts.


The answer is impregnable. This word is an adjective that means something is so well made that it cannot be entered or taken by force. This is often used to describe forts, which are important defensive structures during the Middle Ages in Europe. If a fort is described as “impregnable,” it can resist all attacks.

Origin of the Word

The English word “impregnable” came from two different languages:

the Old French word “imprenable" the Latin prehendere, which is the origin of the French word  

The French “imprenable" means something is impossible to capture or to take.

Example of Sentences The case of the prosecution must be as impregnable as Fort Knox* for the accusations to stick.  The Romans were famous for building impregnable fortresses during their rule. He had constructed an impregnable argument before he even told her the truth. The Lannisters’ castle with its high walls and well-trained soldiers was virtually impregnable until it was conquered by a barbarian army.

*Fort Knox is an idiomatic expression for a place that is so well-protected that it’s impossible to enter.
