9. A real estate agent works on a commission of 5.75% for the sale of a home. If the agent sold two homes one for 449,000 and another for 550,000. What was the total amount of commission?


Answer:

PHP 52,543.75

Step-by-step explanation:

The total amount of commission for the sale of the two homes is PHP 52,543.75. This is calculated by adding the commissions for the two homes, which are PHP 25,902.50 for the first home (449,000 x 5.75% = 25,902.50) and PHP 26,641.25 for the second home (550,000 x 5.75% = 26,641.25). The total commission is 25,902.50 + 26,641.25 = 52,543.75.

11. 1. What is the essence of consent in the contract of sale? 2. What will happen to the contract of sale when the price is inadequate? Can there be a sale without a price? 3. Enumerate and define the kinds of contract of sale.4. Distinguish Contract of Sale and Contract to Sell. 5. Distinguish Contract to sell and conditional deed of sale.​


Answer:

1. The essence of consent in the contract of sale refers to the mutual agreement between the buyer and seller on the essential terms of the sale, including the subject matter, price, and conditions of delivery and payment.

2. If the price is inadequate, the contract of sale may still be valid, but the buyer may be able to seek a price reduction or cancel the contract. There cannot be a sale without a price, as the price is a necessary element of the agreement.

3.The kinds of contract of sale include:

Absolute sale: a sale where ownership of the goods is transferred immediately to the buyer.

Conditional sale: a sale where ownership of the goods is transferred to the buyer only upon the occurrence of a specified condition.

Sale on approval: a sale where the buyer has the right to inspect the goods and return them within a specified period if they are not satisfactory.

Sale or return: a sale where the buyer has the right to return the goods and receive a refund if they are not sold within a specified period.

Installment sale: a sale where payment is made in installments over a specified period.

4. The main difference between a contract of sale and a contract to sell is that in a contract of sale, ownership of the goods is transferred to the buyer immediately upon the agreement, while in a contract to sell, ownership remains with the seller until certain conditions are met, such as full payment by the buyer.

5. A contract to sell and a conditional deed of sale are similar in that ownership of the goods remains with the seller until certain conditions are met. The difference is that a contract to sell is an agreement to transfer ownership in the future, while a conditional deed of sale is a present transfer of ownership that is subject to certain conditions, such as payment or delivery.

22. 3. A real estate agent receive 3% commission on the first P 2,500,000 of a sale and 5% of the rest. How much commission is received on the sale of P 7,500,000 property?help Po​


Answer:

The commission received on the sale of P 7,500,000 property is P 337,500.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the commission received on the sale of a P 7,500,000 property, we need to first find out how much of the sale falls under the 3% commission rate, and how much falls under the 5% commission rate.

The first P 2,500,000 of the sale falls under the 3% commission rate. So, 3% of P 2,500,000 is P 2,500,000 x 0.03 = P 75,000.

The remaining P 5,000,000 of the sale falls under the 5% commission rate. So, 5% of P 5,000,000 is P 5,000,000 x 0.05 = P 250,000.

Now, we add the commission earned from the 3% rate and the 5% rate to find the total commission:

P 75,000 + P 250,000 = P 325,000

Therefore, the real estate agent receives P 325,000 as commission on the sale of P 7,500,000 property.
