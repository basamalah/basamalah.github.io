1. Lis.deped.gov.ph enrollment masterlist 2016


Ito ay isang portal sa deped website kung saan ay ini-enroll ang bawat mag-aaral na kasalukuyang mag-aaral sa bawat grade level sa bawat paaralan. Bawat guro ay may kanya kanyang official username at password para maka-access sa website https://lis.deped.gov.ph at mai-enroll sa facility na ito ang mga mag-aaral.

Kailangan na i-click ang masterlist upang ma-download ang mga talaan ng mga mag-aaral na na-i-enroll na LIS Facility. Sa pamamagitan ng masterlist din dapat mag-click upang ma-postang iba pang detalye at inpormasyon tungkol sa bawat mag-aaral gaya ng araw ng kapanganakan, mga magulang, nutritional status, at promotion mula sa mababang grae level hanggang sa kasalukuyang level. 

2. Masterlist of private schools accredited by deped


The Department of Education (DedEd) has their own system, Basic Education Information System, where they maintain a database of education statistics, profile of public and private schools, learning centers and other education service providers. You can access this system online, and you can search for the masterlist of all privates schools accredited by the DepEd. 

There's a total of 73,972 public and private schools nationwide, Elementary and Secondary. You can also view the school head/principal, contact number, address and school type.

10. Mix solutions to prepare a sulfate salt with ph 7.Options to choose from and their unmixed pH:H2CO3= pH 4Ca(OH)2= pH 12H2SO4= pH 2NaOH= pH 12HCl= pH 2NH3= pH 10


SODIUM SULFATE with ph 7.

   Salts are formed when an acid reacts with a base during a neutralization reaction.

When a strong acid and a weak base reacts, it leads to the formation of acidic salt.When a strong base and weak acid reacts, it leads to the formation of basic salt.When a strong acid and strong base or weak acid and weak base reacts, it leads to the formation of neutral salts.    

Sodium sulfate is a neutral salt, it creates aqueous solutions with a pH of 7. The neutrality of such solutions is due to the fact that Na2SO4 is produced from a strong acid (sulfuric acid) and a strong base (sodium hydroxide).

Hence, a neutral salt and water is formed as products.

  Acidic salts are those which are formed by combination of strong acid and a weak base and have acidic pH of less than 7.

Basic salts are those which are formed by combination of weak acid and a strong base and have basic pH of more than 7.

Neutral salts are those which are formed by combination of weak acid and a weak base or by combination of strong acid and a strong base and have neutral pH equal to 7.

27. Reasons why lis deped accounts are disabled


There are lots of reasons why a website has been disabled. 

Here are a few things to checked:

1. That the subscription was active and that the hosting and/or domain didn’t need renewing

2. That files existed on the server

3. That the DNS or nameservers weren’t pointing elsewhere.

4. The subscription of the domain and hosting has not yet paid or remain unpaid.

5 Too many users of the website.

6 The administrator set the website as disabled.

For DepEd LIS system, they always update their system from time to time for the security of all data in the system. For more reasons you may contact your LIS coordinator because they all the reasons why. They were notified by the Division LIS coordinator.
