23. How to fill up bdo affidavit of loss form?


Answer:

STEPS IN FILLING-UP BDO AFFIDAVIT OF LOSS FORM

BDO have a ready made form for Affiavit of Loss available in their website. Before we proceed in the steps in filling-up the BDO form let's define first what is an affidavit of loss.

Affidavit of Loss

It is a legal document that declares physical loss of an item through theft or physical destruction by outside means. the form will contain details regarding the loss, the owner's name and any information pertaining to the security, such as serial number or date of issue.

Let us now proceed to the steps in filling up BDO form:

In the upper part of the form tick the box wether what item was lost. (e.g. Passbook, ATM Card, Certificate, Manager's Check or others that is needed to be specified)Put your name on the Afficant/s blankNext, indicate your civil status and address of residence.Then, you will see a statement such as these:

After being duly sworn to in accordance with the law, do hereby depose and state THAT:

There will be a couple of questions that needs to be answered after those statement fill in those blanks indicating the following per item:

For no(s):

Bank branch where the account was openedType of Account that was lost, Include the last balance which you remember.Nothing to be done in this item, just read what was indicated.Reason how or why the specified subject was lost..Nothing to be done in this item, just read what was indicated.Nothing to be done in this item, just read what was indicated.Nothing to be done in this item, just read what was indicated.For this item tick the box of the applicable action to be made into the account for the lost of the subject.Nothing to be done in this item, just read what was indicated.Nothing to be done in this item, just read what was indicated.Nothing to be done in this item, just read what was indicated.

in this statement after all items have been filled in:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I / we have set my/our hands at ____________________________ Philippines on this ______day of _____________, 20____.

Indicate your name, and the date of filling up the form.

Affix one specimen of your signature with printed name on one of the boxes.Next is the signature and the date when the form was filed in the presence of your witnesses. (which is usually the people in the bank) so just leave it blank.

For these items:

Doc. No. Page No. Book No. Series of :

These are for the notarization of the document.

For Notarization provide multiple copies that are usually required by the notary public. Normally they require one copy to be left in their office.

Viola, you now have your completely filled up BDO affidavit of Loss form

26. Affidavit of financial support stating monthly support provided philippines


And affidavit of financial support is a legal document in the Philippines where someone, generally the father of an illegitimate child will need to execute to document how much financial support he would be giving to the child. In the affidavit, the document should state the amount of the monthly support and how it would be provided (ATM, cash, check).

The law on violence against women and their children RA 9262 considers withholding of allowances or support to a child as a form of economic abuse. The Philippines is rather strict on this one. Hence, a father who is not able to provide for their children, when they have the means to do so, could be liable under the law.
