29. A rectangular-sized basketball court measures 94 feet by 50 feet. Find the area of the basketball court.


[tex]\pink{Answer} ⤵[/tex]

Length = 94 feet

Width = 50 feet

Area = Length × Width

= 94 feet × 50 feet

= 4700 feet squared

Perimeter = 2(Length + Width)

= 2(94 feet + 50 feet)

= 2(144 feet)

= 288 feet
