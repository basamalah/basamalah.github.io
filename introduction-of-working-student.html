As the demands of high school education continue to increase, many students find it challenging to complete their school work on time. Body:

Some of the common problems encountered by SR. Furthermore, many students face additional challenges outside of school, such as part-time jobs, extracurricular activities, and family obligations, which can make it difficult for them to find the time and energy to complete their school work. In some cases, students may also struggle with personal challenges, such as mental health issues or learning disabilities, which can impact their ability to complete their assignments on time.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, the problems encountered by SR. HIGH SCHOOL students at VMCACFI in completing their school work on time are diverse and multifaceted. By understanding the specific challenges faced by these students, educators and school administrators can develop targeted interventions and support systems to help them overcome these obstacles and improve their time management skills. This, in turn, can lead to better academic performance and increased success in school.
