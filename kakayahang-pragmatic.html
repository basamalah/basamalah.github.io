1. Kakayahang pragmatic


Ang kakayahang pragmatiko ay tumutukoy sa isang kakayahang sosyo-linggwistika na ginagamit ng mga tao sa araw-araw. Kabilang na rito ang pagkakaroon ng kakayanang makaintindi ng sinasabi o paggalaw ng tao at kung ito ay angkop sa nangyayaring sitwasyon.

 

Kasama rito ang pagtukoy sa emosyon o ibig sabihin ng tinuran o sinabi ng isang tao. Mahalaga ang pagkakaroon ng kakayahang pragmatiko dahil nakakatulong ito upang maipahayag ang ating nararamdaman at naiisip ng mas madali.

4. Pragmatics is the formation of words


The answer is False--Pragmatics is NOT the formation of words. Pragmatics is the study of meaning through context. It takes into account the intended meaning of the language rather than the literal meaning. The linguistic study on the formation of words is called Morphology. It breaks down the parts of a word or the morphemes and analyzes it.

26. Meaning of Pragmatic competence?


In linguistics, pragmatic competence is the ability to use language effectively in a contextually appropriate fashion. Pragmatic competence is a fundamental aspect of a more general communicative competence.
