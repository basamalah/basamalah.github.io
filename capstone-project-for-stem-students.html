5. CAPSTONE PROJECT Theme: ENVIRONMENT Title: STUDENT’S WILL GIVE WAY TO COMMUNITY SERVICE THROUGH REMINDERS. •INTRODUCTION •BACKGROUND •ABSTRACT •OBJECTIVES •EXECUTION sana po matulong po ninyo ko dito ty​


Answer:

Introduction:

The environment is an essential aspect of our lives, and its protection is crucial for our survival. However, with the fast-paced and busy lives that many people lead today, it can be easy to forget to take care of our surroundings. This project aims to encourage students to engage in community service and remind them of their responsibility to the environment.

Background:

Many students have the desire to participate in community service, but they often lack the motivation or reminders to do so. The lack of opportunities and information about community service activities in their local areas can also hinder their participation. This project aims to bridge this gap and provide students with regular reminders about community service opportunities.

Abstract:

The project will develop a mobile application that will remind students of their responsibility to the environment and encourage them to participate in community service activities. The application will send reminders to students about upcoming community service events and provide them with information about the events, such as the location, time, and type of activity. Students can also use the application to register for events, track their participation, and receive feedback on their performance.

Objectives:

The objectives of the project are as follows:

1. Develop a mobile application that will remind students about their responsibility to the environment and encourage them to participate in community service activities.

2. Provide information about community service events to students, including location, time, and type of activity.

3. Allow students to register for community service events, track their participation, and receive feedback on their performance.

4. Increase student participation in community service activities and promote a sense of responsibility towards the environment.

Execution:

The project will involve the following steps:

1. Conduct research on existing mobile applications for community service and environmental awareness.

2. Develop a design for the mobile application and user interface.

3. Develop the mobile application using appropriate software development tools.

4. Test the mobile application for functionality, usability, and performance.

5. Launch the mobile application and promote it among the target audience.

6. Monitor usage and feedback on the mobile application and make necessary improvements.

In conclusion, this project aims to encourage students to engage in community service and remind them of their responsibility to the environment. By providing regular reminders and information about community service opportunities, the project hopes to increase student participation in community service activities and promote a sense of responsibility towards the environment.

8. paano gumawa ng didecation sa capstone project?​


Answer:

Upang gumawa ng dedication sa isang capstone project, maaari mong gawin ang mga sumusunod:

1. Unawain kung ano ang layunin ng dedication sa iyong capstone project. Ang dedication ay nagpapakita ng pagpapahalaga at pasasalamat sa mga taong nagbigay ng suporta sa iyo habang ginagawa mo ang iyong proyekto.

2. Pumili ng mga taong gusto mong bigyan ng pagpapahalaga sa iyong dedication. Maaari itong maging mga magulang, guro, kaibigan, o kahit na isang mentor.

3. Magsulat ng mga pangungusap na nagpapakita ng pasasalamat sa kanila at naglalarawan ng kanilang papel sa iyong proyekto.

4. Ipakita ang iyong dedication sa tamang lugar sa iyong capstone project. Halimbawa, maaari itong ilagay sa unang pahina ng iyong papel o sa hulihan ng iyong presentasyon.

5. Siguraduhin na ang iyong dedication ay maayos na inayos at naayos at magbigay ng tamang pormat.
