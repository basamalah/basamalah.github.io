13. ayah can paint a house in 3 hours Princess can do it in 4 hours. How long will it take the two working together?​


Answer:

1.71 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

It's a work problem. You can set up this way.  

Ayah's rate+Princess' rate=total rate where rate=1 job over hours

         1/3     +       1/4           =          1/x

Find "x" and start the problem by multiplying both sides  by the LCD (12x).

sooo...

Let the rate that Ayah can paint the room = 1/3.

Let the rate that Princess can paint the room = 1/4.

Let X equal the unknown, which is how long will it take if we work together

 

The equation is therefore:

         1/3*X +1/4*X = 1

         Use common denomination of 3*4=12

 

(4X + 3X)  =  1  This implies that 7X/12 = 1. Solving for X you get X=12/7 = 1.71 hours is the answer.
