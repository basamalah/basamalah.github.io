1. what is the use of tsaang gubat​


Answer:

Carmona retusa (Vahl) Masamune, also known as Tsaang Gubat, is an affordable, herbal medicine to help relieve abdominal pain and diarrhea in adults. It contains alpha-amyrin, beta-amyrin, and baurenol which have shown analgesic activity, anti-diarrheal and anti-spasmodic activity.

Explanation:

Answer:

Carmona retusa (Vahl) Masamune, also known as Tsaang Gubat, is an affordable, herbal medicine to help relieve abdominal pain and diarrhea in adults. It contains alpha-amyrin, beta-amyrin, and baurenol which have shown analgesic activity, anti-diarrheal and anti-spasmodic activitiy.
