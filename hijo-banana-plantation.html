6. 3. It takes 8 days for 35 laborers to harvest bananas on a plantation. How long will 20 laborers take to harvest bananas on the same plantation?​


Answer:

35x8=20x

280=20x

x=14 days

Answer:

14 days

Step-by-step explanation:

This is an inverse proportion problem.

8 : 35 = x : 20

Since this is an inverse proportion, we have to divide the number of laborers, inverse the quotient fraction, then multiply it to the days to get x.

20/35 = 4/7

The inverse of this fraction is 7/4

7/4 × 8 days = 14 days
