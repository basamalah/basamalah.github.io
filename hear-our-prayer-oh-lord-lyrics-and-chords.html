7. why is "Our Father" called the Lord's prayer?​


Because this prayer is dedicated to our Lord who creates everything.

because Lord’s Prayer, also called Our Father, Latin Oratio Dominica or Pater Noster, Christian prayer that, according to tradition, was taught by Jesus to his disciples. It appears in two forms in the New Testament: the shorter version in the Gospel According to Luke 11:2–4 and the longer version, part of the Sermon on the Mount, in the Gospel According to Matthew 6:9–13. In both contexts it is offered as a model of how to pray.
