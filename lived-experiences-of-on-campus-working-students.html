Lived Experiences of On-Campus Working Students (Frigillano et al., 2015) Title and author/s : Kind Strength 4. Weakness 5. Importance :​


Title and author/s: "Lived Experiences of On-Campus Working Students" by Frigillano et al., 2015.

Kind: This is a descriptive study that uses a mixed method approach (qualitative and quantitative) to explore the lived experiences and struggles of on-campus working students.

Strength: The study provides insights into the experiences and struggles of on-campus working students, which can help in developing interventions and support programs to improve their welfare. The use of a mixed method approach provides a more comprehensive understanding of the phenomenon being studied.

Weakness: The sample size is relatively small (22 participants) and the study was conducted in a specific campus (ISAT U-Miagao Campus), which may limit the generalizability of the findings.

Importance: The study is important as it sheds light on the experiences and struggles of on-campus working students, which is a growing phenomenon in many universities. The findings can inform the development of policies and programs that can address the needs of these students and improve their academic performance and overall well-being.
