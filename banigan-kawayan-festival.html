1. tungkol saan ang banigan festival


The Kawayan-Banigan Festival Parade is a yearly dance parade of pageantry and colorful mats, winding through the town key streets, reliving the two main source of livelihood of Bsayanon bamboos and mat weaving- through their music, dance and drama. It is celebrated every September 28 &29.

Ang kawayan festival ay isang Uri Ng bagdiriwang sa Samar ginaganap Ito Kada Septyembre 29 ipinagdiriwang nila Ito kada-taon Bilang pagpapakilala sa kanilang mga produkto na gawa sa kawayan tulad Ng basket at iba Pa.
