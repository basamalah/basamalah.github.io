25. mother made of pancake with a radius of 11.5 cm what is the circumference of the pancake​


Answer:

Given: Radius = 11.5 cm

Formula: Circumference = 2πr

Solution:

C = 2πr

C = 2(3.14)(11.5)

C = (6.28) (11.5)

C = 72.22 cm

The circumference of the pancake is 72.22 cm.
