12. Sonia’s room is shaped a rectangle. The length of her room is 9 feet and the width is 9.5 feet. What is the perimeter of sonia’s room? What is the solution?


Answer:

To find the perimeter of Sonia's room, we need to add up the length of all four sides of the rectangle.

The formula for the perimeter of a rectangle is:

Perimeter = 2 × (length + width)

Substituting the given values, we get:

Perimeter = 2 × (9 + 9.5)

Perimeter = 2 × 18.5

Perimeter = 37 feet

Therefore, the perimeter of Sonia's room is 37 feet

16. Eight years ago the sum of Sonia's age and Cita's age is greater than 26. Sonia is 4 years older than Cita.What is Cita's possible age? Did help po pls ☹️


Answer:

20

Step-by-step explanation:

Let Cita's age be x.

Then, Sonia's age would be x + 4.

We know that eight years ago, the sum of their ages was greater than 26. This means that:

(x - 8) + (x + 4 - 8) > 26

Simplifying this expression, we get:

2x - 12 > 26

Adding 12 to both sides, we get:

2x > 38

Dividing both sides by 2, we get:

x > 19

Since we are looking for Cita's age, we know that she must be at least 20 years old, since x cannot be a decimal or fraction.

Therefore, Cita's possible age is any whole number greater than or equal to 20

23. sonia and her sister may saved money. their total savings was 272 pesos. if sonia saved 68 pesos. how many times greater was may's savings than sonia?


Answer:

May's savings is 3 times that of Sonia's savings.

Step-by-step explanation:

let x be their, Sonia and May's, total savings

let y be Sonia's savings

Let z be May's savings

x = 272 pesos

y = 68 pesos

z = x - y

z = 272 - 68

z = 204

204/68 = 3/1 = 3:1
