3. In preparation for the implementation f the curriculum, all teachers are required to write a lesson plan to guide teaching. However, DepEd issued an order that exempts teachers with two or more years of teaching experience to write a detailed lesson plan .This orders refers to


Answer:

DepEd Order No. 42, s. 2016, also known as the Policy Guidelines on Daily Lesson Preparation for the K to 12 Basic Education Program. The order exempts teachers with at least two years of teaching experience from preparing a detailed lesson plan, provided that they submit a weekly or monthly lesson plan that outlines the topics to be covered and the activities to be undertaken. The exemption is intended to give experienced teachers more flexibility and autonomy in planning their lessons, while ensuring that they still meet the learning objectives and standards set by the curriculum.

8. The lesson plan is a pedagogical tool for planning the teaching activity that materializes?a. The teaching planb. The disciplinary contentc. The pedagogical political projectd. The course plan​


Answer:

The lesson plan materializes the disciplinary content, as it is characterized as a pedagogical instrument for planning the teaching activity.

Therefore, the correct answer is b. The disciplinary content.

The lesson plan and the organization of disciplinary content:

The lesson plan includes not only the step by step of the classes to be taught in a given period of a week or a month, depending on the stage of education, as in early childhood education, where this planning can be daily but the delimitation of the content to be experienced in the classroom.

The themes worked are usually linked to skills and abilities in development, such as the exhibition of board games, which allow, in addition to a playful teaching, the improvement of student logical reasoning, especially within the exact areas, proving to be excellent methodologies of teaching in the most elementary grades.

In the absence of a lesson plan, the teacher can feel lost, consequently, the lessons lose their meaning.

21. why 5es lesson planning is more ideal to use when teaching science​


Answer:

The 5E model of science instruction is an inquiry-based approach that differs from traditional methods of science instruction.  What are those differences?  The main one is that with the 5E model the student leads the learning and the teacher acts as a guide.

￼￼

What is 5E Model for Science Instruction?

The 5E Model of Instruction is an inquiry-based model. It is student-led, with the teacher acting mainly as the facilitator. Through open-ended questions, real-life experiences, guided investigations, hands-on projects and research, students gain a deep understanding of the scientific topics that are covered in the unit. Each stage of the model serves as a foundation to the next, creating a coherent model that frames lessons, activities, and units.
