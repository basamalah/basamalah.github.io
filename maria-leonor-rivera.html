LEONOR RIVERA

    Leonor Rivera is Rizal's true love. Despite the several disasters in his brief life, he grieved her wedding and subsequent passing more than the 11 years he spent courting her.    

INFIDELITY OF LEONOR

    Since Rizal was then a well-known filibustero, Leonor's mother opposed her daughter's romance with him, hence the two did not become together. All letters written to her beloved were kept a secret from Leonor. Leonor reluctantly agreed to let her marry Henry Kipping, the man her mother had chosen, assuming that Rizal had long forgotten about her.
