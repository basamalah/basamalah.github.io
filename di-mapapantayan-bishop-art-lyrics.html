3. Mapapantayan Kasing kahulugan


Answer: Maaabot o makakapareho ng antas/lebel

Mapapantayan

Ang kasingkahulugan ng salitang ito ay maaring matutumbasan, matatapatan, magagaya, o kaya naman ay maaabot.

Nangangahulugan ito na maabot ang lebel at maging parehas ito sa isang bagay, katangian, tao, at o kahit anong gusto nitong mapantayan. Halimba ng mga bagay na pwedeng pantayan ay tangkad, taas, grado, ranggo, iskor, bilang, at bigat.

13. bishop function in chess


Answer:

The bishop chess piece moves in any direction diagonally. Chess rules state that there is no limit to the number of squares a bishop can travel on the chessboard, as long as there is not another piece obstructing its path. Bishops capture opposing pieces by landing on the square occupied by an enemy piece.
