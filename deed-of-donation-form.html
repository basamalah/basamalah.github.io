209,715,200

Step-by-step explanation:

First,find an:

solution:

n=25

a1=1

r=2

an=a1r^n-1

an=1(2)^25-1

an=1(2)^24

an=16,777,216

Find the sun using the formula Sn=n/2(a1+an)

Sn=25/2(1+16,777,216)

Sn=25/2(16,777,217)

Sn=209,715,200

Thus,the billionaire donated 209,715,200 centavos.
