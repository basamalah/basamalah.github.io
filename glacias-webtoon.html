2. What is the pov of true beauty in webtoon?


[tex]\huge{\boxed{\mathfrak{Answer}}}[/tex]

In the Webtoon "True Beauty," the point of view on true beauty is that it comes from within a person, rather than from their physical appearance. The story follows the life of Lim Ju-kyung, a high school student who is insecure about her looks and struggles with low self-esteem. She becomes an expert at makeup and transforms her appearance, leading others to believe that she is naturally beautiful.

However, throughout the story, it is revealed that true beauty comes from accepting and loving oneself, regardless of external appearances. The characters in the story, including Lim Ju-kyung, learn that self-confidence and self-acceptance are more important than physical beauty.

The Webtoon also portrays the dangers of society's unrealistic beauty standards and the negative impact they can have on individuals' self-worth and mental health. The message of the Webtoon is that true beauty is not based on external appearance or societal standards, but rather on self-love, acceptance, and confidence.
