2. Tuition fee is increased by 25% since five years. If the tuition fee then was ₱5 000, how much is the tuition fee now?


Answer: ₱6,250


Step-by-step explanation:

To get the answer,

First, you multiply it by 0.25.

It becomes:

5,000 × 0.25 = 1,250.

But, since the tuition is increased by 25%, we add 5000 to 1250.

5000 + 1250 = 6250.

Therefore, tuition today costs ₱6,250.

9. Raven's current schoolyear tuition fee is P114,400.00. This is due to the4% tuition fee increase implemented by the school. How much was thetuition fee of Raven last school year?​


Answer:

P109,824 - tuition fee of Raven last school year.

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution:

*Find the tuition fee increase:

P114,400 * 4% (0.04) = P4,576

*Subtract the current school year tuition fee and the tuition fee increase

P114,400 - P4,576 = P109,824

Final Answer: P109,824 was the tuition fee of Raven last school year.
