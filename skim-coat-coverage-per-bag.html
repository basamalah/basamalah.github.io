12. Newlywed couple Anna and Kristoff decided to have a semicircular window in their house with a radius of 12 inches. They wanted the window to be laminated with a sunblock coating that costs P50.00 per square inch to apply. What is the total cost of coating the window?


Answer:

Total cost = Php 11309.73

Solution:

In order to figure out the total cost of the coating that will be used, we first need to determine the are of the semicircular windows where the coating will be installed. The area of the semi-circular window is given by  (https://brainly.ph/question/1631280).

A = (π * r²)/2.  

Hence,

The area of the semicircular window can be solved as follows:

A = (π * 12²)/2 ; (r = 12 in, π = 3.1416)

A= 226.195 in²

To know the total cost of coating let us multiply the cost per square inch to the area of the semi-circular window.

Total cost = (Php 50 / in²) x 226.195 in²

Total cost = Php 11309.73

27. Find the cost per pound of chocolate coated corn flakes made from 40 pounds of chocolate which costs php200,00 per pound and 120 pounds of cornflakes which costs php88,00 per pound ​


Answer:

The cost per pound of chocolate coated corn flakes is Php116.00

Step-by-step explanation:

Cost of chocolate = 40 pounds x Php200.00/pound = Php8,000.00

Cost of cornflakes = 120 pounds x Php88.00/pound = Php10,560.00

Total cost of chocolate coated corn flakes = Cost of chocolate + Cost of cornflakes

= Php8,000.00 + Php10,560.00

= Php18,560.00

divide the total cost by the total weight of the chocolate coated corn flakes:

Total weight of chocolate coated corn flakes = 40 pounds + 120 pounds = 160 pounds

Cost per pound = Total cost of chocolate coated corn flakes / Total weight of chocolate coated corn flakes

= Php18,560.00 / 160 pounds

= Php116.00/pound

Therefore, the cost per pound of chocolate coated corn flakes is Php116.00.
