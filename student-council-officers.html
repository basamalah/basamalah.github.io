Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Identify the given values

Given:

yo = 350

t = 4 years

r = 3% = 3/100 = 0.03

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

a. exponential function

b. Total total number of members of student council after 4 years

Step 3: Find the exponential function and solve for the total number of members

of student council after 4 years

Equation:

The mathematical model is given by:

[tex]A(t) = P{(1 + r)}^{t} [/tex]

where: A(t) - future number of members based on

number of years t

P - present number of members

r - rate of increase

t - total number of years

a. Find the exponential model

Substitute the value of P and r in the formula,

[tex]A(t) = 350{(1 + 0.03)}^{t} [/tex]

[tex]A(t) = 350{(1.03)}^{t} [/tex]

b. Solve for the total number of members of student council after 4 years

Substitute the value of P, r and t in the formula,

[tex]A(t) = 350{(1 + 0.03)}^{4} [/tex]

[tex]A(t) = 350{(1.03)}^{4} [/tex]

[tex]A(t) = 350{(1.12550881)}[/tex]

[tex]A(t) = 393.93[/tex]

Rounding off,

[tex]A(t) = 394[/tex]

19. 40 students made the student council. 5/8 of the students were girls. How many boys made student council ?


The answer is: 15 boys.

Let's compute for this question. The question says:

"40 students made the student council. 5/8 of the students were girls. How many boys made the council?"

Ok, so if the girls comprises 5/8 of the council, what is the fraction part of boys. To find out, subtract 5/8 from 8/8 (since the denominator is 8)

8/8 - 5/8 = 3/8

It means that 3/8 of the council are boys. Let's now do the operations.

40 / 8 = 5

5 x 3 = 15

So, the answer will be 15 boys.
