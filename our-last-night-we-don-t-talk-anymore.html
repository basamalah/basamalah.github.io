21. why dont we consider moulds and mushrooms plants anymore?​


Answer:

Molds are neither plants nor animals; they are part of the kingdom Fungi. Molds can multiply by producing microscopic spores similar to the seeds produced by plants.

Mushrooms aren't plants because they don't make their own food (plants use photosynthesis to make food). The underground part of the fungus uses enzymes to "digest" other substances that it can use as food.
