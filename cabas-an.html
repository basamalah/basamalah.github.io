3. What is the function of the following structures? 1. Atria2.Ventricles3.Superior vena cava4.Inferior vena caba5.Valves​


⊱┈──────────────────────┈⊰

[tex]\sf\blue{{\: ANSWER :}}[/tex]

1. The two atria are thin-walled chambers that receive blood from the veins.

2. Ventricle, muscular chamber that pumps blood out of the heart into the circulatory system.

3. The superior vena cava is a vital structure in the human circulatory system that helps drain large amounts of deoxygenated blood from the head, eyes, neck, and upper limbs into the upper left chamber (atrium) of the heart.

4. The inferior vena cava is ultimately responsible the transport of almost all venous blood (deoxygenated) from the abdomen and lower extremities back to the right side of the heart for oxygenation.

5. The valves keep blood moving through the heart in right direction.

13. maria violita bought 10 bottles of alcohol from caba donga store she paid an amount of Php546. 65 what is the cost of each bottle of alcohol? what are given​


Answer:

What is asked? The question is asking for the cost of each bottle of alcohol.

What are given? The information given is that Maria Violita bought 10 bottles of alcohol from Caba Donga store and paid a total of Php546.65

Formula: The formula for determining the cost of each individual item when the total cost and number of items are known is:

cost of each item = total cost / number of items

Solution/s?​ To solve for the cost of each bottle of alcohol, you can plug in the given values of total cost and number of items into the formula:

cost of each bottle = Php546.65 / 10 bottles = Php54.665 per bottle.

So, the cost of each bottle of alcohol is Php54.665.
