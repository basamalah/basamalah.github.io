18. CHALLENGE 2. Read the following materials and express your opinions about them. Write your answers on the lines provided. 1.Student should speak English at all times. 2.Get snow white skin in two week!​


Answer:

1. I disagree that Filipino students should speak English at all times. While English is an important language for communication and global interaction, it should not be imposed as the only language of instruction or communication. Students should be given the opportunity to express themselves in their native language and should not be made to feel inferior for not being fluent in English. It is also important to recognize and celebrate the cultural and linguistic diversity of the Philippines.

2. The idea of "getting snow white skin in two weeks" is problematic and reinforces harmful beauty standards that prioritize lighter skin tones. Such advertisements can lead to body shaming and low self-esteem among individuals who do not have fair skin. It is important to promote diversity and embrace all skin colors and types, rather than perpetuating the idea that lighter skin is superior. Additionally, the safety and effectiveness of such products should be evaluated before they are promoted to the public.
