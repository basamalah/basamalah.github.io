10. The theme is “there is joy waiting” will serve as the central focus of the art and write an explanation consist of 3-5 sentences


Answer:

The theme "there is joy waiting" suggests that happiness can be found, even in difficult or challenging situations. It encourages us to look for the good, to be hopeful and optimistic, and to trust that better things are on the horizon. This theme can be interpreted in many different ways in art and writing, from depicting scenes of joyful moments or highlighting the beauty in everyday life, to exploring the inner workings of the human mind and the ways in which we can find joy within ourselves. Ultimately, the idea is to remind us that joy is all around us, and we just need to be open to experiencing it.
