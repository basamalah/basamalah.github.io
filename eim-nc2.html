10. What is the differences of NC1, NC2, and NC3, in TESDA?


First, let's have a background about national certificates (NC).

The Skills Certificate Equivalency Program (SCEP) is a collaborative partnership between TESDA and the Civil Service Commission, which aims to give government skilled workers eligibility for public service. This requires awarding TESDA Competency Certificates (NC or COC) to those who are expected to demonstrate expertise in particular skills with success. Such certificates shall be provided equivalent eligibility by the CSC.

SCEP shall grant civil service eligibility to competent and skilled government employees and those seeking entrance into the service for Category I positions.

Category I positions refers to positions in government with qualification standard (QS) requiring eligibility thru an appropriate TESDA-issued National Certificate (NC) or Certificate of Competency (COC).

The National Certificate is a certificate in higher education in a field of technology offered by higher institutions globally.

Now to answer the main question, technically, all National Certificates have the same function, which corresponds to a job or work you are applicable or qualified to apply. The higher the level of national certificate means you have more experience, and would give you better credentials and eligibility to a higher position or title at civil service.

15. What is the importance of NC2?


NC2 is important to get a skilled job. This competency certificate is a certification of personal skills. It is issued by TESDA upon passing the assessment skills. TESDA assesses and certifies mid-level skilled manual workers through the Philippine TVET Competency Assessment and Certification System. This national certificate is accepted across the world.

Receiving NC2  

These are the steps to receive NC2 from TESDA:

Proceed to any accredited TESDA assessment centers near you Submit a completely filled-out application form and self-assessment guide for your specialization. Go on to the assessment center’s cashier and pay the assessment fee Come early at the assigned assessment center venue on your scheduled assessment day Get your competency assessment result summary Get your assessment result Claiming of your TESDA NC2 and COC

Skill with NC2

These are some skills with NC2:

Bread and Pastry Production NC2 Dressmaking NC2 Driving NC2

24. Why does nc2 important


A National Certification is given by TESDA to Filipinos who passed an assessment after taking a training on a specific course or program (Bartending, Cosmetology, Bread and Pastry, etc.)

For skilled workers who want to reach greater heights, a National Certificate is important because it will prove your expertise in a specific field. Though it is not required in most workplaces, a NC II would be an edge if someone will apply in reputable companies or institutions.

A National Certificate is like a diploma that proves you have the necessary skills and knowledge to work in that specific field. It lets you gain credibility in your workplace.

Combination - Number of selections without arrangement (nCr)

[tex]\frac{2n!}{3!(2n-3)!} \div \frac{n!}{2!(n-2)!}\\= \frac{(2n-3)!(2n-2)(2n-1)(2n)}{3!(n-3)!} * \frac{2!(n-2)!}{(n-2)!(n-1)(n)}\\= \frac{(2n-2)(2n-1)(2n)}{3!} * \frac{2!}{(n-1)(n)}\\= \frac{4(2n-1)}{3}[/tex]
