2. Semi detailed lesson plan in english grade 4 adjectives


I. Objective/s
1. Students will describe people's appearance and characteristics.
2. Students will use adjectives in a sentence.
II. Materials
1. Photos of family members (Students were informed in advance to bring the photos)
2. Videos of different people and their interaction
III. Methodology
1. Lead-in
The teacher will ask the students to bring out the photos of their family members.
2. The teacher will ask the students to describe how their family members look like. The teacher calls 5 students. As they answer, the teacher will write the words they say on the board.
3. The teacher will ask another 5 students to describe the characteristics/qualities of their family members. As they answer, the teacher will write the words they say on the board.
4. The teacher then tells the student that in the English language, these words that describe people are called adjectives.
5. The teacher shows sentences with adjectives in it and asks the students to locate the describing words. The teacher guides the students in doing this.
6. The teacher distributes a worksheet and this has to be completed in the class.

25. Semi detailed lesson plan about synonyms and antonyms


I. Topic: Synonyms and Antonyms
II. Objective: To describe objects using synonyms and antonyms
III. Level: Grade 4
IV. Materials: 
word cards, different objects, worksheet
V. Methodology: 
Warmer/Lead-in: 
The teacher will show different objects in class. He/she will ask the students to tell something about each object using one word only. As the students recite, the teacher will write their answers on the word.
Focus:
The teacher will ask the students to read the answers written on the board. He/she will then ask if there is any way to say those written words in a similar way. The teacher will give an example from the ones written on the board. The teacher will ask if there is any way to say those written words in an opposite way. The teacher will give an example from the ones written on the board. The teacher will then introduce the language focus. The words that we use to say a word in a similar way are called Synonyms. The words that we use to say a word in an opposite way are called Antonyms.
Exercise:
Game
The teacher will distribute a word card to each student. The students will stand up, roam around and find their Synonym partner. The students are given only 5 minutes to find their partner.
The teacher will distribute a word card to each student. The students will stand up, roam around and find their Antonym partner. The students are given only 5 minutes to find their partner.
Test:
The teacher will distribute Synonyms/Antonyms worksheet to the students.
