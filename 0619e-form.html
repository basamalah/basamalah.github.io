17. 6-10. What is the importance of farm record keeping? 11-15. What are the different forms prepared in the farm?​


Answer:

6-10. The importance of farm record keeping is that it helps farmers to track and monitor the performance of their operations. By keeping records of their activities and the results of those activities, farmers can identify trends and patterns that may be affecting their yields and profits. This can help them to make more informed decisions about their operations, such as when to plant, what to plant, and how to manage their resources.

11-15. Some of the different forms that are typically prepared in the farm include:

Production records, which track the quantities of crops and livestock produced

Financial records, which track the income and expenses of the farm

Inventory records, which track the quantities of supplies and materials used in the farm

Sales records, which track the quantities and prices of crops and livestock sold

Employee records, which track the hours worked and pay rates for farm workers

Weather records, which track the temperature, rainfall, and other meteorological data for the farm.
