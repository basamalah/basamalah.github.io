21. Monitoring the implementation of the curriculum and its effects is necessary because in time, its design will become obsolete. Which of the following should be answered to monitor the curriculum? I. Does the actual curriculum match the design? II. Are the students acquiring the knowledge, skills, and attitudes through the designed curriculum? III. Are the learners able to demonstrate the acquired knowledge, skills, and attitudes?


Curriculum Monitoring Checklist

All of the options listed are important to consider when monitoring the implementation of a curriculum.

I. It is important to ensure that the actual curriculum being taught matches the intended design, as any deviations from the design may impact student learning outcomes.

II. It is crucial to assess whether students are acquiring the intended knowledge, skills, and attitudes that are targeted by the curriculum.

III. Observing whether students are able to demonstrate their newly acquired knowledge, skills, and attitudes is essential to confirm that the curriculum is effective and meeting its intended objectives.

Therefore, all of the options mentioned are important to consider when monitoring the implementation of a curriculum.
