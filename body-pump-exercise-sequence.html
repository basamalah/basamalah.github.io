16. why is the human heart called a double pump? A.its pump inside and outside, to circulate blood throughout the body.B.it pumps on upper and lower side to circulate blood throughout the body .C. it pumps alternating up and down to circulate blood throughout the body. D.its pumps on every side,the left and the right, to circulate blood throughout the body​


Answer:

A.Its pump inside and outside,to circulate blood throughout the body

Explanation:

Your heart is a single organ,but it acts as a double pump.

The first pump carries oxygen-poor blood to your lungs,where it unloads carbon dioxide and picks up oxygen.

It then delivers oxygen-rich blood to your heart.

The second pump delivers oxygen-rich blood to every part of your body.

29. pumps the blood throughout the body​


Ans: Heart

Explanation :

The right side of your heart receives oxygen-poor blood from your veins and pumps it to your lungs, where it picks up oxygen and gets rid of carbon dioxide. The left side of your heart receives oxygen-rich blood from your lungs and pumps it through your arteries to the rest of your body.
