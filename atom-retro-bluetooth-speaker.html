RATE - [tex]\boxed{\bold{\green{6\%}}}[/tex]BASE - [tex]\boxed{\bold{\green{Php \: 850,000}}}[/tex]PERCENTAGE - [tex]\boxed{\bold{\green{Php \: 51,000}}}[/tex]

2.) father bought a brand new bluetooth speaker with discount at 20% discount or ₱1,100. the original price of the bluetooth speaker is ₱5,500.

RATE - [tex]\boxed{\bold{\green{20\%}}}[/tex]BASE - [tex]\boxed{\bold{\green{Php \: 5,500}}}[/tex]PERCENTAGE - [tex]\boxed{\bold{\green{Php \: 1,100}}}[/tex]
