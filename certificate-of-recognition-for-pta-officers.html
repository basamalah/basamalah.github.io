1. out of 400 PTA members and officers,320 attended the PTA meeting.what percent of the OTA members and officers were present ​


ANSWER:80% of the OTA members and officers were present.

SOLUTION:

1. Identify the given values and the unknown

Percentage (P) - 320

Base (B) - 400

Rate (R) - ? (Unknown)

2. Identify the formula for the unknown

Percentage - B × R

Base - P/R or P ÷ R

Rate - P/B or P ÷ B

Since the rate is the unknown, we use the formula Rate = P/B

3. Solve

Rate = P/B

Rate = 320/400

Rate = 0.8 (move two decimal places to the right to convert to percent)

Rate = 80%
