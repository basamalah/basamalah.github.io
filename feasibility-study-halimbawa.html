16. Ano ang Halimbawa ng feasibility study?


FEASIBILITY STUDY

Isa itong pag-aaral at pagtatasa sa praktikalidad ng isang inihahaing plano lalo't ginagamit pang-negosyo.  (Feasibility study is an assessment of the practicality of a proposed plan or method.)


Ito ay pagtatantya kung magiging matagumpay ba ang isang proyekto sa pamamagitan ng pag-aaral nito gamit ang pagtutuos o accounting para makita ang kaugnayan nito sa ekonomiya, teknolohiya, pagiging legal at pagtatalaga ng proyekto.

Ang mga gumagawa ng feasibility study ay naghahanap ng positibo at negatibong kalalabasan ng isang proyekto at kung makabuluhan ba itong gamitan ng oras at kapital na pinansyal. (It is an analysis of how successfully a project can be completed, accounting for factors that affect it such as economic, technological, legal and scheduling factors. Project managers use feasibility studies to determine potential positive and negative outcomes of a project before investing a considerable amount of time and money into it.)

18. 1. What is the Feasibility study of agri-business?the feasibility of agribusiness?​


Answer:

A feasibility study of agribusiness is an analysis of the potential profitability and sustainability of a proposed agricultural business venture. It typically includes an examination of factors such as market demand for the products or services, production and operating costs, competition, and potential risks and opportunities. The study may also include a review of the availability and suitability of land, labor, and other resources required for the business. The goal of the study is to determine whether the proposed venture is viable and worth pursuing, and to identify any potential challenges or issues that need to be addressed before proceeding.

Answer:

Prior to entering a new business, it is important to understand the nature of the markets, the expected financial returns and the risks that surround these expected outcomes. A feasibility study provides financial metrics based on cash-flow projections and determines the viability of the project.
