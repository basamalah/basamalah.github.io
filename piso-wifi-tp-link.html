3. What is disadvantage of piso wifi vendo?​


Answer:

One of the main disadvantages of piso wifi vending machines is that they can be easily exploited by hackers and cybercriminals. Hackers can gain access to the system's administrative panel, which allows them to change the pricing or steal money from the machine. This can lead to financial losses for the owner and inconvenience for the users who may have lost money or experienced connectivity issues.

Another disadvantage is that piso wifi vending machines require a stable and fast internet connection to function properly. If the internet connection is slow or unreliable, the machine may experience frequent disconnections or fail to provide a satisfactory user experience. Additionally, if the machine is not maintained regularly, it can become dirty, damaged or out of order, leading to inconvenience for users and additional costs for the owner.

Finally, piso wifi vending machines can be expensive to set up and maintain, especially if they require frequent repairs or upgrades. The initial cost of purchasing the hardware and software can be high, and ongoing costs such as electricity, internet connection, and maintenance fees can add up over time. As a result, owners must carefully weigh the benefits and costs of investing in a piso wifi vending machine before deciding whether to install one in their business.
