1. What is GAMABA means?


What is GAMABA means?

• This is the another term use for National living Treasures award or Gawad sa Manlilikha ng bayan (GAMABA)

• GAMABA is an award given to the  creators of the Country. Most specially to the most deserving Pinoy talent and a motivation for both artists and aspiring artists.

• GAMABA is awarded either in one person or a group of people  

• Awardees have proved themselves excellence in their craft or art. They are also  willing to share their talents and skill with the people in their community to help preserve the art form in generations to come.

WHAT IS THE BASIS OF THE GOVERNMENT IN GIVING THIS AWARD?

• This award known as Gawad sa Manlilikha ng bayan ( GAMABA) is implemented under Republic Act No. 7355 through the National commission for culture and the Arts (NCAA). This government agency serves as the highest policy making and coordinating body for culture and arts of the state.

• Their performance should possess diverse heritage and cultural traditions  that transcend their beginnings to become part of our national character.

• Performers should  perform age-old customs, crafts and ways of living to the attention and appreciation of Filipino life.

• The artist is given an award because these artists remains true to themselves and have remained truthful to their art.

• The citizen should engaged in any traditional art uniquely Filipino

• Has a distinctive skills have reach such a high level of technical and artistic excellence.  

WHAT IS THE ROLE OF NATIONAL COMMISSION FOR CULTURE AND THE ARTS (NCAA)?

• They are take in charge for the search for the finest traditional artist of the land

• They ensure that there is a program that will transfer the skills to another;

• They are the one who undertakes and measure to promote a genuine appreciation of and instill pride among our people about the genius of the Manlilikha ng bayan.

24. what is the significance of GAMABA?


SIGNIFICANCE OF GAMABA

A GAMABA or Gawad sa Manlilikha ng Bayan is the award given to all  traditional folk artists to give honor to their and support to their talents and to assure that  their skills and crafts are preserved. GAMABA is  was institutionalized through Republic Act No. 7355. GAMABA motivates traditional folk artists to share their skills to others so that there will be future predecessor of their skills. GAMABA conducts the quest for the best customary craftsmen of the land, embraces a program that will guarantee the exchange of their abilities to other people, and attempts measures to advance a veritable enthusiasm for and ingrain pride among our kin about the virtuoso of the Manlilikha ng Bayan.

GAMABA ENVISIONEDAs envisioned under R.A. 7355, "Manlilikha ng Bayan" will mean a resident occupied with any customary workmanship extraordinarily Filipino whose particular abilities have arrived at a significant degree of specialized and creative greatness and have been given to and generally rehearsed by the current age in his/her local area with a similar level of specialized and imaginative skill.

27. What is the significance of Gamaba?


What is the significance  of GAMABA?

1. To encourage the finest traditional artist to pursue to share their skills and continuously transfer it to others and to remain truthful to their art.

2. It also motivates artists to develop their skills, works and ideas and to teach it to the young generations  

3. It also help traditional folks to instill their pride as  manlilikha ng bayan.

4. GAMABA also help the traditional work of artist to promote their craft  both local and international.

5. GAMABA aimed to preserve old aged customs, crafts and way of living of the people.

6. It also aimed that Filipino people will be able to appreciate and realize their  Filipino life.This also promotes the preservation of the Filipino culture through arts.
