2. What is the main point of particular work of sampaguita Y poesias varias ​


Explanation:

Sampaguitas y otras poesías varias[1] (Jasmines and Other Various Poems), also known as Sampaguitas y poesías varias,[2] (Jasmines and Varied Poems) is the first book of poetry published by a Filipino in Europe. The poems were written in the Spanish language by Pedro Paterno, a Filipino poet, novelist, politician,[1] and former seminarian.[2] The Tagalog word sampaguita (uses the Spanish-style spelling of "sampagita") in the title of the book refers to the Jasminum sambac, a species of jasmine that is native to the Philippines and other parts of southern Asia.[1] Paterno read verses from the book at the Ateneo de Madrid.[2]

17. If x varias directly as y and x = 75 when y= 15,what is the value of y when x = 25​


GIVEN

[tex] \\ \\ [/tex]

x varies directly as y and x = 75 when y= 15

[tex] \\ x = ky \\ \\ [/tex]

Evaluate the given values

[tex] \\ (75) = k(15) \\ \\ [/tex]

Multiply 15 to k

[tex] \\ (75) = 15k \\ \\ [/tex]

Flip the equation

[tex] \\ 15k = 75 \\ \\ [/tex]

Cancel 15 by dividing to both sides.

[tex] \\ \frac{15k}{15} = \frac{75}{15} \\ \\ [/tex]

Simplify

[tex] \\ k = \frac{75}{15} \\ \\ k = 5 \\ \\ [/tex]

value of y when x = 25

- use the given constant of variation or k= 5

[tex] \\ x= ky \\ \\ [/tex]

Evaluate the values

[tex] \\ (25) = (5)y \\ \\[/tex]

Multiply 5 to y then flip the equation

[tex] \\ 25 = 5y \\ \\ 5y = 25 \\ \\ [/tex]

Divide 5 to both sides

[tex] \\ \frac{5y}{5} = \frac{25}{5} \\ \\ [/tex]

Simplify

[tex] \\ y = \frac{25}{5} \\ \\ y = 5 \\ \\ \\ [/tex]
