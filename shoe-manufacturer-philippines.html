The shoes factory formulated on equation P(n)=500+70 which represents the number of manufactured shoes (n) and the cost of manufactured (P).

A. What is the cost of manufacturing 40 shoes?

[tex]{ \mathbb{ \small{ \blue{ANSWER: }}}}[/tex]

Equation: (P (n) = 500n+70)

Let n = number of manufactured shoes P = cost of manufacture shoes

Solution: (given : n=40 )

P (n) = 500n+70P(40) = 500(40+70P(40) = 20,000+70P(40) = 20,070

Therefore, the cost of manufacturing 40 shoes Php. 20,070
