7. The sum of robin's age and Bruno's is 45. Seven years ago, Robin was 16 years more than one-half, as old as Bruno was then. How old is Bruno?


Answer:

y = 17 yrs. old (Bruno's age)

Step-by-step explanation:

The sum of robin's age and Bruno's is 45. Seven years ago, Robin was 16 years more than one-half, as old as Bruno was then. How old is Bruno?

                            PAST(7 yrs. ago)                       PRESENT        

Robin                  x - 7 = (1/2)(y-7) + 16                           x

Bruno                                y - 7                                      y

x + y = 45                              eq 1

x - 7 = (1/2)(y-7) + 16              eq 2

from eq 1, move y to the right side

x = 45 - y                              eq 3

substitute eq 3  to eq 2

45 - y - 7 = (1/2)(y-7) + 16

solve for y

y = 17 yrs. old (Bruno's age)

x = 45 - 17

x = 28 yrs. old (Robin's age)

29. The sum of Robin's age and Bruno's age is 45.Seven years ago,Robin was 16 years more than one half as old as bruno was then.How old was Bruno? :)


Sum of present ages:
     x + y = 45
           y = 45 - x

Present ages:
   Robin: x
   Bruno: 45 - x

Ages seven years ago:
   Robin:  x - 7
   Bruno: 45 - 7 - x  or   38 - x

Equation:
  
   x - 7 = 16 + ¹/₂ (38 - x) 
   
   x - 7 = 16 + 19 - x/2 
 
   x - 7 = 35 - x/2
  
   (2) (x-7) = (35-x/2) (2)

    2x - 14 = 70 - x
   
    2x + x = 70 + 14
   
    3x = 84

    3x/3 = 84/3
   
    x = 28

Present ages:  Plug-in 28 to x
   Robin:  28 years old
   Bruno:  45 - 28 = 17 years old

Ages seven years ago:
   Robin: 28 - 7 = 21 years old
   Bruno: 17 - 7 = 10 years old

ANSWER:  Bruno was 10 years old (seven years ago).

Check
Sum of their ages at present is 45.
   28 + 17 = 45
   45 = 45   (True)

Comparison of ages seven years ago:
   21 = ¹/₂ (10) + 16
   21 = 5 + 16
   21 = 21   (True)
