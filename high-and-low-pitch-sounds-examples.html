1. give at least 5 examples of high pitch sounds and low pitch sounds


High-pitched sounds are produced by nails scratching on a blackboard, whistles blowing, shoes squeaking, birds chirping, and babies crying. Some low-pitched sounds are the lions roaring, thunder clashing, large bells clanging, piano’s lower keys playing, and large drums hitting. Pitch is the highness or lowness of sound.

 

PITCH

Pitch is one of the properties of sounds. To know the properties of sound, refer to this link: https://brainly.ph/question/2017896. Pitch is the quality produced by the vibrations of sound. Pitch could be high or low. It all depends on the speed of the vibrations made by the sound.

HIGH- OR LOW-PITCHED SOUNDS

High-pitched sounds are sounds that vibrate faster than normal. These sounds have high-frequency waves. It is characterized as sharp and piercing. It is usually unpleasant to hear.

Low-pitched sounds are slow vibrating sounds. These are produced by low-frequency waves. It is described as deep and quiet. It is sometimes difficult to hear.
