3d - 6 = 3d

3d - 6 + 6 = 3d + 6

Simplifying:

3d = 3d + 6

Next, we can isolate the "d" term on one side of the equation by subtracting 3d from both sides:

3d - 3d = 3d + 6 - 3d

Simplifying:

0 = 6

This last equation is not true, as 0 cannot equal 6. Therefore, the original equation 3d - 6 = 3d has no solution for "d".
