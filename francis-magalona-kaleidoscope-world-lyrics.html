"Kaleidoscope World" is a song by Filipino rapper Francis Magalona that highlights the issue of inequality in society. The lyrics speak of a world where people are divided by wealth, status, and power, and where the struggle for survival is a daily reality for many. The song's title, "Kaleidoscope World," is a metaphor for the way in which society's problems are constantly shifting and changing, creating a never-ending cycle of inequality. The song, with its powerful and thought-provoking lyrics, is a call to action for individuals to take notice of the inequality around them and to work towards creating a more just and equitable society.
