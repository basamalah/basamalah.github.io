26. Ibigay ang kahulugan ng iba-ibang tempo.​


Answer:

Tempo: the speed at which a passage of music is or should be played.

Largo: played with a slow tempo and dignified style.

Presto: performed at a quick tempo.

Allegro: allegro distinguishes a movement that's meant to be played very quickly.

Moderato: (especially as a direction after a tempo marking) at a moderate pace.

Andante:  performed at a moderately slow tempo

Vivace: played in a lively and brisk manner.

Ritardando: gradually decreasing in speed.

Accelerando: gradually increasing in speed.
