5. in photograph,Ezra is 9cm tal and her brother keith is 10cm tall. Ezra's actual height is 153 cm what is keith actual height?


170cm ang sagot, kasi 90 percent ng height ng lalaki yung babae.

Answer:

Keith's actual height is 170 cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Ratio:

Scaled height of Ezra is to scaled height of Keith (in photograph)

⇒  9 : 10

Actual height:

Ezra ⇒ 153 cm

Keith ⇒  y

Find the actual height of Keith "y" using direct proportion:

9 : 10 = 153 : y

9y = (10) (153)

9y/9 = 1,530/9

y = 170 cm

Keith's actual height is 170 cm.

Jessie and Zoe = 266
