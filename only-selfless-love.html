1. what is the message of the song only selfless love?


THE MESSAGE OF THE SONG "SELFLESS LOVE"

The song "Selfless Love" presents three important concepts: the world, the family, and the love.

In the song, it was clearly said that the world is facing problem, the world is struggling with the challenges, and the key to overcoming these problems is unity among families and the selfless love that is within the family.That is, as a whole, the message of the song "Selfless Love" is about encouraging and calling all families around the world to be united and show selfless love to overcome the struggles that the world is facing right now.
