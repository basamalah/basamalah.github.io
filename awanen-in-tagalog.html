26. Climate change: an awakening of humanity essay


ESSAY OF "An awakening of humanity for Climate Change"

Not only has an impact on the environment, climate change also has an impact on human health, which is often experienced by the community, especially the marginalized people. Mosquitoes, flies, and similar insects breed from cold areas to warmer areas. In order to adapt and reduce risk, the public needs to know more about climate change, at least related to the characteristics of climate change so that people can anticipate its impacts, one way to educate the public is through the role of the media.

The national and local media should provide a larger portion and page to cover the issue of climate change and its relation to the environment. Climate change is not just a story, not a coachman's debate, not a coffee shop talk as a fad. Climate change requires us to understand it, then act. Climate change is a context in which many stories and facts will be revealed.

Furthermore, it is inappropriate for state leaders to link climate change or environmental issues with the economy as mere political elite negotiations. It would be better to be linked with humanity. So, it is necessary to change the relationship between the environment and the economy, into the environment with humanity, or perhaps through a humane development paradigm. Talking about climate change and its relation to the environment is not only for today or for the short term, but for the long term for the sake of a better future for our children and grandchildren.
