4. What is the meaning of the mango tree in the play New Yorker in Tondo


What is the meaning of the mango tree in the play New Yorker in Tondo.

Answer: The “Mango Tree” symbolizes their cherished memories. The four of them namely Kikay, Tony, Nena and Totoy were childhood friends. Every happy and sorrowful moments that they encountered was witnessed by the mango tree. The mango tree was also a symbol of their love and friendship.

5. is it important for us to know the play "New Yorker in Tondo"? Who​


Answer:

New Yorker in Tondo is a play about a run-of-the-mill girl from Tondo named Kikay. She visited New York and returned a very different person. Her new attitude, mannerisms, outfits, and ideas stun her family and friends. This causes emotional unrest between all of them. She convinces her mom, Aling Atang, to change into what looks like a high-society matrona and insists everyone call her mom, Mrs. Mendoza. She enrages her secret fiancé Tony with her “change of heart.” Thus causing Tony to seek an engagement with their other childhood friend, Nena, to the dismay of Totoy, the rowdy kabarakada who has secretly liked Nena ever since. As you can imagine there was plenty of squabbling and fistfights among the four friends. The play ends with a new love emerging between Nena and Totoy and the relationship between Kikay and Tony rekindled.

9. Give the message of the story New Yorker in Tondo


New Yorker in Tondo is a play about a girl named Kikay who has fallen in love with the concrete jungle, New York, and has forgotten about her native land, the Philippines. New Yorker in Tondo is a very comical play but also evokes a deep and serious message. In the story, the protagonist neglects her motherland and changes her ways, even her name, and adopts the lavish lifestyle of a New Yorker. She willingly leaves friends and vows, and her lover. As fate brings her back to her roots, she encounters different conflicts with the characters and in the end is found struggling to gain her lover back.  A reconciliation awakens her to the reality.

22. Why is the drama, "New Yorker in Tondo" considered a satire?


Answer:

As a classic satirical play, it deals with human folly, which are neither painful or destructive. It depicts accurately the average Filipino's faux love for the country which was completely lost when exposed to a foreign country's cultural aesthetic.

28. New yorker in tondo full story by marcelino agana jr


New Yorker in Tondo by Marcelino Agana Jr.
      
In the story “New Yorker in Tondo” by Marcelino Agana, Jr., it presents a story which portrays a Filipina who came home from New York after 10 months.        

Kikay, Agana’s main character, adopted the style of living of a New Yorker making her treat her friends in opposite way that she used to. Later, when she discovered the engagement of his fiancé to another girl, it made her realize that doing that kind of attitude will not make her happy.       

A young girl in Tondo named Kikay was sent to New York to study. Before she left the country, she was secretly engaged to Tony. For Kikay’s future, they both sacrificed the love they have, and promised that they will wait for each other. In this part, it is good that the author shows that love can wait and they can be together once the main character finishes her studies. But later in the story, Kikay stopped responding to the letters of Tony, which makes Tony to consider himself a free man, which contradicts in the positivity about the idea of love can wait. She has returned to Tondo, and she has adopted the culture of a New Yorker. She even applied it to her mother. This shows the negative trait of a Filipino when it comes to foreigners; they abandoned their own culture and adopt the new one for them to consider civilized. Tony and their other childhood friends visited Kikay, but they are not prepared for what attitude she has shown to them. Later Kikay changed upon discovery that his love is engaged to one of her childhood friends.

The story shows that we must love our own country and be proud of our culture. Similar to what have happened to Kikay, she was the one who was troubled for what she had done. A real Filipino nurtures and loves his own.

30. new yorker in tondo give 5 difficult meaning​


Explanation:

1. A person from New York who visits or stays in Tondo, a district in Manila, Philippines, for a certain period of time.

2. A person from Tondo who has adopted the culture, lifestyle, or attitude of a typical New Yorker, perhaps after visiting or living in New York.

3. A cultural clash between the cosmopolitan lifestyle of a New Yorker and the gritty, urban reality of Tondo, which is known for its poverty, overcrowding, and crime.

4. A commentary on the universal desire to experience a different way of life or explore new places, even if it means leaving one's home behind.

5. A representation of the idea that identity is fluid and can be shaped by one's environment, experiences, and interactions with other people.
