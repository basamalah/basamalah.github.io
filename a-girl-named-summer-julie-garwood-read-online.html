30. The mean weight of loida jackie and jen is 55 kilograms. a.) what is the total weight of these girls?b.) julie weighs 60kg, what is the mean weight of the four girls?


1. If the mean weight of Jen, Loida and Jackie are 55, it means that whatever the sum of the weights of these 3 girls divided by 3, will equal to 55. Writing that out mathematically:

let s = sum of weights of the 3 girls

[tex]\frac{s}{3} = 55 kg[/tex]

we can simply multiply 3 to both sides to get the actual sum of weights of the three girls: 

[tex]3(\frac{s}{3}) = 3(55 kg)[/tex]
[tex]s = 3(55 kg) = 165 kg[/tex]

2. Adding Julie in, if her weight is 60, we can add that to the total weight, and divide the total by 4 now, to get the average weight of the girls:

[tex]s = 165 + 60[/tex]
[tex]mean = \frac{165 + 60}{4} = 56.25 kg [/tex]
