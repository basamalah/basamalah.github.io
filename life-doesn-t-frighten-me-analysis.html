10. We defend a queen who doesn t care about us and this woman cares about her lover and her land


Answer

"We defend a queen who doesn't care about us and this woman cares about her lover and her land." This was a caption associated with a photo of a Female IRA volunteer. The original caption was "A woman IRA volunteer on active service in West Belfast with an AR18 assault rifle".

It was a photo during the war of Independence in Ireland. The Irish Republic Army's (IRA) goal was to drive the British out of Ireland and claim independence. The war resulted in a Ceasefire Anglo-Irish Treaty that later caused an Irish Civil War.

The fake story of the Photo

A photograph taken in Ireland in 1972 of a girl shooting from the gun of her fiancé who was wounded in a battle against the British army, her wounded fiancé survived after being transported by car to a safe place, while his girlfriend clashed with British soldiers until she was killed.

When the commander of the Battalion of The English Soldiers found out that they were fighting a woman, he ordered his soldiers not to touch her body and allowed the Irish to bury her and they heard the English commander repeat this sentence:

"We defend a queen who doesn't care about us. And this woman cares about her lover and her land."

24. Explain this quote “Power doesn‟t corrupt people, people corrupt power”.


Power is an inanimate concept that in itself isn't evil, such as money is in itself not evil. It is the way they are used that makes them corrupt and malicious. People in power have the choice to use their power for the greater good, and cannot blame their actions on how power corrupted them. It is not what is outside a person that corrupts them, but what is within. It is their greed, pride, and own selfishness that urges people to take advantage of their power in order to satisfy their insatiable desires.
