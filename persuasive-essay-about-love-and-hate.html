Most students hate mathematics becauseof it's complexity and difficulty. Most people hate those which they cannot understand. Mathematics has different fields, geometry, algebra, trigonometry, calculus and many more. All of this makes most students nose bleed especially those not gifted with mathematics. It makes peoples head hurt especially given complex equations. Mathematics is fundamental in everyday life as it is needed yet due to its difficulty, people fail to understand it's nature that's why math is students least favorite subject as because if they fail to solve those equations, it might affect their grades and that is why most students hate mathematics.

Most of students hate mathematics because at the first place they have the anxiety or "oh my god it is very difficult" moment. It is the collective thinking of everybody that makes them think too that math is hard. But if you will think it as easy like 1+1 =2, then a+a = 2a, you will find it really easy.

Times will come that you will be stuck to a problem, what can we do about it? of course try and try to solve until we arrive at the correct answer.

Mathematics is not just about the numbers we know that has been taught to us by our school teachers but it is all around us.

For instance,

You are buying chocolates in the store. Knowing that one piece of chocolate costs you Php 1.00 is already a math.

Sleeping at an early hour to get enough rest is also math. You will count for example at 10 am until to your waking hours like 6 am. It is a full 8-hour sleep.

Giving your crush a flower or chocolates on Valentine's Day and assume that you will earn significant "chance" to win her heart is also a math!

As studying higher math, of course we should understand its foundations and fundamentals. All the underlying principle is very simple, it is just some of the teachers can't really teach them well to every students because some students may catch it up fast, some may not. And what is happening is some students were left behind from the topic yesterday and then having a new lesson the other way will get them stuck, making them hate mathematics.

Know that mathematics will give us the understanding and a whole new level of perspective to view things in life.
