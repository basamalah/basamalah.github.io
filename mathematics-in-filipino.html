8. Did their discovery of "Science and Mathematics" help the Filipinos when their struggle arose in the people​


Answer:

The discovery and advancement of science and mathematics have undoubtedly helped Filipinos in various aspects of their lives, including during times of struggle and conflict.

In the field of science, medical breakthroughs and advancements have significantly improved the health and well-being of Filipinos. For instance, the development of vaccines and antibiotics has helped control and treat diseases that were once deadly, such as tuberculosis and polio. These medical advancements have also played a critical role in addressing public health crises such as the COVID-19 pandemic.

Furthermore, science and technology have led to the creation of new industries and job opportunities in the Philippines, promoting economic growth and development. The Information Technology and Business Process Outsourcing (IT-BPO) sector, for example, has become a significant contributor to the country's economy, providing jobs to millions of Filipinos.

In the field of mathematics, the use of mathematical models and techniques has helped solve various problems, including those in the fields of finance, engineering, and natural sciences. For example, mathematical models have been used to analyze the impact of climate change and natural disasters on the Philippines, leading to better disaster risk reduction strategies.

In terms of education, the teaching of science and mathematics has been a crucial component of the Philippine education system. Science and mathematics education helps Filipinos develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and scientific literacy, enabling them to understand and engage with the world around them.

During times of struggle and conflict, the skills and knowledge gained from science and mathematics education can be essential in finding solutions and resolving issues. For example, during natural disasters, scientists and mathematicians can provide valuable insights and advice to aid in relief efforts and minimize the impact of the disaster on the affected population.

In conclusion, the discovery and advancement of science and mathematics have significantly helped Filipinos in various aspects of their lives, including during times of struggle and conflict. These fields have played a critical role in improving health and well-being, promoting economic growth, and developing problem-solving skills and scientific literacy.
