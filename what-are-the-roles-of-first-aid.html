24. what the 5 Roles of First Aid​


Answer:

The five roles of first aid are:

1. Preserve life: The primary role of first aid is to preserve life by ensuring that the person receives the necessary medical attention immediately. The first aider should assess the person's condition and provide basic life support if required.

2. Prevent further harm: First aid aims to prevent the injured person's condition from worsening by providing immediate care and taking steps to prevent further injury or harm. This may include immobilizing a fracture, stopping bleeding or preventing an injury from getting infected.

3. Promote recovery: First aid can help promote recovery by minimizing the extent of the injury or illness and supporting the person's body in healing itself. This may include providing pain relief, keeping the person comfortable, and providing support for their emotional well-being.

4. Protect the unconscious: When a person is unconscious, the first aider should take steps to protect them from further harm, such as clearing the airway, providing oxygen, or turning the person onto their side to prevent choking on vomit.

5. Summon professional help: If the person's condition is serious or life-threatening, the first aider should call for professional medical help as soon as possible. This may involve calling an ambulance, contacting a doctor, or taking the person to the nearest medical facility.
