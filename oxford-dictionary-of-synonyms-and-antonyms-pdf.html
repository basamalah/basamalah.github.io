10. Is a book like dictionary that contains the synonyms and antonyms of the words


A dictionary is a powerful book used to get the definition or meaning of a word. A dictionary can also give the synonym and antonyms of a word. In some versions of dictionaries, they can even provide the origin of a word.

The other important information that a dictionary can give:

Pronunciation and Spelling

The pronunciation of a word using the standard rule can be found just beside the word it defines. It is written together with spelling variations, syllabication, and its part of speech.

Etymologies

Refers to the origin of a word. Most of the word origins come from French, Spanish, Greek, Latin, or German. Dictionaries also provide the year, or even the exact date, when the word was first used.

Syntactic Peculiarities

Refers to how the words should be used to be grammatically correct in a sentence or phrase. Some dictionaries even provide sample sentences with proper capitalization.

Illustration

Some versions of dictionaries give illustrations to make the meaning or definition of a word more understandable. The usual illustrations we see in a dictionary are parts of the eye, anatomy, devices, circuits, or even the solar system.
