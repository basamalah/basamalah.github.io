11. ano obig sabihin ng payaw?​


Answer:

páyaw (CEBUANO)

- A kind of bamboo raft used in fishing, used to attract fish. It is left in deep waters with weighted coconut fronds suspended from it, on which algae develop and attract fish which can then be caught with a net.
