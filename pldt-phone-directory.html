1. looking up a specific phone number in a telephone directory​


Telephone directory, commonly called a telephone book, telephone address book, phone book, or the white and yellow pages, is a listing of telephone subscribers in a geographical area or subscribers to services provided by the organization that publishes the directory. Its purpose is to allow the telephone number of a subscriber identified by name and address to be found.

The phone comes from the word tele and phone which means far and sound. So the telephone is the delivery of information from one party to another remotely, both in their own environment and office environment.

The telephone is the most effective means of communication. Therefore, the demand for telephones is increasing. The telephone is a part of human life as a business support tool. Telephone users are found in various layers of society, both individuals and institutions. Now the telephone has been modified into a multi-purpose communication tool and a variety of models.

The types of telephone directories that you can use include the following:

The phone book is a collection of telephone information data, such as individual/company name data.The yellow pages are part of the public telephone book, but are often called the yellow pages separately.A special telephone book is a book that contains names of individuals/companies/agencies, so that it can be used at any time quickly.
