7. are secondary effct of lava blasted into the air​


Answer:

Primary effects are produced directly by the volcanic activity. Examples include lava flows, ash-flows, lateral blasts, ash-falls, and gases. Secondary effects are the result of primary effects. Examples include lahars/mudflows, floods, fires, and tsunamis.
