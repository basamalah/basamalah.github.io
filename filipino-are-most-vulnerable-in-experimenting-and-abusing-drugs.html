9. 1: How can a person lower the risk of using and abusing illegal drugs? 2: How can an individual stay safe in the face of risk factors that make him or her vulnerable to drug use and abuse? ​


1. There are several ways a person can lower the risk of using and abusing illegal drugs:

- Educate yourself on the dangers and risks of drug use

- Surround yourself with positive influences and social support

- Seek help and support from family, friends, or professionals if you are struggling with addiction or substance abuse

- Practice stress management techniques such as exercise, mindfulness, or therapy

- Avoid situations or environments where drug use is common

- Have a plan in place for how to refuse drugs if offered to you

- Seek alternative ways to cope with life stressors or emotional issues instead of turning to drugs

2. To stay safe in the face of risk factors that make an individual vulnerable to drug use and abuse, here are some strategies:

- Avoid places or situations where drugs are present and easily accessible

- Develop healthy coping mechanisms to deal with stress and emotional challenges

- Seek support from friends or family members who can provide emotional support and guidance

- If struggling with addiction, seek professional help from a therapist, counselor, or treatment center

- Know your triggers and avoid them if possible

- Stay accountable by surrounding yourself with positive people who support your recovery journey

- Create a self-care routine that promotes physical, mental, and emotional wellness.
