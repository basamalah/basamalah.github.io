2. ano ang instraktura ng ynares sa politika?​


Si Rebecca "Nini" Alcantara Ynares (ipinanganak noong Setyembre 26, 1949) ay isang Pilipina na politiko mula sa lalawigan ng Rizal, Pilipinas. Una siyang nahalal bilang gobernador ng lalawigan noong 2001 at muling nahalal noong 2013 pagkatapos ng isang termino ng kanyang asawang si Casimiro Ynares Jr. at ng dalawang termino ng kanyang anak na si Casimiro Ynares III. Answer:

Ventilators= 40

Hospital Beds= 140

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

2:7 , 180 total

Equation:

180= 2:7

180= 2x + 7x

180= 9x

[tex]\frac{180}{9\\} =\frac{9x}{9}[/tex]

20= x

Substitution:

Ventilators (2x)= 2(20)=40

Hospital Beds (7x)= 7(20)=140

Checking:

40 ventilators + 140 hospital beds = 180 total

23. definition of san andreas​


Answer:

The San Andreas Fault is a continental transform fault that extends roughly 1,200 kilometers through California. It forms the tectonic boundary between the Pacific Plate and the North American Plate, and its motion is right-lateral strike-slip.


24. What kind of fault is located within San Andreas fault system in the movie San Andreas


The San Andreas Fault is a continental transform fault that extends roughly 1,200 kilometers (750 mi) through California. It forms the tectonic boundary between the Pacific Plate and the North American Plate, and its motion is right-lateral strike-slip (horizontal).

In short it is called (Continental Transform Fault)
