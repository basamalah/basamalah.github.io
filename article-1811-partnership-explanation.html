When a partnership for a fixed term or particular undertaking is continued after the termination of such term or particular undertaking without any express agreement, the rights and duties of the partners remain the same as they were at such termination, so far as is consistent with a partnership at will.

What is partnership at will?

A form of parternship that arises where no fixed term has been agreed for the duration of the partnership or the partnership has been entered into for an undefined term. A parternship at will may be dissolved at any time by a partern serving notice on the other partern(s).

PARTNERSHIP WITH A FIXED TERM A partnership wherein the term for which the partnership is to exist is fixed or agreed upon or one formed for a particular undertaking,and upon the expiration of the term or completion or the particular enterprise,the partnership is dissolved,unless continued by the partners.
