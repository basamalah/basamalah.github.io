26. The length of a computer monitor is twice as long as its width. The area of the monitor is 2 ft². What are the dimensions of the monitor


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebraic expression in finding area, as well dimensions.

Formula for finding area of rectangle is lw.

StatementThe length of a computer monitor is twice as long as its width. The area of the monitor is 2 ft².

Mathematical Equation

l = 2w

A = lw

2 = (2w)w

Solution

2 = (2w)w

2 = 2w²

1 = w²

Solve for unknown variable using extracting square root.

w² = 1

√w² = √1

w = ± 1

Note: Use the positive solution there is no negative dimension.

w = 1 ft.

l = 2w

l = 2(1)

l = 2 ft.
