16. What is the Basic information/History of sinukwan Festival


Answer:

The annual celebration was initiated in 1998 to lift the moral of those affected by the eruption of Mt. Pinatubo and to relive the Kapampangan spirit during those trying times. Sinukwan Festival was named after Aring Sinukwan known as the god of ancient Kapampangans, and celebrated every first week of December.

Answer:

Panagbenga

Panagbenga Festival ( transl. Flower Festival) is a month-long annual flower occasion in Baguio. The term is of Kankanaey origin, meaning "season of blooming". The festival, held in February, was created as a tribute to the city's flowers and as a way to rise up from the devastation of the 1990 Luzon earthquake.
