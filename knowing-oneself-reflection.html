6. 3 Barriers in Knowing oneself​


3 Barriers in Knowing oneself are lack of self-love, lack of self-esteem, and lack of self-care

Knowing Yourself

Knowing oneself is one's attempt to see and understand the strengths and weaknesses that exist within him so that he is able to control all actions when dealing with other people or even himself.

Knowing yourself means fully understanding all the elements within yourself, be it psychological, physical, moral, potential and social elements.

In essence, we as humans do have two entities, namely with regard to the physical and spiritual. Physically, perhaps we can know and understand it, but what about spiritually. This shows that knowing yourself is not easy.

So that we can know and understand ourselves as well as possible, begin to determine the character that is our specialty, then do reflection to understand our identity and personality.

10. Learning About Others to know oneself​


Answer:

Learning about others to know oneself is true. It is only when we meet other people that we can know what values we should have and should not have.
