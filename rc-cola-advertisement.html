Answer:

The length of RC is 19

Step-by-step explanation:

Remember that the diagonals of a rectangle are equal in measurement.

Thus , We will equate the given expressions about the Diagonals.

RC = KO

3x+7 = 8x-13

7+13 = 8x-3x

20 = 5x

4 = x

» Substitute the value of x to get the lengths:

RC = 3x+7

RC = 3(4)+7

RC = 12+7

RC = 19
