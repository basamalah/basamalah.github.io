If I were the SK Chairman of our barangay, I will offer first aid skills training to the community because it is very important to know first aid because it will save a life that is in danger. There are remote areas in a barangay and professional helps might take time to appear so it is very helpful that youth like us know the first aid.

WHAT IS FIRST AID?

First aid is the basic or temporary immediate help/treatment by someone with medical training given to a person under illness or injury that prolongs a his life until professional helps arrive. Situations that may need first aid include the following:

Drowning

Choking

Sprain/Strain

Fracture

Nosebleed

Insect/Animal bite

Burn

Cut

Dehydration

Stroke

Heart Attack

Fainting

Seizure

Bleeding

HOW TO OFFER FIRST AID TRAINING TO OUR COMMUNITY

I will coordinate with our City health workers or any medical team that serves our barangay through a request letter asking to spare some time for a first aid skills training to the youth of our barangay on a weekend. Adults are also welcome to attend.
