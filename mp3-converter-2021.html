17. How do I convert decimals like this 0.2021202120212021 2021 is repeating


The decimal 0.20212021... in fraction form is 2021/9999.

Let:

[tex]0.20212021...=0.\overline{2021}=d[/tex]

Multiply both sides of the equation by 10000;

[tex]2021.\overline{2021}=10000d[/tex]

When expanded,

[tex]2021+0.\overline{2021}=10000d[/tex]

[tex]2021+d=10000d[/tex]

[tex]2021=9999d[/tex]

Divide both sides by 9999, yielding the value of d;

[tex]\boxed{\displaystyle d=0.\overline{2021}=\frac{2021}{9999}}[/tex]
