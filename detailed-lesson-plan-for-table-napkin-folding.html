7. SOP for experience of the 11 GAS STUDENTS in folding a table napkin​


1. Place the clean napkin face up on a flat surface.

2. Fold the napkin in half horizontally to create a rectangle.

3. Turn the napkin 180 degrees.

4. Fold the two top corners towards the middle to create two triangles.

5. Push the two bottom corners of the napkin towards each other to create a fan shape.

6. Flip the napkin over.

7. Fold the napkin in half horizontally to create a triangle.

8. Pick up one side of the triangle and fold it towards the other side to create a smaller triangle.

9. Fold the "tail" of the triangle up into the triangle.

10. Flip the napkin over and fold both sides of the napkin in to the centre.

11. Flip the napkin over again, and make any final crease adjustments you might need in order to create the desired shape.

12. The 11 GAS students should be able to complete the above steps with minimal guidance and practise it multiple times until they are able to create a neat, presentable folding of a table napkin.

Importance of table napkin foldingWhat is table napkin folding?

It is a type of creative folding that is done with the use of a napkin. It can be done or made as art, as a work, or as a hobby. It is popularly and widely known to be encountered as a table design or decoration in fancy restaurants. Usually, a clean, pressed, smooth, and starched square cloth napkin is used, which can either be linen or cotton, for best and perfect result.

What are some table napkin folding techniques?

There is a wide array of techniques and styles when it comes to table napkin folding. Here's a few of some basic table napkin styles:

Standard single pocket napkin foldAngled pocket napkin foldBow tie napkin foldDouble diamond napkin foldFleur-de-lis napkin foldTriangle napkin foldKnotted napkin fold

Why is napkin folding important?

Napkin is one of the most essential element in table decoration. It adds color, design, and it makes the table design aesthetically pleasing and appealing to the eyes.

It is also important not just to add color, but to also highlight the theme of the event. For example, it is a flower-themed event, the table napkin fold is most likely to look like a little something like a flower.
