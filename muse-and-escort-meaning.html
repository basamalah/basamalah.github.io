2. in how many ways can a muse and an escort be elected from a clss composed of 16 and 19 girls​


Answer:

304

Step-by-step explanation:

The number of ways of choosing an escort from 16 boys is 16.

The number of ways of choosing a girl from 19 girls is also 19.

Thus the number of ways of choosing an escort and a muse by fundamental principle of counting is 16x19=304.
