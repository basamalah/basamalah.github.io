10. This is a requirement in applying for the issuance of an ecc.


A requirement in applying for the issuance of an ECC (Environmental Compliance Certificate) is the fulfillment of the requirements in the Environmental Impact Statement System (EIS) and stating a commitment to implement the environmental management program as determined by the Ministry of Environment and Natural Resources – Environmental Management Agency (DENR-EMB).

The DENR-EMB’s ECC application process must comprise the items listed below:

-A letter of request to the DENR-EMB as a first step in requesting ECC

-Related project description files (including materials and processes or manufacturing technology to be used)

-Estimated product type and volume, project capacity, and disposal

-Documents that serve as ownership proof for the project you want to approve

-A location map for the region

-Documents pertaining to the project's labor recruitment criteria

In the meanwhile, the ECC Certificate management process is :

- Preparing an Environmental Management Plan and Impact Analysis

- Send the DENR-EMB the application letter for consideration and a subsequent site inspection.

If all requirements are met and there is no significant environmental harm as a result of the project, approval and ECC are provided.
