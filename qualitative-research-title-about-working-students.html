26. example of qualitative research about humss student???​


Answer:

This qualitative research study aims to explore the experiences of Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) students in a public university in the Philippines. The study will focus on the challenges and successes that HUMSS students face in their academic journey. Semi-structured interviews will be conducted with 10 HUMSS students from different backgrounds and levels of academic achievement. The interviews will explore topics such as their motivations for choosing HUMSS, their experiences with coursework, their relationships with faculty and peers, and their plans for the future. The data collected from these interviews will be analyzed using thematic analysis to identify common themes among the participants’ experiences. The results of this study will provide valuable insights into the unique challenges faced by HUMSS students, as well as potential strategies for improving their academic success.
