11. In quadriteral GARP with GA // PR and GP // AR.If the perimeter is 324 m and GP=92.Find the measure of all the other sides.​


Answer:

GA = 70

GP = 92

AR = 92

PR = 70

Step-by-step explanation:

since GP and AR is parallel and GA and PR is also parallel then the parallel side have congruent measure

92 + 92 = 184

324 - 184 = 140

divide by 2 because you still have the two parallel lines that are congruent

140 divided by 2 = 70
