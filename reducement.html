6. Why is meiosis I referred to as the reduction division? The amount of cells at the end is reduced. The amount of genetic material is reduced. The amount of steps in the process is reduced. The amount of spindle fibers involved is reduced.


The meiosis I is referred to as the reduction division because the amount of genetic material is reduced here.

It is in anaphase I where the reduction of the number of chromosomes occur. The reason for this is that gametes have no need to be a diploid daughter cell, this is because gametes will find the other half/set of chromosome on a gamete from the opposite sex. This is why gametes exist only as haploid cells and not diploid. This is what makes meiosis important. It ensures that the next generation of organisms will retain the same number of chromosomes as their parents. This prevents anomalies and genetic diseases which reduce the survival rate of organisms.

12. Which of the following statements pertaining to a vacuum flask (thermos) is not correct A.silvering reduces radiation loss B.vacuum reduces radiation loss C.vacuum reduces conduction loss D.vacuum reduces convection loss


The answer is letter B. vacuum reduces radiation loss.

The main function of vacuum flask or thermos is to reduce heat transfer by conduction or convection. Thus statements in Letter C and D are correct. The silvering in the vacuum reduces the radiation loss. Thus the statement in letter A is also correct. This shows that statement in letter B. which states that vacuum reduces radiation loss is not correct because the vacuum can only reduce ]the heat transfer by convection and conduction but not by radiation.

 

Thus, among the statement above, the statements that pertain to a vacuum flask (thermos)  that is not correct is the statement in letter B. vacuum reduces radiation loss.

Answer:

To reduce the fraction 1525 to the lowest term, you can divide the numerator and denominator by their greatest common factor. To find the greatest common factor, you can prime factorize both the numerator and denominator and find the factors that they have in common. For example:

1525 = 355*11

The greatest common factor of 1525 is 3, so you can divide both the numerator and the denominator by 3 to get the lowest term:

1525 ÷ 3/3 = (1525/3) / (3/3) = 505/1

The result is 505/1, which can be simplified to just 505.

25. What are reducing and non-reducing sugars?


Answer:

Answer:

Reducing sugars are sugars where the anomeric carbon has an OH group attached that can reduce other compounds. Non-reducing sugars do not have an OH group attached to the anomeric carbon so they cannot reduce other compounds. All monosaccharides such as glucose are reducing sugar.
