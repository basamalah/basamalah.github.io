22. Selene's height is 5 feet and 4 inches. A registration form asks for her height in centimeters. What should she write on the registration form? Plsss I need ans and solution! Thank u in advance​


Answer:

162.56

Step-by-step explanation:

1 foot = 30.48cm

1 inch = 2.54cm

5 feet = (30.48)(5) = 152.4 cm

4 inches = (2.54)(4) = 10.16 cm

152.4 + 10.16 = 162.56cm
