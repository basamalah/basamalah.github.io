2. Sollicitudo Rei Socialis reflection​


(:ANSWER:)

Sollicitudo Rei Socialis (The Concern of the Church for the Social Order)

Pope John Paul II, 1987

Pope John Paul II, 1987In Sollicitudo Rei Socialis, Pope John Paul II celebrates the twentieth anniversary of Populorum Progressio by updating the Church’s teaching on the “development of peoples” and changes that took place in the preceding two decades.

Populorum Progressio was inspired by the Church’s desire to help the millions of people who lived in a state of poverty and underdevelopment. The document concluded by noting that “development is the new name for peace,” (Paragraph 10) another mission of the Church.

What is the message of Sollicitudo Rei Socialis?

The encyclical presents differences between progress and development, and insists that true development cannot be limited to the multiplication of goods and services, but must contribute to the fullness of being a human being. In this way the moral nature of real development is meant to be shown clearly.

24. Jay and Rey are comparing their heights. If Jay's height is 120³∕₄ cm and Rey's height is 96¹∕₃ cm. What is the difference in their heights?


Answer:

24 and 5/12 centimeters

Step-by-step explanation:

ASKED:

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE IN THEIR HEIGHTS?

GIVEN:

Jay's height = 120¾ cm

Rey's height = 96⅓ cm

OPERATION/S TO BE USED:

SUBTRACTION and FINDING THE LCD OF FRACTIONS,

LCD means "Lowest Common Denominator"

SOLUTION:EXPRESS THE GIVEN AS BINOMIALS

120¾ ---> 120+¾

96⅓ ---> 96+⅓

FIND THE LCD OF THE FRACTIONS (2nd term of the binomials)

Find their LCD to make them "Similar Fractions". That way, it would be easier to

subtract them.

120+¾ ---> 120 + 9/12

96+⅓ ---> 96 + 4/12

To get the LCD, multiply the numerator and denominator of each fraction by the

denominator of the other fraction.

3 = 3•3 = 9 or 9/12

4 4•3 12

1 = 1•4 = 4 or 4/12

3 3•4 12

SUBTRACT

120 + 9/12

- ( 96 + 4/12 ) ---> Subtract the numerators

24 + 5/12 of the fractions.

ANSWER:

The difference in their heights is 24 and 5/12 centimeters.
