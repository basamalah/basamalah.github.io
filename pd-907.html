22. A 907 g ball is pulled up a slope as shown in the diagram. Calculate the potential energy it gains.


The potential energy it gains = 1.778 J

Further explanation  

Work is the transfer of energy caused by the force acting on a moving object  

Work is the product of force with the displacement of objects.  

Can be formulated  

[tex]\tt W=F\times d[/tex]

W = Work, J, Nm  

F = Force, N  

d = distance, m  

Work done is equal to the change in potential energy

[tex]\tt W=\Delta PE=-m.g.(h_2-h_1)[/tex]

Because the ball is pulled up, so the Force (F)= - mg sin θ (picture attached)

[tex]\tt W=-mg~sin~\theta\times d[/tex]

[tex]\tt sin~\theta=\dfrac{20}{50}[/tex]

so the potential energy :

m = 907 g = 907.10³ kg

d = 50 cm = 50.10⁻² m

g = 9.8 m/s²

[tex]\tt W=-907\times 10^{-3}\times \dfrac{20}{50}\times 9.8\times 50\times 10^{-2}\\\\W=-1.778~J\\\\\Delta PE=1.778~J[/tex]
