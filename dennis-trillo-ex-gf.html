4. Together Dennis,Rey and Luis earn 72000 pesos a month,Dennis earns 6,000 pesos more than Rey and 9,000pesos more than Luis.how much does Dennis earns?


29,000 pesos

Let x represent for Rey's and y for Luis' earnings every month.

Hence, Dennis' earnings every month can be represented by

x + 6,000   or   y + 9,000

Two equations can then be formulated

Equation 1:  [tex]x+6,000=y+9,000[/tex]

Equation 2:  [tex]x+6,000+x+y=72,000[/tex]

Simplify y in Eq. 1

[tex]x+6,000=y+9,000\\y=x+6,000-9,000\\y=x-3,000[/tex]

Substitute x-3,000 for y in Eq. 2 and find x

[tex]x+6,000+x+y=72,000\\x+6,000+x+(x-3,000)=72,000\\x+6,000+x+x-3,000=72,000\\3x+3,000=72,000\\3x=72,000-3,000\\3x=69,000\\x=23,000[/tex]

Dennis' earnings per month

[tex]x+6,000\\23,000+6,000\\29,000[/tex]

Additional solution: Luis' earnings per month

[tex]y+9,000=29,000\\y=29,000-9,000\\y=20,000[/tex]

29. together,dennis,rey and luis earn 72000pesos a month.if dennis earns 6000 pesos more than rey and 9000 pesos more than luis,how much does dennis earn?


Let:
x = Rey's earnings
y = Luis's earnings
z = Dennis' earnings

Given:
x + y + z = 72,000 eq. 1
6000 + x = z eq. 2
9000 + y = z eq. 3


6000 + x = z
x = z - 6000 eq. 4

9000 + y = z
y = z - 9000 eq. 5

Substitute the eq. 4 and 5 to eq. 1
x + y + z = 72,000
(z - 6000) + (z - 9000) + z = 72,000
z - 6000 + z - 9000 + z = 72,000
z + z + z - 6000 - 9000 = 72,000
3z - 15000 = 72,000
3z = 72,000 + 15,000
3z = 87,000
3z/3 = 87,000/3
z = 29,000

Therefore, the ANSWER is 29,000. It is the money earned by Dennis.

Checking:
Rey's earnings
x = z - 6000
x = 29,000 - 6,000
x = 23,000

Luis's earnings
y = z - 9,000
y = 29,000 - 9,000
y = 20,000

x + y + z = 72,000
23,000 + 20,000 + 29,000 = 72,000
72,000 = 72,000
