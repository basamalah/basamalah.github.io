25. Briefly describe each type of oral communication activity and give examples each​


Answer:

There are two types of oral communication. First, Formal communication refers to the flow through proper, predefined channels and routes of official information (Example: Classroom Lecture, Meetings and Speeches). Second, Informal communication is the informal and informal form of communication in which information is spontaneously exchanged between two or more individuals without complying with the official laws, protocols, structures, formalities and chain of command prescribed (Example: Conversation with your family in dinner table or on phone conversation).
