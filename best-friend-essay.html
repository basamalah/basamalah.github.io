2. Essay: life with my best friend


Answer:

Life with my bestfriend

"No man is an island." It is said that no one can live alone without any human interaction. Maybe because, there is something more to a human that just himself.

Anybody will agree to me when I say that we have different source of happiness. We can be happy just by doing the things we love, may it be as extreme as sky diving or as relaxing as a day at the spa. Everything that we love doing reflect on what we are as a person and it may sometimes be hard to find someone who can get along the things we want to do. The only thing happier for me than just spending the day doing the things we love is actually being able to do it with someone we love. The best experiences happen when we have someone to share it with. A bestfriend who knows a lot of things about you from your wildest dreams to your little thoughts is the best one to share it with. A life with my bestfriend is one of the best moments of my life. I think these moments will not be that special without my bestfriend. I truly believe that no man is an island for above the moments that happened and will happen is the person who is beside me through it all.

My life will not be as happy as I think it would be without my best friend. Yes, there is more to a human than just himself, because above this, is the person next to him, ready to pick him up whenever he falls and ready to jump when he jumps, no other than, the one dearest to his heart, his bestfriend.
