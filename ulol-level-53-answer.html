27. A ball is shot from ground level with an initial velocity of 250 m/s directed at an angle of 53 to the horizontal. how much time elapses before the ball strikes the ground. please do not answer this if your not sure of your answer nonsense-report i will follow you if you answer this correctly


Given values:

vi = 250 m/s

θ = 53°

-----------------------------------------------------------

Solution:

t = (2vi sin θ) / g

t = [2(250 m/s)(sin 53°)] / 9.81 m/s²

t = 40.71 s

Therefore, the time elapsed before the ball strikes the ground is 40.71 s.

Note: If the value of g used is 9.8 m/s², the answer is 40.75 s.
