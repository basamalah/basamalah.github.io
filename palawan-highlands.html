What is music of Visayas?

MUSIC OF THE VISAYAS MUSICAL STYLE OF THE VISAYANS IS A SONG-AND-DANCE DEBATE BETWEEN A MAN AND A WOMAN KNOWN AS BALITAW. THE THEME IS ABOUT THE LOVE AND MARRIAGE USUALLY PERFORMED BY SINGERS, DANCERS AND ACTORS.

What is the music of Mindoro?

The Ambahan is a lyrical chant song of the pure Mangyans. It is composed of seven syllables in one line that rhyme. The number of lines found in several Ambahan ranges from three to 134. There is no specific tone style or instrument used as accompaniment.

What are the instrumental music of Palawan?

Palawan musical instruments can be classified into four groups: Chordophones, Aerophones, Idiophones, and Membranophones.
