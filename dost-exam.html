26. What is the meaning of IPCC,DOST,NOAH,LGUs


Answer:

IPCC- Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change

Founded on 1988 by Bert Bolin and Headed by: Hoesung Lee

Responsibility

- IPCC is an intergovernmental organization in the United States that has the role in providing informations about the climate change, they are also the ones who states how this climate gives either a big or low impact, possible risks it may give out, and gives advises how are we gonna react to it.

DOST- Department of Science and Technology

Founded by Corazon Aquino on January 30, 1987

Department Head/Secretary: Fortunato Tanseco Dela Peña (since 2016)

Responsibility

Department of Science and Technology is a Philippine executive department that has been given the role or responsibility to manage all the works relate to science and technology in the country, in giving out rules and policy about the chemical or food formulations for they ensure safety in  these food substances therefore, they have the ability to approve or decline any formulations upon testing it.

NOAH- Nationwide Operational Assessment of HazardProject NOAH/NOAH-Wise Project

The NOAH-Wise Project or also known as the Project NOAH is the disaster risk reduction program of the Philippines. This program was on the administrative hands of DOST(Department of Science and Technology) on the past years by is currently on the hands of the UP or University of the Philippines. This program was created to avoid disasters in the country. They provide things and important information to turn away people's eyes from any natural danger. This program started on July 6, 2012 by the executive Mahar Lagmay.

But, on 1st of March 2017, it was given an order to shut down because of fund insufficiency

LGUs- Local Government Units

Aquilino Pimentel Jr., was considered as the father of LGU.

These are the Local Government in the Philippines, that has three divisions, provinces, municipality, and barangay. The authorities that has the been given the responsibility to collect taxes, ability in making the development of the community, they also maintain the registration of the voters.
