2. sinigang na hipon with measurement ​


Answer:

Ingredients:

1pc Onion

2pcs Tomato

1pc Finger Chili

4 cups water 0.5 cup

Radish 0.5 cup

Okra 0.5 cup

Sitaw 1

package MAGGI® Magic Sinigang Original Sampalok Mix

1 tbsp patis

0.5kg Shrimp

1 cup Kangkong

Step1

Simmer onion, tomatoes and finger chili in a water for 15mins.

Step2

Add radish, okra and sitaw with 2-minute intervals.

Step3

Pour 22g MAGGI MAGIC SINIGANG® Original Sampalok Mix and fish sauce . Add shrimp and simmer for 1 minute.

Step4

Stir in kangkong. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve immediately.

17. how to cook pork Sinigang ​


Answer:

Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the onion; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes. Season with salt. Stir in the ginger, tomatoes, and pork chops. Cover and reduce heat to medium-low. Turn the pork occasionally, until browned. Pour in the water and tamarind soup base. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat. Continue simmering until the pork is tender and cooked through, about 30 minutes. Stir in green beans and cook until tender.
