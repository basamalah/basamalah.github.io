20. good and baad effect of summertime​


good effects:

- longer days mean more time to enjoy outdoor activities and spend time with friends and family.

- the warmer weather can boost our mood and energy levels.

- vitamin D intake increases due to more sun exposure, which can benefit our overall health.

- summer vacations can provide a much-needed break from work or school, allowing us to recharge and return to our responsibilities with renewed focus.

bad effects:

- hot and humid weather can be uncomfortable and even dangerous for some people, particularly those with certain health conditions.

- increased exposure to the sun can lead to sunburn, dehydration, and other health risks.

- summer can be a busy and expensive time with travel, events, and other activities, which can add stress to our lives.

- for some people, summertime can exacerbate feelings of loneliness or isolation if they don't have access to social activities or support systems.
