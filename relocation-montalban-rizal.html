Answer:

It was a cold winter night in the small mountain village of Montalban. The wind was blowing fiercely, and the snow was falling heavily. The villagers were huddled around their fires, trying to stay warm.

Suddenly, a strange sound filled the air. It was a deep, rumbling noise that seemed to come from the sky. Everyone stopped what they were doing and looked up in awe.

High above them, a giant bird was soaring through the night sky. Its wingspan was so wide that it seemed to fill the entire sky. Its feathers were white as snow, and its eyes glowed like stars.

The villagers watched in amazement as the bird flew higher and higher, until it disappeared into the night sky. They knew that this was no ordinary bird – it was a sign from the gods that something special was about to happen in Montalban.

The next day, news spread throughout the village that a great warrior had come to town. He had come from far away to help them fight against an evil force that threatened their way of life. The villagers welcomed him with open arms and soon he became their leader.

With his help, they were able to defeat the evil force and restore peace to Montalban. From then on, whenever the wind blew fiercely through the village, they would remember the great warrior who had come from far away on the wings of a giant bird – into the wind of Montalban.
