12. A corn plant is experiencing a period of hot, dry, windy weather. How will this weather affect the corn plant processes necessary for survival?


If this kind of weather continues, survival for the corn plant is in danger, unless it is properly irrigated.  Hot and dry plus windy weather is considered to be extreme for most corn farmers.  Windy weather can makes the soil dry faster which in turn dehydrates most plants like corn.  Added to the situation is hot and dry, so basically this would damage the corn plant.  Windy weather is oftentimes the reason for insect pests population to migrate faster. This will add more adverse effects on the scenario.
