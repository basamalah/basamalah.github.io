4. At a fruit stall, ⅛ of the fruits are apples,¼ are oranges,and ⅖ of the remainder are mangoes, if there are 72 mangoes,what is the total number of friuts​


Answer:

There are 247 fruits in the stall..

How did I get the answer?

Given:

1/8 = apples

1/4 = oranges

2/5 of the remainder = mangoes

Amount:

Apples = ?

Orange = ?

Mangoes = 72

Solution:

1/8 (apples) + 1/4 (oranges) + 2/5 (mangoes) + ( 5/5 - 2/5 = 3/5) ( remainder )

Make all of them into similar fractions by finding the LCD

1/8 = 5/40 = apples

1/4 = 10/40 = oranges

2/5 = 16/40 = mangoes

3/5 = 24/ 40

LCD: 40

Then, we will find how many fruits are in 1/40 by doing the following:

72/ 16 = 4.5

4.5 = 1/40

5/40 = apples = 22.5

10/40 = oranges = 45

16/40 = mangoes = 72

24/ 40 = remainder = 108

108 + 72 + 45 + 22.5 = 247.5 or 247 ( no one would eat a half fruit lol )

Hope it helps.. ^^
