ANSWER:

Cleopatra VII Philopator was queen of the Ptolemaic Kingdom of Egypt from 51 to 30 BC, and its last active ruler. A member of the Ptolemaic dynasty, she was a descendant of its founder Ptolemy I Soter, a Macedonian Greek general and companion of Alexander the Great.

Born: 69 BC, Alexandria, Egypt

Died: August 10, 31 BC, Alexandria, Egypt

Nationality: Macedonian

Place of burial: Tomb of Antony and Cleopatra

Spouse: Ptolemy XIV Philopator (m. 46 BC), Mark Antony (m. 32 BC–30 BC), More

Children: Cleopatra Selene II, Caesarion, Ptolemy Philadelphus, Alexander Helios

SINO SI CLEOPATRA

Answer:

Si Cleopatra ay reyna ng Ehipto, na kilala sa kasaysayan at pagsasadula bilang tagahanga ni Julius Caesar at kalaunan bilang asawa ni Mark Antony. Si Cleopatra ay naging soberanya sa pagkamatay ng kanyang ama, si Ptolemy. Maliit na batang babae ni Haring Ptolemy XII Auletes, si Cleopatra ay tiyak na magiging huling reyna ng tradisyon ng Macedonian na namamahala sa Ehipto sa pagitan ng pagkamatay ni Alexander the Great noong 323 BCE at ang pagpapalawig nito ng Roma noong 30 BCE. Ang linya ay itinatag ng pangkalahatang Ptolemy ni Alexander, na naging Haring Ptolemy I Soter ng Ehipto. Si Cleopatra ay taga-Macedonian na pabagsak at halos wala, kung mayroon man, ang dugong Egyptian, kahit na ang Classical na manlilikha na si Plutarch ay nag-compose na siya lamang sa kanyang tahanan ang nagpunta sa paraan upang matuto ng Egyptian at, para sa mga kadahilanang pampulitika, nag-istilo sa kanyang sarili bilang bagong Isis, isang titulong kumilala sa kanya mula sa dating Ptolemaic na soberanong si Cleopatra III, na nagpahayag din na siya ang naninirahan na halimbawa ng diyosang si Isis. 4. HOW DID CLEOPATRA DIED/WHENIS CLEOPATRA DIED


Answer:

Rather than fall under Octavian's domination, Cleopatra died by suicide on August 12, 30 B.C., possibly by means of an asp, a poisonous Egyptian serpent and symbol of divine royalty. Octavian then executed her son Caesarion, annexed Egypt into the Roman Empire, and used Cleopatra's treasure to pay off his veterans.

16. Mahusay ba na pinuno si Cleopatra?


Cleopatra VII was actually the last independent ruler of Ancient Egypt, yet there have been arguments about whether or not she contributed to the society that she ruled.
The Last Ruler of Ancient Egypt
Cleopatra VII was the last ruler of the Macedonian / Ptolemian Dynasty that had ruled over Ancient Egypt ever since its invasion by Alexander the Great in 333 BC.Cleopatra VII added a certain amount of glamour to the Macedonian / Ptolemian Dynasty and foreign rulers were attracted about the possibility of marrying her.However although Cleopatra VII was an attractive diplomatic match when it came to marriage, who ever she married could, and actually did affect what happened to Ancient Egyptian society.
A Queen Unable to Reverse Decline
In many respects it could be argued that Cleopatra VII was unable to make any positive contributions to Ancient Egyptian society.Ancient Egyptian society was to a large extent in a long yet gradual process of decline.The aristocratic as well as the royal sections of Ancient Egyptian society were heavily influenced by Greek culture courtesy of the lasting influence of Alexander the Great's conquest of the country.
However the Greeks and the Macedonians no longer dominated the Ancient World, the Romans did.In fact the Romans had conquered not only Ancient Greece they controlled much of the Middle East and Northern Africa by the time that Cleopatra VII became the Queen of Egypt.

Cleopatra VII realised that the future of Ancient Egypt as an independent country depended upon the relationship that she developed with the Romans.
Mistakes That Led To Disaster
Cleopatra VII made mistakes with the relationships she had with the Romans, misjudgements that led to the Roman conquest of Ancient Egypt itself.She had a relationship with Julius Caesar, and then married Mark Anthony.Unfortunately for Cleopatra Mark Anthony lost his war with Augustus Caesar.Mark Anthony was killed at Actium following, which Cleopatra killed herself.Augustus then conquered Egypt.

29. Saang Pamilya galing si Cleopatra? ​


Answer:

Siya ay anak na babae ni Ptolemy XII, hari ng Egypt, at malamang na ang kanyang asawa at kapatid na si Cleopatra V, marami siyang kapatid na lalaki at babae, na kilala natin ang dalawang nakababatang kapatid na sina Ptolemy XIII Dionysus at Ptolemy XIV, dalawang nakatatandang kapatid na sina Berenice IV at Cleopatra VI at isang nakababatang Arsinoe IV. Ipinanganak siya noong 69 BC. sa Alexandria, ang kabisera ng isang independiyenteng estado, sobrang mayaman, ngunit mahina sa politika at militar. Ang pamilyang Cleopatra ay kabilang sa pamilyang Macedonian ng mga Ptolemies, na nagmula sa isa sa mga heneral na si Alexander the Great, Ptolemy, na, pagkamatay ng dakilang mananakop, ay dumating sa kapangyarihan sa Egypt.

Ang ama ni Cleopatra na si Ptolemy XII ay isang mahina at malupit na pinuno, noong 58 BC nawalan siya ng kontrol sa estado. Ang kanyang anak na babae na si Berenice ay dumating sa kapangyarihan, na nagpakasal sa kanyang pinsan, ngunit sa lalong madaling panahon ay iniutos na kiskisan siya upang makapag-asawa muli. Noong 55 BC. Nagpasya si Ptolemy XII na mabawi ang kapangyarihan sa anumang gastos at nagtagumpay siya. Si Berenice at ang kanyang asawa ay nakuha at pinaandar.
