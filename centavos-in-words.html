20 pieces of 25 centavo coins and

1 peso= four 25 centavo coin
so 4x.25=1.00

Answer:

The purse contains 17 ten centavo coins and 20 twenty-five centavo coins.

Computation:

Representation

[tex]Number\:of\:25\:centavo\:coins\:(0.25)\to \boxed{x+3}\\\\Number\:of\:10\:centavo\:coins\:(0.10)\to \boxed{x}[/tex]

Solving for the number of 10 centavo coins.

[tex]0.25(x+3)+0.10x=6.70\\0.25x+0.75+0.10x=6.70\\0.35x=6.70-0.75\\0.35x=5.95\\x=5.95/0.35=\boxed{17}[/tex]

Solving for the number of 25 centavo coins.

[tex]x+3=17+3=\boxed{20}[/tex]
