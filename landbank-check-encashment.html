1. Authorization letter for cheque encashment


Even though online payment is widely acceptable all around the world, still accepting of bank check is still part of the practice. Processing of checks have different requirements depending on the bank. The bank may be requiring a personal appearance from the holder of the check to process it. But somehow, in some situations, bank may allow it without their appearance but will surely ask for additional requirements including an authorization letter. Below is the example of a an authorization letter:  

(Date)

(Name of the branch manager or other person-in-charge)

(Position of the person-in-charge)

(Name of the bank)

(Branch Address)

Dear Mr. or Ms. (name of the person-in-charge)

Greetings! I (Name of the holder of the check), hereby authorizing (name of the person whom be processing the check) to process my check with check number (insert check number) on my behalf due to the reason that I cannot personally drop by the bank to process it because (state the reason).

Attached to this letter is my valid ID, (type of ID) with ID number (include the number). If there would be other additional requirement, you may directly reach me on through this number (insert number) and email address (insert email address).  

I am hoping for your kind consideration on this matter. Thank you very much.  

Respectfully yours,

(holder's name and signature)

5. ano ang ginagampanan at tungkulin ng landbank​


Answer:

The Land Bank of the Philippines is a government financial institution that strikes a balance in fulfilling its social mandate of promoting countryside development while remaining financially viable.

This dual function makes LANDBANK unique. The profits derived from its commercial banking operations are used to finance the Bank's developmental programs and initiatives.

Over the years, LANDBANK has successfully managed this tough balancing act as evidenced by the continued expansion of its loan portfolio in favor of its priority sectors: the small farmers and fishers, a good part of which are agrarian reform beneficiaries; micro and SMEs; agri- and aqua-projects of local government units and government-owned and controlled corporations; communications, transportation, housing, education, health care, environment-related projects, tourism, and utilities.

8. When encashing a check worth P10,000, Mrs. Garcia asked for the whole amount in P20 and P50 bills. The teller gave her 320 bills in all. How many bills of each kind did she receive?​


Answer:

Mrs. Garcia received 200 20-peso bills.

Mrs. Garcia received 120 50-peso bills

Step-by-step explanation:

We can start solving the problem using algebra. Let x be the number of 20-peso bills, and y be the number of 50-peso bills. We know that:

x + y = 320 (the total number of bills)

20x + 50y = 10,000 (the total value of the bills)

We also know that x and y are both positive integers, since they represent the number of bills.

From the first equation, we can express y in terms of x:

y = 320 - x

We can substitute this into the second equation:

20x + 50(320 - x) = 10,000

Simplifying, we get:

20x + 16000 - 50x = 10,000

-30x = -6000

x = 200

So, Mrs. Garcia received 200 20-peso bills. We can use this to find the number of 50-peso bills:

y = 320 - x = 320 - 200 = 120

Mrs. Garcia received 120 50-peso bills

22. Gawain 1 LANDBANK,GSIS,DBP


Ano ang LANDBANK?

- Ang Landbank ay isang bangkong pag-aari ng gobyerno sa Pilipinas na may espesyal na pagtuon sa paglilingkod sa mga pangangailangan ng mga magsasaka at mangingisda.

Ano ang GSIS?

- Ang Government Service Insurance System ay nagbibigay ng social security coverage sa mga empleyado sa pampublikong sektor. Opisyal nitong sinimulan ang mga operasyon nito noong 31 Mayo 1937 na ang programa ng seguro sa buhay bilang tanging negosyo nito.

Ano ang DBP?

- Ang Development Bank of the Philippines ay isang development banking na institusyon na nakatuon sa pagbuo ng bansa sa pamamagitan ng pagtulong sa mga kritikal na industriya at sektor, pagtataguyod ng entrepreneurship partikular na sa kanayunan, pagtulong sa pagbuo ng mas produktibong mga komunidad, at pagsusulong ng pangangalaga sa kapaligiran.
