ADVISORY

An advisory is a report or an official announcement that informs individuals about bad weather, diseases or serious problems. Hence, the abovementioned is an example of an Advisory. In addition, an advisory is quite similar in structure and purpose to a persuasive writing since it tries to advise or convince the reader of your findings or recommendations. In order to be successful, the researching of the topic must be balanced and thorough. It needs to address even the down sides of the advisory you are recommending, but with material to convince the reader that the benefits far outstretch any drawbacks. Hence, the goal of the DOH advisory is to ensure the health safety of Filipino people and to convey information of the possible effect of COVID-19.
