9. Based on your experience, what rules do you find restricting? List down five that restrict you? Why?​


Answer:

1. Time restraints: With a limited number of hours in a day, I find it difficult to accomplish all the tasks I have set out to do. This often restricts me from pursuing my passions or engaging in activities that are important to me.

2. Financial constraints: Money is often a major restriction when it comes to pursuing various opportunities, both professionally and personally. Without the necessary funds, I can't take advantage of certain experiences or resources that could be beneficial for growth and  development.

3. Mental barriers: My own thought processes can be a major obstacle when it comes to achieving my goals. Fear of failure and self-doubt can often prevent me from taking risks and pushing myself outside of my comfort zone.

4. Physical limitations: Whether it's due to an injury, illness, or disability, physical limitations can be a huge impediment when it comes to reaching goals and growing as a person. I have had to learn how to work around some of these issues in order to still make progress in my life.

5. Bedtime rules: I find bedtime rules to be particularly restricting because it limits the amount of time I can spend doing things that I enjoy. It also means that I have to be in bed earlier than I would like, which can reduce my productivity during the day.
