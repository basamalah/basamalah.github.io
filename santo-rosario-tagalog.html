11. Mr. Santos and Mr del Rosario are business partners How much does each receive if a profit of P130,000 is divided in the ratio of 7 to 3 in favor of the Mr Santos ​


Answer:

Mr. Santos will receive ₱91,000, while Mr. del Rosario will get ₱39,000.

[tex]\\[/tex]

Explanation:

Based from the given problem, the profit of ₱130,000 will be divided by the business partners, Mr. Santos and Mr. del Rosario, in the ratio of 7:3 favoring Mr. Santos.

[tex]\\[/tex]

Given:

Profit = ₱130,000

Mr. Santos: 7x

Mr. del Rosario: 3x

[tex]\\[/tex]

Solution:

Step 1: Find the value of x

[tex] \large \sf 7x+3x = ₱130,000[/tex]

Add the ratio

[tex] \large \sf 7x+3x = ₱130,000 \implies 10x=₱130,000[/tex]

Divide both sides by 10

[tex]\large \sf 10x=₱130,000 \implies \LARGE \frac{10x}{10} = \frac{₱130,000}{10}[/tex]

Cancel

[tex]\LARGE \sf \frac{\cancel{10}x}{\cancel{10}} = \frac{₱130,00\cancel{0}}{1\cancel{0}} \implies \large x= ₱13,000[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the amount they'll receive by multiplying the value of x by the ratio

Mr. Santos: [tex]\large \sf 7x = 7(₱13,000) = \bold{₱91,000}[/tex]

Mr. del Rosario: [tex]\large \sf 3x = 3(₱13,000) = \bold{₱39,000}[/tex]

Step 3: Check if the sum of the products matches the profit

[tex]\large \sf ₱91,000+₱39,000=130,000[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

The sum of the products matches the profit, therefore, Mr. Santos will indeed receive ₱91,000, while Mr. del Rosario will get ₱39,000.

28. what is the importance of santo rosario chruch of camiguin?​


Answer:

The Santo Rosario Church of Camiguin, also known as the Church of the Immaculate Conception, is an important religious and cultural landmark in the island province of Camiguin in the Philippines. It is considered one of the oldest and most historic churches in the country, having been built in the late 18th century by Spanish friars.

The church is known for its unique architecture, which combines both Baroque and Byzantine styles, and its stunning interior décor, which includes intricate carvings, murals and stained glass windows.

Aside from its aesthetic and historical value, the Santo Rosario Church also holds significant religious importance to the local community. It is a place of worship and a hub for various religious activities, including processions and festivals. Many devout Catholics also consider it as a pilgrimage site, and it attracts visitors from all over the country and even from abroad.

Overall, the Santo Rosario Church of Camiguin is an important cultural and religious landmark in the Philippines, representing the country's rich history and strong Catholic heritage.
