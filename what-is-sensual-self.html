14. what is more attainable to fulfill? Sensual Pleasure or Mental tranquility? ​


Answer:

It is difficult to say which is more attainable, sensual pleasure or mental tranquility, as it can vary depending on an individual's situation and circumstances. Both can be desirable and fulfilling, but they can also be difficult to achieve and maintain.

Sensual pleasure refers to the enjoyment of physical sensations, such as taste, touch, smell, and sight. It can be very satisfying and pleasurable, but it can also be fleeting and subject to change. For example, a delicious meal may provide temporary pleasure, but it can also lead to discomfort if it is too rich or spicy.

Mental tranquility, on the other hand, refers to a state of calm and peace of mind. It can be very satisfying and fulfilling, but it can also be difficult to achieve and maintain in the face of life's challenges and stresses. It can require mental discipline and effort to cultivate, and it can be easily disrupted by negative thoughts and emotions.

In the end, it may depend on an individual's priorities, values, and circumstances. Some people may find sensual pleasure to be more attainable and fulfilling, while others may prioritize mental tranquility. It is ultimately a personal decision.
