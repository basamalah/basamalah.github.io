If 20kg is 25% of the owner.

Remember that " is " represents equal sign and " of " represents multiplication.

[tex] \\ [/tex]

Solving ,

[tex] \boxed{ \begin{array}{}20 = 25\% × owner \\ \\ 20 ÷ 25 \% = owner \\ \\ 20 ÷ 0.25 = owner \\ \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \boxed{ 80 = owner} \\ \\ \end{array}}[/tex]

[tex] \\ [/tex]
