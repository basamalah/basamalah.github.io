19. prayer for online class


Answer:

Lord, we offer to you our class today.

We pray that through your Divine Guidance,

we would learn how to listen attentively to the inputs of our teacher.

May we value each other’s contributions as a building block towards harmony and peace.

Grant that we recognize YOU in each of our classmates and teachers.

As we leave for home, we pray that you keep us safe from harm and illnesses.

And we would be able to apply correctly what we have learned from school.

All these we ask in your powerful name.

Amen.

28. short opening prayer before online class


In every event, it must be started by praying. The aim is to ask that the event that we held out run smoothly and be given blessings from God. And one of events or activities started with praying is school. There will be one student or teacher who leads the prayer.

Here is an example of a short opening for prayer.

In the name of God, the most gracious and the most merciful. Praise be to the Lord of the worlds. I seek refuge in You from the knowledge that does not benefit, from a heart that is not humble, from a soul that is not satisfied, and from a prayer that is not heard (not answered).

Thank you for the blessings given today so that we can learn together again. O our Lord, make our knowledge useful knowledge, teach us what is useful for us and add knowledge to us. make my affairs easy for me, and remove the stiffness from my tongue, so that they may understand my words.

May God bless my mother, God bless my father, God bless my sisters, God bless my brothers, God bless my teachers, God bless me and make me to be a good child.

And at the end of the study, we must also pray. This is an example of prayer after studying:

O our God, thank you for what you have given us in today's lesson. Make the knowledge we get today useful. Thank You Lord that you have been with us throughout this lesson And that you are with us right now. Inspire us as we leave this place To love and serve You always.

Indeed, I entrust to You what You have taught me, so return it to me when I need it. Don't make me forget it. O Lord of nature.

30. short opening prayer before online class start​


There is a short opening prayer before the online class has started​.

We offer you our study class today. We pray that through your blessing, We will learn how to listen and pay attention to the inputs of our teacher or others. May we appreciate their effort in imparting their knowledge to us. May we participate actively in the class, discussions, and activities to learn more.As we begin this meeting, we come to you today asking for your blessing, wisdom, and support. Let us grow closer as a group and nurture the bonds of community.Give us peace of mind as I prepare for this study time available to us. It gives us insight that we might understand what we are studying and help us remember it when it comes.Head us through this day in all we do, all we say, and all we feel. We will give you our class. We will offer you all the teachers and staff who work in our school. We give you all the children who learn here. We pray for our school, and we hope our school can be a place of great adventure, creativity, and discovery.Your name is at the most important than everything. We adore you. We pray that your name is blessed now and forever, in good times and bad, from east to west. Your name is worthy of praise. Your name is mighty.We pray for your wisdom over their lives, work, livelihood, and families. Guide them with your blessing. May they be empowered by your Spirit to see their students' needs and inspire those under their care. We bring our students' parents, guardians, and caregivers before you.
