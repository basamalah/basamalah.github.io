1. angat hydroelectric plant in norzagaray bulacan​


Answer:

The Angat Hydroelectric Plant is a power plant located in Norzagaray, Bulacan, Philippines. It is a hydroelectric power plant that generates electricity from the water flow of the Angat River, which is supplied by the Angat Dam. The power plant has a capacity of 246 megawatts and is operated by the National Power Corporation (NPC).

The Angat Dam, which supplies water to the power plant, is a multi-purpose dam that serves as a major source of water for Metro Manila and nearby provinces. It is also a tourist attraction that offers activities such as fishing, boating, and camping.

The Angat Hydroelectric Plant plays a significant role in the power generation of the Luzon grid, which supplies electricity to the island of Luzon. It is also a renewable energy source that helps reduce the country's dependence on fossil fuels and contributes to efforts to mitigate the effects of climate change.

21. Responsibilities of brgy tanod


BARANGAY TANOD

There are few other names for barangay tanod. It is also called as barangay police officer. Sometimes it is called as BPSO, which stands for barangay public safety officer or barangay peacekeeping and security officer, or barangay police safety officer. The barangay tanod is the lowest level of law enforcement officer in the Philippines.

RESPONSIBILITIES OF BARANGAY TANOD

Given that the barangay tanod is the lowest level of law enforcement officer in the Philippines, then we can infer that the barangay tanod's responsibilities is mainly concerned in peace-keeping.

Here are the responsibilities of barangay tanod:

Assists in crime prevention and promotion of public safety; Does or conducts patrol / ronda in the barangay; Responsible in reporting any illegal activities in the barangay; They also monitor potential or suspicious people who may cause harm in the barangay;They can also perform other functions as may be directed by the Punong Barangays.
