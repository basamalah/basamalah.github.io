15. the martinez family went to hawii on june 15, 1991 and came back on december 15, 2009.how many years and months did they stay in hawaii?


Answer:

18 years, 6 months

Step-by-step explanation:

This one's quite simple.

2009-1991 = 18 years.

Since they returned past July 15, 2009, that means they were there more than 18 years.

Assigning number to months, December is 12, June is 6, so

12-6 = 6 months.

Since all the dates are in the 15th, it means that they didn't extend anymore days.

So, they stayed in Hawaii for 18 years, 6 months.
