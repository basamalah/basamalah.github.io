16. Mood,Idea,Message,Reflection of mountain and staurolyte 1997 by Ronald Davis​


Answer

*The answer to this question is more on personal opinion. So, you can use this as a guide to answering it:

Mountain and Staurolite (or Staurolyte), 1997 by Ronald Davis​

Mood

Boring, dull, and lifeless but later becomes interesting

Idea

The idea is to have the audience thinking, "what is the relationship between the mountain and the Staurolite?"

Message

Always learn to analyze things carefully before criticizing.

Reflection

A staurolite is a type of mineral found in metamorphic rocks on mountain ranges. Metamorphic rocks are rocks that changed from their original properties. So, what I experience in this digital painting is the change in its mood. It was boring at first but later became interesting. I think the mountain is made up of the staurolites.
