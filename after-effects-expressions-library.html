15. It is quite noisy at home, ___ I finish my homework in the library after classes. ​


Answer:

SoIt is quite noisy at home, so I finish my homework in the library after classes. ​ ⇔ "so" is a conjunction used to connect the reason, solution, and explanation.#CarryOnLearning

Answer:

so

It is quite noisy at home, so I finish my homework in the library after classes.

Explanation:

Let's understand what CONJUNCTIONS are!Conjunctions are a part of speech that link and connect words, phrases, and clauses in a sentence.Conjunctions help with the overall flow of a sentence as it helps add more detail and thought to it.There are three (3) kinds of conjunctions: coordinating, subordinating, and correlative.Example: Cassandra made some food because she was hungry.  &  Although times are rough, we must fight hard and stay strong.

Lets's understand the COORDINATING CONJUNCTIONS!Coordinating conjunctions connect two independent clauses.These kinds of conjunctions use the FANBOYS- for, and, nor, but, or, yet, and so.If we observe the sentence above "It is quite noisy at home, so I finish my homework in the library after classes.", we can see that the underlined group of words are independent clauses.REMEMBER! Add a comma before the coordinating conjunction.Example: Mike prepared a gift for Norton, for it was his birthday.

Let's understand the Coord. Conjuction "so"!"so" as a coordinating conjunction is used to show results or decisions.If we observe the sentence above "It is quite noisy at home, so I finish my homework in the library after classes.", we can see that 'so' introduced finishing homework at the library as a result/decision of the noise at home.
