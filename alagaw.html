2. Saan matatagpuan ang alagaw dito sa Pilipinas?


Answer:

Alagao or Adiyo is a small tree species with hairy and fragrant leaves. These are often used ingredients in cooking.

Identity

Alagao with scientific name Premna odorata, is known in English as Fragrant Premna. Here in the Philippines, the alagao is called lassi of the Igorot guachal, tibangñgen of Bontok and atiñgi of Gaddang.

What happened to you

Alagao is a kind of small tree with hair and fragrance leaf. It takes to meters up. These wide and slightly rounded leaves reach to inches long. Under each leaf are short hairs with bad odor when crushed. Its flowers are pale green cluster. Purple fruits are round and known and are at five millimeters.

Content of ingredients

Alagao leaves contain essential oils that give it a typical smell. It has glycoside catalpol and asystasioside.

Characteristics of the parts

The leaves and the growing flowers are used as sweat and medicine for cough or gastritis.

Benefits of the plant

Alagao is known by Kapampangan because its bud leaves are used to cooking simmer and bopis.

Some of the native uses of Alagao are the following:

• For the cold.

Boiled Alagao leaf mixed with a little sugar and calamansi helps in rubbing phlegm caused by coughing.

• For the gastritis.

For the elderly, the boiled Alagao soup is enough. For younger, Alagao leaves will be crushed and mixed with some coconut oil before applying it to the child's stomach.

• For the headache.

The crushed leaves are being applied to the forehead and its edges.

• For the parasites.

Boiled Alagao tops are good against worms. It is also being applied to the skin so as not to be bitten by flea and lice.

• To easily urinate.

Alagao leaves are just laid on the bladder so it's easy to pee.

• Another one of the effective uses of Alagao is the ability to cleanse and kill microbes. Using this baby bathing and washing for girls.

• It is also believed that chewing the roots of Alagao can improve heart health.

• Alagao soup can allegedly help with tuberculosis. Because of this, Alagao is one of the ingredients of seven-seven tea including banaba, guava, pandan, manga, licorice, and cilantro.

Local studies

According to Alagao studies published on fairs that tackle herbs, found out that this plant are several qualities that can be cultivated for commercial medicine:

Beauty of the skin. Acetoside located in Alagao leaf has been proven to motivate the increase of skin collagen. This is a great help to those who want to lose their scar after severe wound or want to avoid skin wrinkles. Diosmetin is also one of the glycoside from Alagao that minimize varicose veins and hemorrhoids.

Microbes and parasitic killer. Alagao juice prevents increasing Escherichia coli, a type of bacteria that can cause severe abdominal pain and diarrhea. Alago was also recognized as one of New Caledonia's herbal medicines which is good against the cause of leishmaniasis. Alagao can also prevent the spread of virus that can cause vesicular stomatitis to farm animals.

Possible cure for cancer and liver disease. Malaysia's 29 plants are one of the 29 plants that canceled cancer cells when they were exposed to sunlight. According to another study, Alagao is also as effective as silymarin in liver protection.

Supply

Here in the Philippines, Alagao is located in the forests of Batan, in northern Luzon like the mountain of Banahaw and the islands of Mindanao.
