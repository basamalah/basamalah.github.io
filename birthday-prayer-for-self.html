26. How do prayer and self reflection help you to be holy? ​


Answer:

It intend to bring you into a quiet place with God where you can intimately converse with him about your experiences and emotions.

Spiritual growth is a long process of learning to abide in the presence of God. When you meet Jesus, you aren’t done. Instead, you begin on a lifelong journey of learning to become more and more like him.

A necessary part of this process is taking an honest inventory of your life to see where you need to seek transformation through the power of the Spirit. Self-reflection is a powerful tool for you to grow and encounter God in the inner workings of your heart. It may not come naturally to those living in the modern world, but it is a spiritual practice that can powerfully deepen your relationship with God.

29. Compose a prayer asking for the guidance of the Holy Spirit in the process of self-assessment, self-reflection, self-disclosure and self-development.


Answer:

In the name of Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit, Amen.

Father in Heaven, I ask for your guidance, peace, and comfort, especially during this time of the COVID-19 pandemic. A lot have changed and a lot have been affected in our daily battles with it. I thank you, Lord that you have continued to protect me and my family. Thank you also for your daily provision, your strength, and your love for us.

Lord, right now I am in the process of assessing myself, knowing myself better, and understanding my personality traits, hopes, and dreams. It has been a rigorous process for me since I have not truly figured out what I wanted to do moving forward. However, I trust in your ways that if I seek you first, you would be able to give me everything that I ask. I ask the Holy Spirit to come into my life and offer me guidance and direction as I continue with living a good Christian life. I seek for your leading on how to deal with the people around me, my family, my friends, and neighbors, so that I can be a better citizen of my country. I also ask you to guide me in my studies so that I will be an excellent student.

Dear God, I humbly call upon your name to bestow on me the wisdom of being your good and faithful servant. Through your guidance, I wish to pursue a better life, to grow as a person, and to be strong in my mind and spirit so that I am able to face challenges in a positive way. Please also help me to acknowledge that I have limitations so I may know that you are God, you are all-knowing, and all-powerful.

Lord, let me be your instrument in making the world a better place. Show me ways to extend my help to those who are in need. Allow me to find opportunities to give to the needy and vulnerable. Please offer me the ability to be empathic so that I will be able to lend a helping hand and an ear to those who need it. Let me also be a wise counsel to anyone who wants receive wise words. Use me, mold me, guide me, and walk beside me. My life is yours and I give everything back for your glory and honor. In Jesus’ name, Amen.

30. make your own prayer for the priest and your self (English)​


Answer:

Gracious and loving God, we thank you for the gift of our priests.

Through them, we experience your presence in the sacraments.

Help our priests to be strong in their vocation.

Set their souls on fire with love for your people.

Grant them the wisdom, understanding, and strength they need to follow in the footsteps of Jesus.

Inspire them with the vision of your Kingdom.

Give them the words they need to spread the Gospel.

Allow them to experience joy in their ministry.

Help them to become instruments of your divine grace.

We ask this through Jesus Christ, who lives and reigns as our Eternal Priest.
