x + y = 245

-y = -45

y = 45

24. Learning Task 1: Give the 6 steps and each meaning in making a project plan. (enhancing/decorating bamboo, wood, and metal products).​


[tex] \bold{ANSWER:}[/tex]

There are 6 parts of a project plan such as follows...

1. Name of Project – the project to be done.

2. Objectives – the reason for making the project.

3. Materials – the list of the materials and their description.

4. Design or Drawing of the Project – a illustration of the project to be done.

5. Procedure – steps in making the project accompanied by illustration for clarity and understanding.

6. Evaluation – check the quality of the finished product. A scorecard or a rubric is used for evaluating the project. It consists of a set of criteria with questions to be answered by the student, the teacher and the parents.
