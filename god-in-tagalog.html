10. our deep faith in god was adopted from the portuguese in tagalog


Answer:

The Philippines has its belief and faith in God, not because of the Portuguese group. Instead, we Filipinos adopt these values after the expansion and colonization of the Spaniards in the year March 21, 1521. The remarkable person behind the discovery of the Philippines is an expeditioner and navigator born Portuguese Ferdinand Magellan. Therefore Magellan serves and sails for the country of Spain. After Discovering the Island of the Philippines (Las Filipinas), Magellan immediately lay and discussed the idea and approach of catholicism to the Filipinos. The reason why days after the arrival of Spaniards, the first-ever mass in the Philippines happened and was held on March 31, 1521, in the place called "Mazaua".

16. describe gods and godesses(lahat sila kahit tagalog)​


Answer:

god and goddess, generic terms for the many deities of ancient and modern polytheistic religions. Such deities may correspond to earthly and celestial phenomena or to human values, pastimes, and institutions, including love, marriage, hunting, war, and the arts. While some are capable of being killed, many are immortal. Although they are always more powerful than humans, they are often described in human terms, with all the flaws, thoughts, and emotions of humans.
