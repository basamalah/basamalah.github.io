20. Which equation represents choosing the first 6 players in a game of volleyball from a choice of 10 players?


C(10,6) = 10! : 6! (10-6)!  or C (10,6) = P(10,6) : 6!

Solution:

We have to choose the first 6 players in a volleyball game from a choice of 10 players.

We make use of a combination

C(n, r) =     n! : r!(n - r)!

where n = 10 and r = 6

C(10, 6) = 10! : 6! (10 - 6)!

or (another answer)

Recall that:

P(n-r) = n! : (n-r)!

C (n,r) = P (n,r) : r!

C (10,6) = P(10,6) : 6!
