23. Delilah does 184 jumping jacks in 4 minutes. She does her jumping jacks at a constant rate. How many jumping jacks can Delilah do per minute? jumping jacks


Let x be the number of jumping jacks Delilah can do per minute.

184 : 4 = x : 1

(184)(1) = (x)(4)

184 = 4x

184/4 = 4x/4

46 = x


Therefore, Delilah can do 46 jumping jacks per minute.
