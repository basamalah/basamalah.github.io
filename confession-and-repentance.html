4. Difference from True repentance and selfish repentance ​


Answer:

True Repentance is often characterized by a godly anger about the terrible nature of sin. This zealous indignation is concerned with God’s glory and the flourishing of the image of God in humanity. True Repentance does not regret parting ways with sin, hates sin and accepts godly counsel and accountability.

Selfish Repentance is to be concerned excessively or exclusively with oneself. Selfishness is one of the biggest obstacles to having the kind of close relationship with God and inspiring relationships with other people that many of us desire.
