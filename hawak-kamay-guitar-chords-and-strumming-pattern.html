29. Which string of a guitar vibrates fastest when strummed


Answer:

Answer: The thinnest string or s1 has thehighest frequency and has the fastest vibration when strummed. The faster the vibration, the faster the frequency the higher the pitch. The highness or lowness of sound is known as thepitchof a sound or a musical note

Answer:

E or the First String.

Explanation:

Notice that when we strum the thinnest string, we hear higher pitch. This is because, thinner strings vibrate faster resulting into higher frequency. Factors such as length, tension and density also play a role in string's vibration. But in standard tuning, the first string vibrates fastest.
