25. In the next round Leila is supposed to pick a number between 1 and 10 and follow the same instructions. She gives her final result as 108. Julius immediately replies: “Hey, you cheated!” Describe how Julius knew that Laila cheated?


If Leila followed the same instructions for picking a number between 1 and 10, multiplying it by 2, adding 5, multiplying the result by 50, adding 1769, and then subtracting her birth year, the final result should always be the same regardless of the number she picked between 1 and 10. This is because the last step, which is subtracting her birth year, is a constant number that does not change based on the number she picked.

Therefore, if Leila's final result is 108, and Julius knows Leila's birth year, he can easily calculate the number Leila picked by working backward through the steps.

First, he can subtract Leila's birth year (let's say it's 1995) from 108, which gives him 108 - 1995 = -1887.

Then, he can add 31 to -1887 (since 31 is the constant result of the last step), which gives him -1856.

Next, he can divide -1856 by 100, which gives him -18.56. This means that Leila must have picked a decimal number, which is not possible since she was asked to pick a whole number between 1 and 10.

Therefore, Julius can conclude that Leila cheated and did not follow the instructions properly.
