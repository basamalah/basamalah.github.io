10. A tablespoon of syrup is about 10 ml. how many liters of syrup can a tablespoon hold?


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

1000 ml / 1 L : 10 ml / x L

1000/1 = 10/x

multiply both side by x

1000/1 x = 10

or

1000x = 10

divide both side by 1000

x=10/1000

10/1000 = 0.01

x=0.01
