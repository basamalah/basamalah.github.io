Answer:

The amount of storage space that 170 songs will take up on your device depends on the file size of the songs and the format in which they are downloaded. On average, a song in MP3 format will take up approximately 5 megabytes (MB) of storage space, so 170 songs would take up around 850 MB of space. If the songs are in a higher quality format like WAV or FLAC, they may be larger in size and take up more storage space.

In general, the size of a file is determined by the quality of the file and the amount of data it contains. Higher quality audio files will be larger in size because they contain more data, while lower quality audio files will be smaller in size because they contain less data. The size of a file can also be affected by the type of compression used to create it. Some file formats, like MP3, use lossy compression, which removes some of the data from the original file in order to reduce its size. Other file formats, like WAV and FLAC, use lossless compression, which does not remove any data from the original file. Lossless compressed files will be larger in size than their lossy compressed counterparts because they retain all of the data from the original file.

27. 1. The Google Play Music allows member to download songs for P150.00 each after paying a monthly service charge of P500.00. The total monthly C(x) of the service in peso is C(x) = 500+ 150x, where x is the number of songs downloaded. a) Find the inverse of the function. b) How many songs were downloaded if the member's monthly bill is P1550.00?​


Answer:

a) The inverse of the given function is [tex]\bold{C^{-1}(x)=\dfrac{x-500}{150}}[/tex].

b) A total of 7 songs.

Problem

As stated in the word problem, a Google Play Music subscriber can download songs for 150 pesos apiece for a monthly fee of 500.00 pesos. The function that describes the total monthly service is [tex]C(x)=500+150x[/tex], where [tex]x[/tex] is the number of songs downloaded.

We are required to find (a) the inverse of the function [tex]C(x)[/tex] and (b) determine the maximum number of songs a subscriber can download for a monthly bill of 1,550.00 pesos.

Solution

SOLVING FOR (A)

The function we have is [tex]C(x)=500+150x[/tex]. To get its inverse, let [tex]C(x)[/tex] be [tex]y[/tex].

[tex]\Rightarrow\ y=500+150x[/tex]

Then, interchange [tex]x[/tex] and [tex]y[/tex].

[tex]\Rightarrow\ x=500+150y[/tex]

Afterward, isolate the [tex]y[/tex].

[tex]\Rightarrow\ x-500=150y[/tex]

[tex]\\\Rightarrow\ \dfrac{x-500}{150}=y[/tex]

[tex]\\\rm{\text{Using symmetric property of equality,}}[/tex]

[tex]\\\therefore\ \bold{y=\dfrac{x-500}{150}}[/tex]

Therefore, [tex]\bold{C^{-1}(x)=\dfrac{x-500}{150}}[/tex].

[tex]\tt{Note:}[/tex] The symbol for an inverse function is a function with -1 above such as [tex]f^{-1}(x)[/tex] and [tex]G^{-1}(x)[/tex].

SOLVING FOR (B)

Given

[tex]C(x)=500+150x[/tex]If [tex]C(x)=1,550[/tex]

Required

[tex]x[/tex], the number of songs

Solution

Substitute the given value into the function [tex]C(x)[/tex].

[tex]\Rightarrow\ 1550=500+150x[/tex]

Evaluate [tex]x[/tex].

[tex]\Rightarrow\ 1550-500=150x[/tex]

[tex]\\\Rightarrow\ 1050=150x[/tex]

[tex]\\\Rightarrow\ \dfrac{1050}{150}=x[/tex]

[tex]\\\Rightarrow\ \bold{x=7}[/tex]

Since [tex]x=7[/tex], this means that a subscriber with a monthly fee of 1,550 pesos can download 7 songs.
