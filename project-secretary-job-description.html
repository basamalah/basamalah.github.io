2. One secretary can do a particular typing job in 5 hour and other secretary can do the same job 4 hour. How many hours will take if the job is done by the two secretaries working together?


Answer:

It will take 2 ²/₉ hours to do the same job when working together.

More people working on the same job lessens the number of working hours than when a person works alone.

Step-by-step explanation:

Work Rate:

First secretary: 1/5

Second secretary: 1/4

The job: 1 (typing job)

Number of hours to finish the job: x hours

Working together: 1/x

Equation:

1/5 + 1/4 = 1/x

LCD: (4)(5)(x)

(4)(5)(x) [1/5 + 1/4 = 1/x] (4)(5)(x)

4x + 5x = 20

9x = 20

9x/9 = 20/9

x = 20/9

x = 2 ²/₉ hours
