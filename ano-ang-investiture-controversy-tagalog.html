7. b. Hanapin ang kahulugan o nagawa ng mga sumusunod na konsepto.1. Investiture controversy2. Pope Gregory 73 Henry 44. Concordat of worms 5. Santo papa​


Answer:

1. Investiture controversy - it was a conflict between the Pope and the Holy Roman Emperor over the right to invest bishops with symbols of their office. The controversy lasted from 1075 to 1122 CE.

2. Pope Gregory 7 - He was a medieval pope who reigned from 1073 to 1085. He is known for his role in the Investiture Controversy and his efforts to reform the Church.

3. Henry 4 - He was a Holy Roman Emperor who reigned from 1056 to 1106. He was involved in the Investiture Controversy and had a conflict with Pope Gregory 7.

4. Concordat of Worms - It was a treaty signed in 1122 CE that ended the Investiture Controversy. It established the compromise that bishops would be elected by church officials but would receive their symbols of office from the emperor.

5. Santo papa - This is the Italian term for "Holy Father," which is a title used to refer to the Pope, the leader of the Roman Catholic Church.

26. Ano nga ba ang Iconoclastic Controversy


a dispute over the use of religious images (icons) in the Byzantine Empire in the 8th and 9th centuries. The Iconoclasts (those who rejected images) objected to icon veneration for several reasons, including the Old Testament prohibition against images in the Ten Commandments (Exodus 20:4) and the possibility of idolatry. The defenders of the use of icons insisted on the symbolic nature of images and on the dignity of created matter.


27. ano ang ibig sabihin ng investoture controversy


Investiture Controversy

Isang tunggalian ng interes sa simbahan at pamahalaan kaugnay ng mga ideya ni Papa Gregory VII noong 11th Century. Hindi nagustuhan ni Haring Henri IV (Hari ng Germany) ang ideya ng Papa. Dahil dito humingi ng tulong ang hari sa mga obispong German na pababain sa pwesto si Papa Gregory VII. Bilang tugon, idineklara ng Papa na ekskomulgado si Henry IV sa simbahang Katoliko. Hiniling ng Hari na alisin ang ekskomulgasyon sa kaniya ngunit nang hindi ito ginawa ng Papa, tumayo si Henri IV nang nakayapak sa labas ng palasyo ng Canossa sa Italy ng tatlong araw noong 1077. Hiniling ng Hari na alisin ang parusanv ekskomulgasyon sa kanya. Bagaman pinatawad din kalaunan ng Papa ang hari, ang nasabing insidente ay lalong nagpatibay sa kapangyarihan ng simbahan. Upang malutas ang isyu, nagkaroon ng kasunduan ang Simabahan at si Haring Henri IV na tinawag na "Concordat of Worms" na kumilala sa dalawang tungkulin ng Obispo bilang lider-espiritwal ng Simbahan at Panginoong maylupa. Kinilala nito ang Simbahan bilang nagsasariling institusyon na pinamumunuan ng Papa na hindi napapasailalim sa sinumang hari.
