1. Characteristic of Sports?


Answer:

1. Physical Training

All forms of athletic competition require physical strength, stamina, speed, skill, and technique. The individual who displays these qualities will be able to win prizes or awards.

2. Success or Loss

Some forms of athletic competition have winners and losers. Some sports have playoffs while others do not.

3. Mental Training

Mental training involves concentration, discipline, courage, perseverance, self-control, and teamwork. These traits are necessary in order to succeed at any level of athletics.

4. Enjoyment

Some people participate in sports for the pleasure they find from exercising their bodies and developing themselves mentally. Others enjoy watching others compete because it gives them a thrill to see people show what they can do with their minds and bodies. No matter why they play or watch, most athletes/spectators enjoy being part of a team and having fun together.
