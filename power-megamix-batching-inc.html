16. A ceramic artist can shape a pot in 3 minutes. It takes 10 minutes to fire one batch of pots in the kiln. If there are p pots in a batch, which expression represents the time it takes to shape and fire one batch of pots? How long will it take the potter to form and fire a batch of 10 pots?


3 minutes to shape 1 pot

10 minutes to fire 1 batch of pots

Let p represent the number of pots in a batch.

Expression : 3p + 10

Calculation : 3p + 10 = 3 ( 10 ) + 10 = 30 + 10 = 40

It will take 40 minutes for the potter to form and fire a batch of 10 pots.

Number of pots: p

Total time to form a batch: f(p)

(a) Expression (or function):

3p + 10

or

f(p) = 3p + 10

(b) Total time when p = 10

f(10) = 3(10) + 10

f(10) = 30 + 10

f(10) = 40

Total time for a batch of 10 pots: 40 minutes
