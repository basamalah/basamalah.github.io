3. why is it important to use square tiles and estimating the area of the figure.please pahelp Po​


Area is measured in "square" units. The area of a figure is the number of squares required to cover it completely, like tiles on a floor. Area of a square = side times side. Since each side of a square is the same, it can simply be the length of one side squared.

21. Differentiate adhesive and sealant?​


Answer:

Adhesives are generally made to make two surfaces stick together so that the surfaces can't be separated. Sealants have lower strength and high elongation/flexibility and are not used to bond materials together while adhesives are meant to stick two things together by adhesion.
