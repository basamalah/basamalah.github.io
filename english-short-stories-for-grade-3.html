Once upon a time, in a vast, lush forest, there were many different kinds of frogs. Each of them had a unique appearance, habitat, and behavior.

The first kind of frog was the Poison Dart Frog. It was a small, brightly colored frog that lived in the rainforest. It was known for its toxic skin, which could kill predators or even humans who touched it. The Poison Dart Frog was also very fast, which helped it escape from danger.

The second kind of frog was the Tree Frog. It was a small, green or brown frog that lived in trees.
