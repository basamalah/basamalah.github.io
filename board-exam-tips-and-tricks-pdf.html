17. enumerate the 7 search engine tricks and tips​


1. Use Advanced Search Operators: Advanced search operators like “site:”, “intitle:”, and “inurl:” allow you to search for specific types of content.

2. Use Quotation Marks: Using quotation marks allows you to search for an exact phrase, instead of individual words.

3. Use Wildcards: Wildcards are a great way to search for multiple variations of a word or phrase.

4. Use Related Searches: Related searches are a great way to explore similar topics and find results related to your search.

5. Use Search Filters: Search filters are a great way to narrow down the results of your search to only the most relevant.

6. Use the Date Range Filter: The date range filter allows you to limit your search results to a specific time period.

7. Use Multiple Search Engines: Using multiple search engines can help you get a more comprehensive view of your topic.
