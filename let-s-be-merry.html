6. What is the linear speed of a child on a merry-go-round of radius 3.0 m that has an angular velocity of 4.0 rd/s?A.) 0.75 m/sB.) 10 m/sC.) 12 m/sD.) 13.0 m/s


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & r = \text{3.0 m} \\ & \omega = \text{4.0 rd/s} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the linear speed.

[tex]\begin{aligned} v & = \omega r \\ & = \text{(4.0 rd/s)(3.0 m)} \\ & = \boxed{\text{12 m/s}} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the answer is C.) 12 m/s.

15. A merry-go-round moves in a circle at a constant speed. Is the merry-go-round accelerating? Explain your answer.


Good Day

Problem:

A merry-go-round moves in a circle at a constant speed. Is the merry-go-round accelerating? Explain your answer.

Merry go round is accelerating even the speed is constant.

Acceleration is the rate of change in velocity over a certain period of time.

When the speed is constant, it doesn't mean that its velocity is constant. Speed is a scalar quantity it doesn't have direction unlike velocity which is a vector quantity and it has a direction. In merry go round, the velocity (direction) is always changing even if its speed is constant which means it is accelerating.

Only at a constant velocity you have a zero acceleration.

23. Suzy is holding on to the outer edge of a merry –go round that is 1.8 m in diameter. Susan’s weight is 40 kg, and the velocity of the merry-go –round is 2.5 m/s. What is the centripetal force? Please help me


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The formula for the centripetal force of an object is

[tex]\boxed{F_{c} = \frac{mv^{2}}{r}}[/tex]

where:

[tex]F_{c}[/tex] = centripetal force

m = mass

v = velocity

r = radius

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Listing the given values

m = 40 kg

v = 2.5 m/s

r = d/2 = 1.8 m / 2 = 0.9 m

Solving for the centripetal force

[tex]F_{c} = \dfrac{mv^{2}}{r}[/tex]

[tex]F_{c} = \dfrac{(\text{40 kg})(\text{2.5 m/s})^{2}}{\text{0.9 m}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{F_{c} = \text{277.78 N}}[/tex]

Therefore, the centripetal force is 277.78 N.
