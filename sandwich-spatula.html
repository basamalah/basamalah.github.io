1. what is the use of spatula in making a sandwich​


Answer:

h

Explanation:

It is used to apply food spreads, such as deviled ham, peanut butter, jelly, egg salad, butter, dressings, and many other spreads over bread slices.

Answer:

A small flat, round bladed utensil that is serrated on one side and smooth on the other, appearing somewhat like a round spatula. It is used to apply food spreads, such as deviled ham, peanut butter, jelly, egg salad, butter, dressings, and many other spreads over bread slices.


2. ways how to clean and sanitize sandwich spatula​


Answer:

1. fill the sink with soapy water.

2. drop the spatula into the water and let soak for a minute or so.

3. scrub the spatula with a cleaning rag and rinse the spatula with hot water.

4. dry the spatula with a clean rag to get rid of any excess moisture
