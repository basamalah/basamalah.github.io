8. waves that are 4m long pass a lighthouse with a speed of 1m/s.what is frequency of the wave​


Answer:

0.25 Hz.

Explanation:

frequency = wave speed / wavelength

In this case, the wave speed is given as 1m/s and the wavelength is 4m. So we can substitute these values into the formula:

frequency = 1 m/s / 4 m

frequency = 0.25 Hz

Therefore, the frequency of the wave is 0.25 Hz.

W = mgh = 0.1*10*1m =1 J ( 1 joule of work needed).

Neglecting air resistance, the speed the apple will hot the ground with is independent on its mass.

v = sqrt(2gh) =sqrt(2*10*1) = 4.5 m/s

Power = E/t

t needed to hit the ground is given by:

gt^2/2 = h = 1m

t = sqrt(2h/g) = sqrt(0.2) =0.45 s

So power spent until hitting the ground is 1/.45 = 2.2 W
