                 The Second Homecoming

            Rizal wanted to personally talk to Despujol concerning his Borneo colonization project. He was in Hong Kong at that time but he left the country on June 21, 1892, along with his sister Lucia. I felt really proud of his action because of his initiatives to give the landless Filipinos to at least settle in north Borneo. I wish he was not steadfast in his decision because he had no idea that a secret case had been filed against him for the anti-religious and anti-patriotic campaign.

            Rizal and Lucia arrived in Manila at noon on June 26, 1892, and on the same day, at 7:00 PM, he was able to talk with Despujol who agreed to pardon his father. On the evening of July 3rd, Rizal lead a meeting in Tondo, Manila which was attended by more than 20 Filipinos including Andres Bonifacio and Apolinario Mabini. I was so amazed by how Rizal was able to convince fellow Filipino heroes and spearhead a conference for the country.

In my opinion, they are slowly getting into awakening their patriotism and love for the country.
