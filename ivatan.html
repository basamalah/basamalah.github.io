4. What is VAKUL of Ivatans


WHAT IS A VAKUL?

> It is an umbrella-like gear for the head that protects the Ivatan farmers from the heat of the sun and the rain too. It is also usually worn by the Ivatan women.

WHAT IS IT MADE OF? It is made of Philippine date palm— a tree that is usually found in Batanes.

5. Geographical about ivatan


The Ivatan people are an Austronesian ethnolinguistic group native to the Batanes and Babuyan Islands of the northernmost Philippines. They are genetically closely-related to other ethnic groups in Northern Luzon, but also share close linguistic and cultural affinities to the Tao people of Orchid Island in Taiwan.
