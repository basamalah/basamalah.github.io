16. Give the frequency and energy of the dental X-ray used by your dentist which has a wavelength of 10 nm. *


Answer:

f = 3.0 × 10¹⁶ Hz

E = 2.0 × 10⁻¹⁷ J

Explanation:

Calculating the frequency

f = c / λ

f = 2.998 × 10⁸ m/s / 10 × 10⁻⁹ m

f = 2.998 × 10¹⁶ s⁻¹

f = 3.0 × 10¹⁶ Hz

Calculating the energy

E = hf

E = (6.626 × 10⁻³⁴ J•s)(2.998 × 10¹⁶ s⁻¹)

E = 2.0 × 10⁻¹⁷ J
