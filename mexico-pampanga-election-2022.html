10. news about the election 2022.


Ferdinand Marcos Jr. Is Bound for Presidency of the Philippines

Early counting put Mr. Marcos, the son and namesake of the country’s former dictator, more than 15 million votes ahead of his top presidential rival in one of the country’s most consequential elections in decades.

Protests break out as Philippines election returns a Marcos to presidency.

SUMMARY:

Marcos leads unofficial tally by wide margin over rivalsPoll body dismisses appeals seeking to disqualify MarcosAbout 400 anti-Marcos protesters rally outside poll commissionOfficial vote count is expected at the end of the month

Marcos, better known as "Bongbong", trounced bitter rival Leni Robredo to become the first candidate in recent history to win an outright majority in a Philippines presidential election, marking a stunning comeback by the son and namesake of an ousted dictator that has been decades in the making.

Marcos's runaway victory in Monday's election now looks certain with about98% of the eligible ballots counted in an unofficial tallyshowing he hasnearly 31 million votes, double that of Robredo.

An official result is expected around the end of the month.

Though Marcos, 64, campaigned on a platform of unity, political analysts say his presidency is unlikely to foster that, despite the margin of victory.

Many among the millions of Robredo voters are angered by what they see as a brazen attempt by the disgraced former first family to use its mastery of social media to reinvent historical narratives of its time in power.

Around 400 people, mostly students, staged a protest outside the election commission on Tuesday against Marcos and citing election irregularities.

As the vote count showed the extent of the Marcos win, Robredo told her supporters to continue their fight for truth until the next election.

"It took time to build the structures of lies. We have time and opportunity to fight and dismantle these ," she said.

PAG-AALSA SA PAMPANGA

NOONG 1583, maraming katutubo ng Pampanga ang ipinadala ng mga Espanyol upang magtrabaho sa minahan ng ginto sa Ilocos.

Hindi sila pinayagang makauwi sa panahon ng pagtatanim. Bunga nito, kinapos sa pagkain ang Pampanga at Maynila nang sumunod na taon. Sinasabing mahigit sa isanlibo ang namatay sa gutom sa Lubao, Pampanga pa lamang.

Dahil sa taggutom noong 1584, nagpasyang mag-alsa ang mga Pampango. Hiningi nila ang tulong ng mga taga-Borneo sa kanilang planong pasukin ang Maynila upang lipulin ang lahat ng Espanyol.

Sa kasawiang-palad, isang katutubong babaeng kasal sa isang sundalong Espanyol ang nagkanulo sa plano. Maraming Pampango ang inaresto at binitay.

ANG isa pang sanhi ng pag-aalsa sa Pampanga ay ang masamang trato lng tinanggap ng mga pumuputol ng troso mula sa katiwala ng trosohan sa lalawigan.

Isanlibong Pampango ang waling buwang pinagtrabaho sa ilalim ng mapang-aping kalagayan. Nag-alsa ang mga ito at inihayag ang kanilang intensyon na maghimagsik sa pamamagitan ng pagsunog sa sityo ng kanilang kampo.

Si DON FRANCISCO MANIAGO ang pinili nila bilang lider na mula sa bayan ng Mexico, Pampanga. Ngunit ang pag-aalsang ito ay walang nagawa para sa mga Pilipino dahil sa mga banta ng pagsuway ng kanilang lider at pakikipagsabwatan sa mga Espanyol.
