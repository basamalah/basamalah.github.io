Find:   [tex]S_{\infty}=?[/tex]

[tex]S_{\infty}=\frac{a_1}{1-r}\\S_{\infty}=\frac{2}{1-(-\frac{1}{4})}\\S_{\infty}=\frac{2}{1+\frac{1}{4}}\\S_{\infty}=\frac{2}{\frac{4}{4}+\frac{1}{4}}\\S_{\infty}=\frac{2}{\frac{5}{4}}\\S_{\infty}=2*\frac{4}{5}\\\boxed{S_{\infty}=\frac{8}{5}}[/tex]
