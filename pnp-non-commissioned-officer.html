5. Discuss the functions of the PLEB office in the PNP organization.​


Functions of the PLEB office in the PNP organization.​

Below are the functions of PLEB as part of the Philippine National Police Organization.

The PLEB has the authority to hear and decide citizen complaints against any uniformed member of the PNP that have been formally filed with it or directed to it. Unlike other types of cases filed with regular courts, the PLEB decides each case in its entirety.

Any citizen's complaint against PNP officers and members will be directed to the PLEB, which will act as the central receiving entity. Within three (3) days of receiving the complaint, the PLEB shall take cognizance of it or submit it to the appropriate disciplinary or adjudicatory authority.
