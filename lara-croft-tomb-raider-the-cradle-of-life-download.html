14. lara’s father is 36. he is 16 years older than four times lara’s age. how old is lara?


Answer:

Lara is 5 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Evaluate the ages of Lara and Lara's father in an algebraic sentence

Lara's age = x

Lara's father's age = 4x + 16

So the expression would be

4x + 16 = 36, find x

4x + 16 = 36

4x = 36 - 16

4x = 20

4x / 4 = 20 / 4

x = 5

Checking

Let x = 5

4x + 16 = 36

4(5) + 16 = 36

20 + 16 = 36

36 = 36 -- true
