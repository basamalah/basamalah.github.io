17. Identify the fallacy in the following statements. Explain your answer. •Kopiko is the best coffee because everyone is drinking it. You need to show evidence that you did not cheat in the last election. •If you are pro-mining, then you are supporting the destruction of the environment.​


Answer:

The fallacy in the statement "Kopiko is the best coffee because everyone is drinking it" is the argument from popularity or ad populum fallacy. This fallacy assumes that because something is popular, it must be true or correct, without providing any actual evidence or reasoning to support the claim.

The fallacy in the statement "If you are pro-mining, then you are supporting the destruction of the environment" is a false dilemma fallacy or a black-and-white fallacy. This fallacy presents only two extreme and opposing options as the only choices, without considering any nuances or other alternatives. In reality, it is possible to support responsible and sustainable mining practices that do not harm the environment.
