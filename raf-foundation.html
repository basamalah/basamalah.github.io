19. difference between shallow foundation and deep Foundation​


Answer:

Difference between Shallow and Deep Foundation For a Shallow foundation, the depth of foundation is equal to or less than its width. Whereas for Deep foundation, the depth of footing is equal to or greater than its depth. A shallow foundation is located at very low depth, whereas a deep foundation is constructed at more depth.

26. differentiate the biological foundations from the environmental foundations of human development?


Biological foundation of human development includes genetics, heredity, traits, character.

Human grow and development according to their genetic make up. They inherited the traits and characteristics of their parents. Some child have the same attitude and character to their parents as part of its genetic make up. If the parents are both good person, the tendency for the child will also become a good person in the future base on genetics. If the child have the genes of a criminal, the tendency of the child of becoming a criminal in the future is high. However, to control negative characteristics of the child also depends in the environment which nurture the child. The child will learn how to control its negative behavior because of its environment.

Environmental foundation of human developments include how the environment nurture the child. The influence of the environment on Human.

Good environment helps the child to control its negative behavior and promotes positive behavior. On the other hand, Negative environment promotes negative behavior of the child.
