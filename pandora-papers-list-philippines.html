1. List atleast three traits or attributes of *Zeus*Prometheus*Epimethius*Pandora​


Answer:

*Zeus: Zeus was the most powerful of the Greek gods and had a number of powers. His most famous power is the ability to throw lightning bolts. His winged horse Pegasus carried his lightning bolts and he trained an eagle to retrieve them. He could also control the weather causing rain and huge storms.

*Prometheus: Prometheus was known for his high and wily intelligence: he knew how to steal the fire from Hephaestus's forge and bring it back to the humans, he knew to side with Zeus and not the Titans during the Titanomachy in order to avoid being punished with the rest of the Titans and sent to Tartarus. Prometheus was also intelligent enough to know not to except gifts from the gods; this being evident during Pandora’s box legend — he told his brother Epimetheus to not accept any gift from the gods for no good would come from this — especially after they had stolen the fires and Zeus would be angry.

*Epimetheus: Before his brother could get to the all-important task of crafting mortals, Epimetheus gave all of the best characteristics to animals. Strength, speed, courage, cunning, fur and feathers - all of these traits he gave to beasts instead of men.

*Pandora: The gods gave her many traits including beauty, curiosity, charm, and cleverness. Hence her name “Pandora,” meaning “all gifted” or, alternately, “a gift to all.”

23. what is the meaning of the word pandora​


Answer:

In Greek mythology, Pandora (Greek: Πανδώρα, derived from πᾶν, pān, i.e. "all" and δῶρον, dōron, i.e. "gift", thus "the all-endowed", "all-gifted" or "all-giving") was the first human woman created by Hephaestus on the instructions of Zeus. As Hesiod related it, each god cooperated by giving her unique gifts.

Epimetheus received Pandora as a gift from the gods; a human created by the gods specifically to punish the humans. The marriage of Epimetheus and Pandora is not explicit in any source, but only implied; from their union, Pyrrha, their daughter, was born.
