9. Choose at least one maternal health concern before.during and after pregnancy, and discuss each concern. The answer the follow-up questionMATERNAL HEALTH CONCERNSBefore or Pre-During PregnancyAfter or Post-PregnancyPregnancy​


Maternal Health Concerns Before, During, and After Pregnancy

Among the many maternal health concerns before, during, and after pregnancy is the mother having certain diseases that may affect her ability to conceive, the baby, or both the mother and the child. An example of maternal health concern before pregnancy is when the mother-to-be has hepatitis. Meanwhile, one maternal health concern during pregnancy is hypertension. Lastly, a typical maternal health concern after pregnancy is postpartum depression.

Discussion on the Maternal Health Concerns Before, During, and After Pregnancy

Maternal health concern before pregnancy (pre-pregnancy)

Example: hepatitis Hepatitis is a liver infection. With hepatitis B in particular, this can be spread from a person to another via body fluids. If a woman has hepatitis and wants to conceive a baby, the risk that she may pass it on to the baby during delivery is very high.

Maternal health concern during pregnancy

Example: hypertensionThis is also known as high blood pressure. Having hypertension during pregnancy increases the risk of complications such as preeclampsia.This may lead to placental abruption or preterm birth, among others.

Maternal health concern after pregnancy (post-pregnancy)

Example: postpartum depressionThis is a serious mental illness concerning many mothers after they give birth. It may be due to hormonal changes, tiredness, or overwhelming emotions, among others. Symptoms include sadness that doesn't go away, not caring for the baby, losing interest in many things and more.
