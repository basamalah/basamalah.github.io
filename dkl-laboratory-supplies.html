4. Insufficiency of water supply it's affect on the laboratory activities performance of the H.E students in ASAFI.how to explain this? pleas help me​


Answer:

Insufficiency of water supply can have a negative impact on the laboratory activities performance of Higher Education (H.E) students in the ASAFI school. This is because many laboratory activities require a sufficient supply of water for the students to conduct experiments and carry out procedures properly. If there is not enough water available, students may be unable to complete their experiments or may be forced to use alternative methods that are less effective or accurate. This can lead to a decrease in the quality of the students' work and may affect their overall performance in the laboratory. Additionally, a lack of water can create a safety hazard in the laboratory if students are unable to properly clean and dispose of hazardous materials. Overall, insufficiency of water supply can have a detrimental effect on the laboratory activities performance of H.E students in ASAFI.
