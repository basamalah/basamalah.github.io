Question

Find the probability that the letter is "consonant" if a letter is to be selected from the word Filipino?

Answer:

b. 1/2

SOLUTION:

The word FILIPINO has 8 letters of which there are 4 consonants,

F   L   P   N

We can then solve for the probability as,

P = FO/TO

where:

P = Probability = ?

FO = Favored Outcome = 4 consonants

TO = Total Outcome = 8 letters

solve for P,

P = FO/TO  

P = 4/8

simply the fraction,

P = 1/2

[tex]\therefore \large\boxed{\text{\sf The Probability is $\frac{1}{2}$}}[/tex]
