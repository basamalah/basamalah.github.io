Hello, Love, Goodbye is a 2019 romantic drama film directed by Cathy Garcia-Molina and produced by Star Cinema. The movie features Kathryn Bernardo as Joy and Alden Richards as Ethan, two overseas Filipino workers in Hong Kong who fall in love despite their different goals and backgrounds.

The film starts with Joy arriving in Hong Kong to work as a domestic helper, leaving behind her family and boyfriend in the Philippines. She meets Ethan, a bartender who dreams of opening his own restaurant someday. Despite their initial disagreements, they soon develop feelings for each other. However, their relationship is complicated by their respective plans for the future - Joy wants to save enough money to start her own business back home, while Ethan is torn between pursuing his dream and taking care of his sick mother.

As Joy and Ethan's relationship deepens, they are forced to confront the challenges of their situation and make difficult decisions about their future together. The movie explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the struggles faced by Filipino migrant workers abroad.
