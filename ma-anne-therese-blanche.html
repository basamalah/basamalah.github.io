19. Ma. Jo Ann is 1.6 m tall. Her father is 2.0 tall. Her father casts a shadow of 5 m. How long would Ma. Jo Ann's shadow (d) be? 2.0m -5m 1.6m​


Answer:

We can use proportions to find the length of Ma. Jo Ann's shadow (d).

We know that Ma. Jo Ann's height is 1.6 m and her father's height is 2.0 m. We also know that her father's shadow is 5 m.

Using proportions, we can set up the following equation:

father's height / father's shadow = Ma. Jo Ann's height / Ma. Jo Ann's shadow

Substituting the values we know:

2.0 m / 5 m = 1.6 m / d

Simplifying:

d = (5 m x 1.6 m) / 2.0 m

d = 4 m

Therefore, Ma. Jo Ann's shadow would be 4 meters long.

2. flinch or grow pale from shock, fear, or a similar emotion.
"many people blanch at the suggestion"

Explanation:

BLANCH AT (SOMETHING):

To balk or hesitate when faced with something unpleasant. This phrase can also include or describe one's visible paleness, as to "blanch" something means to whiten it.

EXAMPLE:

All of my friends ran into the creepy haunted house, but I blanched at the sight of it.

Stella blanched at the plate of cooked ants that had been set before her.

BLANCH WITH (AN EMOTION)

To become visibly pale as a result of feeling a particular emotion.

EXAMPLE:

All of my friends ran into the creepy haunted house, but I blanched with fear when I saw it.

Stella blanched with disgust at the plate of cooked ants that had been set before her.
