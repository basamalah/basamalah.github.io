16. "Who qualifies as a Historians?"​


Answer:

Generally, historians are individuals who have studied and trained in the field of history and engage in research, teaching, writing, or other activities related to the study of the past.

To be considered a historian, one usually needs to have a formal education in history, such as a bachelor's or master's degree in history or a related field. Some historians may also hold a Ph.D. in history, which allows them to conduct original research and contribute to the field through scholarly publications.

However, being a historian is not only limited to having a degree in history. People who engage in historical research, writing, and teaching outside of academic institutions can also be considered historians. For example, journalists, genealogists, archivists, and museum professionals may also conduct research and produce historical work.

Ultimately, what qualifies an individual as a historian is their ability to conduct thorough and accurate research, analyze historical evidence, and present their findings in a way that contributes to our understanding of the past.

29. . who are qualified to vote in the philippines ?


To become able to vote in the Philippines, you need to become a registered voter. The requirements needed are:

(a) a Filipino Citizen
(b) 18 years old or older
(c) have resided in the Philippines for 1 year or more
(d) have resided in the city or municipality you will register for 6 months or more.

If you meet all this requirements, you can file in your registration at your nearest COMELEC office.
