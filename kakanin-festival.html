5. Why kakanin is called kakanin? ​


Answer:

The name kakanin is derived from two Tagalog words: “kain” (to eat) and “kanin” (rice).

Explanation:

It's an umbrella term for sweets made of glutinous rice and coconut milk, two ingredients that tropical countries like ours have in abundance. These ingredients are usually employed in one of two forms.

12. ano ang kayarian ng kakanin​


KAYARIAN

kayarian ng kakanin

Salita: kakanin

Kayarian: inuulit  

Salitang-ugat: kanin

Panlapi: ka-

Ang salitang "kakanin" ay nasa kayariang inuulit dahil binubuo ito ng pantig o bahagi ng salita na inulit. Ang isang pangngalan o pang-uri ay inuulit kung mayroon itong inulit na salita o ang pantig/bahagi lamang ng salita ang inulit.
