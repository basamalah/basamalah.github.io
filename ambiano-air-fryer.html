1. give the expanded definition for the word air fryer​


Answer:

An air fryer is a kitchen appliance that cooks food by circulating hot air around it using a convection motor. It resembles a rice cooker or pitcher that fries food without the use of electricity. There are also buttons for adjusting the temperature and time. To use an air fryer, first put your food in the basket, then set the time and temperature, and then wait for the food to cook. You can eat a variety of foods.

19. how to clean deep fat fryers​


Answer:

To clean a deep fat fryer, follow these steps:

1. Cool down the fryer: Allow the fryer to cool down completely before cleaning.

2. Drain the oil: Drain the oil from the fryer and dispose of it properly.

3. Wipe the interior: Wipe the interior of the fryer with a paper towel or cloth to remove any remaining oil or food particles.

4. Fill with warm, soapy water: Fill the fryer with warm water and add a small amount of dish soap.

5. Let it soak: Let the fryer soak for about 15-20 minutes.

6. Scrub the interior: Use a non-abrasive scrubber or brush to scrub the interior of the fryer, paying special attention to any areas with stubborn stains.

7. Rinse with clean water: Rinse the fryer with clean water to remove all soap residue.

8. Dry thoroughly: Dry the fryer thoroughly with a clean towel or paper towel.

9. Clean the exterior: Wipe down the exterior of the fryer with a damp cloth.

10. Reassemble the fryer: Once all the parts are dry, reassemble the fryer.

25. what current flows through a air fryer plugged into a 110 volt circuit if it has a resistance of 15 ohms?​


Answer:

Current is 4.4 A

Explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

R = 25 Ω

V = 110 V

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Current

Step 3: Solve for Current

Equation:

The formula for Current is given by:

I = V/R

where: I - Current

R - Resistance

V - Voltage

Solution:

Substituting the value of V and R in the formula,

I = (110 V)/(25 Ω)

I = 4.4 A
