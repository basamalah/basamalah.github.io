13. What roles of the family, students, teachers and school authorities in the campaign of the government against illegal drugs


Answer:

In the campaign against illegal drugs, the family, students, teachers, and school authorities all play important roles in supporting the government's efforts.

The family can play a vital role in preventing drug abuse by promoting healthy lifestyles and providing support and guidance to family members who may be at risk of drug abuse. Parents can educate their children about the dangers of drug use and create a positive and supportive environment that helps prevent drug use.

Students can support the government's campaign by educating themselves about the risks associated with drug use and making informed decisions about their own behavior. They can also promote drug-free lifestyles among their peers and help to create a positive and supportive school environment that discourages drug use.

Teachers and school authorities can help to support the government's campaign by providing education and information about the risks of drug use to students and their families. They can also work to create a safe and supportive school environment that promotes healthy behaviors and discourages drug use. Additionally, they can identify and support students who may be at risk of drug use and provide appropriate interventions and support services.
