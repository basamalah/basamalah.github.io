29. David were able to harvest 2,920 kgs of Lanzones and sold it to his 10 regular customers with equal number of kilograms. If the Lanzones is P15 per kilo, how much would each customer pay for the Lanzones? ​


Answer

Each customer will pay P4, 380 for the lanzones.

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we will divide 2, 920 kg to the 10 regular customers.

2, 920 / 10 = 292.

Each customer will get 292 kg of lanzones, now let's solve for the price each customer will pay for. Since the price of a kilo of lanzones is P15, we will multiply 292 kg and P15.

292 kg x P15 =  P4, 380.
