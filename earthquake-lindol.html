1. what is lindol o earthquake ​


Answer:

Earthquake is the shaking of the surface of the Earth, resulting from the sudden release of energy in the Earth’s lithosphere that creates seismic waves


2. ano ang earthquake?patulong po bakit nagkakaroon ng earthquake o lindol satin at ano ang tectonic at volcanic earthquake?​


Answer:

Ang Lindol ay sanhi ng isang mabilis na

paglabas ng enerhiya na nang-gagaling sa ilalim ng lupa. Madalas ang mga lindol sanhi ng pag-galaw ng fault sa ibabaw na bahaging mundo (crust). Ang enerhiyang lumalabas ay sumasanga sa lahat ng direksyon mula sa kanyang pinanggalingan,ang pokus, sa anyo ng mga seismikong alon.

A Volcano Tectonic Earthquake is caused by the movement of magma beneath the surface of the earth. The movement results in pressure changes where the rock around the magma has experienced stress.

(First picture)

Ito ay nangyari noong 1990 sa Summer Capital City of the Philippines na Baguio City. Ang lakas ng lindol ay umabot sa Magnitude 7.8 na lindol na ang sanhi ay ang malawakang pinsala na natamo ng Northern at Central Luzon.

8. 1. What happens to the surface during an earthquake?Ano ang nangyayari sa ibabaw habang may lindol?​


Answer:

Earthquakes often cause dramatic changes at Earth's surface. In addition to the ground movements, other surface effects include changes in the flow of groundwater, landslides, and mudflows. Earthquakes can do significant damage to buildings, bridges, pipelines, railways, embankments, dams, and other structures.
