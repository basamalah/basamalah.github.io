19. Inside a air-conditioned room,metallic objects generally feel cooler to touch than wooden objects.Why is this so ?


      Metallic objects feel cooler to touch than wooden objects in an air-conditioned room because metals are good conductors and dissipators of heat, while wood is a poor conductor and does not dissipate heat as effectively. The cool air temperature of the room allows metallic objects to conduct heat away from the skin more efficiently, making them feel cooler to the touch.
