A song (with indigenous tune and lyrics).

He has a song (but) I don’t remember what it is.

N-, mag-

To sing (an indigenous song).

Magkalang dibuhi' la'i si Danit.

(People) sang (indigenous songs) last night in Danit.

That person knows how to sing (indigenous songs).

TYPES OF VOCAL MUSIC

barajanji song sung in a group at maggunting

batsa one magmawulud, high voice

janjam one or two magmawulud, low voice

jikil one or two magmawulud, low voice

kalang song with indigenous tune

kanta western type song

kata-kata story which is sung

lunsey song, similar to kalangan

maglambu'-lambu' to sing funny rhymes

magolgan to sing ad lib accompanied by a keyboard

mawulud Arabic song, solo or duet

nasib to chant during duwa'a

(mag)pandak one or two magmawulud, mostly reading

parang sabil Tausug singing like a ballad

sa'il ballad

tarasul short poetic composition in Tausug

yaya' lullaby

OTHER WORDS CONNECTED WITH SINGING

dūs to sing together

lugu' tune, to sing, chant, hum

lu'uy melodious sound, instrument or voice

suwala voice

ugey to rhyme
