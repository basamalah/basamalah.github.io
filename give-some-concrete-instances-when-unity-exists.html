23. Give some concrete instances when unity exists even though there are differences happening : among people.​


Answer:

Unity can exist even when differences arise among people in various settings such as politics, sports, religion, nation, and workplace. In politics, despite having diverse political opinions and beliefs, people come together to form a government that works towards the common good. Similarly, in sports, people of different backgrounds and cultures work together towards a common goal of winning the game. Religious unity occurs when people of different religions come together during times of crisis to help each other, despite having different beliefs and practices. National unity is celebrated during events such as Independence Day, Republic Day, or sporting events, where citizens of a country unite in their love for their nation. Lastly, in the workplace, people of different backgrounds and skills come together to achieve a common goal and the success of the organization.
