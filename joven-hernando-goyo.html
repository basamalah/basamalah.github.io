11. goyo Ang batang general critique​


Explanation:

"Goyo: Ang Batang Heneral" is a beautifully made movie that offers a glimpse into Philippine history. However, it may not be suitable for viewers who prefer a fast-paced plot or those who are not familiar with the historical context of the film.

"Goyo: Ang Batang Heneral" is a Filipino historical film that depicts the life of General Gregorio del Pilar during the Philippine-American War.

Positive points of the movie:

The film offers a glimpse into Philippine history, particularly the heroism and sacrifices of General Goyo and his fellow soldiers.The cinematography is visually stunning and captures the beauty of the Philippines during that era.The acting performances, especially by Paulo Avelino as General Goyo, are commendable.The film tackles relevant themes such as patriotism, love for country, and the complexities of war.

Negative points of the movie:

The plot can be confusing and disjointed at times, making it hard for the audience to follow the story.The film's pacing is slow, which may make it difficult for some viewers to stay engaged throughout the entire movie.Some historical inaccuracies were present in the film, which may misinform or confuse viewers who are not well-versed in Philippine history.

26. what is the theme? by Hernando tellez​


Answer:

The barber knows that if he kills Captain Torres, some good might come from it. The captain is a bad person who has been cruel and brutal to the rebels that the barber works with; when he exposes his neck to the barber, he is offering his life to him. He knows the barber might kill him and still gives him the opportunity. However, the barber recognizes that violence is a cycle. The brutality of the regime the rebels are fighting won't stop because Captain Torres dies. He's merely a symptom of a larger problem of oppression and violence. He'll just be replaced by a different man who will murder the rebels and work to suppress their cause.
