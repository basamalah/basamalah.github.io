Solution:

Let x be the first natural number

2x is the second natural number

Expression :

2x + x = 9

Solve :

2x + x = 9

Add all the same variables.

3x = 9

Perform Division Property of Equation.

3x = 9 - Divide 3 to all sides of equation.

3 3

Solve.

x = 3

Answer.

Hence, x is equal to 3

What are the two natural numbers?

If x is equal to 3

2x + x = 9

2 (3) + 3 = 9

6 + 3 = 9

9 = 9

Hence, x is equal to 3 is correct.

The Two Natural numbers are 3 and 6.
