Answer:

11%

Step-by-step explanation:

3600 - 3200 = 400

400 is n% of 3600

400 = percentage

3600 = base

n% = rate

Formula and Solution:

Rate = Percentage/Base x 100

Rate = 400/3600 x 100

Rate = 11.111111111

Rate = 11%
