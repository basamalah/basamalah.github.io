3,200 sq. cm

area of 1 tile = (8cm)²

area of 1 tile = 64cm²

area of the floor = 64cm² x 50

area of the floor = 3,200 cm²

11. a rectangular room has square tiles on the floor. There are 14 tiles along one wall and 17 tiles along an adjacent wall. how many tiles cover the floor of the room?​


Answer:

238

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the number of floor tiles, find the area of a rectangle by using [tex]A=lw[/tex]. Since we cannot find which one is length and which one is height, just multiply both numbers. Since [tex]14 \times 17 = 238[/tex], there are 238 tiles covering.
