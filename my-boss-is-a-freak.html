16. what is the summary of you're my boss movie? ​


Answer:

"You're My Boss" is a 2015 Filipino romantic comedy film directed by Antoinette Jadaone. The film follows the story of Georgina (Toni Gonzaga), a workaholic marketing executive who is tasked by her boss to secure a deal with a wealthy businessman named Pong (Coco Martin) for their company's rebranding. However, Pong mistakes Georgina for a tour guide and asks her to show him around Manila, which she reluctantly agrees to in order to seal the deal.

As they spend more time together, Georgina and Pong develop a close bond and begin to see each other in a new light. However, Georgina is hesitant to pursue a romantic relationship with Pong because of their professional ties and the fact that she is his boss during the day. Eventually, they confess their feelings for each other and decide to take a chance on their relationship.

The film explores themes of work-life balance, personal growth, and taking risks in love and career. It features the picturesque locations and cultural landmarks of Manila, showcasing the beauty of the Philippines. With its witty humor, charming characters, and heartwarming moments, "You're My Boss" is a delightful romantic comedy that highlights the importance of following your heart and taking chances.
