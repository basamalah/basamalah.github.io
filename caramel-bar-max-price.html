21. After receiving a 30 percent discount ,max paid 210 for an item. what is the regular price of the item


After receiving a 30 percent discount ,max paid 210 for an item. Than the normal price of the item is 300.

Step-by-step explanation:

Be discovered:

After receiving a 30 percent discount ,max paid 210 for an item.

Question:

What is the regular price of the item

Asked:

Price before discount = 100%

discount = 30%

Price to be paid = 100% - 30% = 70%

Price to be paid = 70% = 210

Normal price = (100% : 70%) x 210

Normal price = 300

The normal price of the item is 300.

Among the formulas in the chapter of social arithmetic:

Selling price = Purchase price + profit

Purchase price = Selling price - profit

Selling price = Purchase price - loss

Purchase price = Selling price + loss

Price to pay = Normal price - discount

Normal price = Price to pay + discount
