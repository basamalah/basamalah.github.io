10. story of hope viewing genre 1​


There are many different stories of hope that fall under the genre of "viewing genre 1". This could include films, television shows, or even plays that are meant to inspire and give hope to their audience. One example of a story of hope in this genre is the film "The Pursuit of Happyness."

In "The Pursuit of Happyness," Chris Gardner is a single father who is struggling to make ends meet. He takes on a low-paying internship at a stock brokerage firm in the hopes of landing a permanent job and providing for his son. However, he faces many challenges along the way, including homelessness and financial struggles. Despite these obstacles, Chris never gives up and continues to work hard in his internship. Through determination and perseverance, he eventually lands the job and is able to provide for his son and create a better life for himself.

In "This Is Us," the show explores the lives of several characters who are all connected in some way. Each episode focuses on a different character and their struggles and triumphs. Some of the characters face challenges such as terminal illness, addiction, and relationship struggles. Despite these hardships, the show ultimately presents a message of hope and the idea that even in the darkest times, there is always a light at the end of the tunnel. The characters find strength in their connections with each other and are able to overcome their challenges and find happiness and fulfilment in their lives.

Overall, both "The Pursuit of Happyness" and "This Is Us" are stories of hope that are meant to inspire and give the audience a sense of optimism. Despite the challenges and hardships that the characters face, they never give up and continue to fight for a better life. These stories remind us that even in the most difficult situations, there is always the potential for growth, change, and happiness.
