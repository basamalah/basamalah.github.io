15. Emilio did a science experiment in which he found the boiling point of olive oil to be 572° F. According to the formula C = (F - 32) × 5/9 , what is the boiling point of olive oil in degrees Celsius ?


Answer:

300 degrees Celsius

Given: 572° F

Unknown: Olive oil in °C

Formula: °C = (°F - 32) × 5/9

Solution: (572°F − 32) × 5/9 = 300°C

Final Answer: 300°C
