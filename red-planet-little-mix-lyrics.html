10. Which planet is known as the "Red Planet"?


Answer:

Mars.

Explanation:

The planet Mars is known as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance in the night sky. This is because of the iron oxide or rust present on its surface, which gives it a distinctive red color. Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and is considered as one of the most promising candidates for human exploration in the future.

Answer: Mars

Explanation:

The planet that is known as the "Red Planet" is Mars. It is called the Red Planet because of its reddish appearance in the night sky, which is due to the presence of iron oxide (rust) on its surface. The iron oxide on Mars gives the planet its distinctive reddish hue, which is particularly visible during times when Mars is closest to Earth. The reddish color of Mars is also evident in images captured by spacecraft that have visited the planet, such as the Mars rovers and orbiters.
