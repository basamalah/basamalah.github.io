9. What is the index of refraction of the tempered glass if the speed of light has changed from 3.0x108 m/s to 1.84x108  m/s?​


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & c = 3.0 \times 10^8 \: \text{m/s} \\ & v = 1.84 \times 10^8 \: \text{m/s} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the index of refraction.

[tex]\begin{aligned} n & = \frac{c}{v} \\ & = \frac{3.0 \times 10^8 \: \text{m/s}}{1.84 \times 10^8 \: \text{m/s}} \\ & = \boxed{1.63} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the index of refraction of tempered glass is 1.63.

16. only one stanza, 4 lines about sectional planes in anatomy​


    The median plane, the sagittal plane, the horizontal plane, and the coronal plane.

The areas of anatomy consist of:

    Anatomical planes are planes that pass through the body in an anatomical position. Anatomy is experiencing a progressive development in understanding the structure and function of the human body.

1. The median: plane is the plane that divides the body into the right and left planes.

2. The sagittal: plane is the plane that divides the body into two planes from a certain point (does not divide exactly two parts). This plane is parallel to the median plane.

3. Horizontal plane: the plane that lies across the body (X-Y plane). This plane divides the body into upper (superior) and lower (inferior) planes.

4. Coronal plane: a vertical plane that passes through the body, which is perpendicular to the median or sagittal plane. divides the body into a front (frontal) and back (dorsal) planes.

    The types of anatomy are vertebrate anatomy, fish anatomy, amphibian anatomy, reptile anatomy, bird anatomy, mammal anatomy, invertebrate anatomy, human anatomy, and arthropod anatomy.

23. one of the effect of high heat index during the dry season is hypothermia, true or false? ​


Answer:

No. Its false.

Explanation:

Hypothermia is caused by prolonged exposures to very cold temperatures. When exposed to cold temperatures, your body begins to lose heat faster than it's produced. Lengthy exposures will eventually use up your body's stored energy, which leads to lower body temperature.

When your body temperature drops, your heart, nervous system and other organs can't work normally. Left untreated, hypothermia can lead to complete failure of your heart and respiratory system and eventually to death. Hypothermia is often caused by exposure to cold weather or immersion in cold water.

26. ano ang literaturang grey (grey literature)​


Answer:

Grey literature (or gray literature) is materials and research produced by organizations outside of the traditional commercial or academic publishing and distribution channels. Common grey literature publication types include reports (annual, research, technical, project, etc.), working papers, government documents, white papers and evaluations. Organizations that produce grey literature include government departments and agencies, civil society or non-governmental organizations, academic centres and departments, and private companies and consultants.
