SSG Treasurer Speech

My name is _______ (Your name), and I'm running for treasurer of this wonderful class of ours, fellow students, and educators. I think some of my suggestions will help us increase our spending capacity and enable us to take a fantastic class vacation.

I believe I am qualified for this position in part because I have experience handling and managing a budget. My mother is actually the Chief Financial Officer of my parents' business, so I believe that I can use the skills she has imparted to manage our budget. I am highly skilled at handling money sensibly. If given the chance to serve as your representative, I would endeavor to use the budget wisely so that we could make savings while still having plenty of money.

I also have suggestions for how to increase that money if I'm elected. As a group, we can invest in stocks in hopes of seeing our money double or even treble. Let's now discuss particular tasks and my strategy for using the budget. If I were elected, I would ask the class to decide at the start of the year whether we wanted to use our budget for lots of little extracurricular events and a cheap field trip. An extremely large class trip might also be planned if we decided to save all the money and just use it for a few activities throughout the year.

You should have complete control over where your money goes since it is your money in the first place. If you elect me as treasurer, I will not make any purchases without first getting your approval. I would just be there to help with coordination and make sure everything gets done. A suggestion box would also be available in case you had any ideas for fun piza nights or celebrations that we could explore as a class. I've said it before, and I'm sure I can keep it: whatever we spend our money on is absolutely up to you.

We can definitely make this year amazing, in my opinion. If you choose to elect me, I'll make sure that every dollar is spent carefully and that my records are reliable. You have my complete attention. I'm only calling for your vote, that's really all.

Vote for _______(Your name) as Treasurer.
