If I were invited as a guest speaker for a commencement exercise in the elementary school where I graduated as valedictorian, I would prepare for the speaking and listening engagement by doing the following:

First, I would review my own experiences and accomplishments since graduating from that school, and think about the lessons I have learned and the advice I would like to share with the graduating class of 2022.

Next, I would research the current state of education and the job market, and consider how the graduates can prepare themselves for the challenges and opportunities they will face in the future.

I would also prepare a speech that is engaging, informative, and inspiring, and practice delivering it several times to ensure that it flows naturally and connects with the audience.

Additionally, I would make sure to arrive at the commencement ceremony early, and introduce myself to the organizers and the graduates, so that I can get a feel for the atmosphere and the audience before I give my speech.

Overall, my goal would be to create a positive impression of myself as a successful and accomplished alumna who is willing to share her experiences and advice with the graduating class of 2022. I would strive to be engaging, informative, and inspiring, and to leave the graduates feeling motivated and ready to tackle the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.
