2. All potting soil,compost,and bark mulch will be covered with a tarp or dark plastic.why?​


Answer:

covered by tarp

Explanation

Rain will both erode the topsoil and leech away nutrients. For long term storage, compacting the top layer of the pile will help against erosion, though the use of a tarp is still recommended. Of course, the best option is to avoid the need for storage in the first place.

6. Josh has a circular swimming pool that has a 21 foot diameter. How many square feet of tarp would Josh need to buy in order to cover the pool?


Multiply the radius by itself to square the number (6 x 6 = 36). Multiply the result by pi (use the button on the calculator) or 3.14159 (36 x 3.14159 = 113.1). The result is the area of the circle in square feet--113.1 square feet.
