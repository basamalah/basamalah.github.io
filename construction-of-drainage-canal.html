What is Sanitary and Drainage System?

Sanitary and Drainage Systems allows the fr.ee flow of water and in most cases prevents accumulation that can lead to flooding. Drainage systems also prevent the accumulation of stagnant water, which can encourage mosquitoes to breed.

What is the purpose of sanitary and drainage system?

The purpose of the sanitary drainage system is to remove effluent discharged from plumbing fixtures and other equipment to an approved point of disposal.

Why is it important to have a proper drainage system in the house or any establishment?

A well-designed drainage system is essential to remove any excess water from the house and helps in the prevention of water-related damage to the home. It is also important to ensure that clogged-up water does not affect the work being done during the construction phase.

What is the advantages of sanitary and drainage systems?The field-scale benefits of drainage can be summarized as follows: Drainage promotes beneficial soil bacteria activity and improves soil tilth.
