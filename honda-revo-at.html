15. what is acceleration of a Honda Accord with a constant velocity of 50 km/h for 20 seconds​


The acceleration of a Honda Accord with a constant velocity of 50 km/h for 20 seconds is zero, since acceleration is the rate of change of velocity and the velocity is constant. In other words, the car maintains the same speed over the 20-second period and does not accelerate.

Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity over time. Since the velocity of the Honda Accord is constant at 50 km/h, the acceleration is zero. In other words, the car is not accelerating, it is maintaining a constant speed.

21. determine the weight of a 2007 honda accord with a mass of 1,450,000 grams


#BrainlyFast

Problem:

Determine the weight of a 2007 honda accord with a mass of 1,450,000 grams.

Given Data:

m (mass) = 1,450,000 g

g (gravity) = 9.8 m/s²

w (weight) = ? unknown

Solution:

We will convert first the mass in grams (g) to kilogram (kg).

Conversion factor (1 kg = 1000 g)

1,450,000 g × 1 kg/1000 g = 1,450 kg

To solve for weight, we can use the formula w = mg since weight = force:

w = mg

   = 1,450 kg × 9.8 m/s²

   =  14, 210 kg.m/s²

   = 14, 210 N

Answer:

weight is equal to 14, 210 kg.m/s²

Weight is the same as force and the unit for both weight and force is Newton (N). Weight on earth may not be different on other planet since weight on Earth depends on the force of gravity on Earth (9.8 m/s²) which is different from other planet.
