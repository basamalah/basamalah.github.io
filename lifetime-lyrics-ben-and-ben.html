16. allan and ben will share the ammount 960 pesos such that allan has twice as much as ben. how much is bens share?


Ben's share: x
Allan's share:  2x

x + 2x = 960

3x = 960

3x/3 = 960/3

x = 320

Substitute 320 to x:

Ben's share: x = Php 320
Allan;s share: 2x = 2(320) = Php 640

ANSWER:  Ben's share is Php 640.

Check:

320 + 640 = 960
960 = 960

20. Allan and Ben will share the amount 960 such that Allan has twice as much as Ben. How much is Ben is share?


Ben will have 320 pesos while Allan will have 640 pesos in that way Allan's money is twice the amount of Ben's share. And 320 plus 640 is equal to 960.let x = Ben's share
2x = Allan's share

[tex]x + 2x = 960\\ \frac{3x}{3}= \frac{960}{3} \\x=320[/tex]

Therefore, the ansswer to your question is 320.
