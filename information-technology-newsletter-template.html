27. Make a newsletter that will provide information about the Do's and Dont's before, during and after volcanic eruption.​


Answer:

Stay indoors and away from volcanic ashfall areas as much as possible. When it is safe to go outside, keep your gutters and roof clear of ash as heavy ash deposits can collapse your roof. If there is a lot of ash in the water supply, do not use your dishwasher or washing machine.

Dont's

Stay away from areas downwind from the volcano to avoid volcanic ash. Stay indoors until the ash has settled unless there is a danger of the roof collapsing. ... Driving can stir up volcanic ash that can clog engines, damage moving parts, and stall vehicles. Avoid driving in heavy ash fall unless absolutely required.

28. Similarities and Differences of Newspaper and Newsletter


Similarities of Newspaper and Newsletter

1. Both newspaper and newsletter are regularly published.

2. Both reading materials are obtained by many.

3. Both can contain advertisements and important announcements.

4. Both are physically printed and circulated even through mails.

5. Both serve the purpose of updating the readers.

Differences of Newspaper and Newsletter

1. They differ on features, functions, readership, and types.

2. Newspaper is available daily while newsletter can only be available each week.

3. Newspaper can be read by millions while a newsletter can only be obtained by thousands.

4.  Newspaper contains news on business, crime, entertainment, politics and sports while newsletter contains specific topic for specific group.

5. Newspapers are used to inform the general public while newsletters are intended for specific groups, organization, clubs, or employees.
