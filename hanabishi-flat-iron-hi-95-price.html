5. How many calories of heat will be needed to raise the temperature of 250g of iron from 40 ⁰C to 95⁰C? the specific heat of iron is 0.11cal/g⁰C


Answer:

Q = 302.5 cal

Explanation:

Heat (Q) = mass (m) * specific heat (c) * change in temperature (ΔT)

Given:

Mass (m) = 250 g

Specific heat (c) = 0.11 cal/g°C

Change in temperature (ΔT) = (95 - 40) °C = 55 °C

Plugging these values into the formula:

Q = 250 g * 0.11 cal/g°C * 55 °C

Q = 302.5 cal

23. For the reaction 2Fe + 3S → Fe2S3, how many grams of sulfur are involved in this reaction if 95 grams of iron are needed to react with sulfur?


Answer:

83 g

Explanation:

Step 1: Write the balanced equation

2 Fe + 3 S ⇒ Fe₂S₃

Step 2: Calculate the moles corresponding to 95 g of Fe

The molar mass of Fe is 55.85 g/mol.

95 g × 1 mol/55.85 g = 1.7 mol

Step 3: Calculate the moles of S needed to react with 1.7 moles of Fe

The molar ratio of Fe to S is 2:3. The moles of S needed are 3/2 × 1.7 mol = 2.6 mol

Step 4: Calculate the mass corresponding to 2.6 moles of S

The molar mass of S is 32.07 g/mol.

2.6 mol × 32.07 g/mol = 83 g

28. Father’s Day is approaching and Sarah wants to surprise his father with a new leather belt. Upon canvassing 40 belts, she found out that the average price is Php. 950 with standard deviation of Php 150. construct a 90%, 95%, and 99% confidence interval.


Answer:

To construct the confidence intervals, we will use the formula:

CI = X̄ ± Zα/2 * (σ/√n)

where:

X̄ = sample mean

Zα/2 = critical value

σ = population standard deviation

n = sample size

For a 90% confidence interval, the critical value (Zα/2) is 1.645:

CI = 950 ± 1.645 * (150/√40)

CI = 950 ± 48.95

CI = (901.05, 998.95)

For a 95% confidence interval, the critical value (Zα/2) is 1.96:

CI = 950 ± 1.96 * (150/√40)

CI = 950 ± 52.18

CI = (897.82, 1002.18)

For a 99% confidence interval, the critical value (Zα/2) is 2.576:

CI = 950 ± 2.576 * (150/√40)

CI = 950 ± 67.07

CI = (882.93, 1017.07)

Therefore, we can say with 90% confidence that the true average price of leather belts is between Php 901.05 and Php 998.95, with 95% confidence that it is between Php 897.82 and Php 1002.18, and with 99% confidence that it is between Php 882.93 and Php 1017.07.
