12. Give five examples each reality and fantasy


Reality & Fantasy

What is REALITY?

When you look up in the dictionary, reality is the state of being real and true. With that, we can say that reality has basis and it can be provable by things that really exists. These things are what you hear, see, feel, and experience.

Five (5) examples of reality Women have the ability to give birth and life to another human being. A caterpillar will soon turn into a beautiful and colorful butterfly. Smoke belching can cause air pollution and can destroy the environment. Eating healthy foods will make your life longer.Dogs can bark and birds can hum.

What is FANTASY?

In a dictionary, fantasy is creating unrealistic or impossible things, images, and happenings. Fantasy can also be related to fiction wherein everything is all made up by humans' creative minds.

Five (5) examples of fantasyThe man fell in love with the mermaid.The turtle and the monkey had a race on who's the first one to get to the top of the coconut tree.Aladdin and Jasmine rode the magic carpet and flew around the city.The mice and the birds worked together to make a dress for Cinderella.The fox blew away the three little pigs' house which is only made of straw.
