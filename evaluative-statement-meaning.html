Parts of Research : This pertains to the makeup of a research. It consists of the introduction, Methodology, Discussion, Meaning or mostly known as Definition of Terms, Conclusion and References. Each parts have serves specific purpose.

Introduction: This is tge first part of a reaserch which presents the background

information (related literature review), scope, and focus of the research.

Methodology : It explains how research was

conducted, who are the respondents, where is it held and who will be responsible in data gathering.

Discussion: It presents the information

gathered from the research by using a table, line graph, bar graph and others.

Meaning: It is mostly known as the Definition of Terms, where essential terms used in a research were defined.

Conclusion : The conclusion evaluates the results of the research, whether it is valid and reliable that it can be recommended to others.

Reference : This lists all sources used in the research, usually follows the APA method where the book, journal, author, website page, publication company, year of publication and year of extraction/reading is cited.
