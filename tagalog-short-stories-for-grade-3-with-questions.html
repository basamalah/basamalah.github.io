Explanation:

Once upon a time, in a vast, lush forest, there were many different kinds of frogs. Each of them had a unique appearance, habitat, and behavior.

The first kind of frog was the Poison Dart Frog. It was a small, brightly colored frog that lived in the rainforest. It was known for its toxic skin, which could kill predators or even humans who touched it. The Poison Dart Frog was also very fast, which helped it escape from danger.

The second kind of frog was the Tree Frog. It was a small, green or brown frog that lived in trees. The Tree Frog was excellent at climbing and had sticky pads on its toes, which helped it cling to branches. The Tree Frog was also known for its loud croak, which it used to communicate with other Tree Frogs.

The third kind of frog was the Bullfrog. It was a large, green or brown frog that lived near ponds and lakes. The Bullfrog was known for its deep croak, which could be heard from a great distance. It was also an excellent swimmer and could jump long distances, making it an excellent hunter.

The fourth kind of frog was the Goliath Frog. It was the largest frog in the world and lived in the rainforest near rivers and streams. The Goliath Frog could grow up to a foot long and weigh over six pounds. Despite its size, it was a gentle giant and fed on insects and small animals.

The fifth kind of frog was the Glass Frog. It was a small, transparent frog that lived in the rainforest. The Glass Frog was known for its unique appearance, as its internal organs could be seen through its skin. It was also an excellent climber and could blend into its surroundings.

As the sun set over the forest, each kind of frog settled into its habitat. The Poison Dart Frog climbed onto a leaf, the Tree Frog nestled in a tree, the Bullfrog sat by the pond, the Goliath Frog settled near the river, and the Glass Frog perched on a branch. Despite their differences, each kind of frog was important to the ecosystem and played a vital role in the forest's balance.

19. magbigay ng isang short story na may 5 question nakailang ng abcd for grade 5​


Ang Batang Mahilig sa Sisiw

Si Miguel ay isang batang mahilig sa sisiw. Tuwing may pagkakataon, siya ay nag-aalaga ng mga itlog at nagbabantay ng mga sisiw sa kanilang bakuran.

Isang araw, habang nagbabantay siya ng mga sisiw, nakita niya ang isang magnanakaw na nag-aabang ng tamang pagkakataon para magnakaw ng mga manok at sisiw. Sinubukan ni Miguel na tumakbo palabas ng bakuran upang magsumbong sa kanyang mga magulang, ngunit nahulog siya at nasugatan ang kanyang paa.

Sa kabila ng sakit ng kanyang paa, nagtagumpay si Miguel na magpaalam sa kanyang mga magulang tungkol sa plano ng magnanakaw at nagawang maiwasan ang pagnanakaw. Sa huli, napatunayan niya sa kanyang sarili na kaya niya ang mga bagay na dati niyang inakala na hindi niya kaya.

Narito ang ilang mga tanong tungkol sa kwento:

1. Ano ang hilig ni Miguel?

2. Ano ang nakita niya habang nagbabantay siya ng mga sisiw?

3. Anong nangyari kay Miguel habang siya ay tumatakbo?

4. Anong ginawa ni Miguel upang maiwasan ang pagnanakaw?

5. Anong natuklasan ni Miguel tungkol sa kanyang sarili sa dulo ng kwento?

Ang Lobo at ang Kabayo

Kahit may kaliitan, maaaring sakmal-sakmalin ka ng masibang Lobo. Kapag natipuhan ka ng hayop na ito, magtago ka na at tiyak na lalapain ka nito. Kapag nangangalisag na ang mga balahibo at pinaggigiyagis na nito ang mga pangil, umakyat ka na sa pinakamataas na puno o tumakbo kaya sa pinakamalayong burol upang di abutan at lapain ang iyong katawan mula ulo hanggang talampakan.

Upang makapanloko, ang Lobo ay nagbabait-baitan. Isang tanghali ngang ito ay ikut nang ikot sa kagubatan ay natanawan nito ang isang Kabayong nanginginain sa talahiban. Inggit na inggit ang naglalaway nang Lobo sa pagnguya-nguya ng Kabayo.

"Gustung-gusto mong nguyain ang mga talahib. Nakita kitang sarap na sarap sa panginginain kaya hindi na kita sinaluhan upang ikaw ay lubos na masiyahan. Di ka ba magpapasalamat man lang at ipinaubaya ko sa iyo ang buong talahiban?"

"Naloloko ka na ba?" galit na nag-aalma ang Kabayo. "Dapat mong malaman na una, ang talahiban ay hindi sa iyo at ikalawa, ang mga Lobong tulad mo ay hindi ngumunguya ng talahib. Lumayu-layo ka diyan at baka masipa lang kita. Alis diyan!"

Sa takot ng Lobo ay walang lingong tumalikod at umalis ito.

====================================

Aral: Ang panloloko ay hindi gawaing makatao.
