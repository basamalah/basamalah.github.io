8. The diameter of circle E is 10cm. Chords FG and HK are 6cm long and they are equal. How far are they from E?Pa help with explanation na maiintindihan ko thank you!!(maayos po sana)​


Answer:

The chords FG and HK are 4 cm away from the center of the circle.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's call the distance from the center of the circle to each chord "d".

The formula for the distance from the center of a circle to a chord is given by:

d = (sqrt(R^2 - L^2)) / 2

where R is the radius of the circle, and L is the length of the chord.

So, for chord FG and HK, we have:

d = (sqrt(R^2 - 6^2)) / 2

d = (sqrt(100 - 36)) / 2

d = (sqrt(64)) / 2

d = 8 / 2

d = 4

23. Which chords are major chords and minor chords ​


ANSWER

MAJOR CHORDS V.S MINOR CHORDS

•A major chord has what's called a “natural third.” It's the third degree of the chord's respective major scale.

•A minor chord has what's called either a “minor third” or a “flat third.” It's the third degree of the chord's respective minor scale.
