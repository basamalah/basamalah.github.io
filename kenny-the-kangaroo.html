Kenny : _________________________________________________ Roger : _________________________________________________ Kenny : _________________________________________________ Roger : _________________________________________________ Kenny : _________________________________________________ Roger : _________________________________________________ Kenny : _________________________________________________


Answer:

Kenny : If I were the mayor, I would assess the effect of the

pandemic.

Roger : If I assess its effect, I will plan how to respond on it.

Kenny : If I plan our response, I will call for more budget.

Roger : If I call for more budget, I will have more resources.

Kenny : If I will have more resources, I will be able to help more community people.

Roger : If I will be able to help more community people, I will inspire private organizations to help too.

Kenny : If I will inspire private organizations to help too, I will inspire private individuals as well.

Roger : If I will inspire private individuals as well, I will be able to lessen the problems caused by the pandemic.

Kenny : If I will be able to lessen the problems caused by the pandemic, I will lessen the burden on the people.

Roger : If I will lessen the burden on the people, I will spark hope among them.

Kenny : If I will spark hope among them, I will lead them to a better tomorrow.

Explanation:

Conditionals describe the result of something that might happen (in the present or future) or might have happened but didn't (in the past).
