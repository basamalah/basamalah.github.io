6. What is a camera? Who is/are the inventor of Camera?​


Question: What is camera?

Answer:

A camera is an optical instrument used to capture an image. At their most basic, cameras are sealed boxes with a small hole that allows light in to capture an image on a light-sensitive surface. Cameras have various mechanisms to control how the light falls onto the light-sensitive surface.
