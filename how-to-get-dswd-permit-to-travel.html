4. What program of DSWD is part SAP? how does it function?​.


Answer:

The Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program (4Ps) is a social development program of the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) that is part of the Social Amelioration Program (SAP). The 4Ps provide financial assistance to poor households to help them improve their access to health and education services, and to increase their human capital. The program provides poor households with a monthly cash grant of Php 500-Php 1,400, depending on the number of members in the household. The grant is paid directly to the mothers of the families, who are the program beneficiaries. The program also provides health insurance coverage, and encourages the beneficiaries to participate in nutrition and health activities. The program also encourages the beneficiaries to send their children to school, and provides additional cash grants for children aged 6-14.
