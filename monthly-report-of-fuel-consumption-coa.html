12. how does excessive usage of fossil fuels affetc earth? cite three ways that help limit the consumption of fossil fuels​


Excessive usage of fossil fuels can have a number of negative impacts on the earth. Here are three ways that can help limit the consumption of fossil fuels:

Increasing the use of renewable energy sources: Renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power do not produce greenhouse gases and can help reduce our reliance on fossil fuels.

Improving energy efficiency: Making buildings, vehicles, and appliances more energy efficient can reduce the amount of energy needed to power them, which in turn reduces the demand for fossil fuels.

Promoting public transportation and carpooling: Encouraging the use of public transportation and carpooling can reduce the number of cars on the road, which will reduce the demand for gasoline and other fossil fuels.

24. A certain family has an average consumptions of 25,000 watts in one month. What is the approximate monthly electric consumption in kilowatt-hour?


Answer:

The family's approximate monthly electric consumption is 0.0336 kilowatt-hour.

Step-by-step explanation:

In the problem, it was stated that the family's average electric consumption in one month is 25,000 watts.

Since we want our final answer to be in kilowatt-hour, we need to convert watts to kilowatts. We know that:

1 kilowatt = 1,000 watts

So,

[tex](\frac{25,000 watts}{1})(\frac{1 kilowatt}{1,000watts}) = 25 kilowatts[/tex]

Therefore, the family consumes 25 kilowatts of electricity in a month.

We now need to know the number of hours in a month. Since there are approximately 30 days in a month and 24 hours in a day, we can just multiply them to know how many hours are there in approximately a month.

30 days × 24 hours = 720 hours

We can now divide the monthly consumption of the family by the total number of hours in a month.

25 kilowatts ÷ 720 hours = 0.0347222222 kilowatt-hour

25 kilowatts ÷ 720 hours ≈ 0.0347 kilowatt-hour
