22. advantages of edge computing over cloud computing


Answer:

By processing data closer to the source and prioritizing traffic, edge computing reduces the amount of data flowing to and from the primary network, leading to lower latency and faster overall speed. Physical distance is critical to performance as well.

The main benefits of edge computing over cloud computing are: Better data management. Lower connectivity costs and better security practices. Reliable, uninterrupted connection.

26. benefits of cloud computing?


Benefits of Cloud Computing

In simple words, cloud computing is relying on the use of internet as a storage and computing resources. It works like this, you will buy a "server" on the internet. Like any other server computers, you can connect clients to your server. Clients which would be connected on your computer through the use of a portal using the internet. Once they are logged in to the portal, your client is now connected to the server. They could share, download and copy files from your server. The main purpose of these online servers using cloud is for storage purposes and to share any important files to another branch or location of your company which is located in a different geographical location. Other term for cloud computing is "internet-computing".

Cloud computing offers different advantages such as: Broad network access : since internet has unlimited scope, you can share your resources to almost the whole universe. As long as there is an internet available, you can expect that cloud computing will work. Economical and cost- effective : maintaining servers requires energy and money. Instead of buying and maintaining servers, you can buy online servers and wouldn't have to worry where to put them and how to maintain them. Security : Cloud servers required different log in and keys that would ensure the safety and security of your servers. Contract can be cancelled anytime : Your agreement with the online company or business who is lending the server to you can be terminated anytime if you are not happy with their service anymore.
