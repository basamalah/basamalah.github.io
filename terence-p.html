26. Jack, Terence, Sharon and Alex are neighbors. Jack is half as old as Sharon. Sharon is three years older than Alex. Alex’s and Sharon’s ages added together equal 17 years. Terence is eight. Who is the youngest?


Answer: Jack

Step-by-step explanation:

We need to list down first the given clues.

Clue #1 - There are four persons.

Clue #2 - Jack is half as old as Sharon.

We can represent this one in a mathematical expression.

Jack's age - x

Sharon's age - 2x

Clue #3 - Sharon is three years older than Alex

So we can also express this as:

Alex's age - y

Sharon's age - y + 3

Clue #4 - Sharon's and Alex's ages add up to 17.

So we can form an equation out of this one.

Sharon's age + Alex's age = 17

(y + 3) + y = 17

Using algebraic manipulation to find the value of y, we combine like terms.

y + y + 3 = 17

2y + 3 = 17

Subtract both sides by 3.

2y + 3 - 3 = 17 - 3

2y = 14

Divide both sides by 2 to get y.

2y/2 = 14/2

y = 7

So y = 7, and we know that y is the value of Alex's age.

To get the age of Sharon, we will just substitute 7 to y + 3, which is 7 + 3 = 10.

So Sharon's age is 10.

But we also know that Jack's age is half of Sharon so:

Sharon's age = 2x

10 = 2x

Divide both side by 2

10/2 = 2x/2

x = 5

Therefore, Jack's age is 5 years old.

Now compiling all the calculated values.

Jack - 5 years old

Sharon - 10 years old

Alex - 7 years old

Terence - 8 years old

Therefore the youngest of the four kids is Jack.
