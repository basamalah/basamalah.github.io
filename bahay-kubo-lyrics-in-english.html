Song Metrics

Bahay Kubo is a song by The Grail Singers with a tempo of 157 BPM. It can also be used half-time at 79 BPM or double-time at 314 BPM. The track runs 1 minute and 4 seconds long with a F key and a major mode. It has low energy and is somewhat danceable with a time signature of 3 beats per bar.

Cavan. The song is about a bahay kubo ( lit. 'Cube House' in English), a house made of bamboo with a roof of nipa leaves, surrounded by different kind of vegetables, and is usually sang by Filipino school children and as familiar as the "Alphabet Song" and "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star" from the West.

24. bahay kubo in english


Bahay Kubo

Ang bahay kubo sa salitang Ingles ay tinatawag na Nipa Hut.

Ito ay tumutukoy sa isang uri ng tirahan na karamihan ng parte o bahagi ay gawa sa mga parte ng punong niyog.

Ang bahay kubo ay tinatawag din na payag o kamalig, ito ay tanyag at makikita sa bansang Pilipinas na ginagamit ng mga indigenous people doon.
