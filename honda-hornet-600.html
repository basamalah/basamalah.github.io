9. determine the weight of a 2007 honda accord with a mass of 1,450,000 grams


#BrainlyFast

Problem:

Determine the weight of a 2007 honda accord with a mass of 1,450,000 grams.

Given Data:

m (mass) = 1,450,000 g

g (gravity) = 9.8 m/s²

w (weight) = ? unknown

Solution:

We will convert first the mass in grams (g) to kilogram (kg).

Conversion factor (1 kg = 1000 g)

1,450,000 g × 1 kg/1000 g = 1,450 kg

To solve for weight, we can use the formula w = mg since weight = force:

w = mg

   = 1,450 kg × 9.8 m/s²

   =  14, 210 kg.m/s²

   = 14, 210 N

Answer:

weight is equal to 14, 210 kg.m/s²

Weight is the same as force and the unit for both weight and force is Newton (N). Weight on earth may not be different on other planet since weight on Earth depends on the force of gravity on Earth (9.8 m/s²) which is different from other planet.

11. How can we express the letters in the word hornets using roster method? Name set b


Answer:

B = {h, o, r, n, e, t, s}

Step-by-step explanation:

A roster method is one of the three ways of naming or describing all the elements of a certain set.

The three ways are:

Roster Method Rule MethodSet-Builder Notation

The roster method is the simplest and most straightforward out of the three since all you have to do is to list and write all the elements.

A thing to consider before writing the set is the no repeat elements constraint. We cannot repeat an element in a set. However, we don't need to do this since all the letters in the word hornets are distinct.

Using this, we can simply write B = {h, o, r, n, e, t, s}.
