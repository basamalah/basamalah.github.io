2. Why do the emulsion of lipids, water, and ethanol appear cloudy?


Answer:

Because lipids are insoluble in water and soluble in ethanol (an alcohol). After lipids have been dissolved in ethanol and then added to H2O, they will form tiny dispersed droplets in the water. These droplets scatter light as it passes through the water so it appears white and cloudy.
