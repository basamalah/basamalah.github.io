14. A food company produces grated cheese made from two types of cheese. One type of cheese costs P29 per kilogram and the other costs P31 per kilogram .How much of each type of cheese was used in making 200kg of cheese worth P29.50?


Hello po,

So let's say the company uses x kilograms of the first type of cheese.

Since they made 200kg of cheese, they used 200-x kgs of the second type.

That's why the equation would be:

29x + 31(200-x) = 29.50(200)

This is just equating the prices (price/kg x kg) of each type of cheese.

Simplifying the above equation:

29x + 6200 - 31x = 5900

-2x = -300

x = 150 kg of the first cheese (P29/kg)

This means that we have

200 - 150 = 50kg of the second cheese (P31/kg).

We can check this:

150kg x P29/kg + 50kg x P31/kg = P5,900 for 200kg, or P29.5/kg.

FYI, this is what is commonly called a "mixture problem". Just in case you want to search for other practice problems.

Hope this helps!

30. which type of grater is typically used to grate the zest of lemons and other fruits? ​


which type of grater is typically used to grate the zest of lemons and other fruits?

Answer:

A Microplane

Explanation:

A Microplane is a long and thin grater with small teeth perfect for zesting lemons and limes. For most recipes, this is the best tool because it creates fine pieces of zest that don't contain any pith. A microplane is a handheld grater with sharp holes in it. Grating foods with a microplane is faster and more consistent than a box grater.
