2. In EFG, mE = 30, mF = 60, and mG = 90. Which of the following statements about EFG are true? Check all that apply. A. EF = 2 • FG B. EG = 2 • FG C. EF = • FG D. EG = • FG E. EG = • EF


In EFG, mE = 30, mF = 60, and mG = 90, we should determine what number represents m, E, F, and G.

 

Through the process of deduction, we can safely deduce that the value of m is 30, or m = 3 while E, F, and G, has the values of 1, 2, and 3, respectively.

 

Now, we supply the values and check if they equate:

A.      EF = 2 • FG  =  1(2) = 2(2x3)

B.      EG = 2 • FG = 1(3) = 2(2x3)

C.      EF = • FG = 1(2) = 2(3)

D.     EG = • FG = 1(3) = 2(3)

E.      EG = • EF = 1(3) = 1(2)

 

We solve:

A.   2 = 12

B.    3 = 12

C.    2 = 6

D.   3 = 6

E.    3 = 2

 

If the questions that were supplied are correct, nothing in the selection would apply and be true for EFG.

Radius is the line segment connecting the center of the circle and a point on the circle.

Diameter is a chord that passes through the center of the circle.

Center angle is an angle whose vertex is the center of the circle.
