Subic Bay is a bay on the west coast of the island of Luzon in the Philippines, about 100 kilometers northwest of Manila Bay. Aside from its tranquil beaches, a sanctuary of exotic animals,marine and nature activities, it is also known for its energetic nightlife.

Tagalog

Ang Subic Bay ay isang look sa kanlurang baybayin ng isla ng Luzon sa Pilipinas, mga 100 kilometro sa hilagang-kanluran ng Manila Bay. Bukod sa mga payapang tabing-dagat nito, isang santuwaryo ng mga kakaibang hayop, mga aktibidad sa dagat at kalikasan, kilala rin ito sa masiglang nightlife nito.

13. How the subic bay metropolitan authority created​


Answer:

Section 13 of Republic Act No. 7227 created the Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority (SBMA) to develop and manage the Freeport which provides tax and duty-free privileges and incentives to business locators in the special economic zone. Richard Gordon, then the mayor of the City of Olongapo, became the first SBMA chairman.

23. Suppose there are 3 ways to travel from Leyte to Manila and 4 ways to travel from Manila to Subic. How many ways are there to travel from Leyte to Subic through manila​


Answer:

5040 ways

Step-by-step explanation:

We will add the number of ways to travel from L to M and from M to S since we have to go to Subic from Leyte.

We determine if the problem is a Computaion or a Permutation. In travelling, you cannot repeat the same way you took since that would mean going back to your starting point. Therefore, this is an example of Permutation.

7! = 5040

or

7 x 6 x 5 x 4 x 3 x 2 x 1 = 5040
