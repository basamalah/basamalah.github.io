16. what is suppositories for?


A suppository is a form of medication that is encapsulated in a cylindrical gel or easy-melting substance. It is meant to be inserted into the rectum or the vagina so that it may melt upon contact with the moist environment and quickly absorbed into the bloodstream. This is a fast way of introducing medication to the body.
