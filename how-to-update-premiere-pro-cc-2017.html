24. what are the 4 parts of adobe premiere pro explain each function​


Answer:

The Project panel is where you can view and manage all of your assets, such as media files, sequences, and compositions. You can also access the project library and effect controls here.The Source Monitor is used to preview and trim clips before adding them to the timeline. You can also use this panel to add markers to clips and view metadata information.The Program Monitor is where you can view your timeline sequences and make adjustments to your compositions. You can also use this panel to make color corrections, add audio effects, and view real-time playback.The Timeline is where you can arrange and edit your media files and sequences. You can drag and drop clips onto the timeline, add transitions and effects, adjust the audio levels, and create titles and animations.
