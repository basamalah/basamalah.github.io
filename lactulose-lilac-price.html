8. > Research Using a Dictionary Use your dictionary to find the meanings of the word listed below. Then, write simple sentences using this words. WORDS: 1. Propagate 2. Enrich 3. Associations 4. Chore 5. Lilac 6. Virtues


Explanation:

Propagate- to cause, to continue, or increase by sexual or asexual reproduction.

ex. We are discovering new ways to propagate plants without seeds.

Enrich- to make rich or richer especially by the addition or increase of some desirable quality, attribute, or ingredient.

ex. He used manure to enrich the soil.

Association- the act of associating or the state of being associated. (brotherhood, institution, organization)

ex. An association of local business leaders.

Chore- the regular or daily light work of a household or farm. A routine task or job.

ex. The children were each assigned different household chores.

Lilac- a widely cultivated European shrub of the olive family that has cordate ovate leaves and large panicles of fragrant pinkish-purple or white flowers. A variable color averaging a moderate purple.

ex. The perfume of that lady is a fresh lilac fragrance.

Virtues- a particular moral excellence or MORALITY.

ex. A lady of honor and Virtue.
