1. letter for barangay captain asking permission for festival in there barangay​


Answer:

Sample only. Reference purposes only.

Explanation:

Dear Barangay Head,

I am writing to request permission for an upcoming event that I am planning to hold in our barangay. The event is aimed at promoting environmental awareness and education among the residents of our community.

The event will take place on [date] at [time] in [location], which is a public area within our barangay. The activities for the event will include a tree planting activity, an information booth on environmental conservation, and a short lecture on the importance of sustainable living.

I would like to ask for your permission to use the public area for this event. I assure you that we will comply with all the rules and regulations set by our barangay to ensure the safety and orderliness of the event.

If granted permission, we will be responsible for the setup and cleanup of the area before and after the event. We will also make sure to coordinate with the barangay officials and staff to ensure that everything is in order.

I believe that this event will be a great opportunity for our community to come together and learn about environmental conservation. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

(your name)
