30. what is the history of pili nuts?​


Answer:

Natives of the Philippine archipelago were the first to gather Pili nuts growing wild below the tall jungle canopy. These ancient inhabitants of the Philippines used the kernel for food and boiled the pulp of the nut to make it edible. At some point, these ancient people began planting Pili seedlings near their huts.
