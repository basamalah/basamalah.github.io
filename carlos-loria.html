Answer:

Carlo's present age is 12 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Present age: x

Age in 4 years: x + 4

Age 4 years ago: x - 4

Equation:

x + 4 = 2(x-4)

x + 4 = 2x - 8

2x - x = 8+4

x = 12

Carlo's present age is 12 years old.

Present age: x = 12 years old

Age in 4 years: x + 4 = 12+4 = 16 years old

Age 4 years ago: x - 4 = 12-4 = 8 years old

16 = 2(8)
