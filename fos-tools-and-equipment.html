Answer:

1) THE FRONT OFFICE TOOLS, EQUIPMENT AND PARAPHERNALIA INCLUDE;

TOOLS:

Reception Desk-Is a Front Desk in a hotel where guests can books rooms or ask question/s.

Filling Cabinet-Is a piece of office furniture usually used to store paper documents in file folders.In the other term it's an enclosure for drawers.

Key Rack-Key Rack rther key holder are administrative personnel who are tasked with opening and closing a store and performing various administrative duties.

Safety Deposit Box-An individually secured container usually a metal box,used to keep valuables important documents,and sentimental keepsakes protected.

EQUIPMENT:

Computer-That can be instructed to Carry out sequences of arithmetic or logical operations automatically via computer programming.Also these programs enable to perform an extremely wide range of tasks.

Software-Is a set of instructions,data or programs used to operate computers and execute specific tasks.Its an generic term used to refer to applications,scripts and programs that run on device.

Internet-Is a vast network that connects computers all over the world.Through the internet ,people can share information and communicate anywhere with an internet connection.

Printer-Is a device that accepts text and graphic outpot from the computer and transfers the information to paper, usually to standard size sheets of paper.

Telephone-Converts sound, typically and most effeciently the human voice,into electronic signals that are transmitted via cables and other communication channels to another telephone which reproduces the sound to the receiver.

Fax Machine-Is a device that is used to send documents electronically over a telephone network.

Cash Register-A business machine that usually has a money drawer, indicate the amount of each sale,and records the amount of money received.

Credit Card Machine-Is a device that reads credit and debit cards in order to process and complete a transaction such as a sale or withdrawal.

PARAPHERNALIA:

Reservation Form-Is a primarily used by reservation department or by the hotel sales team to write down the booking details over phone or when they go for a sales call on client site

Pen-Is a common writing instrument used to apply ink to a surface, usually paper,for writing or drawing.

Calculator-Is a machine which allows people to do math operations more easily.Are found everywhere.

Cash Receipt-Is a printed acknowledgement of the amount of cash received during a transaction involving the transfer of cash or cash equivalent.

Guest Folio-An account which associated with a single hotel guest.All single guest information is also saved into the guest folio.

Log Book-Is a record of important details,events in the management, operation and navigation of a ship

Key Card-Is a small plastic card that can be used instead of a door key, bearing magnetically encoded data that can be read and processed by an electronic device.
