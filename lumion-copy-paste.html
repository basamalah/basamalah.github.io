Answer:

In summary, while copy-pasting can be a valuable tool, it is important to use it responsibly and be aware of its limitations. It's always better to ensure that the information you copy and use is accurate, relevant, and cited properly.

Explanation:

Copy-pasting is a useful tool that makes it easy to duplicate information from one source to another, saving time and effort. It is helpful in research, writing, and data entry, particularly when dealing with large amounts of data.
