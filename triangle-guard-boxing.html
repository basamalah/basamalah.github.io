6. 3. A roving guard in a certain high school is instructed not to go more than 200 meters from the guard houseJoseph is sleeping under a tree approximately 185 meters west and 190 meters south of the guard house Is itpossible that the roving guard will find Joseph?​


This problem can be converted to a triangle problem.

Use Pythagorean Theorem.

c²=a²+b²

c²=185²+190²

[tex]c = \sqrt{(34225) + (36100)} [/tex]

c = √70,325

c = 265.19 meters = distance of Joseph to the gaurd house

265.19 meters > 199 meters

Since the gaurd can only go up to 199 meters, he will not be able to find Joseph.
