18. The sum of the ages of Bobby and Mercy is 60. Nine years ago Bobby was three years more than one-half as Mercy was then. Find their age now.with solution pls


Answer:

Mercy is 39 years old and Bobby is 21 years old


Step-by-step explanation:

Define x:

Let Mercy be x


Find Bobby's age:

9 years ago, Bobby was 3 years more than 1/2 as Mercy then

Bobby's age 9 years ago = 1/2 (x - 9) + 3

Bobby's age now = 1/2 (x - 9) + 3 + 9

Bobby's age now = 1/2 x  - 9/2 + 3 + 9

Bobby's age now = 1/2 x  + 33/2


Solve x:

The sum of their age is 60

x + (1/2 x + 33/2) = 60

x + 1/2 x + 33/2  = 60

3/2 x = 87/2

x = 87/2 ÷ 3/2

x = 39


Find their age:

Mercy = x = 39 years old

Bobby = 60 - 38 = 21 years old


Answer: Mercy is 39 years old and Bobby is 21 years old
