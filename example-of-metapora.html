20. what is simili and metapora give me example please


Simile is a figure of speech that makes a comparison; it always uses the words LIKE or AS. Ex. As wise as an owl.

Metaphor is also a figure of speech which describes a person or an object by referring to something that shares the same characteristics; It doesn't use like or as. Ex. My brother is boiling mad.

Halimbawa:
