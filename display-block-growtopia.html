6. A block is pulled by constant force of 40N.If the work done by the force on the block is 680Nm.What is the displacement of the block?


Answer:

The displacement of the block is 17 meters.

Explanation:

Work is a force acting on a body while the body undergoes a displacement.

Please see the attached illustration of the problem given below:

The formula for work is:

[tex]W=Fs[/tex]

where

W    is the work, unit is in Newton-meter (N.m)

F     is the force, unit is in Newtons (N)

s     is the displacement, unit is in meters (m)

For the given information:

[tex]F=40N[/tex]

[tex]W=680N.m[/tex]

[tex]s=?[/tex]

Solving for the displacement, use the formula for work, then substitute the given information, we get:

[tex]W=Fs[/tex]

[tex]680N.m=(40N)s[/tex]

Divide both side of the equation by 40N, we get:

[tex]s=\frac{680N.m}{40N}[/tex]

[tex]s=17meters[/tex]

Therefore, the displacement is 17 meters.

26. A block is pulled by a constant force of 40 N. If the work done by the force on the block is 780 Joules. What is the displacement of the block W = F X D​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The work done by an object exerting a force F at a distance d is given by

[tex]\boxed{W = Fd}[/tex]

where:

W = work

F = force

d = distance

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for d

W = Fd

[tex]d = \dfrac{W}{F}[/tex]

[tex]d = \dfrac{\text{780 J}}{\text{40 N}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{d = \text{19.5 m}}[/tex]

Therefore, the displacement is 19.5 m.
