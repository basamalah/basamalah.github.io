4. 1. Which mixture/s is/are uniform in appearance? Why?2. Which mixture/s is/are non-uniform in appearance? Why?PIVOT 4A CALA16​


1. A homogeneous mixture is simply any mixture that is uniform in composition throughout. As long as each substance is mixed in enough to be indistinguishable from the others, it is a homogeneous mixture.

2. A heterogeneous mixture is simply any mixture that is not uniform in composition - it's a non-uniform mixture of smaller constituent parts. By contrast, a mixture that is uniform in composition is a homogeneous mixture.

8. what is the force acting on an object moving in uniform circular motion that is directed toward the center? ​


Answer:

According to Newton's second law, a non-zero net force is needed to cause acceleration. In the case of an object moving in a circular path the acceleration is directed toward the center of the circle. Therefore the net force is also directed toward the center. This net force is often called the centripetal force.

29. what is the force acting on an object moving in uniform circular motion that is directed toward the center?​


Answer:

the net force

Explanation:

The final motion characteristic for an object undergoing uniform circular motion is the net force. The net force acting upon such an object is directed towards the center of the circle. The net force is said to be an inward or centripetal force.
