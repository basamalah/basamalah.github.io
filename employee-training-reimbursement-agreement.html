8. 2. If the solidary debtor obtained remission of the whole obligation, is he entitled to reimbursement from his co- debtors?

1220. The remission of the whole obligation, obtained by one of the solidary debtors, does not entitle him to reimbursement from his co-debtors. (n) No right to reimbursement in case of remission. The reason for the above article is that the debtor who obtains remission pays nothing to the creditor.
