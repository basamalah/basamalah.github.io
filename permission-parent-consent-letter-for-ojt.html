2. Parent consent letter letting student attend activities in school


An example of a consent letter letting student to attend activities in school.

A consent letter is useful for many occasions. One example is how teachers know whether their students are allowed to take part in a school activity by means of a signed consent letter from the students' parents. The nature of this letter is formal, and usually it includes a signature from the guardian of the person who's asking for the permission to do something. There is also no fixed template, so any modification is always welcome in a consent letter.

A consent letter from parents goes something like:I, a guardian/ parent of [student name], who is a student at [school name and grade] have therefore allow [student name] to take part in [activity name] between [the schedule]. Here lies my signature, [signature and full name of the parents/ guardian].

" The Self-esteem of Students with OFW Parents in the Junior High School Department of Private Institutions: Basis for Improvement of an Existing Guidance Development Program"

Dear Respondents,

I would like to ask your assistance in my research survey.  I am currently enrolled in Master of Arts in Nursing in St. Augustine University  and I am in the process of writing my thesis entitled: " The Self-esteem of Students with OFW Parents in the Junior High School Department of Private Institutions: Basis for Improvement of Existing Guidance Development Program" . The purpose of the research is to determine the self-esteem of young individuals with overseas working parents as basis for an improvement of an existing guidance program.

Your participation in this research is voluntary and your responses will be kept confidential. No one other than the researcher/s will know your individual answers to this survey questionnaire.

Thank you so much for your assistance and May the Almighty Father bless you in all your endeavors in life.

Sincerely yours,

KEEZIEN T. ALCORAN

Researcher
