14. Diana is 6 years older than Danica. Four years ago, she was twice as old as Danica. Find their present age. Form the table:Equation:Solution:Answer:​


Problem:

Diana is 6 years older than Danica. Four years ago, she was twice as old as Danica. Find their present age.

Solution:

Let x be the Danica's Age

Then, let x + 6 be the Diana's age

Five years ago, she was twice as old as Danica.

(x + 6) - 4 = 2(x - 4)

Solve the value of x for the equation problem.

(x + 6) - 4 = 2(x - 4)x + 2 = 2x - 8x - 2x = -8 - 2-x = -10x = 10

Substitute the Diana's age to the present age which is the value of x to the solving problem.

x + 6(10) + 616

Answer:

Therefore, the present age of Danica is 10 yrs old and Diana is 16 yrs old.
