1. the role of emotional intelligence in leadership effectiveness:Ameta analysis,state of the problem?​


Answer:

The state of the problem regarding the role of emotional intelligence in leadership effectiveness is that it has become increasingly important for leaders to possess emotional intelligence skills in order to effectively lead their teams and organizations. Emotional intelligence refers to the ability to recognize and understand one's own emotions and those of others, and to use this information to guide decision-making and behavior.

Research has shown that leaders who possess higher levels of emotional intelligence are better able to manage their own emotions and those of their team members, communicate more effectively, build stronger relationships, and make better decisions. However, there is still a need for more research to better understand the specific ways in which emotional intelligence impacts leadership effectiveness.

To address this need, many studies have conducted meta-analyses of existing research on emotional intelligence and leadership effectiveness. These meta-analyses have consistently found a positive correlation between emotional intelligence and leadership effectiveness, indicating that emotional intelligence is an important factor in effective leadership.

Overall, the state of the problem is that while there is already evidence supporting the role of emotional intelligence in leadership effectiveness, there is still more research that needs to be done to fully understand this relationship and how it can be leveraged in practical leadership settings.

17. importance of emotional intelligence​


Answer:

Emotional intelligence is the capacity to understand and manage your emotions. The skills involved in emotional intelligence are self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills

Explanation:

Answer:

1. Self-Awareness  

It is to have knowledge about oneself, mainly the strengths and weaknesses. This enables a person to know how to react to different people and different situations that arise in the workplace. People who are self-aware, are in most cases, self-confident too. The only way to improve one’s Self-awareness is to step out from their comfort zone.

2. Self-Regulation  

It is the art of regulating one’s emotions to be able to manage and have self-control over it. also, necessitates the need to manage one’s thoughts, emotions, and behavior to get positive results.

3. Motivation  

People with high EI tend to be more positive and optimistic in their approach. Thus, making themselves highly motivated. Also, they are more flexible with their work and possess the ability to handle any situation.

4. Empathy  

Empathy is very important for every working person, especially for people in leadership roles. It is very important to put oneself in the other person’s shoes to ensure there is a good mutual connection between the employees.

5. Social Skills

A person with high EI always cares for others and also tends to get along well with everyone.
