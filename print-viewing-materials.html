17. printed viewing materials and online viewing materials ​


Printed Viewing Materials:

These are physical media such as books, magazines, newspapers, brochures, and posters, which are printed on paper or other materials. They are commonly used for information dissemination and advertising.

Online Viewing Materials:

These are digital media, such as websites, videos, e-books, and online articles, that can be viewed on the internet. They are easily accessible and can be updated in real-time, making them a popular choice for information dissemination and advertising.
