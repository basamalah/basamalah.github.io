Answer:

Alliyah and Abigail together can type 90 words per minute. How fast can Abigail type 700 words if Aliyah can type 300 words in 4 minutes?

~~~~~~~~~~~

Together they type 90 words per minute.

Individually, Aliyah can type 300 words in 4 minutes, or 300/4 = 75 words per minute.

Hence, Abigail types 90-75 = 15 words per minute.

To type 700 words, Abigail needs 700%2F15 = 46 2%2F3 minutes, or 46 minutes and 40 seconds.

Going back to the problem above, we need to express the mathematical phrases to algebraic equation.

Equation:

Let x be Cynthia's age.

Now:

"Abigail is 8 years older than Cynthia."

Cynthia - x

Abigail - x + 8

20 years ago:

"Twenty years ago, Abigail was three times as old as Cynthia."

Cynthia - 8 + x - 20

Abigail - 3 (x - 20)

8 + x - 20 = 3 (x - 20)

Solution:

x - 12 = 3x - 60

-3x + x = -60 + 12

-2x = -48

-2x/-2 = -48/-2

x = 24

Cynthia is 24 years old now.

Abigail = x + 8

= 24 + 8

= 32

Abigail is 32 years old now.
