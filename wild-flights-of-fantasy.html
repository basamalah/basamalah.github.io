17. There are 250 flights a day in an airport. 7/10 of the flights are international and the rest are domestic flights. How many of the flights are domestic?


Answer:

75 flights are domestic

Step-by-step explanation:

In this problem, we are given of total of 250 flights in a day and 7/10 of them are international. We need to calculate how many are domestic flights.

Step 1: Write the mathematical sentence of the problem.

250 - (250 x 7/10) = ?

Step 2: Determine how many are total international flights.

250 x [tex]\frac{7}{10}[/tex] = 175

Step 3: Subtract the number of resulting international flights from the total number of flights to determine the number of domestic flights.

250 - 175 = 75

Therefore, the total number of domestic flights is 75.

18. 1. There are 220 flights a day in an airport. 5/10 of the flights are international and the rest are domestic flights. How many of the flights are domestic?​


Answer:

110

Step-by-step explanation:

To find out how many flights are domestic, we need to first find out how many flights are international. We can do this by multiplying the total number of flights by the fraction of flights that are international: 220 flights * 5/10 = 110 flights.

Since there are 220 flights in total and 110 of them are international, there must be 220 - 110 = 110 domestic flights. Answer: \boxed{110}.
