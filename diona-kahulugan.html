24. Differentiate from Diona from Dalit​


Answer:

Diona is a pre-Hispanic rhyming poem of three lines with seven syllables in each line expressing a complete thought.

The dalit is a type of short Filipino poem, consisting of four lines with eight syllables each. There is a controversy regarding its origin. One school of thought states that the dalit is Spanish in origin, particularly because its syllabification is even or pares.
