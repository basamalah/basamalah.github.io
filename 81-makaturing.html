1. mount makaturing volcano located in?​


Answer:

Mt. Makaturing is located in the province of Lanao del Sur in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao. In short words, it is located in Mindanao, in the province of Lanao del Sur.


2. interesting facts of makaturing​


Answer:

Makaturing, is a stratovolcano on Mindanao island in the Philippines. It is found in the province of Lanao del Sur in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao. Makaturing has an elevation of 1,940 metres and a base diameter of 29 km. It is part of a string of volcanoes called the Central Mindanao Arc.

Last eruption: 1882

Elevation: 1,940 m
