PHILIPPINE LITERATURE

DYNAMIC is the best word to describe Philippines regional literature. It is dynamic because it paints a picture of various imagery, emotions, and themes. One could say that the dynamic literature of the Philippines is reflective of the experiences it had in the course of history.

Philippine literature is very impactful and affective to the Filipinos. Even before the Spanish Era, during the Pre-Spanish Period, Philippine literature was thriving. Folk songs and folk tales all take part in Philippine literature. These were passed from generation to generation which is why these literature can still be appreciated today.

During the Spanish Era, Philippine literature was used as a tool to spark nationalistic ideas in the Filipinos. Philippine literature was Jose Rizal’s tool to awaken revolution through his works Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo. During the regime of the Americans and the Japanese, Philippine literature continued on through poems and stories.
