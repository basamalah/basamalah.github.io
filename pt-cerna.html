6. Nelson Tero, Jay Dela Cerna and Paul Hermosilla are fond of buying stocks in crypto currency. What would be their simple interest after 5 years when they invested USD 1,000,000 if the value of crypto is at constant 5% rate. Please Help​


Answer:

In order to calculate the simple interest, we need the principal amount, the interest rate, and the time period.

Given:

Principal amount: USD 1,000,000Interest rate: 5% (constant)Time period: 5 years

Simple Interest = (Principal x Interest Rate x Time Period) / 100

Simple Interest = (1,000,000 x 5 x 5) / 100

Simple Interest = USD 250,000

So, Nelson Tero, Jay Dela Cerna, and Paul Hermosilla would earn a simple interest of USD 250,000 after 5 years if they invested USD 1,000,000 in cryptocurrency at a constant 5% rate.
