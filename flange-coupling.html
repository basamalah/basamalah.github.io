1. The total force of 125 kn is required of flange coupling that has 5 bolts has also shearing stress of 30 mpa for bolt. determine the required diameter of bolt.


If the total force of 125 kilo-Newton is required for flange coupling that has 5 bolts and also has a shearing stress of 30 mega-Pascal, the required diameter of the bolt is 0.07 meters.

How to solve it?

We know that the formula for shear stress is [tex]\tau=\frac{F}{A}[/tex].

Where:

[tex]\tau[/tex] is the shear stress

F is the applied force

A is the cross-sectional area

Note: The formula for getting the cross-sectional area is [tex]\frac{\pi }{4 d^2}[/tex].

Solution:

d= [tex]\sqrt{ \frac{4F}{\pi \tau} }[/tex] = [tex]\sqrt{\frac{4(120kN)}{\pi (30MPa)} }[/tex] = 0.072836562 meters
