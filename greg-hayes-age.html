3. Joe's present age is three times Greg's present age. Five years ago, the sum of their ages was 14. What is each boy's present age?​


Present Ages of Joe and Greg:

Joe is 6 years old.

Greg is 18 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Age Word Problem

Age problems are word problems dealing with the present ages of people, in the past or the future. The problem above is asking for the present ages of Joe and Greg. To find the answer, you have to identify the known and unknown in the problem.

Given:

Joe - three times Greg's present age

14 - the sum of their ages five years ago

Present Age:

Let x be Greg's present age.

Then, 3x be Joe's present age since his age is three times as Greg's.

Five Years Ago:

Greg - x - 5

Joe - 3x - 5

Equation:

(x - 5) + (3x - 5) = 14

Solution:

Greg's Present Age:

Combine similar terms.

(x - 5) + (3x - 5) = 14

4x - 10 = 14

4x = 14 + 10

4x = 24

4x/4 = 24/4

x = 6

Greg's present age is 6 years old.

Joe's Present Age:

To find Joe's age, plug in the value of x which is 6.

3x = 3(6) = 18

Joe's present age is 18 years old.

Final Answer:

Greg's present age is 6 years old.

Joe's present age is 18 years old.

Checking:

(x - 5) + (3x - 5) = 14

(6 - 5) + (18 - 5) = 14

1 + 13 = 14

14 = 14 ✔

Reverse engineering.

(X / 2) + 4 = 29.

(29 - 4) x 2 = X

29 - 4 = 25

25 x 2 = 50

Therefore, 50 is the answer.
