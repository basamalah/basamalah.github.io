1. Write an "informative speech" about Mobile Games . The speech should also include not less than 5 cohesive devices.​


Answer:

Good evening everyone,

Today I'd like to talk to you about mobile games - a topic that I'm sure is near and dear to many of our hearts. Mobile games have become an increasingly popular form of entertainment over the years, with millions of people around the world spending hours each day playing their favorite games on their phones or tablets.

Firstly, let me begin by defining what we mean by mobile games. Mobile games are games that are played on smartphones, tablets, or other handheld devices. They come in a variety of genres, from casual games like Candy Crush to more complex games like role-playing games and first-person shooters.

Now, let's talk about why mobile games are so popular. One of the main reasons is their accessibility. With most people carrying their smartphones with them at all times, mobile games are always within reach. In addition, many mobile games are free to download and play, making them an easy and affordable way to pass the time.

Another reason for their popularity is the social aspect. Many mobile games allow players to connect with friends and other players online, making the gaming experience more interactive and engaging. This also creates a sense of community among players, which can be particularly important during times of isolation and social distancing.

Despite their popularity, some people may view mobile games as a waste of time or a distraction from more important things. However, research has shown that playing mobile games can have several benefits, including improving cognitive function, reducing stress and anxiety, and increasing social interaction.

Now, let's move on to cohesive devices. Cohesive devices are words or phrases that help to connect ideas and make a text or speech more coherent. Here are some examples of cohesive devices I will be using in this speech:

Firstly, as I mentioned earlier, cohesive devices can be used to indicate the order of ideas. In this speech, I've used "firstly" to introduce my first point about defining mobile games.

Secondly, cohesive devices can be used to add information to a previous point. For example, I used "in addition" to add more information to my point about the accessibility of mobile games.

Moreover, cohesive devices can be used to show contrast between ideas. For instance, I used "despite their popularity" to introduce a contrasting point about the negative perceptions some people have of mobile games.

Furthermore, cohesive devices can be used to summarize or conclude an argument. Towards the end of my speech, I'll use phrases like "in conclusion" and "to sum up" to wrap up my main points.

Finally, cohesive devices can be used to give examples to support a point. For example, I used "from casual games like Candy Crush to more complex games like role-playing games and first-person shooters" to give examples of different types of mobile games.

To sum up, mobile games are a popular form of entertainment that provide accessibility, social interaction, and cognitive benefits. They are not just a waste of time, but can have several positive effects on our well-being. So next time someone tells you to put down your phone and stop playing games, you can tell them that you're actually improving your cognitive function and reducing stress!

Thank you.

Persuasive Speech on the Benefits of Mobile Games

Ladies and gentlemen,

Today, I would like to talk to you about mobile games and why they are more than just a source of entertainment. Mobile games have become a global phenomenon and are enjoyed by people of all ages. However, many still view them as a waste of time or even harmful to our well-being. I am here to persuade you otherwise.

Firstly, let's consider the cognitive benefits of mobile games. Studies have shown that playing games can improve cognitive function, such as attention, memory, and problem-solving skills. Games like Candy Crush, Sudoku, and Brain Training apps can stimulate the brain and enhance mental agility.

Secondly, mobile games can be a great stress-reliever. Whether it's a quick round of Angry Birds or a longer gaming session, playing games can be a fun way to unwind and forget about our daily stresses. It can also provide a sense of achievement and boost self-esteem.

Thirdly, mobile games can be an excellent social activity. Many games allow players to connect with others and play together, creating a sense of community and shared experience. Online multiplayer games like Fortnite and Among Us have become popular worldwide and provide a fun way to connect with friends and family.

Moreover, mobile games can also be educational. Many games are designed to teach new skills or reinforce existing ones. For example, language learning games like Duolingo can help users improve their language skills while having fun.

Lastly, mobile games can be a source of inspiration and creativity. Many games allow players to create their content, such as levels, characters, and stories. This encourages creativity and can even lead to the development of new skills like coding and game design.
