28. LIMANG MGA NAITATAG NI SHARIF KABUNGSUAN NA SULTANATO​


Answer:

﻿Maguindanao, Sulu o Sulo, Cotabato, at Lanao – ito ang apat na sultanatong itinatag ni Sharif Kabungsuan.

Si Mohammed Kabungsuan o mas kilalang Sharif Kabungsuan ay isang tubong Johor, Malaysia na dumako sa Pilipinas upang ipangaral ang relihiyong Islam.

Siya ay pumunta sa ikatlong pinakamalaking pulo ng bansa, ang Mindanao. Doon ay kanyang sinimulan ang apat na sultanatong nabanggit sa itaas.

Isang uri ng pamahalaan ang sultanato. Nakapangasawa si Sharif Kabungsuan ng isang lokal at doon na umusbong ang kanilang paghahari sa lalawigan.

Kung ikaw ay bibisita sa Mindanao, partikular na sa lalawigan ng Maguindanao, ay may isang bayan doon na ipinangalan kay Sharif Kabungsuan.

Explanation:

Kabungsuwan is generally regarded as the one who introduced Islam in the Lanao and Maguindanao areas in Mindanao arriving in the area in the early 16th century. There are several tarsilas or written genealogy on Kabungsuwan though most of these state that he brought in men when he landed in Mindanao, his group are composed of seafarers, there was initial force with his group's interaction with the locals and that there were already Muslims in Mindanao when his Kangungsuwan and his men landed near the mouth of the Pulangi River. Kabungsuwan formed alliances with influential royal families of Sulu, Borneo, and Ternate. This led to Islam becoming the dominant religion around Lake Lanao by the 19th century.[5]
