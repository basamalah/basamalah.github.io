7. What Are These Stages Of Human Lifespan That Erik Erikson?


Answer:

Psychoanalyst Erik Erikson's stages of psychosocial development theorize a model of human psychological growth made up of eight stages that cover the entire lifespan from birth to old age.

Explanation:

Psychoanalyst Erik Erikson's stages of psychosocial development theorize a model of human psychological growth made up of eight stages that cover the entire lifespan from birth to old age. Each stage is defined by a central crisis that the individual must grapple with to move on to the next stage.

20. what is generativity by erik erikson?


Answer:

The term generativity was coined by the psychoanalyst Erik Erikson in 1950 to denote "a concern for establishing and guiding the next generation." He first used the term while defining the Care stage in his theory of the stages of psychosocial development.
