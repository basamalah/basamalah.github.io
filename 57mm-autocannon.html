Answer:

1. Since C is the midpoint of line CB, then line CA is equal to line CB. If line CA measures 20cm, then line CB also measures 20cm.

2. Since point M is between points S and G, then the length of line SG is equal to the sum of the lengths of line SM and line MG. If SM measures 5mm and MG measures 27mm, then SG measures:

SG = SM + MG = 5mm + 27mm = 32mm

3. Since point A is the midpoint of line MC, then line MA is equal to line MC. If line MA measures 15mm, then line MC also measures 15mm.

4. Let x be the length of line GL. Since E is found 2/3 of GL, then:

E = 2/3(x)

Since GI = EL, then:

37mm = x + 2/3(x)

Multiplying both sides by 3:

111mm = 3x + 2x

111mm = 5x

x = 22.2mm

Since GC = GL - CL, and CL = 2/3(GL), then:

GC = GL - 2/3(GL) = 1/3(GL)

GC = 1/3(22.2mm) = 7.4mm

Therefore, GC measures 7.4mm.

5. Since point A is the midpoint of line MP, then line MA is equal to line AP. If MA measures 9cm and AP measures 13cm, then:

MA + AP = MP

9cm + 13cm = MP

22cm = MP

Therefore, MP measures 22cm.
