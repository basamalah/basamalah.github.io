29. Darlene started her savings account with $75.30. The account has a simple interest rate of 3.96%. How much will she have at the end of 2 years? Explain your thinking.


Answer:

Darlene has P 81.26 in her savings after two years.

Step-by-step explanation:

Simple

Interest = Principal×Interest Rate x

Time

= 75.30 x 3.96% x 2

= 5.96

= 75.30 + 5.96 = 81.26

Darlene has P 81.26 in her savings account at the end of two years. She earns P 5.96 as interest in her money in two years. Her money earns an interest of P 2.96 per year.

I = Prt   where 

Principal (P) is the amount of money deposited or borrowed. 

Interest Rate (r) is a percent of the principal earned or paid. 

Time (t) is the length of time the money is deposited or borrowed.
