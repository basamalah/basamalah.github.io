1. Percy is currently twice as old as Nico. If 8 is subtracted from Nico’s age, and 4 is added to Percy’s age, Percy will be four times as old as Nico. What is Percy’s age now?


Percy’s age : 36, Nico'age : 18

Further Explanation  

One variable linear equation is an equation that has a variable and the exponent number is one.  

Can be stated in the form:  

 [tex]\rm \boxed{\bold{ax=b}}[/tex]

or  

ax + b = c,

where a, b, and c are constants, x is a variable  

Whereas the two-variable linear equation is a linear equation that has 2 variables and the exponent is one  

Can be stated in the form:  

[tex]\rm \boxed{\bold{ax+by=c}}[/tex]  

x, y = variable  

The above problem can be stated as the two-variable linear equation, with:

P = Percy's age

N = Nico's age

Percy is currently twice as old as Nico, expressed in the form of algebraic equations :

[tex]\rm P=2N~(eq~1)[/tex]

If 8 is subtracted from Nico’s age, and 4 is added to Percy’s age, Percy will be four times as old as Nico, expressed in the form of algebraic equations :

[tex]\rm 4(N-8)=4+P~(eq~2)[/tex]

we combine the 2 equations above :

[tex]\rm 4(N-8)=4+2N\\\\4N-32=4+2N\\\\4N-2N==32+4\\\\2N=36\\\\N=18\\\\P=2N\RightarrowP=2.18=\boxed{\bold{36}}[/tex]

26. what are the characteristics of percy jackson​


Answer:

Percy Jackson is described as handsome, with messy jet black hair, a "Mediterranean" complexion, and sea-green eyes like his father Poseidon. His mother notes that Percy looked just like his father, and his friend Hazel says that he has the looks of a Roman god.

Percy is good-natured, friendly, brave, and willing to risk his life to save his friends, strangers, and even his enemies. He is angered by Thalia Grace because their personalities are similar, they argue even though they are good friends. Percy has a sarcastic sense of humor and is funny in his writing.

Even when Percy faces temptation, he chooses loyalty over money, rewards, and personal gain. Percy is an example of a true hero because he doesn't compromise his integrity or trustworthiness for power and fame. He often rescues those he loves without any thought of his own safety.
