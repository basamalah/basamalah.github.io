12. What is the summary of The Parable of the Rainbow Colors?


Answer:

Summary of The Parable of The Rainbow:

The colors Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo and Violet are having arguments on who should be the best color. It reached to a point that they are actually quarreling with each other.

The below are the claims of each other on why they should be the best color:

RED:

The color of blood, and the most striking color. represents love and bravery.

ORANGE:

The color of sunrise, represents health and strenght.

YELLOW:

The color of the sun, the color of gaiety and warmth.

GREEN:

The symbol of life, the color of fields and greenery.

BLUE:

The color of the expansive sky and sea, represents patriotism.

INDIGO:

The color of silence, represents thoughtfulness and prayers.

VIOLET:

The color of royalty and power, represents wisdom and authority.

The quarreling stopped when rain interrupted. Rain informed the colors to that God asked him to stop their arguments on who is the best color and that God has made a decision. Rain said that God decided that the colors shall not be seen separate but as one after an out pour of rain. The group of colors shall also be named as RAINBOW.
