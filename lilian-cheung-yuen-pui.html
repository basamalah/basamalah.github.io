Let "x" be the number of PUIs;

2:3 = 60:x

Convert to fraction form

2/3 = 60/x

Multiply (3) and (x) both sides.

(Cross Multiplication)

(x)(3)(2/3) = (x)(3)(60/x)

Simplify

2x = 180

Divide both sides by (2)

x=90
