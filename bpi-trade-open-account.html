2. How to know if bpi account is still active?


To check if your BPI account is still active, just try to access it at any BPI ATM or call their 24-hour hotline, 89-100.

Only active accounts can be viewed on BPI expressonline. If the status of your account becomes inactive or closed, your account will not be displayed anymore. The system automatically removes online access to these accounts because of their status.

If you want to know other BPI account if it is active, you can go to any BPI branch over-the-counter, then try to ask the teller (make a reason, like you want to deposit to the account). She can then check if the account you're inquiring is still active.

5. (tco 8) the _____ keeps world trade open by cutting tariffs and other barriers, creating freer trade and settling disputes.


The World Trade Organization began on January 1st 1995. It is a place where member governments go, to try to sort out the trade problems they face with each other.  WTO's work includes the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT) wherein countries have faced barriers and wanted them lowered, the negotiations have helped to liberalize trade. But the WTO is not just about liberalizing trade in some circumstances its rules  support maintaining trade barriers - for example to protect consumers or prevent the spread of disease.

The documents provide the legal ground rules for international commerce. They are essentially contracts, binding governments to keep their trade policies, within agreed limits. The system's overriding purpose is to help trade flow as freely as possible- so long as there are no undersirable side-effects because this is important for economic development and well-being.

21. In accounting for debt investments that are classified as trading investments


Answer:

Debt investments classified as trading are reported at fair value, with unrealized holding gains and losses reported as part of net income. Trading and available-for-sale debt securities should be reported at fair value, whereas held-to-maturity debt securities should be reported at amortized cost.

23. Which of the following statements about trade is not correct? a. Trade generates jobs in both export and import sectors of an economy. b. The gains from open trade are always greater than the losses. c. Open trade and investment does create winners and losers. d. Trade does not influence culture. e. Trade is the two-way flow of exports and imports of goods and services.


The statements above about trade that is not correct is a (D) Trade does not influence culture.

Explanation :

Trade refers to the process of exchanging goods or services between economic actors, namely traders (producers/distributors) and buyers (consumers). Since transactions are consensual, trading generally and preferably benefits both parties. In finance, trading refers to the buying and selling of securities or other assets.

Trade can affect culture, especially during the globalization era. Trade can bring a culture to new areas that become consumers. This is because culture is packaged in the form of goods or services.

An example is how western culture can influence eastern countries with various products such as food, technology, fashion to various public services that were not previously found in eastern countries such as the Southeast Asian region. So the statement "Trade does not influence culture" is not true.

29. Epekto ng Open Trade​


EPEKTO NG OPEN TRADE

Ang open trade ay ang malayang pakikipagkalakalan ng isang bansa. Nagiging madali ang pagluwas o pag-angkat ng mga produkto dahil sa pagtanggal ng mga balakid sa kalakalan. Dahil walang buwis ang mga produkto mula sa ibang bansa, ang mga ito ay naipagbibili sa murang halaga kaysa sa lokal na mga produkto.

NARITO ANG MGA ILAN NG MGA EPEKTO NG OPEN TRADE:

Pagtindi ng kahirapan sa ating bansa dahil sa pagsusulong at pagbibigay ng pabor sa mga dambuhalang kapitalista at dayuhang namumunuhan. Ang ating bansa ay nahihirapan hanggang ngayon dahil sa Covid-19.

Pagdami ng mga taong walang trabaho dahil natatalo ang maliliit na negosyo. Dumadami ang mga tao na walang trabaho dahil sa maliit na negosyo.

Pagbaba ng halaga ng sahod ng mga manggagawa. Pinapaba nila ang sahod ng mga manggagawa.

Pagsasara o pagkalugi ng mga lokal na kompanya at industriya na hindi makasabay sa pandaigdigang kompetisyon. Pinasara o pinalugi nila ang mga lokal na kompanya ay industriya dahil hindi sila makasabay sa pandaigdigang kompetisyon.
