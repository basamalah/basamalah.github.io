6. which of the following statements describes the difference between 8¹² and 12⁸?A. in 8¹², 8 is used as a factor 12 times while in 12⁸, 8 is used as a factor 12 times.B. in 8¹², 12 is used as a factor 8 times while in 12⁸, 12 is used as a factor 8 times.C. in 8¹², 12 is the base and 8 is the exponent while in 12⁸, 8 is the base and 12 is the exponent.D. in 8¹², 12 is the exponent and 8 is the base while in 12⁸, 12 is the base and 8 is the exponent. ​


EXPONENT

—————————————————

Question:

Which of the following statements describes the difference between 8¹² and 12⁸?

Answer: D. In 8¹², 12 is the exponent and 8 is the base while in 12⁸, 12 is the base, and 8 is the exponent.

—————————————————

Choices:

⦾ A. In 8¹², 8 is used as a factor 12 times while in 12⁸, 8 is used as a factor 12 times.

⦾ B. In 8¹², 12 is used as a factor 8 times while in 12⁸, 12 is used as a factor 8 times.

⦾ C. In 8¹², 12 is the base, and 8 is the exponent while in 12⁸, 8 is the base and 12 is the exponent.

⦿ D. In 8¹², 12 is the exponent and 8 is the base while in 12⁸, 12 is the base, and 8 is the exponent.

—————————————————

Explanation:

• Letter D is the correct answer. In 8¹², 12 is the exponent and 8 is the base while in 12⁸, 12 is the base, and 8 is the exponent. Always remember, that base indicates the number that will be multiplied by itself, while, the exponent is the number that indicates how many times that base will be multiplied by itself.
