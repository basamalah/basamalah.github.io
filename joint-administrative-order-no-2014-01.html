10. arrange the intergers in each group in the ascending order or according to their order on the number line from left to right[tex] - 3 - 2 - 4 - 01[/tex]​


[tex]\Large\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

Arrange the intergers in each group in the ascending order or according to their order on the number line from left to right. [tex] - 3, - 2, - 4, - 01[/tex]

Ascending order means greatest number to lowest number. By arranging this given integers we must figure out first the greatest number among them and then the next one and the next one and so on.

[tex] \qquad \qquad \sf{ - 01, - 2, - 3, - 4} \\ \qquad \qquad \sf{ - 3 - 3 - 4} \\ \qquad \qquad \sf{ - 6 - 4} \\ \qquad \qquad \sf{ - 10} \\ [/tex]

Therefore, when we arrange the given integer the outcome will be -01, -2, -3,-4 and by solving it we get -10 as our answer.
