16. del superior gobierno

Ang Del Superior Govierno ay ang kauna-unahang pahayagan sa Pilipinas na lumaganap noong 1811 hanggang 1832.

WHAT IS DEL SUPERIOR GOBIERNO

The Del Superior Gobeirno is the first Philippine newspaper was established in 1811. Del Superior Govierno was published with the Spanish Governor General himself as editor. Its intended readers were the local Spaniards and therefore the content was primarily news from Spain.
