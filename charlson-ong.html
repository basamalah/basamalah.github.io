Charlson Ong's award-winning novel An Embarrassment of Riches (2000) creatively narrates the history of the Chinese diaspora as an overdetermined event in the Southeast Asian Region. The novel explores the politics of belonging, displacement, identity, and territory through a/n (re)imagined nation built primarily on Philippine historical and cultural contingencies. This essay seeks to explore the junctures and vicissitudes of the Chinese diaspora in the region through the following questions: How is hybridity manifested by selected characters and other cultural elements in the narrative? Second, how do the perceptions of homeland, marked by ambivalence and contradiction, operate in the narrative? And third, how does the memory of the characters' homeland or the lack of it contribute to identity formation? Furthermore, an analysis of the novel's form has been conducted, possibly asserting the notion that the genre is also a hybridized entity. This paper illustrates the complexity of the Chinese diaspora in the region and its bearing to identity formation.

29. Dr. Lao, a physicist, and Dr. Bela-ong, a biologist, partnered for a research project sponsored by the Department of Science and Technology. Dr. Lao is four years older than Dr. Bela-ong. In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. How old is Dr. Bela-ong?


AGE-RELATED PROBLEMS

Problem:

Dr. Lao, a physicist, and Dr. Bela-ong, a biologist, partnered for a research project sponsored by the Department of Science and Technology. Dr. Lao is four years older than Dr. Bela-ong. In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. How old is Dr. Bela-ong?

Answer: Dr. Bela-ong is 36 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us gather the clues that we can use to answer this age related word problem:

Dr. Lao is 4 years older than Dr. Bela-ong. So let us say the the variable x - 4 represents Dr. Bela-ong's age while x represents Dr. Lao's age. These are their present ages.In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. This one is quite tricky but we need to analyze this one. After 8 years, Dr. Lao will be 8 times older than Dr. Bela-ong's age 30 years ago so: x  + 8  is Dr. Lao's age after 8 years and (x - 4) - 30 is Dr. Bela-ong's age 30 years ago. In an equation, we can express this as:x + 8 = 8[ x - 4 - 30 ]    => Combine like terms.x + 8 = 8[ x - 34]          => Distribute 8 to all terms inside the brackets.x + 8 = 8x - 272           => Transpose each terms to combine like terms.8x - x = 272 + 8           => Simplify.7x = 280                     => Divide both sides by 7 to isolate x.x = 40

So we already have the value of x which is Dr. Lao's age. By direct substitution, we can obtain Dr. Bela-ong's age:

Dr. Bela-ong's age = x - 4 = 40 - 4 = 36

Therefore, Dr. Bela-ong's age is 36 years old.
