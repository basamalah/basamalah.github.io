7. Excuse Letter for Being absent in school due to Fever


Answer:

EXCUSE LETTER

Excuse letter is a formal written notification  asking for apology,  it is a note of explanation of an absence. Excuse letter is expected from a students who has unexpectedly missed a day in school.  Excuse letter should include time and the reason of absence. The letter is usually short and simple that contains basic facts.

Parts of an excuse letter

1. Heading - It includes the return address and date

2.Greetings

3. Body

4. Closing

Example of an excuse letter

May 7, 2019

123 Kamias Street, Paranaque

Metro Manila, Philippines

Dear Maam:

Good day!

Please excuse me for being absent in your class today, July 5, 2019 because I have a fever. I was not able to go to school because I feel so dizzy.  I hope that you will understand me regarding this matter and allow me to have a remedial activities on the lesson I had missed.

Thank you for your kind consideration.

Sincerely yours,

ANGEL VEM

Grade 3 - Ilang-Ilang

24. Excuse letter for being absent in school because of birthday of grandpa


AN EXCUSE LETTER FOR BEING ABSENT IN SCHOOL

(Date)

Dear Ma’am/ Sir,

Good day.

Please excuse me for being absent from your class because my grandfather is holding his birthday celebration today and he wishes the presence of all his grandchildren which includes me.  

I appreciate your consideration regarding this matter. I promise to be present tomorrow.

Respectfully yours,

(NAME WRITTEN IN UPPERCASE WITH SIGNATURE ABOVE IT)

Your pupil/student

WHAT IS AN EXCUSE LETTER?

An excuse letter is usually used in schools for pupils/students to be excused from their class or be exempted from school works because of some important or valid reasons.

HOW TO WRITE AN EXCUSE LETTER Write the date on top of the paper, may it be on the left or right side depending on the style of the letter that you want to use. Write you greetings like Ma’am or Sir regarding what gender does your teacher have or if there are both, you can use Ma’am/ Sir just like the sample above. Write the body of your letter starting of  “Please excuse me” since that is the purpose of your letter - to be excused and then state your reason of being absent. You can promise something that you will really do for the next day just like the one written in the sample or you may not promise at all. In the ending part of your body, you may say “Thank you for your kind consideration”or “I hope that you will give me a kind consideration regarding this matter” or you can copy from the sample above. Lastly, write your complimentary close which in the sample is written as “respectfully yours” because it means that you respect  the one or ones whom you give the letter to. Below it, is your name written in all capital letters and your signature above it. THREE STYLES OF WRITING A LETTER Full block style - all the parts are written on the left side of the paper and there is no indention in the paragraphs in the body of the letter just like the sample Modified block - the return address (if business letter), date, complimentary closing and the signature are written somewhere on the right side of the paper; the paragraphs in the body of the letter are indented Semi-block - all parts or elements of the letter is written on the left side but the paragraphs in the body of the letter use indention
