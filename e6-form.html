To express 5.2 as a fraction, we can observe that it has one decimal place. Therefore, we can write 5.2 as:

5.2 = 52/10

To express 52/10 in its lowest terms, we can divide both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common factor (GCF), which is 2:

52/10 = (2 * 2 * 13)/(2 * 5) = 26/5

Therefore, the fraction equivalent of 5.2 in its lowest terms is 26/5.
