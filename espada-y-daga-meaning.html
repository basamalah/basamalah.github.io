2. espada e daga word and danger with which employs​


Answer:

ESPADA Y DAGA (sword and dagger) - which employs a long blade and short dagger, it is a system that is unique to Filipino Martial Arts due to its combination of long and short weapon, which teaches the partitioner to transition from Long to mid or mid to short ranges and vice versa. This can be achieved by using footwork that can transcend all three ranges (largo, Medio, corto)
