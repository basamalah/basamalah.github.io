20. Mrs. Margarita has a garden shaped like a half-shaped circle with a diameter of 55 m. What's the area of Mrs. Margarita's garden? (Give your answer correct to 2 decimal places.) a. The area is 1187.31 square meters. b. The area is 1188.09 square meters. c. The area is 1187.50 square meters. d. None of the above​


Answer:

A. The area is 1187.31 square meters

Step-by-step explanation:

A= πr²

r= d/2

A= π(55/2)²

A= 2374.625

In the given situation, the garden is a semi circle so we'll divide the area to 2.

2374.625/2= 1187.31 m²
