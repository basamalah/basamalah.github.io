10. An event has 50 female contestants and 13 male contestants. A random sample of 12 contestant is drawn. What is the probability that exactly 8 of the contestant will be female?


Answer:

0.143855763 or 14.3855763%

Step-by-step explanation:

Probability of selecting female = 50C8

Probability fo selecting male = 13C4

Using Hypergeometric Probability Distribution:

P = [ (50C8) x (13C4) ] ÷ (63C12)

Probability = 0.143855763 or 14.3855763%
