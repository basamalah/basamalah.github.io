10. Liza has P24 more than Karen, Together, they have P174. How much money has Liza?​


Answer:

Liza has P99.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's use variables to represent the amounts of money that Liza and Karen have.

Let x be the amount of money that Karen has.

According to the problem, Liza has P24 more than Karen, so Liza has x + 24.

Together, they have P174, so we can write an equation:

x + (x + 24) = 174

Simplifying the equation:

2x + 24 = 174

Subtracting 24 from both sides:

2x = 150

Dividing both sides by 2:

x = 75

Therefore, Karen has P75, and Liza has P75 + P24 = P99.

To check, we can verify that their total amount of money is P174:

P75 + P99 = P174

So the answer is Liza has P99.

Liza's Age = L

Ana's Age + Liza's Age = 45

A = 45 – L

6 years ago, Ana's Age is twice Liza's Age

A – 6 = 2(L – 6)

(45 – L)–6 = 2L –12

45 – 6 – L = 2L – 12

39 + 12 = 2L + L

3L = 51

L = 51/3

L = 17
