8. who among the opera composer of romantic period attract you the most?why?1.Franz Peter Schubert2.Giuseppe Verdi3.Giacomo Puccini4.Richard Wagner 5.Georges Bizet​


Answer:

Richard Wagner

Explanation:

Richard Wagner composed dramatic operas and expressive musical works that had a revolutionary influence on the course of Western music, either by extension of his discoveries or in reaction against them. Among his major works are The Flying Dutchman (1843), Tannhäuser (1845), Lohengrin (1850), Tristan und Isolde (1865), Parsifal (1882), and his great tetralogy, The Ring of the Nibelung (1869–76). No composer has had so deep an influence on the course of his art, before or since. Entrepreneur, philosopher, poet, conductor, one of the key composers in history and most remarkable men of the 19th century, Wagner knew he was a genius. He was also an unpleasant, egocentric and unscrupulous human being.

23. What does sonnet 29 by george santayana means? (line by line analysis)


Explanation:

One thing that is unique about sonnet 29 by George Santayana is that the topic is social and communal rather than individual and romantic. This is a poem in which the poetic speaker defends himself from pessimistic elements in his society who see death and suffering as inherently tragic.
