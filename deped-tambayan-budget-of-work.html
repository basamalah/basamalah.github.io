17. work sample of budget plan ​


Answer:

Here is a simple work sample of a budget plan:

Step-by-step explanation:

Budget Plan for the Month of May 2023

Income:

- Salary: $4,000

- Freelance Work: $500

Total Income: $4,500

Expenses:

- Rent: $1,200

- Utilities: $150

- Groceries: $400

- Transportation: $200

- Internet & Phone: $100

- Entertainment: $150

- Savings: $1,000

- Miscellaneous: $200

Total Expenses: $3,500

Net Income: $1,000

In this example, the budget plan is for the month of May 2023. The income sources for the month include a salary of $4,000 and $500 from freelance work, bringing the total income to $4,500. The expenses for the month include rent, utilities, groceries, transportation, internet and phone bills, entertainment, savings, and miscellaneous expenses, totaling $3,500. The net income for the month is $1,000, which can be used for additional savings or unexpected expenses.

This is a simple example of a budget plan, and the actual plan may vary depending on individual circumstances and financial goals. It is important to create a budget plan that is realistic and achievable, and to revisit and adjust the plan as needed.
