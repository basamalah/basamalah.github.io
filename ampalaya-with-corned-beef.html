11. baket walang mais sa corned beef?


Answer:

Dahil hindi specifically umiibig sabihin na ang pangalan na ''corned beef'' ay ito ang mga ingredients na karne at mais. Originally, ang salitang “corn” ay galing sa Germanic na salita na “kurnam,” umiinig sabihin na “small seed.” Ang pangalan ng corned beef ay galing sa proseso mg dry curing na pang-preserve ng karne noon. Ang slice ng beef ay linagyan ng “corns” (large, coarse pellets of salt), na ipinapalabas ang moisture at para maiiwasan ang karamihan ng bacteria.


12. appearance of corn beef​


Answer:

Corned beef, or salt beef in the UK and Commonwealth, is salt-cured brisket of beef. The term comes from the treatment of the meat with large-grained rock salt, also called "corns" of salt. Corned beef may still be pink in color after cooking. Sometimes, sugar and spices are also added to corned beef recipes.
