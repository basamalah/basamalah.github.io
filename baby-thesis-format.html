Title: The Effect of Different Levels of Chicken Manure on Survival and Growth of Pomelo Seedlings

Introduction:

Pomelo (Citrus maxima) is a popular fruit tree in many parts of the world due to its delicious fruit and health benefits. However, like any other plant, pomelo seedlings require proper nutrients and care for optimal growth and survival. One of the important nutrients needed by plants is nitrogen, which is essential for growth and development. Chicken manure is an excellent source of nitrogen, and it also contains other essential nutrients that can promote the growth and health of plants.

Objective:

The objective of this study is to determine the effect of different levels of chicken manure on the survival and growth of pomelo seedlings.

Methodology:

The study will be conducted in a randomized complete block design (RCBD) with four treatments and five replications. The treatments are as follows: T1 (Control), T2 (2% chicken manure), T3 (4% chicken manure), and T4 (6% chicken manure). The pomelo seeds will be germinated in a nursery for 3 weeks before transplanting to plastic pots filled with soil and chicken manure mixtures. The growth parameters such as plant height, stem diameter, and leaf area will be measured weekly for a period of 8 weeks. The data collected will be analyzed using analysis of variance (ANOVA) and means separated using Duncan's multiple range test.

Expected Results:

It is expected that the use of chicken manure as a source of nitrogen will have a positive effect on the growth and survival of pomelo seedlings. It is also expected that the growth and survival rate of the pomelo seedlings will increase as the level of chicken manure increases.

Conclusion:

The results of this study will provide valuable information on the use of chicken manure as a source of nitrogen for pomelo seedlings. The findings will be beneficial to farmers and gardeners who are interested in growing healthy pomelo trees. The study will also highlight the importance of proper fertilization and care for the growth and survival of pomelo seedlings.

19. please can someone help how to make baby thesis my topic is all about “The effect of different levels of chicken name on survival growth of pomelo seedling"​


Sure! I can provide you with some guidance on how to make a baby thesis with the topic "The effect of different levels of chicken manure on survival and growth of pomelo seedlings." Here's a basic outline that you can follow:

1. Introduction

- Provide a brief overview of pomelo and its significance as a fruit tree.

- Introduce the concept of using chicken manure as a potential fertilizer for pomelo seedlings.

- State the purpose of your study and your research question, such as "What is the effect of different levels of chicken manure on the survival and growth of pomelo seedlings?"

2. Literature Review

- Review relevant literature on the topic, including previous research and studies related to the effects of different types of fertilizer on plant growth and survival.

- Discuss the potential benefits and limitations of using chicken manure as a fertilizer for pomelo seedlings based on existing literature.

3. Methodology

- Describe the materials and methods you used in your study, including the experimental design, sample size, and data collection procedures.

- Explain how you applied different levels of chicken manure to the pomelo seedlings, and any controls or treatments that you used.

- Provide details on how you measured survival and growth of the pomelo seedlings, including any data collection tools or techniques used.

4. Results

- Present your findings in a clear and organized manner, using tables, graphs, or other visual aids to display your data.

- Interpret your results and discuss any trends, patterns, or statistical significance that you observed.

- Address any limitations of your study and potential sources of error.

5. Discussion

- Discuss the implications of your findings and how they relate to your research question.

- Compare your results with previous research and studies in the field.

- Provide explanations for any unexpected results and suggest possible areas for further research.

- Conclude with a summary of your main findings and their significance.

6. Conclusion

- Provide a concise summary of your study's main findings and their implications.

- Restate your research question and state whether your results support or refute your hypothesis.

- Offer recommendations for future research or practical applications of your findings.

7. References

- List all the sources you cited in your thesis using an appropriate citation format, such as APA or MLA.

Remember to follow the guidelines and requirements set by your institution or supervisor for formatting, structure, and other specific details for your baby thesis. Good luck with your research!
