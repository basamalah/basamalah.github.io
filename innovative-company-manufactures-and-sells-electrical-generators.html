30. Global company manufactures 100 cellphone units each month. The cellphone sells for 28,767.85 pesos each. If the company sells 50 units, how much does it get?


The company will get 1,438,392.50 pesos.

The steps for solving the problem:

If the company sells 50 units, it will receive a total=

28,767.85 pesos x 50 units = 1,438,392.50 pesos.

Hence, the company will get 1,438,392.50 pesos.

In math, multiplication is the process of combining numbers in order to find their product. The product is the result of the multiplication and is obtained by multiplying the two numbers together.
