Many cities elected recent immigrants as mayors. The population grew as the cities expanded. Agriculture became an important part of the economy in most cities.


Answer:

The statement "The population grew as the cities expanded" is true about Reconstruction era cities such as San Antonio and Galveston. Following the Civil War and the end of slavery, many newly-freed African Americans moved to cities, particularly in the South, in search of economic opportunities and a better life. This led to an increase in the population of these cities as they expanded.

28. ang iriga city ba ay pantangi o pambalana​


Answer:

Explanation:

Ang Iriga City ay Pangngalang Tiyak o Pantangi dahil ito ay may tiyak na pangalan. Ang pangngalan ng isang lugar tulad ng Iriga City, Maynila, Albay Manila Zoo, Rizal Park at marami pang iba ay itinuturing na Pantangi.

Ang pangngalang pantangi ay pangngalang nagsisimula sa malaking titik na tumutukoy sa tiyak na ngalan ng tao, hayop, bagay, lugar, kathang-isip o pangyayari na ibinubukod sa kauri nito.
