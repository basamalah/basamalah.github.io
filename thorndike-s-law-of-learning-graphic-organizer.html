Law of readiness: Explain their relevance to the law of readiness 2.Law of exercise:Explain their relevance to the law of exercise. 3 Law of effect:Explain their relevance in the law of effect .4 Law of primacy:Explain their relevance to the law of primacy 5.Law of Recency:Explain their relevance to the law of recency 6.Law of intensity:Explain their relevance to the of intensity.​


Answer:

Thorndike's Law of Learning is a psychological principle that states that behavior, reinforced by positive or negative reinforcement, increases or decreases in strength. These laws were postulated by American psychologist Edward Lee Thorndike in the early 20th Century, and helped to facilitate a shift from mentalism to behaviorism in psychology. The laws of learning contain five fundamental principles.

1.The Law of readiness states that positive reinforcement is most effective when the learner is prepared to learn the desired behavior or task.

2.The Law of exercise states that the more an individual practices a behavior or task, the easier it will be for them to learn and remember the behavior or task.

3.The Law of effect states that behaviors that are followed by positive reinforcement will be more likely to be repeated and those followed by negative reinforcement will be less likely to be repeated.

4.The Law of primacy states that the order of learning plays a role in how the material is eventually remembered.

5.The Law of recency states that the most recent information learned is the most likely to be remembered.

6.The Law of intensity states that the strength of reinforcement affects the strength of learned behavior, meaning more intense reinforcement leads to stronger and more frequent learned responses.

Answer:

Thorndike's Law of Learning, also known as the Law of Effect, states that behaviors that are followed by positive consequences are more likely to be repeated, while behaviors followed by negative consequences are less likely to be repeated. This law is important in shaping human behavior and learning.

The Law of Readiness states that individuals must be ready and willing to learn for effective learning to take place. It is important to create an environment where learners are motivated and prepared to engage in the learning process.

The Law of Exercise states that repetition and practice are necessary for learning to occur. Consistent reinforcement of behaviors and concepts helps to strengthen neural pathways and create lasting learning.

The Law of Primacy states that the first experience or exposure to a concept is likely to have a lasting impact on future learning. Therefore, it is important to provide accurate and effective instruction from the beginning.

The Law of Recency states that more recent experiences are more likely to be remembered than earlier experiences. It is important to provide opportunities for learners to review and practice recently learned concepts to promote retention and transfer.

The Law of Intensity states that the more emotionally intense an experience or stimulus is, the more likely it is to be remembered. Teachers can use this law to create engaging and stimulating learning experiences that promote retention and motivation.

Ang kanyang trabaho sa comparative psychology at ang proseso ng pag-aaral ay humantong sa teorya ng koneksyonismo at tumulong na ilatag ang siyentipikong pundasyon para sa sikolohiyang pang-edukasyon

Answer:

Edward Lee Thorndike (Agosto 31, 1874 - Agosto 9, 1949) ay isang Amerikano psychologist na ginugol ang halos buong karera niya sa Mga Kolehiyo ng Guro, Unibersidad ng Columbia. Ang kanyang trabaho sa paghahambing sikolohiya at ang pag-aaral ang proseso ay humantong sa teorya ng koneksyonismo at tumulong ilatag ang pundasyong pang-agham para sa sikolohiyang pang-edukasyon. Nagtrabaho rin siya sa paglutas ng mga problema sa industriya, tulad ng mga pagsusulit sa empleyado at pagsubok. Siya ay kasapi ng lupon ng Psychological Corporation at nagsilbi bilang pangulo ng American Psychological Association noong 1912.[1][2] A Pagsusuri sa Pangkalahatang Sikolohiya Ang survey, na inilathala noong 2002, ay niraranggo ang Thorndike bilang ikasiyam na pinakasilang na psychologist ng ika-20 siglo[3] Si Edward Thorndike ay may isang malakas na epekto sa teorya ng pampalakas at pagtatasa ng pag-uugali, na nagbibigay ng pangunahing balangkas para sa mga batas na empirical sa pag-uugali ng sikolohiya sa kanyang batas ng epekto. Sa pamamagitan ng kanyang mga kontribusyon sa larangan ng sikolohiya sa pag-uugali ay dumating ang kanyang pangunahing mga epekto sa edukasyon, kung saan ang batas ng epekto ay may malaking impluwensya sa silid-aralan.
