12. Hanapin ang CPI, PURCHASING POWER OF PESO AT INFLATION RATE MARKET GOODS TAON:2020 3,300 TAON 2021 4,100with solving ​


Answer:

0.805 or 80.5% is the Purchasing Power of Peso

Explanation:

To find the CPI (Consumer Price Index) and the Purchasing Power of Peso, we need to use the following formula:

CPI = (Cost of basket in current year / Cost of basket in base year) x 100

Purchasing Power of Peso = (1 / CPI) x 100

First, let's find the CPI using the given information:

Cost of basket in 2020 = 3,300

Cost of basket in 2021 = 4,100

CPI = (4,100 / 3,300) x 100

CPI = 124.24

Therefore, the CPI for 2021 is 124.24.

Now, let's find the Purchasing Power of Peso:

Purchasing Power of Peso = (1 / CPI) x 100

Purchasing Power of Peso = (1 / 124.24) x 100

Purchasing Power of Peso = 0.805

Therefore, the Purchasing Power of Peso is 0.805 or 80.5%. This means that the Peso's purchasing power decreased by 19.5% (100% - 80.5%) between 2020 and 2021 due to inflation.
