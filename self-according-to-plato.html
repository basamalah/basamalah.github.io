7. plato understanding of self​


Answer:

Plato’s Understanding of Self

Plato believes that humans are made up of three materials – the body, the mind, and the soul. He also believe that the soul is distinct from the body, and it is indestructible and eternal.

Explanation:

Plato’s belief about the distinction of body, mind, and soul gave rise to the belief that when a person dies, the soul remains unaffected and leaves the body. The body decomposes, but the soul will exist eternally. This is similar to the Christian belief that humans have a body, spirit, and soul. In addition, Plato also believes that the soul is what makes us unique, and it gives us our identity.
