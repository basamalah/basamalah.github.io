20. Hannah is 9 times as old as her daughter in 3years she will only be 5 times as old as her daughter how old is each now​


Answer:

Hannah is 27 yrs old, while her daughter is 3 yrs old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Identify

present age of Hannah an her daughter

let,

(age now)

daughter = xhannah = 9x

(in 3yrs)

daughter = x + 3hannah = 9x + 3

Setup Equation:

"in 3years she will only be 5 times as old as her daughter"

9x + 3 = 5(x + 3)

Solution:

Solve for x

9x + 3 = 5(x + 3)

9x + 3 = 5x + 15

9x - 5x = 15 - 3

4x = 12

4x/4 = 12/4

x = 3 <--- daughter's age

Substitute 3 of x in Hannah present age equation.

hannah = 9x

= 9(3)

hannah = 27

Check:

9x + 3 = 5(x + 3)

9(3) + 3 = 5(3 + 3)

27 + 3 = 5(6)

30 = 30 ✔

Answer:

Hannah is 27 yrs old, while her daughter is 3 yrs old.

27. D= light skin, d=dark skin. Hannah's mother is homozygous dark skin and Hannah's father is heterozygous light skin. What is the probability that Hannah has dark skin? ​


Answer:

50%

Explanation:

Punnett square

-----D-----d

d--Dd---dd

d--Dd----dd

There are two genotypes of Dd and two of dd. Dd is light skin, dd is dark skin. Therefore, the chance of having dark skin is 50%.

Mothers genotype: dd

Fathers genotype: Dd
