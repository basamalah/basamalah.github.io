22. What is the History of Silay south elementary school?​


Answer:

It was from the persistent efforts of Don Emilio Gaston, the town President and his Vice President Atty. Roque Hofeliña who donated the 4 hectare lot school site that made possible the organization of Silay South Elementary School.

Answer:

History. It was from the persistent efforts of Don Emilio Gaston, the town President and his Vice President Atty. Roque Hofeliña who donated the 4 hectare lot school site that made possible the organization of Silay South Elementary School.

They followed standard plans designed by Architect William Parsons and were built between 1907 and 1946. The earlier ones were built of wood, while those that came later were partly made of cement. The North Elementary School was completed in 1907 while the South Elementary School was finished in 1919.
