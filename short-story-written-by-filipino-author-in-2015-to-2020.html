18. 2015 to 2020 does indicate a function why or why not​?


Answer:

The natural world is full of relationships between quantities that change. When we see these relationships, it is natural for us to ask “If I know one quantity, can I then determine the other?” This establishes the idea of an input quantity, or independent variable, and a corresponding output quantity, or dependent variable. From this we get the notion of a functional relationship in which the output can be determined from the input. For some quantities, like height and age, there are certainly relationships between these quantities. Given a specific person and any age, it is easy enough to determine their height, but if we tried to reverse that relationship and determine age from a given height, that would be problematic, since most people maintain the same height for many years.

25. give a short reflection to the pixels 2015 movie ​


Answer:

Pixels is a sci-fi comedy movie that tells the story of a group of arcade game champions who are recruited by the US government to stop an alien invasion that takes the form of classic video game characters such as Pac-Man and Donkey Kong. The movie features a star-studded cast including Adam Sandler, Kevin James, Josh Gad, and Peter Dinklage.

From a technical perspective, the movie has impressive visual effects that bring the pixelated video game characters to life in a creative and nostalgic way. The movie also has some hilarious moments, and the chemistry between the main cast members is enjoyable to watch.

However, some critics have pointed out that the movie suffers from a weak and predictable plot, and that the humor can be hit-or-miss. They also criticize the inconsistent pacing and lack of character development.

Overall, while Pixels may not be a cinematic masterpiece, it is a fun and entertaining movie that will appeal to fans of 80s video games and light-hearted comedies.

28. GIVE A SHORT REFLECTION TO THE ARK MOVIE 2015​


Explanation:

God-fearing Noah and his wife Emmy work a farm in a barren landscape with their married sons Shem ,Ham and Japheth and their wives and naïve teen-aged son Kenan. On a visit to the pleasure loving city Kenan falls for the hedonistic life-style of Sabba, daughter of Noah's atheist friend, wealthy trader Paul, and he visits her on a nightly basis. When an angel tells Noah to build an ark because his world will soon be engulfed by a huge flood Noah takes the news seriously but his family do not believe him and when he tries to warn the citizens he is reviled and pelted with rocks. Eventually Emmy shames the boys into helping Noah and the ark is completed though there is no sign of rain. Godly fugitives from the city arrive and, when the flood comes, are welcomed on board with a host of animals and birds but Kenan elects to stay with Sabba and they are drowned along with the other unbelievers. After the flood has abated the angel reappears to show Noah a rainbow, a symbol of hope.
