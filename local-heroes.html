1. what is difference of national hero and local hero​


Answer:

A national hero is someone who is recognized and celebrated for their contribution to their country's history or culture on a national level. They are often seen as symbols of national pride and identity, and their legacy is remembered and honored by the entire nation.

On the other hand, a local hero is someone who is admired and respected within their own community for their achievements or contributions. They may not be as well-known or celebrated on a national level, but they hold a special place in the hearts of the people who know them personally or have been impacted by their actions.

In summary, the main difference between a national hero and a local hero is the level of recognition and impact they have on a wider scale.

2. Why is it that it is much easier to study the life of national heroes than that of local hero? Give an example of local heroes and national heroes. Develop this topic in not less than 75 words.​


WHY IS IT THAT IT IS MUCH EASIER TO STUDY THE LIFE OF NATIONAL HEROES THAN THAT OF LOCAL HEROES?

Because national heroes are well recognized on a national level, which implies that they are known by the whole country, their lives are much easier to study than those of local heroes. Local heroes, on the other hand, are only known at the local level, that is, in specific locations such as municipal and provincial levels. Because the achievements of national heroes are well-known, it is common for local heroes' efforts to go unnoticed or unnoticed by those who live near them.

Jose Rizal, Andres Bonifacio, Emilio Aguinaldo, Apolinario Mabini, and Marcelo H. Del Pilar are among the national heroes. Teachers, doctors, other front-line medical health workers, police officers, and other public officials, on the other hand, might be characterized as local heroes. They may not be well-known on their own, but their cumulative actions can be attributed to them as a "hero."

A hero is a person who has had a positive impact on the Philippines and is admired and recognized for their bravery, amazing achievements, and noble traits. A person who symbolizes all of us and has made substantial positive contributions to society's growth and development is known as a national hero. Knowing our heroes is to know ourselves: our beliefs, our collective history, who we are as Filipinos, and what makes us so wonderful as a nation and as individuals. Recognizing our heroes is a reflection of who we are as a people, as well as our culture, tradition, and history.

7. How will you consider Lapu-lapu ---- a local or national hero? Why? Explain.​


Answer:

because he saved his country

Lapulapu[2][3][4] or Lapu-Lapu (fl. 1521), whose name was first recorded as Cilapulapu,[5] was a datu of Mactan in the Visayas. Modern Philippine society regards him as the first Filipino hero because of his resistance to imperial Spanish colonization. He is best known for the Battle of Mactan that happened at dawn on April 27, 1521, where he and his warriors defeated the forces of Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan and his native allies Rajah Humabon and Datu Zula.[6][7] Magellan's death ended his voyage of circumnavigation and delayed the Spanish occupation of the islands by over forty years until the expedition of Miguel López de Legazpi in 1564. Legazpi continued the expeditions of Magellan, leading to the colonization of the Philippines for 333 years. Monuments of Lapulapu have been built all over the Philippines to honor Lapulapu's bravery against the Spaniards. The Philippine National Police and the Bureau of Fire Protection use his image as part of their official seals.

Besides being a rival of Rajah Humabon of neighboring Cebu, very little is reliably known about the life of Lapulapu. The only existing primary source mentioning him by name is the account of Antonio Pigafetta, and according to historian Resil B. Mojares, no European who left a primary record of Magellan's voyage/vessel "knew what he looked like, heard him speak (his recorded words of defiance and pride are all indirect), or mentioned that he was present in the battle of Mactan that made him famous."[8] His name, origins, religion, and fate are still a matter of controversy.

Ang bayaning lokal na si Jose Rizal.

Si Jose Rizal ay isang taong may pambihirang lakas ng intelektwal, na may pambihirang talento. Mahusay siya sa malawak na larangan ng pag-iisip tulad ng medisina, tula, sketching, arkitektura, sosyolohiya at iba pa.Kaya, ang pagkamatay ni Rizal ng mga kolonyal na pinunong Espanyol noong siya ay napakabata ay isang malaking kawalan para sa Pilipinas at sa mundo sa Pangkalahatan Ngayon, mga tao. Pinarangalan siya ng mga Pilipino bilang kanilang pambansang bayani.

Sa simula pa lang, nagpakita na si Jose Rizal Mercado ng maagang katalinuhan. Natuto siyang magbasa ng mga liham mula sa kanyang ina sa edad na 3, at marunong bumasa at sumulat sa edad na 5. Jose Rizal Mercado college sa Ateneo Municipal de Manila, pagkatapos ay nagtapos sa edad na 16 na may pinakamataas na marka. Kumuha siya ng postgraduate program doon upang magsurvey ng kadalubhasaan sa lupa.

Isinagawa ni Jose Rizal ang pakikibaka sa kakaibang paraan, ito ay sa pamamagitan ng mga nobela bilang isang anyo ng pagpuna sa kolonyal na Espanyol, tulad ng mga nobelang "Noli Me Tangere" at "El Filibusterismo".

Sa parehong mga nobela at editoryal, ipinakilala ni Jose Rizal ang ilang mga reporma ng kolonyal na sistema ng Espanyol sa Pilipinas. Itinaguyod niya ang kalayaan sa pananalita at pagpupulong, pantay na karapatan sa harap ng mga korte para sa mga Pilipino, at ang paghirang ng mga paring Pilipino. Dagdag pa rito, hiniling ni Rizal sa Pilipinas na maging isang lalawigan sa Espanya, na may kinatawan sa lehislatura ng Espanya (Cortes generales).

Minsan ay ipinaglaban ni Rizal ang kalayaan para sa Pilipinas. Gayunpaman, itinuring siya ng kolonyal na pamahalaan na isang mapanganib na radikal, at idineklara siyang isang kaaway ng estado. Hanggang sa hinatulan ng kamatayan si Jose Rizal, noong siya ay 35 taong gulang. Si Jose Rizal ay kasalukuyang nasa buong Pilipinas para sa kanyang katalinuhan, katapangan, paglaban sa paniniil, at pakikiramay.
