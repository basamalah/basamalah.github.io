1. Dictionary-Thesaurus-Atlas-Encyclopedia-Almanac-​


Answer:

DICTIONARY

- reference book that lists words in order usually, for Western languages, alphabetical and gives their meanings.Basically, a dictionary lists a set of words with information about them. The list may attempt to be a complete inventory of a language or may be only a small segment of it.

THESAURUS

-(plural thesauri or thesauruses) or synonym dictionary is a reference work for finding synonyms and sometimes antonyms of words. They are often used by writers to help find the best word to express an idea: to find the word, or words, by whichan idea may be most fitly and aptly expressed.

ATLAS

- is a collection of maps; it is typically a bundle of maps of Earth or of a region of Earth.

ENCYCLOPEDIA

-reference work that contains information on all branches of knowledge or that treats a particular branch of knowledge in a comprehensive manner.

ALMANAC

-book or table containing a calendar of the days, weeks, and months of the year; a record of various astronomical phenomena, often with climate information and seasonal suggestions for farmers; and miscellaneous other data. Almanacs began to gain real prominence only after the development of printing.

4. To look for examples. Read their definitions and look for a sample.• dictionary • atlas • newspaper • manual• thesaurus • almanac • encyclopedia


Answer:

- Dictionary: A reference book that contains words listed in alphabetical order, along with their meanings, pronunciations, and often usage examples. Sample sentence: "I used the dictionary to look up the definition of a new word I came across in my book."

- Atlas: A collection of maps, typically showing geographic features, political boundaries, and other geographical information. Sample sentence: "I used the atlas to locate the capital cities of different countries around the world."

- Newspaper: A printed or online publication that provides news, current events, and other information on a wide range of topics. Sample sentence: "I read the newspaper every morning to stay updated on the latest news and events."

- Manual: A book or document that provides instructions or guidance on how to do something or operate a device. Sample sentence: "I referred to the manual to assemble the new furniture I bought."

- Thesaurus: A reference book that provides synonyms and antonyms for words, often used to find alternative words or expand vocabulary. Sample sentence: "I used the thesaurus to find a more precise word to use in my writing."

- Almanac: A yearly publication that contains information on various topics, such as weather forecasts, astronomical data, and statistical data. Sample sentence: "I consulted the almanac to find out the sunrise and sunset times for my area."

- Encyclopedia: A comprehensive reference work that provides information on a wide range of subjects, typically arranged alphabetically or thematically. Sample sentence: "I used the encyclopedia to research a historical event for my school project."

Answer:

Atlas - An atlas is a collection of maps; it is typically a bundle of maps of Earth or a region of Earth.

Thesaurus-A synonym dictionary,is a reference work for finding synonyms and sometimes antonyms of words.

Dictionary - a reference source in print or electronic form containing words usually alphabetically arranged along with information about their forms, pronunciations, functions, etymologies, meanings, and syntactic and idiomatic uses.

Newspaper - publication usually issued daily, weekly, or at other regular times that provides news, views, features, and other information of public interest and that often carries advertising.

Almanac- an annual publication listing a set of events forthcoming in the next year. It includes information like weather forecasts, farmers' planting dates, tide tables, and other tabular data often arranged according to the calendar.

Encyclopedia - set of books giving information on many subjects or on many aspects of one subject and typically arranged alphabetically.

What is an Almanac?

In the traditional sense, an almanac is a publication containing astronomical and meteorological data for a given year that often includes other miscellaneous information.

What is an Atlas?

An atlas is a bound collection of maps, charts, plates, or tables illustrating a subject. In addition to being about geography they can illustrate any number of subjects, from anatomy or botany to history or zoology.

What is Dictionary?

A Dictionary is more than just books filled with definitions. There are some filled with definitions, some filled with origins of words, and some filled with encyclopedia-type articles.

What is an Encyclopedia?

A book or set of books giving information on many subjects or on many aspects of one subject and typically arranged alphabetically.
