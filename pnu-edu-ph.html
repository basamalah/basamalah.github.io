14. Pnu gumawa ng limang sentence


Answer:

Simple Sentence

- Kailangan mo ng simuno at panaguri

Example:

Sina Ana at Teresa ay naglaba.

Ang simuno ay sina Ana at Teresa at ang panaguri ay naglaba

Compound sentence

- Kailangan ng Dalawang simple sentence na ipinaguugnay ng conjunction

Example:

Si Ana ay naglaba samantalang si Ruben ay nagpaplantsa.

Ang isang simple sentence ay Si ana ay naglaba

At ang isa naman ay Si Ruben ay nagplantsa.

Ang con

Complex sentence

- Kailangan ng 1 simple sentence at 1 dependent clause na ipinagdurugtong ng subbordinating conjunctions

Example:

Natutulog si Gomez ng 12 oras noong siya ay bata pa.

Ang simple sentence ay Natutulog si Gomez ng 12 oras.

Ang conjunction:

noong

Ang dependent clause ay noong siya ay bata pa

24. .com, .edu, and .net are examples of _____

INTERNET DOMAINS

There are many kinds of Internet sites that you might find during the course of search. These sites are created by different people or different organizations for different objectives. Sites are can be searched or can be remembered through its own address called uniform resource locator. The three-letter code preceded by a dot (.), simply known as the domain.

Types of Domain Names

.edu.com.gov.netDomain Names and their Equivalent

2.com- This site is mostly belong to commercial entities, some which are profit oriented.

3.org- This site belongs to Non-profit organizations.

4.gov- This site belongs to government organizations.

5.net- This site belongs to Internet service providers.

27. A kilo of grapes costs Php 185.50 how much 3.5 kilos costs?pnu po computation nya? ​


Answer:

To determine the cost of 3.5 kilos of grapes, you can use proportion:

1 kilo costs Php 185.50, therefore:

3.5 kilos cost x

Cross-multiply:

1 x 3.5 = 3.5

3.5 x 185.50 = 649.25

Therefore, 3.5 kilos of grapes would cost Php 649.25.
