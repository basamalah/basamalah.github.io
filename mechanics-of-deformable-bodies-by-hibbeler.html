3. The mechanical deformation of rocks are called crystallization processes?


Yes, when rocks deform they are said to strain. It can be observed that a strain is a change in size, shape, or volume of a material which includes any kind of movement of the material, including tilting. We have to note that stress is a force acting on a material that produces a strain, therefore stress is a force applied over an area. However, if stress is not equal from all directions then we say that the stress is a differential stress such as Tensional, Compressional, and Shear which causes the rock to slip.

These are the following stages that a rock passes through when it is subjected to increasing stress of deformation.

Elastic Deformation (wherein the strain is reversible) Ductile Deformation (wherein the strain is irreversible) Fracture (irreversible strain wherein the material breaks)

22. In your own words, describe the mechanisms by which semicrystalline polymers (a) elastically deform and (b) plastically deform, and (c) by which elastomers elastically deform.


Answer: Semicrystalline polymers have a mix of crystalline and amorphous regions in their structure, and they can experience both elastic and plastic deformation. Elastic deformation is reversible and occurs when the polymer stretches in response to a stress, but returns to its original shape when the stress is removed. This is caused by the movement of amorphous regions within the polymer structure. On the other hand, plastic deformation is irreversible and occurs when the polymer permanently deforms in response to a stress. This is caused by the breaking of chemical bonds between the polymer chains, which allows the chains to slide past each other and rearrange themselves. Elastomers, which are a type of polymer with a high level of elasticity, also experience elastic deformation, but this is caused by the movement of long polymer chains within the material. When an elastomer is subjected to a stress, the chains stretch and rearrange themselves, and when the stress is removed, the chains return to their original configuration, causing the material to return to its original shape.
