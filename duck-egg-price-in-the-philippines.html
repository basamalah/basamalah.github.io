4. The ratio of duck eggs to chicken eggs in an egg store is 3:5.If there are 108 duck eggs in the store,how many chicken eggs are there?a.The equation is ___________b.Solve__________​


PROPORTION

》happens when two ratios is equal

PROBLEM

The ratio of duck eggs to chicken eggs in an egg store is 3:5. If there are 108 duck eggs in the store,how many chicken eggs are there?

ANSWER

Let x be the missing number of chicken eggs.

A. The equation is 3:5 = 108:x or [tex] \bold {\frac{3}{5} = \frac{108}{x} } [/tex]

B. Solve

[tex] \frac{3}{5} = \frac{108}{x} \\ \\ \bigg( \frac{3}{5} = \frac{108}{x} \bigg) (5x) \\ \\ 3(x) = 108(5) \\ \\ 3x = 540 \\ \\ \boxed{ x =180 }[/tex]

Therefore, there should be a total of 180 chicken eggs.

ANG SAGOT!

[tex]\sf\large\bold{180 \: chicken \: eggs \: are \: there}[/tex]

SOLUSYON!

Kapag kayo ay nag mumultiply o nag didivide ng ratio. Kailangan muna nating alamin kung ano ang nawawalang terms nito. Tatandaan na ang ratio ay merong means at extremes.

----------------------------------------------------------

A. Ano ang equation nito?

Sa ratio naman ay ( 3 : 5 = 108 : N )Sa fraction naman ay ( ⅗ = 108/N )

B. Paano i-solve ang problem?

Ang una nating gagawin ay icoconvert natin ang ratio sa fraction.

[tex]\sf\large\bold{ \frac{3}{5} = \frac{108}{n} }[/tex]

Ang susunod naman ay imumultiy natin ang 180 sa 5.

[tex]\sf\large\bold{5 \times 108 =540 }[/tex]

Then, ang kabuuan ng 5 × 108 ay ididivide natin sa 3 upang makuha ang tamang kasagutan.

[tex]\sf\large\bold{540 \div 3 = 180}[/tex]

C. Ano ang kasagutan?
