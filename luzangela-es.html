29. If ES=5x-7.5 and CS=3x+1.5,Find ES​


Answer:

15

Step-by-step explanation:

1st Find x :

5x - 7.5 = 3x + 1.5

combine the like terms // transpose

5x - 3x = 7.5 + 1.5

2x = 9

x = 9/2

2nd substitute the ans in x to ES:

5(9/2) - 7.5 = 15

ES = 15
