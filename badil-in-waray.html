5. what is the waray waray traditional clothing?​


Answer:

Barong tagalog for men and the balintawak dress or the patadyong and kimona combination for women.

Explanation:

Waray-Waray traditional clothing includes the barong tagalog for men and the balintawak dress or the patadyong and kimona combination for women. The barong tagalog is a formal shirt for men made of piña, abacá, or jusi fabric and features intricate embroidery. The balintawak dress is a two-piece outfit for women, consisting of a loose blouse and a skirt, while the patadyong and kimona combination features a wraparound skirt and a blouse with butterfly sleeves. These traditional clothing are typically worn during formal events, festivals, and cultural celebrations in the Waray-Waray region of the Philippines.

Ang pangkat ng mga wikang Waray ay binubuo ng Waray, Waray Sorsogon at Masbate Sorsogon.
