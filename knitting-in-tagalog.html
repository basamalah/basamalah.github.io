9. carol can knit a sweater in 19.6 minutes . how many sweater can she knit at this pace if she knits non stop for an hour.


If Carol can knit a sweater in 19.6 minutes, here is the answer if she can knit at this pace non-stop for an hour.

Answer:

Carol can knit 3 sweaters if she knits non-stop for an hour.

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Consider that Carol can knit one sweater in 19.6 minutes.

2. We were asked how many sweaters can she knit non-stop for an hour. We know that an hour has 60 minutes.

3. Therefore, we divide 60 minutes by 19.6 minutes to determine how many sweater can she knit.

4. 60 minutes ÷ 19.6 minutes = 3 sweaters.

Therefore, Carol can knit 3 sweaters if she knits non-stop for an hour.

That is the answer if she can knit at this pace non-stop for an hour.

26. why is it important to use a knit gauge when making a knitted article​


Answer:

It is the number of stitches needed to create a piece of fabric of a given size. The purpose of knitting gauge is to act as a sort of guideline for the project. If you want your project to perfectly replicate the sample, you will have to match the designer's recommended gauge.
