10. a toyota car travelling at a rate of 70 km per hour leaves the house 2 hours after a kia car has left and overtakes it in five hours at what way does the kia car was traveling​


Answer:

The travel rate of Kia is 50 km per hour

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Toyota car travelling at a rate of 70 km per hour.

Toyota leaves the house 3 hours after Kia car.

Toyota overtakes the Kia after 5 hours.

Problem:

What way does the Kia car was travelling?

Solution:

Lets compute first the total travel of Toyota car until ot overstakes the Kia car and make it a bases to get the travel of Kia car. https://brainly.ph/question/1893247

Toyota travel 5 hours when it overtakes the Kia, since Toyota travel 70 km per hour, let's multiply the hours and the km of Toyota's travel.

70 x 5 = 350km   in 350 km Toyota overtakes the Kia.

Kia travel 2 hours before Toyota leave and overtakes in 5 hours, so let's compute the total hours of Kia until Toyota overtakes.

2 + 5 = 7 hours     Total hours Kia travel and its equivalent km is 350km, because in 350km Toyota overtakes Kia.

Let's divide 350km to the travel time of Kia which is 7 hours to get the travelling km per hour of Kia.

350 ÷ 7 = 50 km   The travel rate of Kia car per hour.

Toyota travel 70 km per hour while Kia travel 50 km per hour.
