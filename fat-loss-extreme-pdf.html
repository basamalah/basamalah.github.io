6. how to loss the fat on this part of the face next to the nose and under the eyes​


Answer:

The fat around the nose and under the eyes is known as subcutaneous fat and can be difficult to reduce through diet and exercise alone. However, here are some tips that may help reduce the appearance of fat in this area:

1. Facial exercises: Regularly performing facial exercises such as smiling, puckering your lips, and raising your eyebrows can help tone the muscles around the face and reduce the appearance of fat.

2. Good nutrition: Eating a healthy, balanced diet that is rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean protein can help reduce overall body fat, including the subcutaneous fat around the face.

3. Hydration: Drinking plenty of water can help flush out toxins from the body and reduce puffiness and bloating around the face.

4. Facial massage: Massaging the face can help increase blood flow and stimulate lymphatic drainage, which can reduce the appearance of puffiness and fat around the face.

5. Getting enough sleep: Adequate sleep is crucial for overall health and can help reduce stress levels, which can contribute to the accumulation of fat around the face.

It is important to note that losing fat in specific areas of the body can be difficult and may take time. It is also important to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new diet or exercise routine.

Bipolar disorder, formerly called manic depression, is a mental health condition that causes extreme mood swings that include emotional highs (mania or hypomania) and lows (depression).

When you become depressed, you may feel sad or hopeless and lose interest or pleasure in most activities. When your mood shifts to mania or hypomania (less extreme than mania), you may feel euphoric, full of energy or unusually irritable.
