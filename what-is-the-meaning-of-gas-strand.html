30. what is the similarities between humms and gas strand​


Similarities between HUMSS and GAS

1. Both strands are part of the Senior High School curriculum.

2. Both strands are under the Academic track.

3. Both strands focus on developing the cognitive and logical skills of learners.

4. Both strands offer a wide range of electives and specializations.

5. Both strands involve the same core subjects such as English, Mathematics, and Science.

6. Both strands provide the opportunity to earn a diploma after completing the program.

7. Both strands have a variety of learning and assessment activities that are designed to assess the learners' mastery of the different skills and knowledge that are being taught.
