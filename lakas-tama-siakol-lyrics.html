But there are also people who defended it for the freedom and they are the heroes that we know today, they fought for the freedom even if their life will be the price. 2. kailan nag simola ang bandang siakol​


Answer:   Siakol is a 4-piece Filipino rock band who is one of the several groups who spearheaded the 90's Philippine alternative rock explosion and known for their hit songs "Lakas Tama", "Peksman", "Kanto", "Biyaheng Impyerno" and "Bakit Ba". The band was a prominent fixture of the Tunog Kalye era and very popular to college & high school students in the Philippines during the 90s. The band name and ownership is disputed between the band and the former vocalist Noel, who left in 2020
