1. one of the objectives of first aid?​


Answer:

to give a person a first medical treatment before going to hospital

Answer:

The 5 main aims of first aid are:

➪preserve life.

➪prevent the escalation of the illness or injury.

➪promote recovery.

➪pain relief.

➪protect the unconscious.

Explanation:

First aid is emergency care given immediately to an injured person. The purpose of first aid is to minimize injury and future disability. In serious cases, first aid may be necessary to keep the victim alive.
