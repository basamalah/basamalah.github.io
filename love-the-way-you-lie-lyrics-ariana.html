30. love is not a lie true or false​


Answer:

true

Love isn't a lie, but what we think of it is. Love is just the state of mind, and the emotions that follow, when you feel comfortable with letting down all of the barriers and filters that life made you put up.Think about how all the different things in life teach you to say "i have to be like this" or "i cant do that", "i have to think like this" or "i am that", they you force you to change,adapt,hide, or accentuate things within yourself to fit these ideals in order to keep your ego.so when you are with a person and you feel you can give 100% of your self and be in your natural state of mind without holding back, its like an immense relief to your consciousness, like being accepted and saying i don't have to try anymore, I'm good enough. Feeling like that is easy to get attached to which is why people do some of the most crazy things to hold on to that feeling. This is where the emotions come in, its like you feel some form of happy when you have it, some form of upset when you don't, so you do things that may test the limit of your mental capabilities just to hold on to it. Losing love is like losing a piece of your self because its like losing a place where you are at peace with your self. It's 100% acceptance. The more barriers you have up, the harder it is to love, which is why trust is so important in a relationship.This is also the reason why love is harder to find in this era because society itself is one big barrier.This doesn't make love a lie, just easily mistaken with other things
