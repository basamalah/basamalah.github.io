26. what do you think are their reasons or purposesfor giving their speeches?Teacher,lawyer,and comedian.​


Teachers make speeches to inspire students about their studies and also to impart knowledge to students.Lawyers give speeches to fight for everyone's rights from harassment, intimidation, criminality. Lawyers try to win the cases they handle and to get justice.Comedians give speeches to make us happy and all our bad moods fade away from being entertained by comedians.

Explanation:

Speech is also referred to as the activity of speaking in front of many people. The speech is done using good language and can be accepted by the listener. Generally, people who make speeches will convey their ideas to other people or listeners. Speech is an expression of thoughts into words. Speech addressed to the crowd. Speech is done to convey an opinion or description. Speech is done by someone orally. The information in the speech explains something or a problem. The speech will be done using clear sentences at a certain time in front of many people.

The main structure of a speech text is the opening which consists of four parts:

Greetings.Respect, usually mentioning people starting from those who have the highest positions in the forum.Thanksgiving, usually containing thanksgiving for the orator (the person giving the speech) for being given the opportunity to deliver a speech and gather with guests.Introduction to the main topic.

The most important part in a speech text is the content section because in this section someone who is giving a speech will convey his goals and messages to the listeners. The content of the speech to be delivered must contain important information. The content of the speech should also be accompanied by convincing reasons. In addition, the contents of the speech text can also be added with factual sentences and accompanied by reliable sources whose validation can be known. The goal is for the reader to believe and follow the message conveyed in the speech.
