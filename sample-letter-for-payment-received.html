28. For every insurance policy Mr. Lorenzo sold, he receives 20% of the first payment of his customer, and 8.5% of the second and third payments. If the annual payment on a transacted policy is 124,000, how much income will he receive from this transaction?


Hello!


We are given that there is an annual payment of P124,000, and Mr. Lorenzo receives 20% on the first payment, and 8.5% on the second and third.


So, at year 1: he gets P124,000 x 20% = P124,000 x 0.2 = P24,800

At year 2, he gets P124,000 x 8.5% = P124,000 x 0.085 = P10,540

For year 3, he gets the same as year 2, which means that the total he will receive is:


24,800 + (2 x 10,540) = P45,880


Since the payments for each year are the same, you can also just add all the percentages and multiply it to P124,000:


P124,000 x (20% + 8.5% + 8.5%) = P124,000 x 0.37 = P45,880


which is the same answer.


Hope this helps!
