First the idea of corporation — It’s a legal construct that allows pooling of capital for big economic efforts. Limited liability means many small investors can cooperate, and do so with limited liability. The only thing at risk is their investment. This is far more efficient economically than a company with individual liability.

Next — global. That allows another dimension of cooperation. At the start of the industrial revolution, nations would specialize, one nation ny in iron, another in coal, a d third in leather. That lowers cost by product BUT adds shipping and trade overheads for finished goods with many components.

With global corporate form, the modern free market was possible.
