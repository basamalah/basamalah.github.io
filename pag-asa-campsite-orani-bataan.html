Bataan Province

Bataan peninsula is located southwards from the western shores of central Luzon, forming the enclosed and well-sheltered Manila Bay to the east that is nearly cut off from the open China Sea in the west. The narrow outlet separates the peninsula from Corregidor Island and Cavite to the south. The provinces of Zambales and Pampanga form common boundaries to the north. About 80% of Bataan is mountainous or hilly with Mount Mariveles and Mount Natib dominating the interior. Most of the agricultural portion of Bataan is in the north and east. The province experiences two pronounced seasons; dry from November until April and the wet from May to October. During World War II it was the scene of heavy fighting between Allied and Japanese forces from January 6, 1942, to April 9, 1942. Bataan fell to Japan on April 9 and was retaken by an American force on February 17, 1945.

12. the perimeter of a rectangular campsite is 64 meter and its area is 207 meter square. Find the length and breath of the campsite


Answer:

length of the rectangle is 23 meters, and its breadth is 9 meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

A RECTANGLE is a quadrilateral with 2 pairs of parallel equal sides. By definition, the PERIMETER OF A RECTANGLE is the sum of all its side lengths. The word BREADTH is actually a synonym of the word "width". It is the distance between the left and right sides of the rectangle. The LENGTH is basically the longer side of the longest side of the given dimension.

Adding all the sides, we have the perimeter

we assume L as the length, and W as the width

P = L + L + W + W or

P = 2L + 2W

Since the value of the perimeter is given, we equate it to the equation.

64 m = 2L + 2W    as equation 1

The formula in finding the AREA OF A RECTANGLE is described by the equation

A = L x W

Or simply the product of the length and the width

207 m^2 = (L)(W)   as equation 2

Rearranging equation 1, and substituting it to equation 2, we have

64m - 2L = 2W

W = (64m - 2L)/2

W = 32m - L    rearranged equation 1

207 m^2 = (L)(W)

207 m^2 = (L)(32m - L)

207 m^2 = 32L - L^2

By factoring, we have

(L-23)(L-9) = 0

L = 23m ; L = 9m

Using the first value (23m) to find for the W or the width or the breadth

W = 32m - L

W = 32m - 23m

W = 9m

Using the second value (9m)

W = 32m - L

W = 32m - 9m

W = 23m

Since the values of W are just the same with the values of L, we then conclude that the longer value is the length, and the shorter is the width or the breath.

Therefore, the length of the rectangle is 23 meters, and its breadth is 9 meters.
