28. ist various types Viewing materials lan stinguish them Plss pasagot​


Answer:

There are two basic types of viewing materials. These are print and non-print.

Answer:

Here are some examples of viewing materials and how to distinguish between them:

• Books - Books are printed materials that contain text and can be either fiction or non-fiction. They can be distinguished by their size, cover design, and format (hardcover, paperback, etc.).

• Newspapers - Newspapers are printed materials that contain current news and information. They can be distinguished by their size, layout, and content (local, national, international).

• Magazines - Magazines are printed materials that contain a variety of articles, photographs, and advertisements. They can be distinguished by their subject matter (news, fashion, entertainment, etc.) and frequency of publication (weekly, monthly, etc.).

• Websites - Websites are electronic resources that contain text, images, and other multimedia content. They can be distinguished by their domain name (e.g., www.google.com) and the type of information they provide (news, shopping, social networking, etc.).

• Television - Television is a medium that delivers audio and visual content through the airwaves or via cable or satellite. It can be distinguished by the type of programming it offers (news, movies, sports, etc.) and the channel or network it is on (e.g., CNN, HBO, ESPN).

• Films - Films are visual works that are presented in a cinematic format, typically shown in a movie theater or on television. They can be distinguished by their genre (action, comedy, drama, etc.), length (feature film or short film), and format (live action, animation, documentary, etc.).
