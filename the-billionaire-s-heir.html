23. According to the last will and testament of Don Juan,5 heirs will divide his wealth. Heir 1 will receive 20% of his fortune,20% of the remaining to heir 2,and so on with heir 5 to receive 262,400. What is the value of Don Juan's wealth?


To solve this problem,

Let P be Don Juan’s fortune to be divided by his 5 heirs

20 % to Heir 1 is the same as

0.20P

The remaining will be

P - 0.20P = 0.80P

20% of the remaining is for the Heir 2

0.8P * 0.2 = 0.16P

The remaining will be

0.8P - 0.16P = 0.64P

20% of the remaining is for the Heir 3

0.64P * 0.2 = 0.128P

The remaining will be

0.64P - 0.128P = 0.512P

20% of the remaining is for the Heir 4

0.512P * 0.2 = 0.1024P

The remaining will be

0.512P - 0.1024P = 0.4096P

20% of the remaining is for the Heir 5

0.4096P * 0.2 = 0.08192P

Since Heir 5 has received 0.8192P or 292,400. We will now get the value of P

0.08192P = 292,400

P = 292,400/0.08192

P = 3,569,335.9375



Answer. Don Juan has $3,569,335.94 amount of money in his wealth
