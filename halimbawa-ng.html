3. sampung halimbawa ng stresssampung halimbawa ng eustresssampung halimbawa ng distresssampung halimbawa ng depression​


Answer:

Explanation:

I can provide you with general information about stress, eustress, distress, and depression.

Stress is a natural and normal response of the body to a perceived threat or challenge. It is the body's way of preparing itself to deal with a situation. Examples of stressors include work-related stress, financial problems, family issues, and health concerns.

Eustress, on the other hand, is a positive form of stress. It is the type of stress that motivates and energizes a person, helping them achieve their goals. Examples of eustress include the excitement of starting a new job, getting married, or having a child.

Distress is the negative form of stress. It can occur when a person experiences an overwhelming and uncontrollable situation or when a person perceives a threat that they cannot cope with. Examples of distress include the loss of a job, the death of a loved one, or a traumatic event.

Depression is a mental health disorder that affects a person's mood, behavior, and overall well-being. It is a serious medical condition that requires treatment. Symptoms of depression include persistent sadness, loss of interest or pleasure, fatigue, insomnia or hypersomnia, and feelings of worthlessness or guilt.

If you are experiencing distress, depression, or any other mental health issue, it is important to seek professional help from a mental health provider. They can provide you with the support and treatment you need to manage your symptoms and improve your quality of life.
