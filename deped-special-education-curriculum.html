10. choose five competencies in the deped curriculum guide of your specialization .choose the most appropriate assessment tool each competency .​


The Department of Education (DepEd) identifies competencies in teaching to ensure that educators have the necessary skills, knowledge, and attitudes to effectively facilitate learning and provide quality education to students. Competencies in teaching refer to the specific knowledge, skills, and attitudes that teachers need to possess to be effective in their roles as educators.

Here are the suggested answers to your activity:

Oral Communication
Assessment tool: Speech delivery with a rubric that assesses the clarity, organization, and delivery of the speech.

Critical Thinking
Assessment tool: Multiple choice and open-ended questions that assess the student's ability to analyze and interpret information.

Scientific Inquiry
Assessment tool: Lab report with a rubric that assesses the student's ability to design and conduct experiments, collect data, analyze results, and draw conclusions.

Numeracy
Assessment tool: Math test with a variety of questions that assess the student's understanding of basic arithmetic, algebraic concepts, and problem-solving skills.

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Literacy
Assessment tool: Practical exam that assesses the student's ability to use various software applications, such as word processors, spreadsheets, and presentation software.
