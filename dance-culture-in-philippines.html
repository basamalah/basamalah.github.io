21. Discuss the history of Philippine Culture giving emphasis on dance?​


Answer:

Dance has always played a significant role in Filipino culture. From the Tinikling, one of the oldest dated dances, to other folkloric dances like the Pandanggo, Cariosa, and Subli, and even more modern-day dances like the ballet, there is no doubt that dance in the Philippines has integrated itself into society over many years and is deeply embedded in culture. Each of these dances has its own origins and has a specific function, demonstrating how diverse Philippine dances have become.
