11. ___ 5. The place of pregnancy that starts from week 13 to 27. a. First trimester b. Second Trimester c. Third Trimester d. Second and third trimesters


The correct answer is letter b. The pregnancy in week 13 to 27 is under the second trimester. This is maternal counting of trimester during pregnancy:

1st trimester- from 1st week to 12 weeks (or 1st to 3rd month)2nd trimester- from 13 weeks to 27 weeks (or from 4th to 6th month)3rd and final trimester- from 28 weeks to 40 weeks (7th to 9th month)

Pregnancy may be a full term and ready for delivery when it falls to 37th week but can be overdue when it turns to 40 weeks.

Pregnancy Milestone

What can you expect during second trimester? This is time that a pregnant mother can feel that there is a real baby! It is because she will feel the following symptoms:

Growing belly or your abdomen and breastsYou can feel the Braxton Hicks contractionsSkin changes and usually became more brownish to tintedNasal problems because of difficulty if breathingDental issues because of inflammationContinuing DizzinessLeg cramps because of limited blood circulation Vaginal discharge as usual

The second trimester would be the great feeling because you can feel the movement and kicking of the baby. You will also know through ultrasound the gender of the baby!

17. explain briefly each of the stages of development during pregnancy first trimester second trimester and third trimester​


First trimester – conception to 12 weeks. Second trimester – 12 to 24 weeks. Third trimester – 24 to 40 weeks.

Explanation:

The process of prenatal development occurs in three main stages. The first two weeks after conception are known as the germinal stage, the third through the eighth week is known as the embryonic period, and the time from the ninth week until birth is known as the fetal period.

22. Additional ActivitiesActivity 1: Describe ItIn this activity, you will describe the experiences of the pregnant woman inevery trimester of their pregnancy.Directions: Give the description of every trimester for pregnant women.First TrimesterSecond TrimesterThird Trimester​


Answer:

FIRST TRIMESTER

The first trimester begins on the first day of your last period and lasts until the end of week 12. This means that by the time you know for sure you're pregnant, you might already be five or six weeks pregnant! A lot happens during these first three months

SECOND TRIMESTER

The second trimester of your pregnancy is from week 13 to week 28 - roughly months four, five and six. As well as feeling and looking more pregnant during these weeks, you may also have more energy than you did in the first trimester

THIRD TRIMESTER

The third trimester of your pregnancy is from week 29 to week 40 - months seven, eight and nine. Feelings at this stage of pregnancy tend to go from tiredness and worry to excitement about the baby. Your baby continues to grow, and as the third trimester progresses they'll have a better chance if they're born early.

Your questions may be pregnancy from 29 weeks to birth. The correct answer is letter c. This phase of pregnancy is known as the third and final trimester of the pregnancy. Pregnancy can be divided into three trimesters (there are three months per group) and they are:

first trimester from 1st to 3rd month or 1st week to 12 weekssecond trimester from 4th to 6 month or the 13 weeks to 28 weeks pf pregnancythird trimester from 7th to 9 months or the 29 weeks to birth of the baby

Pregnancy Milestone

The third trimester tend to be tiring and exciting.

You can be worried if the baby will delivery earlier from the expected date or will become overdue. You may wonder if the baby will deliver normally or through cesarean section.
