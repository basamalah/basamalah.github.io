11. Characteristics of teaching as pedagogy


Answer:

- Teaching as pedagogy involves the transfer of knowledge and skills from a teacher to a student.

- It requires the use of effective communication and instructional techniques to convey information and promote learning.

- Pedagogy involves a learner-centered approach where the teacher focuses on the needs and interests of the student.

- It involves creating a positive learning environment that is conducive to learning and encourages student participation.

- Teaching as pedagogy also involves assessing and evaluating student learning to ensure that learning objectives are met.

- It requires the teacher to be knowledgeable, skilled, and experienced in the subject matter being taught.

- Pedagogy also involves adapting teaching methods and strategies to meet the diverse learning needs of students.
