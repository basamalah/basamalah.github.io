15. Critical Thinking: Fibonacci sequence1. If f6 = 50 and f5 = 31 , What is the value of f3?​


Answer:

f3 = 12

Step-by-step explanation:

Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers that when you add consecutive terms, you will get the next term.

Example:

0,1,1,2,3,5,8,13,21,...

Rule: The next number is found by adding up the two numbers before it.

Since we are given that the 6th term is 50 and the 5th term is 31, we can do the process backwards to find the 3rd term in the sequence. Instead of adding the consecutive terms, we need to subtract them to get the previous term.

50 (6th term) - 31 (5th term) = 19 (4th term)

31 (5th term) - 19 (4th term) = 12 (3rd term)

Therefore, the 3rd term in the sequence is 12.
