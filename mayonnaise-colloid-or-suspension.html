28. II. Direction : Determine if the following mixtures are Solution, Suspension or Colloid1. Mayonnaise​


Answer:

Colloid

Explanation:

Mayonnaise is an emulsion. Emulsions are colloids where both phases of the mixture are liquids that can't be normally combined. Mayonnaise is an emulsion colloid made from polar vinegar and nonpolar oil emulsified by egg yolk that prevents their separation.
