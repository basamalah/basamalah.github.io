The distance between the lighthouse and the ship forms a right triangle with the ship's path and the line connecting the lighthouse to the ship. From this triangle, we can use the Pythagorean theorem to find the distance between the ship and the lighthouse:

distance^2 = (10)^2 + (8)^2

distance = sqrt(164) or about 12.80 miles

Now that we know the distance, we can figure out how long it will take the ship to travel this distance at a speed of 12 miles per hour:

time = distance / speed

time = 12.8 / 12

time = 1.07 or about 1 hour and 4 minutes

Therefore, the ship will be 8 miles from the lighthouse about 1 hour and 4 minutes after it departs from the dock at noon.
