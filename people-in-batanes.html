14. What do the arts and crafts of the people of Batanes tell you about their culture?


Answer:

The arts and crafts in Batanes show so much about their culture. Their crafts like Vakul and Soot represents how people value and love their people. It also shows their resourcefulness in using localized materials. Their art seen in paintings are realistic in theme and depiction. It expresses the Filipino Bayanihan spirit, how simple their life is, and how they love nature.

Explanation:

The Batanes Vakul is a head gear used by Ivatan women. This craft is used to protect them from the heat of the sun and the rains. On the other hand, Soot is for Ivatan men. It is a rain cape made from fronds that cover the upper part of the body down from the neck. The Yaru Nu Artes Ivatan is the one and only art gallery in town of Basco. "Yaru” in their local Ivatan language means cooperativism (bayanihan). For them, they come together to contribute for a common cause.

23. (pls help)The latest census released in the year 2016 by the Philippine Statistical Authority (PSA)has shown that from the total population of Batanes, fifty percent comes from the town ofBasco. If there are 8 500 people living in Basco, Batanes, how many people are living inBatanes?


Answer:

The total population of Batanes is 17,000 people, if fifty percent comes from the town of Basco.The formula for finding the percentage of a number is:part / whole x 100 = percentage. We know that the part is 8,500 and the whole is 17,000.8,500 / 17,000 x 100 = 50. Therefore, 50% of the total population of Batanes is 8,500 people.

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: The formula for finding the percentage of a number is: part / whole x 100 = percentage

Step 2: We know that the part is 8,500 and the whole is 17,000.

Step 3: Substitute the given values in the formula:

8,500 / 17,000 x 100 = 50

Step 4: To get the whole, you need to divide the part by the percentage expressed as a decimal.

8,500 / 0.50 = 17,000

Step 5: Therefore, 50% of the total population of Batanes is 8,500 people, and the total population of Batanes is 17,000 people.
