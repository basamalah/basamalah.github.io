21. chickens lay eggs which hatched into chicks a few weeks later is this an example of external or internal fertilization? explain your answer..


Hens mate with roosters and the roosters deposit sperm with the hens to fertilize the eggs. The eggs develop surrounded by that hard shell you crack to make breakfast. So, the deposit is external but the fertilization is internal.

And no, roosters don’t have penises. They sort of bump butts with the hens to make the exchange. Once you see that little dance in the barnyard, you never forget it. It doesn’t really look like anyone is having much fun.
