28. Problem solving:Exponential functionA certain barangay in Pagadian City has a population of 10,000 that is increasing at the rate of5% each year. Find the population of that barangay after 2 years.helpppp po​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Let:

Po = original population

Pn = new population

P = change in population

r = rate of growth

t = time in years

Solution:

At two years, t = 2, the change in population P is

P = (Po)(r)(t)

P = (10,000)(0.05)(2)  (Note: 5% = 0.05)

P = 1000

The new population after time t is given by the equation:

Pn = Po + P

Pn = 10,000 + 1,000

Pn = 11,000

Therefore, the population of the barangay in Pagadian City after 2 years will be 11,000
