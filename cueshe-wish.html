4. Students spend their time going to classes, studying, working, and (they wish) they had time for a social life. Which among the choices can best replace the phrase in the parenthesis to achieve parallelism [{ A. Going to wish}] [[B. They Wish]] {{C. Wished}} ((D. Wishing))


Answer:

D. Wishing

Explanation:

Students spend their time going to classes, studying, working, and (they wish) they had time for a social life. Which among the choices can best replace the phrase in the parenthesis to achieve parallelism

[{ A. Going to wish}] [[B. They Wish]] {{C. Wished}} ((D. Wishing))

D. wishing

the first verb is a simple present that pertains to an action done regularly. However,  the following verb phrases are in present continuous.  

D.)  wishing-- is the correct answer as it parallels and is consistent with the tense of the rest of the gerunds or verbs.

Also, the conjunction ‘and’ demands that the words it separates are at least consistent in the category.  

Example:

I like to hike, swim and playing golf (x) (wrong)

I like to hike swim, and play golf (o) (correct)

The mother cleans the balcony.

Mother is a SINGULAR form of a NOUN, while Cleans is a PLURAL form of a VERB. The rule is that you must not combine two singular or plural noun and verb.

PATTERN:  (Singular noun) + (Plural verb) OR (Plural noun) + (Singular verb).
