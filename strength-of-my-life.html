Here are three sources of happiness that can make you feel happy throughout the day.

Feelings of love and affection

The source of happiness that will never be timeless is love and affection. This is something that has to do with the quality of your social relationships with other people. Someone who has good social relations with anyone, surely his days will be filled with happiness.

The quality you have in yourself

One's ability to develop self-quality to always spread love and kindness is the right step to find a source of happiness that never ends.

In contrast to those who are busy pursuing happiness outside of themselves, such as pursuing wealth, wanting a high position, and other material things. People who pursue happiness from outside themselves, will not be able to see the true source of happiness.

Ability to appreciate things

The next source of happiness is the ability to appreciate everything. Being appreciative indicates that you are able to see things from a different angle and definitely from the positive side. You are able to see positive things from various sides of a life. This is why you are always good at being grateful in various situations and conditions.
