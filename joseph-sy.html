21. in the figure below, SY and EY are secants.If SY=24cm, TY=6cm and LY=8cm. How long is EY?​


Answer:

11.25cm

Given:

SY =24cm

TY =6cm

LY =8cm

Step-by-step explanation:

If the vertex of two secants is outside the circle, then the lines outside the circle (TY or LY) time the whole line (SY or EY) is equal in other words or numbers,

TY . SY = LY . EY

Just substitute the given to the formula:

6cm . 15cm = 8cm . EY

90 = 8 EY

Divide both side by 8

90/8 = EY or on decimals 11.25cm
