13. a shop repaired 40 vehicles (cars and motorcycles) in a month. the total number of wheels was 100 how many cars and motorcycles were repaired?​


Answer:

10 cars and 30 motorcycles

Step-by-step explanation:

Pabrainliest po :)

Let x be the number of cars

Let y be the number of motorcycles

Total vehicles = x + y

40 = x + y

x = 40- y **Equation 1**

Number of wheels = 4x(for cars) + 2y(for motorcycle)

100 = 4x + 2y **Equation 2**

Substitute Equation 1 to 2

100 = 4(40 - y) +2y

100 = 160 - 4y +2y

100 = 160 -2y

2y = 60

y = 30

and substitute y to Equation 1 gives us

40 = x +30

x = 10

Therefore the answers are x = 10 and y = 30

120,000 = repair + cost of car

let x = cost of car

120,000 = 1.2x

x = 120,000 / 1.2 = 100,000
