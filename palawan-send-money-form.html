There is no days to limit to claim money in Palawan Express as long as it is there for a year. The remittance may be claimed a year given that there is a charge or penalty after thirty calendar days. After thirty days, it will be subjected to a handling fee which is one percent (1 %) of the principal amount or amount sent to you. This charge will be per month.
