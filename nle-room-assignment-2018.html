9. find the actual time from february 20 2018 to december 15 2018


Answer:

The time between February 20, 2018, and December 15, 2018, can be calculated by finding the difference between the two dates.

Counting the days between the two dates:

February has 28 days and March has 31 days, so the number of days between February 20 and March 20 is 28 + 31 = 59.

April, June, September, and November each have 30 days, so the number of days between March 20 and December 1 (excluding December) is 30 x 4 = 120.

December has 31 days, so the number of days between December 1 and December 15 is 15.

Therefore, the total number of days between February 20, 2018, and December 15, 2018, is:

59 + 120 + 15 = 194 days.

So, the actual time between the two dates is 194 days.

12. essay of a sona 2018


Here is a guide to help you write your essay on SONA 2018 of President Duterte which happened last July 23, 2018.

An essay is a short piece of writing on a particular subject.

To help you regarding your essay of latest SONA 2018 speech (note that SONA 2018 date was on July 23, 2018), here is a quick SONA 2018 summary:

> His introduction for SONA included mentioning the names of key guests during the event, and recalling his oath-taking that happened about two years ago.

> Drugs – He said that the war on drugs will still be “relentless” and he gave criticisms about the human rights groups who are against his anti-drug campaign.

> Corruption – He mentioned that Congress successfully passed the Ease of Doing Business Act. He urged all agencies to stop red tape and make processes efficient. He contradicted "lousy, corrupt bureaucracy". He said he needs to fire friends due to corruption.

> Mindanao – He promised to increase significantly the budget for Mindanao. He also promised to sign the Bangsamoro Law within 48 hours after Congress passes the approved version to Malacanang. He also recognized the soldiers and policemen who died fighting in Marawi City. He said he welcomes with open arms terrorists who will surrender to authorities.

> Foreign policy – He vowed to assert independent foreign policy. He mentioned reenergized ties with China and promises to defend the West Philippine Sea. He said to seek stronger bonds with fellow ASEAN countries.

> Telecommunications and internet – He re-emphasized his promise to ensure affordable, reliable, affordable, and secure internet telecommunications services. He promised to lower interconnection rates between industry players.

> Tax reform – He told Congress that he wants to sign TRAIN Law 2 by end of 2018. He mentioned that TRAIN Law is for the sustainable growth that leaves no Filipino behind.

> Rice – He asked businesses to cooperate in stabilizing rice prices. By the end of July 2018, rice tarrification would have to be submitted to Congress.

> Health – He called Congress to pass the universal health bill. He mentioned that government is currently institutionalizing the unified implementation of “No Balance Billing” policy.

> Federalism – He urged Filipinos to support the shift to a federal system of government. He mentioned that he has no illusions of occupying office one day longer than what the Constitution permits.

On labor –

> Overseas Filipino Workers – He said that the rights of OFWs are the administration’s foremost foreign policy concern.

> Contractualization – He said that the efforts of government resulted to the regularization of 300,000 workers. He urges Congress to pass a law prohibiting all forms of contractualization.

> Coconut Farmers – He urged Congress to pass bills creating coconut farmers’ trust fund.

On environment –

> Boracay Closure – He defended his move of closing the island as an environmental protection measure. He warned Local Governments Units (LGUs) to do their part and not to wait before the national government comes in.

> Land Use Act – He called Congress to pass a law to create a national land use act policy which will address competing land requirement for food, housing, business and environmental conservation.

> Disaster Management – He mentioned that we must learn from Typhoon Yolanda and other disasters. He called Congress to pass a law to create a disaster management department whose resources are way better than existing disaster response agencies.

> Warning to Miners – He warned irresponsible miners to stop destroying watersheds, forests and water forms.

19. reaksyon of sona 2018


Narito ang gabay para makatulong sa iyo na magsulat ng reaksyon tungkol sa SONA 2018 ni Presidente Duterte noong Hulyo 23, 2018.

Para makasulat ng reaksyon sa SONA ni Duterte, kailangan mong maaral ang paksa na ibinigay sa inyo. Dito iikot ang mga reaksyon at panig mo sa papel na iyong isusulat na may kaugnayan sa SONA at pamumuno ni Pangulong Duterte.

> Droga – Sinabi ng Pangulo na ang “war on drugs” ay magpapatuloy. Nagbigay siya ng kritisismo tungkol sa mga grupo na bumabatikos sa kumpanya laban sa droga.

> Korapsyon – Sinabi niya na napasa na ng Kongreso ang “Ease of Doing Business Act”. Hinikayat niya lahat ng mga ahensya na ihinto na ang “red tape” at pabilisin ang mga proseso sa kanilang mga ahensya. Nabanggit ng Pangulo na kinailangan niyang alisin ang mga kaibigan niya sa puwesto dahil sa pangungurakot ng mga ito.

> Mindanao – Nangako ang pangulo na palalakihin niya ang budget para sa Mindanao. Nangako rin siya na pipirmahan niya ang batas tungkol sa Bangsamoro sa loob ng 48 na oras pagkatapos ipasa ng Kongreso sa Malacanang ang aprubadong bersyon ng batas. Pinarangalan din niya ang mga sundalo at pulis na nasawi para lumaban sa Marawi. Bukas-kamay raw niyang sasalubungin ang mga teroristang susuko sa mga awtoridad.

> “Foreign policy” – Nangako siya na magsaysay ng “independent foreign policy”. Nabanggit niya na palawigin pa ang relasyon ng Pilipinas sa Tsina at ipagtatanggol daw niya ang West Philippine Sea. Nais niyang magtaguyod ng mas maayos na relasyon sa mga bansa na kasama sa ASEAN.

> Telekomunikasyon at Internet – Nabanggit niya ulit ang pangako niya sa pagkakaroon ng abot-kaya, ligtas at maasahang serbisyo ng internet. Nangako siya na pababain ang presyo ng mga nagbibigay ng serbisyo ng internet.

> Reporma sa buwis – Sinabihan niya ang Kongreso na nais niyang pirmahan ang TRAIN Law 2 bago matapos ang taong 2018. Nabanggit niya na ito ay para sa pagtataguyod ng pang-matagalan na progreso para sa mga Pilipino.

> Bigas – Sinabihan niya ang mga negosyante na makipagtulungan para sa pagpapatatag ng presyo ng bigas. Aniya, bago matapos ang Hulyo 2018, dapat ay maisumite na sa Kongreso ang tinatawag na “rice tarrification”.

> Kalusugan – Tinawag niya ang pansin ng Kongreso para maipasa ang “Universal Health Bill”. Nabanggit niya na pinapalawig niya ngayon ang pagpapatupad ng polisiya sa “No Balance Billing”.

> Pederalismo – Hinikayat niya ang mga Pilipino na suportahan ang paglipat sa pederalismo na sistema ng gobyerno. Aniya, wala raw siyang plano na manatili nang lagpas na kahit na isang araw pagkatapos ng kanyang termino na naaayon sa Konstitusyon.

> Mga manggagawa sa ibang bansa – Sinabi ng pangulo na ang karapatan daw ng mga manggagawa sa ibang bansa o OFWs ang prayoridad ng “foreign policy”.

> Kontraktwalisasyon – Sinabi ng pangulo na ang pagsusumikap ng gobyerno ay nagresulta sa regularisasyon ng 300,000 na mga manggagawa.

> Mga magsasaka ng niyog – Sinabihan niya ang Kongreso na magpasa ng mga batas para sa “trust fund” ng mga magsasaka ng niyog.

> Boracay – Ipinagtanggol niya ang pagsasara ng Boracay bilang proteksyon sa kapaligiran. Binalaan niya ang mga pamahalaang lokal (LGU) na gawin ang trabaho nila at huwag nang hintayin na pumasok sa eksena ang pamahalaan.

> Land Use Act – Tinawag niya ang atensyon ng Kongreso upang ipasa ang “National Land Use Act Policy” na tutugon sa mga pangangailangan ng lupa para sa pagkain, tirahan at pagpapanatili ng maayos na kapaligiran.

> Pamamahala ng Kalamidad – Sinabi niya na kailangan nating matuto sa Bagyong Yolanda at sa iba pang mga nakaraang kalamidad. Tinawag niya ang atensyon ng Kongreso para bumuo ng sangay para sa pamamahala ng kalamidad.

> Pagmimina – Binalaan niya ang mga iresponsableng minero na huwag sirain ang mga kagubatan, palatubigan at iba pang mga anyong tubig.

24. You are a financial Manager of Chevron Corp. You need to assess the effectiveness of working capital management of the company for 2018 using the following data. What is the 2018 Receivable turnover? 2017 Account Receivable = 15,353,000 2018 Account Receivable = 15,050,000 2017 Inventory = 5,585,000 2018 Inventory = 5,704,000 2017 Accounts Payable = 14,565,000 2018 Accounts Payable = 13,953,000 2017 Sales = 134,674,000 2018 Sales = 158,902,000 2017 Cost of Sales = 95,114,000 2018 Cost of Sales = 113,997,000 2017 Purchases = 95,114,000 2018 Purchases = 123,435,000 10.20 O 10.45 10.60​


Answer:

The formula for receivable turnover is:

Receivable turnover = Sales / Average Accounts Receivable

To calculate the average accounts receivable, we add the beginning and ending balances and divide by 2:

Average Accounts Receivable = (Beginning Accounts Receivable + Ending Accounts Receivable) / 2

Therefore, the average accounts receivable for Chevron Corp. in 2018 is:

Average Accounts Receivable = (15,353,000 + 15,050,000) / 2 = 15,201,500

And the receivable turnover for 2018 is:

Receivable turnover = 158,902,000 / 15,201,500 = 10.45

Therefore, the 2018 Receivable turnover for Chevron Corp. is 10.45.
