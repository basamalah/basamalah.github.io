1. Ano ang kahulugan ng Nineveh at Assyria?Nineveh-Assyria-​


Answer:

Nineveh - was an ancient Assyrian city of Upper Mesopotamia, located on the outskirts of Mosul in modern-day northern Iraq. It is located on the eastern bank of the Tigris River and was the capital and largest city of the Neo-Assyrian Empire, as well as the largest city in the world for several decades

Assyria- also called the Assyrian Empire, was a Mesopotamian kingdom and empire of the ancient Near East and the Levant that existed as a state from perhaps as early as the 25th century BC


2. Destruction of Nineveh​


Answer:

In 612 BCE the city of Nineveh was sacked and burned by the allied forces of the Persians, Medes, Babylonians, and others who then divided the region between them. The area was sparsely populated thereafter and, slowly, the ancient ruins became buried in earth.

Katanungan:

9. Ang mga ito ang sumalakay sa Nineveh noong 606 BCE.

Kasagutan:

Sila ay ang mga Medes, Chaldeans, at Scythians.

Sinalakay at nilusob nila ang Nineveh noong 612 B.C.E. sa pamamagitan ng kanilang pinag-samang puwersa at hukbo sa ilalim ng mga kumander na si Cyaxares, na isang hari ng mga Medes, at Napobolassar, na isang hari ng mga Chaldeans. Ang kumander ng hukbo ng Inperyong Assyrian ay si Sin-shar-ishkun, na isang hari ng Imperyong Assyrian. Ang labanang ito ay isang mapagpasyang tagumpay para sa hukbo ng mga mananalakay. Naganap din ang iba't iba pang mga pangyayari tulad ng pagkawasak ng Nineveh, pagkakatatag ng Imperyong Chaldean, at ang Inperyong Assyrian ay malubhang humina. Dahik dito, ang kabisera ng Imperyong Assyrian ay inilipat sa Harran na sinalakay at nilusob ng mga hukbo ng Medes at Chaldeans sa ilalim ni Napobolassar at Cyaxares noong 609 B.C.E.

14. sanaysay battle of mactan an battle mactan covid​


Answer:

The Battle of Mactan (Cebuano: Gubat sa Mactan; Filipino: Labanan sa Mactan) was a fierce clash fought in the Philippines on 27 April 1521. The warriors of Lapulapu, one of the Datus of Mactan, overpowered and defeated a Spanish force fighting for Rajah Humabon of Cebu under the command of Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan, who was killed in battle. The outcome of the battle resulted in the departure of the Spanish crew from the archipelago, and delayed the Spanish colonization of the Philippines by 44 years until the conquest by Miguel López de Legazpi in 1564–1565.

17. During World War II, which battle denied the Japanese the opportunity to attack Australia? Battle of the Coral Sea Battle of Midway Battle of Guadalcanal Battle of Iwo Jima


The Battle of Coral Sea which took place in May 1942 is the main reason for the failed invasion. This had also been the very first air-sea combat in history. This skirmish happened because the Japanese wanted to gain control over the Western Pacific. Also known as “The Battle that Saved Australia”, this clash at Coral Sea went on for four days until the American and Australian forces strategically put an end to it.

American soldiers greatly relied geographically on Australia as a means to get to Japan. This has been the main reason why Japan wanted to advance in this area.  However, the damage done to the Japanese carrier ship Shokaku fighter jets had significantly decreased Japanese forces in another battle which occurred the following month - The Battle of Midway.

Answer:

B.Polytheist

Explanation:

Ang politeismo ay ang paniniwala na maraming mga diyos. Ang salitang "poly" ay nagmula sa wikang Griyego na "marami" at ang "theism" ay mula sa salitang Griyego na "Diyos."Maaaring ang Politeismo (Polytheism) ang pinakadominanteng pananaw tungkol sa Diyos sa kasaysayan ng mundo. Ang pinakakilalang halimbawa ng politeismo sa sinaunang panahon ay ang mitolohiyang Griyego at Romano (Zeus, Apollo, Aphrodite, Poseidon atbp.). Ang pinakamalinaw na modernong halimbawa ng politeismo ay ang Hinduismo (Hinduism) na may mahigit sa 300 milyon na mga diyos. Kahit na sa esensya, ang hinduismo ay pantheistic o naniniwala na ang lahat ay diyos, naniniwala rin ito na marami ang diyos. Kapansin pansin na kahit sa mga relihiyon na naniniwala sa maraming diyos, mayroon pa ring pinakamataas na Diyos na higit sa ibang mga diyos, halimbawa, si Zeus sa Griyego/Romanong mitolohiya at si Brahman sa Hinduismo.
