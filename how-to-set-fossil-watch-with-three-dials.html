3. how does excessive usage of fossil fuels affetc earth? cite three ways that help limit the consumption of fossil fuels​


Excessive usage of fossil fuels can have a number of negative impacts on the earth. Here are three ways that can help limit the consumption of fossil fuels:

Increasing the use of renewable energy sources: Renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power do not produce greenhouse gases and can help reduce our reliance on fossil fuels.

Improving energy efficiency: Making buildings, vehicles, and appliances more energy efficient can reduce the amount of energy needed to power them, which in turn reduces the demand for fossil fuels.

Promoting public transportation and carpooling: Encouraging the use of public transportation and carpooling can reduce the number of cars on the road, which will reduce the demand for gasoline and other fossil fuels.

9. What three features must a fossil have in order to be an index fossil?​


Answer:

A useful index fossil must be distinctive or easily recognizable, abundant, and have a wide geographic distribution and a short range through time. Index fossils are the basis for defining boundaries in the geologic time scale and for the correlation of strata.
