17. Boyboy had 4 boxes of Buko Pie. He gave 18 cut to his classmates and 16 cuts to his cousins. There were 6 cuts of Buko pie left. If each of the 4 boxes had the same parts of Buko Pie, how many cuts were there in each pan?


Answer:

10

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us analyze the problem.

Boyboy has four (4) boxes of Buko Pie. He gave eighteen (18) cuts of Buko Pie to his classmates and sixteen (16) cuts to his cousins. Now, there are six (6) cuts of Buko pie left. If each of the four (4) boxes had the same number of parts of Buko Pie, how many cuts were there in each pan?

We will add the numbers of Buko Pie given and left. It means that we will add 18, 16, and 6 together.

18 + 16 + 6 = 40

It means that there are 40 pieces of Buko Pie in total.

Boyboy had four (4) cartons of Buko Pie, that statement says. To find how many pieces are in each pan, we need to divide the number of parts by the number of boxes. It means that we will divide 40 by 4.

40/4 = 10

Therefore, there are ten (10) cuts in each box of Buko Pie.
