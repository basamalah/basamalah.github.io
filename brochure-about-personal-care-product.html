15. brochure about mental health (definition of stress, signs that you are stressed, ways on how to take care of mental health​


Answer:

Mental Health Brochure

Definition of Stress:

Stress is a natural psychological and physiological response to the demands of daily life. It is a normal part of life and can be positive or negative. Positive stress can help you stay focused, energetic and alert, while negative stress can interfere with your daily activities and harm your mental and physical health.

Signs that you are stressed:

Fatigue or lack of energyInsomnia or sleeping too muchChanges in appetite or eating habitsDifficulty concentratingHeadaches, muscle tension or painIncreased irritability or angerFeelings of anxiety or depressionWays on how to take care of mental health:

Exercise regularly: Regular exercise has been shown to reduce stress and improve mental health. Aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity each day.

Practice mindfulness: Mindfulness is the practice of being present in the moment and paying attention to your thoughts and feelings. This can help reduce stress and improve mental health.

Connect with others: Strong relationships with friends, family and community can help provide support and reduce stress.

Get enough sleep: Aim for 7 to 9 hours of sleep each night. Lack of sleep can increase stress and negatively impact mental health.

Manage your time effectively: Setting priorities and avoiding overcommitment can help reduce stress and improve mental health.

Seek support: If you are feeling overwhelmed or struggling with mental health issues, reach out to a trusted friend, family member or mental health professional for support.

Taking care of your mental health is important. If you are experiencing stress or mental health issues, it is important to reach out for help and support
