Chinese musical theaters operate as representations of Asian theater  art forms within the musical arts competence of the world.

The Chinese theatre is  exceptional on its own.  In the Middle Ages there was a troupe, where  only men or women performed male and female roles.  Furthermore, the character 's existence could be identified not only by an  actor performing a part but also by the make-up added to his face.  The audience interpreted the role  type according to the colour.  The red color for instance showed the hero 's loyalty and  integrity, the gold one was used for the mythical heroes, etc.

The most important rule in traditional theater is that all participants must have not  only the skill in acting, but also a musical ability, as well as oratory craft.  They must possess outstanding skills in dance and martial arts, the latter  being required to create plays on military themes, favorite for the Chinese.

Opera is one of  the varieties of Chinese theater.  A dramatic production makes  Chinese opera almost indistinguishable.  The musicians need not only  single voices but also creative talent.  It should be noted that the popular Peking Opera has  to date been considered the best opera house in China.
