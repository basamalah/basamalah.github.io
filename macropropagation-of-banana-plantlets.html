20. 11. In vegetative propagation, bamboo and other grasses are examples of:a. tubersb. cormsc. rhizomesd. plantlets​


Answer:

C. rhizomes

Explanation:

Bamboo rhizome is the underside of a bamboo stem (culm). However, rhizomes are not roots, roots grow beneath the rhizome and culms erect above the rhizome. Axillary buds on the rhizome may produce new rhizomes, or bamboo shoots that grow into culms. Like the culm, rhizomes have nodes and interns.
