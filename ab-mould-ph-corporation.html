7. describe the mode of reproduction in bread moulds​


Answer:

Bread mold reproduces asexually, using mitosis to form spores, and sexually. Sexual reproduction involves contact between opposite mating strains of the fungus to form a zygospore. Bread mold, Rhizopus stolonifer, most often reproduces asexually.
