If a product costs $ 100.00 with VAT included, how much does it cost without VAT? If the price of a pack of energy saving light bulbs is priced at $ 240.00, plus 16% VAT, what would its price be, including VAT?


Answer:

1. The cost without vat is 86.21

2. It's price including vat is 278.40

Step-by-step explanation:

VAT is a tax that is usually 16% on the value of certain products. If a product costs $ 100.00 with VAT included, how much does it cost without VAT?

Solution :

100 / 1.16 = 86.21 cost without the VAT

To solve for VAT :

100 - 86.21 = 13.79 vat

If the price of a pack of energy saving light bulbs is priced at $ 240.00, plus 16% VAT, what would its price be, including VAT?

Solution :

240 x 16% = 38.40 vat

240 + 38.40 = 278.40

It's price including vat is 278.40

20. how does the VAT and E-VAT differ from each other​


Answer:Difference between VAT and non VAT

When a business entity is VAT registered, it is subject to 12% sales tax on its gross sales or receipts. Such sales tax is referred to as VAT or Output Tax. On the other hand, if a business entity is NON-VAT, it is subject to 3% sales tax on its gross sales or receipts. Such sales tax is referred to as Percentage Tax.
