A prefix is a letter or a group of letters that attaches to the beginning of a word and helps to indicate or modify its meaning.
A suffix is a letter or a group of letters that is usually added onto the end of words, to change the way a word fits into a sentence grammatically.

because to add integers having the same sign, keep the same sign and add the absolute value of each number.
