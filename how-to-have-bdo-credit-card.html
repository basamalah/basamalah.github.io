1. Suppose that the balance of a certain credit card is 43,744.53 pesos. The credit card company charges 3.5% per month. How much is the total balance owed to the credit card company?​


The Credit Card company charges a monthly fee of 3.5%

Every Month the Credit Card Company charges you for Php 1,531.06.

That is, 43,744.53 x 0.035 = 1,531.06

Your total balance after that is  Php 42, 213.47

Answer:

45275.59 pesos

Step-by-step explanation:

Credit Card Fee = 3.5%

Find the fee:

Fee = 3.5% of 43,744.53 pesos

Fee = 0.035 x 43,744.53

Fee = 1531.06

Find the balance owed:

Credit Card balance = 43,744.53 pesos

Fee = 1531.06 pesos

Total = 43,744.53 + 1531.06

Total = 45275.59 pesos

Answer: 45275.59 pesos
