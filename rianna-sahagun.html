3. what is the brief history of san juan sahagun parish​


Answer:

The town of Candon was established as a parish on June 29, 1591 under the patronage of St. Francis of Assisi but changed later to San Juan de Sahagun. It started as a convent house for Augustinian Order without a resident priest. In 1594, it was established as a priory with the right to vote in the provincial chapters
