18. what is the purpose of auger bits materials?​


Answer:

Auger drill bits are used to cut larger holes in wood or similar materials and feature extra-large flutes to enable the cut chips to be removed from the hole. Similarly, spade drill bits (also known as paddle bits) have a flat wide blade that can be used to cut larger holes in wood.
