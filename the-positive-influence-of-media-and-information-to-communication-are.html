Explanation:          

Communication is a process when a person or persons, groups, organizations, and society create, and use information to connect with the environment and other people. Communication can be verbal and nonverbal. Media is one component of communication, namely as a messenger from the communicator to the communicant. Media serves as a means to resolve limitations on the senses, space, and time. Therefore, the existence media makes it easy for all people to obtain information.

Social media reduces barriers in communication, making it easier for everyone to express their thoughts to the world. Social media also helps expand one's knowledge, interpersonal communication skills of an individual are being affected in the process. While the understanding of information in general, information is data that has been processed into another form that is more useful, namely knowledge or information intended for recipients in making decisions, both now and in the future.

Information media can be concluded as a tool for collecting and rearranging information so that it becomes useful material for the recipient of the information. Communication media is an accurate tool for conveying messages and information to the recipient of the message. With the presence of communication media, the messenger can convey a message or information. Information provides events and conditions in a given society, indicates power relations, and facilitates various kinds of innovation. That way, the general public can obtain information related to their needs and interests and as a source of new knowledge.

There are various types of media that are often used to communicate and disseminate information, including television, radio, newspapers or newspapers, magazines, internet websites, and so on.The information presented form of facts or data about a matter. Information for the community is very important in empowering their lives to improve. With the flood of information for the community, it is possible for more people to gain knowledge and knowledge that is usually only owned by professional groups so that it can be promoted.
