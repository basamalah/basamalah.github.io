26. A coat regularly sells for $59.00. It is on sale for 20% off. What is the sale price of the coat?


Answer:

$47.20.

Step-by-step explanation:

Original price : $59.00 ( 100 % )

100 % - 20 % = 80%.

100 % - $59.00

1 % - $59.00 / 100 = $0.59

80 % - $0.59 x 80 = $47.20

Hence, the sale price of the coat is $47.20.
