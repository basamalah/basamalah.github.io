30. There are 12 maya birds and 15 love birds in an aviary. What is the ratio of maya birds to love birds?


Answer:

4:5

Step-by-step explanation:

the original ratio would be 12:15.

next, we simplify the ratio. we do that by finding the highest common factor of 12 and 15, which would be 3. we now divide both 12 and 15 by 3, making the simplified ratio 4:5
