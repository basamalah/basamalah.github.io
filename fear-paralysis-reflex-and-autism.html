6. ano ang autism spectrum​


Answer:

Ang autism spectrum disorder (ASD) ay isang developmental disability dulot ng mga pagkakaiba sa utak na lumalabas sa pag-uugali, pakikipag-ugnayan, wika, at minsan, talino ng isang tao. Ito ay tinatawag na "spectrum disorder" dahil ang kondisyon ay maaaring maging banayad hanggang malubha, sabi ng ang US National Institute of Mental Health (NIH).

Answer & Explanation:

Ang Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) ay isang problema o karamdaman na nagsisimula nang maaga sa pagkabata at tumatagal sa buong buhay ng isang tao. Nakakaapekto ito sa kung paano kumilos at nakikipag-ugnayan ang isang tao sa iba, nakikipag-usap, at natututo. 16. how to cure sleep paralysis ?​


Answer:

There is no cure for sleep paralysis. The treatment consists of managing the risk factors that trigger the condition. In many cases sleep paralysis is a one-off occurrence and the person does not have a recurrence. Most of us may expect to experience sleep paralysis at least once in our lives. However, for people who experience the condition frequently, it would make sense to see a doctor to get a formal diagnosis.

17. What are autism attacks? ​


Answer:

Adults on the autism spectrum may be prone to anxiety or distress, which in extreme situations could lead to panic attacks. Panic attacks are a terrifying experience where the body reacts as if it is in immense danger, in a situation where most people would not be afraid.
