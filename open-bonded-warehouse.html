23. : The Rosemarie Cosmetic company has a warehouse of 20 high pallet racks serviced by counter-balanced fork trucks. The aisles between racks are 12 feet wide. Storage space has become a premium with new product lines being added. A warehouse expansion is currently being planned. Question: How can the warehouse handling system be improved without expanding the warehouse?​


Answer:

The Rosemarie Cosmetic company can improve its warehouse handling system by reducing the number of pallets per rack. Currently, the racks are stocked with 20 pallets each; however, this can be reduced to 15, which will allow for more space between pallets and therefore make it easier for fork trucks to move around the warehouse.

Other improvements could include increased lighting and better signage. The current lighting is not sufficient enough to illuminate all areas of the warehouse; therefore, it would be beneficial to install additional lights that can provide sufficient illumination throughout the entire warehouse. In addition, signage should be added to clarify where certain items are stored so that employees do not have difficulty finding what they need when they need it.
