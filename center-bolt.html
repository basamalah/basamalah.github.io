10. The total force of 125 kn is required of flange coupling that has 5 bolts has also shearing stress of 30 mpa for bolt. determine the required diameter of bolt.


If the total force of 125 kilo-Newton is required for flange coupling that has 5 bolts and also has a shearing stress of 30 mega-Pascal, the required diameter of the bolt is 0.07 meters.

How to solve it?

We know that the formula for shear stress is [tex]\tau=\frac{F}{A}[/tex].

Where:

[tex]\tau[/tex] is the shear stress

F is the applied force

A is the cross-sectional area

Note: The formula for getting the cross-sectional area is [tex]\frac{\pi }{4 d^2}[/tex].

Solution:

d= [tex]\sqrt{ \frac{4F}{\pi \tau} }[/tex] = [tex]\sqrt{\frac{4(120kN)}{\pi (30MPa)} }[/tex] = 0.072836562 meters

Let's denote the number of bolts produced by machine B per day as "x".

According to the problem statement, machine A produces three times as many bolts as machine B, so the number of bolts produced by machine A per day is 3x.

Machine C produces 13 more bolts than machine A, so the number of bolts produced by machine C per day is (3x + 13).

The total production is given as 4997 bolts per day, so we can write:

x + 3x + (3x + 13) = 4997

Simplifying this equation, we get:

7x + 13 = 4997

Subtracting 13 from both sides, we get:

7x = 4984

Dividing both sides by 7, we get:

x = 712

So, machine B produces 712 bolts per day.

Using the values of x and 3x, we get:

Machine A produces 3x = 3(712) = 2136 bolts per day.

Machine C produces (3x + 13) = (2136 + 13) = 2149 bolts per day.

Therefore, machine B produces 712 bolts per day, machine A produces 2136 bolts per day, and machine C produces 2149 bolts per day.

PROBABILITY TULONGA manufacturer of bolts knows that the lengths of bolts they manufacture follow a normal distribution with a mean of 8 cm with standard deviation of 0.05 cm. c) If the company destroys bolts that are longer than 8.12 cm or shorter than 7.85 cm, how many percent of the bolts will be destroyed?​


To solve this problem, we need to find the area under the normal curve that represents the probability of a bolt being longer than 8.12 cm or shorter than 7.85 cm.

First, we need to calculate the z-scores for these values using the formula:

z = (x - μ) / σ

where x is the bolt length, μ is the mean, and σ is the standard deviation.

For 8.12 cm:

z = (8.12 - 8) / 0.05 = 2.4

For 7.85 cm:

z = (7.85 - 8) / 0.05 = -3

Next, we need to find the area under the normal curve to the left of z = -3 and to the right of z = 2.4. We can use a standard normal distribution table or a calculator to find these probabilities.

Using a calculator, we can find:

P(z < -3) = 0.0013

P(z > 2.4) = 0.0082

To find the total probability of bolts being destroyed, we add these probabilities:

P(z < -3 or z > 2.4) = P(z < -3) + P(z > 2.4) = 0.0013 + 0.0082 = 0.0095

Finally, we convert this probability to a percentage:

0.0095 * 100% = 0.95%

Therefore, about 0.95% of the bolts will be destroyed by the company.
