22. Club sandwich is it hot or cold​


Answer

A Clubhouse sandwich or Club sandwich is best served warm. However, it can also be served hot or cold depending on the type filling between the bread.

Explanation

A basic Club sandwich is just like a BLT (Bacon, Lettuce, and Tomato) sandwich but with an additional ingredient of ham, poultry meat like chicken or turkey, and/or fried bacon accompanied with a mayonnaise dressing. It is commonly served into four sliced portions with each piece being held together by cocktail sticks or toothpicks (for those cheaper Club Sandwich).

The Clubhouse sandwich has a lot of versions worldwide. Some Club sandwiches have eggs in it that are cooked either sunny side up or scrambled. Sometimes, hot fillings like roasted beef are added which makes it into a hot sandwich. Other versions use cold cuts as fillings which then makes the Clubhouse a cold sandwich
