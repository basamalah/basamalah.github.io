28. DIRECTION: Write the meaning of the following Acronyms. 1. UPS 2. SPS 3. AC 4. amps 5. SP​


Answer:

1. UPS - Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS)

A UPS, or a uninterruptible power supply, is a device used to backup a power supply to prevent devices and systems from power supply problems, such as a power failure or lightning strikes.

2. SPS - Shock Protection System

Circuit to detect if the person is receiving an electric shock and emit wireless signals in case the person is receiving an electric shock.

3. AC - Alternating Current

Alternating current is an electric current which periodically reverses direction and changes its magnitude continuously with time in contrast to direct current which flows only in one direction.

4. AMPS - Automatic Message Processing System

Any organized assembly of resources and methods used to collect, process, and distribute messages largely by automatic means

5. SP - Service Pack

a periodically released update to software from a manufacturer, consisting of requested enhancements and fixes for known bugs.
