15. the sum of Michael and Angelo's age is 40, Michael is 4 years older than Angelo. How old is Michael? Representation:Solution:Conclusion:Checking:​


Answer:

Michael is 22 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Representation:

A(age) = x

M(age) = x + 4

Solution:

x + x + 4 = 40

2x + 4 = 40

2x + 4 - 4 = 40 - 4

2x = 36

2x/2 = 36/2

x = 18

A(age) = 18

M(age) = x + 4

= 18 + 4

= 22

Conclusion:

Michael is 22 years old.

Checking:

x + x + 4 = 40

18 + 18 + 4 = 40

36 + 4 = 40

40 = 40

16. What are the 3 violations of Dictator Ferdinand Marcos. Sr. in the video that was discussed by Atty. Cel Diokno?


Ferdinand Marcos, Sr. Violations

According to Atty. Cel Diokno, the late president Ferdinand Marcos, Sr. committed three violations. These were done during his presidency term from September 11, 1917–September 28, 1989. the longest in the history of presidents in the Philippines.

These three are the following:

3,257 known extrajudicial killings,

35,000 documented tortures,

77 disappearances with 70,000 imprisonment

These took place during the Marcos dictatorship based on the documentation of Amnesty International, Task Force Detainees of the Philippines, and similar human rights monitoring organizations governed and spearheaded by public government officials.

Why were they considered violations?

The number 1 reason authorities monitored these violations is that the late president, who is the current president's father,  violated human rights. Although the government has been doing its best to eliminate or possibly minimize violations of human rights, there are still unheard cases that need to be looked into.
