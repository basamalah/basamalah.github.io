Step 2: Lay the duvet cover on the bed and make sure that the cover’s opening is at the bed’s foot

Step 3: Place the duvet on top of the cover.

Step 4: Clip the two bottom corners of the comforter to the two bottom corners of the duvet cover. You can use safety pins, paper clips, or you can hold the corners tightly.

Step 5: Go to the bed’s head area and begin rolling the duvet towards the foot of the bed. This will make them look like a burrito – the duvet cover as the burrito wrapper and the comforter as the burrito filling.

Step 6: Once you reach the foot of the bed, insert the comforter’s end inside the duvet cover.

Step 7: Unroll the duvet burrito from the foot up to the head of the bed until you have a completely covered duvet.
