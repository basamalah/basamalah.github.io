she is 70 steps away from the gorilla, she moved 5 steps, so the remaining is 65. the gorilla make 4 steps( since 2 steps of gorilla is equivalent to 3 steps of the girl, meaning the gorilla take 6 steps nearer to the girl) we subtract 6 from 65, so the girl is 59 steps away from the gorilla.

17. Karlo is driving his car at 40 m/s when he suddenly saw a dog running on the road ahead. He stepped on the brakes and comes to a stop in 4. 0 seconds. What was the acceleration of the car?.


ACCELERATION

[tex]__________________________[/tex]

Given:(Vi) Initial Velocity = 40 m/s(Vf) Final Velocity = 0 m/sTime = 4.0 sAnswer & Solution:

[tex]\sf \large A = \frac{vf \ - \ vi}{time} [/tex]

[tex]\sf \large A = \frac{0 \: m/s \ - \ 40 \ m/s}{4.0 \: s} [/tex]

[tex]\sf \large A = \frac{- \ 40 \ m/s}{4.0 \: s} [/tex]

[tex]\sf \large A = \boxed{ \sf -10 \: m/s^{2} }[/tex]

↬ Hence, the acceleration of the car is -10 m/s^2
