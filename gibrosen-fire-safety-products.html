4. do's and don'ts of fire safety


Answer:

Fire Safety Don’ts:

1. Don’t try to extinguish by yourself and then call 911. Call 911 immediately. Fires double in size every minute.

2. If the fire is too large, evacuate. If you have practiced your escape plans everyone will make it out safely.

3. Don’t re-enter your home once you have evacuated.

4. Never leave space heaters unattended or left on in the room where you are sleeping.

Fire Safety Dos:

1. Stay below the smoke. The chemical makeup of smoke does serious damage to your brain and lungs.

2. Tell arriving firefighters the location of the fire or trapped family members. This will drastically increase the odds of the fire going out quickly and drastically shorten the time for the firefighters to find your family member.

3. Close the door to the room where the fire is located. This will confine the fire to a smaller area.

4. Know how to safely operate a fire extinguisher.

Use and store flammables away from ignition sources, such as cigarettes.

Keep equipment in good working order.

Report and repair all gas leaks immediately.
