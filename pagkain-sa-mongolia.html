"Ang sistema ng edukasyon ng Mongolia ay binubuo ng nursery, kindergarten, primaryang paaralan, sekundaryong paaralan, at unibersidad pag-aaral. Bawat halagang (administrative distrito ng Mongolia) ay may higit sa isang nursery school at kindergarten. May mga pribadong nursery at kindergarten na paaralan din. 25. Ano ang klima sa mongolia


Mongolia is high, cold, and dry. It has an extreme continental climate with long, cold winters and short summers, during which most precipitation falls. The country averages 257 cloudless days a year, and it is usually at the center of a region of high atmospheric pressure.
