Lesson 12: MAT WEAVING ( pp. 174-175) ➢A mat, banig in Tagalog, is used for sleeping or sitting on the floor. ➢Banig or brown mat is a traditional hand-woven mat. ➢ Banig weaving is a genuine treasure handed down as a tradition or a trade from one generation to another as it is widely practiced in the country. ❖In Basey, Samar in the Visayas, weaving has become one of the major source of livelihood. ❖People of Basey make fine woven mats which are made of tikog leaves, a special reed grass which grow in swampy areas along fields.
