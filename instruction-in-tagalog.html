1. tagalog of remedial instruction


Ang remedial instruction ay isang programa na ang layunin ay i-target ang mga pangunahing kasanayan tulad ng pagbasa, pagsulat, at mathematical skills upang itaas ang kakayahan ng mag-aaral sa pamantayang inaasahan sa kanilang edad.

Ito din ay ginagamit para sa mga mag-aaral na nahuhuli sa kanilang pag-aaral o hindi maganda ang performance sa klase. Ang pangunahing batayan nito ay matulungan ang mga mag-aaral na makahabol sa kanilang mga kaklase at kaedaran, nang sa gayon ay maiwasan ang patuloy na mga isyu sa pag-aaral ng estudyante.

Mga uri ng remedial instructions:

small group tutoring - ang guro ay gumugol ng panahon upang turuan ang maliit na grupo ng mga mag-aaral

one to one tutoring - ang guro ay nakapokus lamang ang pagtuturo sa isang mag-aaral

private tutoring - magbabayad para sa pribadong serbisyo ng guro o tutor

specialist tutoring - mga trained specialist ang nagtuturo sa mag-aaral

volunteer tutoring - ang mga nagtuturo sa mga mag-aaral ay nagbulontaryo magbigay ng kanilang serbisyo

peer tutoring - kapwa mag-aaral ang nagtuturo sa kaklase

computer assisted interventions - ang mga aralin ay tinatalakay sa tulong ng kompyuter

18. Both Tagalog and English were used as the medium of instruction in all levels of education in public schools. Select one: a. True b. False


English and Tagalog

The answer is TRUE, both the Tagalog and English were and are used as a medium in all levels of education present in public schools.

In the presence of different areas of subject of learning in public schools, the use of the Filipino and the English language are defined as limited by a certain aspect. For the use of Filipino language for instructions and teaching to children, it is mostly used in relatable subjects that will mostly tackle the country.

In subjects such as:

Physical Education Social Studies Health

The English language is mostly used in subjects that were based mostly from the learning or teachings also applicable to the other western countries such as:

Math Science

These two require an understanding of the English language since it is mostly written and described in that field of language. Both the Filipino and English used in instructions in public schools actually benefit the students that are learning it. As it widens there an area of learning and understanding by teaching and helping them overcome their own limitations as public school students.

Though the issue between the use of the English language in most of the subjects in schools; in order to comply with the fast growth of globalization or expanding our connection with the foreign countries. It gives way to the opening of new courses served for students such as other language training like Korean and Chinese.

By this change or upgrade in schools, our own Filipino language gets less and less priority to the growth of the young generation. Which shows how much we give consideration of the other countries’ set qualifications to us Filipino people.

This solution can indeed increase our international employment rate as we almost become one with foreign countries. But our language is already influenced enough to lose its major identity that we shouldn’t give less attention to it, it is already part of our culture and our very lives as a citizen.
