3.If 560 out of ten thousand students in a school are boys, then write decimal form for a part of the school for boys?

Answer:
Courtesy : @daintyexlibris
The equivalent decimal for a part of the school that consist of girls is 0.944.To solve this word problem, here are two options that you may use. Both options will arrive at 0.944 as the answer.Option no. 1:Total number of students in school = 10,000 studentsNumber of boys in school = 560 studentsTherefore, the number of girls in school = 9,440 students (10,000 minus 560)To solve for the decimal equivalent for the number of girls in school = 9,440 divided by 10,000 = 0.944Option no. 2:Total number of students in school = 10,000 studentsNumber of boys in school = 560 studentsThe decimal equivalent for the number of boys in school = 0.056 (560 divided by 10,000)To solve for the decimal equivalent for the number of girls in school = 1.00 minus 0.056 = 0.944(We used 1.00 because it is the decimal equivalent of 100%, wherein total number of boys in school plus total number of girls in school should be equal to 100%.)
