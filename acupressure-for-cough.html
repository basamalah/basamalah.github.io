23. difference between acupressure and reflexology (yung maayos sana na sagot, salamat)​


Answer:

Reflexology uses reflexes that are in an orderly arrangement resembling a shape of the human body on the feet, hands, and outer ears. Acupressure uses over 800 reflex points that are found along long thin energy lines called meridians that run the length of the entire body.
