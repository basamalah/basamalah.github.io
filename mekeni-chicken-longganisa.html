18. what tools of longganisa​


Answer:

Longganisa are usually fresh or smoked sausages, typically made with varying ratios of lean meat and fat, along with garlic, black pepper, salt (usually coarse sea salt), saltpeter, muscovado or brown sugar, and vinegar. Variants may add paprika, chili, anise liqueur, and other spices.

Prepare longganisa without any pork fat or reduce the amount used based on your own preferences. Typically, the ratio suggested is 4:1 lean ground meat to fat, or even as much as 2:1. If you do reduce the amount of pork fat used in the recipe, then you may want to also adjust the amount of spices.

29. Make a justification of your food by explaining how come you say that the "component" of the foods is or containing Lipids, Protein and Carbohydrates".Foods:Shrimpschicken Prito/ Chicken w/Pineapple egglongganisaTinulang IsdaCooked RiceGrilled Fishpa help po​


Answer:

1. Shrimps: Shrimps are known to be high in protein and also contain a good amount of lipids. They are an excellent source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are beneficial for various body functions.

2. Chicken Prito/ Chicken w/Pineapple: Chicken is a great source of protein and it contains a small amount of lipids. Pineapple is a great source of carbohydrates and vitamins.

3. Egg: Egg is an excellent source of both protein and lipids. The yolk is high in fat and cholesterol, while the egg white is mostly protein.

4. Longganisa: Longganisa is a Filipino sausage that is made from ground pork meat. It is high in protein and lipids and contains carbohydrates from the seasoning used in making the sausage.

5. Tinulang Isda: This Filipino fish soup is high in protein and lipids. The soup also contains carbohydrates from the vegetables used, such as tomatoes and onions.

6. Cooked Rice: Rice is a rich source of carbohydrates. It is a staple food in many cultures and provides a good source of energy.

7. Grilled fish: Fish is a great source of protein and essential fatty acids. Grilled fish also contains a small amount of fat, which adds flavor to the dish.
