2. The Summer Olympics happen every four years. Suppose the first ever Summer Olympics

was held last 2012, in which year will the 14th Summer Olympics occur?

Is the sequence arithmetic or geometric?

Arithmetic Sequence

What are the given?

a1 = 2012

d = 4

a14 = ?

Formula:

an = a1 + (n-1) d

Solution:

an = a1 + (n - 1) d

a14 = 2012 + (14 - 1) 4

a14 = 2012 + (13) 4

a14 = 2012 + 52

a14 = 2064

Final Answer:

The 14th Summer Olympics will occur at the year of 2064.
