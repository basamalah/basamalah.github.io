It was usually told how long each person takes to do a job with a similar quantity of work, and you are asked how long it will take the two of them to complete the job when they work together.

Work ProblemIt was written in fraction formUse algebraic expressionThe equation usually done as 1/x+1/y=1/t

The expression 1/x+1/y=1/t, wherex is the time consumed by first person to complete the joby is the time consumed by the second person to complete the jobt is the time consumed when they work together to complete the job

It will done by applying your knowledge in fraction, basic math concepts and other mathematical principles.

In the given problem, the secretary can complete the task in 15 mins. and the assistant secretary can do in 32 mins. The equation will be.

[tex] \frac{1}{15} + \frac{1}{32} = \frac{1}{t} [/tex]

Just Simplify

[tex] 480t(\frac{1}{15} + \frac{1}{32} ) = ( \frac{1}{t} )480t[/tex]

[tex]32t + 15t = 480[/tex]

The best model can describe the situation is, the job can complete if they work together is equal to the equation 32t + 15t = 480.
