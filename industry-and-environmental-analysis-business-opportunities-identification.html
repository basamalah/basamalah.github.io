6. Activity 1: SWOT Analysis: Identify the possible Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats in your proposed business​


SWOT ANALYSIS OF MANGO RUSH PRODUCT

Strengths-  Unique flavor, healthy ingredients, convenient packaging

Weaknesses- Limited appeal, competition, seasonal availability.

Opportunities- Expanding product line, Targeted marketing, Partnerships

Threats - Economic factors, Health concerns, Regulatory changes.

Strengths of Mango RushUnique Flavor- Mango Rush has a distinct flavor that is not found in other beverages, making it stand out in the market.Healthy Ingredients- Mango Rush is a nutritious fruit, and it may be able to leverage on its health benefits in order to appeal to health-conscious consumers.Convenient Packaging- Mango Rush may be packed in a convenient manner, such as a bottle or can.

Weaknesses of Mango Rush

Limited Appeal-  Because not all consumers appreciate the taste of mango, Mango Rush's potential market may be limited.Competition-   Mango rush may encounter competition from different flavored beverages, both fruit-based and non-fruit-based.Seasonal Availability- Mango is a seasonal fruit, so Mango Rush may only be available during certain times of the year.

Opportunities

Expanding product line - Mango Rush's product range could eventually grow to include other fruit-based beverages, such as pineapple or strawberry.Targeted Marketing-  Mango Rush may be marketed to health-conscious consumers as well as those who want to explore new and unusual flavors.Partnerships-  Mango Rush might potentially collaborate with other firms or events to improve brand exposure and revenue, such as music festivals or health wellness expos.

Threats

Economic factors -  Economic factors like as inflation and changes in consumer buying patterns may have an impact on mango rush demand.Health concern- If there are health concerns or negative press regarding mangoes or fruit-based beverages in general, Mango Rush sales may suffer.Regulatory changes- Changes in regulations or rules affecting beverage production or sales could also pose a threat to mango rush.
