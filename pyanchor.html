14. Pancho and Bruce were asked to fill the tank with water and they just finished it in 30 minutes. Pancho can fill the tank in x minutes alone, while bruce is thrice as fast as pancho? ​


Step-by-step explanation:

Tap X can fill a tank in 10 minutes and tap Y can fill it in 15 minutes. How long will it take to fill the tank if both taps are on?

----

Tap X rate: 1/10 job/minute

--

Tap Y rate: 1/15 job/minute

---

Together rate: 1/x job/min

----

Equation:

rate + rate = together rate

1/10 + 1/15 = 1/x

Multiply thru by 30x to get:

3x + 2x = 30

5x = 30

x = 6 minutes (time to fill the tank together)

30. What anchors the spindle?


Answer:

The centriole is an organelle responsible for anchoring the spindle fibers :))

Explanation:

The centriole is an organelle that forms during interphase (growth period) and is that anchor for the spindle fibers and helps shorten the spindle during anaphase to pull apart chromosomes and move them to the opposite ends of the cell.
