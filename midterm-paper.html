2 C₂H₆ + 7 O₂ → 4 CO₂ + 6 H₂O

Step 2: Calculate the volume of CO₂ produced by using dimensional analysis.

Based on the balanced chemical equation, 2 moles of C₂H₆ is stoichiometrically equivalent to 4 moles of CO₂. Note that the molar mass of C₂H₆ is 30.07 g/mol and there are 22.414 L of gas in 1 mole of gas.

Step-by-step explanation:

So, 40% of the midterm less =40 over 100 x 80 is equals to 32
