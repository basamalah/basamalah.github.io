26. why is rescuing important?​


Answer:

Rescuing is important because it saves lives and helps people in times of danger or emergency. When someone is in distress or danger, rescuing them can mean the difference between life and death. It is a humanitarian act that shows compassion and empathy towards others. Rescuing can also help prevent further harm or damage to property or the environment. It is a crucial part of emergency response and disaster management, and it requires specialized skills and training to be done effectively and safely. Overall, rescuing is important because it helps protect and preserve human life, and it reflects our shared responsibility to care for one another in times of need.
