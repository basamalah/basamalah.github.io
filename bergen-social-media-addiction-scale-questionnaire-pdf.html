28. Social media addiction research introduction


The social media has greatly influenced every individual in terms of their socialization and behavior.  However, its usage has led to social media addiction among its users especially the teenagers to young adults.

Social media addiction is a behavioral crisis that refers to the over usage of social media.  This can be linked to other related gaming disorders.  The social media addiction becomes detrimental when a person spends more hours using the social media and neglecting his or her every day productivity like academic performance, work output and production, and decline in interpersonal relationships.

A high percentage of people consider the social media as a useful tool for socialization, however real time relationships among people have changed into virtual relationships which lost the essence of connection and relationship itself.
