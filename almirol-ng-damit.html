12. Almirol is added to water for the final rinse of blankets and pants to make them stiff upon drying. What type of mixture is almirol?


Answer:

It is likely that almirol is a type of colloidal mixture when added to water for the final rinse of blankets and pants. Colloids are mixtures in which one substance is dispersed evenly throughout another, resulting in a mixture that is intermediate between a solution and a suspension. In the case of almirol, the almirol particles are dispersed evenly throughout the water, giving the mixture a uniform appearance and allowing it to coat the blankets and pants evenly. When the water evaporates, the almirol particles remain on the surface of the fabric, giving it a stiff texture.
