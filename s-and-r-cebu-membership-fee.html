18. A gym membership charges a $50 activation fee and a $17 monthly fee. If you spend $356, how many months did you have your membership? A 18 months B 22 months C 24 months D 20 months


PROBLEM:

A gym membership charges a 50 dollars activation fee and a 17 dollars monthly fee. If you spend 356 dollars, how many months did you have your membership?

A 18 months

B 22 months

C 24 months

D 20 months

SOLUTION:

My first step is to take away the amount of activation fee from the amount you spend.

[tex]356 - 50 = 306[/tex]

Then, divide it by 17 since we're looking for the number of months if the monthly fee is 17 dollars.

[tex]306 ÷ 17 = 18[/tex] months

ANSWER:

The answer is 18 months. Letter A.
