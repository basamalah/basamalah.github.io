6. Why did you choose criminology as your profession and for your career in near future


I choose Criminology as my future profession and career. It is because this course is a gateway to be accepted as a law enforcer.  Criminology means the study of crime. Criminology students learn the causes of crime related to Science, Psychology, or social factors. Incident crimes will be analyzed through strong research skills.

Areas of Criminology

These are the major areas of Criminology:

Sociology of Crimes and EthicsLaw Enforcement AdministrationCrime Detection and InvestigationCriminalisticsCriminal Law and JurisprudenceCorrectional Administration

Career Path

Graduates of BS in Criminology may pursue a career path as:  

Criminology InstructorLaw EnforcerForensic PhotographerCourt Peace OfficerDiscipline OfficerPrivate Investigator
