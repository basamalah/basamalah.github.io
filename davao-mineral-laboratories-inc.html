Yes, because it still has a definite chemical composition.c. No, because it is made up of carbon atomsd. No, because it is not made through a natural process.​


A synthetic diamond is prepared in a laboratory by

placing carbon under high pressure. This is not laboratory-made diamond a mineral, because it is not made through a natural process.

About Synthetic Diamond

It is undeniable, this precious stone has become one of the aesthetic commodities of today's humans. Unfortunately, the diamond process itself takes a very long time. Hundreds, even millions of years are needed to "cook" mineral carbon in the bowels of the earth.

Humans speed up the process by creating a suitable laboratory or environment. The result, is a diamond that is very similar to a natural one, difficult to distinguish with the naked eye.

The process of making synthetic diamonds is entirely in human hands. You can get diamonds to order, ranging from size, color, shape, even the level of radiance.

To the naked eye, their luster and even shape are indistinguishable from natural diamonds. No one can tell the difference. Even gemologists need a lot of time and a sophisticated set of tools to detect which diamonds are natural and which are not.

Both natural and synthetic diamonds have certificates indicating their status, size, model, color, weight, including where the diamond was made or mined. One of the laboratories that issued this diamond is G.I.A (Gemological Institute of America).
