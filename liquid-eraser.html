9. Jin had 50% fewer erasers than Dwayne. After Dwayne gave 15 of his erasers to Jin, Jin had 40% fewer erasers than Dwayne. How many erasers did Jin have at first?​


Answer:

Jin had 50% erasers than Dwayne. So:

Jin's erasers = 50% of Dwayne's erasers.

Dwayne gave 15 erasers to jin, then he had 40% erasers. So:

Jin's erasers - 15 = 40% of Dwayne's erasers.

Now let jin's erasers be x and Dwayne's erasers be y. Therefore we can write the above statements as:

x = 50% × y

=> x = 0.5y ___(1)

x + 15 = 40% × y

=> x - 15 = 0.4y

=> x = 0.4y + 15.___(2)

From (1) and (2):

0.5y = 0.4y + 15.

0.1y = + 15.

y = 150.

Now, x = 50% × y

= 50% × 150

= 75.

Eraser tool changes pixels to either the background color or to transparent, Magic eraser tool is an eraser tool that erases an area that is similar in color to the clicked pixel, while background eraser tool erases the background while maintaining the edges of an object in the foreground.
