Answer:

This text is a short story in the genre of folk or fairy tale. The features include the use of a traditional storytelling style, including the use of phrases like "Once upon a time" and "And she says to her daughter." The structure of the text is simple and follows a linear narrative, with a clear beginning, middle, and end. The story is also characterized by its use of a moral lesson, in this case, the moral lesson is about the importance of being patient and not being too greedy.
