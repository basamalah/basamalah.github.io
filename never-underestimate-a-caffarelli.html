12. Why does the gdp deflator underestimate inflation g?


Answer:

The GDP deflator underestimates true inflation. The reason is that the GDP deflator reflects the prices of all goods and services produced domestically consumers, whereas the CPI reflects the prices of all goods and services bought by domestic consumer.
