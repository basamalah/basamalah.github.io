3. Which description shows the best balance of physical, social, and mental/emotional health? A. April has low self-esteem because of her weight and is working on making more friends. B. Barbara plays hockey, eats well, and has frequent conflicts with her teammates. C. Marta has a large circle of friends and eats primarily junk food and chocolate. D. Terrence exercises for 30 minutes a day, eats well, and enjoys new challenges.


WHAT IS BEING ASKED?

In the given descriptions, choose the one that shows balance of physical, social, mental or emotional health.

ANSWER

The correct answer is OPTION D - Terrence exercises for 30 minutes a day, eats well, and enjoys new challenges.

Option A is not the correct answer because April has low self-esteem, therefore, there is no balance between the emotional health and social health.

Option B is not also the correct answer for having frequent conflicts with teammates is showing unhealthy social aspect. There is no balance in the physical health and social health.

Option C is not also the correct answer because there is no balance in the physical and social health shown in the circumstance that Marta has a lot of friends however eats junk foods and chocolate.

Option D is the correct answer there is a balance in the physical and mental/emotional health. This is evident in Terrence's act of doing exercise, eats well and eager and excited to new challenges.
