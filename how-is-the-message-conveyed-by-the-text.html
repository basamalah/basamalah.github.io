1. How is the message conveyed by the text


HOW IT CONVEYED BY THE TEXT:

The communication conveyed by writing or typing. Here we can convey with good emotions and impressions.

• Sending it like communicate with friends and family

• We can convey it with emotion and tone through text

• We can make a communication with a good impression

HOW IT CONVEYED BY THE VIDEO:

Message on video communication are data communications that conveyed us to send and receive information in the form of images.

• The information communication in the video is in the form of continuous pieces of frames.

• Video communication is only in the form of images (visual), there is no sound data.

• Sending information in the form of images (visuals) with people in different locations.

• It is also more practical and can save time and money.

EXPLANATION:

1. How it conveyed by the text:

• Sending communication is like texting friends and family

One of the reasons people send a text is that they want to communicate with other people. So how do you make your business sound human? It's best to send text quickly, fun and expressively - just like you would with friends and family.

• You can convey emotion and tone via text

Appropriate grammar is needed to help others understand what we want to convey. Using the right language to convey emotions and empathy to others is important. Our brains process images, especially facial expressions, much faster than words. Using emoticons can help you convey more meaning in fewer words, and do it more clearly.

• Create a text with a good impression

When you have a conversation with someone, you don't need to include the same details every time. Remember, even if you're sending a text from a computer, a text's signature isn't the same as an email signature. The text needs to be written succinctly and make a good impression on others.

2. How the communication conveyed by the video:

• Video communication is data communication that allows you to send and receive information in the form of images.

• So, the information message is in the form of continuous pieces of frames, so that when combined they form a moving image or video. Oh yes, video communication is only in the form of images (visuals), there is no voice data. Examples of video communication media include CCTV, outdoor videotron, and so on.

• Now, with video communication, we can also communicate or send information in the form of images (visuals) with people from different locations. So, it is more practical and can save time and costs.
