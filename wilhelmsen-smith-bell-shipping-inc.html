3. How important the master molders and the bell smiths are in the story of the soul of the great bell​


Answer:

Very Important

Explanation:

In the story of "The Soul of the Great Bell," the master molders and bell smiths are crucial characters. They are responsible for creating the great bell, which is a symbol of the city and its people. The process of forging the bell is difficult and dangerous, and the master molders and bell smiths must use their skills and expertise to craft it. Their work represents the collective effort and ingenuity of the city, and the success or failure of the bell will have a profound impact on the people. As such, the master molders and bell smiths are essential to the story and its themes.
