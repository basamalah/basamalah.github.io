14. what would 3 | 4 mean as the key on a stem and leaf plot bruhhh help me


Answer:

In a stem and leaf plot, the numbers to the left of the vertical line are the "stems," and the numbers to the right of the vertical line are the "leaves."

If "3 | 4" appears as a key on a stem and leaf plot, it means that the numbers in the dataset that have a "3" in the tens place will be represented by the stem "3," and the numbers with a "4" in the ones place will be represented by the leaf "4."

For example, if the dataset contained the numbers 23, 34, 37, 43, 48, and 59, the stem and leaf plot would look like:

2 | 3

3 | 4 7

4 | 3 8

5 | 9

In this case, "3 | 4" means that the number 34 will be represented in the plot by a "4" leaf on the "3" stem.

One way in which religion and philosophy differ is that religion often involves a set of beliefs that are based on faith and are accepted without question, while philosophy is centered on the use of reason and critical thinking to explore and understand the world. However, there are also some similarities between religion and philosophy.
