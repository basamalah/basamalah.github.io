"Work Immersion" is one of the Department of Education's programs for "Senior High School" students. This is one of their requirements before they graduate. This aims to give them knowledge about the different types of jobs they want to enter.

It requires (80) eighty hours to perform the actual work or can also just imitate the actual work. Can't exceed (8) eight hours per day in performing the task. Students are guided by the school and company employees as they work.

All work assigned to students is tailored based on the chosen “Strand” of the students.

Although, there is no attached salary from the company or the school, there are still many positive views about the program because the students can use their knowledge based on the specialty they have studied. 5. permission in memorandum of agreement​


Answer:

A memorandum of agreement is a kind of cooperative agreement that is intended to document the understanding of certain parties (two or more) related to their cooperation on a project or in the achievement of an objective. Unlike a memorandum of understanding, a memorandum of agreement is more likely to impose certain obligations on the parties.

7. What is the purpose of partnership agreement?​


Answer:

The features of a partnership agreement include setting forth the names of the individual partners that form the business; stating the purpose for which the partnership is established and the principal place of business; outlining the amount of money each partner invests in the enterprise; and establishing directives about the distribution of profits between the partner.


8. Is a Memorandum of Agreement a contract? Why? Why not?​


Answer:

Similar to a contract, a memorandum of understanding is an agreement between two or more parties. Unlike a contract, however, an MOU need not contain legally enforceable promises. While the parties to a contract must intend to create a legally binding agreement, the parties to an MOU may intend otherwise.

In order to prepare students for all curriculum exits, work immersion offers Senior High School students the chance to familiarize themselves with the workplace, simulate employment, and apply their abilities in areas of specialization or applied topics in an actual work setting.

In-school Home-Based

One of the ADMs provided by the Department of Education is homeschooling (DepEd). It gives students access to formal education while allowing them to continue learning outside of the classroom. As learning facilitators, authorized parents, guardians, or tutors assume the position of the teachers.

Community-Based

Community-based services are those that are delivered to individuals, groups, or families in their homes or other places of habitation while using the community as the primary client system.

Facilities for daycare centers are provided.Regular Supply of School SuppliesAdditional Feeding.ICT Education Initiative.Assistance in disaster relief and medical missions.

School Industry Partnership

Academe-industry (A-I) relationships that would guarantee fresh knowledge and innovation from academia are those that are considered "valuable." They are shared with businesses, and their combined efforts result in tangible advantages.
