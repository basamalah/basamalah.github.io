4. there are 12 bundles of pencils, each bundle has equal number of pencil, if there are a total of 180 pencils, how many pencils are in each bundle​


Answer:

there are 15 pencils each bundle

Explanation:

180/12=15

Answer:

So to get the number of Pencils (N) we use bundles (B) and total pencils (P).

Use the formula : Number of pencils = Total number of pencils divided by 12 bundles.

N = P ÷ B

N = ?

B = 12

P = 180

N = 180 ÷ 12

N = 15 is the number of pencils in each bundle.

To prove, we count the pencils = number of pencils in each bundles multiply by the amount of bundles to get the total pencil

N x B = P

15 × 12 = 180

8. There are 784 popsicle sticks, bundled in groups of 100. how many bundles are there?​


To find the number of bundles, we need to divide the total number of popsicle sticks by the number of sticks in each bundle.

The total number of popsicle sticks is 784.

The number of sticks in each bundle is 100.

To find the number of bundles, we need to divide 784 by 100:

784 ÷ 100 = 7.84

Since we can't have a fraction of a bundle, we round up to the nearest whole number, which is 8.

Therefore, there are 8 bundles of 100 popsicle sticks each.

15. Group bundling is a type of bundling developed in Japan.​


Answer:

In marketing, product bundling is offering several products or services for sale as one combined product or service package. It is a common feature in many imperfectly competitive product and service markets.[1] Industries engaged in the practice include telecommunications services, financial services, health care, information, and consumer electronics. A software bundle might include a word processor, spreadsheet, and presentation program into a single office suite. The cable television industry often bundles many TV and movie channels into a single tier or package. The fast food industry combines separate food items into a "meal deal" or "value meal".

A bundle of products may be called a package deal, in recorded music or video games, a compilation or box set, or in publishing, an anthology.

26. Joy fullness in tagalog​

Ito ay maaring maipahayag sa pamamagitan ng tawa, ngiti, at positibong enerhiya. Sa kultura ng mga Pilipino, ang kagalakan ay madalas na kaugnay ng mga pagdiriwang, pagtitipon, at pakikipag-ugnayan sa mga kaibigan at pamilya. Ito ay isang pinahahalagahang kalidad na pinaniniwalaang nagdadala ng magandang kapalaran at positibong enerhiya sa buhay ng isang tao.
