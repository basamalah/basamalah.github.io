12. Sonny had 13 dozens of doughnuts. He sold 9 12 dozens of doughnuts. How many dozens were not sold? with solution pls


Answer:

4 dozens were not sold.

Step-by-step explanation:

13 x 12 = 156 (Sonny had 156 pieces of doughnuts)

9 x 12 = 108 (Sonny sold 108 pieces of doughnuts)

156 - 108 = 48 (Sonny did not sell 48 pieces of doughnuts)

48 ÷ 12 = 4 (Sonny did not sell 4 dozens of doughnuts)
