14. kkk, what is kkk clan​


Answer:

KKK or the Kataastaasan, Kagalanggalangang Katipunan - was a Philippine revolutionary society founded by anti-Spanish colonialism Filipinos in Manila in 1892; its primary goal was to gain independence from Spain through a revolution.

Founded: July 7, 1892, Recto Avenue, Manila

Main organ: Kalayaan (dated January 1896, published March 1896)

Official language: Tagalog, regional languages

Supreme President (Kataas-taasang Pangulo, Presidente Supremo): Deodato Arellano (1892–1893); Román Basa (1893–1895); Andrés Bonifacio (1895–1897)

Legal status: Defunct

Founders: Andrés Bonifacio, Deodato Arellano, Ladislao Diwa, Teodoro Plata, José Dizon, Valentín Díaz

Answer:
