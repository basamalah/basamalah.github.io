16. Why was john the baptist killed?​


Answer:

John the Baptist was imprisoned after saying that King Herod's marriage with Herodias was against God's law because Herodias was previously married to King Herod's brother, Phillip. At a party for King Herod, Herodias's daughter pleased everyone with her performance, so King Herod promised to give her anything she wanted. Herodias urged her daughter to ask for the head of John the Baptist on a tray because she was annoyed by John the Baptist. So King Herod ordered to behead John the Baptist.

29. Why jesus is different from our baptist and his baptist?


Answer:

Like other Christian denominations, Baptists believe that Jesus and God are the same; they are distinct, and yet, make up the same three-part deity known as the Trinity. While God, Jesus and the Holy Spirit make up the Trinity, Baptists believe all three are the same deity, just different representations of it.
