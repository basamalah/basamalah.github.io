7. An arch over an entrance is semi-circle with an opening that is 5 feet wide write an equation that models the arch


Answer:

If the opening is 5 feet wide, I assume you mean from the center point of the straight edge to the top of the arch. Thus the radius of the arch is 5 feet. Since it is on top, I assume the arch points upward and so the equation is y = √(25-x2)

This equation can be found from the equation of a circle (x-h)2+(y-k)2=r2. The point (h,k) is the center of the circle (here we defined the center to be (0,0)) and r=5 so r2=25. Thus x2+y2=25. Then you solve for y but only use the positive answer since we're talking about the top semicircle.

VoIP or Voice Over IP is the one that allows people to make voice calls through the internet. It is the transmission of voice and other contents like multimedia (videos and pictures) over IP or internet protocol networks. Some VoIP allows people to call others using the same technology (VoIP) while others allow people to call someone who is using a telephone number, whether it is mobile, long distance or even long distance number.

Problem:A parabolic arch has a height of 16m and a width of 28m at the base. If the vertex of the parabola is at the top of the arch, how wide is the arch at the point 11m above its base?

​

Solution:

x² = -4ay

(x, y)

(14, -16)

14² = -4a(-16)

196 = 64a

196/64 = a

a = 3.0625

x² = -4ay

x² = -4(3.0625)y

x² = -12.25y

x² = -12.25(-5)

x² = 61.25

x = √61.25

x = 7.826 m

Width of arch at the pt 11m above the base:

W = 7.826(2)

W = 15.652 m

To go to the top of the Arch, there’s a tram in each leg of the arch. Each tram is a chain of eight egg-shaped compartments, each capable of holding five passengers.
