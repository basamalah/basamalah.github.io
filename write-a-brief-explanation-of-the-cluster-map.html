TEXT - Text is any item that can be read, whether this object is a piece of literature, a street sign, an arrangement of buildings on a city block, or a type of clothing. This is a cohesive collection of signs that communicate an informativeness message of some sort. This set of signs is shown not by its physical appearance or the medium in which it is depicted but by the content of the information message. STILL IMAGES -Photography, drawing, painting. A still image is a single static image, as distinct from a moving image (i.e. a movie). This term is used in photography, visual media and the computer industry to emphasize that it is not a matter of film, or of very precise or pedantic technical writing, such as normal. AUDIO -Audio more commonly refers to sound as it is transmitted in the form of a signal.

ANIMATION - Animation is a process by which figures are manipulated to act as moving images. In conventional animation, pictures are drawn or painted by hand on transparent celluloid sheets to be shot and shown on film.

INTERACTIVITY - In computers, interactivity is a conversation between a human being (or probably another living creature) and a computer program. (Programs that run without immediate user interaction are not interactive; they are generally called batch or background programs.)

VIDEO FOOTAGE - The footage is raw, unedited material as originally shot by a film camera or captured by a (often special) video camera, which must usually be edited to make a motion picture, a video clip, a television show or similar finished work.

12. Question: What is Cluster map?


Answer:

Clustering or mapping can help you become aware of different ways to think about a subject. It is a brainstorming device that helps organize your ideas into manageable parts. It also allows you to see, at a glance, the aspects of the subject about which you have the most to say, so it can help you choose how to focus a broad subject for writing.
