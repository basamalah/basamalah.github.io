3. make a request letter to your baranggay asking for permission to conduct on outreach program​


[Your Name]

[Your Address]

[City, Province]

[Date]

[Barangay Captain's Name]

[Barangay Hall Address]

[City, Province]

Dear Barangay Captain [Last Name],

Warm greetings to you and the rest of the barangay council! I am writing this letter to request for permission to conduct an outreach program in our barangay.

Our group, [Name of organization], is composed of concerned citizens who aim to help the less fortunate members of our community. We have planned an outreach program that will provide assistance to the indigent families in our barangay. The program includes the distribution of basic necessities such as food, clothing, and school supplies, as well as the provision of free medical and dental services.

We would like to seek your approval to conduct the outreach program in [Name of venue or location] on [Date and Time of the outreach program]. We will ensure that the program will not disrupt the peace and order in the barangay, and we will coordinate with the barangay officials and security personnel to maintain order and safety during the event.

We are aware of the current situation with the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, and we will strictly adhere to the health and safety protocols mandated by the national and local government. We will implement social distancing measures, provide face masks and face shields to our volunteers and beneficiaries, and conduct regular sanitation and disinfection procedures during the program.

We hope that our proposal will merit your approval, and we look forward to working with the barangay council in providing assistance to our less fortunate fellowmen. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

[Name of Organization]

[Your Name]

[Your Address]

[City, Province]

[Postal Code]

[Date]

(Barangay Captain Name]

[Barangay Address]

Dear Barangay Captain,

I am writing to request for your permission to conduct an outreach program in our community. Our group aims to help those in need, particularly the less fortunate individuals.

We assure you that we will abide by the rules and regulations of the barangay, and will ensure that the program will not cause any harm or disturbance to the community.

We hope for your favorable response regarding this matter.

Thank you for your time and attention.
