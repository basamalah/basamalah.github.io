10. what is the history of pundaquit? San Antonio Zambales ​


Answer:

Pundaquit is a barangay in San Antonio, Zambales, Philippines. It has a history dating back to pre-colonial times when it was inhabited by indigenous Aeta tribes. During the Spanish colonial period, Pundaquit fell under Spanish rule, and later saw logging as a major industry in the late 19th to early 20th centuries. After gaining independence, Pundaquit experienced development and modernization, becoming known for its beaches, landscapes, and tourism. Today, Pundaquit is a popular tourist destination known for its natural beauty, with fishing, agriculture, and tourism as its main livelihood sources.
