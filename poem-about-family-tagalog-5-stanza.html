Filipino culture, so rich and so vast

A history filled with struggles and triumphs from the past

A people who are known for their warmth and their heart

Their love for family and togetherness, a bond that will never part

Where love and support are always in abundance and rife

Generations coming together, sharing stories and food

Creating memories and traditions that will forever be imbued

The love for fiestas and celebrations, oh so grand

Bringing the community together, hand in hand

Dancing and singing, laughter and cheer

The strength and resilience of the Filipino people

Their determination and hard work, a trait that is ingrained

Filipino culture and family, forever intertwined.

28. four stanza poem about filipino family​


Answer:

The Family That Was...

Filipino Style

I come home from school and do my homework

Before dark I watch my mom prepare for supper

Wait for the other sibling to get home from work

Around six one by one they come home to gather

Lively we all sit together around the feast table

Talking laughing and tell stories while we eat

Vote which game we would play after dinner

Scrabble or Chess but Bingo wins as the favorite

I always start out with the most number of coins

The youngest of six I am usually the slowest to call

I never worry because my father will be home soon

He always empties his pocket and put it in my bowl

Sometimes the girls would fix dessert or evening snack

Eating in front of the TV together we stay up and watch

And when the basketball is on I would cry the whole time

Not because the soap is on but I like and enjoy the attention
