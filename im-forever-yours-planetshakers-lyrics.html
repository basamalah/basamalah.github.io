8. Is there really such as forever? explain your answer.​


Answer:

Well, not everybody has an explanation for this. But if I were to answer i believe there is no such thing as forever. I'm not saying this because I'm being bitter but I'm just stating the fact.

Explanation:

pa brainliest po.

Explanation:

Today, we hear it from lot of people saying, "Forever is a myth", "Nobody stays Forever".

It has become a general perspective, that everything changes.

Does anyone stop and think why, when this word 'Forever' stike our mind, the only thing we think is that it is impossible that anything or anyone stays till the end?

Lets stop and think for a while, do we actually put that efforts to make things last forever. Don't we just give up when things go a bit wrong or something is not as per our wish and will.

Lets take an example. We become friends with someone.We find that person perfect. We keep on talking entire day, hang out every weekend, have all the best memories we can. Then all of sudden, we feel, that this person is not the way we thought that person to be. We start seeing faults in the person. Slowly and gradually the image of the person that we had, starts to perish and we keep on blaming the person, you are like this and you are like that.

So what is the problem here? Is the person not right? Did we make mistake in choosing the person? Or is there a problem with the mindset that we have?

The first mistake that we make is creating an image of the person, with whom we havent stayed or talked or even dont know, as per their outer appearance or their social behaviour. If somebody talks with everyone in a good and polite way, we assume that person should be like that always. If somebody is rude and arrogant, we assume the person to be like that.

Second mistake that we make is spending too much time with the person or giving so many efforts that we sometimes forget our own importance. We do things that we don't wish to just to make that person happy or to start a friendship. Not being ourselves from the first, ultimately creates a false image.

Then eventually we realize that the efforts are going in vain as we start realizing that the person is not the person we thought. We don't realize, that nobody is perfect and nobody can just be same like us. Humans were created differently and every human has their own flaws.

The fourth mistake that we make is closing out a friendship or relation, just when we realize, things are not as per our thinking. Our ego becomes more important than the person whom we thought would stay forever. Instead of talking to the person, we start talking about the person.

And last but not the list, instead of realizing our own mistake, we keep on blaming the person.Whenever there is an argument, instead of listening, we keep on fighting and do not even try to listen to what the other person has to say. We don't put the same effort, that we actually did, to start the friendship or relationship.

To summarize, it has become very much essential in today's world that we accept every human just the way they are. Accepting the flaws of anyone and learning to listen to what the other person has to say, is the key to Forever.

Let's stop blaming and pointing faults in other people, take a breathe for a while and start the friendship all our again.

Let's not small argument and misunderstanding, end the years of friendship or relationship.

Believe in Forever again and instead of expecting others to stay Forever, "Make your own effort to be the Forever".
