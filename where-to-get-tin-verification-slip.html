Let x be the amount of 10% alloy to be added to alloy 1.

Weight of final alloy must be x+20.

0.1(x)+20(0.15)=0.12(20+x)

0.1x+3=2.4+0.12x

0.1x-0.12x=2.4-3

-0.02x=-0.6

x=-0.6/-0.02

x=30 lbs
