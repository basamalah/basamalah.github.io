9. A homozygous white horse is crossed with homozygous red horse and resulted to an offspring with roan phenotype. Which of the following pattern of inheritance is exhibited? a. Incomplete Dominance b. Segregation c. Codominance d. Sex related inheritance​


Question

A homozygous white horse is crossed with homozygous red horse and resulted to an offspring with roan phenotype. Which of the following pattern of inheritance is exhibited?

A. Incomplete Dominance

B. Segregation

C. Codominance

D. Sex related inheritance​

Answer

C. Codominance

In shorthorn cattle, when a homozygous white horse (WW) is crossed with a  homozygous red horse (RR), all the offspring are roan-a spotted, white and red or red milky color. When a homozygous red horse is bred with a homozygous white horse? The white and red hairs in the coat of the offspring that is made by homozygous red horse and a homozygous white horse is an example of codominance.

CONDOMINACE:

Codominance means that neither allele can mask the expression of the other allele. An example in humans would be the ABO blood group, where alleles A and alleles B are both expressed. So if an individual inherits allele A from their mother and allele B from their father, they have blood type AB.

"Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron" is an animated movie released in 2002, produced by DreamWorks Animation and directed by Kelly Asbury and Lorna Cook. The film tells the story of a wild mustang named Spirit, who lives in the American West during the late 19th century.

Spirit is a strong and independent horse, who is fiercely protective of his herd and territory. He is captured by a group of American soldiers, who are trying to tame and break the wild horses to use them for transportation and farming. Spirit refuses to be broken and repeatedly tries to escape, but is eventually tamed by a Native American boy named Little Creek.

Together, Spirit and Little Creek embark on a journey to find a way for Spirit to return to his herd and freedom. Along the way, they face many challenges, including dangerous predators, harsh weather conditions, and encounters with the American soldiers. They also form a deep bond and friendship that transcends their differences.

The film is unique in that it features very little dialogue, instead relying on the narration of Spirit's thoughts and emotions. The soundtrack includes songs by Bryan Adams, who also co-wrote and performed the movie's theme song, "Here I Am."

Overall, "Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron" is a beautifully animated film with a powerful message about freedom, loyalty, and the importance of preserving the natural world.
