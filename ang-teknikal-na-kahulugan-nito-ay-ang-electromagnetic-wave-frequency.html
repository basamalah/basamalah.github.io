14. As frequency increase, the speed of an electromagnetic wave is? ​


Answer:

As frequency increase, the speed of an electromagnetic wave is also increasing.

Answer:

speed of light

Explanation:

The speed of a wave is often calculated as the product of frequency and wavelength. This gives many students the wrong impression that the speed of a wave depends upon frequency and wavelength. But don't be fooled! The speed of a wave is independent of its frequency and wavelength. A change in the frequency value will not result in a change in the speed value. Rather, changing frequency will cause a change in the wavelength in such a manner that the speed value turns out the same. While the speed can be calculated from knowledge of the frequency and wavelength, its value is not affected by changes in frequency and wavelength.
