thus length of 1 side of the cube  =   ∛8 = 2
Area of 1 surface = 2* 2= 4sq in
therefore total surface area = 4* 6 = 24sq ins    /  6  surfaces

Sub: Expressing concern over students’ craze for using Internet as a homework tool
