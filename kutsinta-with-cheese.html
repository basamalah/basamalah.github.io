½ cup all-purpose flour.
1 cup brown sugar.
3 cups water.
1½ tsp lye water.
2 tsp anatto seeds.

24 hours(60mins/1hour) = 1,440 minutes

Mhel eaten cheese burger = 1,440/15
Mhel eaten cheese burger = 96 cheeseburgers

Mhel will eat 96 cheese burgers during the entire contest.
