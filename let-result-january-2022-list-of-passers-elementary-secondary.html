12. 1.Similarities between elementary and secondary2.similarities between elementary and tertiary3.similarities between secondary and tertiarypakit sagot Po pls lang Po :(​


Answer:

Similarities

Both have allocated lunch hours in which you can have a cooked lunch from the school itself, or a packed lunch from home

Both have a sports facility, whether indoors or outdoors

Both have assemblies in which you are given school news

Both usually have musical and sporting extracurricular activities

Both separate students on ability

Both work towards a final set of government standard exams

Differences

Secondary schools tend to larger, further apart, and with more classrooms

Secondary schools often have assemblies within year groups, whereas Primary Schools are just by Key Stage or the entire school

Secondary schools don’t tend to organise events like discos or bake sales, it is the students who suggest them and essentially do all the work , and the school just gives them permission.

Secondary schools focus more on independent learning, and there is more emphasis on homework, whereas many primary schools don’t even give homework.

Secondary school students tend to have more of a developed relationship with their teachers, either good or bad.

During breaks or lunch(some schools, like mine, do not have breaks, just a fourty minute lunch time), secondary school students don’t play in the same way as you do in Primary School. Instead students either just talk, join a club, or play sports formally.

Secondary schools expect you to take notes whilst the teacher is talking, whereas in primary school you do far more vocational learning

Despite the greater amount of clubs available to join in secondary school, students tend to join less than they did in primary school.

Sports are far more serious, and you are expected to be better at them, in secondary school

Secondary schools tend to separate abilities into different classes (in my school this is twelve ‘sets’ per year group), but in primary school you remain in the same class and just sit in different tables.
