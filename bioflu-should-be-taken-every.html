13. Is it okay to take bioflu and biogesic at the same time?


Answer:

No, it is not ok.

Explanation:

Biogesic and Bioflu are both brands of paracetamol. Paracetamol is a common over the counter drug used to lower the body temperature when fever kicks in. It also provides relief to other common symptoms of fever like headache and body pain.

Despite being the same brand of paracetamol, physicians are advising the public not to take two different brands at the same time. The reason behind this is that these “medicines” could unleash a deadly combination of side effects that can damage the liver permanently. If left unchecked, it can also be fatal.
