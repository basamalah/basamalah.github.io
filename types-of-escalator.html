13. An escalator has an angle of elevation of 10 degrees and a vertical rise of 6 m. find the length of the escalator.


Answer:

The length of escalator is 35 meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Vertical rise of elevator: 6 meters  (opposite of the angle)Angle of elevation: 10°Length of escalator: x  (hypotenuse)The length and vertical rise of elevator and the distance between its foot and base of vertical rise along the ground form a right triangle.

Use trigonometric functions to solve for x, the length of elevator:

Sin θ = opposite/hypotenuse

Find the length of elevator, x:

Sin 10° = 6 meters/x

x = (6 meters) / (Sin 10°)

x = (6 meters) / (0.1736)

x ≈ 34.56 meters

x ≈ 35 meters

The length of escalator is 35 meters.

29. What is Escalating Assertion?​


Answer:

Escalating Assertion. Occurs when the other person fails to respond to your basic assertions, and you become increasingly firm. May include the mention of resulting action on your part. For example, "If you don't complete the work on my car by 5:00 tomorrow, I will call the Better Business Bureau."
