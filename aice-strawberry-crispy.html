19. if three more strawberries are added to plate a how many strawberries must be added to plate b so that the ratio of the number of strawberries in plate a to the number of strawberries in plate b remains the same as before? plate a:2 strawberriesplateb:8 strawberries​


Answer:

12

Step-by-step explanation:

If plate A has 2 strawberries and plate B has 8, then the ratio is 2:8. Adding 3 strawberries to plate A would make 5.

2 is 1/4 of 8. So, 5 has to be 1/4 of Plate B. 5x4 is equal to 20.

The new ratio is 5:20. Though, that is not the final answer.

If it is asking for how many strawberries is needed so the ratio will be the same, and the ratio is 1/4 to 1, then that means we subtract 8 from 20 which will equal to 12. Hence, the answer is 12.
