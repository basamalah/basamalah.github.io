Explanation:

The Meccan surahs are, according to the timing and contextual background of supposed revelation (asbāb al-nuzūl), the chronologically earlier chapters (suwar, singular sūrah) of the Qur'an. The traditional chronological order attributed to Ibn Abbas became widely accepted following its adoption by the 1924 Egyptian standard edition.[1] The Meccan chapters are believed to have been revealed anytime before the migration of the Islamic prophet Muhammed and his followers from Mecca to Medina (Hijra). The Medinan surahs are those supposed revelations that occurred after the move.

Meccan surahs are typically shorter than Medinan surahs, with relatively short verses (āyāt), and mostly come near the end of the Qur'an. (As a general rule, the chapters of the Qur'an are ordered from longest to shortest.) Most of the chapters containing Muqatta'at are Meccan.[2]

The division of chapters into 'Meccan' and 'Medinan' is primarily a consequence of stylistic and thematic considerations. Classification of the chapters into these periods is based upon factors such as the length of the verse and the presence or absence of certain key concepts or words (e.g. al-Rahman as name of God).[3]

17. what is meaning of kwentong ada


Answer:

Fairy Tales

Explanation:

"Kwentong Ada" is the Filipinism for fairy tales. Fairy tales are imaginative stories for children that typically features imaginary beings and lands.

Some of the more known fairy tales or kwentong ada in the Philippines are the following:

The Creation Story - where the first man and woman emerged out of a bamboo. This is also the origin of the phrase "putok sa buho".The Flood Story - where Lumawig's sons caused the flooding of the Earth. How the Tingguian Learned to Plant - where Kadaklan sent Kaboniyan to teach Dayapan how to plant rice.

Filipino fairy tales differ from western fairy tales as most of our stories are unique to our own culture and varied depending on which region they are from. There are religious overtones as well since ancient Philippines was deeply religions and devout to worshiping various deities.

20. ayah can paint a house in 3 hours Princess can do it in 4 hours. How long will it take the two working together?​


Answer:

1.71 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

It's a work problem. You can set up this way.  

Ayah's rate+Princess' rate=total rate where rate=1 job over hours

         1/3     +       1/4           =          1/x

Find "x" and start the problem by multiplying both sides  by the LCD (12x).

sooo...

Let the rate that Ayah can paint the room = 1/3.

Let the rate that Princess can paint the room = 1/4.

Let X equal the unknown, which is how long will it take if we work together

 

The equation is therefore:

         1/3*X +1/4*X = 1

         Use common denomination of 3*4=12

 

(4X + 3X)  =  1  This implies that 7X/12 = 1. Solving for X you get X=12/7 = 1.71 hours is the answer.
