16. advantage and disadvantage of pawnshop​


Answer:

The biggest advantage of pawn shops is that there is no need to wait for a very long time to get the funds raised. On the contrary, the money can be raised in a very short period of time which can be just a few minutes.

The most significant drawback of a pawn shop loan is its cost. Interest rates and finance charges for pawn shop loans are often high. It's common to see interest rates between 5% and 25% a month. Another disadvantage is that if you don't repay your loan on time, the pawn shop can sell your item.

27. 3. LJ pawned several pieces of jewelry with Agencia de R. C. Sicam to secure aloan. Armed robbers entered the pawnshop and took away the jewelry of LJand other clients of the pawnshop from the vault which was left open sinceit was a Saturday. There was no showing that a security guard was stationedat the pawnshop. When LJ demanded the return of the jewelry she hadpawned, the pawnshop had failed to do so because it was lost in the robbery,and raised among other defences, non-liability due to fortuitous event. Isthe defense of the pawnshop acceptable?​


Answer:

No, simply because ROBBERY PER SE IS NOT A FORTUITOUS EVENT.

Explanation:

Robbery is a foreseeable event, and thus the pawnshop should've exercised due care in handling their jewelries. The fact that the vault was kept open and there was no security guard on the premises proved the negligence on the part of the pawnshop.

To be free from liability for reason of fortuitous event, the pawnshop must, in addition to the case itself, be free from any concurrent or contributory fault or negligence.
