1. sample letter of salary deduction due to negligence


Date

MEMO

FROM: Human Resources  Department

TO: (name of employee)

RE: Salary deduction due to negligence

This is to notify you of the negligence you have incurred last week which resulted to damage to properties and the lost resources of the company.

In line with this, we are hereby deducting from your salary, with the amount of Php 5,000.00, as computed in all the damages done.  

We will be deducting through an installment basis for it to not be a burden to you.  The deduction schedule will be observed as follows:

July 15 – P1,000.00

July 31 – P1,000.00

August 14 – P1,000.00

August 31 – P1,000.00

September 15 – P1,000.00

This is for your reference.

Thank you.
