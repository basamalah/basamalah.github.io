27. what is the importance of communication and global globalization in bachelor of arts in english language? ​


What is globalization? what is the impact of globalization on communication?

Globalization is a result of using a good solution for a problem all over the world in the same way. As a result of massive trade flows, and quick traveling, telephone-, internet- and email exchanges, all or most people in the world CAN talk and exchange ideas and work together in solving problems.

The different cultures are growing to look like each other. The great pro is that our horizon of everyday work can be enormous large and flexible. A manufacturer in a village in Germany can use ideas from America, tools from China, marketing in europe and mass production for parts in any country in the world. So good ideas grow very quick, and disperse over the globe.

The con however is that if many things are done better and quicker and different than in traditional ways of working and doing, that traditional way of working will get smaller and smaller. And so will the traditional way of thinking and acting.

People who are not as fast in changing, or are not part of the change, will feel threatened and alienated by these trends.

So the big challenge is: how to have the fruits of global thinking and preserving valuable traditions?

I don’t know the answer, I just try to describe the inherent contradiction of globalisation aspects.
