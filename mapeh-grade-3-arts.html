21. mapeh arts what i have learned grade 8​


Explanation:

personal academic records or experiences. However, here are some general topics and skills that are typically covered in Grade 8 MAPEH Arts

1.Elements of Art: Students learn about the basic elements of art, such as line, shape, color, texture, form, and value.

2.Principles of Design: Students study the principles of design, including balance, contrast, emphasis, movement, pattern, rhythm, and unity.

3.Art History: Students learn about significant artists and movements in art history, including the Renaissance, Impressionism, and Post-Impressionism.

4.Drawing and Painting: Students learn techniques for drawing and painting, including still life, portrait, and landscape.

5.Sculpture: Students learn about the three-dimensional art form, and create sculptures using materials such as clay, wire, and paper.

6.Music: Students learn about music theory, notation, and history, and develop their singing and instrumental skills.

7.Dance: Students learn various dance styles, such as modern, ballet, and folk, and improve their coordination and body control.

8.Theater: Students learn about the different elements of theater, including acting, stage design, and production, and develop their communication and collaboration skills.

Overall, the MAPEH Arts curriculum aims to foster creativity, critical thinking, and appreciation for different art forms, while also developing fundamental skills in the arts.
