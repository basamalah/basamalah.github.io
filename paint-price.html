6. Two cylindrical cans of paint sell for the same price. One can has a diameter of 8 inches and a height of 12 inches. The other has a diameter of 12 inches and a height of 8 inches. Which can contain more paint, hence the better buy? Explain​


Answer:

The second cylinder can carry more paint.

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula for a volume of a cylinder is:

πr²h.

To get the radius, divide the diameter by 2.

Cylinder 1:

V1 = πr²h

V1 = (3.14)(4in)²(12in)

V1 = 602.88in³

Cylinder 2:

V2 = πr²h

V2 = (3.14)(6in)²(8in)

V2 = 904.32in³

The second cylinder has more volume and hence, can carry more paint.

13. Tanvi teaches art lessons at a day camp and needs to get supplies. She purchases 40 Bright Streak paint sets from the Color Splash art store. She uses a coupon she received in the mail for $10 off her purchase. Tanvi spends $310 in all. Which equation can you use to find p, the original price of each Bright Streak paint set? What was the original price of each Bright Streak paint set?


$8

The equation we can use to find p, the original price of each Bright Streak paint set, is:

p * 40 - $10 = $310

We know that Tanvi purchased 40 paint sets and used a $10 coupon, so the total amount she paid was $310. Rearranging the equation, we get:

p * 40 = $320

Dividing both sides by 40, we get:

p = $8

Therefore, the original price of each Bright Streak paint set was $8.
