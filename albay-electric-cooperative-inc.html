7. Silver the best conductor of all materials, why people choose to use cooper in for electrical wiring​


Although silver is the best conductor among all metals, it is rarely used for electrical wiring because of its high cost. Copper, on the other hand, is cheaper, widely available, and still an excellent conductor, with a conductivity that is only slightly lower than silver. Additionally, copper has better mechanical properties, making it more durable and easier to work with than silver. Copper is also less prone to corrosion compared to silver, which can form an oxide layer that reduces its conductivity over time. Therefore, copper is the preferred choice for electrical wiring due to its combination of affordability, availability, and electrical conductivity.

Answer:
