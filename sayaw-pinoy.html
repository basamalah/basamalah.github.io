16. 2. If you were given a chance to be one of the dance performers in Sayaw Pinoy, what dance would you want to perform and why? ​


If I were to choose a dance to recommend for Sayaw Pinoy, I would suggest the Tinikling dance.

EXPLANATION:

Tinikling is a traditional Philippine folk dance that involves two people rhythmically beating, tapping, and sliding bamboo poles on the ground and against each other in coordination with the music. It requires a high level of skill and coordination, as the dancers must quickly move their feet in and out of the moving bamboo poles without getting hit.

Tinikling is a popular dance in the Philippines, and it represents the country's rich cultural heritage. It is also a fun and exciting dance to watch, and it would be a great addition to Sayaw Pinoy's lineup. Plus, it would showcase the dancers' agility, grace, and precision, making for an engaging and visually stunning performance.

Gamit ang pamaypay o panyo, ang mga mananayaw ay ay mga hakbang ng pagtataguan at panunukso na naghahayag ng damdamin sa isa’t isa. May mga iba pang bersyon ang sayaw na Cariñosa pero ang “Taguan” ang pinakakaraniwan sa lahat.
