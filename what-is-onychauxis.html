1. define of onychauxis ​


Answer:

Onychauxis is a nail disorder that causes fingernails or toenails to grow abnormally thick. ... This thickening of the nail may force the nail plate (the part you paint with nail polish) to separate from the nail bed. Though toenail fungus can cause similar symptoms, onychauxis isn't caused by a fungus.
