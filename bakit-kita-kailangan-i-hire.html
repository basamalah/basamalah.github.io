12. Situation #4Leslie, will you hire her in your company?I will not hire her, she looks weak and inexperienced.Bias or Prejudice?Explanation:​​


Answer:

Prejudice

Explanation:

Base on my observation, we all know that Prejudice talks about "judgement" which is what Leslie did due to fact or reason that the applicant "looks weak and inexperienced"

The speaker has made a judgement about Leslie based on her appearance and perceived level of experience, without considering her qualifications or skills. This type of bias is unfair and can lead to discrimination against individuals who do not fit into certain societal norms or expectations. It is important to evaluate job candidates based on their qualifications, experience, and skills, rather than their appearance or other superficial factors. Failure to do so can result in missed opportunities for the company to hire highly qualified and capable individuals, leading to negative consequences such as decreased productivity and reduced competitiveness.
