1. kahulugan ng ct scan​


Answer:

computerized tomography scan

Answer:

A CT scan, or computed tomography scan, is a medical imaging procedure that uses computer-processed combinations of many X-ray measurements taken from different angles to produce cross-sectional images of specific areas of a scanned object, allowing the user to see inside the object without cutting


2. what is the importance of ct scan?​


Answer:

Why are CT scans important? CT scans are a valuable diagnostic tool. They are able to detect some conditions that conventional X-rays cannot because CT scans can show a 3D view of the section of the body being studied. CT scans are also useful for monitoring a patient's progress during or after treatment.

CT scan is most commonly performed to the human brain - to determine the cause of a stroke, or to assess serious head injuries. Other uses of a CT scan include: To detect abnormalities in the body, such as tumours, abscesses, abnormal blood vessels, etc, when they are suspected by symptoms or other tests.

CT scans can detect bone and joint problems, like complex bone fractures and tumors. If you have a condition like cancer, heart disease, emphysema, or liver masses, CT scans can spot it or help doctors see any changes. They show internal injuries and bleeding, such as those caused by a car accident

20. 2. The total cost of producing and marketing "X" units of a certain commodity is given as: CT = 8000X400x² + x³ 40,000 "X" is the average cost a minimum. ​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the minimum average cost, we need to differentiate the total cost function with respect to X and set it equal to zero. Then, we can solve for X to find the value that minimizes the average cost.

First, we take the derivative of the total cost function with respect to X:

d(CT)/dX = 8000(400)(2x) + 3x² - 0 [Note that the constant term 40,000 differentiates to 0]

Simplifying, we get:

d(CT)/dX = 6,400,000x + 3x²

Next, we set this expression equal to zero to find the critical point(s):

6,400,000x + 3x² = 0

Factor out an x:

x(6,400,000 + 3x) = 0

This gives us two solutions: x = 0 and x = -6,400,000/3. However, x = 0 is not a valid solution since we are looking for a positive average cost. Therefore, we consider only the second solution:

x = -6,400,000/3 ≈ -2,133,333.33

This value of x is negative, which does not make sense in the context of the problem. Therefore, there is no minimum average cost for this function.
