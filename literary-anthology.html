Answer:

Anthology

Anthology is a collection of writings or songs. Anthologies can focus on just one (1) genre-such as apoetry anthology. Or an anthology can include various types of writing grouped around a specific time peeiod or subject. One example would be an anthology of Southern Literature or an anthology of Latin American Literature.

Anthologies typically have an editor instead of a single author. Even when they are focused on the writings of one author, such as an anthology of the works of Shakespeare, an editor often choses the works that are included and arranges yhem in order to be printed.

Examples of an Anthology

A collection of Shakespeare's poetry

A collection of comic strips about the Peanuts Gang

A collection of short stories by writers from the American South

A collection of essays and speeches fom the Civil Rights Movement

We then present a framework for incorporating such declarative knowledge into word problem solving.
