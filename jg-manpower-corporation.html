19. Given:UJ=UG. N is the midpoint of JG prove:UNJ=UNG


➛Question:

Given:UJ=UG.

N is the midpoint of JG prove:UNJ=UNG

Answer:

Given that UJ=UG, we can draw a line segment from U to the midpoint N of JG, dividing the triangle into two smaller triangles:

U

/ \

/ \

/ \

J-------G

\ /

\ /

N

We know that N is the midpoint of JG, so we can write:

NJ = NG/2 (since N is the midpoint of JG)

Similarly, we can write:

UN = UJ/2 (since N is the midpoint of UJ)

We are given that UJ = UG, so we can substitute UG for UJ in the above equation to get:

UN = UG/2

Since NJ = NG/2, we have:

UNJ = UN + NJ = UG/2 + NG/2 = (UG+NG)/2

Similarly, we can show that UNG = UN + NG/2 = (UG+NG)/2

Thus, we have shown that UNJ=UNG, so we have proven that UNJ is congruent to UNG.

24. What stimulates the release of renin from the juxtaglomerular (jg) apparatus?


Answer:

Similar to cardiac tissue, juxtaglomerular cells harbor β1 adrenergic receptors. When stimulated by epinephrine or norepinephrine, these receptors induce the secretion of renin. These cells also respond directly to a decrease in systemic blood pressure which is manifested as a lower renal perfusion pressure.

30. explanation of manpower agencies​


Answer:

A manpower agency, also known as a staffing agency, recruitment agency, or employment agency, is a business that connects job seekers with employers. These agencies act as intermediaries between job seekers and employers, providing a range of recruitment and employment-related services to both parties.

Explanation:

For job seekers, manpower agencies provide a convenient and efficient way to find work. Instead of searching for job openings on their own, job seekers can register with a manpower agency, which will help them find suitable job opportunities based on their skills, qualifications, and experience. Manpower agencies may also offer services such as resume preparation, interview coaching, and job search assistance, which can help job seekers stand out in the competitive job market.
