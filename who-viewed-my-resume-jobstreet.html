29. Among the four types of resume which is the most appropriate for senior high school graduate who wants to apply for job? explain.​


Answer:

The most appropriate type of resume for a senior high school graduate who wants to apply for a job would be a functional resume. Unlike a chronological resume, a functional resume highlights an individual's skills and accomplishments, rather than focusing on their work history. Since a high school graduate may not have an extensive work history, a functional resume allows them to showcase their skills, volunteer work, relevant coursework or projects, leadership roles, and extracurricular activities that could demonstrate their potential to contribute in a job. This type of resume also effectively emphasizes transferable skills, such as problem-solving, critical thinking, communication, and team collaboration, which could be valuable in various industries. Overall, a functional resume can help a high school graduate create a strong first impression on potential employers despite having limited professional experience.
