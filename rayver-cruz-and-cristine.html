24. 1. Rayver can build 4 ⅘ of his robot toy collection for 1 hour. How manyrobots can he build in 1 ⅔ hr?​


In the given problem, you need to apply your knowledge and skills in alegbra and basic math especially in fraction.

Given: 4 robots per hour 1 ⅔ hours

Problem: How many robots can he build?

Solution:

If he can build 4 robots in one hour, thus he already have 4 robots in 1 hour and just find how many robots he can build in ⅔ of hours.

2/3 × 4 = 2.67, so it can build 2 additional robots in 2/3 hours.

Thus, 4 + 2 = 6, he can build 6 robots in 1 ⅔ hours.

Other Solution

In this kind of problem, you can simply multiply the two.

First Convert 1 2/3 in Improper fraction.

1 2/3 → 5/3

5/3 × 4 = 20/3

20/3 = 6.67

Since the question is all about entity, the answer is only 6. Decimal value is not included, and do not round off.

Final Answer: 6 Robots
