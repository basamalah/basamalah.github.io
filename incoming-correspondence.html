Answer:

The one major factor influencing demand is the price of the good or service. When the price of a good or service increases, the demand for it may decrease because consumers are less willing to pay the higher price. On the other hand, when the price of a good or service decreases, the demand for it may increase because it becomes more affordable for consumers. Other factors that can influence demand include income, market size, and the desire to purchase a particular good or service, but the price of the good or service is generally the most important factor.
