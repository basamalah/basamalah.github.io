3. Peter and Eric pitched a tent in a shape of a pyramid. The base of the tent is 3meters wide by 4meters long .the tent had a slanted height of meter.what was the surface area of the tent


Answer:

In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebraic expression and basic mathematics especially in solving word problems about surface area.

Problem

Peter and Eric pitched a tent that had a shape of a pyramid. The base of the tent is 3 meters wide by 4 meters long. The tent was 5 meters high. What was the surface area of the tent?

Given

Base of tent is 3 m by 4 m

h = 5 m

Solution

Surface area of triangle prism is the sum of all area of faces.

Triangular Face 1 = 1/2BH → 1/2(3)(5) = 7.5 m²

Triangular Face 2 = 1/2BH → 1/2(4)(5) = 10 m²

Rectangular Base = LW → (3)(4) = 12 m²

Surface Area = 2(7.5) + 2(10) + 12

Surface Area = 47 m²

THE CORRECT ANSWER IS 47 m²
