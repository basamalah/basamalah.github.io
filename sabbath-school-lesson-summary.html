2. 1. Make you own Sabbath design or plan. Whatever program you make that would bring good purpose and outcome of the way you observed the Sabbath day holy. (this program will be from Vesper's; Sabbath School, Divine; and AYF) 2. After your plan or design, state your reflection of your personal experience of the Sabbath day of rest.​


Answer:

My Sabbath design or plan would be as follows:

Vesper's: I would start my Sabbath day by attending a vesper service at my local church. This would involve singing hymns, reading from the Bible, and listening to a sermon or devotional.

Sabbath School: After the vesper service, I would attend a Sabbath School class. This would involve discussing a specific topic or passage from the Bible in a small group setting.

Divine Service: After Sabbath School, I would attend the Divine Service at my local church. This would involve more singing, reading from the Bible, and listening to a sermon.

AYF: After the Divine Service, I would participate in an Adventurer or Youth group activity. This would involve playing games, doing crafts, and learning more about the Bible.

My personal experience of the Sabbath day of rest was very fulfilling. By taking the time to attend church services and participate in Bible studies, I felt a deeper connection with my faith and my community. The Adventurer or Youth group activities were also a great way to bond with other young people and learn more about the Bible in a fun and interactive way. I felt refreshed and recharged by taking a break from my daily routine and focusing on my spiritual well-being. Overall, observing the Sabbath day in this way helped me to feel more grounded and at peace.

Answer:

C. The Sabbath is the seventh day of the week.

Explanation:

It is written in the Ten Commandments which can be found in Exodus 20:8-11,

"Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy.

Six days shalt thou labour, and do all the work:

But the seventh day is the sabbath of the Lord thy God: in it, thou shalt not do any work, thou, not thy son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates:

For in six days the Lord made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them is, and rested the seventh day: wherefore the Lord blessed the sabbath day, and hallowed it."

The Sabbath is a 4th commandment in the Ten Commandments of God given through Moses in two tables of stones in Mount Sinai during their journey when they left Egypt.

In the Portuguese Bible, we can see what day is the sabbath.

"Lembra-te do dia do sabado, para o santificar." (Exodus 20:8)
