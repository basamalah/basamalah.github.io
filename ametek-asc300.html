----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{QUESTIONS:}[/tex]

1.) Find the surface area of a Trapezium Given:

TOP - 3meters

Height- 4meters

Base- 6meters

-----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{FORMULA:}[/tex]

[tex]A = \frac{1}{2} (a + b)h[/tex]

-----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{ANSWER:}[/tex] 21 m2 or sq.m.

-----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

[tex]A = \frac{1}{2} (a+ b)h[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{1}{2} (3 + 4) \times 6\\ =\frac{1}{2} (7)× 6\\ =\frac{7}{2}×6 \\=3.5×6 \\ =21[/tex]

-----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{EXPLANATION:}[/tex]

a → top

b → height

h → base

-----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{QUESTIONS:}[/tex]

2.) Find the surface area of a rectangle Given:

width - 7 meters

length - 7 meters

-----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{FORMULA:}[/tex]

[tex]A= L × W [/tex]

-----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{ANSWER:}[/tex] 49 m2 or sq.m.

-----------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \pink{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

[tex]A = length × width [/tex]

[tex]= 7 × 7 [/tex]

[tex] = 49 [/tex]
