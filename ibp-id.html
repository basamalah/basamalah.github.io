29. The ide of triangle are 3,6 and 8. If the larget ide of a imilar triangle i 16 what i the hortet ide?.


Problem:

The sides of a triangle are 3,6 and 8. If the largest side of a similar triangle is 16, what is the shortest side?

3:6:8 = x:y:16

3/x = 8/16

8x = 3(16)

x = 6

6/y = 8/16

8y = 6(16)

y = 12

The shortest side is 6 (units).
