23. 15 POINTS PPL!! 8th grade math! Lines L and M are parallel. Line T is transversal. What is the measure of angle 3 if m<9 = 110°?


Answer:

The measure of <3 is 70°

Because <3 = <8 and <4 = <9

Answer:

m∠3 = 70°

Step-by-step explanation:

∠3 and ∠9 are supplementary. The total measure of their angles is 180°.

Proof:  Alternate exterior-interior angles are supplementary.

Equation:

m∠3 + m∠9 = 180

Find m∠3 when m∠9 = 110°:

m∠3 = 180° - 110°

m∠3 = 70°
