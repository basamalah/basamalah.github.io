2. is it important and relevant to give a paper and pencil test to student? is a is it still necessary in modern times?​


IS IT IMPORTANT AND RELEVANT TO GIVE A PAPER AND PENCIL TEST TO A STUDENT? IS IT STILL NECESSARY IN MODERN TIMES?

Technically, we are on our journey in modern times but not everybody is able to walk at the same pace as we are.  I think and I still agree that giving a paper and pencil test to a student is still important, especially to the students, in remote areas, that are not able to cope and flow in these gradual changes and progress. Having a paper and pencil test would be just like any normal day of examination where the students' powers and knowledge flow through their papers and pencils. It is their way to pour and devour poverty and continue to dream high. Paper and pencil test is still necessary because it still gives hope that everything would still get normal just like how it seemed two years ago, where we could still meet our friends at school, write our emotions and dreams, and go running while having a fun time. Discouraging or stopping paper and pencil tests would make everything, but a beautiful memory.
