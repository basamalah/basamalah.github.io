28. why tabular form of presenting data more advantageous than textual form?​


Answer:

Readers tend to get bored reading the textual presentation of data.

Explanation:

Since Textual Presentation of Data presents information in a paragraph or sentence form (this includes enumeration of important characteristics and emphasizing the most significant features of the set of data), readers tend to get bored reading such texts. While on the other hand, in Tabular Presentation of Data, datas and informations were arranged into tables. It is presented brief and concise. One thing that readers likes the most in tabular form is that different classes and the number of cases which fall into each class were given on sight.
