18. What is tenor in Music?


Answer:

Tenor, highest male vocal range, normally extending approximately from the second B below middle C to the G above; an extremely high voice, extending into the alto range, is usually termed a countertenor (q.v.). In instrument families, tenor refers to the instrument of more or less comparable range (e.g., tenor horn).

25. Middle male voice,lies between bass and tenor tenor voices.it is the common male voice.​


Answer:

baritone

A baritone is a type of classical male singing voice whose vocal range lies between the bass and the tenor voice types, overlapping both of them. It is the most common male voice. The typical baritone range is from A2 (the second A below middle C) to A4 (the A above middle C).
