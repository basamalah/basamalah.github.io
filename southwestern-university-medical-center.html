13. It is a protected area that represents a habitat to biodiversity conservation which can be found in the southwestern part of the Philippine archipelago on the midwestern coast of Palawan.​


Answer:

Tubbataha Reef Natural Park

It is a protected area that represents a habitat to biodiversity conservation which can be found in the southwestern part of the Philippine archipelago on the midwestern coast of Palawan.

You are describing the Tubbataha Reef Natural Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in the Sulu Sea, Philippines. It is known for its diverse marine life, including over 600 species of fish and 360 species of coral. It is a popular destination for diving and snorkeling.

Tubbataha Reef Natural Park is a protected area of about 97,030 hectares, it is situated in the middle of the Sulu Sea, about 150 miles southeast of the Palawan mainland. The park is made up of two coral atolls, the North Atoll and the South Atoll, which are separated by a deep channel. It is also home to many marine species such as sharks, whales, dolphins, turtles, and many other species of fish and birds. The park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is considered one of the most important marine environments in the world. It is also a popular destination for diving and snorkeling, as the coral reefs and marine life are considered some of the most diverse and beautiful in the world. However, the access to the park is limited to only a few months a year and only a limited number of visitors are allowed to visit the park each year to minimize impact on the ecosystem.

24. an animist ethnic group inhabiting highland areas in southwestern mindanao​


Answer:

T'boli

Explanation:

The T'boli (Tagabili to lowlanders) are an animist ethnic group inhabiting highland areas in southwestern Mindanao, centering on Lake Sebu (TauSebu is another of the people's names).
