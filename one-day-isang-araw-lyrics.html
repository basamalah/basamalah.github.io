7. what will happen in iron nail in vinegar for 1day, 2,days, 3days,4days and 5 days​


Answer:

The nail will be corroded. Whether the nail will be visibly damaged will depend on the concentration of the vinegar and the specific alloy of the nail, and so for the most part the nail will not be affected, but some of the nail’s iron will form ferrous acetate.

Explanation:

Vinegar contains acetic acid. In contact with iron ferrous acetate is formed. At room temperature in a dilute solution of acetic acid (vinegar) the reaction proceeds very slowly.

Explanation:

 Based on plannedparenthood.org , The 1st day of your menstrual cycle starts on the 1st day of your period (AKA menstruation). TAON, BUWAN, ARAW, ORAS, SEGUNDO

Isang buwan=  30  days or 31 days

Isang taon= 12 months

Isang taon =  365 days or a leap year of 366 days

Isang araw= 24 hours

Isang minuto = 60 seconds

Isang oras = 60 minutes

• A year is the orbital period of the Earth moving in its orbit around the Sun.  The abbreviations y and yr are commonly used for year.  

• The Gregorian calendar, or modern calendar, presents its calendar year to be either a common year of 365 days or a leap year of 366 days, as do the Julian calendars.
