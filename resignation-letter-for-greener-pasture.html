7. Making a resignation letter


In writing a resignation letter state clearly that you now have the intention of leaving the company and also provide your last day of employment so that the transition process can be started immediately.

Include a message of thanks to maintain a positive relationship with your superiors and have a graceful exit.

An offer of help during the transition period will also be appreciated and shows a sign of goodwill on your part.

Refrain from writing any complaints or critiques in your resignation letter because that is not the right medium for complaints and critiques.

16. why is it important to make resignation letters than just to leave the job​


Answer:

Writing a resignation letter is a polite and proper way to cut your ties with the company. A resignation letter serves as a notice for your company to let them know that you are leaving. Providing enough notice gives them enough time to prepare and look for your replacement. This is very convenient, especially if the one resigning is an employee with a significant position or role in the company or organization.

22. What is resignation letter


A resignation letter is a formal letter that expresses an employee’s intent to leave a position or the office that he is currently working with.  It states the reason of leaving of the employee, and his gratitude towards his employers for giving him an opportunity to work in their company.

Usually, a resignation will take its effectivity after one month of rendering the intent.  This is to provide an ample time for the employee to turnover his liabilities and workloads, and to process his outgoing documents such as his clearance.  In addition, this will serve as a period of hiring a new employee for the employer to have someone take over the expected job vacancy in the office.

23. paano gumawa ng resignation letter​


Ang isang resignation letter ay isang sulat na nagpapahayag ng iyong intensyon na magbitiw sa iyong trabaho. Narito ang ilang mga hakbang upang gumawa ng isang resignation letter:

Magsimula ng isang bagong dokumento at bigyan ito ng isang formal na header, katulad ng "Resignation Letter" o "Letter of Resignation."Magtakda ng isang petsa na kung saan ilalabas mo ang iyong resignation letter. Ito ay dapat na sa loob ng ilang araw mula ngayon.Sa unang parirala, pasalamatan ang iyong employer sa mga oportunidad at karanasan na ibinigay nila sa iyo.Sa pangalawang parirala, ilahad ang iyong intensyon na magbitiw sa trabaho. Siguraduhin na ilagay ang petsa ng iyong huling araw ng trabaho.Sa huling parirala, magbigay ng isang maikling paliwanag kung bakit mo pinagdesisyunan na magbitiw. Hindi mo kailangang magbigay ng detalyadong paliwanag, ngunit maaaring magbigay ng isang simpleng dahilan kung gusto mo.Sa huli, pasalamatan ang iyong employer muli at magbigay ng iyong mga contact details kung kinakailangan.Sa huli, ilagay ang iyong pirma sa ibaba ng sulat at ilagay ang iyong pangalan sa ibaba ng iyong pirma.
