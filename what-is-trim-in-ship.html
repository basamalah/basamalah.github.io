10. the ralation of draft and trim of the ship​


Answer:

The value of trim depends on how the vessel is loaded. If the aft of the vessel is heavier, then the draft at aft will be higher, and the vessel is said to 'trim by aft'. Similarly, if the weight of the forward is higher, it 'trims by forward'.
