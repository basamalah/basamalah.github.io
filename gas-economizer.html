3. what is the connection between the economics branch of social science in Gas strand?​


Answer:

The connection between economics as a branch of social science and the GAS strand is that economics is one of the key disciplines studied within the social sciences. As a subject, economics is concerned with understanding how individuals, organizations, and governments allocate resources to satisfy their needs and wants in a world of scarcity. It also seeks to understand how markets and economic systems function, and how policies and decisions made by individuals and institutions can impact the economy.

In the GAS strand, students are exposed to a broad range of academic disciplines, including the social sciences, which encompasses economics. Studying economics as part of the GAS strand provides students with an understanding of how economic systems function, the impact of economic policies on society, and the role of individuals, firms, and governments in the economy.

The study of economics also helps students develop critical thinking skills, such as analyzing data, making informed decisions, and evaluating the consequences of different economic policies. These skills are essential in various fields, including business, law, public policy, and social sciences.

Explanation:

In summary, the connection between economics as a branch of social science and the GAS strand is that the study of economics within the GAS strand provides students with an understanding of how economic systems work, develops critical thinking skills, and prepares them for various academic and professional fields that require an understanding of economic concepts and principles.
