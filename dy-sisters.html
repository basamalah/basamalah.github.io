11. what is a dying declaration?​


Answer:

It constituted the last words of a dying person. Dying declaration is based on the maxim “Nemo moriturus praesumitur mentire” It means a man will not meet his maker with a lie in his mouth. The statements made by a person as to the cause of his death or as to circumstances of the transaction resulting in his death is called a dying declaration.
