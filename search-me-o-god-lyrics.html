Example lyrics:

1. "One day you will wake up and find that you are missing me"

- The Script (The man who can't be moved)

• The cohesive device used in the lyric is the word 'and'. The word is used to add or combine.

2. "Because baby I could build a castle out of all the bricks they threw at me"

- Taylor Swift (New Romantics)

• The cohesive device used by Taylor Swift is the word 'because'. She used the word to tell a certain result.

3. "Sometimes in my tears I drown but I never let it get me down"

- Matisyahu (One Day)

• The cohesive device used in the lyric of the song One day is the word 'but' The word is used to qualify.

What are cohesive devices?

- Cohesive devices refer to specific words that combine and link clauses and ideas to create smooth sentences. These words help guide readers to clearly understand written or published works.

Conjunctions, substitutions, and lexical items are all types of cohesive devices.
