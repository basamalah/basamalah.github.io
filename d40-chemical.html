21 ÷ 2 = 10.5

Now, dividing 21 by 3 we get,

21 ÷ 3 = 7

Again, dividing 7 by 3 will give a fraction number. Therefore, taking the next prime number, 5, into consideration.

21 ÷ 5 = 4.2, again a fraction number.

         

So the next prime number is 7.

7 ÷ 7 = 1
