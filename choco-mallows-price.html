2. Cost of a bag of choco: 70.00

Cost of a bag of milk: 50.00

3. Joey's candies: 22 pieces

Rico's candies: 9 pieces

Step-by-step explanation:

2. Let x = bag of choco, and;

bag of milk = (x - 20), then;

bag of choco:

x + 2(x-20) = 170

x + 2x - 40 = 170

3x = 170 + 40

3x = 210

x = 210/3

x = 70

bag of milk:

= (x-20)

= 70 - 20

= 50

Checking:

x + 2(x-20) = 170

70 + 2(70-20) = 170

70 + 2(50) = 170

70 + 100 = 170

170 = 170

3. Let x = Rico's candies, and;

(2x+4) = Joey's candies, then;

Rico's candies:

x + (2x+4) = 31

3x = 31 - 4

3x = 27

x = 27/3

x = 9

Joey's candies:

= (2x+4)

= 2*9 + 4

= 18 + 4

= 22

Checking:

x + (2x+4) = 31

9 + (2*9 + 4) = 31

9 + (18 + 4) = 31

9 + 22 = 31

31 = 31
