19. Keddy bought a computer at a discount of 20%.After discount, the price is $980.What is the usual price of the computer?


Answer:

$1,225

Step-by-step explanation:

20% discount is applied, so Keddy only pays for 80% of the original price.

80% = 0.8

0.8X = 980; where X is the usual price of the computer.

Divide both sides by 0.8

[tex]\frac{0.8x}{0.8} = \frac{980}{0.8}[/tex]

0.8 can be canceled since 0.8 divided by 0.8 equals to 1.

X = 980/0.8

X = $1,225

Therefore, the usual price of the computer is $1,225.
