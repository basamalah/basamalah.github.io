S= 2 km/h

D= 5 km

Required:

T=?

Solution:

Formula: T = D/R

T = D/R

= 5 km / 2km/h

= 5/2 h or 2 1/2 h

S= 8 mi/h

T= 45min.

Convert 45mins to h

45mins × (1h/60min)

D=?

Solution:

D = R × T

= 8mi/h × 0.75 h

The average velocity of the horse is 5.28 m/s.

Velocity

The velocity is the rate of change of displacement with time.

Average velocity

The average velocity of an object is the ratio of total distance to total time of motion.

v = (38 + 0.5(38))/(9 + 1.8)

v = 57/10.8

v = 5.28m / s

Thus, the average velocity of the horse is 5.28 m/s.

"The Dog and His Reflection"

Attached is a picture of the story being told.

One day, a dog came across a piece of meat, and as he was strolling near a stream carrying it in his jaws, he noticed another dog in the water carrying another piece of meat. The fact that it was his own reflection and not another canine eluded him.

He extended his lips to catch the other meat because he was dissatisfied with his own, but at that very moment, his meat dropped into the water and was carried away until he could no longer see it. Because of his greediness, he therefore continued to be hungry that day.

However, he didn't stop to think before dropping his bone and lunging at the dog in the river, which resulted in him having to swim for his life to get to the shore. He eventually succeeded in scrambling out, and as he stood there mournfully reflecting on the good bone he had lost, he understood what a not genius dog he had been. Being greedy is a really foolish trait.

The Dog and His Reflection's lesson is that avarice can result in expensive unwise behavior. A dog in the tale is returning home with a bone.

30. a horse is trotting along at a constant speed of 8 miles per hour.how many miles will it travel in 45 minutes? pls,anwer..​


Answer:

6 miles per 45 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

1 mph  / 4 = .25 miles per 15 minutes

We can just multiply this formula by 8 since it is 8 mph

8 mph / 4 = 2 miles per 15 minutes

but the question asks for 45 minutes, not 15, so we can multiply our answer by 3 since 45 / 15 = 3 (15 x 3 = 45)

2 miles per 15  x  3  = 6 miles per 45 minutes

(or just find 3/4 of 8 since 45 is 3/4 of 60)
