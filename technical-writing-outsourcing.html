6. Differentiate technical writing from non technical writing and give examples


Technical Writing :

Technical writing is a piece of writing which focuses on factual and straight forward content and technical papers are published to inform and instruct and educate the user about some specific topic. There exists specific readers who prefers technical papers. It gives readers information about some technical topics or it gives directions on how to do something.

For example writing any articles on GeeksforGeeks related to computer science field comes under technical writing.

General Writing :

General writing refers to any piece of writing which focuses in general subjects of writing and general papers are published for amusement of the reader. But sometimes many general topics also gives life lessons, moral, inspiration etc. There is not such specific set of audience or readers like technical writing. It is written on subjective tone and purely personal style.

For example writing any prose or story which will be published in a local magazine comes under general writing

10. what is the impact of outsourcing disciplines on employees who are being outsourced and employees who may stay​


Answer:The impact of outsourcing on employees who are being outsourced can be significant, as they may lose their jobs or be required to transition to a new role with a different employer. This can lead to feelings of insecurity, stress, and uncertainty about the future. On the other hand, for employees who may stay, outsourcing can also have a negative impact as they may feel apprehensive about the stability of their own jobs and the future of the company. However, outsourcing can also have positive impacts, such as cost savings and improved efficiency for the company, which can potentially lead to job growth and career opportunities in the long term. Ultimately, the impact of outsourcing will depend on various factors, such as the specific industry, the nature of the work being outsourced, and the strategies employed by the company to mitigate the effects on its employees.
