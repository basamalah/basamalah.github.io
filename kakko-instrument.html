Explanation:

Instruments that produce sound by vibrating themselves.

2 - Membranophones:

Instruments that produce sound by a vibrating membrane.

3 - Chordophones:

Instruments that produce sound by vibrating strings.

4 - Aerophones:

Instruments which produce sound by vibrating columns of air.

5 - Electrophones:

Instruments that produce sound electronically.

Answer:

Kakko drums are usually laid sideways on stands, and are played on both drumheads with sticks known as bachi. Kakko drums have been used in taiko ensembles, but are also used in the gagaku form of older Japanese court music.
