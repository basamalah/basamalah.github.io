21. In a survey, 205 people indicated they prefer GMA-7, 160 indicated they prefer ABS¬CBN, and 40 indicated they don't enjoy either station. Find the probability that if a person is chosen at random, he prefers GMA-7.​


he total number of people surveyed is:

205 (prefer GMA-7) + 160 (prefer ABS-CBN) + 40 (don't enjoy either) = 405

The probability that a person chosen at random prefers GMA-7 is:

P(prefer GMA-7) = number of people who prefer GMA-7 / total number of people surveyed

P(prefer GMA-7) = 205 / 405

P(prefer GMA-7) = 0.506 or 50.6% (rounded to one decimal place)

Therefore, the probability that if a person is chosen at random, he prefers GMA-7 is 0.506 or 50.6%.
