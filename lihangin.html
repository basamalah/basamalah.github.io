Answer:

The two gods were constantly at war until they decided to unite through the marriage of their children, Lidagat and Lihangin. Lidagat was Magwayen's daughter and goddess of the sea, while Lihangin was Kaptan's son and god of the wind. When Lihangin died he left control of the winds to Likalibutan.
