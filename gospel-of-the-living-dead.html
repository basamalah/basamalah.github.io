14. how chimpazee live to not be dead​


How chimpanzees manage to stay alive

Like all living things, chimpanzees require the fulfillment of their basic needs in order to thrive. These necessities consist of items like food, drink, shelter, and defense against predators. A variety of actions and tactics are used by chimpanzees, which are intelligent and adaptive creatures, to locate and acquire the resources they require to survive. Chimpanzees live in social groups in the wild, where they cooperate to obtain food, erect shelters, and defend themselves from predators. Chimpanzees kept in captivity are given food, drink, and shelter as well as safety from predators. Chimpanzees can live and prosper as long as they have their fundamental requirements met and take precautions to stay safe.

Chimpanzees are able to live long, healthy lives due to a variety of factors, including solid social ties, Chimpanzees form close ties with one another and live in social groupings. They receive support, safety, and assistance from these social ties in locating food and other resources. Chimpanzees may find food and shelter in a variety of habitats and can adapt to a wide range of environmental conditions. Because they are cognitive, chimpanzees can use tools and find solutions to challenges in order to get food and other resources. Because they are muscular and agile, chimpanzees can evade predators, hunt for food, groom one another, and engage in other healthful habits like picking parasites off one another's fur.

21. what is these are plant organisms that live on dead,decaying or. living matter​


A saprophyte, also referred to as a saprobe or saprotroph, is any organism that feeds and grows on dead organisms. Since saprophytes rely on dead plant and animal bodies for food, rather than producing their own as autotrophs do, they are heterotrophs.
