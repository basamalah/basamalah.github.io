1. Dula ng patria Amanda Dula ng patria amanda


Dula ng Patria Amanda sulat ni Amando Navarette Osorio na sinalin naman sa Filipino ni Joey A. Arrogante.  

Ito ay tungkol kay Patria Amanda noong pangahon na ang Katipunan ay umalsa laban sa pamahalaan sa Balintawak at si Bonificaio ay sumigaw ng kalayaan. Sa kabilang banda, sila'y nagtatalo ng kaniyang kasintahang si Felipe Libre na lilisan upang mamundok upang ipaglaban ang layunin at makiisa sa himagsikan.

Si Felipe ay umalis at naligaw ngunit sa tulong ni Damon natagpuan nila ito at ibinalita din sa kanila na bobombahin ang kanilang bayan. Agad hinanap ni Felipe si Patria na papunta rin naman ng bundok.

Si Patria ay sumama rin sa pakikidigma, siya ay nag bihis tulad ng isang mandirigma. Agad naman siyang pinapuntahan kay Damon.

Si Damon ay bumalik sa Heneral at kay Felipe hatid ang mensaheng patay na si Patria. Tinanong ng Heneral kung nakita nito ang bangkay at kung nakita pang buhay ngunit si Damon ay nanghula lamang na si Patria ay patay na kaya ito ulit ay pinahanap. Pagdating ni Patria sa Heneral, agad namang nagbiro si Damon na patay na si Felipe. Hinimatay si Patria, agad naman lumapit si Felipe kay Patria para gisingin. Natapos ang kwento ng patugtugin ng Heneral ang "Awit sa Yutang Natauhan."

18 mi : 5 h = x : 4h
Cross multiply to simplify. Multiply 18 to 4 and 5 to x, which results to:
5x = 72 (divide both sides by 5)
x = 14.4 milesThis is a problem on direct proportion. That is 
     [tex]\frac{18\:miles}{5\:hours}=\frac{x\:miles}{4\:hours}[/tex]
Cross multiply
     [tex]\left(18\:miles\right)\left(4\:hours\right)=\left(x\:miles\right)\left(5\:hours\right)[/tex]
     [tex]72=5x[/tex]
     [tex]x=14.4\:miles[/tex]

28. Amanda was task to buy vegetables in a nearby flea market. To do this she had to take walk about 55m east to reach her friends house. Both of them will walk another 220m to reach the flea market. Assuming that they got there in 5minutes, how fast did Amanda and her friend were walking? What is Amanda's total distance? What is the displacement? Illustrate Amanda's path of travel. What is Amanda's speed and velocity?​


Explanation:

To find the speed at which Amanda and her friend were walking, we need to use the formula:

speed = distance / time

We know that Amanda walked 55m to reach her friend's house, and then both of them walked another 220m to reach the flea market. So Amanda's total distance is:

55m + 220m = 275m

We also know that they got to the flea market in 5 minutes. To find their speed, we need to convert the time to seconds:

5 minutes x 60 seconds/minute = 300 seconds

So their speed is:

speed = distance / time = 275m / 300s = 0.92 m/s

To find the displacement, we need to find the straight-line distance from Amanda's starting point to the flea market. We can use the Pythagorean theorem to do this:

displacement = sqrt(55^2 + 220^2) = 227.57m

To illustrate Amanda's path of travel, we can draw a straight line from her starting point to the flea market.

Amanda's speed is 0.92 m/s. Her velocity is the speed in a particular direction. Since she walked east to reach her friend's house and then continued east to reach the flea market, her velocity is also eastward, and the magnitude is 0.92 m/s.
