16. How do you find the angle thingy?


Answer:

a = 40°

Step-by-step explanation:

We use the External Angle Theorem where the exterior angle is equal to the sum of the remote angles. Which in this case the remote angles are a° and 87 °. We'll just have to solve for a.

127 = 87 + a

127 - 87 = 87 + a - 87

40 = a

Thus a = 40°
