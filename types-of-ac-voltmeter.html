2. what is the difference between ac voltmeter and dc voltmeter​


Answer:

The major difference between DC voltmeter and AC voltmeter is that the DC voltmeter measures the peak value of DC voltage while AC voltmeter measures the RMS value of AC voltage. Another significant factor which differentiates DC voltmeter and AC voltmeter is their working principle.
