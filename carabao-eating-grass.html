26. carabao and grass effects of the interaction


Answer:

The interaction and effect between carabao and grass are described as a mutualistic relationship. They both depend on each other in the exchange of nutrients and gases for breathing. The carabao mainly depends on grass for their food and energy. Grass releases oxygen when they perform photosynthesis, carabao breath out CO2 when breathing. This exchange of carbon-oxygen gases is necessary for survival to both of them.

28. a carabao is tied to a peg using a 9 m string. how wide is the area within which the carabao can roam and eat grass​


Answer:

254.34 sq. m.

Step-by-step explanation:

The string will act as the radius of the circle. The formula for the area is "pi x r squared or pi x r x r" where r is the radius and pi is a constant with a value of 3.14

pi x r x r

= 3.14 x 9 x 9

=254.34 meter squared or sq. m
