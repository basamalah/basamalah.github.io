30. 5. Manny killed his wife under exceptional circumstances and was sentenced by the RTC of Dagupan City to suffer the penalty of destierro during which he was not enter the city. While serving sentence, Manny went to Dagupan City to visit his mother, Later, he was arrested in Manila. What crime committed?​


Answer:

Based on the given information, Manny was sentenced to suffer the penalty of destierro, during which he was not allowed to enter the city of Dagupan. However, he violated the terms of his sentence by going to Dagupan City to visit his mother. This is a violation of the court's order and can be considered as contempt of court.

Therefore, the crime committed by Manny is contempt of court, specifically direct contempt, which is committed in the presence of or so near the court as to obstruct or interrupt the proceedings before the same. Manny's act of going to Dagupan City, which is prohibited by his sentence, is a direct violation of the court's order and can be considered as contempt of court.
