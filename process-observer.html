Since those other observers measure a longer time at, the effect is called time dilation. The Earth-bound observer sees time dilate (get longer) for a system moving relative to the Earth. The process of watching someone or something. An observation can also be the collected information itself.

A scientist looking at a chemical reaction in an experiment

Air flight controllers watching their monitors for airplane movements and locations
