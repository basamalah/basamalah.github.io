4. There are 18 Davaoenos who participated in a seminar. this is 18% of the total number of the participants. how many attended the seminar?


Answer:

100

Step-by-step explanation:

We need to convert 18% to fraction first.

18% = 0.18 in decimal form0.18 = 9/50 in fraction form

So let x be the total number of participants.

[tex]18 = \frac{9}{50} x[/tex]

To eliminate 9/50, we should multiply both sides  to its reciprocal

[tex]18×\frac{50}{9} = (\frac{9}{50} × \frac{50}{9}) x[/tex]

100 = x

Hence, there are a total of 100 participants who attended the seminar

*Basic Education Seminar Program:

The rationale behind the Basic Education Seminar Program is to provide an avenue for individuals to learn and improve their knowledge and skills in various areas of education. Through this program, participants will have the opportunity to gain a better understanding of fundamental concepts in various fields of study, learn practical skills that they can use in their daily lives, and receive guidance on how to pursue further education and career opportunities. The program aims to empower individuals through education, promoting personal and professional growth, and contributing to the development of a more educated and skilled community.

*Barangay Waste Recycling Project:

The Barangay Waste Recycling Project is a sustainable solution to the growing problem of waste management in local communities. The rationale behind this project is to promote environmental awareness and encourage community participation in reducing waste and preserving natural resources. By establishing a waste recycling system, the project aims to reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators, promote the reuse of materials, and create a more sustainable community. The project also provides an opportunity for community members to work together towards a common goal, fostering a sense of unity and cooperation.

*Mental Health Awareness and Counseling Services:

The rationale behind the Mental Health Awareness and Counseling Services is to address the growing mental health concerns in the community. The program aims to provide individuals with the necessary tools and resources to manage their mental health, reduce the stigma associated with mental illness, and increase access to mental health services. Through education and awareness campaigns, the program seeks to promote understanding and empathy towards those struggling with mental health issues. The counseling services provided aim to offer support and guidance to those in need, empowering individuals to take control of their mental health and improve their quality of life
