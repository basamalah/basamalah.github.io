Using the venn diagram,

Subtract 2 from the both number of students in cheerdance and chorus to get the number of the one who only took cheerdance and chorus.

18 - 2= 16 only took cheerdance
26-2 = 24 only took chorus

And 2 who took both. To identify the no. of students that are enrolled,

16+24+2 = 42 are enrolled

Then subtract 42 from 50 to identify those who aren't enrolled.

Therefore, there are 8 students in the group who aren't enrolled.

CHEERDANCE/ CHEERDANCING

Was started in Great Britain during the 1860s and brought to the US by the year 1880.

It was initially done by men and later evolved to be more graceful when done by women.

The idea was discovered in the crowd showing their support for a team in a football game in Princeton University in the year 1884. They noticed that chanting can help in boosting the energy and spirit of the players in the game.

In the Philippines, Cheerleading Philippines (CPF) is an organization that focused on the confederation of different associations or governing bodies of Cheer dance and teams in the country.

They are founded in the year 1993, and they support, sponsor, sanction so that different cheerleading teams can represent the country in a world championship.
