6. o captain,my captain" is an example of a/an​


Answer:

"O Captain! My Captain!" is an example of a poem. It was written by Walt Whitman in 1865 as a tribute to Abraham Lincoln, the 16th President of the United States, after his assassination. The poem speaks to the loss and mourning of a leader, but also the hope for a continued journey and progress. It is considered as one of the most famous works of Whitman and it's widely read and studied in American literature.

12. Captain ali is the captain of the ship the captain of the ship is ali what is the name of the ship?

Captain Ali is the captain of the ship

The captain of the ship is Ali

What is the name of the ship

Why does the answer is "What"?

14. captain ali is the captain of the ship. the captain of the ship is captain ali. what is the name of the ship?


WHAT.

WHAT is the name of the ship.  

Or

The name of the ship is WHAT.

There is no other name mentioned in the riddle except for Ali and What. Ali is the captain and his name can’t be the same name of the ship. This riddle is usually recited orally as to confuse the guesser of what is really the answer since one can play with the intonation of the last line making it ambiguously asking or revealing. Since this is a riddle, the person being told must make a guess.  

In writing, you may intentionally put a period at the end, or just simply write it as a question. Either way is acceptable though some would argue once the answer is revealed.

Some also calls this as a catchy or catching question because it’s goal is to challenge someone to catch the hidden message.
