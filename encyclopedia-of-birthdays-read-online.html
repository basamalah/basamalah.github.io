1. What is the difference between online encyclopedia and traditional encyclopedia￼


Answer:

Online encyclopedias are one of the most recent additions to the Internet. They are similar to traditional printed encyclopedias in that they contain compilations of articles on numerous topics. What makes these online encyclopedias unique is that any Internet user may submit a new entry or make an editorial modification to one that already exists. As a consequence, the encyclopedia is written by the whole Internet community. Although the concept is appealing, collaborative online encyclopedias suffer from a number of significant flaws that render them far less beneficial than traditional printed encyclopedias.
