28. If the measure of GCF is 12° less than the measure of BCG, what is the measure of FCD?


Problem:

If the measure of GCF is 12° less than the measure of BCG, what is the measure of FCD?

Solution:

m∠BCG + m∠GCF + m∠FCD = 180⁰

m∠BCG + (m∠BCG - 12⁰) + m∠FCD = 180⁰

2m∠BCG - 12⁰ + m∠FCD = 180⁰

m∠FCD = 180⁰ + 12⁰ - 2m∠BCG

m∠FCD = 192⁰ - 2m∠BCG
