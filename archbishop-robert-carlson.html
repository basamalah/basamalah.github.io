9. He was the secretary of the archbishop of manila during rizal's execution


You are referring to Father Manuel Villaclara, the secretary of Archbishop Bernardino Nozaleda y Villa during the time of José Rizal's execution. Father Villaclara was present at Rizal's execution and later wrote a letter to the archbishop describing the day's events. This letter, known as the "Villaclara Letter," is an essential primary source for historians studying Rizal and the Philippines during this period.

Father Villaclara's presence at Rizal's execution and his subsequent letter have also played a role in the ongoing controversy surrounding the "Rizal Retraction," a letter that Rizal is alleged to have written just before his execution. Some people believe that the Rizal Retraction is a forgery, and the Villaclara Letter is often cited as evidence to support this view.

Carlson sisters received (13 + y) ÷ 5 pieces of jewelry.

What is asked?

How many pieces of jewelry did each sister receive? Write your answer as an expression.

What are given?

jewelry from Grandma Gertrude - 13

jewelry from Grandma Fien - y

What is the formula?

(jewelry from Grandma Gertrude + jewelry from Grandma Fien) ÷ 5 = N

To know how many pieces of jewelry each Carlson sister gets, we need to add the pieces of jewelry that Grandma Gertrude and Grandma Fien gave to the Carlson sisters. Then after getting the sum, we need to divide the pieces of jewelry by 5 because there are five Carlson sisters.

Solution:

Substitute the values.

(jewelry from Grandma Gertrude + jewelry from Grandma Fien) ÷ 5

(13 + y) ÷ 5
