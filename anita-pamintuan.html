1. pamintuan mansion story​


Answer:

Pampanga, Philippines — Located in Angeles City, Barangay Sto. Rosario in Miranda Street lies a home that has been standing for more than 100 years; the Pamintuan Mansion is a mansion also known as the Museum of Philippine Social History (Museo ng Kasaysayang Panlipunan ng Pilipinas) when it was inaugurated as this last 2015.Showcasing how diverse the culture of Filipinos are, the Pamintuan mansion is the place that shows the collective results of all our colonial experiences and influences. Even before its inauguration, many visitors appreciate and applaud how it exhibits different aspects of our social history. poster   It is also a place to see how the local heritage has developed over the years. Even more than just this museum, the whole of Angeles is creating a lot of efforts to upgrade the whole area to a district that features the diverse sides of the Filipino heritage.This museum has a lot of things to display that will highlight how Filipinos live their day to day life through the many artworks, collections, and various exhibits. This is a good contrast for all the other museums that we have since other museums mostly feature art that is about the heroes of the country or it narrates some political or religious eventsIn the museum, there are nine galleries that can be explored by guests to discover how the lives of Filipino grew.

Summary of Shawl for Anita

'A Shawl for Anita' is a short story about the mother of Anita who loves to knit. She felt a sense of uselessness in life and felt as if she was living for nothing anymore when her mother grew older. Anita then decided to ask her mother to knit a shawl for her. The narrator of the story, who is Anita's older sister thought it was a silly thing to do because their mother is very old to knit anymore. But Anita's purpose in doing so is for their mother to regain her purpose in life by doing the things she loves to do. Anita thought that by so doing, their mother can reminisce the joys she had before and once again discover new joys in her continuing life.

12. What is the theme of a shawl of Anita?


'A Shawl of Anita' is a short story about Anita's mother who loves to knit. When her mother grew older, she felt a sense of uselessness in life and felt as if she was living for nothing anymore. Anita then decided to ask her mother to knit a shawl for her. Her older sister, who is the narrator of the story, thought it was a silly thing to do because their mother is very old to knit anymore. The theme of the story focuses on family love and finding our purpose in life. It also talks about compassion towards one another and the passions we can develop as an individual, as well as the great love of a mother to her children.

14. are you in favor of the conversation of pamintuan mansion into a philippine social history museum? why?​


Answer:

This mansion holds tons of memories coming from the past decades. For instance, President Emilio Aguinaldo used this mansion as his headquarters during the battle with the Americans. From Kawit Laguna, Aguinaldo transferred his headquarters in the Pamintuan Mansion. This is where he carried out his different tasks as the first president of the republic.

It was also converted into an Angeles Hotel as operated by a Chinese businessman. It became a home for many American forces back in World War II. Eventually, it was converted into a satellite office of the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) back in 1993 up to the year 2009. With these significant historical events that transpired in the Pamintuan mansion, it is indeed a treasure to behold
