2. Character in finding dory​


Answer:

Dory: a blue tang fish with short-term memory loss

Marlin: a clownfish and Dory's friend from the first movie, "Finding Nemo"

Nemo: a young clownfish and Marlin's son

Hank: a "septopus" (an octopus missing one of its eight tentacles) who helps Dory navigate the Marine Life Institute

Destiny: a near-sighted whale shark who becomes Dory's friend

Bailey: a beluga whale who also lives at the Marine Life Institute and helps Dory and her friends

Jenny and Charlie: Dory's parents who she tries to find and reunite with throughout the movie

Becky: a loon bird who helps Dory and her friends in their quest

Explanation:

There are many more characters in the movie, including other sea creatures who live at the Marine Life Institute and help Dory along the way.
