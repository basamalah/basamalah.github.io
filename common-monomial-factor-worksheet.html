29. factoring by greatest Common monomial factor​


Answer:

Factoring by greatest common monomial factor is a method of factoring a polynomial by finding the largest monomial that divides all the terms in the polynomial. This monomial is called the GCMF, and it is multiplied by the quotient of each term to obtain the factored form of the polynomial.
