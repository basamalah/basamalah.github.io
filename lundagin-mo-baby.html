4. Help me guys ! Summary of “Lundagin mo baby” (go for it) by Gilda Cordero-Fernando.


“Lundagin Mo Baby, (Go For It)” by Gilda Cordero-Fernando Summary

When times get rough, there are several motivational quotes that uplift the dampened spirits. “Lundagin Mo Baby” is not just another new colloquial term but a phrase that expresses encouragement. Reaching new heights can only be attained if you aim to jump for it. There is no perfect time to say it but in every moment where feelings of desperation and hopelessness are at their peak. If there’s another phrase that best matches it, it would be this: “aim high.”

18. According to the text "Lundagin mo Baby! (Go for it) how can Filipinos change their colonial mentality?


Answer:

By being optimistic. Remember how the OFW pursued going overseas despite of strange environment, people, language ang culture? She was driven by optimism. She always think of the good things she can cause to other people and the idea of helping her family without capital made her go for it. Being optimistic changes colonial mentality because it helps us disregard the feeling of inferiority and shame.
