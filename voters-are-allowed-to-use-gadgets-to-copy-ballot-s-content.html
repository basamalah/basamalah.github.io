3. 1. An election ballot asks voters to select three city commissioners from a group of six candidates. In how many ways can this be done? ​


Answer:

20 ways

Step-by-step explanation:

Using the Combination Formula:

C(n,r) = n! / r!(n-r)!

C(6,3) = 6! / 3!(6-3)!

C(6,3) = 6! / 3!3!

C(6,3) = 6×5×4×3! / 3!3!

C(6,3) = 6×5×4 / 3!

C(6,3) = 6×5×4 / 3×2×1

C(6,3) = 5×4

C(6,3) = 20 ways
