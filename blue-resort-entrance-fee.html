6. ina resort entrance fee per person is 80 baht in a group of 10 people it fixed at 70% if the resort give additional additional 50% discount for every person that exists then how much is entrance fee paid by twenty one person with solution plss​


Answer:

Given:

Entrance fee per person = 80 baht

Group size = 10 people

Discount for a group of 10 people = 70%

Additional discount per person = 50%

Number of people = 21

To find:

Entrance fee paid by 21 people after discounts

Solution:

The group of 10 people gets a discount of 70%, which means they will pay only 30% of the total entrance fee.

Total entrance fee for 10 people = 10 x 80 = 800 baht

Entrance fee for 10 people after 70% discount = 800 x 0.3 = 240 baht

So, the entrance fee for 1 person in the group of 10 = 240 / 10 = 24 baht

Now, every person gets an additional 50% discount. Therefore, the new entrance fee per person for the group of 10 people will be:

24 - (24 x 0.5) = 12 baht

The remaining 11 people will each pay 80 baht, and they will also receive a 50% discount:

80 - (80 x 0.5) = 40 baht

Therefore, the total entrance fee paid by 21 people is:

(10 x 12) + (11 x 40) = 120 + 440 = 560 baht

Therefore, the entrance fee paid by 21 people after discounts is 560 baht.

28. last summer the entrance fee in sandy's resort is 50 pesos for adults and 30 pesos for children .If 100 when to the resort one day during summer and the owner collected 3180 pesos .How many of them were adults and how many were children​


Step-by-step explanation:

Let's assume that the number of adults who went to the resort was "A" and the number of children was "C".

From the problem, we know that the entrance fee for adults is 50 pesos and for children is 30 pesos. We also know that 100 people went to the resort and the owner collected a total of 3180 pesos.

Using this information, we can set up two equations:

A + C = 100 (equation 1, because the total number of people who went to the resort was 100)

50A + 30C = 3180 (equation 2, because the owner collected a total of 3180 pesos)

We can use equation 1 to solve for A in terms of C:

A = 100 - C

Substituting this expression for A into equation 2, we get:

50(100 - C) + 30C = 3180

Expanding the brackets and simplifying, we get:

5000 - 20C = 3180

Subtracting 5000 from both sides, we get:

-20C = -1820

Dividing both sides by -20, we get:

C = 91

Therefore, 91 children went to the resort that day. We can use equation 1 to find the number of adults:

A + C = 100

A + 91 = 100

A = 9

Therefore, 9 adults went to the resort that day.

In summary, there were 9 adults and 91 children who went to the resort, and the owner collected a total of 3180 pesos.
