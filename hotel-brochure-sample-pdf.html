10. gumawa ng sample brochuremga kalamidad​


Answer:

KALAMIDAD: ANG PAG-IINGAT AT PAGSISIYASAT

Ang pag-iingat at pagsisiyasat ay mahalaga sa pagharap sa mga kalamidad. Ang mga kalamidad ay maaaring magdulot ng pinsala sa tao, ari-arian, at kapaligiran. Upang maiwasan ang pinsala, mahalagang magkaroon ng kaalaman tungkol sa mga kalamidad at ang paraan upang harapin ito.

Kung ano ang Mga Kalamidad?

Ang mga kalamidad ay natural na pangyayari na nagdudulot ng pinsala o pagkawasak. Ang ilan sa mga ito ay: lindol, baha, bagyo, sunog, landslide, tornado, tsunami at iba pa. Ang lahat ng ito ay nagdudulot ng malaking pinsala sa tao at ari-arian.

Paano Makakaiwas sa Kalamidad?

• Maghanda ng isang plano para sa panahon ng kalamidad. Magplano para sa evacuation o pagtakas patungo sa isang ligtas na lugar.

• Maghanda ng emergency kit na mayroong gamit tulad ng food and water supplies, first aid kit at iba pa.

• Maging alerto para sa anumang babala o impormasyon tungkol sa kalamidad.

• Magkaroon ng kaalaman tungkol sa paraan upang harapin ang bawat uri ng kalamidad.

• Maging handa upang magbigay ng tulong o suporta para sa mga apektado nito.

Paano Makakatulong?

• Sumali o maging bahagi ng isang organisasyon na tumutulong para maibsan ang pinsala dulot ng kalamidad.

• Kumilos upang maprotektahan ang sarili laban sa anumang uri ng pinsala dulot nito.

• Magbigay tulong o suporta para maibsan ang pinsala dulot nito.

Sa pamamagitan nito, mas mapoprotektahan mo ang iyong sarili at pamilya laban sa anumang uri ng pinsala dulot nito. Kaya't huwag mag-atubiling magkaroon kaalaman tungkol dito!


11. Sino may sample codes ng reservation hotel? I need it for thesis.


Reservation status codes are attached to the guest reservation to indicate the status of the booking made by the guest. Eg:- Booking confirmed by credit card, tentative bookings which has to be released by 6 PM, Travel Agent Voucher confirmed etc.

Confirmed: Once payment method and/or billing instructions are confirmed a reservation status can be selected as confirmed. A reservation should only be confirmed upon receipt of a credit card or deposit payment.

Tentative: If payment method and/or billing instructions have not been guaranteed then the reservation should be set to the status of Tentative.

Waitlist: If the hotel or room type is fully booked then the customer should be given the option to waitlist their reservation.

Company Guarantee: If a reservation is made by a company and also billed to the same company then reservation status 'Company guarantee' may be used.

Travel Agent Guarantee: A travel agent booking with a valid IATA number and Travel agent voucher may be selected as code Travel Agent Guarantee'.

6 PM Release: A reservation is not guaranteed with payment or voucher and the hotel will hold it till 6PM in the evening.

Examples of Reservation Status Codes:

No. Reservation Status

1 Confirmed

2 6 PM Release

3 Tentative / Provisional

4 Waitlist

5 Turn away

6 Cancelled

7 No Show

8 Group Confirmed

9 Group Waitlist

10 CC Guaranteed

An executive housekeeper is responsible for the overall cleanliness and orderliness of a residence or commercial establishment. They typically oversee a team of housekeeping staff and may be in charge of hiring, training, and scheduling employees. In some cases, executive housekeepers may also be responsible for budgeting and ordering supplies.

The role of an assistant executive housekeeper is to support the executive housekeeper in the running of the housekeeping department. This includes tasks such as supervising staff, managing budgets, ordering supplies, and dealing with customer queries. The assistant executive housekeeper also helps to develop and implement housekeeping policies and procedures.

The floor supervisor is responsible for the daily operations of the floor. They ensure that the floor is clean and organized and that all staff are following company procedures. They also work closely with the management team to ensure that the floor runs smoothly and efficiently.

A public area supervisor is responsible for the cleanliness and upkeep of all public areas in a commercial or residential building. This may include the lo_by, hallways, restrooms, and other common areas. The supervisor may be responsible for hiring and training cleaning staff, developing cleaning schedules, and ensuring that all areas are properly stocked with supplies. In some cases, the supervisor may also be tasked with handling customer complaints or coordinating with other building staff to resolve issues.

A coordinator in a housekeeping department is in charge of a variety of tasks. They may be responsible for scheduling maid service, taking inventory of cleaning supplies, and coordinating the work of the housekeeping staff. In larger establishments, a coordinator may also be responsible for training new housekeeping employees.

A linen/uniform supervisor is responsible for managing the linen and uniform inventory for a housekeeping department. They ensure that all linen and uniforms are clean and in proper repair and that there is an adequate supply on hand to meet the needs of the department. They also oversee the laundry staff and coordinate with other departments to ensure that all linens and uniforms are returned promptly.

A laundry supervisor is responsible for managing the department's laundry staff and ensuring that all laundry services are performed according to hotel standards. The supervisor may also be responsible for overseeing the maintenance of cleaning equipment and ensuring that the department's budget is followed.

A room attendant is responsible for cleaning guest rooms and ensuring that they meet the standards set by the hotel. They may also be responsible for stocking supplies, such as linens and toiletries, and for checking in with guests to ensure that they are satisfied with their stay.

A public area attendant is responsible for cleaning and maintaining all public areas in a housekeeping department. This includes lobbies, hallways, restrooms, and any other areas that are open to the public. The public area attendant must ensure that these areas are clean, safe, and presentable at all times.

A houseman is an employee who works in the housekeeping department of a hotel. The main function of a houseman is to assist the housekeeping staff in their duties. This includes tasks such as cleaning guest rooms, making beds, and vacuuming floors. Housemen may also be responsible for setting up meeting rooms and event spaces, as well as delivering items to guests' rooms. In some cases, housemen may also be responsible for laundry and maintenance tasks.

A linen room attendant is responsible for keeping the linen room clean and organized. They may also be responsible for stocking linen, towels, and other supplies.

A valet/laundry attendant is responsible for providing laundry and valet services to guests in a hotel or other lodging facility. They may work in the housekeeping department or in a separate laundry facility. Their duties may include washing and pressing guests' clothing, collecting and delivering laundry to guests' rooms, and operating the hotel's washing equipment. They may also be responsible for keeping the laundry area clean and organized.
