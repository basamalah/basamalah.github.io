13. what is moore's law?​


Answer:

Moore's law is the observation that the number of transistors in a dense integrated circuit doubles about every two years. Moore's law is an observation and projection of a historical trend. Rather than a law of physics, it is an empirical relationship linked to gains from experience in production

14. Mandy drew a horizontal line. Dale drew a line cutting through the line drawn by Mandy, forming two pairs of vertical angles such as the measure of one pair is acute and the measure of the other is three times the acute measure. How many degrees is the larger measure? Type the number only.


Answer: 135°

Step-by-step explanation:

The lines drawn by Mandy and Dale are intersecting lines forming linear pairs. Each linear pair is made up of an angle with the acute measure and a larger angle with a measure that is three times the acute angle. Let x be the measure of the acute angle, and the measure of the larger angle be 3x.Since the angles will form a linear pair, their sum must be 180∘. Thus, x+3x=180∘. Simplifying, we have4x=180∘.Dividing both sides by 4, we havex=45∘.Therefore, the larger angle must have a measure of 3x=3(45∘)=135°.
