Answer:

DO NOT ALLOW RUST TO ACCUMULATE ON THE TOOLS THAT ARE MADE OF IRON, STEEL, AND OTHER SIMILAR  METALS (KALAWANG) APPLY OIL TO THE MOVING PARTS OF TOOLS, SUCH AS PLIERS, TIN SNIPS, AND HAND DRILLS, TO PREVENT THEM FROM RUSTING (LAGYAN NG OIL) TAKE PRECAUTION NOT TO DROP TOOLS BECAUSE THEY MIGHT BREAK (WAG IHUHULOG) KEEP THE TOOLS PROPERLY SHARPENED.
