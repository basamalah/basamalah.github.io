As for rhyme scheme: rhyme scheme commonly defines the pattern of rhyme [s] (recurring pattern of sound) at the end of a verse or stanza.

Rhyme schemes have the ability to change from line to line or from stanza to stanza. This means that a rhyme scheme often completes or sets a pattern for a stanza to follow.
