14. it is stage of infection where is the sick person recovers from the sickness slowly and feels better


Answer:

The stage of infection where the sick person recovers from the sickness slowly and feels better is called the convalescent stage.

Explanation:

During this stage, the body is recovering and repairing any damage caused by the infection. The symptoms gradually subside and the person may return to normal activities. It is important to note that even though the person may feel better, they may still be contagious and should continue to take precautions to avoid spreading the infection to others.
