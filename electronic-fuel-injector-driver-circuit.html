1. What is fuel injector of the diesel engine ?​


In a petrol engine, the fuel and air are mixed with each other a long time before they are transported into the cylinder, whereas in a diesel engine, the diesel fuel injector quite literally injects the fuel directly into the cylinder, where it then combines with the air.

8. How to check the injector voltage?​


Answer:

Voltage Testing

Turn the ignition key to the "On" position. You do not need to start the engine for this test.

Disconnect the electronics plug wire from the fuel injector.

Turn the multimeter to "volts." Insert the black and red multimeter leads onto each side of the fuel injector electronics plug. ...

Read the multimeter.

Answer:

Throttle Body Injection and Port Injection

Throttle body fuel injection is the earliest and simplest type of fuel injection. In Throttle body injection system, one or two fuel injectors are located in the throttle body which is mounted directly to the intake manifold.Port fuel injection system uses a separate fuel injector to each cylinder.
