11. What enabled sub-saharan kingdoms and societies to develop before Europeans?


Answer:

Sub-Saharan African kingdoms and societies were able to develop before Europeans due to a combination of factors, including their access to natural resources, their trading networks, their political systems, and their cultural values. Sub-Saharan Africa was home to a variety of strong, independent societies that were able to exploit their natural environment and create powerful trading networks with other regions in Africa and the Middle East. These societies had strong political systems, with centralized monarchies and decentralized city-states. They also had complex cultural values that allowed them to develop unique social and religious institutions. Finally, they had access to new technologies and goods from the East, which they used to create a sophisticated agricultural and trading economy.

Question :-

It took Ronald 6 hours to walk to Nilo's house at 2 kilometers per hour, how far is Nilo's house?

Answer :-

Nilo's house is 12 kilometers far.

[tex] \blue{ \overline{ \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad}}[/tex]

Solution :-

As per the provided information in the given question, we have been given that the speed is 2 km/h. The time is 6 hours. We have been asked to find or calculate the distance.

To calculate the distance, we will use the formula below :-

[tex]\qquad\quad\bigstar \: \: \boxed{\sf{ \: \: \: Distance = Speed \times Time \: \: \:}}[/tex]

Substitute the given values into the above formula and solve for Distance :-

[tex]\sf:\implies{Distance = Speed \times Time }[/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies{Distance = 2 \: km/h \times 6 \: hours }[/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies\bold{Distance = 12 \: km}[/tex]

Therefore :-

Nilo's house is 12 kilometers far.
