3. a small fruit smoothie is 18 ounces while a large fruit smoothie is 21.96 ounces the large fruit smoothie is ____%larger ​


Answer:

First, subtract 18 from 21.96.

21.96 - 18 = 3.96

That is the amount it was increased by.

Now, to find the percent increase, divide 3.96 by 18.

3.96 ÷ 18 = 0.21444

In percents, that is equal to 21.444%.

The large fruit smoothie is 21.444% larger.

8. What is alkalinity? ​


Answer:

Alkalinity is the capacity of water to resist acidification. It should not be confused with basicity which is an absolute measurement on the pH scale. Alkalinity is the strength of a buffer solution composed of weak acids and their conjugate bases.
