Kaye's momentum can be calculated by using the equation: momentum = mass x velocity

Given that Kaye's mass is 50.0 kg and she is standing still, her velocity is 0 m/s.

So, Kaye's momentum = 50.0 kg x 0 m/s = 0 Ns

Note that momentum is a vector quantity and it has a direction, in this case, since she is standing still, her momentum is zero.
