Sides:

21 ft

14 ft

Angles:

76°

Since two sides are given, we can use the pythagorean theorem.

[tex] {c}^{2} = {a}^{2} + {b}^{2} [/tex]

Solution:

[tex] {21}^{2} - {14}^{2} = {b}^{2} [/tex]

[tex] \sqrt{ {21}^{2} - {14}^{2} } = b[/tex]

[tex] \sqrt{441 - 196} = b[/tex]

[tex] \sqrt{245} = b[/tex]

[tex]15.65 = b[/tex]

Answer:

Leo's sister is 15.65ft away from his brother.
