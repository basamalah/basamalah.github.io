15. the amount of commission is directly proportional to the amount of sales. an agent receive 22,400 on the sale of a car worth 448,000. how much would commission be on a luxury car worth 2,400,000?


The commision is 5%

So the commision in the luxury car will be 120 000

Commission varies directly proportional to the amount of sales:

⇒ Commission = kSales


Solve k:

When sales = 448.000, commission = 22,400

22,400 = k x 448,000

k =  22,400 ÷ 448,000

k = 0.05

⇒ Commission = 0.05 x Sales


Find the commission for 2,400,000

Commission = 0.05 x Sales

Commission = 0.05 x 2,400,000

Commission = 120,000


Answer: The commission is 120,000


------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Alternative Solution:


448.000 → 22,400

Find 1 unit:

1 → 22,400 ÷ 448,000 = 0.05

FInd 2,400,000 units:

2,400,000  →  0.05 x 2,400,000  = 120,000


Answer: The commission is 120,000
