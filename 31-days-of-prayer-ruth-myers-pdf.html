30. The total of Ruth and Mary’s ages is 15. Ruth is 7 years older than Mary. Ruth is ___ years old.


Answer:

The total of Ruth and Mary’s ages is 15. Ruth is 7 years older than Mary.

Ruth is 11 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Age sum = 15Mary = xRuth = x + 7

Create an equation:

x + (7 + x) = 15x + 7 + x = 152x + 7 = 152x = 15 - 72x = 8x = 4

Find age:

Mary = x = 4 years oldRuth = x + 7 = 4 + 7 = 11 years old

Sum of their age:

4 + 11 = 15
