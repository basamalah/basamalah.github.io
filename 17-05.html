18. 4. Leni is to board a flight at 05:35 hours . Her flight will take 7 hours and 10 minutes to reach the destination, but her flight got delayed by 20 minutes. At what time will Leni reach her destination if there is no further delay? * a.13:05 b.02:55 c.14:05 d.02:05


Answer:

The time that Leni will reach her destination if there is no further delay is 01:05 p.m.

Step-by-step explanation:

She is to board the flight at 5:35

Since it takes 7 hours and 10 minutes to reach the destination, add 7:10

The final sum is 12:45, but since it got delayed by 20 minutes, the final sum is now 12:65.

But wait, it's 65 minutes. Not to worry, just remove the 6 from the minutes and add 1 to 12 and you get 13:05, or 01:05 p.m.
