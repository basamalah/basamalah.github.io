Examples of Subject-Verb Agreement Overall, subject-verb agreement is a very simple idea. For subjects and verbs to agree, the numbers need to agree. For instance, if a person is riding a bike, you need to express that one person rides one bike.

Answer:

Subjects and verbs must AGREE with one another in number (singular or plural). Thus, if a subject is singular, its verb must also be singular; if a subject is plural, its verb must also be plural.
