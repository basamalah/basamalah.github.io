11. Abigail is 8 years older than Cynthia. Twenty years ago Abigail was three times as old as Cynthia. How old is each now?


Answer:

Abigail is 32 years old.Cynthia is 24 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Age Word Problem

Age problems are word problems dealing with the ages of people currently, in the past or in the future. To find the answer, we need to analyze the problem carefully and determine the relationship between the numbers.

Going back to the problem above, we need to express the mathematical phrases to algebraic equation.

Equation:

Let x be Cynthia's age.

Now:

"Abigail is 8 years older than Cynthia."

Cynthia - x

Abigail - x + 8

20 years ago:

"Twenty years ago, Abigail was three times as old as Cynthia."

Cynthia - 8 + x - 20

Abigail - 3 (x - 20)

8 + x - 20 = 3 (x - 20)

Solution:

8 + x - 20 = 3 (x - 20)

x - 12 = 3x - 60

-3x + x = -60 + 12

-2x = -48

-2x/-2 = -48/-2

x = 24

Cynthia is 24 years old now.

Abigail = x + 8

= 24 + 8

= 32

Abigail is 32 years old now.

Checking:

"Twenty years ago Abigail was three times as old as Cynthia."

Abigail = 32 - 20 = 12

Cynthia = 24 - 20 = 4

3(4) = 12

12 = 12 ✔
