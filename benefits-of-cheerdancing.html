19. in a group of 50 student,18 took cheerdance,26 took chorus,and 2 took both cheerdance and chorus.how many students in the group are not enrolled in either cheerdance or chorus?


Using the venn diagram,

Subtract 2 from the both number of students in cheerdance and chorus to get the number of the one who only took cheerdance and chorus.

18 - 2= 16 only took cheerdance
26-2 = 24 only took chorus

And 2 who took both. To identify the no. of students that are enrolled,

16+24+2 = 42 are enrolled

Then subtract 42 from 50 to identify those who aren't enrolled.

Therefore, there are 8 students in the group who aren't enrolled.

21. what is cheerdancing?


Cheerdancing came from the two words “cheer” and “dance”. To cheer is to shout short, motivating words to boost the morale of a team or a player. On the other hand, to dance is to create movements to express emotions or gestures, usually with a rhythm.  

Cheerdancing is rooted from cheerleading. What is cheerleading? It is a performance of a routine, dominated by gymnastic moves combined with cheering or yelling, which aim to lead the crowd to cheer for a certain team during a game or sport.
