8. A cylindrical silo has a circumference of 18.5 m. Find its diameter


Answer:

Diameter is 5.88 m

Step-by-step explanation:

Find the diameter of a cylinder.

Given

C = 18.5 m

With the given circumference, we can use the equation of C = 2πr to derive a radius equation, where diameter is equivalent to twice of a circle's radius.

Use the equation,

radius = C/2π

where, π = 3.14

r = C/2π

= 18.5/2(3.14)

= 18.5/(6.28)

r = 2.94 m

Now, after we obtain the radius

Use the equation,

diameter = 2r

d = 2r

= 2(2.94)

d = 5.88 m
