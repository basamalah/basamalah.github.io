11. If the probability that your DVD player breaks downbefore the extended warranty expires is 0.015, what isthe probability, that the player will not break downbefore the warranty expires?​


Answer:

The probability that the DVD player will not break down before the warranty expires is 0.985.

Answer:

The probability that the DVD player breaks down before the extended warranty expires is 0.015, which means that the probability that it does not break down before the warranty expires is the complement of this probability.

The complement of an event A is the probability of the event "not A" occurring, which is equal to 1 minus the probability of A occurring. So, the probability that the DVD player does not break down before the warranty expires is:

1 - 0.015 = 0.985

Therefore, the probability that the DVD player will not break down before the warranty expires is 0.985.
