24. Search for an example of a worksheet. Explain the different columns included in the worksheet.


Accounting Worksheet Columns Included:

-Unadjusted Trial Balance

-Adjusting Entries

-Adjusted Trial Balance

-Income Statement

-Balance Sheet

Explanations:

Unadjusted Trial Balance

A column contains all the company’s assets, liability, expenses, and revenue accounts, which are used in a concerned year.

Adjusting Entries

All the accounts of the company, which requires the passing of the adjustment entry will get listed in the adjustment column.

Adjusted Trial Balance

The adjusted trial balance will be prepared by combining the entries of the previous two columns, Unadjusted trial balance, and adjustments.

Income Statement

A column contains the values concerning the expenses and revenue accounts only. In this case, if the value of total revenue exceeds the expense column, then the difference will be the net income of the year of the company as it is earning more revenues in the year than what it is spending on its expenses.

Balance Sheet

A column contains the values with respect to the assets, liabilities, and owner’s capital only.

30. it will allow you to place text on multiple lines within a single cell rather than have the text spread over multiples cells in the worksheets.


ANSWER

The feature you are describing is called "text wrapping." Text wrapping allows you to enter text into a single cell and have it automatically wrap to multiple lines within that cell, rather than being cut off or spilling over into adjacent cells.

To enable text wrapping in Microsoft Excel, you can select the cell or range of cells you want to format and then do the following:

Right-click and select "Format Cells" from the menu.In the Format Cells dialog box, click on the "Alignment" tab.Under "Text Control," check the box next to "Wrap text."Click "OK" to apply the formatting.

Once text wrapping is enabled, you can enter text into the cell as normal, and it will automatically wrap to multiple lines within the cell as needed. You can also adjust the height of the row to fit the wrapped text by dragging the row border downward.
