13. A trip from Calapan City to Puerto Galera took 2 4/5 hours during rainy season.The return trip took only 2 1 /4 hours. How much shorter was the return trip?​


Answer:

33 mins

Step-by-step explanation:

An hour is equal to 60 minutes, 1/5 of 60 is 12. 12 x 5 is 60, 12 x 4 is 48, 12 x 3 is 36 and so on. 4/5 of 60 is 48, so the trip from Calapan City to Puerto Galera took 2 hours and 48 minutes.

1/4 of 60 is 15, so the return trip took 2 hours and 15 minutes.

48-15= 33

The return trip was shorter by 33 minutes.

23. The story of Sto. Nino in Calapan Yung tagalog po please ​


Answer:

English

The church and parish mission of Calapan was founded in 1679 by Fr. Diego de la Madre de Dios, an Augustinian Recollect. Under Fr. de la Madre de Dios, the church was fortified with stone walls. Due to frequent Moro raids in the island, two watchtowers and twenty cannons were also installed in the church and its environs. In another Moro raid on October 23, 1754, Fr. Andres de Jesus y Maria, then the parish priest of Calapan, and many people taking refuge in the church, were taken as hostages and were brought to Mindanao. In August 1881, the church was burned, and was reconstructed in 1887. Revolutionists in 1898 prior to the Philippine-American War occupied the church. In 1936, the Apostolic Prefecture of Mindoro, then comprising the present-day provinces of Oriental Mindoro, Occidental Mindoro and Romblon, was carved from the territories of the dioceses of Jaro and Lipa. Towards the end of the World War II in 1945, the church this time was occupied by the Americans and was turned over to the Filipinos in the same year. In 1951, the apostolic prefecture was elevated to Apostolic Vicariate of Calapan. In 1962, the present cathedral was finished. The Calapan vicariate subsequently lost territories to establish the Diocese of Romblon in 1974 and the Apostolic Vicariate of San Jose in Mindoro in 1983

Tagalog

Ang misyon ng simbahan at parokya ng Calapan ay itinatag noong 1679 ni Fr. Diego de la Madre de Dios, isang Augustinian Recollect. Sa ilalim ni Fr. de la Madre de Dios, ang simbahan ay pinatibay ng mga pader na bato. Dahil sa madalas na pagsalakay ng mga Moro sa isla, dalawang tore ng bantay at dalawampung kanyon din ang ikinabit sa simbahan at sa mga paligid nito. Sa isa pang pagsalakay ng Moro noong Oktubre 23, 1754, si Fr. Si Andres de Jesus y Maria, ang kura paroko noon ng Calapan, at maraming taong sumilong sa simbahan, ay dinala bilang mga hostage at dinala sa Mindanao. Noong Agosto 1881, sinunog ang simbahan, at muling itinayo noong 1887. Sinakop ng mga rebolusyonista noong 1898 bago ang Digmaang Pilipino-Amerikano. Noong 1936, ang Apostolic Prefecture ng Mindoro, na binubuo noon ng kasalukuyang mga lalawigan ng Oriental Mindoro, Occidental Mindoro at Romblon, ay inukit mula sa mga teritoryo ng mga diyosesis ng Jaro at Lipa. Sa pagtatapos ng Ikalawang Digmaang Pandaigdig noong 1945, ang simbahan sa pagkakataong ito ay sinakop ng mga Amerikano at naibigay sa mga Pilipino sa parehong taon. Noong 1951, ang apostolikong prefecture ay itinaas sa Apostolic Vicariate ng Calapan. Noong 1962, natapos ang kasalukuyang katedral. Ang Calapan vicariate pagkatapos ay nawalan ng mga teritoryo upang itatag ang Diocese of Romblon noong 1974 at ang Apostolic Vicariate of San Jose sa Mindoro noong 1983
