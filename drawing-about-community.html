Rough roads are often a major problem in many communities, as they can cause damage to vehicles, increase travel time, and make it more difficult for people to access necessary services.

Individuals can work with their community to create change by engaging in various activities such as organizing meetings, discussing the issue with local officials, creating petitions, and even volunteering to help repair the roads.
