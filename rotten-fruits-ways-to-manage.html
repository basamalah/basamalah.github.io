1. rotten fruits way to manage


If the fruits are already rotten (advance state of decay), I think it would be best to just throw them away or bury them. By doing the latter, it would help in fattening up the soil for your next gardening project. In case the fruits aren’t so bad, you can use it to feed pets such as rabbits, tortoises and any other creature that you have around the house. Some may still be used for human consumption, a good example of this is the preferred use of very ripe bananas to make banana bread. The ripest bananas, the ones that are sold individually for 1 peso, are sought after by bakers because it gives the best flavor when making banana bread.

But while the fruits are at their prime, it is best to consume or refrigerate them to prolong its freshness.
