1. Differentiate functional and non functional tools and equipment. And write some examples on functional and non-functional tools and equipment. (5pts)​


Answer:

Non-functional tools and equipment are those that are not able to perform their regular function because of impaired and damaged parts. Examples of these are the following: Hammer with a broken handle Screwdriver with a broken handle Long Nose Pliers with damaged jaw.

Functional tools and equipment - those that are in good condition and can perform their regular functions. They include screwdrivers, hammers, pliers, wrenches, and pullers.
