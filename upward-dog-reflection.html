21. A 0.01 kg ball falls freely from a height, hits the floor at 15 m/s, then reflected upward at 10 m/s. Determine the impulse.


Answer:

Mass (m) = = 0.01 kg

Initial velocity (vo) = -15 m/s

Final velocity (vt) = 10 m/s

Wanted : Impulse (I)

------------------------------------------------------

The impulse (I) equals the change in momentum (Δp)

[tex]I = m \: v _{t} - m \: v_{o} = m( v_{t} - v_{o}) [/tex]

Impulse :

I = 0.01 (10 – (-15))

= 0.01 (10 + 15)
