14. What is the total pressure in a canister that contains oxygen gas at a partial pressure of 804 mm hg, nitrogen at a partial pressure of220 mm hg, and hydrogen at a partial pressure of 445 mm hg?


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & P_{\text{N}_2} = \text{220 mmHg} \\ & P_{\text{O}_2} = \text{804 mmHg} \\ & P_{\text{H}_2} = \text{445 mmHg} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the total pressure by using Dalton's law of partial pressures.

[tex]\begin{aligned} P_{\text{T}} & = P_{\text{N}_2} + P_{\text{O}_2} + P_{\text{H}_2} \\ & = \text{220 mmHg + 804 mmHg + 445 mmHg} \\ & = \boxed{\text{1,469 mmHg}} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the total pressure of the gas mixture is 1,469 mmHg.
