30. Rex and Rey can plant a field in 4 hours together. It takes Ray 8 hours to do this alone. Without Rex, how long will it take Rex to do the same job?


Answer:

8 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

x = Rex

y = Rey

t = both

[tex]\frac{1}{x} + \frac{1}{y} = \frac{1}{t}[/tex]

[tex]\frac{1}{x} + \frac{1}{8} = \frac{1}{4}[/tex]

x = 8
