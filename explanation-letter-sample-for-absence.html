14. In what part/s of the map are there no earthquake? Give short explanation on the absence of earthquakes on those regions of the map​


Answer:

A part of the map that has no earthquake is called the shadow zone.

Explanation:

It is the area of the earth from angular distances of 104 to 140 degrees from a given earthquake that does not receive any direct P waves. The shadow zone results from S waves being stopped entirely by the liquid core and P waves being bent (refracted) by the liquid core. In Philippines, Palawan is a part of shadow zone.
