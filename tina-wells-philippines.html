4. Marta's age is thrice more than Tina. And the total age of Marta and Tina is 40. Express the Algebraic form of this condition.​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebraic expression and basic mathematics especially in solving word problem about application of algebraic expression.

Problem

Marta's age is thrice more than Tina. And the total age of Marta and Tina is 40. Express the Algebraic form of this condition.

Algebraic Expression

Let x is the age of Tina.

Marta's age = 3x

3x + x = 40

4x = 40

4x - 40 = 0

Answer

4x - 40 = 0

If you want to solve the age of Marta and Tina.

4x = 40

x = 40/4

x = 10

Marta = 3x

Marta = 3(10)

Marta = 30

Tina is 10 years old and Marta is 30 years old.
