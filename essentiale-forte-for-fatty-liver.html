1. what is the main cause of fatty liver?​


Causes of fatty liver disease. Eating excess calories causes fat to build up in the liver. When the liver does not process and break down fats as it normally should, too much fat will accumulate. People tend to develop fatty liver if they have certain other conditions, such as obesity, diabetes or high triglycerides.

8. compare fatty acid synthesis with fatty acid degration​


Answer:

(1) Fatty acid synthesis takes place in the cytosol whereas the degradation takes place primarily in the mitochondrial matrix. (2) All the intermediates in fatty acid synthesis are linked to the-SH group of acyl carrier protein (ACP) but in the degradative pathway the intermediates are linked to -SH group of coenzyme-A.

12. saturated fatty acids and unsaturated fatty acids​


Answer:

Saturated fatty acids lack double bonds between the individual carbon atoms, while in unsaturated fatty acids there is at least one double bond in the fatty acid chain. Saturated fats tend to be solid at room temperature and from animal sources, while unsaturated fats are usually liquid and from plant sources.
