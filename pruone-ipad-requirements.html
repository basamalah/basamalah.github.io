2. The usual price of an iPad Mini was $1720. At a IT fair, Iris bought the iPad Mini at a discount of 30%. How much did she pay for the iPad Mini?


Answer:

$1,204

Step-by-step explanation:

1720 (usual price)

30 (percent discount)

516 (money discount)

1720×.30=516

1720-516=1,204

or

1720=100%

100%-30%=70%

1720×.70=1,204
