2. ano ang kahulugan ng pick up lines


A pick-up line or chat-up line is a conversation opener with the intent of engaging an unfamiliar person for romance, dating or even sometimes to belittle somebody. Overt and sometimes humorous displays of romantic interest, pick-up lines advertise the wit of their speakers to their target listeners.
