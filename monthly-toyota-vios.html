17. How much should you invest today to provide your only son a brabd new Toyota car worth P850,000 as a grafuation gift 10 years from now if money is worth 15% compounded monthly ?​


Answer:

P = ₱191,432.25

Step-by-step explanation:

Given

[tex]A = 850000\\t = 10\\r = 0.15\\n = 12[/tex]

Asked

[tex]P = ?\\[/tex]

Formula

[tex]P = \frac{A}{(1 + \frac{r}{n})^{nt}}[/tex]

Solution

[tex]P = \frac{A}{(1 + \frac{r}{n})^{nt}}\\\\P = \frac{850000}{(1 + \frac{0.15}{12})^{(12)(10)}}\\\\P = \frac{850000}{(1 + \frac{0.15}{12})^{120}}\\\\P = \frac{850000}{(1 + 0.0125)^{120}}\\\\P = \frac{850000}{(1.0125)^{120}}\\\\P = \frac{850000}{4.44021322894}\\\\P = 191432.247997\\\\P = 191432.25[/tex]

Final Answer

P = ₱191,432.25

The principal investment required to get a total amount of ₱850,000.00 from compound interest at a rate of 15% per year compounded 12 times per year over 10 years is ₱191,432.25.
