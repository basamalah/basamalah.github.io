26. 2.At the Ideal Bazaar, 3 handkerchiefs are sold for p 56.25. The same kind of handkerchiefsare sold at P 186.00 per dozen at ABC store. How much cheaper does a handkerchief issold at ABC store than at Ideal Bazaar?​


Answer:

The cheaper is at the ABC store.The difference of the price is P3.25

Step-by-step explanation:

Ideal Bazaar:

[tex]56.25 \div 3 = 18.75[/tex]

Thus, P18.75 per handkerchief in The Ideal Bazaar.

=========================================

ABC Store:

Dozen = 12

[tex]186 \div 12 = 15.5[/tex]

Thus, P15.50 per handkerchief in The ABC Store.

=========================================

Operation to be used: Subtraction

How much cheaper?

[tex]18.75 - 15.5 = 3.25[/tex]
