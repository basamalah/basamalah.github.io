14. the median of isosceles trapezoid CAFE is 22 .Given CE // AF and CE = 2× and AF = ×+2 , what is the value of X?​


Answer:

To solve this problem, we need to use the property of the median of an isosceles trapezoid which states that the median is equal to the average of the two parallel sides.

Let x be the value of CE, then AF is equal to x+2.

The formula for the median of an isosceles trapezoid is:

Median = (base1 + base2) / 2

Substituting the values we know, we get:

22 = (x + x+2) / 2

Multiplying both sides by 2, we get:

44 = 2x + 2

Simplifying the equation, we get:

2x = 42

x = 21

Therefore, CE is equal to 21, and AF is equal to 23.

Checking our answer, we can confirm that the median is indeed equal to 22:

Median = (CE + AF) / 2 = (21 + 23) / 2 = 22

So the value of X is 21.
