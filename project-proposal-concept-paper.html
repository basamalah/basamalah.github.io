1. What is the difference of a concept paper and project proposal


Answer:

The concept paper is the initial writing step of the research project in which you gather your initial thoughts. The research proposal includes an extensive outline of what will be researched, the methods of research, the possible problems involved with the research, and a literature review of what is already known.
