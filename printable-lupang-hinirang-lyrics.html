7. Who composed the lyrics of lupang hinirang?​


Answer:

Julián Felipe

Explaination:

'Chosen Land'), originally known in Spanish as "Marcha Nacional Filipina", is the national anthem of the Philippines. Its music was composed by Julián Felipe in 1898, and the lyrics (words) were adapted from the Spanish poem Filipinas, which was written by Filipino soldier José Palma y Velásquez a year later.

9. who composed the lyrics of Lupang Hinirang?​


Answer:

Julian Felipe

Explanation:

Answer:

Julián Felipe

Explanation:

"Lupang Hinirang", lit. Chosen Land; originally titled in Spanish as the Marcha Nacional Filipina ("Philippine National March"), is the national anthem of the Philippines. Its music was composed in 1898 by Julián Felipe, and the lyrics were adapted from the Spanish poem Filipinas, written by José Palma in 1899.
