1. Why is it entitled the transparent sun? a story by linda ty-casper.


THE TRANSPARENT SUN

by Linda Ty-Casper

EXPLANATION OF THE TITLEThe photosphere is about 500 kilometers thick because the Sun's atmosphere changes from transparent to opaque in a few hundred kilometers. When we talk about the size of the Sun, we usually mean the area surrounded by the photosphere.

This would then explain that there are power struggles in the story. When a sun is transparent, you see more of the sunspots.

In the story, the sun represents the family history of Don Julio. He wasn't really a descendant of Don Marciano as he claimed to be. And in his family history, he has spots that can be seen through its transparent nature.

The story denotes that everything is more than meets the eye.

SUMMARY OF THE TRANSPARENT SUN

Sepa, Don Julio's cousin visted his house to retrieve a piece of jewerly pawned to them. However, Don Julio's young wife does not want to give it back.

Sepa and Don Julio had a conversation about the past and how it is very important for Sepa to get that necklace back.

LINDA TY-CASPERLinda Ty-Casper is a well-known Filipino author. Belinda Ty was born in Manila, Philippines in 1931. Her father worked for the Philippine National Railways, while her mother was a teacher and textbook author. Her grandmother told her stories about the Philippine struggle for independence, which she later incorporated into her novels. She graduated from the University of the Philippines and Harvard Law School. However, erroneous and biased statements in books at Widener Library transformed her into an advocate of Filipinos' right to self-definition/determination through faithfully researched historical fiction.

6. Carla is 21 years older than her daughter, Linda. The sum of their ages is 37.How old is Linda?


Let Carla's age be C, and Linda's age L. "Carla is 21 years older than Linda" can be represented as

C = 21 + L

"The sum of their ages is 37" can be represented as

C + L = 37

We can put these two equations into a system of linear equations in two variables

C = 21 + L
C + L = 37

(21 + L) + L = 37
21 + 2L = 37
2L = 16
L = 8

Therefore Linda is 8 years old.

If also solving for Carla's age,

C + 8 = 37
C = 29

Therefore Carla is 29 years old.Linda: x

x = 8

Required no. of ways = No. of ways of selecting 1 black ball and 2 non-black balls (White and Red) + No. of ways of selecting 2 black balls and 1 non-black balls (White and Red) + No. of ways of selecting 3 black balls and 0 non-black balls (White and Red).

Required number of ways = Number of ways of selecting 3 balls out of given 9 balls (without any restriction) - Number of ways of selecting 3 balls when no black ball is to be selected.
