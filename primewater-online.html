27. Limewater chemically is an aqueous solution of


Limewater is the common name for a dilute aqueous solution of calcium hydroxide. Calcium hydroxide, Ca(OH)2, is sparsely soluble at room temperature in water (1.5 g/L at 25 °C). "Pure" (i.e. less than or fully saturated) limewater is clear and colorless, with a slight earthy smell and an astringent/bitter taste.

29. features that exclusive to primates​


Answer:

Some features exclusive to primates are:

- lack of strong specialization in structure- prehensile hands and feet, usually with opposable thumbs and great toes- flattened nails instead of claws on the digits- acute vision with some degree of binocular vision- relatively large brain exhibiting a degree of cortical folding- prolonged postnatal dependency
