22. please check the grammar:1)Frank would have been jailed if he was not rescued by his brother. is this correct example of third conditional sentence?​


Answer:

Yes. It is an example of third conditional sentence.

Explanation:

These sentences are truly hypothetical and unreal, because it is now too late for the condition or its result to exist.

To make a sentence in the third conditional, we use, If + past perfect, would/wouldn't have + past participle. If you had told me about the meeting, I would have come. If you had told me about the meeting, I wouldn't have missed it.
