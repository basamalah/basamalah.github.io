5. Duties and responsibilities of senators in the philippines


Senate of the Philippines

The Senate of the Philippines is also known as the upper house (House of Representatives; lower houe) of the legislative branch of the government. The Senate is made up of 24 senators that are given 6 years each term (maximum of two terms only) and 12 are voted every 3 years.

Duties and ResponsibilitiesEvery bill or resolutions have to be discussed by the senate before passing it for the president's signature.They may propose ammendments about money bills that some from the House of Representatives.They approve and denounce treaties.Only them has the power to try or convict an impeached official.
