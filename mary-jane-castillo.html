29. marijuana, also know as “mary jane” is a narcotic drugs?​


Answer:

Marijuana is a psychoactive drug that is derived from the cannabis plant. It is often used recreationally for its mind-altering effects, and also has some medicinal applications. While marijuana is classified as a Schedule I controlled substance in the United States, which means that it is considered to have a high potential for abuse and no currently accepted medical use, it is not technically considered a narcotic drug. Narcotic drugs, on the other hand, are a specific class of drugs that are used to treat pain and include opioids like morphine, codeine, and fentanyl.
