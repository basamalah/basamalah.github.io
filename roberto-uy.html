14. Emmanuel is 8 years older than roberto. two years ago emmanuel was twice as old as roberto. how old are they now?


Solution:

Let (x) be Roberto's age
and (x + 8) be Emmanuel's

2(x - 2) = (x + 8) - 2
2x - 4 = x + 6
x = 10

Substitute:

x = 10
x + 8 = 10 + 8 = 18

Answer: Emmanuel is 18 years old while Roberto is 10 years old.
