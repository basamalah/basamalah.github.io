18. Choose 2 statements from the CREED and explain why these phrases are considered as your personal identity as children of God.


1. I believe in God the Father Almighty

This statement concludes that as children of God, we are tasked to believe in Him as well as in His plans for us.

2. I believe in Jesus Christ, His only Son, our Lord.

For God so loved the world, He gave us His only Son, and that is Jesus. This statement is my identity as children of God because it implies that God loved the world (which includes us, His children), so much that He blessed us with Jesus Christ, to save us from our sins.
