7. Base on the story of Ariel S. Tabag entitled “Voice Tape” list atleast 2 local practices in wakes that were describe in the story. Brainliest ko makasagot. Report ko yung mali.


Answer:

One culture that can be reflected from the story is the practice of wakes which are done by Filipinos.

The corpse is put inside a coffin where it will be displayed usually for three to seven days. Filipinos mourn for their loved ones and honor those who die for they believe that afterlife exists. Most Filipinos believe in superstitions especially during funerals. As stated by Dante, old women prohibited sweeping during funeral for anyone who disobeys would have a lot of lice. Another superstition is that they should cut the chicken’s head to avoid deaths from their family. They also practice moving the coffin with the feet of the person at the front.

16. Directions: Solve the given problem. - 1. Mr. Ariel is planning to build a gazebo. At what angle should he connect the trusses to frame the roof to form a regular hexagon? a. Given: n=_____b. Solution: Formula: S = (n - 2) 180° S = L-2) 180 S = (4) S =_____Formula to find the angle to connect the trusses = S/6 c. Mr. Ariel should connect the trusses at______degrees​


Answer:

a. Given: n=6

b. Solution:

Formula: S = (n - 2) 180°

S = (6-2) 180

S = (4)180

S = 720°

Formula to find the angle to connect the trusses = S/6

=720°/6

=120°

c. Mr. Ariel should connect the trusses at 120° degrees
