25. What makes the water swirl?​


Answer:

Things on the surface of the object are more likely to spiral in a certain direction due to the influence of the Coriolis force. The water in your sink, along with everything else on the surface of the planet, is subject to the influence of the Coriolis force, which is caused by the rotation of the world.

Explanation:

Answer:

This effect accounts for the spinning ice skater as well as the water swirling down the drain. As the water moves around, gravity pulls it closer to the drain. Any particular drop of water gets closer to the drain just as the ball got closer to the tube. As that distance decreases, the “size” in the equation decreases.
