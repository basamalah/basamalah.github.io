24. Write an essay about this topic: "Why should we not fear death?​"​


Death is a natural process that happens to everyone. It is not something to be feared, but something to be welcomed. Death is the end of one life and the beginning of another. It is the end of pain and suffering, the end of regrets and the beginning of a new journey.

There are many reasons to not fear death. First of all, death is the end of all pain and suffering. If we are killed in an accident, our pain will be over. If we die of old age, we will no longer experience the pain of being ailing. Death is the ultimate release from pain and suffering.

Secondly, death is the end of our physical body. This body is temporary, and after death it will be gone forever. We will no longer be burdened by physical limitations, and we will be able to enter into the eternal life that is waiting for us.

Finally, death is the end of our individual life. We will no longer be responsible for our own actions, and we will be joined with other humans and animals in the eternal life of heaven or hell.

So, why should we not fear death? Death is the end of our pain and suffering, the end of our physical body and the end of our individual life. These are all blessings, and we should not be afraid of them.
