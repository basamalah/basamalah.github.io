15. erupted for a long period of time but is still considered an active volcano?A. Mt. Iraya B. Mt. Kanlaon C. Mt. Mayon D. Mt. Taal​


Answer:

Mount Iraya

Mount Iraya is a volcano that has erupted more than 500 years ago (in 1454), but PHIVOLCS still consider it as an active volcano. This is because there are two conditions that PHIVOLCS look at when considering if a volcano is active: either the volcano should have erupted within the last 600 years, or, the geological samples from the volcano should yield an age of 10,000 years. In the case of Mount Iraya, the volcano was able to suffice the first condition.

Explanation:

Mount Kanlaon, Mayon Volcano, and Taal Volcano are all active volcanoes, but they have recent eruption events that took place in 2017, 2018, and 2020, respectively.
