Answer:

WHAT IS ASK?

• The total weight of the three pupils.

WHAT ARE THE GIVEN?

• 32.58 kilograms

• 33.56 kilograms

• 35.86 kilograms

WHAT ARE THE OPERATION USE?

• addition

WHAT IS THE NUMBER SENTENCE?

• 32.58 kg + 33.56 kg + 35.86 kg = N

WHAT IS THE SOLUTION?

• 32.58 + 33.56 = 66.14 kg

• 66.14 + 35.86 = 102 kg

WHAT IS THE ANSWER?

• The total weight of the three pupils is 102 kilograms.
