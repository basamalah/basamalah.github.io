25. joseph changed the color of his jeepney he consumed 3 1/4 liters of blue paint and 2 2/3 liters of white paint how many liters of paint did joseph use ehat is asked in the problem​


Answer:

5 11/12 liters of paint

Step-by-step explanation:

I. Add the whole numbers:

3+2=5

II. Find the Least Common Denominator (LCD) of the proper fractions 1/4 & 2/3:

4 is factorable to 2x2 while 3 can no longer be factored so

2x2x3=12

LCD=12

III. Change the proper fractions to similar fractions (with the same denominator):

Divide the denominators (4 & 3) with the LCD so

12÷4=3 & 12÷3=4

Then multiply the quotient to the numerators (1 & 2) so

3x1=3 & 4x2=8

IV. Add the final similar fractions which are:

3/12 + 8/12 and the answer to that is 11/12

IV. Add both the sum of the whole numbers from earlier and the sum of the similar fractions:

5 + 11/12= 5 11/12

Firstly, modern jeepneys have a more streamlined and modern design compared to traditional jeepneys, which have a more boxy and utilitarian appearance. Modern jeepneys are typically larger, with more seating capacity, and are designed to be more comfortable for passengers.
