10. What is the role of designated public attorneys under this law?​


Answer: The role of designated public attorneys is crucial in ensuring that PWDs have access to justice and are able to assert their rights under the law. By providing legal representation and assistance to PWDs, these attorneys help to level the playing field and to address historical and structural inequality that have prevented PWDs from enjoying their rights and privileges. They also help to promote the inclusion and participation of PWDs in all aspects of society, which is essential for their overall well-being and the development of an equitable and just society.

12. region vii - central visayas


Answer:

Central Visayas is an administrative region in the Philippines, numerically designated as Region VII. It consists of four provinces: and three highly urbanized cities: Cebu City, Lapu-Lapu, and Mandaue. Major islands are the eponymous Cebu, Bohol, and Siquijor, together with the eastern part of Negros.
