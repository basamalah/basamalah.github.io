17. Brief intro of Chess​


Brief Introduction to Chess

Chess is one of the oldest and most popular board games worldwide. It is a game played by two opponents on a board with 64 alternating black (or dark-colored) and white squares. This popular board game is believed to have begun in India more than a thousand years ago. However, others say chess was invented in China around 200 B.C. to represent a battle happening at that time.

Objective and Elements of Chess

The goal of this game is to capture the 'King' of the other player. Below are some of the basic elements of chess:

One of the players will be White and the other will be Black.Each player has 16 characters or pieces with different functions. One of each group is the king that is to be protected.Players will make moves alternately.Each move the characters make is based on a set of rules on how they can move.Each player will then attempt to put the opponent's "King" into a "checkmate" position wherein the King won't be able to avoid capture.

Good morning/afternoon everyone,

I am here today to talk to you about the importance of recycling and how it can benefit not just us but the environment as well. Our world is facing an environmental crisis, and it is up to us to take action and do our part. Waste management is a significant problem that requires a multi-faceted solution, and recycling is one of the key solutions that can help us to sustainably manage our waste before it impacts the environment negatively.

Body:

Recycling is a simple but an important activity that we can all take part in. Recycling refers to the process of reusing materials that would otherwise end up in the landfill or oceans, creating pollution in the environment. By recycling, we can reduce the amount of waste produced and, at the same time, conserve natural resources. Recycling works by collecting items that can be recycled and processing them to make new products. These products are then converted back into raw materials that can be made into new items instead of extracting new materials from the environment.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, recycling is a necessary solution that can help us sustainably manage our waste and protect the environment. Recycling is not just about throwing waste into specific bins but also about embracing a mindset that reduces, reuses and recycles. We should all take part in recycling, and it begins with simple practices such as using reusable products, limiting the use of single-use plastics, and disposing of waste properly. If we all take part and make a concerted effort, we can work towards creating a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable world for ourselves and future generations. Thank you for listening.

27. Intro of Hijacking flight


Answer:

Airplane hijacking, also known as aircraft piracy, is the illegal seizure of an aircraft by an individual or a group of individuals. The hijackers use force or threat of force to take control of the plane, usually with the intent to use it as a bargaining chip to achieve their political or personal demands. Hijacking flights can be a frightening and dangerous experience for passengers, crew members, and even the hijackers themselves. The hijacking of commercial flights became a significant global security issue in the late 20th century, leading to the establishment of increased security measures and protocols to prevent and respond to hijackings.
