10. MATH CHALLENGE AGONSA: An ice cream machine is being sold by a couple since they wanted to venture into another business. They were trying to sell it for 10% off of the marked price but no one wanted to buy it. Then they tried to sell for 20% of the first sale price still no one bought the machine Finally, they offered it for 25% off the second sale price when someone bought it for P32.400.00. What was the original price of the ice cream machine? 204 m ​


Answer:

P43,200.00

Step-by-step explanation:

A: Asked

Find the original price of the ice cream machine.

G: Given

The machine was first sold at 10% off but nobody bought it.

The machine was then sold at 20% off the first sale price but still nobody bought it.

The machine was sold at 25% off the second sale price of P32,400.00.

O: Operation

To solve this problem, we need to work backwards from the final sale price to the original price. We can use the following formula:

Original price = Final price ÷ (1 - Discount rate)

N: Unknown

The original price of the ice cream machine.

S: Solution

The machine was sold at 25% off the second sale price of P32,400.00, so the second sale price was 75% of the original price.

Using the formula, we can write:

x = 32,400.00 ÷ 0.75

x = 43,200.00

Therefore, the original price of the ice cream machine was P43,200.00.

15. An ice cream machine is being sold by a couple since they wanted to venture into another business. They were trying to sell it for 10% off of the marked price but no one wanted to buy it. Then they tried to sell for 20% of the first sale price still no one bought the machine. Finally, they offered it for 25% off the second sale price when someone bought it for P32,400.00. What was the original price of the ice cream machine?​


Answer:

The original price of the ice cream machine was, 60,000 pesos

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's denote the original price of the ice cream machine as x.

According to the problem, the couple tried to sell it for 10% off the marked price, which means they were trying to sell it for 0.9x. However, no one wanted to buy it at that price.

Then they tried to sell it for 20% off the first sale price, which is equivalent to selling it for 0.8(0.9x) = 0.72x. Again, no one bought it.

Finally, they offered it for 25% off the second sale price, which means the machine was sold for 0.75(0.72x) = 0.54x = P32,400.00.

To find x, we can solve for it:

0.54x = P32,400.00

x = P32,400.00 / 0.54

x = P60,000.00

Therefore, the original price of the ice cream machine was P60,000.00.
