Is there a minus count or a plus count? 2. Hotel XYZ had 88% occupancy out of 250 rooms last night. There were no Out of Order rooms last night. Of all the guests that were checked-in, 220 are due for check out today, and an expected 190 reservations will come today. How many rooms are committed for today? And how many rooms are still available for sale (if there are any)?


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving word problem about mathematical operation.

Problem 1Given

450 room hotel had 385 rooms occupied last night

317 are due for check out today

expecting 295 arrivals for today

Problem

Compute for room availability.

Solution

450 - 385 + 317 - 295

= 65 + 317 - 295

= 382 - 295

= 87

Answer

87 rooms

Problem 2Given

88% occupancy out of 250 rooms last night

220 are due for check out today

expected 190 reservations will come today

Problem

How many rooms are committed for today?

How many rooms are still available for sale?

Solution

88% of 250 = 220, total occupied

220-220 = 0, total occupied after check out

0 + 190 = 190, total occupied after reservation

250 - 190 = 60, still available

Answer

190 rooms are committed for today

60 rooms are still available for sale
