Problem:

Sam weighs 495 N. She climbs a flight of stairs 6.0 m high going to the second floor of the two-storey house. What is Sam's potential energy at the second floor? Going down the  stairs, Sam stops 2.0 m from the second floor. What is the change in her PE?

Note:

The formula for the Potential Energy is ( P.E. = m × g × h ) or ( P.E. = F × h ). The gravitational acceleration on Earth is 9.8 m/s² .

Given Data:

F ( Force ) = 495 kg ·m/s² or  495 N

h ( Height ) = 6 m

P.E. ( Potential Energy ) = ? unknown

Solution:

The formula for the Potential Energy is (  P.E. = F × h ).

P.E. = F × h

      = 495 kg ·m/s² × 6 m

      = 2,970 kg·m²/s²

      = 2,970 J

Answer:

The potential energy is 2,970 kg·m²/s² or  2,970 J

Answer:

Process:

1. Meet with the couple to discuss their needs and preferences for the house. Take note of their ideal layout and number of rooms.

2. Sketch a floor plan of the house based on the couple's preferences. This should include dimensions for each room and common areas.

3. Review the floor plan with the couple and make any necessary revisions.

4. Estimate the cost of building the house based on the size and materials required. This should include an itemized breakdown of costs for each section of the house including labor.

5. Present the floor plan and cost estimate to the couple and explain the breakdown of costs.

6. Discuss any options for reducing costs or alternative materials that could be used to lower the overall expense.

7. Review and finalize the floor plan and cost estimate with the couple.

8. Begin construction of the house according to the agreed upon plan and budget.

20. Is mezzanine applicable for two storey house?


Answer

A Mezzanine can be built in any structure as long as it doesn't violate the National Building Code of the Philippines.

Explanation

According to the National Building Code of the Philippines (NBCP) Republic Act (RA) 6541, Mezzanine floors shall have a clear ceiling height not less than 1.80 meters above and below it. Meaning, a floor should have a ceiling height of at least 3.70 to 4 meters to build a Mezzanine following Philippine standards.

Additional Information

A Mezzanine is an open space platform between the floor and the ceiling. It is usually seen in industrial structures, warehouses, malls, and modern townhouses. This is because a Mezzanine can help add more space on a floor level.
