3. How many career stages do the philippine professional standards for teachers have?


Beginning, proficient, highly-proficient, and distinguished are the four levels of the Philippine professional standard for teachers.

    To ensure that teachers are adequately prepared to implement the K–12 Program, the PPST shall serve as the foundation for all teacher learning and development initiatives. It can also be applied to the hiring and advancement of teachers.

Candidates for Beginning Teachers (Career Stage 1) must have completed the Recognized qualifications for admittance into the teaching profession. Teachers are encouraged to advance to Career Stage 2 during the next two to three years.Professional independence is exhibited by proficient teachers (Career Stage 2) in the use of skills essential to the teaching and learning process. The standards that should be met by all teachers by the end of the first two stages are demonstrated in this level.Highly proficient educators (Career Stage 3) routinely demonstrate demonstrate a high level of proficiency in their teaching methods. Those are outstanding practitioners who collaborate and mentor one another. The point at which Master Teachers are anticipated to be is this.Distinguished teachers (Career Stage 4) represent the highest criteria for instruction based on international best practice. They are acknowledged for their contributions to the profession, leadership in education, and those who start partnerships and collaborations.

    The key objective of identifying a teacher's professional development stages is to help them overcome the challenges they face in their development, which will ultimately improve their performance in the classroom and the standard of education.

16. bakit mahalaga ang career path


Career Path

Ang career path ay tumutukoy sa pag-unlad ng isang propesyunal o layunin upang maabot ang nais na posisyon. Ang bawat tao ay naghahangad na umunlad sa larangan na pinagdadalubhasaan at pinagtatrabahuhan. Isa ang career path na maituturing na layunin upang mapalago ang kasanayan ng isang tao.

Bakit mahalaga ang career path?Nagsisilbi itong gabay upang mabigyan ng tuon ng isang tao ang nais niyang patunguhan sa isang organisayon.Tumutulong ito upang mapaunlad ang mga kasanayan ng isang propesyunal; isa sa halimbawa nito ay ang pagkuha ng isang tao ng pagsasanay na makadaragdag sa pagpapaunlad ng kakayahan upang maabot ang naisin sa napiling larangan.Nagbibigay kabuluhan upang ang isang propesyunal ay mapanatili ang sigasig at kaalaman sa pagbibigay serbisyo sa napiling karera.Nagbibigay hikayat sa bawat manggagawa na pagbutihin ang trabaho sa mas malikhaing pamamaraan.

CAREER PATH

Ang career path ay isang gabay kung anong larangan o karera ang iyong tutunguhin na gustong marating at maabot. Ito ang nagsisilbing daan tungo sa pagabot ng mga pangarap at mithiin sa buhay.  Ito rin ay nagsisilbing paraan tungo sa paglago at pagpapaunlad ng sariling kakayahan at kasanayan.

Bakit mahalaga ang career path?

Kahalagahan ng Career Path

Nagsisilbing motibasyon ng bawat isa patungo sa buhay na pinapangarap at inaasam niya. Nagdadala sa isang tao sa daan na nais niyang tahakin. Tumutulong upang maging masigasig at masikap sa isang bagay na pinapangarap. Naguudyok sa isang tao o empleyado upang maging matiyaga sa kanyang layunin sa tinahak na karera. Naghihikayat sa isang tao o sa isang empleyado na pagbutihin pa ang kanyang ginagawa o kanyang napiling karera. Naghahanda sa isang mangagawa o empleyado upang mas mapaunlad pa ang kasanayan at kakayahan.
