7. Activity: Just Complete Me! Complete the two-column proof. Given: Parallelogram CURE with diagonals CR and UE Prove: CR and UE bisect each other. Proof: Statements 1. 2. CU | ER; CE UR 3. CU ER 4.LCUE= ZREU 5. LCHULRHE 6. ACHU ARHE 7. CH = RH;EH = UH 8. CR and UE bisect each other. ​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

The two-column proof is a common proof technique used in mathematics to prove that two lines are parallel.

This two-column proof proves the statement "CR and UE bisect each other" by showing that the two lines are congruent (the same length) and that their adjacent sides are in pairs of congruence (the same length).
