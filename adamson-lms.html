Answer:

C. KL = LM

Step-by-step explanation:

Since L is the midpoint of KM, it means that KL is equal to LM in length, and they are both half the length of KM. Therefore, option C is true:

C. KL = LM

Option A is not necessarily true, as we don't know the exact lengths of KL and KM or LM and KM to compare them.

Option B is also not necessarily true, as we don't know the exact values of LM and KM to determine their difference.

Option D is not true, as 2KM - KL + LM is equivalent to KM + (KM - KL + LM), which does not provide any information about the distances among K, L, and M.

Therefore, the answer is C. KL = LM.
