21. 1. How much distance will be covered in 6.25 hrs at a speed of 55 km/hr?Solution:2. Calculate the time needed to cover a distance 25 km at a speed of a km/hr?Solution:3. In fun run, the fastest runner ran 5km for 28mins. What was the average speed of the runner?4. Rey drives his car of a speed of 80 km/hr. How far will he cover in 2hrs and 15mins?Solution:​


Answer:

1. The distance covered in 6.25 hrs at a speed of 55 km/hr is 343.75 km.
2. The time needed to cover a distance of 25 km at a speed of a km/hr is 25/a hrs
3. The average speed of the runner was 10.70 km/hr.

4. Rey will cover a distance of 180 km in 2 hours and 15 minutes at a speed of 80 km/hr.

Step-by-step explanation:

1. To calculate the distance covered, we can use the formula:

Distance = Speed × Time

Plugging in the given values, we get:

Distance = 55 km/hr × 6.25 hrs = 343.75 km

Therefore, the distance covered in 6.25 hrs at a speed of 55 km/hr is 343.75 km.

2. To calculate the time needed, we can use the formula:

Time = Distance ÷ Speed

Plugging in the given values, we get:

Time = 25 km ÷ a km/hr = 25/a hrs

Therefore, the time needed to cover a distance of 25 km at a speed of a km/hr is 25/a hrs.

3. To calculate the average speed, we can use the formula:

Average speed = Distance ÷ Time

We are given that the fastest runner ran 5 km in 28 minutes, which is 28/60 = 0.467 hours. Plugging in the given values, we get:

Average speed = 5 km ÷ 0.467 hrs = 10.70 km/hr

Therefore, the average speed of the runner was 10.70 km/hr.

4. To calculate the distance covered, we can use the formula:

Distance = Speed × Time

We are given that Rey drives his car at a speed of 80 km/hr and for a time of 2 hours and 15 minutes, which is 2.25 hours. Plugging in the given values, we get:

Distance = 80 km/hr × 2.25 hrs = 180 km

Therefore, Rey will cover a distance of 180 km in 2 hours and 15 minutes at a speed of 80 km/hr.
