12. A 35kg beer keg is called up a 5m long plank, which makes a 30° incline to the ground.What is the GPE of the keg at the top?Write the solution using this formula: GPE=mgh,kg×10ms×m​


Problem :

A 35kg beer keg is called up a 5m long plank, which makes a 30° incline to the ground.What is the GPE of the keg at the top?

Given Data :

mass(m) = 35kggravational field(g) = 10ms^-2 vertical height(h) = 2.5m

Solution :

A 30° incline plane with a hypotenuse of 5m has a vertical height given by:

5.0 × sin30° = 2.5m

Using The GPE or the gravitational potential energy formula :

GPE = mgh

= 35kg × 10ms^-2 × 2.5m

= 875J

Answer :

875J
