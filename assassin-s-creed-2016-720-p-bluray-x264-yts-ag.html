18. 720 is what percent of 3600 = R =P/B​


To find the percentage that 720 is of 3600, we can use the formula:

R = (P/B) x 100%

where R is the percentage, P is the part, and B is the whole.

Substituting the given values, we get:

R = (720/3600) x 100%

R = 0.2 x 100%

R = 20%

Therefore, 720 is 20% of 3600.
