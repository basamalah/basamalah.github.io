HOW MUCH IS JOHN PAUL'S ALLOWANCE?

Answer : P450

Solution:

Let 3x be the age of John Paul allowance

Total amount of their allowance is P600

The equation can be represented like this,

3x+x = 600   add the same terms

4x=600  divide both side with number 4 to retain x

4x/4=600/4

x=150

P150 is the allowance of Daniel

Since John Paul's allowance is represented as this 3x let us multiple the allowance of Daniel which P150(which represented by x) to 3, so the result is P450

Let us Substitute their allowance to our equation to check the answer is correct.

3x+x=600

3(150)+150=600

450+150=600

600=600
