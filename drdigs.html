30. almost the same with pass but the player use different part of their body part such as foot hand and arms and sometimes legs just to save the balla.blockingb.settingsc.serving d.digging​


Answer:

D.

Explanation:

The volleyball dig can keep your team in the game and is a key skill to develop. When the ball is attacked by your opponent, your job is to keep the ball from hitting the floor. A dig is a pass of a hard-driven ball from the other team. Like a pass, your arm position and platform remain the same. The difference is that the ball is coming from a high point above the net and hit in a downward trajectory. When passing the ball is coming from 30 feet away and usually below the height of the net. You must react quickly and adjust accordingly.
