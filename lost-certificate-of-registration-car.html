Answer:

It is generally not allowed to drive a motorcycle on a public road pending the release of the certificate of registration. In most cases, the certificate of registration is required to legally operate a motorcycle on a public road. The certificate of registration serves as proof of ownership and is required to be displayed on the motorcycle at all times when it is being operated on a public road.

If you are caught driving a motorcycle on a public road without a valid certificate of registration, you may be subject to fines and other penalties. It is important to ensure that your motorcycle is properly registered and that you have the necessary documents, including the certificate of registration, before operating it on a public road.

27. Patrick drove his car without registration and license consequences


The consequence for Patrick who drives his car without registration and license is to get punished.

When driving a motorized vehicle, including a car, all drivers must have a vehicle registration and driving license. This is a mandatory regulation in every country because it is a sign that we are able and allowed to drive. If it is not fulfilled, then the driver will get consequences in the form of punishment.

The punishment that will be received will differ in each country depending on what has been determined by law. The punishment that can be obtained are fines, vehicle detention, and if the driver causes an accident that causes victims, some countries also consider it a violation of criminal law and provide detention in prison.
