Research design

The research design is intended to provide an appropriate framework for a study. A very significant decision in research design process is the choice to be made regarding research approach since it determines how relevant information for a study will be obtained; however, the research design process involves many interrelated decisions [1].

This study employed a mixed type of methods. The first part of the study consisted of a series of well-structured questionnaires (for management, employee’s representatives, and technician of industries) and semi-structured interviews with key stakeholders (government bodies, ministries, and industries) in participating organizations. The other design used is an interview of employees to know how they feel about safety and health of their workplace, and field observation at the selected industrial sites was undertaken.

Hence, this study employs a descriptive research design to agree on the effects of occupational safety and health management system on employee health, safety, and property damage for selected manufacturing industries. Saunders et al. [2] and Miller [3] say that descriptive research portrays an accurate profile of persons, events, or situations. This design offers to the researchers a profile of described relevant aspects of the phenomena of interest from an individual, organizational, and industry-oriented perspective. Therefore, this research design enabled the researchers to gather data from a wide range of respondents on the impact of safety and health on manufacturing industries in Ethiopia. 12. pa tulong po ano po pwedeng title sa research yun po sanang may subject of the study, population involved, at locale of the study​


Answer:

Para sa isang pananaliksik na mayroong subject ng pag-aaralan, populasyon, at lokasyon ng pag-aaral, maaari mong gamitin ang mga sumusunod na ideya para sa pamagat ng iyong pananaliksik:

"Exploring the Health Behaviors of High School Students in Metro Manila: A Mixed-Methods Study" - Sa pamamagitan ng pamagat na ito, nagbibigay ka ng impormasyon tungkol sa subject ng pag-aaralan (mga high school students), populasyon (mga residente ng Metro Manila), at lokasyon ng pag-aaral (Metro Manila). Ang pagkakaroon ng "mixed-methods" na pananaliksik ay nagpapakita ng paggamit ng dalawang uri ng datos, tulad ng kwantitatibong at kwalitatibong datos, upang mas malalim na maunawaan ang mga health behaviors ng mga high school students.

"A Study on the Effectiveness of Mindfulness Meditation among Office Workers in Makati City" - Sa pamamagitan ng pamagat na ito, nagbibigay ka ng impormasyon tungkol sa subject ng pag-aaralan (mga office workers), populasyon (mga residente ng Makati City), at lokasyon ng pag-aaral (Makati City). Ang pagkakaroon ng "effectiveness" ay nagpapahiwatig ng isang eksperimento o pagsubok upang matukoy kung gaano kahusay ang mindfulness meditation sa pagpapabuti ng kalagayan ng mga office workers.

"Exploring the Factors Influencing Entrepreneurial Success among Women Entrepreneurs in Cebu City" - Sa pamamagitan ng pamagat na ito, nagbibigay ka ng impormasyon tungkol sa subject ng pag-aaralan (women entrepreneurs), populasyon (mga residente ng Cebu City), at lokasyon ng pag-aaral (Cebu City). Ang pagkakaroon ng "factors influencing entrepreneurial success" ay nagpapakita ng isang pananaliksik na naglalayong malaman ang mga salik na nakaaapekto sa tagumpay ng mga kababaihang entrepreneurs.

Ang mga pamagat na ito ay nagbibigay ng impormasyon tungkol sa subject ng pag-aaral, populasyon, at lokasyon ng pag-aaral, at nagbibigay rin ng isang maikling pangkalahatang ideya sa layunin ng pananaliksik. Mahalaga na mapili mo ang isang pamagat na nagpapakita ng kabuuan ng iyong pananaliksik sa paraang ito upang masiguro na maipakita mo ang mga pangunahing punto ng iyong pananaliksik sa pamamagitan ng pamagat.

The population like the respondents' interviewees. ("Who"?) 4. The time period of the study during which the data are to be collected. ("When"?) Example:​

here's an example of a research title that includes all four pieces of information:

"What Factors Influence Customer Loyalty in Local Restaurants? A Study of Residents in New York City from 2021-2022."

In this title:

The subject matter or topic is "Factors that Influence Customer Loyalty in Local Restaurants"The place or locale where the research is to be conducted is "New York City"The population or respondents/interviewees are "Residents" of New York City
