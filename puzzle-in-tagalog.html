PHILIPPINE FOLK SONG  

Folk songs are the traditional music originated from different community or countries, it reflects the life of common, mostly rural areas or people within the community. The meaning of a folk song arises at the crossing point between its text and the context in which it is used because folk songs are not just a text, it is a statement, verbal and musical act.
