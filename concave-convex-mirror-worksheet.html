6. Compare the concave and convex mirror


Concave mirror reflects incident light which are parallel to the principal axis inward to a real focus which in front of the mirror. Convex mirror reflects light outward so that a virtual focus is formed by the extension of the reflected rays.

7. How can you relate a convex and concave mirrors to convex and concave lense? ​


A concave mirror converges light to a focal point. For lenses, light converges to a point for a convex lens. A convex mirror diverges light, as does a concave lens.

8. Is concave and convex mirror useful?


They are used to focus light. Unlike convex mirrors, concave mirrors show different image types depending on the distance between the object and the mirror. These mirrors are called "converging mirrors" because they tend to collect light that falls on them, refocusing parallel incoming rays toward a focus.

11. 5 applications of concave mirror and 5 applications of convex mirror provide an explanation on how the convex or concave mirrors help on each application???​


Answer:

Applications of concave mirror

Shaving mirrors. A concave mirror is commonly used in shaving mirrors because it enlarges small facial and other body hairs to be viewed clearly when the mirror is placed closer to it. Solar furnaces. Large concave mirrors are used to focus sunlight to reproduce heat in the solar furnace. Ophthalmoscope. With the help of concave mirrors in ophthalmoscopes and a light beamed directed to the eye's pupil, doctors can easily check a patient's eye as it makes the retina visible. Astronomical telescopes. Concave mirrors, about 5 diameters or more, are used as objective in astronomical telescopes.Headlights. Concave mirrors are widely used in vehicles as reflectors.

Applications of convex mirror

Sunglasses. In sunglasses, convex mirror are used to reflect sunlight and prevent it from reaching and harming the eyes.Vehicle mirrors. Convex mirrors are commonly used in vehicles since they can diverge light and can make virtual images.Magnifying glasses. The property of the convex mirrors to diverge light is the reason why convex mirrors are used to make magnifying glasses.Street light reflectors. The ability of convex mirrors to spread light over a wide area is the reason why they are used as reflectors in street lights.Security purposes. Convex mirrors are used for security purposes, particularly at ATMs, in order for the person operating the ATM currently will know if some person is approaching from the back.

Difference between the concave and convex mirror

The difference between convex and concave mirrors is: Convex mirrors have an outwardly bulged reflecting surface, and concave mirrors have an inwardly curved reflecting surface.

A converging mirror is one that is concave and has a converging character, whereas a diverging mirror is one that is convex and has a diverging nature.

The focus and center of curvature of the concave mirror are in front of the mirror, whereas the convex mirror is on the backside.

Because the focus is on the front side, the concave mirror has a positive focal length, but the convex mirror has a negative focal length because the focus is on the backside.

30. concave mirror and convex mirror


Answer:

Concave Mirror

If a hollow sphere is cut into parts and the outer surface of the cut part is painted, then it becomes a mirror with its inner surface as the reflecting surface. This type of mirror is known as a concave mirror.

Convex Mirror

If the cut part of the hollow sphere is painted from inside, then its outer surface becomes the reflecting surface. This kind of mirror is known as a convex mirror.
