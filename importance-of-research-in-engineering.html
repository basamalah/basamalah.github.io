9. what is the importance of quantitative research in engineering?


What is quantitative research?

Quantitative research is a systematic scientific study of the parts and phenomena and the causality of their relationships. The purpose of quantitative research is to develop and use mathematical models, theories and/or hypotheses related to a phenomenon.

What is engineering?

Engineering is the application of science and technology to solve human problems. This is accomplished by applying knowledge, mathematics and practical experience to design useful objects or processes. Quantitative methods are suitable for use in technical fields, such as in science and engineering.

The importance of quantitative research in engineering is quantitative research is of importance because it is very much needed in the field of research using methods and ranging from mathematical techniques, statistics, and also data analysis to collect, handle, interpret, and predict various features of the field of science and technology, for example in performance, development, and dynamics. This type of method is an applied research that is highly developed and has basic research characteristics. Quantitative methods are very well used in technical fields, such as in science and engineering. by using quantitative methods in the right and correct way is to make us successful in the field of engineering. The advantage is that we are able to understand deeply about how to collect, analyze, and communicate data in experiments. This method can measure large samples for various hypotheses, it can make it easier to reach accurate conclusions. another advantage is that it can be more credible because it can review the statistical analysis more deeply. Quantitative research can gather information about technology quickly. Researchers can gather information in real-time scenarios so that statistical analysis can take place immediately. Experiments, surveys, and interviews provide direct answers to use in data collection. Faster acquisition of resources can help make it easier to find correlations that ultimately lead to useful conclusions in engineering fields.
