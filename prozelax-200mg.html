17. The Radioactive isotope gallium-67 is used in the diagnosis of an illness,has a half life of 50 hours. if it started with 200mg of the isotope.How long would it take for the substance to be reduced to 25mg?​


Solution 1:

Given:

half-life, t½ = 50 hours

initial quantity, N₀ = 200 mg

final quantity, N(ₜ)= 25 mg

N(ₜ) = N₀ (½)^(t/t½)

t = t½ ln (Nt/N₀)/ln ½

t = 50 ln (25/200)/ln (½)

t = 150 hours

Solution 2:

time (hour) 0 50 100 150

quantity (mg) 200 100 150 25

To convert from millimeters (mm) to centimeters (cm), we need to divide the number of millimeters by 10. So 8mm is equal to 8 / 10 = 0.8cm.
