16. What is the meaning of maternal?


Maternal refers to anything that has to do with a mother. This usually refers to the time during pregnancy, childbirth or shortly after. Maternal health, for example, relates to the health of a mother during maternity. Various factors are considered to assess a mother and child's health during this time. Frequent check-up, preferably monthly or more is usually suggested to make sure that maternal health is ensured. Any maternal health issues could result in death or other lesser complications.

In the Philippines, in 2017, the maternal death ratio was at 121 deaths per 100,00. That is a huge number. The goal of the country is to reduce such deaths to 90 per 100,000 by 2022. We are still a long way off.  Hence it is important that education of mothers and would-be mothers be done at the community level. Strict adherence to maternal check-up should also be done.
