1- DNA evidence (such as hair or fingerprints)

2- Witness testimony (from those who saw the crime being committed)

3- Video footage (from surveillance cameras or cell phones)

Possible evidence of carnapping includes parts from specific makes or models of cars being found at junkyards or chop shops - these places tend to specialize in handling aftermarket car parts, so if you find anything matching your vehicle there, it's likely to be something of interest.
