3. Provide motivation and direction in life.

4. Interpret the essence of life with action.

Religion in individual life functions as a value system that contains certain norms. In general, these norms become a frame of reference in behaving and behaving so that they are in line with their religious beliefs. As a system of religious values, it has a special meaning in individual life and is maintained as a characteristic form.

It can be witnessed and even seen in real life experience that, how big is the difference between believers who live to practice their religion, and people who have no religion or are indifferent to their religion. On the face of a person who lives by holding fast to his religious beliefs, there is peace in his heart, his attitude is always calm. They do not feel restless or anxious, their behavior and actions will not harm or trouble others.

But if there are dangers that threaten, difficult life, many problems that must be faced, then panic and confusion will dominate his soul, will even culminate to the disruption of his mental health, even further, he may commit suicide or kill other people.

Judging from the function and role of religion in influencing individuals, both in the form of value systems, motivations and life guidelines, the most important influence is to form conscience. Conscience according to Erich Froom in Jalaluddin is a call back to humans to themselves. Erich Froom sees humans as creatures who individually have humanistic potential within themselves.
