1. How would you differentiate the Art Nouveau from the ArtDeco?​


Answer:

Art nouveau is much more decorative, flowing, and floral. Art Deco is sharp and based on straight lines and corners. It's about perfect forms, circles and angles. Geometry plays a big part in Art Deco works made during the 1920's and 1930's.

Answer:

About Cambodia

Cambodia is a Southeast Asian nation whose landscape spans low-lying plains, the Mekong Delta, mountains and Gulf of Thailand coastline. Phnom Penh, its capital, is home to the art deco Central Market, glittering Royal Palace and the National Museum's historical and archaeological exhibits.
