21. 3. What part of this analogy translates into 'as'? Branch: tree:: arm : man Branch O Man​


Colons
The colons are a part of the structure of an analogy. Instead of serving a grammatical purpose, the colons stand in for other words. This is how that analogy would be verbalized: Branch is to tree as arm is to man.
