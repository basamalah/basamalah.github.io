22. Sue and Joan are sisters. Joan is 7 years older than Sue. In 3 years Joan's age will be twice Sue's age. What are their present ages? With solution and x=Sue's age


Answer:

Sue is 4 years old and Joan is 11 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be Sue's age

Let y be Joan's age

y = x + 7   ---equation 1

(y + 3) = 2(x + 3)

y + 3 = 2x + 6

y = 2x + 6 - 3

y = 2x + 3 ---equation 2

Equate 1 and 2

x + 7 = 2x + 3

2x - x = 7 - 3

x = 4

Plug in to equation 1

y = x + 7

y = 4 + 7

y = 11
