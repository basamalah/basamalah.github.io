16. Lisa receive an amount of 20000 deposited in her account on her 18th birthday. If the bank pays 6% interest monthly and no withdrawal are made how much should be credited in her account on his 21st birthday​


Answer:

140,000

Step-by-step explanation:

20,000 (money deposited)

6 (percent interest per month)

3,333.33333... (money interest per month)

36 (months in 3 years)

20,000÷6=3,333.33333...

3,333.33333...×36=120,000

20,000+120,000=140,000
