Responsible Decision-Making

• Limit choices.

• Set deadlines.

• Ask an expert.

• Weigh your options.

• Keep it in perspective.

• Be assertive. Try taking command of the decision-making process.

• Make a plan. If you know you have an upcoming decision to make, it can help to make a plan.

Sound Decision-Making

▪︎What is Sound Judgment and Decision Making?

Sound judgment and Decision-making can be defined as one's ability to objectively assess situations or circumstances using all the relevant information and apply past experience in order to come to a conclusion or make a decision.

Stay calm, observe, and identify the problem.

Stop, think, and analyze the situation. Capture your thoughts as you consider the choices and their consequences.

Responsible Decision-Making: The ability to make constructive and respectful choices about personal behavior and social interactions based on consideration of ethical standards, safety concerns, social norms, the realistic evaluation of consequences of various actions, and the well-being of self and others.
