Step-by-step explanation:

To calculate the distance traveled by the BMW, we need to convert the time from minutes to hours, as the speed is given in km/h:

45 minutes = 45/60 hours = 0.75 hours

Now we can use the formula:

distance = speed x time

Plugging in the values we have:

distance = 64 km/h x 0.75 h

distance = 48 km

Therefore, the BMW travels a distance of 48 km.
