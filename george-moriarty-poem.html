Answer:

1. Describe the philosophy of the speaker

The speaker in this poem has the philosophy of being contented on traditional and simple things. As it has been conveyed in the introduction of his poem, he questioned what makes the others rich and comfortable because on his part, he is comfortable and rich in his own ways. He doesn't compare his life to others and is contented to walk to his grave with his current status in life.

He is rich in his contentment of everything that he possess. In this poem, he conveyed the idea that being rich depends on your satisfactory status as a person. Yo can set your own standard of richness, comfort and happiness.

29. GEORGE TAPAN WHAT IS THE ELEMENTS AND PRINCIPLES OF ARTS: - GEORGE TAPAN.​


Answer:

George Tapan is a Filipino photographer known for his stunning landscape and nature photographs. As a photographer, he uses different elements and principles of art to create visually compelling images.

The Elements of Art are the basic components that artists use to create works of art. They include:

Line - refers to the path of a moving point.Shape - refers to the two-dimensional area created by lines, color, texture, and value.Form - refers to the three-dimensional representation of shapes.Space - refers to the area around, between, and within objects.Texture - refers to the surface quality of an object, including roughness, smoothness, or softness.Value - refers to the lightness or darkness of a color.

The Principles of Art, on the other hand, are the guidelines that artists use to organize and arrange the elements of art in their work. They include:

Balance - refers to the equal distribution of visual weight in a composition.Contrast - refers to the juxtaposition of different elements to create visual interest.Emphasis - refers to the use of certain elements to draw attention to a specific area or object.Movement - refers to the suggestion of motion or direction in a composition.Pattern - refers to the repetition of elements to create a sense of rhythm or unity.Unity - refers to the overall sense of harmony and coherence in a composition.

In summary, George Tapan uses different elements and principles of art to create visually striking photographs that capture the beauty of nature and landscapes.
