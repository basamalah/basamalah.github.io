8. make your own synopsis on the story by following the seven guidelines on how to make a good plot summary ​


Title: The Lost Treasure

Guidelines for creating a good plot summary:

Identify the protagonist: The protagonist of the story is Jack, a young and adventurous boy who dreams of finding a treasure.

Define the conflict: Jack discovers a map that leads him to a hidden treasure, but he has to overcome many obstacles to reach it.

Describe the rising action: Jack sets out on a journey to find the treasure and faces challenges such as crossing a dangerous river, climbing a steep mountain, and solving puzzles.

Highlight the turning point: Jack and his team finally reach the location of the treasure, but they discover that it is guarded by a group of pirates who are also looking for the same treasure.

Explain the falling action: Jack and his team come up with a plan to distract the pirates and reach the treasure. They successfully get hold of the treasure, but one of the team members gets injured during the escape.

Clarify the resolution: Jack and his team return to their hometown with the treasure and the injured member gets medical help. They divide the treasure equally among themselves and become rich.

Conclude with the theme: The story highlights the importance of courage, determination, teamwork, and perseverance in achieving our goals.

Synopsis:

The Lost Treasure is an adventurous story of a young boy, Jack, who sets out on a journey to find a hidden treasure. He faces many challenges such as dangerous river crossing, steep mountain climbing, and puzzles along the way. Finally, Jack and his team reach the location of the treasure but discover that it is guarded by a group of pirates. They successfully distract the pirates and reach the treasure. However, one of the team members gets injured during the escape. Jack and his team return to their hometown with the treasure, and they divide it equally among themselves. The story highlights the importance of courage, determination, teamwork, and perseverance in achieving our goals.
