9. Which of the following statements about the human respiratory system is false?A. The bronchioles branch into bronchi.B. Alveolar ducts connect to alveolar sacs.C. When we breathe in, air travels from the pharynx to the trachea.D. Gas exchange between the lungs and blood takes place in the alveolus. ​


Answer:

D. Gas exchange between the lungs and blood takes place in the alveolus.

Explanation:

During gas exchange oxygen moves from the lungs to the bloodstream. At the same time carbon dioxide passes from the blood to the lungs. This happens in the lungs between the alveoli and a network of tiny blood vessels called capillaries, which are located in the walls of the alveoli.
