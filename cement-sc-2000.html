4. The diameter of a circular cemented garden is 12 meters. The cemented walk around the garden is 2 meters wide. What is the area of the cemented walk? Please answer..


Find the radius of the garden:

Radius = Diameter ÷ 2

Radus = 12 ÷ 2 = 6 m


Find the area of the garden:

Area = πr²

Area = π(6)²

Area = 36π m²


Find the radius of the garden and the walkway:

Radius = 6 + 2 = 8m


Find the area of the garden and walkway:

Area = πr²

Area = π(8)²

Area = 64π m²


Find the area of the walkway:

Area =  64π  -  36π = 28π = 88 m²


Answer: The walkway is 88 m²


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ALTERNATIVE SOLTUON:


Area of the walkway = π(6 + 2)² - π(6)²

Area of the walkway = 64π - 36π

Area of the walkway = 28π

Area of the walkway = 88 m²


Answer: The walkway is 88 m²
