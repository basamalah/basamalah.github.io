Explanation:

Explanations are an integral part of communication and play a vital role in shaping our understanding of the world. Whether it is at work, school, or in our personal lives, the ability to effectively explain ideas and concepts is a critical skill that can help us succeed and thrive.

To make an explanation, one must begin by clearly understanding the topic being discussed. This means doing the necessary research, gathering all relevant information, and organizing it in a way that makes sense. It is also important to consider the audience and tailor the explanation to their level of understanding. One should avoid using technical jargon or complex language that may confuse the listener.

In conclusion, making explanations is a fundamental skill that can have a significant impact on our personal and professional lives. 17. help me please speech about anxiety 200-300 words​


Answer:

Anxiety is a normal and often healthy emotion. However, when a person regularly feels disproportionate levels of anxiety, it can become a medical disorder. Anxiety disorders form a category of mental health diagnoses lead to excessive nervousness, fear, apprehension, and worry. These disorders alter how a person processes emotions and behaves, also causing physical symptoms.

There are several different types of anxiety disorders, including generalized anxiety disorder, social anxiety disorder, specific phobias, and separation anxiety disorder. Symptoms can include restlessness, fatigue, difficulty concentrating, irritability, muscle tension, and sleep disturbance.

Anxiety disorders are treated through a combination of therapy and medication. Therapy can be helpful in identifying and changing negative thought patterns, while medication can help to manage symptoms. It is important to consult with a mental health professional to determine the best course of treatment.

It is also important to take care of your physical health by exercising regularly, getting enough sleep, and eating a healthy diet. These activities can help to reduce symptoms of anxiety and improve overall well-being.

If you or someone you know is struggling with anxiety, know that you are not alone and that effective treatment is available. It is important to seek help and support from friends, family, and medical professionals.
