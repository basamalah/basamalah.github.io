4. Halimbawa ng malamig at mainit n bagay ng solid liquid at gas


Mga Malamig at Mainit na Solid, Liquid, at Gas

Ang Solid, Liquid, at Gas ay mga anyo ng anumang bagay o matter. Solid ang tawag sa bagay na may tiyak na hugis at kabuuan (volume). Liquid naman ang tawag sa mala-tubig na bagay na walang tiyak na hugis ngunit may volume. Ang Gas naman ay bagay na tila hangin at walang tiyak na hugis at volume.

20. halimbawa ng mainit at malamig na bagay solid,liquid at gas​


Solid, liquid, and gas are the phases of matter.

Solid

Particle are intact and very much closer to each other and usually open have more density compared to liquid and gas.

Liquid

Particle are a little bit far away from each other. Water is an example of liquid. It has no definite shape but have a definite volume.

Gas

Particle are far away from each other. It doesn't have a definite shape and no definite volume. It is less dense compared to solid and liquid
