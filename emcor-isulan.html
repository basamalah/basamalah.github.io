Answer:

712.08 kg of lanzones

Step-by-step explanation:

Ricardo harvested a total of 245.75 kg x 15 = 3686.25 kg of lanzones from his 15 lanzones trees.

He delivered 1550 kg to his customer in Tacurong City, so he had 3686.25 kg - 1550 kg = 2136.25 kg left to deliver to his 3 more customers in Isulan.

To find out how many kilograms each of his customers in Isulan received, we need to divide the remaining amount of lanzones by the number of customers:

2136.25 kg ÷ 3 customers = 712.08 kg (rounded to two decimal places)
