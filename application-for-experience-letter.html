1. Application letter example for police with no experience​


Explanation:

Dear [Hiring Manager],

I am deeply interested in the position of police officer at [Police Department], and am confident that my combination of relevant experience and enthusiasm for the job make me an ideal candidate.

I have no police experience, but I do have a strong background in security. I have worked as a security guard for the past five years, during which I have had to respond to emergencies, handle difficult situations and people, and stay composed and alert in high pressure situations. I am also familiar with the laws and regulations in the areas I have worked.

I possess excellent physical conditioning, having trained in martial arts since I was 8 years old. I am also certified in CPR, first aid, and basic medical training. I have a good driving record, and I have a valid driver’s license.

I am confident that I have the qualifications and the ambition to become an excellent police officer. My commitment to the job and my determination to make a difference make me an ideal candidate. I hope to discuss my qualifications in further detail in an interview.

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
