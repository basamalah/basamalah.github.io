It is deciding into something that brings out conflicts.

In an ethical dilemma, three conditions must be met.

First condition - "agent" should be present making a decision which should be bestSecond condition - there are or must be a difference between the two courses of action to be takenThird condition - there is no perfect solution for it

Two types of Ethical DilemmaAbsolute Ethical Dilemma - occurs when ethical standards are to be applied  to a situation but conflicts with each otherApproximate Dilemma - occurs when a decision is to be made but conflicts between one's values, laws, and policies
