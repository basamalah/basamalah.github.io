Most drugs in children are dosed according to body weight (mg/kg) or body surface area (BSA) (mg/m2). 1 g --------------- 1000 mg

y = 100 mg (dosage)

Now, how many tablets will the patient take?

1 tablet ------------------ 50.0 mg

y tablet ------------------ 100 mg

[tex]\dfrac{1}{y} = \dfrac{50.0}{100}[/tex]

[tex]50 * y = 100 * 1[/tex]

[tex]50\:y = 100[/tex]
