5. Doctor Kaboom is skiing. He is not a good skier. If he has a mass of 103 kg, and an acceleration of 14m/s², what is the Force Doctor Kaboom feels when he skis into a tree?​


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & m = \text{103 kg} \\ & a = \text{14 m/s}^2 \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the force

[tex]\begin{aligned} F & = ma \\ & = (\text{103 kg})(\text{14 m/s}^2) \\ & = \boxed{\text{1,442 N}} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the force Doctor Kaboom feels is 1,442 N.

25. Find the area of Doraemon's playroom with 5.36m length and 3m width


Area

[tex]\large\sf{Hiii!}[/tex]

Find the area of Doraemon's playroom with 5.36m length and 3m width

[tex] \sf \underline{Solution:}[/tex]

In finding the area of a rectangle using the formula [tex] \tt{A=(l)(w)}[/tex]

The given are:

3.36m length and 3m width

[tex] \sf \underline{Solving:}[/tex]

[tex] \tt{A=(l)(w)}[/tex][tex] \tt{A=(5.36m)(3m)}[/tex][tex] \tt{A= \green{16.08m^2}}[/tex]

Therefore, the area of Doraemon's playroom is 16.08m²
