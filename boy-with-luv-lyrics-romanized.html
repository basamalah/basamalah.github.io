20. If ∆LUV ≅ ∆YAH, then ∆HAY is congruent to?​


Answer:

∆VUL

Step-by-step explanation:

If ∆LUV ≅ ∆YAH

Then,

LU ≅ YA

UL ≅ AY

UV ≅ AH

VU ≅ HA

LV ≅ YH

VL ≅ HY

In ∆HAY,

HA ≅ VU

AY ≅ UL

YH ≅ LV

So,

∆HAY ≅ ∆VUL
