4. please help me make a speech about "money cannot buy happiness"​


Answer:

It’s not happiness that money buys, It’s the temporary pleasure of materialistic things that we get from money and then it becomes a never ending race. To be truly one needs to understand that Happiness comes from spiritual wealth, not material wealth. To get joy, we must give it, and to keep joy, we must scatter it.

Explanation:

Introduction –

Money is one of the most important things in the modern world. It has become a tool to measure a person’s status, power and influence in society. It is also used to determine an individual’s success but It cannot estimate anyone’s happiness as It is an emotion and can never be bought with money.

Body –

Money can buy materialistic things: It can buy expensive clothes, luxury vehicles, dinner at a 5 or 7 star hotel and countless other things but Can happiness be categorised the same as clothes or cars which can be bought with money. No, It can’t. Money cannot buy love, affection, respect or trust. These are the emotions that bring real joy and happiness.

It’s a simple deduction. If happiness could’ve been bought with money then the rich should’ve been happier than the poor or the middle class. What we observe is complete opposite of it. Rich people are generally stressed and suffer from mental health problems while those who aren’t earning in seven figures are generally happy and satisfied with what they have.
