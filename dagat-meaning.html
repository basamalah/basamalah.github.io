3. what is the meaning of dagat​


Answer:

"DAGAT" isang bahaging likido/fluido o agos sa ating daigdig na malawak na bahagi ng tubig na may alat o asin.Tinatayang ikatlong bahagi ng daigdig ay karagatan kung saan duon nanahan ang napakaraming uri ng hayop gaya ng mga isda,balyena o butanding,pawikan,page,pating at iba pa.Ang tubig dagat ay may maraming uri ng mineral na siyang naging dahilan ng napakayamang bilang ng kaurian ng mga buhay na nilalang dito.Sa katutubong salita ito ay "Atab" na pinagmulan naman ng salitang "taib" o high tide.samantala ang low tide naman ay tinawag na "hibas" sa tagalog, bikol at bisaya.

Answer:

1. SITWASYON: Ang problema sa basura ay lumulubha dahil sa pagdami nito dahil sa mga tao na ang nangungunang salik sa pagdami nito.

KAHULUGAN: Ang mga tao ang nagiging dahilan ng pagdami ng basura at lumalala ang problema dahil sa kanilang hindi tamang pagtatapon ng mga basura.

2. SITWASYON: Ibat ibang may buhay na organismo ang matatagpuan sa karagatan gaya ng hayop at halamang dagat.

KAHULUGAN: Sa karagatan ay mayroong iba't ibang uri ng buhay na organismo tulad ng mga hayop at halamang dagat.

3. SITWASYON: Nakita nina Mang Ninoy at Mang Dino ang pinsala sa karagatan gaya ng pagkasira ng mga korales at bakawan.

KAHULUGAN: Nakita ng mga taong nagngangalang Mang Ninoy at Mang Dino ang pinsala na nangyari sa karagatan gaya ng pagkasira ng mga korales at bakawan.

4. SITWASYON: Halos sampung taon o isang dekada ang pagkasira ng ating kagubatan.

KAHULUGAN: Sa loob ng halos sampung taon o isang dekada ay nagkaroon ng malawakang pagkasira ng ating kagubatan.

5. SITWASYON: Maraming pinagmultang mga may-ari ng minahan dahil sa ilegal na gawain gaya ng kawalan ng kaukulang permiso mula sa DENR.

KAHULUGAN: Maraming may-ari ng minahan ang pinagmulta dahil sa kanilang ilegal na gawain tulad ng pagpapatakbo ng minahan nang walang kaukulang permiso mula sa Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR).

1. Which of these animals is a mollusk?

A. squid

Squids are a group of large cephalopods or types of molluscs that live in the sea. The name "Cephalopod" in Greek means "head leg", this is because the legs are separated into a number of arms that encircle the head. Like all cephalopods, squid are separated by having a distinct head.

2. All of these are arthropods EXCEPT ___________.

B. clam

Shellfish are aquatic animals which include soft-bodied animals. The definition of shellfish is general and has no biological meaning, but its use is widespread and is used in economic activities. In the broadest sense, a clam means any mollusk with a pair of shells.

3. To which group do sea urchin and starfish belong?

D. echinoderms

The phylum Echinodermata is a phylum of marine animals that includes sea stars, sea cucumbers, and some of their relatives. This group of animals is found in almost all ocean depths.

4. Which of the following is the simplest of the invertebrate groups?

A. sponges

Spongilla is a genus of freshwater sponges with over 200 different species. Spongilla was first publicly recognized in 1696 by Leonard Plukenet and can be found in lakes, ponds and slow streams.Spongilla have a leuconoid body form with a skeleton composed of siliceous spicules.

5. Which of the following group of animals are invertebrates?

D. spider , crab , scorpion

Invertebrates or invertebrates are animals that do not have a backbone between the vertebrae. These invertebrate animals are divided into several groups, namely the phyla Protozoa, Porifera, Arthopods, Platyhelmintes, Nemathelminthes, Annelida, Coelenterata, Mollusca, and Echinodermata.

6. Which of the following is NOT classification of arthropods?

C. mollusk

Molluscs are soft-bodied coelomate triploblastic animals. This includes all soft animals with and without shells, such as various types of snails, chitons, shellfish, and squid and their relatives. Molluscs are the second largest phylum in the animal kingdom after the Arthropoda phylum.
