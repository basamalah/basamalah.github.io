24. At 6 am there. are 11 jeeps at the jeep terminal.five more than there were at 5 am. At 3 am,there were half as many jeeps as there were at 5 am. How many jeeps were there at 3 am.​


Answer:

Let's use algebra to solve the problem.

Let x be the number of jeeps at 5 am.

According to the problem, at 6 am there are 5 more jeeps than at 5 am, so we can write:

x + 5 = 11

Solving for x, we get:

x = 6

Therefore, there were 6 jeeps at 5 am.

We also know that at 3 am, there were half as many jeeps as there were at 5 am. So we can write:

3 am jeeps = 1/2 * 5 am jeeps

Substituting x = 6 for 5 am jeeps, we get:

3 am jeeps = 1/2 * 6 = 3

Therefore, there were 3 jeeps at 3 am.
