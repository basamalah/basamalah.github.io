3. meaning of weasel word


A weasel word (also,anonymous authority) is an informal term for words and phrases  aimed at creating an impression that a specific  and/or meaningful statement has been made, when in fact only a vague or ambiguous claim has been communicated,enabling the specific meaning to be denied if the statement is challenged.
