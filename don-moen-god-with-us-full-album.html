4. Ricky collects stamps.He puts 18 stamps on each page of his album. If 17 pagesare full and there are 12 on the 18th page,how many stamps has Ricky​


Answer:

Ricky has 318 stamps.

To solve the problem, we can start by finding the total number of stamps on the first 17 pages, which is 18 stamps per page times 17 pages: 18 x 17 = 306 stamps.

Then, we can add the 12 stamps on the 18th page to get the total number of stamps: 306 + 12 = 318 stamps.

Answer:

Ricky has filled 17 pages with 18 stamps on each page, which gives a total of:

17 pages × 18 stamps/page = 306 stamps.

On the 18th page, Ricky has 12 stamps.

Therefore, the total number of stamps that Ricky has is:

306 + 12 = 318 stamps.
