7. Roy's garden is 18m long and 13m wide. What is the area of Roy's garden?


To find the area of Roy's garden, know first what formula you should use.


Formula to find the are of a rectangle (just consider the garden a rectangle shape):


Area = Width × Length or A = W × L


Given width: 13m (it says "wide" so it's understandable that it's the width)


Given length: 18m (it says "long" so it's understandable that it's the length)


A = 13 × 18


A = 234


Answer: The area of Roy's garden is 234m.

Area = Width × Length

Area = 13 m x 18 m

Area = 234 m²

Answer: The area of the garden is 234 m².

27. 3.The sumof the ages of Roy, Boyet and Bong is 12. Boyet is 3 years younger than Roy and Roy is 6years older than Bong. How old is Boyet?​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving linear equation.

The problem is an application of linear equation having three unknown variables.

Let

x is age of Roy

y is age of Boyet

z is age of Bong

Solution

Create three equation to solve three unknown variables.

First Equation

The sum of The sumof the ages of Roy, Boyet and Bong is 12.

x + y + z = 12

Second Equation

Boyet is 3 years younger than Roy.

y = x - 3

Third Equation

Roy is 6 years older than Bong.

x = z + 6

Solution

x + y + z = 12

y = x - 3

x = z + 6

y = x - 3 → y = (z + 6) - 3

x + y + z = 12 → (z+6) + [(z+6)-3] + z = 12

Simplify

(z+6) + [(z+6)-3] + z = 12

z + 6 + z + 3 + z = 12

3z + 9 = 12

Solve for z

3z + 9 = 12

3z = 12-9

3z = 3

z = 1

z = 1

x = z+6

x = 1+6

x = 7

y = x-3

y = 7-3

y = 4

Check:

x+y+z= 12

7+4+1 = 12 ✓

Answer: 4 years old
