30. poem about sa fair minded person fleeceeee​


Answer:

OLD GOLD

                             "Do unto others as ye would be done by,"

                             Remains a most quotable piece of advice,

                              And is still a worthwhile dictum to apply,

                            Practical, fair-minded, simple and concise,

                             A creed that does not allow one to deny

                             Equality of treatment that should suffice,

                         Ancient wisdom we know as the "Golden Rule,"

                       Which for world peace would be an excellent tool.

                                    But since God is a god of war,

                                Such commonsense leaders ignore,

                                   And since God is a god of greed,

                                Most of us want more than we need,

                                   God, of course, is a god pf hate,

                              So hopes for peace have come too late.

                                Many will argue against this, I know,

                           But that means God isn't running the show.
