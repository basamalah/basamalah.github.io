11. what is the poem recollections of my children about​


Answer:

"Recollections of my Children" is a poem by the English poet William Wordsworth. It is a reflection on the joys of spending time with his children and the sadness of seeing them grow up and move away. The poem expresses the emotional journey of parenting and the bittersweetness of watching one's children leave home. The poem is also a celebration of the love that a parent and child share and the bond that will never be broken.
