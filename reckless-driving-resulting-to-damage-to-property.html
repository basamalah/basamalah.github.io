5. Reckless imprudence resulting to homicide elements


Reckless impruduce must be proved to state that reckless impruduce resulting to homiced elements.

Explanation:

Reckless imprudence consists of voluntarily, but without malicious intent, performing or failing to perform an act that results in material loss due to an unforgivable lack of precautions on the part of the person committing or failing to perform the act, taking into account the occupation or occupation, degree of intelligence, physical condition and other circumstances regarding people, times and places.

The prosecution must demonstrate a direct causal relationship between the driver's negligence and the injury suffered to support the allegation for reckless imprudence resulting to homicide.

According to a G.R. No. 210816. December 10, 2018 there are several elements of reckless imprudence are :

That the offender does or fails to do an actThat the doing or the failure to do that act is voluntaryThat it be without maliceThat material damage results from the reckless imprudenceThat there is inexcusable lack of precaution on the part of the offender, taking into consideration his employment or occupation, degree of intelligence, physical condition, and other circumstances regarding persons, time, and place.

29. Pedro, a bus driver of Biyahe sa Langit Transport, was involved in a collision with a car, damaging the bus. The day.after, Pedro received a notice of termination stating that he is dismissed for reckless driving resulting to damage to company property, effective immediately. Pedro asks you, as his counsel, if the company complied with the procedural due process with respect to dismissal of employees. Was the bus company correct? Support your answer.​


Answer:

No, the bus company did not comply with the procedural due process with respect to dismissal of employees. The company should have given Pedro an opportunity to be heard and present his side regarding the incident. By giving him only 48 hours to submit his explanation, the company did not give Pedro a chance to properly explain and defend his actions, and did not provide him with the necessary due process. Additionally, the company should have conducted an investigation into the incident to determine if Pedro was indeed at fault before terminating his employment.
