Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Management (BSBA-M), also known as BSBA in Business Management, is a four-year degree program designed to equip students with the concepts and principles of Business Management. The program teaches students operations and processes that occur within an organization such as procurement, logistics, quality control, project management, manufacturing and marketing. It focuses on the more authoritative aspects of business like planning business activities, organizing departments and employees, running departments, and generally managing an organization.

Recommended Senior High School Strand

Students who want to pursue a degree in BSBA in Management are encouraged to take the Accountancy, Business and Management strand. This strand provides the basic concepts of business and financial management, and corporate operations that will be helpful for college.
