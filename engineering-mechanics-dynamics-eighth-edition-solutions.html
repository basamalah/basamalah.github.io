24. The second term of an arithmetic sequence is −16 and the eighth term is 8. What is the sum of the first 10 terms?with solution​


Answer:

The sum of the first 10 terms in the arithmetic sequence is 20.

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we have to find their common difference. The formula for the common difference is [tex]d=\frac{a_n-a_k}{n-k}[/tex]. Substitute the given in the formula.

[tex]d=\frac{a_n-a_k}{n-k}=\frac{8-(-16)}{8-2}=\frac{8+16}{6}=\frac{24}{6}=4[/tex]

Their common difference is 4. The formula to get the first term is  [tex]a_2-a_1=d[/tex]. Substitute [tex]a_2[/tex] and [tex]d[/tex] in the given formula.

[tex]a_2-a_1=d\\-16-a_1=4\\-a_1=4+16\\-a_1=20\\\frac{-a_1}{-1}=\frac{20}{-1}\\a_1=-20[/tex]

Now the we have our common difference and and the first term, we can get now the sum of the first 10 terms.

Given:   [tex]a_1=-20[/tex],   [tex]d=4[/tex],   [tex]n=10[/tex]

Formula:   [tex]S_n=\frac{n}{2}[2a_1+(n-1)d][/tex]

Substitute the given in the formula.

[tex]S_n=\frac{n}{2}[2a_1+(n-1)d]\\S_{10}=\frac{10}{2}[2(-16)+(10-1)4]\\S_{10}=5[-32+(9)4]\\S_{10}=5(-32+36)\\S_{10}=5(4)\\S_{10}=20[/tex]

30. Find the eighth and eleventh terms of each geometric sequence.5,30,180,1080,... with solution


Answer and step-by-step explanation:

First, let's find the common ratio by dividing each term by the previous term.

5, 30, 180, 1080, ...

30 ÷ 5 = 6

180 ÷ 30 = 6

1080 ÷ 180 = 6

Thus, the common ratio is 6.

Then we are going to use the geometric sequence formula which is:

[tex]a_{n} = a_{1}( {r}^{n - 1} )[/tex]

where

an is the nth term,

a1 is the first term,

n is the number of terms and

r is the common ratio.

Then let's substitute our values which are:

an = a8 and a11 => for the eighth and eleventh term

a1 = 5

n = 8 and 11

r = 6

After substituting, simplify the exponent first and then multiply.

[tex] \\ a _{8} = 5( {6}^{8 - 1} ) \\ = 5( {6}^{7} ) \\ = 5(279936) \\ = 1399680[/tex]

[tex] \\ a _{11} = 5( {6}^{11 - 1} ) \\ = 5( {6}^{10} ) \\ = 5(60466176) \\ = 302330880[/tex]

Therefore, the eighth term is 1,399,680 and the eleventh term is 302,330,880.
