25. Shane has a collection of 224 stamps. She wants to place the stamps in 2 envelopes. Can she place the same number of stamps in each envelope?


Answer:

Yes, she can.

Step-by-step explanation:

This is how we get the answer.

Simply divide 224 stamps by 2, since Shane has 2 envelopes. If the quotient is a whole number with no remainder, then, it means that the stamps can be divided equally among the envelopes.

[tex]224\div 2=\boxed{\bold{112}}[/tex]  Since 112 is a whole number, then, Shane can divide the stamps equally between the two envelopes. So, the correct answer is YES!
