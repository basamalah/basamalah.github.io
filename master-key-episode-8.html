A real estate agent has 8 master keys to open several new homes. only 1 master key will open any given house. if 40% of these homes are usually left unlocked, what is the probability that the real estate agent can get into a specific home if the agent selects 15 master keys at random before leaving the office


Answer:

Let U denotes the event that the home is left unlocked.

P(U) = 0.4

Let L denotes the event that home is left locked. Events U and L are complementary events, so

P(U) + P(L) = 1

P(L) = 1 – P(L) = 1 – 0.4 = 0.6
