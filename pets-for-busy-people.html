27. A survey found that 20% of pet owners had their pets bathed professionally rather than do it themselves. If 10 pet owners are randomly selected, find the probability that exactly 5 people have their pets bathed professionally.


Answer:

the probability that exactly 5 out of 10 pet owners have their pets bathed professionally is about 0.21.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the probability that exactly 5 out of 10 pet owners have their pets bathed professionally, we can use the binomial probability formula:

P(X = 5) = (10 choose 5) * (0.20)^5 * (0.80)^5

= 252 * 0.00032 * 0.32768

= 0.21

So the probability that exactly 5 out of 10 pet owners have their pets bathed professionally is about 0.21.
