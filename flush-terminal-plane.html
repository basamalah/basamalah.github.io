17. A store sells an environmentally-friendly toilet that uses 5 liters of water per flush. They also sell a regular toilet that uses 2 times as much water per flush. How much water does the regular toilet use per flush?


Answer:

10 liters of water per flush.

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Turn off the water supply to the toilet by turning the shut-off valve clockwise.

2. Flush the toilet to empty the tank.

3. Remove the lid from the tank and locate the float ball and fill valve.

4. Adjust the float ball so that it is set at a lower level than before, allowing less water to enter the tank when it refills.

5. Adjust the fill valve so that it only allows 10 liters of water to enter the tank when it refills.

6. Replace the lid on the tank and turn on the water supply by turning the shut-off valve counterclockwise.

7. Flush the toilet and check that only 10 liters of water is entering into the tank when it refills.
