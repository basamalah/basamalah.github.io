7. a brick wall has 60 bricks in first row , but each row has 3 fewer bricks than the previous one. how many bricks are in the 12th row?


Answer:

There are 27 bricks in the 12th row

Step-by-step explanation:

Arithmetic Sequence:

An = a₁ + (n-1)(d)

Where:

First term, a₁ = 60

Common difference, d = -3 (decreasing number of bricks; keyword: fewer)

Last row/terms, n = 12

a₁₂ = 60 + (12-1)(-3)

a₁₂ = 60 + (11)(-3)

a₁₂ = 60 - 33

a₁₂ = 27

There are 27 bricks in the 12th row.

30. If the mass of the brick is 1, what is the brick's weight? ​


Answer:

2

Explanation:

An intuitive approach would be as follows: The brick weighs 1 kg plus half of what it weighs. So if we have 1 kg of the brick, we are still missing half of it. Thus the 1 kg chunk is one half of it. So the whole brick weighs 2 kg.
