10. The distance from cabatuan to santa Ana Barbara is about 19 kilometers .Santa Barbara is about 15 kilometers away from Iloilo city.About how many kilometers is the distance from Cabatuan to Iloilo​


• Question:

The distance from Cabatuan to Santa Ana Barbara is about 19 kilometers. Santa Barbara is about 15 kilometers away from Iloilo City. About how many kilometers is the distance from Cabatuan to Iloilo?

• Answer:

You just need to add the given distances. Distance from Cabatuan to Santa Ana Barbara plus the distance from Santa Ana Barbara to Iloilo City is equal to the distance from Cabatuan to Iloilo City.

[tex] \boxed {\large \tt15 \: km \: + 19 \: km = 34 \: km}[/tex]

Thus, the distance from Cabatuan to Iloilo City is about 34 kilometers.

30. there are 600 grade 9 students in santa barbara national comprehensive high school the ratio of boys to girls is 3.5 how many boys and girls are there in this school please help meeee to solve the problem​


Question:

there are 600 grade 9 students in santa barbara national comprehensive high school the ratio of boys to girls is 3:5 how many boys and girls are there in this school please help meeee to solve the problem​

Answer:

Boys = 225

Girls = 375

SOLUTION:

Ratio,

3:5 = boys:girls

let

3x = number of boys

5x = number of girls

then the sum of boys and girls must equal to 600,

3x + 5x = 600

solve for x,

3x + 5x = 600

8x = 600

x = 600/8

x = 75

solve for number of boys,

3x = 3(75) = 225

solve for number of girls,

5x = 5(75) = 375

Verify answers,

3x + 5x = 600

3(75) + 5(75) = 600

225 + 375 = 600

600 = 600   ✔✔✔ TRUE

[tex]\therefore \large\boxed{\text{\sf There are 225 boys and 375 girls}}[/tex]
