23. what is the theme of love yourself by justin bieber ​


Answer:

The theme of Justin Bieber's song "Love Yourself" is about moving on from a toxic relationship and learning to love yourself.

Explanation:

The lyrics of the song suggest that the person the song is addressed to (presumably an ex-partner) was not treating Bieber well and was taking advantage of him. However, Bieber has now come to realize that he deserves better and that he needs to focus on himself and his own well-being. The chorus of the song, which goes "Cause if you like the way you look that much / Oh baby, you should go and love yourself," emphasizes the importance of self-love and self-care. Overall, the theme of the song is about recognizing your own worth and learning to love yourself, even in the face of difficult relationships.
