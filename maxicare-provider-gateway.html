3. Fhrp provide alternate default gateways in switched networks where two or more routers are connected to the same vlans.


Answer & Explanation:

First hop redundancy protocols (FHRPs) are mechanisms that provide alternate default gateways in switched networks where two or more routers are connected to the same VLANs. One way to prevent a single point of failure at the default gateway is to implement a virtual router.

24. What are gateway drugs? Why are these drugs called gateways? How can gateway drugs open doors for teenagers like you to try more dangerous drugs?


Addiction always start by taking a small hearing, taste, feel, smell, and look of something that is addicting. From drugs to games addiction, all of them started from seemingly non-harmful amounts to a highly dangerous amounts of intake. And, every addict discovers this by going through a so-called gateway. In particular, occasional drug users use gateway drugs before they become a full-blown drug addict. Now, we try to answer these questions: “what are gateway drugs? Why are these drugs called gateways? How can gateway drugs open doors for teenagers like you to try more dangerous drugs?”

Gateway drugs are substances that people initially use that may eventually lead to the abuse of illegal drugs, such as cocaine. These substances are called “gateway” drugs because they are considered the gateway to forming a habit of regular or even excessive drug use. These drugs also help their users to cope or escape from the reality. There are 3 gateway drugs immediately identifiable and below is the list of the gateway drugs:

Alcohol Nicotine (find out more what nicotine is by clicking on this link: https://brainly.ph/question/689879) Marijuana

Gateway drugs effects manifest physiologically and mentally, including sudden change in moods. Apart from mood altering properties of the aforementioned substances that are technically legal, harmful effects also await users. Specifically, few of the implications and the worst illnesses caused by gateway drugs are listed:  

Lung cancer (what causes this cancer? Find out here: https://brainly.ph/question/689879)  Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease  Heart disease  Stroke  Asthma  Ectopic pregnancy (know more what happens if ectopic pregnancy occurs by clicking on this link: https://brainly.ph/question/297512)  Reduced fertility  Diabetes  Blindness, cataracts, and increased risk of age-related macular degeneration  Other types of cancers in colon, cervix, liver, stomach, and pancreas, among others.  

Because of the aforementioned problems, it is more important for quitting addicts to find a healthier, safer, and cheaper alternative to cigarette smoking. Some of those alternatives are the following:  

Create a vegetable garden or do some general gardening  Do cardio-based exercises such as running and jogging  Take nicotine alternatives such as gum  Find a new hobby that will preoccupy your hands and mind  Do not isolate yourself  Find a relaxing hobby or pastime
