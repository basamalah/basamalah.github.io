4. Antonio is studying Chabacano. A native dialect from Cavite City and Ternate. He started practicing one(1) word for an hour and decided to add two more words every succeeding hour. If the patterns continues,how many Chabacano did he learn in one day?​


Answer:

576

Step-by-step explanation:

This is a matter of arithmetic sequence in which our a₁ is 1, and difference is 2. Our sequence will then look something like 1, 3, 5, ...

Sum of arithmetic sequence = n * (a₁ + aₙ) / 2

aₙ = a₁ + (n - 1)d

a₂₄ = a₁ + (24 - 1)(d)

a₂₄ = 1 + (23)(2) = 47

Sum = 24 * (1 + 47) / 2 = 24 * 48 / 2 = 576

Therefore, he learned 576 words in one day, assuming he was awake for 24 hours.
