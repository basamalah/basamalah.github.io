21. Possible effects of Humss students with disabilities?​


Answer:

Intellectual DisabilitiesLearning DisabilitiesEmotional/Behavioral DisabilitiesPhysical DisabilitiesOther Health ImpairmentAutism Spectrum Disorder

Explanation:

Intellectual disability has an effect on gross motor abilities, language, and social interaction. Break down tasks, teach one idea at a time, or give several chances for achievement to ensure student success.

Learning Disabilities: Affects linguistic milestones and social relationships. Implement instructional cues or emphasize instructions to ensure student success. Concentrate on a single subject at a time to enhance student attention to teaching.

Disabilities of the emotional/behavioural type impair social interactions and connections with peers, as well as cognitive performance. Incorporate all team members, family members, instructors, and all faculty to ensure student achievement. Incorporate a holistic strategy that places an emphasis on the whole student and their team.

Physical disabilities have an effect on both gross and fine motor skills. Consider the classroom environment and its effect on the students' learning. Make use of adaptable technology, such as touch displays.

Other health impairments: impaired communication, cognition, and social interaction. Allow more time for assignment completion, establish daily and weekly plans, and define clear goals to ensure student success.

Autism Spectrum Disorder has an effect on gross motor, fine motor, linguistic, social, and cognitive abilities. Create a program that incorporates visual and instructional cues to ensure student success. Establish daily objectives and routines that provide various avenues for achievement.
