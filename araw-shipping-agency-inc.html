NET LOSS

A negative net income indicates that a company made a loss rather than a profit during a given accounting period. While a company's sales may be positive, its expenses and other costs will have exceeded the money taken in as revenue.

NET INCOME

The net income can be positive or negative. If your revenues exceed your expenses, you will have a positive net income. If your expenses exceed your income, you will have a negative net income, also known as a net loss.

Net income is defined as gross profit less all other expenses and costs, as well as any other income and revenue sources not included in gross profit. Interest on debt, taxes, and operating expenses or overhead costs are some of the costs subtracted from gross to arrive at net income.

22. captain ali is the captain of the ship. the captain of the ship is captain ali. what is the name of the ship?


WHAT.

WHAT is the name of the ship.  

Or

The name of the ship is WHAT.

There is no other name mentioned in the riddle except for Ali and What. Ali is the captain and his name can’t be the same name of the ship. This riddle is usually recited orally as to confuse the guesser of what is really the answer since one can play with the intonation of the last line making it ambiguously asking or revealing. Since this is a riddle, the person being told must make a guess.  

In writing, you may intentionally put a period at the end, or just simply write it as a question. Either way is acceptable though some would argue once the answer is revealed.

Some also calls this as a catchy or catching question because it’s goal is to challenge someone to catch the hidden message.
