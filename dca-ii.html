Half, Equal, or Twice As

1. Which of the inscribed angles coincides? Describe your response.

If two scribed angles intersect the same arc, they are congruent (or intercept different arcs with same measure). Because they both intercept arc BC in this case, angles BAC and BDC are congruent. Additionally, as they both intercept arc AD, ABD and ACD.

2. What is the measure of CD if m/CBD = 54?

The Inscribed Angle Theorem states that the measure of an inscribed angle that intercepts an arc is equal to half the measure of the arc. Here, we divide 54 in half to get 27, the result.

3. What is the measurement of ZACB if mAB=96?

Similarly to number 2, we just divide 96 in half to arrive at 48.
