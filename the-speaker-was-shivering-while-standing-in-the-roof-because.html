15. All the little dragons on the high-tilted eaves of the green roofs (3) shiver to the tips of their gilded tails under that deep wave of sound. The underlined word means to _____. a. fall on one’s knees b. tremble at the loud sound c. be destroyed by the gilded tails


Answer:

The correct answer is  B.

Explanation:

Cambridge Dictionary defines shivering as "the act of shaking slightly" due to cold, fear, or illness. Likewise, tremble has a similar definition.

Although shiver could also mean shatter or being broken, the way it used in this sentence is more appropriately describing the trembling of the little dragons to the tips of their tails caused by the deep wave of sound they heard. Furthermore, there is no act of violence described in the sentence to cause destruction to their gilded tails.

Other terms for shiver include: quiver, shake, vibrate, flutter, shudder, etc.

18. Some of the roofs flew because the wind blew hard cause and effect ​


Answer:

Cause: The high wind blowing over the roof which creates a low pressure in accordance with Bernoulli's principle

Effect: The pressure below the roof is equal to the atmospheric pressure which is now larger than pressure above the roof.

This difference of pressure causes an upward thrust and the roof is lifted up.

21. The two brothers Ronald and Nardo were tasked to paint the roof of the house. Ronald painted 3/8 of the roof while Nardo painted 1/8 of the roof. What part of the roof is already painted?​


Answer:

Ronald painted 3/8 of the roof and Nardo painted 1/8 of the roof. So the total amount of the roof painted is 3/8 + 1/8 = 4/8 = 1/2. So 1/2 of the roof is already painted.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find out what part of the roof is already painted, we need to add the fraction of the roof that Ronald painted to the fraction of the roof that Nardo painted.

1. To add two fractions, we need to have a common denominator. In this case, the denominators are 8. So we don't need to change the denominators.

2. Next, we add the numerators, which represent the parts of the roof that have been painted. Ronald painted 3/8 of the roof, and Nardo painted 1/8 of the roof. So, 3/8 + 1/8 = 4/8.

3. Reduce the fraction if possible, which in this case it can't be divided by any numbers so it remains 4/8.

4. Finally, express the fraction as a decimal by dividing the numerator by the denominator. 4/8 = 0.5

So, the part of the roof that is already painted is 0.5 or half of the roof.
