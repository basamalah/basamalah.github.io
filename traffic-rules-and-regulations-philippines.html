17. Rules and regulation about taking alcohol here in the Philippines ​


In the Philippines, the legal drinking age is 18 years old. It is illegal to sell, serve, or distribute alcoholic beverages to minors. It is also prohibited to sell alcohol from 12 midnight to 8 in the morning. Drinking in public places is generally allowed, but can be restricted by local ordinances. Driving under the influence of alcohol is a serious offense and can result in fines and imprisonment.
