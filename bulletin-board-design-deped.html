Answer:if the area is 63ft² the lenght will be 11

Let the width of the rectangle be ‘x'.ft

Then length= (11+2x) ft………equation(1)

Given area of the rectangle= 63 ft²

Area of rectangle

=length×width

=>x(11+2x)=63

=>11x+2x²=63

=>2x²+11x - 63=0

we have to solve this quadratic equation

=>2x² +18x -7x -63=0

=>2x(x+9) -7(x+9)=0

=>(x+9)(2x-7)=0

When (x+9)=0

Then, x= -9 ft

When (2x-7)=0

Then, x=(7/2) ft

width can't be negative

So width of the rectangle is (7/2) ft.

Now put the value of x=(7/2)in equation(1).

=11+2(7/2) ft.

=18 ft.

Dimension of the rectangle

Length=18 ft.

Width=(7/2)ft. =3.5 ft.
