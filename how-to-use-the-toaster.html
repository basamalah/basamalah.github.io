1. an oven toaster plugged into a 220 v household circuit draws 3.5 of currentA) how much is the resistance in the circuitB) How much power is used by the toaster​


Given:

Voltage (V) = 220V

Current (I) = 3.5A

A. Solving for the Resistance (R):

R = V ÷ I

R = 220V ÷ 3.5A

R = 62.86 ohms

B. Solving for the Power:

P = I²R

P = (3.5)²(62.86)

P = 770. 04 watts

How do you use oven toaster safely?

• Never stick anything into a toaster, you'll be risking a severe electric shock.

• Make sure you clean out the crumb tray frequently.

• If you notice an odd smell or any sparking, unplug the toaster immediately and contact the retailer and/or manufacturer.

What is the first thing you do if the oven toaster is burning?

• If you can grab it without hurting yourself, toss it into your oven and close the door.

• Another option is to douse the flames with baking soda.

• If the food in the toaster isn't greasy, you can also throw water on the fire to kill it.
