3. 10 laborers can repair a road in 15 days, in how many days can 30 laborers can repair the same road


Answer:

5 days

Step-by-step explanation:

This problem shows direct proportion

given:

Note this symbol "²" is not used as to square a number

¹x=10

¹y=15

²x =30

²y=???

¹x¹y=²y²y

(10)(15)=(30)(²y)

150=30²y

150/30=30²y/30

5=²y

30 Laborers can repair the same road in 5 days

#AnswerForTrees

Answer:

The work to repair the road requires 5 days if there are 30 laborers.

Step-by-step explanation:

The problem involves an indirect proportion where when one variable (laborer) increases, the other variable (time or number of days) decreases and vice-versa.

Here, when there are more laborers, the work is completed faster. When there are fewer laborers, the same work is completed slower and requires more number of days.

The indirect proportion formula:

a₁b₁ = a₂b₂

Where:

a₁ = laborers = 10

b₁ = number of days = 15

a₂ = laborers = 30

b₂ = number of days = Unknown

Solve for the number of days, b₂:

(10)(15) = (30)(b₂)

b₂ = 150/30

b₂ = 5 days

The work to repair the road requires 5 days if there are 30 laborers.

Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

First condition:

Worker 1 = 30 construction workersTime 1 = 10 days

Second condition:

Worker 2 = 50 construction workersTime 2 = unknown

The problem gives us clue that the workers have thesame rate , thus we can represent each by

Worker 1 × Time 1 = Worker 2 × Time 2

30 × 10 = 50 × T

300 = 50 × T

300 / 50 = T

6 = T

Therefore , it will took 6 days for 50 workers to do the job
