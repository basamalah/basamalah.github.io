19. spoken poetry english​


Answer:

Spoken word poetry is a performance art that transcends the written form. If you’ve ever watched slam poetry or a dramatic monologue at an open mic night, the intense, emotional delivery may have stayed with you long after it was over. This is the power of spoken word poetry, and it’s meant to be memorable.

It's important to know that I've spent a good portion of my life fighting heroin and opiate addiction, so I have written a lot of poetry about shooting heroin, going through withdrawal, life on the streets, etc.

Modern poets and spoken word artists gather at events called Poetry Slams to express their poems in a forum where competition clashes with camaraderie. Poetry slams are a form of competitive performance poetry where artists vie to win the hearts of random judges selected from the audience and come away with the crown for the day (which generally equates to nothing in monetary terms).
