What one-time payment should he make on his 40thbirthday to pay off this pension plan, if the interest rate is 8% compounded quarterly​


Answer:

12,000 PESOS

Step-by-step explanation:

PRINCIPAL = 10,000 PESOS

RATE = 8% OR 0.08%

TIME = 5 YEARS AND 3 MONTHS

10,000 PESOS × 0.08% = 800 PESOS

800 PESOS × 5 YEARS = 4,000 PESOS

4,000 PESOS × 3 MONTHS = 12,000 PESOS
