30. kailan nagsimula ang roman empire at paano magsimula?​


The Roman Empire began in 753 BCE, when the city of Rome was founded on the banks of the Tiber River in Italy. The Roman Republic was established in 509 BCE, after the Romans overthrew their Etruscan conquerors. The Roman Republic was a federal republic with a complex system of government, including two consuls who served as chief executives, a senate, and a popular assembly.

The Roman Republic lasted for several centuries and expanded through conquest, creating a vast empire that included much of Europe, North Africa, and parts of the Middle East. However, internal conflicts and political struggles eventually led to the decline and fall of the Roman Republic in 27 BCE, and it was replaced by the Roman Empire.

The Roman Empire was ruled by an autocratic government, with the Roman Emperor as the supreme leader. The Roman Empire was known for its military power and its engineering and architectural accomplishments, such as the construction of roads, aqueducts, and public buildings.

The Roman Empire was also notable for its legal system, which influenced the development of Western law, and for the spread of Christianity throughout the empire. The Roman Empire reached its greatest extent under the emperor Trajan, who ruled from 98 to 117 CE. However, the empire began to decline in the 3rd century CE, and it eventually fell in 476 CE, after invasions by barbarian tribes such as the Visigoths and the Huns.
