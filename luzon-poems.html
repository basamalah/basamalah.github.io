Answer:

Beautiful Folk Arts and Crafts of Northern Luzon

(3-stanza Poem)

Northern Luzon rich culture that includes their folk arts and crafts

We can see it when we visit their beautiful land areas

Some of the provinces of Northern Luzon are included in Ilocos Region

Others are in Cordillera Autonomous Region

We can see the finest weaved cloths and baskets in Kalinga

The historic mummy caves and coffins are located in the mountains of Cordillera

Igorots show their artistic expression in their Tinalak basket made from rattan

Bontocs are known for their unique baskets namely Suklang

Kalinga love their textiles with geometric pattern

Ifugaos have this cloth called Ikat which means to bind together

Tattoo is an art that they are also proud of

And the greatest Tattoo Artist is Apo Whang-od.

Northern Luzon has one of the largest ethno-linguistic group in the Philippines. They have rich culture that includes their folk arts and crafts.
