21. why is such commnication is limited on low frequency waves and not for high frenquency ones?


Answer:

High-frequency sound waves are perceived as high-pitched sounds, while low-frequency sound waves are perceived as low-pitched sounds. The audible range of sound frequencies is between 20 and 20000 Hz, with greatest sensitivity to those frequencies that fall in the middle of this range.

Communication tools uses lower frequency waves rather than higher frequency ones because low frequency waves travel farther than high frequency. In addition, low frequency waves also has high material penetration. For example, when using a 2.4ghz or a 5ghz signal for your wifi it is most advisable to use 5ghz if your router is beside you, near you, or nothing is standing in the way between you and router. However, if there are a lot of walls in between you and the router it is best to set it to 2.4ghz signal so that the signal would be more stable.
