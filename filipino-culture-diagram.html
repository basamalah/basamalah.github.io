Positvive Filipino Values/pracitces

1. Bayanihan - it is already common among Filipinos to help one another especially during times of needs and calamities.

2. Religious - Filipinos are known for being God fearing.

3. Utang na loob - Filipinos always give back to those who helped them from the past. They reciprocate kindess with kindess, generousity with gnerousity.

4. Respect - Filipinos embody the value of respect. It can be seen in their gestures and in their words (po at opo).

Negative Filipino Values/practices

1. Crab mentality - we often don't feel happy over the success of others.

2. Colonial mentality - Something that is of foreign origin is better. Western products are prefered over the local ones, western fashions and trends are highly sought. We put in high regard the english language while we are bearly proud of our own.

3. Bahala na mentality - we don't usually plan, we just leave our destiny on luck.

4. Chismoso/Chismosa - we usually speak ill of others, we talk foolish stuffs and spread unverified stories about our fellows.

5. Magastos sa tuwing may handaan - Filipinos don't want to be branded as kuripot (thrifty) they will exhaust all meams just to make sure that they will have something to serve during special occasions, even at the expense of being debt-ridden.
