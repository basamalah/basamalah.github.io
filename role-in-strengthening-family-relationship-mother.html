Strengthening Family Relationship

Family roles are extremely important in the functioning of a healthy family. Understanding our individual roles makes it easier for our families to deal with the ups and downs of everyday family life. Consider family roles to be like the engine in your car. For the motor to run, all of the pieces must work together. When they do, the driving is smooth. When just one part, no matter how big or small, fails to do its job, the entire motor struggles to start and eventually shuts down.

16. Grandmother's role in strengthening family's relationships?​


Our grandmothers play a vital role in strengthening our family relationships.  They serve as the main source of guidance and unconditional love and care, especially to their grandchildren.  In the Filipino culture, grandmothers are usually the caretakers of the children for the parents to work freely.  They serve as the second parents to their children every time the parents are away, hence, making them the secondary disciplinarian and nurturer.  Moreover, grandmothers help in the upbringing of their grandchildren such that they prepare food and serve them at home, and teach them lessons.  But for the unfortunate, grandmothers become the guardian to those who lost their parents.
