The short story, The Sadness Collector is about a girl named Rica who has problems while growing up with her father because her mother works overseas as a domestic helper. After her mother left for Paris she’s use to send different gifts to Rica just to show her love and her presence. To divert Rica's attention, her father told her a story about a big lady who eats sadness away from houses at night. 20. describe the big lady in the story sadness collector


The Sadness collector by Merlinda Bobis tells about a girl who is separated from her mother because she has to work abroad. She is left to the care of her aunties and her father who told her about the story of the Big lady.


The Big lady in the story is pitiful. Physically, she is fat but she cannot do anything about it. She cannot walk because of her big belly so she flies instead. She goes from house to house to collect every tinge and remnant of sadness from people. She cannot help but eat so the protagonist in the story, Rica, tries not to show her emotions to prevent the fat lady from eating further and bursting.
