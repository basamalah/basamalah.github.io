Answer:

The mean expenses of the XYZ Corporation is 9,911.22.

Further explanation:

In statistics, there are three ways to measure the central tendency or the numbers that indicates the center of a data. And these are the following:

Mean – Also called as average. It is the value after adding up all the given numbers then dividing it by the count of numbers in a data.Median – It is the middle number in an ascending list of numbers. If the count of numbers is even, then the median will be the mean/ average of the two middle numbers.  Mode – It is the number that have been repeated the most. The mode does not exist when no number is repeated in the given data.  

Solution for the XYZ Corporation’s mean expenses:Given:  

No Name                         Total Sales    Total Expenses  

1 Quezon City                  14,950.00     4,933.50

4 Cebu City                      18,900.00     6,237.00  

5 Davao City                    45,000.00    14,850.00  

6 Mandaluyong City        23,000.00    7,590.00  

7 Cavite                            22,000.00    7,260.00  

8 Laguna                          21,000.00     6,930.00  

9 Manila                           66,000.00    21,780.00
