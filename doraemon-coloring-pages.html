12. Doraemon can run 10 kilometers in the same amount time that Nobita can 12kilometers. If Nobita can run 1 kilometers per hour faster than Doraemon, how fast canNobita run? ASAP​


Answer:

6 km/hr

Step-by-step explanation:

Formula:

d = st

t = d/s

where:

d = distance

s = speed

t = time

For Doraemon:

d1 = 10 km

s1 = x (unknown)

For Nobita:

d2 = 12 km

s2 = x + 1 ( 1 km/hr faster than Doraemon)

since t1 = t2

d1/s1 = d2/s2

10/x = 12/(x+1) [cross multiply]

10(x+1) = 12x

10x + 10 = 12x

12x - 10x = 10

2x = 10

x = 5 km/hr

s2 = x + 1 = 5 + 1 = 6

therefore, Nobita can run at 6 km/hr

19. What is Doraemon backstory, episodes, characters all about?


Backstory of the Author:

Doraemon is a children's anime that was written back in the 1979 or the date of the very first manga volume. It was written by Fujiko Fujio. However, there was no sign of the last episode..

How? It was because Fujiko Fujio died right before publishing it. His family members had announced that it was because of liver failure.

But, before he died he had said that he wrote it all by himself since his team had abandoned him. He also got the inspiration from a doll. Where he drew the very first character or the main character we are talking about, Doraemon.

About Doraemon and his backstory:

Doraemon on the first early volumes could only run on four legs, like a literal cat. He had the appearance just like on the photo you added on your question. A round blue head with a white head with also a white belly, red collar on his neck with a bell. Whiskers and a red nose. Last but not the least, his 4D pocket.

His 4D pocket had led a lot of adventures and had helped multiple people and situations. Since it is 4D, it's infinite. Meaning he could fit huge and many things in it. Here are some of his gadgets:

Anywhere DoorTakecopterTime ClothSmall & Big LightTime MachineWhat-If TelephoneTranslation JellySuper Gloves

Now, his backstory. How did he lose his ears, and had turned blue? And why did he have a fear of mice?

Well, I will explain.

In certain or special episodes, it was revealed a few times.

It began from where was casually sleeping with his old appearance. He was entirely yellow and still had his yellow old ears. He was originally a cat so of course, he could defend himself from mice all by himself, UNTIL a mouse came by and bit his ears off.

Frightened, he had to be sent to a robot hospital where the robotic doctor had said the ears was only for display. He walked by on a mirror and saw his new self. He had no ears, due to shock, he had fainted and turned blue.

Back from the first episode, 2005 or 2020 version:

On the episode one, Nobita's great great grandchild named after Sewashi had ordered his robot, Doraemon to return into the 21st Century to take care of a kid, Nobita.

Nobita was also known to be a coward, lazy kid. Yet is sincere and honest. He always had a sign of being cheerful and confident in his bright heart.

Soon, he had arrived and met Nobita and introduced him his gadget. The takecopter. It was said that it could help you fly freely in the sky. It was painted with yellow and had 3 bright propellers. With a tap, you will began to float mid-air.

Finally, they became good friends and that's how their adventures began. Sometimes they do it together, but most of the time, they invite their three other friends; Gian/Takeshi, Shizuka and Suneo.
