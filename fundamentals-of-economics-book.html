2. fundamental goals of economics


Answer:

National economic goals include: efficiency, equity, economic freedom, full employment, economic growth, security, and stability. When the general public has chosen what labor and products are to be delivered and in what amounts, it should then choose how these merchandise will be created. There are different elective techniques for creating a decent and the economy needs to pick among them. And the third problem is whom to produce.
