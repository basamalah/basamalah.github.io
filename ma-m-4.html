23. What i the percent by ma % ( m/m ) concentration of ucroe in a olution made by diolving 20. 4 g of ucroe in 90. 8 g of water.


Answer:

18.3%

Explanation:

To find the percent by mass (% m/m) concentration of sucrose in a solution made by dissolving 20.4 g of sucrose in 90.8 g of water, you need to divide the mass of the sucrose by the total mass of the solution and multiply the result by 100%. The total mass of the solution is the sum of the masses of the sucrose and the water:

total mass = 20.4 g + 90.8 g = 111.2 g

Then, you can calculate the concentration of the sucrose as follows:

% m/m concentration of sucrose = (20.4 g / 111.2 g) * 100% = 18.3%

So, the percent by mass concentration of sucrose in the solution is 18.3%.
