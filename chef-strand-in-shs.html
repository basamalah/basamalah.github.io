16. What is the best SHS strand? why?​


Answer:

Hello! Before I answer that, let me first clarify that there is no such thing as the "best SHS strand," because each strand is unique and different from each other, so we cannot really compare them.

But one thing we can do is describe what makes them different from each other.

The Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics (STEM) strand focuses more on science & mathematics, so if you will be taking engineering, technological or science-related courses, take STEM.The Accountancy & Business Management (ABM) strand focuses more on business-related & accounting courses.The Humanities & Social Sciences (HumSs) strand is more on about philosophy, social sciences courses [especially those who would like to take law and other courses] in the future can take this strand.The Technical, Vocational & Livelihood (TVL) strand which has branches in it [such as plumbing, cooking, etc] that focuses more on the livelihood courses & work.The General Academic Strand (GAS) which is more on general knowledge.

So whatever strand you may be in, if it suits your passion & dreams, you're in the best strand, so give it your best shot!
