13. Ann walked 1.5 miles south to her house in 0.5 Hours what is ann speed,what is ann velocityGIVEN:REQUIRED:EQUATION:SOLUTION:ANSWER:​


Answer:

Speed is a scalar quantity that measures how fast an object is moving. It is typically measured in units of distance per time, such as miles per hour or meters per second.

Velocity is a vector quantity that measures both the speed and direction of an object's motion. It is typically measured in units of distance per time, such as miles per hour or meters per second.

Given:

Ann walked 1.5 miles south to her house in 0.5 hours.

Required:

Ann's speed and velocity.

Equation:

Speed = Distance / Time

Velocity = Distance / Time + direction

Solution:

Speed = 1.5 miles / 0.5 hours = 3 miles per hour

Velocity = 1.5 miles South / 0.5 hours = 3 miles per hour South

Answer:

Ann's speed is 3 miles per hour and her velocity is 3 miles per hour South

25. 2. Ann is 2 years older than Betty. Last year Ann was 2 times as old as Betty.How old is Ann?


Let x be the age of Ann (present)

Let (x-2) be the age of Betty (present)

Last Year Ann was 2 times as old as Betty (x-1), and Betty was x-2-1 = x-3 years old.

Last year Ann was  2  times as old as Betty, we can write (x-1) = 2(x-3)
__________________________________
SOLVE:

(x-1)=2x-6

-2x+x=-6+1      (Transposition)

2x-x= 6-1         (divide both sides by -1)

x=5                  (We can say that Ann's present age is 5 years old.)

_______________________________________

CHECKING

To get Betty's present age (x-2) [ is 5-2= 3 ]
(Therefore, Betty is 3 years old today)

Last Year Ann was 4 years old. therefore Betty was 2 years old back then. (Last year Ann was  2  times as old as Betty).

ANSWER
