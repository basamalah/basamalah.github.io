18. give the four strands in senior high school​


You have four strands in this track to choose from General Academic Strand; Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Strand; Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand; and Humanities and Social Sciences (HumSS) Strand.

GAS ( General Academic Strand) - Still undecided on what to take up? Rather than taking a course then transferring to another one because you suddenly realized that it is not a perfect fit, go and take this strand. This will guide you on deciding what you want to pursue. You can choose electives from the different academic strands and get a taste of everything under this track. These subjects include Humanities, Social Sciences, Applied Economics, Organization and Management, and Disaster Preparedness.

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) - Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics are intertwining disciplines when applied in the real world. This strand will focus on your scientific and mathematical skills. The difference of the STEM curriculum with the other strands and tracks is that this will focus on advanced concepts and topics. The aim of the STEM strand is to encourage you to take up science courses in college and address the lack of good science teachers. Under the track, you can become a pilot, an architect, an astrophysicist, a biologist, a chemist, an engineer, a dentist, a nutritionist, a nurse, a doctor, and more careers related to the scientific field. Those who are also interested in Marine Engineering should take this track.

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) - The Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM) strand would focus on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and all things that are accounted. Subjects in the strand will give you the tools you will need on your journey to success in the corporate world. This strand is fit for those who are looking forward to courses such as Commerce, Real Estate Management, Accountancy, Business Administration, Agribusiness, Tourism, and Hospitality Management. This strand can also lead you to careers on management and accounting which could be sales manager, human resources, marketing director, project officer, bookkeeper, accounting clerk, internal auditor, and a lot more.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) - The HUMMS strand is specially designed for those who wonder what is on the other side of the wall. In other words, you are ready to take on the world and talk to a lot of people. And when I say talk to a lot of people, I mean really TALK to a LOT of people. This is for those who are considering taking up journalism, communication arts, liberal arts, education, and other social science-related courses in college. You could be looking forward to becoming a teacher, a psychologist, a lawyer, a writer, a social worker, a law enforcer, or a journalist someday. This strand focuses more on improving your communication and people skills to ensure that you will be able to say your thoughts or share your ideas effectively. 20. Mga hakbang sa pagpili ng strand sa senior high school

Kilalanin ang sarili - Unang hakbang sa pagpili ng tamang strand ay kilalanin ang sarili. Alamin ang mga hilig, interes, at kakayahan. Tandaan na magkakaiba ang mga strand at hindi lahat ay swak sa lahat.

Alamin ang layunin - Mahalagang malaman kung ano ang layunin ng pagpili ng strand. Ano ang layunin sa pagpasok sa senior high school? Ano ang layunin sa pagpili ng isang partikular na strand?

Konsultahin ang guro o gabay-aral - Humingi ng payo mula sa guro o gabay-aral tungkol sa mga nakalap na impormasyon tungkol sa mga strand.
