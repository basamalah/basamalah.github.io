27. An office boy whose mass is 55.0 kg runs up 20 steps, each step is 30.0 cm high in 40.0 seconds. Find the power in watts expended by the office boy?​


Formula:

Power of the potential energy

[tex]P=\dfrac{W}{t} =\dfrac{U}{t} =\dfrac{mgh}{t}[/tex]

where

P = power, m = mass of an object, g = acceleration due to gravity, h = height

Solution:

Given

[tex]m=55.0\text{ kg}\\g=9.8\text{ m/s}^2\\h=20\times30.0\text{ cm}=600\text{ cm}=6\text{ m}\\t=40.0\text{ s}[/tex]

The power expended by the office boy is

[tex]P=\dfrac{(55.0\text{ kg})(9.8\text{ m/s})^2(6\text{ m})}{40.0\text{ s}}\\\\\boxed{P=80.85\text{ W}}[/tex]
