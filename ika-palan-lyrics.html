11. How many possible polygons can be fomed from 8 points on a palane with no three points are collinear?​


Answer:

You can make triangles by choosing any 3 points out of the 8. So that’s 8C3.

You can make quadrilaterals by choosing and 4 points out of the 8. That’s 8C4.

Etc for 5, 6, 7, and 8 points. So far so good.

However, the 4 points can be traversed in two different orders: 1,2,3,4 and 1,3,2,4, which doubles the number of quadrilaterals.
