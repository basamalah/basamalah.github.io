30. similarities of mechanical harvesting and manual harvesting​


Mechanical harvesting systems are designed to achieve the mass removal of the commodity during the harvesting season at once. This method has been practiced by shaking the trunks, limbs, and canopies of plants.

Manual harvesting is a commonly practiced method of fruit harvesting in many parts of the world. ... This method involves harvesting of fruit by hand, using fruit clippers and knives etc. Properly trained workers harvest and handle the fruits with minimum damage.
