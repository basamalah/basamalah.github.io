15. Give the meaning of the given italian name of varios dynamics. Italian name 1.piano 2.forte 3.pianississimo 4.fortissimo 5. Fortississimo 6.mezzo forte 7.mezzo piano 8.decrescendo 9.crescendo


1. Piano- is defined as the sound level when music is played softly.

2. forte- loud. Fortissimo (ff) – very loud. Sforzando (sfz) – a sudden, forced loud. Usually the appreviation is used to show dynamics in a piece of music.

3. pianissimo- to be played even more quietly or softly than pianissimo

4. fortissimo- very loud

5. fortississimo- is an indication to play as loudly as possible; louder than fortissimo.

6. mezzo forte- moderately loud

7. mezzo piano- moderately soft

8. decrescendo- a decrease in loudness in a piece of music.

9. crescendo- the loudest point reached in a gradually increasing sound.
