26. waht is the exposition, rising action, climax, falling action, resolution in Arachne


Arachne: Elements of the Story

Answer:

A story about a young woman whose skills in weaving are amazing, many people recognized her talent. But due to her head getting bigger with compliments she tends to boast about what she can do and that no one will surpass her skill. Then the Goddess of craft Athena appeared and challenged Arachne. There Athena made the rules, whoever loses will never touch a spindle again.  

Exposition: Arachne was a popular weaver that skills aren’t like no other. Due to her efforts in weaving she produced works of art that made the Goddess Athena want curious of her talent. Rising Action: Goddess Athena challenged Arachne to a weaving contest, whereas the loser will have to give up weaving forever. Falling Action: Arachne lost to the Goddess and therefore declared the loser of the contest. And ths, Arachne wasn’t allowed to touch any spindle ever again. Resolution: By the Goddess’ pity she turned Arachne into a creature that’ll have the ability to weave without a spindle.
