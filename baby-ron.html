13. Mar is 4 years older than Ron. Chan is twice as old as Mar. The sum of their 3 ages is 8 times Ron’s age. How old is Ron now?


Answer:

3

Step-by-step explanation:

Mar = M

Ron = R

Chan = C

Mar is 4 years older than Ron:  M = R + 4

Chan is twice as old as Mar: 2M = C

The sum of their 3 ages is 8 times Ron’s age: M + R + C = 8R

M = R + 4

M - 4 = R

R = M - 4 -----------> equation 1

2M = C

2(R + 4) = C

C = 2R + 8 -----------> equation 2

M + R + C = 8R

(R + 4) + R + (2R + 8) = 8R

4R + 12 = 8R

12 = 8R - 4R

12 = 4R

R = 3

-------------------- continuation -------------------------

Mar's Age

M = R + 4

M = 3 + 4

M = 7

Chan's Age

2M = C

2(7) = C

C = 14

Check: "The sum of their 3 ages is 8 times Ron’s age"

M + R + C = 8 R

7 + 3 + 14 = 8 * 3

24 = 24   (nice)
