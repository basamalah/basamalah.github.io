17. Advantages and disadvantages of school rules and regulations


SCHOOL RULES AND REGULATIONS

You can never imagine a school without any rules and regulations. A school without any rules and regulations will never exist, it will never be. The rules and regulations in schools makes the school a conducive environment for the learners. The school becomes orderly and peaceful.

ADVANTAGES OF SCHOOL RULES AND REGULATIONS

Some of the advantages of school rules and regulations are the following:

It develops the sense of discipline in the learners. It molds the learners to become law-abiding citizens in the school, community and in the country. It maintains order inside the school or campus. It promotes equality. Equality among learners inside the school or campus. It inculcate to students the importance of following rules and regulations.

DISADVANTAGES OF SCHOOL RULES AND REGULATIONS

Below are some of the disadvantages of school rules and regulations:

 

If not promulgated effectively and efficiently, it can cause confusion among members of the school especially the learners. If not properly imposed, it can cause chaos and or disorder inside the school. If not strictly imposed, learners might build a mindset that the rules and regulations are powerless so there will be a tendency that the learners will just freely disobey the rules and regulations.

30. school rules and regulations​


Answer:

1. Respect for others and all school and individual property is a characteristic of our school. Good behaviour is expected from all students at all times.

2. The school uniform is to be worn neatly, without jewellery (except for one small stud) and without makeup and coloured nail varnish. Hair must be tidy and the style not extreme. Boys must be clean shaven.

3. Students need a pass to leave the school grounds. All visitors are to report to the main office.

4. The school is a smoke free zone.

5. Students from Years 11-13 can bring vehicles to school if they have a permit from the Dean.

6. All students are expected to be well mannered, pleasant, courteous and cooperative.

7. Alcohol and other drugs and solvents are not permitted in school or at any other official school function.

8. It is a privilege to represent the school so students selected will do so with pride.

9. Cell phones and other electronic devices are not to be used during class time unless specifically directed to do so by a teacher.
