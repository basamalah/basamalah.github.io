What does the song Manang Biday mean?

Manang Biday, Kurditan: is a traditional Ilocano folk song in northern Luzon, especially in Ilocos province. The song alludes to the courtship of a young girl named Manang Biday. Serenading a lover is a Filipino custom. It is still practiced by the Ilocanos to this day.

Manang Biday (Reflections on a Folk Song) is a very sad song by Florante Aguilar at 126 BPM. It can also be used for half court at 63 BPM or double court at 252 BPM. The track runs for 4 minutes 26 seconds in A key and major mode.
