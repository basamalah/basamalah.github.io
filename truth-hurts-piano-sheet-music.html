11. "the truth hurts" is it figurative or literal language​


"The truth hurts" is a figurative language or an idiom. It means that hearing the truth can be painful or uncomfortable, even if it is necessary or beneficial in the long run. It is not meant to be taken literally, but rather as a way to express the emotional impact of hearing something unpleasant or difficult.
