28. 2. Do you think opening a laundry business is good? Why or why not?​


To start a laundry business, here are some things you have to know.

Pros

Unlike a restaurant, convenience store, or grocery store, laundromats have very little physical inventory. That means minimal ordering, rotating, or discarding products, which makes for easier management. It also means you won't have to deal with preparing and storing large amounts of expensive inventory ahead of time

Cons

There's risk involved, just like any business. You really have to bring your A game if you want to be successful. You're competing with other laundromats in the area, as well as multi-housing facilities (like apartments and condos) that may offer laundry equipment, and customers purchasing equipment for themselves.
