15. how external factors such as politics, economics, technological advancement and social issues affect technopreneur's business? ​


Technopreneurs, like all other business owners, are influenced by external factors that impact their operations and profitability. Some of the major external factors that can affect technopreneurs' businesses include politics, economics, technological advancement, and social issues.

Politics can impact technopreneurs' businesses in several ways. For example, changes in government regulations or policies can affect the availability of funding and incentives for technopreneurs. Tax policies can also impact the cost of doing business and the profitability of a technopreneur's venture. Political instability or conflict in a region can also affect the ability of technopreneurs to operate effectively and grow their businesses.

Economics also play a crucial role in influencing technopreneur's businesses. Fluctuations in the global economy, inflation, and changes in interest rates can affect the cost of capital, availability of credit, and the purchasing power of customers. Technopreneurs must be aware of economic conditions and adjust their strategies accordingly to remain profitable.

Technological advancements can have a profound impact on technopreneurs' businesses. The rapid pace of technological change can create opportunities for new businesses to emerge, but it can also render existing business models obsolete. Technopreneurs must be able to adapt and innovate to stay competitive in a rapidly changing technological landscape.

Social issues can also affect technopreneurs' businesses. For example, changes in consumer preferences and trends can affect the demand for products or services. Technopreneurs must be aware of social issues such as environmental sustainability, social responsibility, and ethical concerns and ensure that their business practices align with these values.

In conclusion, external factors such as politics, economics, technological advancements, and social issues can significantly impact technopreneurs' businesses. Technopreneurs must remain aware of these external factors and adjust their strategies accordingly to remain competitive and profitable in the market. A proactive approach to these external factors can help technopreneurs capitalize on opportunities and minimize the risks associated with them.

27. Research further about technopreneurs, social entrepreneurs, intrapreneurs, and extrapreneur in the philippines. among the four which type of entrepreneur do you think will dominate the future of philippine business


Technopreneurs, Social Entrepreneurs, Intrapreneurs, and Extrapreneur In The Philippines

The social entrepreneur will be the area of specialization that rules the Philippine market. Because a social entrepreneur creates organizations that are either established up as for-profit corporations or as not-for-profit corporations, much like a business entrepreneur. According to the Schwab Foundation for Social Entrepreneurship, they propel social innovation and transformation in a number of industries, including education, health, the environment, and business development. They have the power to ameliorate societal issues in our nation. Although it could be a drawback for some, social entrepreneurs benefit from it because of the additional opportunities it presents.

The next type of entrepreneur is an extrapreneur, a hybrid of an intrapreneur and an entrepreneur. According to Kristof de Wulf, extrapreneurs are people outside the firm who care about the brand so much that they impulsively engage in entrepreneurial activities for it. Extrapreneurs are those that think creatively or innovatively, which means they do not simply concentrate on internal organizations while seeking for solutions to their challenges, but also conduct an extra study in other businesses. The next person is the intrapreneur, who, in accordance with Murray Newlands, is a member of a business that takes risks in an effort to address a specific issue.

They play a significant role in raising productivity within businesses. Making decisions for the organization and the business is very difficult and dangerous. Creativity and productivity come naturally to intrapreneurs. Their contributions to a company's ideas are quite beneficial and can assist the enterprise's success. The Technopreneurs come last. According to Universiti Kuala Lumpur, technopreneurs are business owners whose primary enterprises involve sectors with a focus on technology. According to US Legal Inc., these people are known as "new age entrepreneurs" because they employ technology to develop fresh, cutting-edge items that they can sell to consumers and profit from. In the same way that their products benefit people by raising their standard of living, businesses generate money as they create new or inventive products.
