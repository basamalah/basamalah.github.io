18. What type of educational assistance is available to PWDs?​


Answer:

PWDs (Persons with Disabilities) are entitled to various educational assistance to help them pursue their academic goals. Here are some types of educational assistance available to PWDs:

Inclusive education: Inclusive education is an educational approach that aims to provide equal opportunities to PWDs by integrating them into mainstream schools. This approach ensures that PWDs receive the same quality education as their non-disabled peers.

Assistive technology: Assistive technology includes tools, devices, and software that help PWDs with mobility, communication, and learning. Examples include hearing aids, screen readers, and voice recognition software.

Special education: Special education is a type of education designed to meet the specific needs of PWDs. This includes personalized instruction, accommodations, and modifications to the curriculum.

Scholarships: Scholarships are financial aid provided to PWDs to help them pursue their academic goals. Many organizations and foundations offer scholarships specifically for PWDs.

Grants: Grants are funds provided by the government or private organizations to support educational programs for PWDs. These grants can be used to fund research, develop new educational programs, and provide resources for PWDs.

Disability support services: Disability support services are provided by colleges and universities to help PWDs with academic and non-academic activities. Examples include note-taking assistance, extended test time, and accessible classrooms.
