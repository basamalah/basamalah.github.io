11. What percent of P 125.00 is P 22.50?


We are looking for the rate:
[tex]Rate= \frac{Percentage}{Base} = \frac{22.50}{125.00} =0.18[/tex]

We multiply 0.18 by 100 since we are looking for the percent or rate. So the rate is 18%.First we need to have an equation, all we have is:
Max Amount x Percentage = Sale Amount
Now if we derive it, we can say that:
Percentage = Sale Amount divided by Max Amount,

Therefore:
P 22.50/P 125
=0.18

Since we are looking for the percent, multiply 0.18 by 100 then the answer is 18%. Hope this helps =)
