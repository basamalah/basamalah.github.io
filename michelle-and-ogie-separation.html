4. what is the shape of ogi and uchiwa​


Answer:

Sensu is compact in shape when folded. It is, so to speak, an uchiwa that can be folded. The angle a Sensu or Ogi when unfolded varies from 90 - 180 degrees, with around 120 degrees being the norm. The shape of the unfolded fan is called 'Ogigata or Senkei' and is used as a term in geometry.

Answer:

What is the shape of ogi and uchiwa​

Sensu is compact in shape when folded. It is, so to speak, an uchiwa that can be folded. The angle a Sensu or Ogi when unfolded varies from 90 - 180 degrees, with around 120 degrees being the norm. The shape of the unfolded fan is called 'Ogigata or Senkei' and is used as a term in geometry.
