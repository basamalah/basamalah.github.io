12. List of japanese food that is common in restaurant


While not all restaurants are known to serve Japanese Cuisine, there are some that do serve such food, such as:
Donburi dishes, such as Gyudon, Katsudon, Butadon, Oyakodon, etc. - basically meals topped over rice.
Sushi - rice mixed with other ingredients, then rolled (covered) with nori wrapper.
Sashimi - thinly sliced raw fish, served with wasabi and soy sauce.
Tempura - usually shrimp, but there's also vegetable tempura
Bento - or boxed lunch. This is a set meal of rice, a main dish, a side dish, and vegetables.
Ramen, Soba, or Udon - Noodle dishes each varying from ingredients and noodle used.
