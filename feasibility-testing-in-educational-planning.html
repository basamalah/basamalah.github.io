28. why feasibility study is necessary in making a business plan?​


Answer:

Feasibility studies are important to business development. They can allow a business to address where and how it will operate. They can also identify potential obstacles that may impede its operations and recognize the amount of funding it will need to get the business up and running.


29. what is the difference between feasibility study and business plan?


Answer:

A feasibility study is done before starting a business, when you have the idea for the business but you want to make sure it's feasible, or advisable. On the other hand, a business plan details how the business will operate. It assumes your feasibility study has been completed and it was determined the idea is viable.
