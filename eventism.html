Initiating Events

Orpheus was known to play the lyre, that even monsters would listen to him play, forgetting they are scary creatures. He loved a woman called Eurydice. They married. Unfortunately, his wife was killed by a poisonous snake when she stepped on it.

Second Events

Third Events

Outcome
