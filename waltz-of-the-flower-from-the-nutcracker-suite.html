Symphonie Fantastique:

1. Euphoric - There are multiple crescendos and harmonic changes that evoke a sense of triumph and exaltation.

2. Melancholic - Some passages have a slower tempo with dissonant chords that create a somber and introspective mood.

3. Tense - The dense orchestration and dramatic pauses generate high tension and expectation, especially during the fourth and fifth movements.

4. Nostalgic - The use of the idée fixe or recurring theme conveys a longing or reminiscence that permeates the whole piece.

5. Nightmarish - The strange, hallucinatory imagery of the final movement, depicting a guillotine and a coven, creates a disquieting and eerie atmosphere.

The Nutcracker Suite:

1. Merry - The lively and festive rhythms of the March and the Waltz of the Flowers summon a joyful and celebratory mood.

2. Dreamy - The delicate and graceful melodies of the Dance of the Sugar Plum Fairy and the Arabian Dance evoke fantasy and reverie.

3. Regal - The grandeur and majesty of the Pas de Deux and the Russian Dance suggest a royal or imperial aura.

4. Nostalgic - The familiar tunes of the Dance of the Reed Pipes and the Waltz of the Snowflakes may trigger memories or associations with childhood or winter festivities.

5. Mysterious - The exotic and oriental flavor of the Chinese Dance and the Tea Dance convey a sense of foreignness and enigma.
