1. personal goal development worksheet? ​


Answer:

Whether we realize it or not, our entire lives are geared around obtaining some sort of life goal. We must learn how to develop clear personal goals that we can use to monitor our individual growth and progress in order to be more active and effective. 

Personal Goal Development Worksheet

1. Personal Goal: Better grades for this year

Stop: Sleeping late at night

Minimize: Using gadgets for gaming

Continue: Keeping my notes orderly

Improve: Reading in advance

Start: Developing a daily personal study plan

2. Personal Goal: Healthy Lifestyle

Stop: Eating junk foods and unhealthy drinks

Minimize: Taking a lot of sugar

Continue: Eating fruits every day; I usually have a banana and an apple a day

Improve: Improve my strength by having some exercise.

Start: Working out at least 30 minutes a day

3. Personal Goal: Learn how to play an instrument

Stop: Saying I'll start learning tomorrow and act on it now.

Minimize: Time scrolling the social media.

Continue: Watching tutorial videos on how to play the instrument.

Improve: Improve my time management skill

Start: Rehearsing on a specific piece

4. Personal Goal: Save Money

Stop: Buying things that I don't need from online

Minimize: Scrolling time on Shopee and Lazada so that I will not be tempted to buy

Continue: Putting coins in my piggy bank every day

Improve: Improve my knowledge of investment and stocks

Start: Talking to a financial advisor

5. Personal Goal: Learn how to cook

Stop: Being lazy on eating healthy and good food

Minimize: Ordering food from Fast Foods

Continue: Reading a lot of cooking books

Improve: Baking skills

Start: Cooking breakfast meals every day.

Goals must S M A R T to be considered smart:

Specific: You must have a specific target on what you want to achieve

Measurable: You must have an indicator for your goal, it could be weight, color, milestone, amount, etc.  

Achievable: Elaborate how the goal will be achieved, what are the steps, and who are the people involved.

Realistic: The results must be achievable and realistic

Time-related: There must be a specific timeframe for the goal to be achieved.

27. How will you enhance the data inserted in a worksheet/Personal Data Sheet?​


Answer:

It is critical to include column headings that accurately describe the data in each column of a worksheet. In professional environments, you will likely be sharing Excel workbooks with coworkers. Good column headings reduce the chance of someone misinterpreting the data contained in a worksheet, which could lead to costly errors depending on your career.

Explanation:

It is very important to proofread your worksheet carefully, especially when you have entered numbers. Transposing numbers when entering data manually into a worksheet is a common error. For example, the number 563 could be transposed to 536. Such errors can seriously compromise the integrity of your workbook.
