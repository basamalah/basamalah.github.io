1. Significant contribution of hilda taba and peter oliva


Hilda Taba and Peter Oliva are two educators that became known in the field of education because of their contributions to educational curriculum development. Taba was known to push for the planning of curriculum, which was designed by the teachers and not handed down by the authorities. Meanwhile, Oliva’s model of curriculum pushed for giving importance to program, plan, content, and learning experiences.

Answer:

Edgar is 43 yrs old

Maring is 59 yrs old

Hilda is 63 yrs old

Solution :

1st: analyze and identify the order & gaps of their ages to formulate a mathematical equation.

Hilda : eldest (1st)

Maring : 2nd

Edgar: 3rd

Lourdes : youngest

Hence, we arrive with the ff formula:

Let [(l+4) + 16] + 4 = Hilda's age

Let (l+4) + 16 = Maring's age

Let l + 4 = Edgar's age

Let l = 39 (Lourdes' age)

2nd: solve for their ages using the formulas

l + 4 = Edgar's age

39 + 4 = 43 : hence Edgar is 43 yrs old

Let (l+4) + 16 = Maring's age

(39 + 4) + 16

43 + 16 = 59 : hence Maring is 59 yrs old.

[(l+4) + 16)] + 4 = Hilda's age

[(39 + 4) + 16] + 4

(43 + 16) + 4

59 + 4 = 63 : hence Hilda is 63 yrs old.
