2. A child is shown covered in bug bites after using an inferior bug spray is a pathos


Answer:

The scenario of a child that is shown, covered by itchy bug bites after making use of an inferior bug spray is an example of Pathos because the scenario is meant to evoke pity and sadness for the child because of the sufferings and pain he is enduring as a result of making use of an inferior bug spray.
