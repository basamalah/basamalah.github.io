30. An Iselco I lineman takes 2 hours to plant 500 streetlight bulbs.An Iselco II lineman take 3 hours to plant 450 streetlight bulbs.if a melarco lineman who can install 400 streetlight bulbs in two hours replaced the iselco II lineman in the installation,how long it take for them to install 1500 streetlight bulb?​


Answer:

It will take them 3 hours and 18 minutes or 3.3 hours to install 1500 streetlight bulbs.

Step-by-step explanation:

Find the rate,

500/2 = 250

400/2 = 200

250/1500 + 200/1500, they each finish this fraction of the job in one hour.

Simplify then solve,

= 1/6 + 1/7.5

= 3/10, is the fraction of the job they can finish together in one hour.

Therefore,

(3/10) (1500) = 450 per hour

they can install 450 bulbs in one hour.

We divide 1500 by 450 to get the total time it will take to install that amount of bulbs,

1500/450 = 33/10

= 3.3 hours

Further simplify,

.3 x 60 = 18

Answer: 3 hours and 18 minutes
