22. EXPLAIN THE FACTORS AFFECTING SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT IN HOTEL INDUSTRY.​


Answer:

Supply chain management in the hotel industry is affected by various factors such as procurement, distribution, transportation, and vendor management. The cost of goods, availability of raw materials, transportation costs, local regulations, supplier reliability, lead times, and seasonality of demand can all impact the supply chain. In addition, the rise of e-commerce and new technologies has increased competition, leading to the need for hotels to optimize their supply chain processes to remain competitive and meet customer demands.
