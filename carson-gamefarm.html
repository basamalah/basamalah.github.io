18. Jayden and Carson are selling T-shirts and sweatshirts with the school logo. Jayden sells 9 T-shirts and 3 sweatshirts for $288. Carson sells 1 T-shirt and 6 sweatshirts for $270. Find the selling price of each item. Write a system of equations to represent this situation.


Answer:

Let

[tex]x - selling \: price \: of \: t - shirt \: \: \: \: \: \: \\ y - selling \: price \: of \:sweatshirts[/tex]

Equation 1,

[tex]9x + 3y = 288[/tex]

Equation 2,

[tex]x + 6y = 270[/tex]

Solve for x and y using either Substitution Method or Elimination Method.

By Substitution Method,

Choose one between the two equations and solve for either x or y then substitute to other equation.

For instance,

(In this case I will choose equation 2)

[tex]x + 6y = 270 \\ x = 270 - 6y[/tex]

Substitute to equation 1,

[tex]9x + 3y = 288[/tex]

[tex]9(270 - 6y) + 3y = 288[/tex]

[tex]2430 - 54y + 3y = 288[/tex]

[tex] - 51y = 288 - 2430[/tex]

[tex] - 51y = - 2142[/tex]

[tex] \frac{ - 51y}{ - 51} = \frac{ - 2142}{ - 5} [/tex]

[tex]y = 42[/tex]

Substitute y = 42 to equation 1 or 2 to solve x.

For Equation 2,

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: x + 6y = 270 \\ x + 6(42) = 270 \\ \: \: \: x + 252= 270 \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: x = 270 - 252 \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: x = 18[/tex]

Therefore, the selling price of T-shirt and Sweatshirt is $18 and $42, respectively.

26. Who feared the following scenario, Malthus, Carson, or Hardin ?1. Overgrazing :2. Population boom :3. Harmful pesticides :​


Answer:

Pesticides are used to protect crops against insects, weeds, fungi and other pests.

Pesticides are potentially toxic to humans and can have both acute and chronic health effects, depending on the quantity and ways in which a person is exposed.

Some of the older, cheaper pesticides can remain for years in soil and water. These chemicals have been banned from agricultural use in developed countries, but they are still used in many developing countries.

People who face the greatest health risks from exposure to pesticides are those who come into contact with them at work, in their home or garden.

Pesticides play a significant role in food production. They protect or increase yields and the number of times per year a crop can be grown on the same land. This is particularly important in countries that face food shortages.

To protect food consumers from adverse effects of pesticides, WHO reviews evidence and develops internationally-accepted maximum residue limits.
