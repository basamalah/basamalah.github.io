REPRODUCE OF DOG

Her pregnancy goes inside her baby so it is called internal fertilization. He is not an egg like a snake. The baby dog comes out of the so-called vulva seen in the female dog. There in the so-called vulva is when you give birth there your baby dog will come out. That is why she is called born alive because the baby goes inside the womb of female dog. So when it comes out he will be a puppy and he will live. Her life cycle will be her baby first, second she will be a puppy, third she will be adolescence and fourth she will be big or adult dog.

REPRODUCE OF FROG

Her pregnancy goes outside her baby so it is called external fertilization. He is an egg like. The baby frog comes out of the so-called egg seen in the female frog. That is why she is called laying egg because the baby goes outside the womb of female frog. So when it comes out he will be a frog and he will live. Her life cycle will be her baby first or she will be eggs, second she will be a tadpole with a legs, third she will be young frog and fourth she will be big or adult.
