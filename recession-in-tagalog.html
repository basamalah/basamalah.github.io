6. example of recessive traits?​


Answer:

Examples of Recessive Traits

For example, having a straight hairline is recessive, while having a widow's peak (a V-shaped hairline near the forehead) is dominant. Cleft chin, dimples, and freckles are similar examples; individuals with recessive alleles for a cleft chin, dimples, or freckles do not have these traits.

When a trait is recessive, an individual must have two copies of a recessive allele to express the trait. Recessive alleles are denoted by a lowercase letter (a versus A). Only individuals with an aa genotype will express a recessive trait; therefore, offspring must receive one recessive allele from each parent to exhibit a recessive trait.

When a trait is recessive, an individual must have two copies of a recessive allele to express the trait. Recessive alleles are denoted by a lowercase letter (a versus A). Only individuals with an aa genotype will express a recessive trait; therefore, offspring must receive one recessive allele from each parent to exhibit a recessive trait.One example of a recessive inherited trait is a smooth chin, as opposed to a dominant cleft chin. Let (S) represent the dominant allele, and (s) represent the recessive allele. Only (ss) individuals will express a smooth chin. To determine the probability of inheritance of a smooth chin (or any other recessive trait), the genotypes of the parents must be considered. If one parent is heterozygous (Ss) and the other is homozygous recessive (ss), then half of their offspring will have a smooth chin.

15. Which is recessive? ​


Answer:

Recessive is a quality found in the relationship between two versions of a gene. Individuals receive one version of a gene, called an allele, from each parent. If the alleles are different, the dominant allele will be expressed, while the effect of the other allele, called recessive, is masked.

Answer:

22. Anu ang recession sa business cycle , tagalog pls.


Recession

Ang recession, sa ekonomiks, ay ang pag-urong  ng ikot ng negosyo sa kung saan mayroong isang pangkalahatang pagbawas sa aktibidad sa pang-ekonomiya. Ang mga rekord o recession ay karaniwang nangyayari kapag mayroong isang malawak na pagbagsak sa paggasta o adverse demand shock.

Ang recession ay isang negatibong epekto o masamang pangitain sa isang negosyo. Ang pag-urong o recession ay nangyayari kapag mayroong dalawa o higit pang magkakasunod na mga bahagi ng negatibong paglago ng ekonomiya, na nangangahulugang mga kontrata sa paglago ng GDP o Gross Domestic Products sa panahon ng pag-urong. Kung ang isang ekonomiya ay nahaharap sa pag-urong o recession, bumababa ang mga benta ng negosyo at kita, na nagiging sanhi ng pagtigil sa pagpapalawak o sa pagpapatakbo ng isang negosyo.
