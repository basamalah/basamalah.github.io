1.What is Kirznerian entrepreneurship?

-Below, we review Kirzner's alertness theory of entrepreneurship. Kirzner argues that entrepreneurs balance supply and demand by detecting market imperfections and exploiting them. Market imperfections are caused by information asymmetry and bounded rationality.

2.How does Ismael Kirzner view entrepreneurship?

-In Kirzner's view, which he and others refer to as a distinct viewpoint of the Austrian school of economics, the main characteristic of the entrepreneur is alertness. The entrepreneur is alert to price differences that others have not noticed and makes a profit by acting on this alertness.

24. What the solution (s+6)(s+-2)


You use the foil method, which stands for (first, outside,inside, and last)

(s+6)(s+-2) or you can write it as (s+6)(s-2)

First multiply the "first"

(s+6)(s-2) • multiply s and s = s^2 (s squared)

Then "outside"

(s+6)(s-2) • multiply s and -2 = -2s

Then "inside"

(s+6)(s-2) • multiply 6 and s = 6s

Then multiply "last"

(s+6)(s-2) • multiply 6 and -2 = -12

So your product are = s^2 - 2s + 6s - 12

Because you have two same factors or two same "digits" which are the one with s (s alone, not with the square) you will have to combine them (the similar ones, in this case -2s and 6s)

Which gives us 4s.

So your final answer will be s^2(s squared)+4s-12
