1. 2. At what phase of the research investigation is the conceptual framerwork written? ​


Answer:

Review of Related Literature

Explanation:

It is because a conceptual framework represents the researcher’s synthesis of the literature on how to explain a phenomenon. It maps out the actions required in the course of the study, given his previous knowledge of other researchers’ point of view and his observations on the subject of research. In other words, the conceptual framework is the researcher’s understanding of how the particular variables in his study connect. Thus, it identifies the variables required in the research investigation. It is the researcher’s “map” in pursuing the investigation.

9. what is conceptual framework in research? ​


Answer:

A conceptual framework illustrates what you expect to find through your research. It defines the relevant variables for your study and maps out how they might relate to each other. You should construct a conceptual framework before you begin collecting data. It is often represented in a visual format.

16. what is the importance of conceptualization in research?​


Answer:

Conceptualization is the process in which the researchers identify key concepts used in the research and provide a unified explanation of those concepts so that both the research team and the audience is on the same page.


17. conceptualization definition in research​


Answer:

Conceptualization is a process of defining meaning of the terms used in a study (e.g., definition using concepts and words) based on previous scholarship. Utilizing prior research provides a basis for creating agreement upon variable conceptualization within the field.
