24. What is the meaning of "The family that prays together stays together"?


Answer:

What does this mean, "The family that prays together stays together"?

It means that the most important thing to a person is his bonding with his family. The family should be the first thing that we should prioritize in life. Our family should be our comfort place where we can express ourselves, feel loved and accept for who we are. Our basis of love, respect, and honesty, depends on how our family showed and portrayed to us. In relation to prayer, our family should also be the first people who care and pray for us. We should help one another and meet each other's needs. As we do this, our relationship becomes stronger and our bond goes unbreakable. We should not be ashamed with our family members because they are our refuge in times of stress and difficulties.

How can we make our family relationship gets strongerStart showing your true personality. We have this tendency to portray a personality acceptable by everybody just to hide the true situations happening inside our family. Those who experience maltreatment by family members tend to rebel against them, doing things that will make them angry or get their attention. With this, they are covering up the pain and disappointment from their own family. As family members, there is a need to be accepted. Still, the respect for you parents and their decisions should be respected too.Don't hesitate to express emotions. Your home is the most suitable place for you to express who you are. The reason why most of us have identity problems because we are unable to express ourselves in our homes. Here we discover our skills, talents, and interests that we have and hone it for future career. People who are accepted at home tends to be confident and can handle stress in every situation they face. As parents, there is a need also for us to make our family feel that we are for them. It is not enough for us to provide for their needs but also be with them when they need us the most. Be there for them. Most of the families nowadays feel that their family members are far apart even though they are just a couple of chairs away from them. Meaning to say, family members don't talk nor care for others because they we're too occupied by the things that they do. Our home should be a place of rest and relaxation, not an extension of workplace and studies. Technologies also creates barriers of communication where family members are more expressive in their social media accounts but in reality, they don't really talk a lot. It is very important to close the gap within our family because if it is too wide, they will look for someone who will understand them.

As to conclude, it is very essential to keep our family ties stronger than every. Love and help each other so that you can face hardships with ease because you are not alone. You have a family to back you up.
