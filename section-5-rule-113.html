4. what is 62% of 113?​


Answer:

54.87%

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution for 62 is what percent of 113:

62:113*100 =  

(62*100):113 =  

6200:113 = 54.87

Now we have: 62 is what percent of 113 = 54.87  

Question: 62 is what percent of 113?  

Percentage solution with steps:

Step 1: We make the assumption that 113 is 100% since it is our output value.  

Step 2: We next represent the value we seek with x.

Step 3: From step 1, it follows that 100%=113.

Step 4: In the same vein, x% = 62.

Step 5: This gives us a pair of simple equations:

100% = 113(1)

x% = 62(2)

Step 6: By simply dividing equation 1 by equation 2 and taking note of the fact that both the LHS

(left - hand side) of both equations have the same unit (%); we have

100%/ x% = 113/62

Step 7: Taking the inverse (or reciprocal) of both sides yields

x%/100% = 62/113

-> x = 54.87%

Therefore, 62 is 54.87% of 113
