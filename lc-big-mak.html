3. Given. LA = LB LC LB = (C Prove: LA = LC​


Given: LA = LB and LC = LB

To prove that LA = LC we can use the transitive property of equality, which states that if A = B and B = C, then A = C.

LA = LB (given)

LC = LB (given)

therefore

LA = LC (by the transitive property of equality)

So by substituting the given information, we can prove that if LA = LB and LC = LB then LA = LC.
