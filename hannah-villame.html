15. D= light skin, d=dark skin. Hannah's mother is homozygous dark skin and Hannah's father is heterozygous light skin. What is the probability that Hannah has dark skin? ​


Answer:

50%

Explanation:

Punnett square

-----D-----d

d--Dd---dd

d--Dd----dd

There are two genotypes of Dd and two of dd. Dd is light skin, dd is dark skin. Therefore, the chance of having dark skin is 50%.

Mothers genotype: dd

Fathers genotype: Dd

23. Compare a first voyage around the world by magellan(Antonio Pigafetta and Magellan song by yoyoy villame.​


Explanation:

Magellan is known for leading the first voyage around the world, which took place between 1519 and 1522. He set out with five ships and 270 men, but only one ship and 18 men completed the journey. Along the way, they faced many challenges including harsh weather conditions, mutiny among the crew, attacks from native tribes, and a lack of provisions.

Antonio Pigafetta was a member of Magellan's crew and chronicled the journey in his diary. His detailed accounts provide a valuable insight into what the crew experienced during their historic journey.

On the other hand, Yoyoy Villame's song "Magellan" recounts the story of Magellan's journey in a light-hearted and catchy manner. The song has become a popular cultural reference in the Philippines.

Although the tone and style of Pigafetta's diary and Villame's song are vastly different, both provide a glimpse into the enduring legacy of Magellan's journey and its impact on world history

25. 01. Identify whether Yoyoy Villame's novelty song "Magellan" is a Historical Primary Source or Historical Secondary Source. Explain your answer. 02. Is Yoyoy Villame's novelty song "Magellan" a credible source of historical details of Ferdinand Magellan's arrival in the Philippines? Explain your answer. pa help po​


Answer:

1. The novelty song "Magellan" by Yoyoy Villame counts as a historical secondary source. After all, it is simply a recounting of historical events based on reports of other historians. It cannot possibly be a primary source since it is not a direct account by someone who lived through those times.

2. In my opinion, it is not a credible source. After all, some of the details in the song were made up so that they would rhyme with a different line. For example, there is no way to tell for sure that he arrived at noon as the song claims. This is also true for this part: "Oh, mother, mother, I am sick . Call the doctor very quick. Doctor, doctor, shall I die? Tell my mama, do not cry . Tell my mama, do not cry . Tell my mama, do not cry." On top of that, it is a novelty song and does not claim to be a reliable source.
