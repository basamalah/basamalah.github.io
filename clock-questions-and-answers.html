28. Suppose it is now November (11th month). What month is 8 months later? Write a 12-hour clock addition equation that supports your answer to the question above.​


Answer:

8 months later would be July

Step-by-step explanation:

The 7th month. In terms of 12-hour clock addition, you can write an equation as follows: 11 + 8 = 7. This equation shows that if you add the number corresponding to the current month (11 for November) to the number of months that have passed since then (8), you get the number corresponding to the month that will be 8 months later (7 for July).
