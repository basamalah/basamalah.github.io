2. criteria for judging dance contest


Answer:

This is an example of criteria for judging a dance contest:

•Choreography (Creativity, Artistry, Style & Originality) - 40%

•Execution - 10%

•Audience Impact- 10%

•(Timing & Coordination, Projection & Expression) - 30%

•Costume - 10%

Why is criteria for judging important?

•It is important for judges to know how to score and important for contestants to know what they should improve and focus on their performance.

#AnswerForTrees

In judging a dance contest, it's either you or the organizers should have a prepared criteria. However, here are the basic criteria for judging a dance contest:

A) Choreography - this includes the sequence of dance steps in a dance act or performance. This criteria must include the creativity, artistry, style, and originality of dance steps.

B) Execution - this includes the projection and expression, timing and coordination of each member during the performance

C) Costume - this is also a vital part in a dance act. Costumes make the performance come to life depending on what they are portraying/depending on the concept of the dance act/performance

D) Audience impact - audience's claps, chants and cheers is also part of the criteria because performances are made for an audience that is why their reaction matters as well.

Criteria for judging should be divided in percentages totaling 100 percent. For example, Choreography is 40 percent, Execution is 30 percent, Costume is 20 percent, Audience impact is 10 percent for a total of 100 percent.

Criteria matters for it to be fair to all the performances being judged.

22. Criteria for judging musical play percentage


Answer:

Criteria for judging musical play and its percentage:

Acting 40%Fidelity to original work 15%Production 20%Stage Presentation 10%Dramatic Achievement 15%

Explanation

Acting - This involves characterization, use of voice and energy levels, movement, teamwork use of stage space.Fidelity to original work - originality to the piece, use of language.Production - method of performance, efficient use of space, relevance of the musical soundtrack.Stage Presentation - costumes, props, audience appeal.Dramatic Achievement - performance craft, level of difficulty.

There are also an additional criteria that will make the performers standout:

Vocal Tone - quality of the voice, pleasing and enjoyable audio.Musicianship - correct execution of notes, phrasing and rhythms.Intonation and Projection - Ability to sing on high pitch, volume, breath and vocal range.Characterization - appropriate and consistent physical mannerisms, tones of voice, rhythm that will portray the specific character.Speech and vocal production - performance are vocally appropriate.Overall impression - The "wow" factor.

28. Criteria for judging zumba dance contest


A zumba dance is a form of exercise fitness program that involves dance and aerobic movements performed through energetic music. The program was developed by Colombian dancer and choreographer Alberto Perez in the 1990s. A zumba dance can also be held as a competition among group of dancers.
