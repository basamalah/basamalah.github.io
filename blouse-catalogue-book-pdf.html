19. 3 ,MS ,dayacap the school librarian outalogued 171 books in 3 days at this rate how many books can sha catalogue in 10 days?​


Answer:

Given:

Number of books catalogued by the school librarian in 3 days = 171 books

We need to find out how many books the school librarian can catalog in 10 days at the same rate.

Let's calculate the rate of cataloging first:

Rate = Number of books / Number of days

Rate = 171 books / 3 days

Rate = 57 books/day

Now, we can use this rate to calculate the number of books that can be catalogued in 10 days:

Number of books catalogued in 10 days = Rate × Number of days

Number of books catalogued in 10 days = 57 books/day × 10 days

Number of books catalogued in 10 days = 570 books

So, the school librarian can catalogue 570 books in 10 days at the same rate
