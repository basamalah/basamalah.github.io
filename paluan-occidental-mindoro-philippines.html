17. makasaysayang pangyayari ng occidental mindoro​


Answer:

Occidental Mindoro (Tagalog: Kanlurang Mindoro, Kinaray-a: Natungdan ka Mindoro, Ilocano: Laud nga Mindoro, Spanish: Mindoro Occidental) (ISO: PH-MDC) is a province in the Philippines located in the Mimaropa region. The province occupies the western half of the island of Mindoro. Its capital is Mamburao, but the largest municipality is San Jose. As of 2015, Occidental Mindoro has 487,414 inhabitants.
