9. Today is magnificent sunny day and the guests as the crown regency hotel are eating dinner on their balconies. A Caucasian accidentally knocks an empty wine bottle off a balcony that is about 80 meters (260 feet) above the granite pathway. The glass would have smashed on that pathway after falling for 4 seconds, however, a quick-witted hotel crew catches the glasses after it has fallen for only 2 seconds. How far above the pathway was the glass when it was caught?​


Answer:

20.38 m

Explanation:

First we have to get the intiial velocity of the wine bottle. To do this, we can use the kinematic formula S=Vit+1/2at^2. Substitute the values we have 80=(Vi)(4)+(9.81)(4)^2. Arranging the equations would give us the value of the initial velocity of 0.38 m/s. Now to get the height to which the glass was caught, we use again the kinematic formula S=Vit+1/2at^2. Substituing the values we have S=(0.38)(2)+1/2(9.81)(2)^2 and therefore 20.38 m
