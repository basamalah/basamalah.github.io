5. Express your gratefulness for having a parent/guardian that is always there to guide and love you in a letter.


Answer:

It is absolutely valuable to have parents who support you, listen to you, and are there for you when the going gets difficult in any sense of the word. If worked on from the start and nourished regularly, the family link can be tremendously powerful. It's the perfect support group for when life becomes too much.

More Answer:

Growing up, children require love. It's not about pampering them or making them feel special. It's about instilling in them the value of love and demonstrating how interpersonal relationships should function.

I'm not sure why people believe that understanding how to love comes naturally to everyone. It's not the case. Even if it's a natural occurrence, we still need to learn how to do it properly. It may be more difficult for parents to develop healthy ties with their children later in life if they do not have excellent relationships with their own children.
