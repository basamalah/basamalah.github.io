29. Suppose you were asked to conduct a survey about the parents' choice for their children's mode of learning for SY 2020-2021. STEP 1. STEP 2. STEP 3. STEP 4. STEP 5.​


Sure, I can provide you with a general outline for conducting a survey about parents' choice for their children's mode of learning for SY 2020-2021. Here are the five steps:

STEP 1: Define the purpose and scope of the survey.

- Determine the specific information you want to gather about parents' choice for their children's mode of learning.

- Decide on the target population (e.g. parents of students in a specific grade level, parents in a specific geographic area, etc.).

- Determine the sample size (i.e. the number of parents you want to survey) and the sampling method (e.g. random sampling, stratified sampling, etc.).

STEP 2: Design the survey instrument.

- Create a list of questions that will help you gather the information you need.

- Decide on the type of questions (e.g. multiple-choice, open-ended, rating scale, etc.).

- Ensure that the questions are clear, concise, and unbiased.

- Consider including demographic questions to help you understand the characteristics of the respondents.

STEP 3: Pre-test the survey instrument.

- Administer the survey to a small group of parents to test the questions and identify any issues with the survey instrument.

- Make any necessary revisions to the survey instrument based on the feedback received.

STEP 4: Administer the survey.

- Decide on the mode of administration (e.g. online survey, paper survey, phone survey, etc.).

- Distribute the survey to the target population.

- Set a deadline for respondents to complete the survey.

STEP 5: Analyze and report the results.

- Collect and organize the responses.

- Use appropriate statistical methods to analyze the data.

- Create a report that summarizes the findings and includes any relevant charts or graphs.

- Draw conclusions and make recommendations based on the results of the survey.
