2. pls give me explanation about James March​


Answer:

James March was an American social scientist and organizational theorist who made significant contributions to the fields of decision-making, leadership, and organizational behavior. He was born in 1928 and passed away in 2018.

March is best known for his work on bounded rationality, which suggests that decision-makers are limited by their cognitive abilities, information availability, and time constraints. He argued that individuals and organizations often make decisions that are not fully rational or optimal, but rather based on heuristics, rules of thumb, and satisficing strategies.

March also developed the concept of garbage can model, which describes how decisions are made in organizations that have multiple problems, solutions, and decision-makers. According to this model, decision-making is a messy and unpredictable process that involves a mix of rational and irrational factors, and can lead to surprising outcomes.

In addition to his work on decision-making, March also studied leadership, power, and organizational culture. He argued that leaders are not necessarily the most powerful or influential individuals in an organization, but rather those who are able to shape the norms, values, and beliefs of their followers.

Overall, James March was a pioneering figure in the field of organizational theory, whose ideas continue to influence research and practice in management and leadership.

9. Hanapin ang kahulugan 1. Partido Komunista 2. Long March 3. Guerilla War 4. Cultural Revolution 5. Great Leap Forward


Answer:

1. Partido Komunista

Ang Partido Komunista ng Pilipinas ay isang pinakakaliwa, Marxist-Leninist-Maoist na rebolusyonaryong organisasyon ng partidoKomunista sa Pilipinas na binuo ni Jose Maria Sison noong Disyembre 6 1968.

2. Long March

Ang Long March ay isang pag-atras ng militar na isinagawa ng Pulang Hukbo ng Partido Komunista ng Tsina, ang nangunguna sa Hukbong Pagpapalaya ng Bayan, upang iwasan ang pagtugis ng hukbong Kuomintang.

3. Guerilla War

Ang pakikidigmaang gerilya ay isang anyo ng hindi regular na pakikidigmakung saan ang maliliit na grupo ng manlalaban, tulad ng mga tauhan ngparamilitar, armadong sibilyan, o mga irregular ay gumagamit ng mgataktika ng militar kabilang ang mga ambus, sabotahe, pagsalakay, maliit napakikidigma, hit-and-run na taktika, at mobility, upang labanan ang isang mas malaki at hindi gaanong mobile tradisyonal na militar.

4. Cultural Revolution

Ang Rebolusyong Pangkultura, na pormal na kilala bilang Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution ay isang sosyo politikal sa Tsina mula 1966 hanggangsa kamatayan ni Mao Zedong noong 1976.

5. Great Leap Forward

Ang Great Leap Forward ng People's Republic of China ay isang pang-ekonomiya at panlipunang kampanya na pinamumunuan ng Partido Komunista ng Tsina mula 1958 hanggang 1962.
