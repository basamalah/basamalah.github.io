11. how to not overheat a computer


Answer:

One of th best ways to avoid computer from being overheated is to Keep your Computer away from windows and vents. This can make your computer warm which may lead to Overheating. Another one is to always clean the fans of the computer and to always close the System's case

Answer:

1. Regularly clean the inside of the CPU. Dust accumulates inside because there is a ventilation system there. You may use compressed air or just dust off the inside. Always make sure that the computer is not plugged in so you won't be electrocuted.

2. Make sure that the computer is not placed in a hot area, make sure that it is well ventilated and that it is not exposed directly to sunlight.

3. Upgrade the cooling inside the CPU. Some people connect more fans to their CPU or just upgrade to a water cooling system.

4. Don't leave the computer on for too long when it is not in use. You will save electricity and it will help you prolong your computer's life.
