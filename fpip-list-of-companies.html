17. Listing method for flipping a coin and rolling a dice


➛Question:

Listing method for flipping a coin and rolling a dice

Answer:Method for flipping a coin:

Hold the coin between your thumb and index finger.Decide which side of the coin you want to be "heads" and which side you want to be "tails."Flip the coin into the air.Let it spin and come down onto a flat surface.Look at the result to see if it landed on the side you designated as heads or tails.

Method for rolling a dice:

Take the dice in your hand.Shake it lightly to ensure that it's evenly balanced.Toss the dice onto a flat surface, making sure it bounces at least once.Wait for the dice to come to a stop.Read the number on the top face of the dice to determine the result.
