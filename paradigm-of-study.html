28.  It is also called the research paradigm, wherein it is a presentation of the study through the use of a diagram. * a. Concept Map b. Conceptual Framework c. Graphic Organizer d. Correlational Diagram ​


Answer:

B.

Explanation:

The research paradigm, also known as the conceptual framework, is a visual representation of the study that aids in understanding complex relationships and interconnections. This tool can take various forms, such as a concept map, graphic organizer, or correlational diagram, to name a few. Its purpose is to provide a comprehensive and organized overview of the research, highlighting key concepts, variables, and their interdependence. By utilizing this approach, researchers can better grasp the intricacies of their study and develop more informed hypotheses and research questions.
