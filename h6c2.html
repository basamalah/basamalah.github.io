2ab = 2 • (4c) • 1

a2 + 2ab + b2 = (a + b)2, where a = 4c and b = 1. (4c + 1)2

23. C. Directions: Factor completely then check the factors using the FOIL Method. 1. 9b4 – 36c2


Answer:

9b⁴-36c²

Factor out 9.

9(b⁴-4c²)

Consider b⁴-4c². Rewrite b⁴-4c² as (b²)²-(2c)². The difference of squares can be factored using the rule: p²-q²=(p-q)(p+q).

(b²-2c)(b²+2c)

Rewrite the complete factored expression.

=9(b²-2c)(b²+2c)

Checking w/FOIL method:

(b²-2c)(b²+2c)

=b⁴+2b²c-2b²c-4c²

=9(b⁴-4c²)

=9b⁴-36c²
