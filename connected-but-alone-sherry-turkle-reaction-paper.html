16. how is connected but alone of Sherry turkles related to problems in our present day world? ​


Explanation:

In "Connected, but Alone?", Sherry Turkle discusses how our increasing reliance on technology for communication and social interaction can lead to feelings of loneliness, disconnection, and a lack of empathy. Turkle argues that as we become more attached to our devices and social media, we may become less capable of forming genuine connections with others.

These ideas are still highly relevant in our present-day world. With the COVID-19 pandemic and the resulting social distancing measures, people have had to rely even more heavily on technology to communicate and maintain social connections. While this has been beneficial in some ways, it has also highlighted some of the negative effects of our reliance on technology, such as increased screen time and feelings of isolation.

Moreover, issues such as cyberbullying, online harassment, and the spread of misinformation have become increasingly prevalent in our digital world. These issues can further contribute to feelings of disconnection and loneliness.

In summary, Turkle's discussion of the challenges posed by our reliance on technology for communication and social interaction is still highly relevant today, as these issues continue to impact our lives in various ways
