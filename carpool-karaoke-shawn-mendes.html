23. Shawn uses 40N of force to stop the cart from running his foot over. If Shawn does 100 J of work, how far away was the cart?


CALCULATE DISTANCE WITH WORK AND FORCE

Shawn uses 40N of force to stop the cart from running his foot over. If Shawn does 100 J of work, how far away was the cart?

ANSWERThe cart was 2.50 meters away from Shawn.

GIVEN

f = 40 N

w = 100 J

d = ?

SOLUTION

Work is known to be equal to the amount of energy exerted in an object multiplied by its distance.

From this, we can create a formula and calculate the distance that the cart with the given force and energy.

[tex]w = f \times d \\ \frac{w}{f} = d \\ d = \frac{100}{40} \\ d= \frac{5}{2} \\ d = 2 \frac{1}{2} \\ d = 2.50[/tex]
