The product element refers to the goods or services that a company offers to its customers. This includes the design, features, and benefits of the product, as well as any additional services or support provided by the company.

The price element refers to the amount of money that customers must pay in order to purchase the product or service. This includes not only the initial purchase price, but also any additional fees or charges that may be associated with the product, such as taxes, shipping, or installation costs.

The place element refers to the distribution channels and locations where the product is made available to customers. This includes the physical locations where the product can be purchased, as well as any online or digital platforms that are used to sell the product.
