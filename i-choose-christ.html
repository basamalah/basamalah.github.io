30. I follow Christ's deeds through...​


Answer: I follow Christ's deeds through denying myself and trying so hard to carry my cross everyday.

Explanation: According to Jesus in Luke 9:23, "...If any of you wants to be my follower, you must give up your own way, take up your cross daily, and follow me."

Jesus died in the cross for our sins. He wants us not only to have eternal life, but to live our lives in full. He wants us to have a life full of purpose.

Jesus is very purposeful. His purpose from the very beginning has always been to spread the love of the Father to everyone-- even the Gentiles (non-Jews). He spreads the love of the Lord through both His words and actions.

I follow His deeds through denying myself everyday.  People can be mean sometimes. Some of them do mean things intentionally, some of them don't. I get tempted to take even, but I am reminded to deny my ugly desires to hurt others and follow Jesus who didn't just choose not to get even to those who hated Him, but also to die for His enemies.

I try hard to take up my cross daily. I have a lot of things that I want to do in mind, but some of them are not of the Lord. It's difficult and most of the time, I fail. I fail, but then I don't give up. I try again. I 'll try and try until God has perfected what He has begun in me.
