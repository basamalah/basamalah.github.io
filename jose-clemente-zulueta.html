28. why did rafael zulueta title his poem like the molave? ​


Answer:

The title itself suggests that we Filipinos of today must be like the Molave. We need to be firm staunch and resilient. We should “not rest in peace!”.

Answer:

Rafael Zulueta titled his poem "Like the Molave" to compare the qualities of the titular tree species, which was known for its strength and endurance, to the Filipino people and their perseverance in the face of hardship.
