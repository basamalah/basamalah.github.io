11. tools used in handicraft and how to take care of them


Answer:

1. HOT GLUE GUN

No matter what type of crafting you’re doing, chances are you’ve run into a situation where you need an adhesive that’s a little stronger than your average glue stick. If you’re like us, you fire up that hot glue gun more often than you would think.

2. SCISSORS

Having a nice pair of scissors on hand that can cut through a wide variety of items isn’t a must-have just for crafting. Think of all the times you use your craft scissors in everyday life, too! Keep several pairs on hand for a variety of craft projects and everyday uses.

3. WRITING UTENSILS: MARKERS, PENS, CRAYONS AND COLORED PENCILS

You take the time to create beautiful crafts, so when it comes time to use writing utensils on them, don’t just grab any old pen from the junk drawer. Invest in a wide variety of colors and styles of markers, pens, crayons and more. These also double as great tools for kids crafting or school projects.

4. CRAFT CORD, THREAD AND WIRE

Craft cord, thread and wire can be used on a wide variety of craft projects, from beading to floral, jewelry making and more. Keep a variety of styles on hand for quick household fixes and crafting uses.

5. GLITTER

Maybe it’s just us, but glitter is one of our crafting essentials. It’s an easy way to add sparkle and shine to a wide variety of projects, making them sure to catch the eye of many. Glitter can be used for large projects or in small doses.
