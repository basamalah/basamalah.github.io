28. The girl scouts from Balibago Elementary school joined the GSP Junior Encampment held at Tanauan City. They left their school at 10:35 am and reached the campsite at 1:40 pm. How many hours did they spend in travelling from their school to the campsite?​


The departure time is 10:35 am, and the arrival time is 1:40 pm. We can convert these times to a 24-hour clock for easier computation.

10:35 am = 10:35 in 24-hour clock

1:40 pm = 13:40 in 24-hour clock

To calculate the elapsed time, we can subtract the departure time from the arrival time:

13:40 - 10:35 = 3 hours and 5 minutes

Therefore, the girl scouts spent 3 hours and 5 minutes in traveling from their school to the campsite.
