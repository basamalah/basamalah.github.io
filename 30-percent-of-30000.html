=====================================

First, converting R percent to r a decimal

r = R/100 = 4%/100 = 0.04 per year,

putting time into years for simplicity,

12 months ÷ 12 months/year = 1 years,

then, solving our equation

I = 30000 × 0.04 × 1 = 1200

I = $ 1,200.00

at a rate of 4% per year
