7. In June 12, 2014, a newly married couple invested Php 240, 000 in an account that pays 13% compounded quarterly for the purchase of house and lot. How much will the couple have on June 12, 2020?


To solve this problem, we need to determine how many quarters have passed between June 12, 2014 and June 12, 2020. Then, we need to calculate how much the initial investment of PHP 240,000 will be worth after that many quarters at an interest rate of 13% compounded quarterly.

To determine the number of quarters that have passed, we first need to determine the number of years that have passed. There are 12 months in a year, so to determine the number of years that have passed, we can divide the number of months that have passed by 12. There are 6 years between 2014 and 2020, so that means there are 6 * 12 = <<6*12=72>>72 months that have passed. Since a quarter consists of 3 months, there have been 72 / 3 = <<72/3=24>>24 quarters that have passed.

To calculate the future value of the initial investment, we can use the formula for compound interest: FV = PV * (1 + r/n) ^ nt, where FV is the future value, PV is the present value (initial investment), r is the interest rate, n is the number of times per year that the interest is compounded, and t is the number of years. In this case, the present value is PHP 240,000, the interest rate is 13% (0.13), the number of times per year that the interest is compounded is 4 (quarterly), and the number of years is 6. Plugging these values into the formula, we get FV = 240,000 * (1 + 0.13/4) ^ (4 * 6) = PHP 613,261.93.

Therefore, the couple will have PHP 613,261.93 on June 12, 2020.


8. Ang pangunahing bansang may mataas na pagluluwas ang pilipinas ayon sa datos ng Philippine Statistical Authority Ng June 2014​


Ang bansang Pilipinas ay may pinakamataas na pagluluwas noong 2014 sa bansang Hapon. 22.4 porsyento ang kabuuang iniluwas ng Pilipinas patungo sa lupa ng mga Hapones. Ito ay nangangahulugan na 13.901 bilyong dolyar ang naibenta ng Pilipinas sa mga Hapon.

Ang pangunahing kalakal na iniluwas ng ating bansa ay mga elektronikong produkto na nagkakahalaga ng 3.129 bilyong dolyar. Sumusunod dito ang mga kalakal na gawa sa kahoy at muwebles sa halaga na 3.023 bilyong dolyar.

Bukod sa bansang Hapon may iba pang mga bansa na nakikipagkalakalan sa bansang Pilipinas tulad ng mga bansang;

Tsina, pangalawang bansa na may pinakamalaking pagluluwas galing sa Pilipinas na bumubuo sa 14.3 porsyento ng kabuuang iniluwas ng Pilipinas.Amerika, ikatlong bansa na may 11.2 poryento ng iniluwas patungo sa kanilang bansa.Singapore, ikaapat na bansa pagdating sa pinakamaraming iniluwas galing sa Pilipinas (7.1 porsyento).Timog Korea, ikalimang bansa na may kabuuan na 6.0 porsyento ng kalakal na iniluwas patungo sa kanilang bansa.

She does this on June 30, 2014.nonsense answer will be report ty


Answer:

Transaction:

Date: June 30, 2014

Accounts Affected:

Debit: Kathy Lee's Drawings Account P 1,300

Credit: Cash/Bank Account P 1,300

Explanation:

Since Kathy Lee withdrew P 1,300 from the business bank account for personal use, the Cash/Bank account is credited because it is decreased by the withdrawal. On the other hand, Kathy Lee's Drawings account is debited because it is increased by the amount she withdrew. This transaction follows the double-entry system because for every debit, there must be a corresponding credit of equal amount
