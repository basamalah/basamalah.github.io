15. What did Julian Banzon discover and why is it an important discovery?


Dr. Banzon gained recognition for his notable researches on the Philippine coconut and other indigenous materials as “renewable sources of chemicals and fuels.” He pioneered in the study not just of the coconut, but also of the sugar cane, in the Philippines, and produced the first authoritative work on the derivation of fuels, specifically ethyl esters, from these two crops. Moreover, he developed new and scientific methods, such as the chemical extraction of surplus coconut oil, in place of the more commonly used physical process.
