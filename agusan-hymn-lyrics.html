21. complete lyrics of dcs hymn \


The acronym DCS means Division of Schools. DCS Hymn or the Division of Schools Manila Hymn is the official song created by the Department of Education as part of any program conducted by every school around Metro Manila or National Capital Region. All public schools or the schools who receive direct management from the Department of Education are required to sing this song in every program, that's why teachers or faculty are mandated to teach their students about this hymn so that students can participate during the singing of the hymn.
