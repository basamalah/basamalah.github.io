3. Acrostic poem using the word first aid


First Aid: An Acrostic Poem

First and foremost safety is one of the best policy

It is one thing one should or cannot take for granted

Relearn or learn what first aid is

So that you can help people who are in need of medical help

To equip yourself with life-saving skills

And by doing so, you can be of great help

In the society, and in your family

Do it now, relearn or learn first aid.

The poem tells us how important safety is (https://brainly.ph/question/2168729). It was emphasized that it is one of the things we should consider first over other things. It is a great skill if one knows even the basic first aid skills (https://brainly.ph/question/87276). We are encourage to relearn if we have forgotten it already or learn if we haven't learned it before. First aid trainings are given by the government or private organizations. These trainings are usually free. Hence, this is a perfect opportunity for everyone to equip themselves life-saving skills. By having skills in first aid, you cannot only help your family but also the society around you. Let us start showing enthusiasm in relearning and learning first aid for we do not know when will danger come (https://brainly.ph/question/231117).
