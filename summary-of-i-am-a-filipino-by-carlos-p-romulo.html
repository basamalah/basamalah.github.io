11. i am filipino carlos p romulo and melvin bonggalloy differences and similarities​


Answer:

The similarities between Carlos P. Romulo and Melvin Bonggolloy, both of whom were Filipinos, include that they both achieved high levels of success as political figures, leaders and writers. Carlos P. Romulo was a journalist, statesman and diplomat, who served as President of the United Nations General Assembly, among many other distinctions. Melvin Bonggolloy was a member of the Philippine Senate, representing Leyte from 1992 to 2001. They both strived for their country and advocated for the Filipino people. However, the main difference between them is that Carlos P. Romulo lived from 1899 to 1985, whereas Melvin Bonggolloy lived from 1945 to 2015.
