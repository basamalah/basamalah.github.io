Several types and suggestions of communication, namely:Organizational Communication

Organizational Communication is communication related to the company's internal problems or directed to institutions or persons within the company. Included in this type are official documents and articles, organizational charts, memorandums, notes, instructions, decrees, instructions, circulars and meetings.

Business Correspondence

Business Correspondence is all company activities related to correspondence. Communicators in business correspondence can be internal public, for example heads of branches, units, divisions, stockholders or the board of commissioners. It can also be the external public such as the government, the press, suppliers, consumers, customers, community and the wider public in general (stakeholders).

Promotional communications

Promotional communication is business communication directed at improving company image and sales, classified as promotional communication, which can take the form of oral/signal and gestural communication. Some of the following are promotional communications, public speaking, advertising, brochures/leafets, offering letters, exhibitions/promotions, billboards, moving signs, and Megatron.
