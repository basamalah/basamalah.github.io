2. What is the comprehension question in the story of the aged mother


Answer:

The comprehension question in the story of the aged mother is, "Why are people scared of growing old?"

Explanation:

The aged mother represents a typical old woman and her story shows how growing old can be frightening and lonely.

So, people are scared of growing old because it can be an isolating and difficult process and because it can feel like you are becoming irrelevant in the world.

5. do you agree that mother tongue or traditional language should be taught in every member of the family?why?answer the question comprehensively with not less than 100 words​


Answer: Generally no, however it also depends on where that family member plans to go in the future and how old they were when they were first introduced to the language. If everyone was fortunate enough to learn the language at an early age, then that is good. But if the person learned to live life without feeling the need to learn it, then there would be no point.

The reason I say no is because being able to communicate seems to be more important than knowing one specific language. It would help the family be closer if everyone could speak the language, but it is not necessarily required to have a stable family, especially to family of immigrants.
