2. Importance of facial photo analysis in orthodontics


Facial photo analysis in orthodontics is important because this allows the professional orthodontist to thoroughly study the patient's mouth. Dental treatment varies depending on the condition of the patient's oral condition.

Why do orthodontists need to record our mouth information?

It is necessary thing to do for these professionals to track and record the progress and condition of the patient's mouth. For instance, if someone is wearing teeth braces, having photographs of the previous and the present conditions may help the practitioner to identify the next steps to help improve teeth formation and achieve viable results.

What are the best ways to take dental photos?

face forward smileprofile relaxprofile smileleft lateral views with retractors onface forward with retractors on

These are only some of the ways dental photos might be useful, most especially when gathering before and after photographs of the patient's mouth.
