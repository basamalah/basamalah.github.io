a = 7 m/s^{2}

Formula:

[tex]a = \frac{v - v0}{t}[/tex]

[tex]v = 35m/s\\\\v0 = 0\\\\t = 5.0s[/tex]

--

Solution:

[tex]a = \frac{v - v0}{t}[/tex]

[tex]a = \frac{35m/s - 0}{5s}[/tex]

[tex]a = \frac{35m/s}{5s}\\\\a = 7 m/s^{2}[/tex]
