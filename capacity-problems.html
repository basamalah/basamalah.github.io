28. The capacity of container A is 2.5 e The capacity of container B is 8 times the capacity of container A. What is the total capacity of both containers?​


The capacity of container A is 2.5 units

The capacity of container B is 8 times the capacity of container A:

Capacity of B = 8 × Capacity of A

Capacity of B = 8 × 2.5

Capacity of B = 20

So the capacity of container B is 20 units.

To find the total capacity of both containers, we add the capacities of A and B:

Total capacity = Capacity of A + Capacity of B

Total capacity = 2.5 + 20

Total capacity = 22.5

Therefore, the total capacity of both containers is 22.5 units.
