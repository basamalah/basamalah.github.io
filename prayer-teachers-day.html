8. should public schools begin the day with a silent prayer time?​


Answer:

No, public schools should not begin the day with a silent prayer time. This would be a violation of the separation of church and state, as mandated by the First Amendment to the United States Constitution.

Thank you for the way in which they give of themselves each day in the classroom, Serving and instructing the next generation of this land. We thank you for them all now.
