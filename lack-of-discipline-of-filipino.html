Answer:

What is an example of lack of discipline?

People think of discipline very differently. For most I would define discipline as the act of keeping commitments to yourself or others regardless of the extenuating circumstances. If you have promised yourself that you will work out even if it is raining and you do not do it you have lapsed in your discipline. If you have committed yourself to giving a certain amount of money to a charity but fail to do it for no good reason you have lapsed in your discipline. I believe that there are acceptable mitigating circumstances regarding discipline and staying true to commitments. Sometimes things just do not work out. Managing those circumstances as well as honoring your commitments, those circumstances not withstanding, is in itself a kind of discipline. Therefore any lapse in judgment or in action regarding your self-made commitments is a lack of discipline.
