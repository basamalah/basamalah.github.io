28. there are 18 north maids and 12 south maids serving in a hotel . give the ratio of the north to the entire group and express then into simple terms?​


Answer:

3/5

Step-by-step explanation

Add 18 north maids and 12 south maids so,

18+12=30

Then give the ratio of the North to the entire group

N : T   (N is north and T is the total)  

18 : 30 find the GCF of these numbers

GCF of 18 and 30 is 6

Divide both the numerator and denominator by the GCD

18 ÷ 6

30 ÷ 6

Reduced fraction:

3

5

Therefore, 18/30 simplified to lowest terms is 3/5.
