3. The sum of Annie's and Anna's age is 30. Anna's age is 9 years more than half of Annie's age. Find their age.​


Annie is 14 and Anna is 16.

Step-by-step explanation:

The first sentence of the problem states that the sum of their ages is 30. We can treat this as a number problem by saying the sum of two numbers is 30.

If we let x to be Annie's age, then Anna's age is 30 - x.

Then, the next sentence states that Anna's age is 9 years more than half of Annie's age. By translating into a number problem, this is the same as saying 30 - x is equal to 9 plus half of x.

[tex]30 - x = \frac{x}{2} + 9[/tex]

Then, we solve for x.

[tex]60 - 2x = x + 18[/tex]

[tex] - 3 x = 18 - 60[/tex]

[tex] - 3x = - 42[/tex]

[tex]x = 14[/tex]

We then arrive at x = 14. Since Annie's age is x, this means that Annie is 14 years old.

Next, since Anna's age is represented by 30 - x, we substitute 14 for x and we find out that Anna is 16 years old.

We can check this: the sum of their ages, 14 + 16, is indeed 30. Then, provided by Annie's age 14, Anna's age is nine years plus half of 14. Half of 14 is 7, and when 9 is added to 7, we get 16.

We further conclude that Annie is 14 years old and Anna is 16 years old.

13. What is the ratio of the ages of Annie and Carl is 12:15. Anne is 72 yrs.old. How old is Carl?


Answer: 90

(72÷12) 15 = 90

6 x 15 = 90

ANG SAGOT!

Makatutulong ang paggamit ng proportion sa problem na ito.

Pero ano muna itong proportion?

Eun eung dalawang fraction na napagigitnaan ng equal sign = na nagsasabing magkaparehas lamang ang fractions na eun.

Katulad na lang ng:

[tex] \frac{1}{2} = \frac{2}{4} [/tex]

Ngayon, gamitin na natin, ha?

[tex] \frac{12}{15} = \frac{72}{n} [/tex]

Sa pag-convert ng ratio to fraction, gagawin mo lang na numerator ang unang number at denominator ang pangalawa.

Then eung n ay eung hinahanap nating sagot na kung saan ay edad ni Carl ngayon.

I-solve na natin sa tulong ni cross-multiplication:

[tex]12n = (15)(72) \\ \frac{12n}{12} = \frac{1080}{12} \\ n = 90[/tex]

Ayan, ang edad ni Carl ay 90 years old.
