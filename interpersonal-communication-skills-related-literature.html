9. what is the importance of intrapersonal and interpersonal skills in communication


             Blessed are all humans with a special ability to interact among themselves. A collection of skills are used to communicate; hard skills, and soft skills. The advanced and technical skills are called hard skills, while soft skills are called interpersonal skills.

            The two "interpersonal and intrapersonal" words are self-explicative. The term 'inter' means both inside and 'intra.' This is called interpersonal contact when a conversation takes place between two or more persons, while if the contact is solely within a single individual, it is intrapersonal.

Interpersonal Skills

          Developing interpersonal skills is a very critical factor for leaders as they are expected to accomplish their objectives and ambitions with what they do. There have to be a variety of things happening to boost contact with others. First, recognizing the need to act properly is necessary in order to inspire listeners towards greater achievements.

Intrapersonal Communication Skills  

           Intrapersonal skills are those communication abilities that exist within an individual's own mind, which are not interpersonal abilities, i.e. interactions with other individuals or personalities. Most times a constructive internal conversation that takes place within the mind and initiates a suitable response and disposition as far as intrapersonal abilities are concerned.

             Although intrapersonal and interpersonal skills are different with respect to the intended audience, there is a connection between them. Intrapersonal skills allow the communicator to connect with him or herself while interpersonal skills allow the communicator to deliver a message to its listeners.

An effective and successful housekeeper has several important skills, including an eye for detail and organization skills. What are housekeeping skills?

Housekeeping skills are skills that allow a housekeeper or maid to be effective at their job. These skills can range from customer service to being detail-oriented, and they may vary based on a housekeeper's specific duties and place of employment. For example, a housekeeper who works in a hospital may have to abide by strict cleanliness guidelines to ensure patients are in a clean and healthy environment.

On the other hand, a personal housekeeper may only perform light cleaning duties such as dusting and vacuuming. Regardless of a housekeeper's duties and place of work, they should have several housekeeping skills to be successful at their job.

Time management skills

Flexibility

Housekeeping hard skills

Time management skills

Housekeepers often work on their own or in a small group of other housekeepers and must complete a set number of cleanings each day. Being able to properly manage time is a key skill needed by a housekeeper to ensure they complete all cleanings on time. They must also be punctual for cleaning appointments and finish cleaning in the time frame expected by the client.

28. Differentiate technical skill vs. conceptual and decision skills vs. interpersonal and communication skills.


TECHNICAL SKILL

It is an ability that is obtained through learning its concept and needs to be honed or practiced often in order for someone to learn and be used to it. Examples of these are Drawing, Dancing, Singing, Biking, Baking, Cooking, and others.

DECISION SKILL

An ability to make wise choices when it comes to addressing situations. This skill uses knowledge on certain things and relevant ideas to be able to find a conclusion and make an action for it.

CONCEPTUAL SKILL

An ability to comprehend a detail or scenarios and employ a wise or creative reaction to it. To put in another way,  an ability to come up with wonderful ideas.

COMMUNICATION SKILL

An ability to conduct a conversation with others or express your thoughts in a good and understandable manner in both verbal and non-verbal communication.

INTERPERSONAL SKILL

This is not just about being able to communicate with others. This is an ability to connect with others or comprehend people. Another way to tell it, being able to be part of a group or be on the same boat with someone.
