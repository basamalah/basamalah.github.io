1. 10 example of Go Foods


Answer:

Go Foods are foods that provide fuel and help us ‘go’ and be active. Some examples of ‘Go’ foods include bread, rice, pasta, cereals and potato. These foods give us the energy to run, swim, jump, cycle and our brain fuel to concentrate. If we don’t eat enough ‘Go’ foods, we can easily feel tired.

Explanation:

Go Foods are part of the basic food group which includes the Go, Grow and Glow foods. These foods are the basic consumption of a person to obtain maximum health development and strength.

Read more about the basic food group: https://brainly.ph/question/1037480

Samples of Go Foods Bread Rice Pasta Cereal Potato Corn Grains Noodles Cake Pancakes

Benefits of Go Foods

Go Foods give us the energy to do our tasks daily. These foods are rich in carbohydrates.
