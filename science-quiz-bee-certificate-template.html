3. Kyla got 60 points in a Science Quiz Bee. This is 75% of the total number of points of the said contest. What is the total number of points in the Science Quiz BeeMay solution po sana kung pano nakua sagot​


Answer:75%

Step-by-step explanation:

Answer:

0.75

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the total number of points in the Science Quiz Bee.

We know that Kyla got 75% of the total number of points, which can be written as:

60 = 0.75x

To solve for x, we can divide both sides by 0.75:

60/0.75 = x

Simplifying the right side:

x = 80

Therefore, the total number of points in the Science Quiz Bee is 80.

To check if our answer is correct, we can verify if Kyla's score of 60 is indeed 75% of 80:

60/80 = 0.75

The result is 0.75, which confirms that Kyla's score is indeed 75% of the total number of points in the Science Quiz Bee.

15. The final round in Mathematics Quiz Bee during the Mathematics Week Celebration consists of 5 questions, The marking scheme is as follows: 5 points for every correct answer; -2 points for every incorrect answer; and 0 point for every question not answered.a. What is the minimum score for the quiz bee?b. What is the maximum score for the quiz bee?c. Write a situation where a contestant scores 5 points in the quiz bee.d. Write a situation where a contestant scores -1 for the quize. If you are one of the contestants, what the tihings you should bear in mind in order to earn the most possible score​


Answer:

a. The minimum score for the quiz bee is -10 points because 5 x (-2) = -10.

b. The maximum score for the quiz bee is 25 points because 5 x 5 = 25.

c. A situation where a contestant scores 5 points in the quiz bee is if they answered 1 question correctly and did not answer or answered the other 4 questions incorrectly.

d. A situation where a contestant scores -1 for the quiz is if they answered 1 question incorrectly and did not answer or answered the other 4 questions correctly.

e. Some things a contestant should bear in mind in order to earn the most possible score are: studying and practicing beforehand, carefully reading and understanding each question, and taking the time to think through each answer before choosing the most correct one.
