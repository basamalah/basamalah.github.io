29. How many calories of heat are required to change the temperature of 100 g of water at 5 Celsius degree? a) 100 b) 250 c) 500 d) 5400


100 g of water at 5 Celsius
Equals 500Solution:

How many calories of heat are required to change the temperature of 100 g of water at 5 Celsius degree? 

Given: temperature = 5°C and mass =100g

So by using this formula:

Heat =  mass x temperature x ( 1 cal / g°C)
Heat = 100g x 5°C x  ( 1 cal / g°C)
Heat = 500°C x  ( 1 cal / g°C)
Heat = 500 calories

Therefore, c. 500 calories of heat are required to change the temperature of 100 g of water at 5 Celsius degree
