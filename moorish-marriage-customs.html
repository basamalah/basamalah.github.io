6. moorish banquet summary​


Answer:

• Moorish - referring to the North African Muslim dynasty that ruled Spain from 711 until 1492.

• Moors - in English, a Moroccan or once a member of what is now Spain and Portugal's Muslim community. The phrase has limited meaning when it comes to describing the ethnic traits of any community, ancient or modern.

• Banquet - a delectable meal. An extravagant and frequently ceremonial supper for a large group of people, usually in honor of someone. It is also being held in recognition of some occasion or achievement.

MOORISH BANQUET ARRANGEMENT

Guests during a Moorish lunch sit on floor cushions or on low divans. They were handed a huge white napkin to place on their knees and use exclusively to clean their mouths. Because the left hand is considered unclean in Moorish belief, it is exclusively used to handle breads, while the right hand is used to handle other things. They don't eat with forks, spoons, knives, or anything else. When eating, you should only use two fingers and your right thumb, and you should never contact your lips, no matter how greasy it is.

16. what is a moorish banquet?


WHAT IS MOORISH BANQUET?

Moorish Banquet - is a feast for honorable Muslims.

• Moorish - referring to the North African Muslim dynasty that ruled Spain from 711 until 1492.

• Moors - in English, a Moroccan or once a member of what is now Spain and Portugal's Muslim community. The phrase has limited meaning when it comes to describing the ethnic traits of any community, ancient or modern.

• Banquet - a delectable meal. An extravagant and frequently ceremonial supper for a large group of people, usually in honor of someone. It is also being held in recognition of some occasion or achievement.

MOORISH BANQUET ARRANGEMENT

Guests during a Moorish lunch sit on floor cushions or on low divans. They were handed a huge white napkin to place on their knees and use exclusively to clean their mouths. Because the left hand is considered unclean in Moorish belief, it is exclusively used to handle breads, while the right hand is used to handle other things. They don't eat with forks, spoons, knives, or anything else. When eating, you should only use two fingers and your right thumb, and you should never contact your lips, no matter how greasy it is.

MOORISH BANQUET MEAL ORDER

FIRST course - a pale green soup.

SECOND course - a whole roasted lamb called mechour.

THIRD course - another roasted lamb but with sauce added to it.

FOURTH course - the pastilla that takes 48 hours to prepare inside it is shrimps, sweetbreads, tripe, olive, iver, clams and fried eggs but it is difficult to eat using your bare hands.

FIFTH course - the coucous or the rice meal.
