Human resources

This function title is often abbreviated to the initials'HR'.Human resources is a relatively modern management term, coined as early as the 1960s - whenhumanity took a shift as human rights came to a brighter light during the Vietnam Era. The origins of thefunction arose in organizations that introduced 'welfare management' practices and also in those thatadopted the principles of 'scientific management'. From these terms emerged a largely administrativemanagement activity, coordinating a range of worker related processes and becoming known, in time, asthe 'personnel function'. Human resources progressively became the more usual name for this function, inthe first instance in the United States as well as multinational or international corporations, reflecting theadoption of a more quantitative as well as strategic approach to workforce management, demanded bycorporate management to gain a competitive advantage, utilizing limited skilled and highly skilledworkers

23. Identify and explain the various human resource management practices in the pre-hiring, and post-hiring stages of the employment cycle.


HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT PRACTICES IN THE PRE-HIRING AND POST HIRING STAGES OF THE EMPLOYMENT CYCLE

The human resource management practices in the pre-hiring and post hiring stages of the employment cycle  are sourcing, screening, shortlisting and selecting the right candidates for the filling the required vacant positions, orientation, training and motivation. The first process in human resources practices the organization is doing selective hiring to assure that they can hire the right people. Next process in human resources practices is  skimming through resumes and cover letters to find the closest applicant-job-description matches keeping in mind qualifications, experience, skill sets, and projected candidate-organization fit. Then, the human resources practices process is giving training to the employees to improve their productivity and performance. Human resource practices process of hiring helps develop a competent staff who make the business achieve its targets in an efficient and effective way.

Human resource process during pre-hiring and post hiring stages are as follows:

1. Recruitment

2.Selection

3. Employee orientation

4.Training

5. Motivation
