Answer:

There is no easy answer.

- vendor

- trader

- dealer

A vendor is a seller - of anything.

He could be selling counterfeit goods on a market stall or multi-million chunks of real estate.

A trader buys and sells.

He could be operating from the back of his van, or he could be trading in commodities at international level.

A dealer?

Another very ambiguous term. A dealer could be an entirely disreputable buyer and seller, involved with very small exchanges - or could be involved at an international level with multi-million deals.
