Answer:

Yes, the COVID-19 pandemic has had a significant impact on medicine practitioners in Panabo City. This has led to a decrease in the number of appointments and fewer revenues for medical practitioners in the city. Additionally, medical practitioners have had to adapt their practices to adhere to distancing guidelines and the additional use of protective gear, which has added to their costs and reduced the efficiency of their services.
