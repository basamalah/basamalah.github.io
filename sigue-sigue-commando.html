11. error of the mean. Activity: Answer the questions that follow. Write your answer on the blanks provided after each item. A promotional box of Commando brand safety matches is included with a certain brand of detergent ban soap. Instead of claiming an average of 48 sticks (as a regular box does), this one claims to have an average of 38 sticks. Five boxes were opened and the number of safety matches in each box was counted, yielding the following data: (35, 40, 37, 38, 40). Pretend that the data set obtained is the population. 1. What is the mean of the data set? 2. What is the variance of the data set? 3. What is the standard deviation of the data set?​


Answer:

1. Mean = (35+40+37+38+40)/5 = 38

2. Variance = [(35-38)^2 + (40-38)^2 + (37-38)^2 + (38-38)^2 + (40-38)^2]/5 = 4.8

3. Standard deviation = sqrt(4.8) = 2.19 (rounded to two decimal places)
