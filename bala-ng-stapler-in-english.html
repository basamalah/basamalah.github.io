Let Alvin equal x + 8 stickers

You will notice you are not given any information in the problem about Bala which is why I would assign x to Bala.

11. what simple machine is stapler and how to demonstrate it​


Answer:

A stapler is an example of a lever. The staple arm of the stapler acts as a lever, with the fulcrum being the hinge of the stapler. When pressure is applied to the top of the stapler, the staple arm moves down, causing the staple to be pushed through the paper and bent.

To demonstrate the lever action of a stapler, you can take a stapler and a small piece of paper. Place the paper on a flat surface and hold the stapler vertically with the staple arm facing down. Press down on the top of the stapler, and the staple arm will move down, pushing the staple through the paper and bending it. This demonstrates how the staple arm of the stapler acts as a lever, with the hinge of the stapler acting as the fulcrum.
