The Feature of Persuasive Texts

The feature of persuasive texts is b. Convince or persuade the readers to believe a view or a stand.

Let’s discuss more persuasive texts

What is Persuasive text?

Is a non-fiction text that has the purpose to convince and persuade the reader of a certain point of view. The persuasive text mostly was discovered in adverts and newspaper columns because the company is trying to convince the consumer that their products are the best and the consumer needs to buy their product.

Persuasive text sometimes can be found in a variety of publications, such as:

1. Advertising

2. Newspaper columns

3. Academic Essay

4. Brochures

5. Campaign Flyers

The Features of Persuasive Text

1. The first sentence expresses the author's point of view.

2. It presents reasons and proof to back up this position.

3. It asks rhetorical questions or questions for which there is no correct response.

4. It occasionally—certainly not always—presents contrasting viewpoints.

5. It employs sentimental language
