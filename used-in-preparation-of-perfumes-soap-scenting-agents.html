6. why does scent of perfume spread in the air?


Its because of its "diffusibility" or "diffusion"

This is one of the "Special Properties of Gases"

Gases have no definite shape or volume. The particles that make up gases constantly move about freely and bounce off one another. Gas molecules are widely scattered, so they have the ability to spread out faster. This property is called diffusibility.

When you spray your perfume inside your room, others who are inside the room can also smell your perfume because of diffusibility.
