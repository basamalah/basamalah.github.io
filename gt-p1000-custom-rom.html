24. Denise organized a fund raising project to help calamity victims.she collected a total of P30,500 consisting of P200,P500 and P1000 bills. The number of P200 is twice the number of P1000 and the number of P500 is 15 less than the number of P1000. How many of each bill did she have?​


Answer:

Let the number of P1000 bills be x.

Then, the number of P200 bills is 2x (twice the number of P1000 bills).

And the number of P500 bills is x - 15 (15 less than the number of P1000 bills).

The total amount collected is the sum of the products of the number of bills and their denominations:

30,500 = 200(2x) + 500(x - 15) + 1000x

30,500 = 400x + 500x - 7500 + 1000x

30,500 = 1900x - 7500

105,000 = 1900x

x = 55

Therefore, Denise had 55 P1000 bills, 110 P200 bills (twice the number of P1000 bills), and 40 P500 bills (15 less than the number of P1000 bills).
