29. A chef wants to steam vegetables for 15 minutes. An 11 minutes hourglass and 7 minutes hourglass are available. How can he use this for steaming?


Start to run both hourglasses at the same time and when the 7 mins hourglass is empty, the chef should start to steam until the remaining 4 mins of the 11 mins hourglass is empty and then flip back the 11 minutes hourglass again until it's empty. So 4 mins plus 11 minutes will be equal to 15 minutes.
