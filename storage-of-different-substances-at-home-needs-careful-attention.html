Intermolecular Forces

Answer:

These substances must be stored on a high place. The storage must be out of reach of children and is far from anything that conducts heat such as kitchen. stove, etc. These materials are highly volatile due to the composition of their intermolecular forces. Most of these has a dipole present which could bond with other materials.

We need extra precautions in keeping these items at home due to the fact that their intermolecular forces could easily bond with others which can cause fire. These have alcohol in their intermolecular forces which could also be dangerous when swallowed or eaten.
