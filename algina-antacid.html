2. What are antacids for?


Antacids are a class of medicines that neutralize acid in the stomach. They contain ingredients such as aluminum, calcium, or magnesium which act as bases (alkalis) to counteract the stomach acid and lower pH. They work quickly and are used to relieve symptoms of acid reflux, heartburn or indigestion (dyspepsia).

Antacids are available as liquids or tablets. Some mixtures contain sodium and may not be suitable for people on a sodium restricted diet. Some products combine antacids with alginates and are used in the treatment of Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD). Alginates are gum-like substances that float on top of the stomach contents, forming a raft that acts like a barrier, preventing gastric acid from escaping back up the esophagus.

Antacids are usually only used short-term or occasionally. Longer acting medicines that work by reducing or preventing the secretion of stomach acid over a 12 to 24-hour period may be more effective for some people.Antacids may be used: To reduce the symptoms of acid reflux which may cause heartburn or inflammation of the gullet (oesophagitis). These conditions are sometimes called gastro-oesophageal reflux disease (GORD). To relieve some of the symptoms caused by ulcers in the stomach and part of the gut called the duodenum.

5. magbigay ng limang halimbawa ng antacid​


Answer:

Narito ang limang halimbawa ng antacid:

1. Maalox - Ang Maalox ay isang antacid na naglalaman ng mga sangkap na magnesium at aluminum. Ito ay ginagamit upang makatulong na maibsan ang sakit ng tiyan, pananakit ng sikmura, at pagpapakulo ng tiyan.

2. Tums - Ang Tums ay isang antacid na naglalaman ng calcium carbonate. Ito ay ginagamit upang makatulong na maibsan ang sakit ng tiyan, pananakit ng sikmura, at acid reflux.

3. Mylanta - Ang Mylanta ay isang antacid na naglalaman ng mga sangkap na magnesium, aluminum, at simethicone. Ito ay ginagamit upang makatulong na maibsan ang sakit ng tiyan, pananakit ng sikmura, at mga sintomas ng gas at bloating.

4. Gaviscon - Ang Gaviscon ay isang antacid na naglalaman ng sodium alginate, sodium bicarbonate, at calcium carbonate. Ito ay ginagamit upang makatulong na maibsan ang acid reflux at iba pang mga problema sa tiyan.

5. Rolaids - Ang Rolaids ay isang antacid na naglalaman ng mga sangkap na calcium carbonate at magnesium hydroxide. Ito ay ginagamit upang makatulong na maibsan ang sakit ng tiyan, pananakit ng sikmura, at acid reflux.

Answer:

Antacids work by counteracting (neutralising) the acid in your stomach. They do this because the chemicals in antacids are bases (alkalis) which are the opposite of acids. A reaction between an acid and base is called neutralisation. This neutralisation makes the stomach contents less corrosive.
