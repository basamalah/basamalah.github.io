It saw the of dictatorship that President Marcos set up on September 21, 1972, when he placed the Philippines under martial rule, initiating a regime that did not only suppress the writers' right to free expression but also created conditions that made collaboration and cooption convenient choices for artists struggling for recognition and survival.
