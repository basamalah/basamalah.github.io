2. magbigay ng halimbawa ng paraphrase, abstrak at rebyu sa tagalog


Ang paraphrase o paraprasis sa tagalog ay nangangahulugan ng pagpapaliwanag sa isang tekstong nabanggit. Halimbawa, ang mga pananalitang "masamang damo" ay maaaring paliwanagin bilang masamang tao. Ang abstrak o abstract naman ay tulad sumaryo dahil naglalaman ito ng pangkalahatang ideya ngunit sa maiksing paraan o parapo. Halimbawa, ang abstrak ng kwneto ng pagong at matsing ay, may isang mayabang na matsing na humamon sa pagong na magkarera sa pagtakbo. Sa kalagitnaan ng kanilang takbuhin ay natulog muna ang matsing sa itaas ng puno dahil nakita niya kung gaano kabagal ang pagong. Inisip niya na mauunahan niya ito kahit matulog siya. Ngunit sa isang iglap ng magising siya, natanaw niya ang pagong na malapit na sa dulo o finish line. Kaya natao ang matsing. Sinasaad ng abstrak ang kwento sa maikling paraan. Samantalang ang rebyu o review sa Ingles ay ang detalyadong pagsusuri ng isang pelikula, kwento, o akda. Ginagawa ito ng isa na may akda upang malaman ang tingin ng iba sa kaniyang ginawa. Halimbawa, ang rebyu ng pelikulang "Kita Kita" ay, nakakabitin at may kirot o panghihinayang sa damdamin ang dulong bahagi ng kwento nito. Kakaiba ang daloy ng istorya at tunay na nakakapukaw ng interes para sa isa.

5. What is a paraphrase and how do you paraphrase?


Answer:

Paraphrasing means formulating someone else's ideas in your own words. To paraphrase a source, you have to rewrite a passage without changing the meaning of the original text. Paraphrasing is an alternative to quoting, where you copy someone's exact words and put them in quotation marks.

Example 1. She made me angry when she was rude at dinner. This paraphrase is an example of a rewording which shortens and simplifies while maintaining the same meaning.

WHAT IS PARAPHRASING?

When we say paraphrasing, this is a method of reconstructing, restating, or making another meaning of composition of a text, statement and passage.

EXAMPLE

Original

The lad wants to play basketball with his friends all day, in order to improve his shooting.

The teenager really wants to increase his accuracy and percentage in shooting, that's why he urged his friends to practice basketball with him the entire day.

24. Paraphrasing must be done properly to avoid plagiarism. What is paraphrasing?


Paraphrasing is an art of copying a phrase or a sentence without exactly copying the phrase or the sentence itself. This can be done by rearranging the sentence structure and also substituting words with its synonyms, thus altering the appearance of the sentence without altering the meaning of it.

For example:
"Playing video games for a long time may affect your attitude and character"
You can paraphrase it to:
"Gaming for extended periods of time might change one's values and personality"
