Judging from the open ended question, we can consider the closest possibilities of the occupations by elimination.

A few pointers that need to be added to the question are the activites and clues given.

The doctor had lunch with the teacher. The teacher and the writer went fishing with Anika. The painter is related to the engineer. The doctor hired the painter to do a job. Kyle lives next door to the writer. Klarence beat Kyle and the painter at tennis. Klarence is not the doctor.  

Here we can start assuming some of the occupations.

First off we have a clue that Klarence "is not the doctor" we then eliminate the possibility right away and leave the remaining occupations.

Now since "Klarence beat Kyle and the painter at tennis", we can assume that Kyle is separated from the painter, which then leads to Anika being the Painter .

So far we have:

Anika is a painter

Now we can go to "The doctor hired the painter to do the job". Here we have a great clue, Klarence is NOT the doctor and we know the painter is Anika. So Kyle is most definitely the doctor.

So far we have:

Anika is a painter. Kyle is a doctor.

Now since we have "The teacher and the writer went fishing with Anika" we can assume that the person is one in the same person. Since they each hold 2 jobs, we can eliminate Anika and Kyle from being the teacher and writer. This then leaves us with assuming that Klarence is the teacher and writer.

So far we have:

Anika is a painter. Kyle is a doctor. Klarence is a teacher and writer.

Now we have "The painter is related to the engineer". Since we know who the painter is (Anika) and Klarence already has 2 jobs, then we can assume that Kyle is the engineer, thus leaving us with the last job which is the lawyer being Anika.

Anika is the lawyer and painter.

Kyle is the engineer and the doctor

12. ₱ 500 is to be divided between Kyle and Andrew so that Kyle will receive ₱35 more than Andrew. How much does each person receive?​


Answer:

Kyle: 267.5

Andrew: 232.5

Step-by-step explanation:

First, you can make an algebraic formula representing the values.

K + A = 500

K = A + 35

Solving this, you substitute K from the second equation to the first.

A + 35 + A = 500

2A = 465

A = 232.5

Since you have the value for Andrew, then:

500 - 232.5 = 267.5

Therefore, Kyle will receive 267.5 pesos, and Andrew will receive 232.5 pesos.
