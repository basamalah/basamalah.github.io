20. who can help me with my math assignment the topic is about picewise function the problem is " A videoke machine can be rented for P1000 for three days, but for the fourth day onwards, an additional cost of P100 per day is added . Represent the cost of renting a videoke machine as a piecewise function of the number of days it is rented.​


Answer:

a) f(x) = 1,000;  0 < x ≤ 3

b) f(x) = 1,000 + 100(x-3);   x > 3

Step-by-step explanation:

Flat or fixed rate for 3 days: Php1,000

Additional rate per day after 3 days: Php100/day

Number of days: x

Total Charges: f(x)

Equation:

Piecewise function:

a) f(x) = 1,000;  0 < x ≤ 3

b) f(x) = 1,000 + 100(x-3);   x > 3

Application:

a)

Number of days rented: 2 days

The function to be used is (a) because x = 2 days

f(2) = 1,000

Total charges is Php 1,000.

b)

Number of days = 5

The function to be used is (b).

f(5) = 1,000 + 100 (5-3)

f(5) = 1,000 + 100(2)

f(5) = 1,000 + 200

f(5) = 1,200

Total charges is Php1,200.

23. what is your idea on the karaoke/videoke phenomenon?​


Stimulates The Brain:

Performing karaoke stimulates your brain because it raises the bar on all of those things you normally do when singing alone or to yourself. It also causes activity in the neurons of your brain. These are the neurons that bring together emotional, physical and psychological activity.

Stress Relief

Singing often makes people happy, especially when they are singing along to their favorite song and let themselves let loose to have a good time. Singing karaoke in front of a crowd also releases endorphins that help to reduce anxiety and stress.
