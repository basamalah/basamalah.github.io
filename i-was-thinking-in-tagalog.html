I have learned a new word it is " Misfit "

It means a person whose behavior or attitude sets them apart from others in an uncomfortably conspicuous way.

In Tagalog, this translates to isang tao na ang pag-uugali o saloobin ay nagtatakda sa kanila na naiiba sa iba sa isang hindi komportableng paraan.

I think the moral or lesson of this chapter is;

Those who know they are bad people and those who are bad people yet who persist in believing they are good.

I can apply this lesson in my daily life by;
