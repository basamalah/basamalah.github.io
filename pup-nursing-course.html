28. why do you think microbiology and parasitology is included in the curriculum of nursing course ?​


Explanation:

Microbiology and parasitology are important components of the nursing curriculum because they provide nurses with essential knowledge about infectious diseases and parasites. This knowledge gives nurses the ability to identify and diagnose potential health issues, as well as develop effective plans of care and treatment. Additionally, nurses need to understand the transmission of infectious diseases in order to provide appropriate preventive measures and interventions. Finally, nurses must be aware of the various medical treatments available for infectious diseases and parasites in order to appropriately refer patients for diagnosis and treatment.

The given are 168 total number of students, [tex]\frac{2}{3}[/tex] will take technical courses and [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex] will take education. We need to know how many students will enroll in nursing.

Step 1: Write the mathematical statement of the problem. Let x be the number of students who will enroll in nursing. So,

[tex]\frac{2}{3}[/tex] + [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex]  + x = 1

Step 2: Rearrange the equation to solving the unknown variable. Hence,

x = 1 - [tex]\frac{2}{3}[/tex]  - [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex]

Step 3: Get the least common denominator to perform subtraction. The LCM for the given fractions is 12. Thus,

x = [tex]\frac{12 - 8 - 3}{12}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{1}{12}[/tex]

[tex]\frac{1}{12}[/tex] is the fraction of students who enrolled in nursing or

Let us check the answer:

168 x [tex]\frac{2}{3}[/tex] = 112 students  ; 168 x [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex] = 42 students

168 - 112 - 42 = 14 students

Therefore, [tex]\frac{1}{12}[/tex] or 14 students will enroll in nursing.
