10. 5. In no more than three (3) sentences, provide situations that differentiate entrepreneurial heart flame and gut game.


Answer:

Entrepreneurial heart flame is when an individual follows their instincts and passion when starting a business. Gut game is when an individual takes calculated risks to make a business successful. Entrepreneurial heart flame would be when a person starts a business without researching the market first, while gut game would be when a person takes the time to do market research before starting a business.

Entrepreneurial heart flame refers to the passion and enthusiasm that drives an entrepreneur to pursue their business goals, while gut game refers to the ability to make quick decisions and take risks based on intuition and instinct. Situations that differentiate the two include a strong desire to create positive change and a deep commitment to the business for entrepreneurial heart flame, while gut game may be demonstrated in situations where quick decisions must be made in response to unexpected challenges or opportunities.
