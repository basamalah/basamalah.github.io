1. A special edition MasterCard has been issued to 785 customers. Of these customers, 401 hold a Visa card, 408 hold an American Express card, and 85 hold a Visa card and an American Express card. Find the probability that a customer chosen at random holds a Visa card, given that the customer is an American Express card hold


The probability that a customer chosen at random holds a Visa card, given that the customer is an American Express card holder is 85/408 = 20.79%.

Explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total number of American Express card holders (N_AE)

Step 2: Calculate the total number of Visa card holders (N_V)

Step 3: Calculate the number of American Express card holders who also hold a Visa card (N_AEV): N_AEV = N_AE x 85/100

Step 4: Calculate the probability that a customer chosen at random holds a Visa card, given that the customer is an American Express card holder (P_V): P_V = N_AEV/N_V = (N_AE x 85/100)/N_V

3. How is a debit card similar to an ATM card?


A debit card is similar to an ATM card as stated below:

1. Both can store monetary value equivalent to your balance in your bank account.
2. They have the same size.
3. Both can withdraw in an ATM machine.
4. Both can be used to pay goods and services. You can now use your ATM and debit card in selected outlets like SM to pay for goods and services.
5. Both requires you to maintain an account in a bank.
