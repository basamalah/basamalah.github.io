The probability of drawing a specific card from a standard deck of 52 cards depends on the type of card. The probability of drawing a specific letter from the word MATHEMATICS depends on the number of occurrences of the letter and the total number of letters in the word.

The probability of getting ACE

a. The probability of drawing an ace is 4/52 or 1/13 since there are 4 aces in a deck of 52 cards.

The probability of getting FACE CARD

b. The probability of drawing a face card is 12/52 or 3/13 since there are 12 face cards (Jacks, Queens, and Kings) in a deck of 52 cards.

The probability of getting RED  CARD

c. The probability of drawing a red card is 26/52 or 1/2 since there are 26 red cards (13 diamonds and 13 hearts) in a deck of 52 cards.

The probability of getting A HEART

d. The probability of drawing a heart is 13/52 or 1/4 since there are 13 hearts in a deck of 52 cards.

The probability of getting FACE CARD

e. The probability of not drawing a face card is 40/52 or 10/13 since there are 40 non-face cards in a deck of 52 cards.

The probability of getting TWO DIAMONDS

f. The probability of drawing the two of diamonds is 1/52 since there is only one two of diamonds in a deck of 52 cards.

The probability of getting JOKER

g. The probability of drawing a Joker is 0 since a standard deck of 52 cards does not contain any Jokers.

The probability of getting RED KING

h. The probability of drawing a red king is 2/52 or 1/26 since there are two red kings (the king of diamonds and the king of hearts) in a deck of 52 cards.

The probability of getting BLACK JACK

i. The probability of drawing a black Jack is 2/52 or 1/26 since there are two black Jacks (the Jack of spades and the Jack of clubs) in a deck of 52 cards.

The probability of getting ACE SPADES

j. The probability of drawing the ace of spades is 1/52 since there is only one ace of spades in a deck of 52 cards.

7. A. Solve: top 1. A card is drawn from a deck of playing cards. What is the probability of drawing: a. a king c. a red card e. 2 or jack g. red or black card i. ace or clubs k. 10 or red card b. face card d. an ace f. heart or the king of spades? h. black card or queen of heart j. face card or diamond 1. number card or spade​


Answer:

a. P(king) = 4/52 = 1/13

b. P(face card) = 12/52 = 3/13

c. P(red card) = 26/52 = 1/2

d. P(ace) = 4/52 = 1/13

e. P(2 or jack) = P(2) + P(jack) - P(2 and jack) = 4/52 + 4/52 - 1/52 = 7/52

f. P(heart or king of spades) = P(heart) + P(king of spades) - P(heart and king of spades) = 13/52 + 1/52 - 1/52 = 13/52 = 1/4

g. P(red or black card) = P(red) + P(black) - P(red and black) = 1/2 + 1/2 - 0 = 1

h. P(black card or queen of hearts) = P(black card) + P(queen of hearts) - P(black card and queen of hearts) = 26/52 + 1/52 - 0 = 1/2

i. P(ace or clubs) = P(ace) + P(clubs) - P(ace and clubs) = 4/52 + 13/52 - 1/52 = 4/13

j. P(face card or diamond) = P(face card) + P(diamond) - P(face card and diamond) = 12/52 + 13/52 - 1/52 = 24/52 = 6/13

k. P(10 or red card) = P(10) + P(red card) - P(10 and red card) = 4/52 + 26/52 - 2/52 = 28/52 = 7/13

l. P(number card or spade) = P(number card) + P(spaded card) - P(number and spaded card) = 40/52 + 13/52 - 10/52 = 43/52
