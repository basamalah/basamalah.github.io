23. which of the following application is applied to microwaves?​


Answer:

Microwaves – Basics, Applications and Effects

Explanation:

Microwaves refer to the electromagnetic rays with frequencies between 300MHz and 300GHz in the electromagnetic spectrum. Microwaves are small when compared with the waves used in radio broadcasting. Their range is in between the radio waves and infrared waves. Microwaves travel in straight lines and they will be affected lightly by the troposphere. They don’t require any medium to travel. 25. 22. This involves dissolving the fertilizer material in water and then applying it with the use of a sprinkler. a. Foliar Application c. Localized Application b. Fertigation d. Applied with the seed​


Answer:

D. Fertigation

Explanation:

Fertigation

Fertigation is a method of fertilizer application in which fertilizer is incorporated within the irrigation water by the drip system. In this system fertilizer solution is distributed evenly in irrigation. The availability of nutrients is very high therefore the efficiency is more. In this method liquid fertilizer as well as water soluble fertilizers are used. By this method, fertilizer use efficiency is increased from 80 to 90 per cent.
