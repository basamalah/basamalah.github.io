4. IGUHIT ANG GRAND STAFF SA CARTOLINA.ANO PO BA YUNG GRAND STAFF​


Answer:

Ang kombinasyon ng dalawang clef sa musika sa isang awitin ay tinatawag na Grand Staff. Ang dalawang clef ay ang G-clef at ang F clef. Bawat linya at espasyo ay may kanya-kanyang tunog.

Explanation:

Ito ang paraan kung paano mo ito iguguhit.

1. Gumawa ka ng 2 staff. Ang Staff ay may limang linya at apat na espasyo.

2. Sa staff na nasa itaas doon mo iguhit ang G clef at sa staff na nasa ibaba ay doon mo iguhit ang F clef.

3. Sa gitna ng staff ay gumuhit ka ng ledger line at lagyan mo ito ng nota na ipinapagamit. Kadalasan ay whole note. Ito ang magsisilbi mong middle C.

Sa baba ng unang linya ng G clef ito ang D o re. Ang 5 linya ng Gclef ay may kaukulang pangalan na E-G-B-D-F  o mi-so-ti-re-fa at ang 4 na espasyo ay F-A-C-E o fa-la-do-mi

Samantalang ang 5 linya ng F clef ay - G-B-D-F-A o so-ti-re-fa-la  at ang 4 na espasyo ay A-C-E-G o la-do-mi-so (ang pagpapangalan ay laging magsisimula sa unang linya sa ibaba ng F-clef)

12. On a map, the distance between village A and village B measures 6 centimeters and the distance between village A and village C measures 9 centimeters. The actual distance from village A to village B is 20 kilometers. If the distance between two points on a map is directly proportional to the actual distance between the locations, how far is it from village A and village C in kilometers?


Using the idea of ratio and proportion you'll have the distance from A to C as 
[tex] \frac{AC}{9cm} = \frac{20km}{6cm} [/tex]
[tex]AC = \frac{(20km)(9cm)}{6cm} [/tex]
cm will cancel out

29. 10.A rectangular shaped village has a perimeter of 12km.If the population density is 2100 people per square km and width of the village is 2km, calculate the approximate population of the village.


Perimeter = 2(length) + 2(width)

Let x be the length, solve for length:

12 = 2x + 2(2)

12 - 4 = 2x

2x = 8

2x/2 = 8/2

x = 4  

length = 4 km

Find the area of the village:

Area = length  ×  width

Area = (4 km) (2 km)

Area = 8 km²

Find the approximate population of the village given the population density:

2,100 people/square km   × 8 square km = 16,800 people

Answer:  16,800 people
