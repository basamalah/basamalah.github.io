5. informal meaning of the following words *supplication *magnanimous *diversityThank you​


Answer:

SUPPLICATION - Think of a supplication as sort of a prayer, a request for help from a deity. The word carries a sense of awe and adoration with it, suggesting something tentative, even servile, a respectful appeal to a higher power.

MAGNANIMOUS - adjective. generous in forgiving an insult or injury; free from petty resentfulness or vindictiveness: to be magnanimous toward one's enemies. high-minded; noble: a just and magnanimous ruler. proceeding from or revealing generosityy or nobility of mind, character, etc.: a magnanimous gesture of forgiveness.

DIVERSITY - the practice or quality of including or involving people from a range of different social and ethnic backgrounds and of different genders, sexual orientations, etc.

19. Does supplication form part of worship?​


Prayer is supplication; Worship is adoration. Prayer is a request; Worship is thanks giving for answering your request. Prayer is a conversation with God; Worship is an expression of gratitude for the outcome of the conversation.
