Moreover, there are interactions that would work best if associated with the transactional or interactive models where the sender(s) and receiver(s) can interchange roles. 29. what are the importance of studying community dynamics and community action


Answer:

Community dynamics refers to all changes that are happening within a community. These changes can happen through community action. Changes in the community happens when policy-makers or stakeholders make an action.

These actions refer to creation or development of products, services and policies that directly benefit a particular community. In the process, factors were considered such as the needs of the community, the priorities of the people and the availability of resources. Community dynamics and community action fall under the umbrella of economics because these two rely heavily on human capital [the labor force]. Their aim is to produce products and services that are of value to the people. Needless to say, money is always involved [financial capital] when we want to implement changes to cover the expenses.

With these being presented, it is but appropriate to say that economics play a vital role in the realization of community dynamics through community actions.
