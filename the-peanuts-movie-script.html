14. The label of the peanut butter indicate that its mass is 150 g and it is 70% peanut .if you consume the whole peanut butter,how much peanut did you eat


Answer:

mass peanut = 105 g

Explanation:

mass peanut = (percent peanut / 100) × mass peanut butter

mass peanut = (70 / 100) × 150 g

mass peanut = 105 g

5.35 kilograms of peanut

Answer:

5.35 kilograms is the weight of the peanuts

Step-by-step explanation:

1000 grams is equal to = 1 kilogram

The given number is 5 which makes 5 kilograms

10 grams is equal to 0.010 kilograms it is 50 which makes 0.050 kilograms

100 grams is equal to 0.100 kilograms it is 300 which makes 0.300 kilograms

Hence, the answer is 5.35 kilograms.
