3. the more you hate the more you love


Answer:

Well you see…love and hate aren't opposites…the greater your hate for someone, the greater your love for them might have been in the past. Also, hate leaves a scar, a deeper one the longer it lasts and festers over time. Think of it like a pool of emotion…hate made that scar…but the deeper someone hurts (yes, hate can often be inspired from pain, not all of the time, but most of the time), the greater capacity they have for happiness and love. After all, a deeper pool means greater volume. On the day you let your hate fade away, you'll find that it never left. It just changed into love instead. Hate goes away only with forgiveness and understanding, and that pool your hate carved will be filled with great love instead. It'll all come to you in an unbelievable swell of emotion. That's the day most people wait for their whole lives, actually. Some people never reach that day, and some do. It all depends on both how their lives play out, and how they handle it.
