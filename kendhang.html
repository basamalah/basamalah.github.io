1. what kind of asian instrument is kendhang? ​


Kendhang/kendang or Gendang is a two-headed drum used by peoples from Maritime Southeast Asia.

Kendhang is an asian historical instrument this instrument is a durable instrument

2. kendhang is from what country


Answer:

The kendhang gendhing is a double-headed membranophone of the Javanese people of the island of Java, Indonesia. It is the largest of three hand drums typically found in a modern Central Javanese gamelan set.

10. What is the instrument that has seven bronze bars placed on top of a resonating frame?A. Gender instrumentB. Bonang instrumentC. Saron instrumentD. Kendhang instrument​


Answer:

C.

Explanation:

The saron is a musical instrument of Indonesia, which is used in the gamelan. It normally has seven bronze bars placed on top of a resonating frame (rancak). It is usually about 20 cm (8 in) high, and is played on the floor by a seated performer.
