22. Impact of implementation of curfew hours to the night vendorspakisagot mo pleasee​


Answer:

The implementation of curfew hours can have a significant impact on night vendors who operate during late hours. These vendors often rely on their earnings from these hours to support themselves and their families. With the curfew in place, night vendors may experience a loss of income and reduced sales, which can greatly affect their livelihood. It may also lead to a decline in the number of vendors operating during the late hours, which can have a ripple effect on the local economy and lead to a loss of jobs.

Moreover, night vendors may face difficulties in adhering to the curfew regulations. For instance, they may face challenges in packing up and leaving their vending locations before the curfew time, especially if they have a significant amount of merchandise to transport. There may also be issues with enforcement, such as the police not understanding that these individuals are night vendors, leading to potential conflicts. Therefore, it is crucial to consider the impact of curfews on night vendors and come up with solutions that can help mitigate the effects of these policies on their livelihoods.

30. advantage and disadvantages of Curfew Hours for Minors (17 years and below)​


Answer:

Advantages of Teenage Curfew

1. It can be a way to give families access to the services they require.

2. Most curfew laws allow teens to be outside after the permitted time for specific reasons.

3. It reduces the threat of human trafficking and child exploitation.

4. Teenage curfew can teach lessons about personal responsibility.

5. Curfews can provide peace of mind for families.

6. It is an opportunity for parents and teens to set healthy boundaries.

7. Curfew laws can work to prevent gang violence.

Disadvantages of Teenage Curfew

1. It provides parents and teens with a false sense of security.

2. Most curfews are set from an autocratic standpoint.

3. Some teenage curfew rules can encourage rebellion.

4. There can be legal consequences for violating curfews.

5. It impacts minority teens more than any other demographic.

6. Teenage curfew laws create unnecessary conflicts with today’s youth and police.

7. The data to support the idea of curfews is lacking.

8. Neighborhoods without curfew sweeps often see less crime.

9. Teenage curfew does not set an example of how the real world works.

10. There are other interventions that reduce youth crime more effectively than curfews.
