19. meaning and message of salidumay


Answer:

Meaning of salidumayDuring the weaving season Salidumay is the reaction of young ladies to men's songs. Pleasant and grateful melodies are the salidumay. Songs act as a symbol of acceptance or rejection in the lives of young people of a century of courtesy.

Message of salidumay

The song plays an significant part in the lives of the young people who loved the Mountains of the Cordillera. Young women embrace or deny a mans who courts this song as part of tradition. They sing with melodies that convey their true feelings to the men who woo them.
