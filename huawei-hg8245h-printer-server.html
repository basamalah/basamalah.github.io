16. Which of the following is the best example of a small office/home office (SOHO) network?A. A home network of four laptops, two tablets, and a printerB. A small business with between 20 and 50 laptops and two printersC. A medium-sized business with between 100 and 500 client devices and several printersD. A large business with over 1,000 total client devices, file servers, and printers​


Answer:

A. A home network of four laptops, two tablets, and a printer

Explanation:

SOHO is the abbreviation for Small Office/Home Office network. These days, many budding entrepreneurs and small business owners prefer to work from home or to maintain only a small office. Budding entrepreneurs and small business owners need small network and access to the Internet for their daily work.

SOHO networks are small LANs (Local Area Networks). Typically, SOHO networks consists of less than 10 computers. Network service servers like DNS server, email server, web server etc., are typically configured outside SOHO network.
