1. reflection about the story of st. claire and st. francis of assisi​


Answer:

Clare brought Francis' vision to another level of spirituality and commitment to the Gospel way of life. Francis said that he did not seek brothers but was given us by God. He probably thought similarly about beginnings of Clare and her sisters as she discerned her calling from God.

Most of what I know of Clare I have learned through Clare’s daughters, our sisters. I think that is a great compliment that after eight centuries, the Poor Clares still reflect what Clare wrote in the Second Letter to Agnes: “Always be conscious of your beginnings.” This is as true for us today as when it was written.

Clare’s beginning was to choose to leave her “comfortable life” and follow the mendicant life of Francis. Yet, when she approached Francis, he did not see her becoming a mendicant, as were the friars. Could he not envision a new way for women to minister like he and the friars or did he see a greater need for prayer? Personally, I think it was some of both. Clare brought Francis’ vision to another level of spirituality and commitment to the Gospel way of life.

Francis said that he did not seek brothers but was given us by God. He probably thought similarly about beginnings of Clare and her sisters as she discerned her calling from God. Clare and Francis both trusted completely in God. In their rules, both embraced a singular way of life: Observing the Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Did Francis see in Clare the contemplative life he sought for himself in his early discernment of God’s will? Francis and Clare both knew the importance of prayer for the Church and the world, to praise God and petition for God’s guidance and grace in our world. This need hasn’t changed.

15. clyde,carlo and claire are siblings clyde is 5years old than claire carlo is twice as old as claire if the total of age is 45years how old is clyde​


AGE PROBLEMAnswer with Step-by-step explanation:

Problem:

Clyde, Carlo and Claire are siblings. Clyde is 5 years old than Claire. Carlo is twice as old as Claire. If the total of age is 45 years old, how old is Clyde?

Solution:

Let x be the age of Claire.

x+5 be the age of Clyde since he is 5 years older than Claire.

2x be the age of Carlo since he is twice as old as Claire.

The sum of their ages is 45 so:

age of Claire plus age of Clyde plus age of Carlo equals 45 which can be expressed in an algebraic equation as:

x + (x+5) + 2x = 45

We can combine like terms and do algebraic manipulations to solve for the value of x.

x + x + 2x = 45 – 5

4x = 40

4x/4 = 40/4

x = 10

Hence, the value of x is 10. However, x is only for Claire's age. We are looking for the age of Clyde which is: x + 5. By direct substitution:

Clyde's age = x + 5 = 10 + 5 = 15

Therefore, Clyde's age is 15.

Now let us continue solving the age of Carlo which is equal to 2x = 2(10) = 20.

So Carlo's age is 20 years old.

To check, their sum should equate to 45, so:

10 + 15 + 20 = 45

45 = 45

Therefore, our answer is correct.
