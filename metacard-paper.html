Henry Moseley & Dmitri Mendeleev

Henry Moseley arrange the periodic table of element according to increasing number of protons or atomic number. Whereas Dmitri Mendeleev arrange the periodic table in accordance to increasing atomic mass.

Subatomic particle:

1. Electron  

Electrons carries negative charge and is located outside the nucleus of an atom. The most important electron is named as the valence electron wherein it is very much important specially in chemical bonding.

2. Neutron

Neutron is located inside the nucleus of an atom and carries neutral charge or no charge. It almost has the same mass with protons.

3. Protons

Protons is located inside the nucleus of an atom and it carries positive charge. The number of protons determine the name of the element or its isotopes.
