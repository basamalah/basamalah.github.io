13. A nutritionist poured 0.70 liter of honey into 0.14 liter plastic cups. How many plastic cups could be filled


Answer:

5 cups

Step-by-step explanation:

To find out how many plastic cups can be filled with 0.70 liters of honey, you need to divide the volume of the honey by the volume of each cup.

0.70 liters / 0.14 liters/cup = <<0.70/0.14=5>>5 cups

Therefore, a nutritionist can fill 5 plastic cups with 0.70 liters of honey.

27. a nutritionist poured 0.70 liter of honey into 0.14 liter plastic cups. How many plastic cups could be filled? ​


5 plastic cups could be filled.

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem

The problem is asking for the number of plastic cups that could be filled by the honey. To find the answer, you have to divide the total liter of honey by the liter of honey that each plastic cup could hold.

Given:

total liter of honey - 0.70 liter

liter of honey that each plastic cup could hold - 0.14 liter

Number Sentence:

0.70 ÷ 0.14 = N

Solution:

Remember that to divide decimals, you have to make the divisor a whole number by moving the decimal poi nt to the right. Then, do it also to the dividend. Move the decimal poi nt depending on the number of moves you made with the divisor.

0.14 √0.70 = 14 √70

5

- 70

Final Answer:
