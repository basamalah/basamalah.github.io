17. antecedent and consequent of a song you are my sunshine??​


In "You Are My Sunshine," the antecedent is the statement "You are my sunshine," and the consequent is the response that follows, which includes lyrics such as "My only sunshine," "You make me happy when skies are gray," and "Please don't take my sunshine away." Essentially, the antecedent sets up the subsequent response or consequence, which is the part that follows and is influenced by the antecedent.
