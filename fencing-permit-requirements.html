Answer:

Let the length of the field be "L".

The perimeter of a rectangle is given by the formula:

P = 2(L + W),

where P is the perimeter, L is the length, and W is the width.

In this problem, the width of the field is given as 60 meters, and the total length of fencing required is 300 meters. We can use these values to set up an equation:

300 = 2(L + 60)

Simplifying the equation, we get:

300 = 2L + 120

Subtracting 120 from both sides, we get:

180 = 2L

Dividing both sides by 2, we get:

L = 90

Therefore, the length of the field is 90 meters.
