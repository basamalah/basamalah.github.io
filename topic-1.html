Qualitative research topic in Physics: The impact of student-led experiments on conceptual understanding of physics topics.

Description of the topic: The purpose of this qualitative research would be to explore the effect of student-led experiments on conceptual understanding of physics topics. The study would involve selecting a group of students from a high school or college physics course and dividing them into two groups: one group that participates in traditional lecture-based learning and the other group that participates in student-led experiments. The qualitative data collection would involve interviews and focus groups to assess the students' conceptual understanding of the physics topics covered in the course. The data would be analyzed using qualitative methods to identify themes and patterns in the students' responses. The results of the study would provide insights into the effectiveness of student-led experiments in improving conceptual understanding of physics and could potentially inform pedagogical practices in physics education.
