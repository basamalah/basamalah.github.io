21. The size of the FOV is determined by the objective magnification. When using an eyepiece-objective system, the FOV from the objective is magnified by the eyepiece for viewing.


Answer:

Field of View = Field Number (FN) ÷ Objective Magnification

For instance, if your eyepiece reads 10X/22, and the magnification of your objective lens is 40. First, multiply 10 and 40 to get 400. Then divide 22 by 400 to get a FOV diameter of 0.055
