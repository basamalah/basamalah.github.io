1. 14. For environmental protection, the electricity usage of Mr. Baniqued's family reduced by 12% to 286 kWh this month. a. Find the electric usage of Mr. Baniqued's family last month. b. How much electricity was saved this month?


Answer:

a. To find the electric usage of Mr. Baniqued's family last month, we can use the formula:

electric usage last month = 286 kWh / (1 - 12%)

where 12% is the reduction in electricity usage and can be expressed as 0.12.

So,

electric usage last month = 286 kWh / (1 - 0.12)

and using the formula, we get:

electric usage last month = 286 kWh / 0.88

Therefore, the electric usage of Mr. Baniqued's family last month is approximately 326.14 kWh.

b. To find how much electricity was saved this month, we can use the formula:

electricity saved = electric usage last month - electric usage this month

So,

electricity saved = 326.14 kWh - 286 kWh

Therefore, the electricity saved this month is approximately 40.14 kWh.

15. Johnny is 3 times as old as Mark. 8 years from now, Johnny will be twice as old as Mark, how old is Mark?


Answer:

Mark is 8 years old now.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let Johnny's age = J and Mark's age = M

eq. 1 J = 3M

eq. 2 (J + 8) = 2(M + 8)

Solve for a variable using substitution.

Let's solve for M first.

(J + 8) = 2(M + 8) Substitute J=3M

3M + 8 = 2M + 16 Simplify

M = 8

Now, we can solve for J.

J = 3M

J = 3(8)

J = 24

Mark is 8 years old, while Johnny is 24 years old. Johnny is 3 times as old as Mark. TRUE.

8 years from now, Mark would be 16 years old and Johnny would be 32 years old. Johnny would be twice as old as Mark. TRUE.
