20. where located of mt. guiwan? mt. kalatungan? mt. cuadrado? mt. cetaceo? mt. tapulao?​


Answer:

Mt. Guiwan

Mt. Guiwan is actually located in the Mamparang Range, a section of the Sierra Madre mountain range which branched out from Aurora province to the town of Alfonso Castaneda, the southeastern tip of the province of Nueva Vizcaya.

Mt. Kalatungan

Mount Kalatungan, also known as Catatungan, is a volcanic mountain located in the province of Bukidnon in the southern Philippines.

Mount Cuadrado

Mount Cuadrado is a mountain and is located in Province of Zambales, Central Luzon, Philippines. The elevation above sea level is 1324 metres. Variant forms of spelling for Mount Cuadrado or in other languages: Mount Quebrado, Mount Quadrado, Mount Cuadrado, Mount Cuadrado (en), Mount Cuadrado, Mount Quadrado, Mount Quebrado.

Mount Cetaceo

(Mount Cetaceo) is a mountain (class T - Hypsographic) in Cagayan Valley (Cagayan), Philippines (Asia) with the region font code of Asia/Pacific. It is located at an elevation of 1,500 meters above sea level.

Mount Cetaceo is also known as Mount Cetacea, Mount Cetaceo.

Mount Tapulao

Mount Tapulao (also known as High Peak) is the tallest mountain in the Zambales Mountain range and in the province of Zambales in the Philippines. The peak, which rise to an elevation of 2,037 metres (6,683 ft), is located in the municipality of Palauig, Zambales.
