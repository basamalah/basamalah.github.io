Hospitality is the friendly and generous reception and entertainment of visitors and guests.

Characteristics of Hospitality:

Generosity KindnessConsideration and attentiveness to guest needsResponsive HelpfulFriendly

Hospitality management is the study and practice of managing hotels, resorts, restaurants, and other hospitality-related businesses.

Hospitality industry is a term that encompasses businesses and services related to accommodating, feeding, and entertaining guests and visitors.
