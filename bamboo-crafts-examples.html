12. What is the difference of bamboo craft and wood craft? ​


Answer:

Bamboo differs from wood as it has a hollow stem and lacks rays or visible pores. In order to process bamboo as a timber, the bamboo fibers are pulled, soaked and then pressed together with adhesives. However, as most bamboo is processed in its original country, the standards may vary.

15. 5 tips in bamboo crafting​


Answer:

A bamboo stand is made using bamboo sticks to work on it. Bamboo shoot is cut into four or eight sections (as required). Thin bamboo strips are singled out using a blade. The bamboo strips are further given finer finish on a milling roller. The strips are treated with boric acid in boiling water.
