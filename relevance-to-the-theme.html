11. How is the theme (Dahong palay) still relevant in today's society?


Answer:

The theme "Dahong Palay" or "Red Rice Stalk" is a metaphor for the struggles of the Filipino people and their determination to overcome adversity. It is still relevant today because many of the social and political issues depicted in the theme, such as poverty, inequality, and resistance against oppression, continue to be relevant and prevalent in contemporary Philippine society. The theme serves as a reminder of the resilience and strength of the Filipino people and their fight for a better future.
