13. is 316 dvisible by 12


Answer:

316 is not divisible by 12

Step-by-step explanation:

The number is divisible by 12 if it is divisible by both 4 and 3.

Divisibility by 4: A number is divisible by 4 if the last two digits is divisible by 4. Since 16 is divisible by 4, 316 is divisible by 4.

Divisibility by 3: A number is divisible by 3 if the sum of digits is divisible by 3. (3+1+6=10). 10 is not divisible by 3, so 316 is not divisible by 3.

Hence, 316 is not divisible by 12.
