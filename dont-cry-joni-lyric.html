Answer:

Written by Conway Twitty himself, “Don't Cry Joni” is a song that tells the tale of a fifteen-year-old girl who developed a crush on a twenty-two-year-old Jimmy living next door. Joni was begging for Jimmy to wait for her so that they may someday get married. ... Joni was brought to tears with this realization.
