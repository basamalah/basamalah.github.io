1. explain briefly the difference between Ukiran halus and Ukiran kasar​


Answer:

Ukiran Halus involves the carving of relief patterns, hilts of keris (short Malay dagger), bed heads and cupboard tops. Ukiran Kasar, refers to the carvings on larger objects like furniture, pillars, windows, room portions and eaves of roofs.
