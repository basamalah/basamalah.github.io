2. how to use leaf bar or example of leaf bar in ms excel?help pls:<​


A leaf bar is a type of chart or graph used in Microsoft Excel to visualize hierarchical data. To create a leaf bar chart in Excel, follow these steps:

Open a new workbook in Excel and enter your hierarchical data into the first columns, with the top-level items in the first row and the sub-items beneath them.

Select the data that you want to include in the chart, including the top-level items and all of the sub-items.

On the Insert tab, click the Bar chart button and select the Leaf Bar chart option from the drop-down menu.

The leaf bar chart will be inserted into the worksheet, with the top-level items represented by bars and the sub-items represented by the leaves.

You can customize the appearance of the leaf bar chart by clicking on it and using the Chart Tools that appear in the ribbon. For example, you can change the colors of the bars, add data labels, and modify the axis labels.
