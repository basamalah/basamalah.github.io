6. what is the difference between the major and minor chords in guitar?


The difference between major and minor chords is this: A major chord consists of a 1st, 3rd, and 5th degree of a major scale while a minor chord is made from the 1st, flatted 3rd, and 5th degrees of a major scale. So the only difference is the 3rd degree

21. Since Harana was used to express affection to a woman through singing with the accompaniment of guitar. Do you think Harana is still applicable now a days? Why or why not?________________________________________________________________________________________________​


Answer:

Depending on the place where you live, harana is still applicable. Places like a small village, small barangays in the provinces or other rural and quiet areas at night, this is a happening to behold. This is a very authentic Filipino culture which we could not ignore and should not be forgotten. Harana should be done at night when it is quiet and almost all people are at sleep. In the big cities, it is almost impossible to do this not unless it is a ' modified' harana, which means doing the singing inside the house of a girl or woman. For some, this is an old -fashioned practice and does not fit already in this modern times so they could say that it's not applicable already nowadays.
