9. how to insert audios in movie maker?​


Answer:

1.Click the Home tab.

2.Click the Add Music command button and click Add Music on the pop-up menu.

3.You’ll note that you can also add audio from AudioMicro, the Free Music Archive, and Vimeo — they all appear under the Find New Music Online heading.

Navigate to the desired audio, select it, and then click Open.

After you add music to your movie, it appears as a named “strip” over the items in the Storyboard. To delete the musical score, just click this strip and press the Delete key.
