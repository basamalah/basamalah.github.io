3. Mr. and Mrs. Jain went to theatre with their 2 children. The children were charged with half of the entrance fee. If the total fee charged was ₹36, then the entrance fee for each adult is_


Answer:

₹12

Step-by-step explanation:

since the adult's fee is x2 the children that means if there are 2 adults then there are 2 (x2) and since there is 2 child that means we have 2(x1)

2+2+1+1 = 6

now, we'll need to divide 36 by 6

36/6 = 6

since the adult's fee is x2 than the children then we'll need to x2(6) for 2 times

6 x 2 = 12(Mr.Jain fee)

6 x 2 = 12(Mrs.Jain fee)

6 x 1 = 6(Child number 1 fee)

6 x 1 = 6(Child number 2 fee)

12 + 12 = 24 + 6 = 30 + 6 = 36(Total fee)

25. ina resort entrance fee per person is 80 baht in a group of 10 people it fixed at 70% if the resort give additional additional 50% discount for every person that exists then how much is entrance fee paid by twenty one person with solution plss​


Answer:

Given:

Entrance fee per person = 80 baht

Group size = 10 people

Discount for a group of 10 people = 70%

Additional discount per person = 50%

Number of people = 21

To find:

Entrance fee paid by 21 people after discounts

Solution:

The group of 10 people gets a discount of 70%, which means they will pay only 30% of the total entrance fee.

Total entrance fee for 10 people = 10 x 80 = 800 baht

Entrance fee for 10 people after 70% discount = 800 x 0.3 = 240 baht

So, the entrance fee for 1 person in the group of 10 = 240 / 10 = 24 baht

Now, every person gets an additional 50% discount. Therefore, the new entrance fee per person for the group of 10 people will be:

24 - (24 x 0.5) = 12 baht

The remaining 11 people will each pay 80 baht, and they will also receive a 50% discount:

80 - (80 x 0.5) = 40 baht

Therefore, the total entrance fee paid by 21 people is:

(10 x 12) + (11 x 40) = 120 + 440 = 560 baht

Therefore, the entrance fee paid by 21 people after discounts is 560 baht.
