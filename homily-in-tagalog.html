6. Identify the similarities and difference of sermon and homily​


Answer:

As nouns the difference between sermon and homily

is that sermon is religious discourse; a written or spoken address on a religious or moral matter while homily is a sermon, especially concerning a practical matter

Answer:

Similarities of Sermon and Homily

In colloquial, non-religious, usage, homily often means a sermon concerning a practical matter, a moralizing lecture or admonition, or an inspirational saying or platitude, but sermon is the more appropriate word in these cases.

Difference between Sermon and Homily

Sermon- is to instruct in doctrine or to encourage us to make major changes in our lives.

Homily- to explain scripture, tell us what is happening in the scripture and how it applies to us today.
