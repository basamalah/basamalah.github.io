21. Testable question, Hypotheses and their Variables1. If plants are watered, then plant height will increase. testable question:independent variable:dependent variable:(3)controlled variable:2.If chocolate is given to the teachers, then amound of homeqorm will decrease. testable question:independent variable:dependent variable:(3)controlled variable:3. If trees have leaves, then bird's nest will increase.testable question:independent variable:dependent variable:(3)controlled variable:4. If the water in the pond is more acidic, then the fish population will increase.testable question:independent variable:dependent variable:(3)controlled variable:5.If food rick in calcium is eaten frequently, then bones strength will increase.testable question:independent variable:dependent variable:(3)controlled variable:​


Answer:

Testable question: Will increasing the amount of water given to plants affect their height?

Independent variable: Amount of water given to plants

Dependent variable: Plant height

Controlled variables: Type of plant, soil quality, sunlight exposure

Testable question: Will giving chocolate to teachers affect the amount of homework assigned?

Independent variable: Chocolate given to teachers

Dependent variable: Amount of homework assigned

Controlled variables: Type of chocolate, amount of chocolate given, school policies on homework assignments

Testable question: Will the presence of leaves on trees affect the number of bird's nests in an area?

Independent variable: Presence of leaves on trees

Dependent variable: Number of bird's nests

Controlled variables: Type of trees, presence of other nesting materials, availability of other food sources for birds

Testable question: Will increasing the acidity of the water in a pond affect the fish population?

Independent variable: Acidity of the water in the pond

Dependent variable: Fish population

Controlled variables: Temperature of the water, presence of other predators or competitors, availability of other food sources

Testable question: Will eating food rich in calcium affect bone strength?

Independent variable: Frequency of eating food rich in calcium

Dependent variable: Bone strength

Controlled variables: Type of food, amount of calcium consumed, overall diet and exercise habits

29. Testable question, Hypotheses and their Variables 1. If plants are watered, then plant height will increase. testable question: independent variable: dependent variable: (3)controlled variable: 2.If chocolate is given to the teachers, then amound of homeqorm will decrease. testable question: independent variable: dependent variable: (3)controlled variable: 3. If trees have leaves, then bird's nest will increase. testable question: independent variable: dependent variable: (3)controlled variable: 4. If the water in the pond is more acidic, then the fish population will increase. testable question: independent variable: dependent variable: (3)controlled variable: 5.If food rick in calcium is eaten frequently, then bones strength will increase. testable question: independent variable: dependent variable: (3)controlled variable:​


Answer:

Testable question: Will watering plants cause an increase in plant height?

Independent variable: Watering plants

Dependent variable: Plant height

Controlled variables: Type of plant, soil type, amount of sunlight, temperature, etc.

Testable question: Will giving chocolate to teachers decrease the amount of homework assigned?

Independent variable: Giving chocolate to teachers

Dependent variable: Amount of homework assigned

Controlled variables: Number of students in the class, subject matter being taught, school policies, etc.

Testable question: Will the presence of leaves on trees increase the number of bird's nests in the area?

Independent variable: Presence of leaves on trees

Dependent variable: Number of bird's nests

Controlled variables: Type of trees, location, climate, availability of other nesting materials, etc.

Testable question: Will an increase in the acidity of pond water result in an increase in the fish population?

Independent variable: Acidity of pond water

Dependent variable: Fish population

Controlled variables: Size of pond, presence of other aquatic life, temperature, etc.

Testable question: Will eating food high in calcium frequently increase bone strength?

Independent variable: Eating food high in calcium

Dependent variable: Bone strength

Controlled variables: Age of the individual, overall diet, physical activity level, etc.
