2.The G Major chord, which forms the root of the G scale, is made up of the notes G, B, and D— the first, third, and fifth notes of the key of G. On the guitar, using this basic G chord position, these notes arrive in this order: G, B, D, G, D, and G.

3. The easy F chord starts with your index finger on the 1st fret of the 2nd string. Then use your middle finger on the 2nd fret of the 3rd string.
